## 2) Client-Facing “What to Expect” Page (paste into Replit/Notion)
### Clip Factory — What to Expect (Clips, Review, Delivery)
Welcome to Clip Factory. We turn long videos/podcasts into ready-to-post vertical clips with strong hooks, accurate captions, clean framing, and platform-safe formatting.

**Legitimacy / business page:** https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3  
**Support:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

#### Step 1 — Intake (Required)
Before editing begins, please complete the **Clip Intake Form** (we’ll send it by email). This prevents missed brand terms, caption preferences, or platform requirements.

#### Step 2 — Review Link (Timestamped Feedback)
We deliver a review link (unlisted video / Frame.io / Vimeo / similar) that supports timestamped comments.

**How to leave feedback (fastest):**
1) Watch the clip once for overall flow.
2) On second pass, leave **timestamped notes** like: “0:07 — change caption to ___” or “0:12 — cut 0.5s earlier.”
3) Consolidate requests into one message when possible (fewer loops = faster turnaround).

#### Revision Window
You have **24 hours** from the time we send the review link to request revisions included in your order. If you need more time, reply before the window ends and we’ll confirm an extension.

#### What You Receive (Delivery Pack)
Your final delivery includes:
- **Final MP4(s)** (ready to post)
- **SRT caption file(s)** (if requested)
- **B-roll / visual cue sheet** (what to overlay + where)
- **Posting suggestions**: first-line caption options + hashtag set + recommended posting notes

#### Quality Standards We Check
- No cropped faces / safe zones respected
- Captions accurate + readable (spelling, punctuation, names)
- Audio leveled (no clipping; voice clear)
- Hook clarity in first seconds
- Clean jump cuts and pacing
- Export settings appropriate for vertical platforms

Email us anytime: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

## 3) Internal Policy — Intake Gate (Prevents Refunds)
**Rule:** No editing begins until the client intake form is submitted.

**Exception (allowed):** If the client replies in writing: “OK to start without intake,” we may begin with default settings:
- 9:16 vertical
- Clean minimal captions
- Hook-first trimming
- UI-safe zone padding

**If exception used:** We explicitly state that any changes due to missing intake preferences may be billable change requests.
