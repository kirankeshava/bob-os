— Overview —
We turn long-form video/podcasts into ready-to-post vertical clips (TikTok/Reels/Shorts style) with:
1) Strong hooks
2) Accurate captions (burned-in) + SRT file
3) Clean pacing/jump cuts
4) B-roll cues (optional overlay prompts)
5) Platform-friendly exports

This page explains our QA standards, how review works, what you receive, and what counts as a free revision vs a paid change.


1) What we need from you (Intake)
Before editing starts, please provide:
- Source link/file (YouTube, Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
- Platforms (TikTok / IG Reels / YT Shorts)
- Your handle(s) + website/CTA
- Caption style preferences (emoji, punctuation, highlight words, profanity rules)
- Brand constraints (safe topics, banned words, required disclaimers)
- Any must-include moments/timecodes (optional)

If anything is unclear, we’ll email to confirm before cutting clips. This prevents avoidable revisions and delays.


2) Our QA Checklist (what we check before you see anything)
A) Content & Hook
- Clip has a clear hook in the first 1–2 seconds (question, bold claim, curiosity gap, contrarian insight)
- The clip delivers on the hook (no bait-and-switch)
- Removed dead air, rambling starts, and filler phrases where possible
- Keeps a single clear idea per clip

B) Framing & Visual Safety
- No cropped faces: eyes/nose/mouth remain fully visible; no “chin cut” unless stylistic and approved
- Subject is centered with safe margins for TikTok/IG UI (avoid lower third + far edges)
- No accidental zooms that degrade quality or cut off gestures
- Any B-roll overlays do not cover face or essential on-screen text

C) Captions Accuracy & Readability
- Captions match spoken words (no meaning-changing errors)
- Proper nouns/brand names checked (client handle, product name)
- Timing is readable (not too fast), line breaks are natural
- High contrast captions; consistent style across the batch
- Sensitive words handled per your preference (bleep/asterisks/avoid)

D) Audio & Loudness
- Voice is clear and intelligible; major background noise reduced
- Audio is leveled consistently across clips
- No clipping/distortion
- Balanced music (if used) never competes with speech

E) Editing & Pacing
- Jump cuts are clean (no jarring mid-word chops)
- No repeated phrases due to cutting
- Visual rhythm matches message (cuts emphasize points)
- Clip ends cleanly (no awkward cut-off) with a natural end beat or CTA

F) Platform & Export Settings
- Vertical 9:16, 1080x1920
- H.264 MP4 export, optimized for web
- Consistent naming convention
- No watermarks unless requested


3) Review Flow (timestamp comments)
We send you a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can comment with timestamps.

How to request revisions:
- Leave a timestamped comment for each change (e.g., “00:07 — change caption word to X”, “00:12 — remove ‘um’”) 
- Group feedback in a single pass when possible (reduces turnaround time)

Revision window:
- You have 24 hours from delivery of the first cut to request free revisions (see policy below).


4) What you receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll get a shared folder containing:
A) Final MP4s
- Ready-to-post vertical videos (per platform spec)

B) Caption file(s)
- SRT for each clip (for platforms that support uploads, repurposing, or archive)

C) B-roll Cue Sheet (PDF or Google Doc)
- A per-clip list of suggested overlays/B-roll moments with timestamps

D) Posting Suggestions
- Recommended first-line caption (hook text)
- Hashtag set(s) per platform/niche
- Posting notes (CTA, pinned comment idea, title line for Shorts)


5) Revisions & Issue Triage (free vs paid)
We aim to be fair and fast while protecting delivery timelines.

Free revisions (within the 24-hour window):
- Caption typos or mis-heard words
- Minor timing tweaks to captions
- Small cuts to remove a filler word, stutter, or dead air (that doesn’t change the clip’s core idea)
- Audio leveling tweaks (if voice loudness is inconsistent)
- Minor reframes to avoid cropped face/UI overlap
- Replacing a B-roll overlay prompt with a similar alternative

Paid changes (quoted before work begins):
- Changing the direction/strategy after delivery (new hook angle, new story structure)
- Swapping to different segments beyond the initially selected highlights (new clip selection)
- Major re-edit: adding new sections, significant restructuring, or changing style across the entire batch
- New branding package after delivery (new fonts/colors/layout beyond minor adjustments)
- Additional versions per platform beyond original scope (e.g., multiple caption styles, A/B variants)
- Revisions requested after the 24-hour window (we can still help—just not free)

Refund protection notes:
- If we missed a stated requirement from your intake (platform, banned words, must-include disclaimer), we fix it fast at no cost.
- If requirements weren’t provided up front, we’ll propose the simplest fix and quote any out-of-scope work.


6) How to get the fastest turnaround
- Fill intake fully (handles, CTA, banned words)
- Provide 3 example clips you like (optional) so we match your style
- Leave timestamped feedback in one pass
