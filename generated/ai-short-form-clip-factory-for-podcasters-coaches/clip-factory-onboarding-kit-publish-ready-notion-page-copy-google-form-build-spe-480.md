1) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each order you’ll receive a single Delivery Pack folder containing:
A) Final clips (MP4)
- Vertical 9:16 exports, named clearly
- One file per clip

B) Caption file (SRT)
- One SRT per clip, filename matches the MP4

C) B-roll cue sheet (Google Doc or PDF)
- Timestamped b-roll prompts and on-screen text notes
- Any “show this” moments called out clearly

D) Posting suggestions (Doc)
- Suggested first-line caption (hook)
- CTA suggestion
- Hashtag set(s) per platform
- Best posting notes if relevant (e.g., pin comment prompt)

2) Quality standards (our QA checklist highlights)
We QC every clip before you see it. Our internal checks include:

Framing / safe zones
- No cropped faces (forehead/chin not cut off unless intentional)
- Subject stays within center-safe area
- Captions and any titles avoid UI zones (typical TikTok/Reels bottom area)

Captions accuracy
- Captions match spoken words closely (no missing key phrases)
- Proper nouns checked when provided (names/brands/terms)
- Readability: line breaks and timing paced for mobile

Audio
- Dialogue is clear and consistent (no sudden loud/quiet jumps)
- Background noise reduced where possible without artifacts
- No clipping/distortion

Editing rhythm
- Jump cuts feel intentional (no awkward mid-word cuts)
- Dead air removed (unless used for comedic/intentional pause)
- Hook is strong in the first 1–2 seconds

Platform formatting
- 9:16 vertical
- Clean export settings suitable for Reels/TikTok/Shorts
- No black bars unless requested

3) Review flow (how to request revisions)
We send a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can comment with timestamps.

How to leave feedback (best practice)
- Use timestamped notes, e.g., “00:07 change caption ‘their’ to ‘there’”
- One comment per issue (avoids missed items)
- If you want a new hook, paste the exact sentence you want on-screen

Revision window
- You have 24 hours from delivery to request revisions included with your order.
- After the window closes, we can still help, but it may count as a paid change depending on scope (see below).

4) What counts as a free revision vs. a paid change
Free revisions (included)
- Typos or caption timing fixes
- Minor framing/safe-zone adjustments
- Audio level tweaks to match the rest of the clip
- Small visual fixes (e.g., text too close to the bottom UI area)
- Swap b-roll within the same concept (e.g., change from “laptop b-roll” to “phone b-roll”) when cues were unclear

Paid changes (scope change)
- Changing the chosen clip moments (new selects / new story)
- New hook angle or rewriting the clip’s narrative after delivery
- Adding new branding packages or style changes not provided during intake
- Additional deliverables not in the original order (extra clips, extra formats)
- Extensive rework due to new instructions provided after delivery

Refund protection note
If something is objectively wrong on our side (caption errors, cropped faces, export issues), we fix it fast within the revision window.

5) What we need from you (Intake)
To avoid delays and prevent back-and-forth, we ask for:
- Where you’re posting (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Your handle + links
- Brand words/phrases to avoid (banned words)
- Any required CTA or disclaimer language
- Tone/style preferences (clean/pro, bold, meme-y, etc.)

6) Response times
- We acknowledge revision requests quickly and confirm what we’re changing.
- Standard revision turnaround: within 24 hours for in-scope edits (unless otherwise stated).
