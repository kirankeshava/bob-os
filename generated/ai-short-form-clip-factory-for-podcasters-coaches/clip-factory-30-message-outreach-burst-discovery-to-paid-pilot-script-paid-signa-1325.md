## G) Discovery call → Paid pilot script (15–20 min)
### 1) Set the frame (1 min)
“Goal: understand your current content workflow and see if we can help you get consistent shorts without extra management. If it’s a fit, I’ll propose a small paid pilot. Cool?”

### 2) Current workflow + pain (5 min)
- “Where do your long-form episodes live (YouTube, Riverside, Spotify video)?”
- “How many episodes/month?”
- “Are you posting shorts today? How many/week?”
- “What’s blocking consistency: time, editing quality, finding highlights, captions, posting?”

### 3) What ‘success’ means (3 min)
- “What’s the #1 outcome from shorts: followers, booked calls, course sales, email subs?”
- “Any examples of creators whose short-form style you like?”

### 4) Offer the solution (2 min)
“Clip Factory takes 1 episode and returns ready-to-post vertical clips: we pick highlights, write hooks, add captions, include b-roll cues, and format for Reels/TikTok/Shorts.”

### 5) Present the pilot (3 min)
**Clip Sprint (2 weeks):**
- Input: 1–2 long videos
- Output: {5–10} clips/week (agree number based on episode length)
- Deliverables: 9:16 MP4 + captions burned in + title/hook text + posting notes
- Turnaround: first batch in 48–72 hours

Close question:
“Want to start with a 2-week Clip Sprint so you can judge quality and consistency?”

### 6) Price anchoring + close (2–4 min)
“If we’re doing this weekly, most clients choose a monthly cadence later. For the pilot, it’s a simple paid sprint to prove results.”

(If they ask price and you need a clean number)
- Option A: “$___ for 2 weeks for ___ clips total.”
- Option B: “We can start smaller with ___ clips as a paid test.”

**Next step:** confirm link delivery method + brand style notes + deadline.

### 7) Capture SaaS signal (2 min, DO NOT PITCH SaaS)
“I’m also tracking what would make this fully self-serve someday. Quick questions:”
- “Would you prefer pasting a YouTube link or uploading a file?”
- “Do you need speaker labels (diarization)?”
- “Do you want auto-highlight detection, or do you prefer approving timestamps?”
- “If a tool exported clips with captions/templates, what would you pay monthly: $99 / $199 / $299+? What would it need to do to be worth that?”
