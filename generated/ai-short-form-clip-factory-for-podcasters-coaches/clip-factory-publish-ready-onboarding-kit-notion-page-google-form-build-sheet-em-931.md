### A) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
We deliver a folder containing everything needed to post immediately:
1. **Final MP4 clips (vertical)** — platform-ready exports.
2. **Captions file (SRT)** — matched to the final edit.
3. **B-roll / cutaway cue sheet (PDF or Google Doc)** — timestamped suggestions (what to overlay and when).
4. **Posting suggestions (doc)** — hook text, first-line caption, CTA suggestion, and hashtag starter set.

---

### B) Quality standards (our QA checklist)
We run a final QC pass before anything is sent:
- **Framing / Safe zones:** Subject’s face is not cropped; text and captions sit inside safe zones for TikTok/Reels/Shorts.
- **Caption accuracy:** Spelling/grammar cleaned (without changing meaning), correct speaker intent, key terms preserved.
- **Caption readability:** Line length balanced, no “walls of text,” punctuation for pacing.
- **Audio leveling:** Speech is clearly audible; loudness is consistent between speakers; harsh peaks removed.
- **Jump cuts:** Cuts feel intentional; no awkward mid-word cuts; pacing supports retention.
- **Hook clarity:** First 1–2 seconds communicate a clear benefit, curiosity, or contrarian claim.
- **No platform issues:** No copyrighted music added unless client provides rights; no disallowed claims added.
- **Export settings:** Correct resolution, codec, and bitrate for clean mobile playback.

---

### C) Review process (timestamp comments)
To keep revisions fast and objective, we use **timestamped comments**.

**How to review:**
1. Open the review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube).
2. Leave notes with **timestamps** (example: “00:07 change caption to …”).
3. Group notes by clip name if reviewing multiple clips.

**Revision window:**
- You have **24 hours from delivery** to submit revision notes.
- Notes submitted after 24 hours may be scheduled later or treated as a new paid change request (see Revision Policy).

---

### D) Delivery timing & handoff
- We deliver a **Review Link** first (for notes) plus the **Delivery Pack folder**.
- Once revisions are applied (if any), we’ll provide a **Final Confirmed** version in the same folder.

---

### E) Revision policy (what’s included vs paid)
**Free revisions (included):**
- Fixing typos or caption timing errors.
- Adjusting caption style to match the agreed format (font size, highlight style, position).
- Minor audio leveling tweaks.
- Minor cut timing improvements that preserve the selected segment.
- Reframing to avoid cropped faces / safe-zone issues.

**Paid changes (out of scope):**
- Picking entirely new moments from the long-form (new clip selection).
- Major re-edit due to new direction (different hook angle, new storyline, new CTA strategy).
- Adding new assets/branding that were not provided at intake.
- Additional versions (e.g., 3 alternative hooks per clip) not included in the original order.
- Revisions requested after the 24-hour window.

**If you’re unsure:** Email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with the clip name + timestamp note and we’ll confirm whether it’s included.
