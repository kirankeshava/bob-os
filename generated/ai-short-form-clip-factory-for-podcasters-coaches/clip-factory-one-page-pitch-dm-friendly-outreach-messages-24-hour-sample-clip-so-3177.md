4) 24-HOUR “SAMPLE CLIP” FULFILLMENT SOP (CONVERT TO DFY)
========================
Goal: Deliver one high-quality sample fast, then upsell to a weekly/monthly clip pack.

A) Intake (5 minutes)
Ask for:
1) Episode link (YouTube preferred; otherwise Drive upload)
2) CTA (follow, comment keyword, lead magnet URL, book call)
3) Audience + positioning (who it’s for)
4) Style notes: fast/slow captions, profanity allowed, words to avoid
5) Brand assets (optional): colors, font, logo

B) Clip selection (20–30 minutes)
- Find 1 segment with a clear claim, contrarian point, or step-by-step advice.
- Target length: 20–45 seconds.
- Ensure the first 1–2 seconds can support a strong hook.

C) Edit spec (quality bar)
- 9:16 crop with speaker framed
- Remove dead air, add jump cuts
- Burned-in captions: large, readable, high contrast
- Add 2–3 hook text options (choose best 1 for the export; include others as notes)
- Add b-roll cues as text notes (e.g., “overlay: screenshot of framework,” “cutaway: chart,” etc.)

D) Delivery (within 24 hours)
Email to: prospect + CC yourself
Subject: Your sample clip is ready (24h)
Body:
- Link to download/view
- 2 alternative hook lines
- Suggested caption text + hashtags (optional)
- Ask: “Want me to produce X clips/week from your next episodes?”

E) Upsell script (send immediately after delivery)
“If this is the direction you want, I can do a weekly clip pack so you’re posting consistently. Typical creators do 3–5 clips/week per episode. Want Starter (3/wk) or Growth (5/wk)?”

F) Conversion criteria
A sample is considered successful if:
- Prospect replies with minor tweak requests (they care)
- Prospect asks turnaround/volume
- Prospect agrees to a paid clip pack call or asks for pricing
