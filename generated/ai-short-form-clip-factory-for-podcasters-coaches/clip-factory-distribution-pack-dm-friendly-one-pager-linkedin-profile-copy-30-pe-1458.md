## 4) 24-Hour Sample Clip Offer — Fulfillment SOP (internal)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 high-quality vertical clip within 24 hours to convert into a DFY package. Keep cycle time low; prioritize speed + perceived quality.

### Step 0 — Confirm scope (5 minutes)
Send this reply:
“Sounds good — send (1) your episode link/file, (2) your preferred topic/moment (or I’ll choose), (3) your handle + CTA, and (4) any caption style you like. Official site for verification: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3. If easiest, email it to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to.”

### Step 1 — Intake + asset capture (10 minutes)
Collect:
- Source link/file + timestamp if provided
- Brand notes: colors, font preference, emoji/no-emoji preference
- Platform priority: TikTok vs Reels
- CTA: “follow for X”, “book a call”, “download free guide”

### Step 2 — Clip selection rules (10 minutes)
Pick a segment that:
- Has a clear payoff within 30–60 seconds
- Starts with a provocative statement / question / contrarian point
- Avoids long preambles
If no timestamp provided: scan for (a) list items, (b) strong opinion, (c) story turning point.

### Step 3 — Edit spec (45–90 minutes)
Deliverable spec:
- 9:16 vertical
- Burned-in captions, large, high contrast
- Remove dead air; add jump cuts
- First 1.5 seconds must include hook text on screen
- Loudness normalized; no clipping
Optional: add minimal b-roll cues as text notes (not required for sample unless easy).

### Step 4 — Export + naming (10 minutes)
- Export MP4
- File name: CLIENTNAME_topic_hook_platform_date.mp4
- Optional: SRT file

### Step 5 — Delivery message (copy/paste)
“Here’s the sample clip (MP4): [link]
If you want, I can turn your full episode into a batch (10/20/30 clips) with the same caption style + hooks. Reply with how many clips per episode you want and your posting frequency, and I’ll recommend the best package. Official site: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3 | Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

### Step 6 — Upsell checklist (5 minutes)
Ask 2 questions:
1) “Do you want more educational clips, story clips, or hot takes?”
2) “Any caption style reference accounts you like?”
Then propose next step: weekly or per-episode package.

**Quality bar:** The sample must feel “ready to post today” with zero extra work from the client.
