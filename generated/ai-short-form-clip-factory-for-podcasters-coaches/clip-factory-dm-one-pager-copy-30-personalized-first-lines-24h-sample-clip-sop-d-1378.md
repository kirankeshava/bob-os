### C) Production Steps (internal checklist)
1) **Pull source**: download segment or note timestamps; confirm audio quality.
2) **Find a highlight**: choose a segment with (a) clear pain point, (b) surprising claim, or (c) actionable steps.
3) **Edit for retention**: remove dead air, tighten breaths, cut to first value within 1–2 seconds.
4) **Captions**: accurate + readable; emphasize keywords; avoid walls of text.
5) **Frame 9:16**: speaker centered; add safe margins for UI overlays.
6) **Hook**: add on-screen hook text in first 1 second (2 variants if possible).
7) **Export**: 1080x1920, H.264, AAC audio; filename: [Client]_[Topic]_[Date].mp4
8) **QC**: watch with sound off; confirm captions synced; no cut-off text.
