## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Clip Factory Review Process (Fast + Simple)**

Thanks for trying Clip Factory. To keep the **10 clips / 48-hour** turnaround fast and consistent, we review using a structured checklist.

**What you’ll receive**
- A delivery email from **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to**
- A folder/link containing:
  - **10 vertical clips** (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts format)
  - A **Clip List** (titles + timecodes + suggested captions/hashtags)
  - Optional: captions-on and captions-off versions (if requested)

**How to review (recommended order)**
1) **Watch the first 2 seconds**: does the hook land immediately?
2) **Check captions**: readable, accurate, not covering faces/UI.
3) **Check pacing**: visual change/pattern interrupt every ~2–3 seconds.
4) **Check claim/compliance** (if your niche has restrictions): remove risky claims.
5) **Check CTA**: matches your offer + feels natural.

**What counts as a “revision” (included)**
Each clip includes **1 revision round** (batch review encouraged). Revisions cover:
- Caption fixes (misspellings, punctuation, minor timing)
- Hook swap from the same segment (if provided options exist)
- CTA text adjustments (end-card text, on-screen CTA line)
- Minor trims (tighten start/end, remove filler)
- B-roll cue swap (replace with different cue or remove)

**What is out of scope for the included revision round**
To protect delivery speed, these require a new request/order:
- Rebuilding the clip around a totally different moment/topic
- Heavy manual motion graphics or custom animation
- Sourcing/licensing paid stock footage/music
- Full re-edit of the original longform episode

Reply to the delivery email (or email **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to**) with:
- **Subject:** Revisions — [Your Name/Brand] — [Episode/Date]
- For each clip:
  - **Clip filename:** (example: CF_JaneDoe_E12_C03_Hook-Offer_v1.mp4)
  - **Timestamp:** (example: 00:00–00:02)
  - **Change requested:** (example: “Replace hook text with: ‘Stop doing X if you want Y’”)
