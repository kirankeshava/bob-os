## A) NOTION PAGE (Publish-ready copy)
**Page title:** Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)

**Intro (top of page):**
Welcome to Clip Factory. We turn your long-form video/podcast into ready-to-post vertical clips with strong hooks, clean captions, and platform-optimized formatting. This page explains our quality standards, review process, what you receive, and what counts as a free revision.

**1) What we need from you (before we start)**
Please complete the Intake Form (link below) and share your raw file(s) or source link(s). If you have brand guidelines, preferred caption style, and your handle/CTA, include them.

**Intake Form:** [INSERT GOOGLE FORM LINK]

**2) Our QA standards (what we check on every clip)**
We run a final QA pass on every deliverable:
- **Framing / safe zones:** No cropped faces. Subject stays centered. Captions stay within safe areas for TikTok/Reels/Shorts UI.
- **Captions accuracy:** Correct words, punctuation, names/brands, and numbers. No missing lines. No duplicated phrases.
- **Caption readability:** High-contrast, consistent style. Comfortable line length. No awkward breaks mid-phrase.
- **Hook clarity:** First 1–2 seconds must communicate the reason to keep watching (clear claim, curiosity, or payoff).
- **Jump cuts / pacing:** Clean edits without distracting stutters. Dead air removed. Natural rhythm preserved.
- **Audio leveling:** Dialogue is consistent and intelligible; no clipping; background noise minimized.
- **Platform formatting:** Vertical 9:16, appropriate bitrate, and clean export settings.
- **Compliance / sensitivity:** Avoids disallowed content you specify (banned words, competitor mentions, sensitive topics).

**3) Review flow (how you give feedback)**
We send a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube). Please leave **timestamped comments** for any changes.
- **Review method:** timestamped comments only (example: “00:07 caption typo: ‘their’ → ‘there’”).
- **Revision window:** **24 hours** after we send the review link/delivery. Feedback received after the window may be scheduled for the next batch.

**4) What you receive (Delivery Pack)**
You receive a single folder containing:
1. **Final MP4 clips** (ready to post)
2. **SRT caption files** (one per clip)
3. **B-roll cue sheet** (time-coded suggestions)
4. **Posting suggestions** per clip:
   - First-line caption (hook line)
   - Suggested hashtags
   - Recommended platform(s)
   - Optional CTA

**5) Revisions policy (what’s free vs. what’s a new request)**
We want you thrilled with the clips. Here’s how revisions work:

**Free revisions (within 24 hours):**
- Caption typos/mistimed captions
- Minor caption formatting fixes (line breaks, readability)
- Small trimming tweaks (remove a pause, tighten 1–2 seconds)
- Audio level smoothing
- Fixing safe-zone issues (cropped face, captions too low)
- Swapping in a nearby alt moment from the same segment (small change)

**New request (not a free revision):**
- Changing the clip’s topic/angle after approval (“use a different section instead”)
- Major rewrite of hook or storyline across the clip
- Adding new assets you didn’t provide before (logos, new b-roll libraries, new brand kit)
- Creating additional versions (multiple caption styles, A/B variants) beyond the agreed scope
- Re-editing because original requirements changed (new CTA, new banned words, new target platform mix)

## F) ISSUE TRIAGE RULES (internal quick decision)
**Fix immediately (free, even if no client asks):**
- Cropped face / unsafe framing
- Captions off-screen / violating safe zones
- Obvious caption typos / wrong speaker
- Audio clipping or unreadable dialogue
- Export corruption / wrong aspect ratio

**Free revision (within 24h):**
- Minor trims, small hook wording tweak, caption formatting, audio leveling, replacing a line with same meaning

**New scope (not free revision):**
- New angle/topic, swapping to different section of source, adding new deliverables/variants, redoing due to changed requirements
