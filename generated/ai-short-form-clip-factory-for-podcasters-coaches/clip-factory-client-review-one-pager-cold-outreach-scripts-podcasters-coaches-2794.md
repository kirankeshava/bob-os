# 1) Client One-Pager — How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Where to review**
You’ll receive either (a) a Google Drive folder link, or (b) a Frame.io review link (if used). Each clip is numbered and includes a short “Hook/Angle” note.

**What “done” means (so we’re aligned)**
Each clip is delivered **ready-to-post** as a vertical video with:
- Hook in the first **1.5 seconds**
- Dynamic captions (high-contrast, mobile-first)
- Light punch-ins / pattern interrupts every **2–3 seconds** (where appropriate)
- Clean audio leveling + basic noise reduction
- Platform-safe formatting for TikTok / Reels / Shorts

**How to review fast (5–10 minutes total)**
1. Watch each clip once without stopping.
2. On the second pass, only note **must-fix** issues.
3. Approve the rest as-is—speed beats perfection for short-form.

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Included revisions focus on **execution**, not new creative direction:
- Caption typos, timing, readability
- Small trims for pacing
- Removing a word/phrase, muting a section
- Adjusting hook text overlay (same angle)
- Volume balance / removing filler words where easy
- Swapping a b-roll cue with another (if provided)

**What does NOT count as an included revision (new scope)**
- Changing the clip topic/angle entirely (“Different hook concept”) 
- Requesting new clip selections beyond the 10 delivered
- Major restructuring, adding new assets you didn’t provide, heavy motion graphics

**How to request revisions (exact format)**
Reply with:
- **Clip #** (or filename)
- **Timestamp** (start–end)
- **Change** (1 sentence)

Example:
- Clip 03 | 00:04–00:06 | Remove the word “basically” and tighten the pause.
- Clip 07 | 00:00–00:02 | Replace hook text with: “3 mistakes costing you leads.”

**Revision window + turnaround**
- Please send revision notes within **48 hours** of delivery.
- We return revisions within **24 hours** (usually faster).
