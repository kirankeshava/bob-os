**Typical clip length:** 15–60s (unless specified)

---

## B) Our Quality Standards (QA Checklist Summary)
We run every clip through final QC before delivery:

### 1) Framing / “Safe Zones”
- Faces never cropped; eyes stay within the top-middle safe region.
- Avoid covering the mouth with captions.
- Keep critical visuals away from UI areas (top/bottom where platform overlays appear).

### 2) Captions & Readability
- Captions match the spoken words (no meaning changes).
- Punctuation and spelling checked.
- Line breaks optimized for mobile reading (no giant blocks of text).
- Proper nouns (names, brands) verified when provided.

### 3) Audio
- Clear voice presence (no harsh peaks / distortion).
- Background noise reduced where possible without artifacts.
- Consistent loudness clip-to-clip.

### 4) Editing Rhythm
- Clean jump cuts (no awkward mouth-open frames).
- Tight pacing; dead air removed.
- Hook is obvious within the first 1–2 seconds.

### 5) Platform Formatting
- 9:16 export.
- No black bars.
- No cropped subtitles.

## D) Revisions Policy (What’s Included vs. Scope Change)
We include revisions that fix mistakes or match the brief.

### Included (Free) Revisions
- Caption typos or mishears (within reason)
- Minor timing tweaks (captions on/off, slight cut adjustments)
- Fixing formatting issues (cropped text, framing, wrong aspect ratio)
- Audio leveling corrections
- Adjustments to match instructions you already provided in the intake (CTA, handle, tone)

### Not Included (Scope Change)
These requests change the brief after work is done:
- New clip selection (different moments) after clips are delivered
- Major re-edit / new style direction not specified initially
- Adding new brand assets late (fonts/colors/logo) and asking to restyle all clips
- Rewriting hooks/CTA strategy across all clips after approval
- Revisions requested after the 24-hour window

If a request is a scope change, we’ll reply with options and next steps.

# 6) ISSUE TRIAGE RULES (INTERNAL QUICK DECISION)

When a client requests changes, categorize:

**A) Fix (free, priority)**
- Anything objectively wrong vs brief: caption errors, cropped face, bad export, wrong aspect ratio, unreadable text, audio clipping, missing deliverable files.

**B) Minor tweak (free within 24h)**
- Small trims, caption timing, small on-screen text adjustments, safe-zone reposition, slight audio leveling.

**C) Scope change (not included)**
- New clip moments, new creative direction, major style overhaul, redoing all hooks, adding brand package late and restyling everything, requests after 24h.

Response for C: acknowledge, restate original scope, offer options (e.g., “we can do that in a new batch / add-on after trial”).
