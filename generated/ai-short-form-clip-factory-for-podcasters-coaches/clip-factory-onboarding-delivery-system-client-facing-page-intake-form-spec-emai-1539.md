## 1) Client-Facing Page Copy (paste into Notion / PDF)
### Clip Factory — What to Expect
**Goal:** Turn your long video/podcast into ready-to-post vertical clips (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) with strong hooks, accurate captions, clean audio, and platform-safe framing.

#### A. What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch/order, you receive a folder containing:
1) **Final MP4 clips (vertical)** — platform-ready exports
2) **Caption files (SRT)** — one per clip
3) **B-roll / on-screen cue sheet** — suggestions for cutaways, overlays, or emphasis moments
4) **Posting suggestions** — first-line caption options + hashtag starter set + recommended posting notes

#### B. QA standards (what we check before you see anything)
We run QC on every clip for:
- **Hook clarity:** first 1–2 seconds clearly set the promise/curiosity
- **Captions:** accurate wording, punctuation, no missing phrases, readable line breaks
- **Audio:** consistent loudness, no harsh peaks/clipping, minimized background noise
- **Framing:** no cropped faces, safe margins for platform UI, subject stays centered
- **Edits:** clean jump cuts, no distracting flash frames, intentional pacing
- **Export settings:** correct resolution, bitrate, and format for vertical platforms

#### C. Review flow (fast + timestamped)
You will receive a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave **timestamped comments**.
**How to request changes:**
1) Open the review link
2) Pause at the moment you want changed
3) Leave a comment describing the change (example: “00:12 captions say ‘their’ should be ‘there’”)

#### D. Revision window (protects speed + deadlines)
- Revisions are accepted within **24 hours** of delivery of the review link (unless otherwise agreed in writing).
- If we don’t hear back within the window, we assume approval and proceed to finalize.

#### E. What counts as a free revision vs paid change
**Free revisions (included):**
- Caption typos/missing words
- Minor timing adjustments to captions
- Small audio leveling tweaks
- Small crop/reframe fixes (e.g., face too close to edge)
- Removing accidental mistakes (flash frames, pops, abrupt cut)

**Paid changes (scope change):**
- Changing clip topic selection after delivery (new selects)
- New hook rewrite + re-editing multiple sections
- Adding new branding packages not provided at intake
- Requesting additional aspect ratios/exports not in the order
- Additional clips beyond the agreed quantity

To request a paid change, reply to the email and we’ll quote and confirm before starting.

## 2) Internal QA Checklist (editor final QC)
**Clip-by-clip QC (must pass all):**
1. **Hook:** clear within first 2 seconds; no dead air; first frame visually compelling
2. **Captions accuracy:** spot-check against audio; names/terms correct; no hallucinated words
3. **Captions readability:** 2 lines max where possible; high-contrast; safe margins
4. **Audio:** consistent loudness across clips; no clipping; voice intelligible on phone speakers
5. **Framing:** face not cropped; eyes/upper body in safe zone; no UI overlap risk
6. **Pacing:** no awkward pauses; jump cuts are clean; remove filler where appropriate
7. **B-roll cues:** cue sheet matches timestamps; cues are optional not mandatory
8. **Branding:** correct font/colors/logo (if provided); consistent placement
9. **Compliance:** no prohibited words/claims based on client constraints; avoid sensitive content flags where possible
10. **Export:** correct file naming; resolution and format correct; plays back without glitches

**Recommended export baseline:**
- 1080x1920 (9:16), H.264 MP4, 20–30 fps (match source), AAC audio, target 10–20 Mbps

- If request is scope change, quote paid change and ask for written confirmation before proceeding.

### Step 6 — Final delivery
- Deliver final MP4s + SRT + cue sheet + posting suggestions in one folder.
- Send final delivery email and confirm revision window closure.

## 8) Revision / Issue Triage Rules (internal quick decision)
**Always free (fix now):** typos, missing words, misheard captions, small sync, minor audio level, minor crop/safe-zone, technical export errors.

**Usually free (if small):** swapping a single line of on-screen text, removing a filler word, minor trimming under 2–3 seconds.

**Paid (quote first):** new selects, new hooks for multiple clips, new brand style added late, new platform formats, additional clips, major re-edit requests that change the core narrative.

**Refund-risk flags (escalate immediately):** missed deadline, broken link/access permissions, wrong aspect ratio, heavy caption inaccuracies, cropped face for entire clip, audio distortion. Fix same day if possible.
