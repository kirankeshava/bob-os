### Section C — Review flow (timestamped comments)
You’ll receive a review link (unlisted) where you can leave **timestamped comments**.
How to review efficiently:
1) Watch each clip once without pausing
2) Rewatch and leave **timestamped notes** for any needed changes
3) Group feedback per clip (Clip 01, Clip 02, etc.)

### Section D — Revision window (24 hours)
To keep turnaround fast and pricing fair:
- You have **24 hours** from the moment we send the review link or final delivery (whichever comes first) to request revisions.
- After 24 hours, revisions may be treated as a new request.

### Section E — What counts as a free revision vs. paid change
**Free revisions (included):**
- Caption typos, punctuation fixes, minor timing alignment
- Minor crop/position adjustments to avoid cutting off faces
- Audio level balancing if a clip is peaking or too quiet
- Fixing mistakes against your submitted intake form (names, banned words, required terms)
- Small hook text tweaks that preserve the same clip concept

**Paid changes (scope change):**
- Changing the clip selection strategy (new moments) after drafts are delivered
- Rewriting hooks across many clips due to new positioning introduced after intake
- New branding elements added late (fonts/colors/logo animation) not provided initially
- Switching target platforms/specs after production (e.g., 9:16 to 1:1)
- Adding new subtitles style packs beyond the chosen style

If something is unclear, we’ll confirm before billing or proceeding.

### Section F — Quality standards we check before sending anything
We QC every clip for:
- Readable captions, correct spelling of names/terms
- No face/camera subject cropped awkwardly
- Clean audio (no clipping; consistent loudness)
- Smooth cuts (no distracting jump cuts)
- Clear hook in first 1–2 seconds
- Correct export settings for vertical platforms
