## What you’ll receive
For each delivery, you’ll receive **10 ready-to-post vertical clips** (9:16) plus:
- Burned-in captions (platform-safe margins)
- Strong hook + on-screen text hierarchy
- Light pattern interrupts (zoom/cut emphasis, text callouts) as appropriate
- Optional **b-roll cues** included as on-screen notes (when requested)
- Exports suitable for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts

## Best way to review (fast + accurate)
1) **Watch clips with sound on** (captions are supportive, not a replacement).
2) Check these in order:
   - **Hook clarity in first ~1.5s** (would you keep watching?)
   - **Caption accuracy** (names, numbers, claims)
   - **Pacing** (no dead air; idea completes quickly)
   - **Brand fit** (tone, CTA, any sensitive phrases)
3) If multiple clips have the same issue, note it once as a “global change.”

## What counts as a revision
Revisions are designed to be fast and focused. Examples:
- Fix mis-captioned words, names, or numbers
- Adjust hook text, caption emphasis, or on-screen text wording
- Trim start/end by a few seconds for punch
- Swap CTA text (e.g., “Follow for more” → “DM ‘COACH’ for details”)
- Remove/replace a line that raises compliance/sensitivity concerns

## What does NOT count as a revision (new scope)
These are new work requests and handled in a next batch:
- Creating brand-new clips beyond the 10 delivered
- Full narrative re-edit from scratch or different angle/topic focus
- Heavy motion graphics packages per clip
- Manual b-roll sourcing for every clip (unless explicitly included in your intake)
