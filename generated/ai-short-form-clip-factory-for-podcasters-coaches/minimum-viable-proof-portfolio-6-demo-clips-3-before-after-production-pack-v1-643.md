B) CAPCUT BUILD SPECS (consistent look)
Project
- Canvas: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps.
- Loudness: normalize to -14 LUFS integrated (or CapCut “Normalize”).
- Export: H.264, 1080x1920, 15–20 Mbps.

Captions (burned-in)
- Style: bold, high-contrast (white text, black shadow/outline).
- Placement: lower-third but above UI-safe area; keep within 10% margins.
- Emphasis: 1–3 keywords per line in yellow.
- Pace: 2–5 words per caption chunk; snap to spoken phrase.

Edit language
- Hook within first 1.0s (no intro).
- Jump cuts every 1.5–3.0s (remove dead air).
- Punch-in zoom 105–115% on key phrases; alternate angles via scale/crop.
- Add subtle motion background blur when using B-roll.

B-roll cues (overlay text for “factory” feel)
- Add tiny top-left callout text (8–12px equivalent) like: “B-ROLL: keys/house exterior” for 0.6–1.0s at each cue.
- Keep cues minimal (2–4 per clip).

Branding
- Small watermark bottom-right: “AI Clip Factory (Sample)”
- End card (last 1.0s): “Want 10 clips/week? agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”
