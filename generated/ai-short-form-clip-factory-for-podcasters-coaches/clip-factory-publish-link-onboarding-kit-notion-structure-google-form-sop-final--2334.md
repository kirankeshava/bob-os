**You receive a “Delivery Pack” per batch** containing:
1) Final vertical MP4 clips (ready to post)
2) Captions file per clip (.SRT)
3) B-roll cue sheet (what to overlay + when)
4) Posting suggestions (caption first line + hashtag set + CTA)

**Typical flow**
1. You complete the Intake Form.
2. We confirm “Pre-Flight” (platform, style, safe zones, any banned words).
3. We deliver a Review Batch via a timestamp-comment link.
4. You have a 24-hour revision window for included revisions.
5. We finalize and deliver the completed pack.

### B) QA Standards (What We Check Before You See Anything)
**Framing & Safe Zones**
- Subject face is fully visible; no cropped chin/forehead.
- Captions and on-screen text stay inside safe zones (no UI overlap on TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
- Visual hierarchy: hook text readable in first 1–2 seconds.

**Captions Accuracy & Readability**
- Captions match spoken words (no major meaning changes).
- Proper nouns checked where possible (names, product names, brands).
- Line length and timing optimized for mobile readability.

**Audio & Loudness**
- Consistent loudness across the clip; no sudden jumps.
- No clipping/distortion; background noise minimized.
- Music/SFX (if used) does not overpower voice.

**Edit Rhythm & Clarity**
- Hook is clear within first 1–2 seconds.
- Jump cuts are intentional; no awkward mid-word cuts.
- Dead air removed; pacing supports retention.

**Exports & Platform Format**
- 1080x1920 (9:16) unless otherwise requested.
- High-quality MP4 (H.264) with clean playback.
- Filename conventions applied (see Delivery Pack section).

### C) Review Process (Timestamp Comments)
**How to leave revision notes**
- Use timestamped comments (e.g., “00:07 change caption to …”).
- Group comments by clip name.
- If you want a different hook, specify the exact angle/tone and whether the claim must stay compliant.

**Revision window**
- Included revisions must be requested within **24 hours** of delivery of the review batch.
- If no notes arrive in 24 hours, we proceed to finalization and close the revision window.

### E) What Counts as a Free Revision vs. a Paid/Out-of-Scope Change
**Included (Free) Revisions (within 24h window)**
- Caption typo/accuracy fixes
- Timing tweaks for captions
- Minor reframes to respect safe zones
- Minor audio leveling adjustments
- Replace a single b-roll cue or tweak on-screen text for clarity

**Not Included (Scope Change / Paid Later)**
- New clip selection from different segment(s) of the long-form
- New hook concept that changes the angle/claim
- Major rewrite of captions for a different voice/tone after approval
- Adding new brand assets not provided in intake
- Multiple rounds beyond the included revision window

**If something is our mistake** (e.g., cropped face, export wrong size, missing files), we fix it ASAP regardless of window.

- Provide 1–2 example clips you love (links) in the Intake Form.
- List any banned words/claims and required CTA.
- Confirm preferred caption style (word-for-word vs cleaned-up).
