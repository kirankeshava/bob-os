========================
1) 24-HOUR DFY FULFILLMENT SOP (END-TO-END)
========================
Goal: deliver 3–8 ready-to-post vertical clips (or the package purchased) within 24 hours of receiving assets, with consistent quality and minimal back-and-forth.

A. Order kickoff (T+0 to T+30 min)
1) Confirm payment received.
2) Send “Intake & Asset Request” email (template below).
3) Create client workspace (Drive folder + tracker row).

B. Asset intake (T+30 min to T+2 hrs)
Required from client:
- Source link(s): YouTube/Riverside/Drive file + permission
- Brand kit: logo (optional), fonts/colors (optional)
- Handle/CTA: @username, link in bio CTA, offer name
- Content goal: leads vs followers vs authority
- “Do/don’t” list: topics to avoid, compliance notes
- Examples: 2 clips they like (optional)

If client doesn’t have a brand kit: default template + neutral style (high-contrast captions, safe margins) and ask for preferences after first delivery.

C. Clip mining & selection (T+2 hrs to T+6 hrs)
1) Skim/scan the long-form content at 1.5–2x speed.
2) Identify 10–15 candidate moments; pick the best 3–8 depending on package.
Selection rules (prioritized):
- Strong hook in first 1.0–1.5 seconds
- One clear idea per clip (avoid multi-topic rambles)
- Emotional/contrarian/curiosity trigger
- Concrete takeaway (step, framework, mistake, proof)
- Minimal context required

D. Edit & build (T+6 hrs to T+18 hrs)
For each clip:
1) Cut for pacing: remove dead air, tighten breaths, remove repeated phrases.
2) Format 9:16 (1080x1920), subject framed mid-chest to top with headroom.
3) Add captions (word-by-word or phrase-by-phrase) with emphasis styling.
4) Add a hook line (on-screen text) if needed (max 6–9 words).
5) Add b-roll cue ONLY if it materially improves clarity (don’t overproduce).
6) Add subtle zoom/punch-ins (optional) every 2–4 seconds if it helps retention.
7) Add end-card CTA (1–2 seconds) matching the client’s goal.

E. QA + export (T+18 hrs to T+22 hrs)
Run the QA checklist below.
Export settings (defaults):
- H.264, 1080x1920, 30fps
- Bitrate 10–20 Mbps
- Audio: AAC 320kbps, -14 LUFS target-ish (avoid clipping)

F. Delivery + upsell (T+22 hrs to T+24 hrs)
1) Upload final exports + a simple “posting notes” doc.
2) Send delivery email with:
- Folder link
- What’s included
- Recommended posting schedule
- One upsell CTA: “Want 12 clips/week? I can turn this into a weekly system.”

========================
2) INTERNAL QA CHECKLIST (PER CLIP)
========================
Hook & structure
- [ ] First 1.5 seconds contains a hook (statement/question/problem/result)
- [ ] Clip has one clear point; no unnecessary preamble
- [ ] Ends cleanly (no trailing audio, no awkward cutoff)

Captions & readability
- [ ] Captions match audio (no major errors)
- [ ] No more than 6–10 words per line (or two short lines)
- [ ] Key words emphasized (consistent style)
- [ ] Captions are inside safe margins (not under UI)

Framing & visuals
- [ ] Subject centered; head not cropped; no distracting background
- [ ] If b-roll used, it supports meaning (not random)
- [ ] Any zooms/transitions are subtle and consistent

Audio
- [ ] Voice is clear; noise reduced if needed
- [ ] No clipping/distortion
- [ ] Music (if used) is low and doesn’t compete with voice

Platform readiness
- [ ] 1080x1920 exported, plays correctly on phone
- [ ] Filename matches naming convention
