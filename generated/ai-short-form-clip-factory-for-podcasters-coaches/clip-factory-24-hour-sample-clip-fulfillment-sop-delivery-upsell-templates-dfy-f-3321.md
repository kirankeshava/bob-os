## Definition of “Sample Clip” (Deliverable)
Deliver **1 vertical (9:16) clip** from a provided YouTube/podcast/video file.
- Length: **20–45s** (default) unless content demands otherwise.
- Captions: **burned-in** (high-contrast, 2 lines max, keyword emphasis).
- Hook: first 1–2 seconds optimized with on-screen headline.
- Output: **1080×1920 MP4**, < 150MB when possible.
- Include: **1 suggested caption text** + **3 hashtag ideas** (optional but helpful).

## 24-Hour Timeline (SLA)
**T+0–1h: Confirm + intake**
- Acknowledge receipt of link and confirm delivery window.
- Ask 2–3 clarifying questions only if necessary.

**T+1–4h: Find segment & outline**
- Scan for 3–6 candidate moments:
  - Strong opinion, counterintuitive claim, clear framework ("3 steps", "here’s why"), story pivot, or specific mistake.
- Choose 1 segment with:
  - Clear beginning and end, minimal context needed
  - Clean audio
  - High emotional contrast or specific value

**T+4–10h: Edit v1**
- Crop/reframe to keep face centered; punch-in cuts for emphasis.
- Remove dead air > 250–400ms where possible.
- Add hook headline (big text) for first 1–2 seconds.
- Add captions; emphasize 3–8 keywords.
- Optional: simple b-roll cue overlays (e.g., “B-ROLL: calendar / chart / screenshot”) if it improves clarity.

**T+10–18h: QC + final render**
- Watch without sound (caption comprehension test).
- Watch on phone (9:16 safe margins, no UI overlap).
- Ensure no caption typos; avoid covering mouth.
- Render MP4 1080×1920.

**T+18–24h: Deliver + upsell**
- Send file + suggested post text.
- Ask for quick feedback + propose paid package.
