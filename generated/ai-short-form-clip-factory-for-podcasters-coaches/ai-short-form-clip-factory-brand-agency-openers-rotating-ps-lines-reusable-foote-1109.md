==============================
A) 10 NICHE-SPECIFIC COLD OPENERS (BRANDS / AGENCIES)
==============================
Use these as the first 1–2 lines, then drop in your normal pitch + CTA.

1) DTC (UGC/Ads angle)
“Noticed you’re posting mostly static/product shots—have you tested turning founder/UGC videos into 15–30s hooks for Reels/TikTok ads?”

2) DTC (post-purchase education)
“Your product needs a quick ‘why it works’ story—are you sitting on longer demos/reviews we could slice into daily vertical clips?”

3) Ecommerce (social proof)
“Your reviews are strong—do you have any long customer interviews/unboxings we can convert into short proof-driven clips?”

4) SaaS (feature → outcome)
“I saw your recent feature announcement—most SaaS posts don’t translate it into a punchy 20s outcome clip. Want to test that?”

5) SaaS (webinar repurpose)
“Do you run webinars or long Looms? Those are gold for 10+ weekly clips with clean captions + hooks.”

6) Agency (done-for-you support)
“If your clients want more Reels/TikTok but editing bandwidth is tight, we can be the clip engine behind your retainers.”

7) Agency (white-label positioning)
“Quick question—do you ever white-label content ops? We generate ready-to-post vertical clips from long footage (hooks/captions/b-roll cues).”

8) Local service (trust + authority)
“People choose [service] based on trust—short clips of FAQs, before/after, and ‘what to expect’ usually outperform polished ads.”

9) Local service (lead-gen)
“Do you have any longer walk-through videos or consultations recorded? We can turn them into 15–30s clips that answer top buyer questions.”

10) Creator-led brand (founder content)
“Founder-led content is crushing right now—are you recording long videos/podcasts that could become consistent daily clips?”

==============================
B) 6 ROTATING MICRO-PS LINES (CREDIBILITY / CLARITY)
==============================
Add ONE PS at the end of a proposal/DM/email to increase trust without adding fluff.

PS #1 (speed)
“PS: Turnaround is fast—1 sample clip in 24h so you can judge quality before committing.”

PS #2 (deliverable clarity)
“PS: Clips come ready-to-post: vertical format, burned-in captions, hook options, and suggested title/description.”

PS #3 (platform formatting)
“PS: We format per platform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) so you don’t get weird cropping, cut-off captions, or low retention intros.”

PS #4 (process transparency)
“PS: Process is simple—send a link, we pull moments, write hooks, caption it cleanly, and return a ready upload.”

PS #5 (quality reassurance)
“PS: If the sample doesn’t match your tone, we’ll revise once—no hard feelings.”

PS #6 (volume promise)
“PS: One long recording can usually yield 8–25 strong clips depending on topic density—happy to estimate once you share a link.”
