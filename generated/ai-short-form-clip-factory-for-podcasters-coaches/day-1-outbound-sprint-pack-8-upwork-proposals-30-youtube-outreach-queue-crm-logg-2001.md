C) CRM LOGGING SOP (simple, daily, measurable)

CRM columns (minimum viable):
- Date Added
- Lead Name
- Segment (YouTube / Coach / Upwork / Brand)
- Platform
- URL (channel or job post)
- Contact (email/DM handle)
- Personalization hook (episode + timestamp)
- Offer (always: “1 free sample clip”)
- Last Touch (timestamp)
- Touch Type (proposal / comment / DM / email / follow-up)
- Status (New, Contacted, Replied, Sample Requested, Sample Delivered, Meeting Set, Won, Lost)
- Next Action (what + date)
- Notes

Daily workflow (2 blocks):
Morning (60–90 min):
1) Send Upwork proposals (6–8). Log each immediately with follow-up dates.
2) Do YouTube comments (10 creators x 2 comments). Log as “Comment1/Comment2”.

Afternoon (45–60 min):
3) DM/email the same creators you commented on. Log as “DM sent”.
4) Follow up on anything T+2 due.

Non-negotiable rule: if it’s not logged, it didn’t happen. Every touch gets a timestamp and a next action date.
