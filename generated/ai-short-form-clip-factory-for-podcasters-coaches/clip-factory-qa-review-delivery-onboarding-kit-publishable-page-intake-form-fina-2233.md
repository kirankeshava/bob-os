1) What we deliver (the “Delivery Pack”)
For each batch, you receive a folder containing:
1) Final vertical MP4 clips (ready to post)
2) Captions file for each clip (SRT)
3) B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + what to overlay)
4) Posting suggestions (caption first line / hook, hashtags, CTA suggestion)

2) Quality standards (what we QA before you see anything)
We run a final QA pass on every clip:

A) Framing / safe zones
- No cropped faces (eyes and mouth remain fully visible)
- Subject stays centered with correct headroom
- Safe zones respected (captions and key text not blocked by TikTok/Reels UI)

B) Captions accuracy
- Captions match the spoken words (no meaning-changing errors)
- Names/brands spelled correctly (based on your intake form)
- Punctuation used to improve readability

C) Audio leveling
- Speech is clear and consistent in volume
- Background noise reduced when possible
- No clipping/distortion

D) Hook clarity & pacing
- Hook is obvious in first 1–2 seconds (strong opening line or visual)
- Dead air removed; tight pacing
- Jump cuts feel intentional (no jarring stutters)

E) Platform formatting
- Vertical 9:16 exports (1080x1920)
- No black bars; no stretched video
- Bitrate and encoding set for clean upload to TikTok/IG/YT Shorts

3) Review flow (timestamp comments)
You’ll receive a review link: [REVIEW_LINK]

4) Revision window (24 hours)
To keep production moving and protect your posting cadence:
- You have 24 hours from delivery to submit revision requests.
- Revision requests must be timestamped.
- We confirm receipt and turn around revisions as fast as possible.

Revision deadline for each batch will be stated in the delivery email.

---
5) What counts as a free revision vs. a change request
Free revisions (included):
- Caption typos or mis-hears
- Minor caption styling consistency fixes
- Small timing adjustments (caption sync, cut timing)
- Cropping/safe-zone fixes (e.g., face too tight)
- Audio leveling tweaks (within reason)
- Export/format issues (wrong size, glitches)

Change requests (not included / scope change):
- New direction after approval (different hook angle, different CTA, different theme)
- New clip selection from the long-form (picking different moments)
- Re-cutting the whole clip for a different structure
- New branding package after work is done (new fonts/colors/logo placement)
- Adding new assets not provided before delivery

If something is ambiguous, we’ll propose the smallest fix that solves your goal first.
