## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Clip Factory)

**What you’ll receive (10 clips / 48 hours):**
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + hook-first pacing
- A simple “Clip Notes” document listing: titles/hooks, source timestamps, and suggested posting captions/CTAs

**Where to review:**
- Reply to this email with feedback, or (if provided) use the review link.

**How to review fast (10–15 minutes total):**
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) Ask 3 questions only:
   - **Hook:** Would this stop someone in the first 1–2 seconds?
   - **Clarity:** Are captions readable on mobile with sound off?
   - **Brand fit:** Does tone match you (confident/calm/direct)?
3) Only then rewatch clips that need changes.

**What counts as a revision (included):**
- Caption corrections (typos, missing words)
- Timing tweaks (trim start/end, tighten pauses)
- Reframe/reposition subject for safe-crop
- Swap one b-roll cue for another (if b-roll is suggested)
- Replace end card CTA text

**What does *not* count as a revision (new scope):**
- Recutting to a different topic or different section of the episode
- New clips beyond the 10 delivered
- Full brand redesign (new fonts/colors/layout) after approval
