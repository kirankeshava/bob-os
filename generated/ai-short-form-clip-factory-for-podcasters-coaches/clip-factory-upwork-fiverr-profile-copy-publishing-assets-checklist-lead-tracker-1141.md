Daily KPI cells (top of sheet):
• Touches today = COUNTIF(Date Sent, today)
• Replies today = COUNTIF(Status, “Replied” where reply date = today)
• Booked this week = COUNTIF(Status, “Booked”)
• Won this month = SUMIF(Status, “Won”, Revenue)
