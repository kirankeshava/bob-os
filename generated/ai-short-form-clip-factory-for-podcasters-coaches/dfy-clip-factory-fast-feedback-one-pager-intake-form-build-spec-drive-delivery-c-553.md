## The goal
We deliver **10 ready-to-post vertical clips within 48 hours**. The fastest way to keep the timeline is **clear feedback in one pass**.

## Where to leave feedback (preferred)
**Frame.io comments** (best) or a Google Doc/Sheet with **timecodes**.

## The only feedback format we accept (fast + unambiguous)
For each clip, please provide:
1) **Clip ID** (we label every clip)  
2) **Timestamp** (e.g., 00:07–00:12)  
3) **Instruction** (replace caption, cut 0.5s, blur, remove word, swap hook)  
4) **Priority**: Must-fix / Nice-to-have

**Example:**
- Clip 03 (IG): 00:01–00:02 — Replace first caption with: “Most agents get this wrong…” — **Must-fix**
- Clip 03 (IG): 00:18 — Remove “um” — **Nice-to-have**

## What counts as a “Revision” (bounded so we can move fast)
A revision is **one consolidated list** of changes to the delivered clips.
- Included: caption text tweaks, trimming, swapping hook line, minor b-roll swap, on-screen text changes, profanity removal, simple blur.
- Not included: new creative direction, different style pack, re-cut from scratch, new music strategy across all clips, changing topics/angles for most clips.

## When you need to respond
To protect the 48-hour turnaround:
- Please send feedback within **12 hours** of delivery.
- No response within 12 hours = we assume **approved** and close the batch.

## “Must-fix” vs “Nice-to-have” (helps us hit deadlines)
- **Must-fix:** compliance, factual errors, brand risk, wrong names, misquotes, wrong CTA, offensive words, major caption mismatch.
- **Nice-to-have:** stylistic preferences, optional b-roll swaps, extra punch-ins, alternate hook experiments.

## If you want more views, give this kind of feedback
- “Make the hook more curiosity-driven: X”
- “Move the payoff earlier (by 2–3s)”
- “Make CTA explicit: ‘Comment GUIDE and I’ll send it’”
- “Remove jargon, simplify to 6th-grade language”
