### A) How to Review (5–10 minutes total)
1) Watch each clip once with sound ON (check hook clarity + pacing).
2) Watch again muted (captions must tell the story).
3) Confirm the clip matches your brand: tone, claims, and any compliance rules you flagged in intake.

### B) What “Pass” Looks Like (quick checklist)
- Hook lands within the first **1.5 seconds**.
- No dead air; pattern interrupts/visual changes roughly every **2–3 seconds**.
- Captions are readable on mobile (safe margins, high contrast, no blocked text).
- No misquotes or meaning changes.
- CTA feels natural and matches your goal (follow, comment, download, book, etc.).

### C) How to Request Revisions (copy/paste format)
Email your revision requests to **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** using this format so we can turn changes around fast:

**Subject:** Revisions — [ClientName] — [Episode/Video Name] — Batch [#]

**Clip file name:** (example: CLIPFACTORY_JaneDoe_E12_C03_v1_2026-04-09.mp4)

**Timestamp(s):** (example: 00:03–00:07)

**Change needed:** (be specific)
- Caption correction: “_____” → “_____”
- Remove sentence / tighten pause / jump cut earlier
- Replace b-roll cue with: (describe)
- Add on-screen text: “_____”
- Change hook option: Use Hook B instead of Hook A

**Priority:** Must-fix / Nice-to-have

### D) Revision Policy (designed for speed)
- Includes **1 revision round** for caption accuracy, timing tweaks, minor pacing, and on-screen text.
- Revisions do **not** include re-cutting the entire clip strategy or selecting completely new moments (that’s a new batch/add-on).
- If you have compliance-sensitive edits (medical/legal/financial), flag them as **Must-fix** and include exact wording.

### E) Best Practices (to avoid delays)
- Batch feedback in **one email** per batch.
- Use **filenames + timestamps** (no “the third one feels off”).
- If you want a specific CTA, provide the exact phrase.
