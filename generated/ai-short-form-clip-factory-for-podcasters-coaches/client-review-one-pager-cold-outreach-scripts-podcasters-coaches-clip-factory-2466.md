## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Where to review**
We’ll deliver your clips as downloadable MP4s and/or a share link (Google Drive/Frame-style review link). Please review on a phone if possible (most viewers will).

**What “done” looks like**
Each clip is formatted for vertical (9:16), includes burned-in captions, and has a clear hook in the first 1–1.5 seconds. We optimize for retention and clarity over cinematic perfection.

**How to leave feedback (fastest method)**
Reply to the delivery email with:
1) **Clip filename** (example: `CLIP-03_TopicHook_v1.mp4`)
2) **Timestamp** (example: `00:04–00:06`)
3) **What to change** (one instruction per line)

**Feedback format (copy/paste)**
- CLIP-03 `00:04–00:06`: Replace “$10k” with “ten thousand”.
- CLIP-03 `00:09`: Remove the word “basically” from captions.
- CLIP-07 `00:00–00:02`: Use Hook Option B from notes.

**What counts as a revision (included)**
- Caption text fixes (typos, wording tweaks)
- Caption timing adjustments
- Reframing (subject slightly left/right/up/down)
- Hook swap (from an alternate hook we proposed)
- Minor b-roll swap (same meaning)
- CTA/end-card text change

**What counts as a new request (not a revision)**
- Choosing entirely different moments not in the delivered set
- Changing the style preset (fonts/colors/layout) after delivery
- Rebuilding multiple clips around a new strategy

**Revision window & turnaround**
- Please send revisions within **48 hours of delivery**.
- We’ll return revisions within **24 hours** for up to **1 revision round** (batch feedback preferred).

**To keep output fast**
Batch your feedback in one email and prioritize the top 3 clips you want perfect first.
