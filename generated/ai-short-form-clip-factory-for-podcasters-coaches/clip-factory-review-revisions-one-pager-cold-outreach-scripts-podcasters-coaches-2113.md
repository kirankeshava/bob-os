## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

**Hi! Here’s the fastest way to review your 10 short-form clips and request revisions without slowing down delivery.**

### What you’ll receive
You’ll get a delivery email from **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** with:
- A folder/link containing **10 vertical clips** (9:16)
- A **Clip Index** (Google Doc/Sheet) listing each clip name, runtime, and source timestamp
- Notes on hook choice, any b-roll cues, and caption style used

You can verify our business page here: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

### How to review (10 minutes)
1) **Watch all clips once at 1.25x speed** (don’t pause). Mark each as:
   - ✅ Approve
   - ⚠️ Minor change
   - ❌ Not usable
2) Watch the ⚠️ and ❌ clips again and leave comments using the format below.

### Revision request format (copy/paste)
For each clip, reply with a list like:
- **Clip Name:** [CLIENT]_E[##]_CLIP[##]_v1
- **Status:** Approve / Minor change / Not usable
- **Change type:** (pick one) Hook / Captions / Crop / Remove segment / Add CTA / Brand style
- **Exact instruction:** e.g., “Change first on-screen line to: ‘3 mistakes killing your listings’”
- **Timestamp (if applicable):** 00:03–00:09

**Best practice:** One message with all revision notes is faster than many messages.

### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions focus on **tight, quick changes**:
- Caption wording/typos
- Emojis/highlight emphasis
- Crop/face framing tweaks
- Removing a short section
- CTA text swap

### What does NOT count as a revision (new request)
These change the scope and usually require a new batch:
- New topic direction or new highlights from scratch
- Major script rewrite / new voiceover
- Re-cutting 10 clips from different timestamps
- Heavy custom motion graphics beyond the preset

### Timing expectations
- If you send revision notes **within 24 hours**, we typically turn revisions **same day** or **next day** (depending on volume).
- If revisions arrive late, we’ll still handle them, but it may shift priority behind other deliveries.

### If you want us to pick what performs best
Just reply: **“Optimize for retention + saves. Prioritize controversial hooks and practical steps.”**
We’ll bias selects accordingly.
