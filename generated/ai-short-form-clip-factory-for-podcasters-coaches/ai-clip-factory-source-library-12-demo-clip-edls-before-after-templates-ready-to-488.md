C) GLOBAL EDIT SPECS (CONSISTENT ACROSS ALL 12)
- Format: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps.
- Safe margins: keep text within ~10% edges.
- Captions: 2-line max, 3–6 words highlighted per beat, highlight color = brand yellow (#FFD34E) or cyan (#38BDF8).
- Cuts: jump cuts every 1.0–2.5 seconds (remove filler).
- Motion: subtle punch-in 105–115% on emphasis words.
- B-roll: use stock (Pexels) or simple overlays (charts, listings, emails) to avoid rights risk.
- CTA (end card 1.0s): “Want these made from your podcast? Email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to” + website proof link.

D) 12 DEMO CLIP EDLs (4 PER NICHE)

====================
NICHE: REAL ESTATE (RE)
====================

RE-1 Storytelling Hook (30–45s)
Goal: punchy narrative about a common homebuyer/seller mistake.
Pick excerpt: creator tells a personal story or ‘I had a client who…’ segment.
EDL:
1) 0:00–0:03 Hook text on screen: “This ONE mistake cost my buyer $12,000.”
   Audio: first punchline sentence.
   Edit: Hard cut + whoosh SFX low.
2) 0:03–0:15 Context: “They fell in love with the house… and skipped (X).”
   Captions: highlight “skipped the inspection / rate lock / comps”.
   B-roll cue: quick overlay of inspection checklist or rate chart.
3) 0:15–0:30 Lesson: “Here’s what you do instead…” 2–3 steps.
   Edit: numbered on-screen bullets (1,2,3).
4) 0:30–0:42 Close: “If you’re buying in 2026, do this before you tour.”
   CTA end card: email + portfolio site.

RE-2 Listicle (35–50s)
Goal: “3 things to do before you list your home.”
Pick excerpt: advice list segment.
EDL:
- Hook 0:00–0:02: “Before you list… do these 3 things.”
- Beat 1 0:02–0:15: “#1 Price for the market, not your neighbor.”
  B-roll: comps screenshot blur.
- Beat 2 0:15–0:30: “#2 Fix the ONE ‘deal-killer’ repair.”
  B-roll: cracked wall / roof.
- Beat 3 0:30–0:45: “#3 Photos > open house.”
  B-roll: camera shutter, bright interiors.
- CTA 0:45–0:50: “Want 10 clips/month from your market updates? Email…”

RE-3 Contrarian Take (25–40s)
Goal: pattern interrupt opinion.
Pick excerpt: creator challenges common advice.
EDL:
- Hook 0:00–0:03: “Stop saying ‘date the rate’… here’s why.”
- Proof 0:03–0:22: 1–2 reasons with a simple chart overlay.
- Reframe 0:22–0:35: “Say THIS instead…”
- CTA 0:35–0:40.

RE-4 Testimonial Style (30–45s)
Goal: simulate client win story.
Pick excerpt: creator mentions a result or case study.
EDL:
- Hook 0:00–0:03: “We got 7 offers in 48 hours (here’s what worked).”
- Steps 0:03–0:35: 3 quick tactics (pricing, staging, distribution).
  B-roll: offer emails blurred, listing photos.
- Result 0:35–0:42: “The winning offer wasn’t the highest…”
- CTA 0:42–0:45.
