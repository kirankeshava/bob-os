# 2) 24-Hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

## Goal
Deliver 1 high-quality vertical clip in **≤24 hours** from client link receipt, optimized for Reels/TikTok and “good enough to post today.”

## What to collect (intake checklist)
Client emails: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
Ask the client for:
1) Source link (YouTube/Drive) + timestamp highlights if they have them
2) Audience + niche (who the clip is for)
3) Desired outcome (DMs, booked calls, newsletter signups, podcast listens)
4) Brand constraints (words to avoid, claims, tone)
5) Caption style preference (clean, punchy, word-by-word emphasis)
6) Any existing brand kit (fonts/colors/logo) (optional)

## Workflow steps
1) **Download / access** the source video.
2) **Find 2–3 candidate moments** (15–45s each):
   - Strong claim + payoff
   - Tactical steps
   - Contrarian take
   - Specific story w/ lesson
3) **Pick the best moment** based on:
   - Clear standalone context
   - Fast hook potential
   - Minimal setup required
4) **Draft 3 hook options** (first on-screen line) and choose best.
5) **Edit vertical 9:16**:
   - Center crop speaker; maintain safe margins
   - Add subtle jump cuts if needed
   - Light noise reduction; normalize audio
6) **Captions**:
   - Burned-in captions with readable size
   - Add emphasis on keywords (max 1–2 styles)
7) **Optional b-roll cues** (if not inserting b-roll):
   - Provide 2–4 timestamped cues like “insert chart screenshot here” or “cut to ‘before/after’ image.”
8) **Export settings**:
   - 1080x1920, H.264, AAC audio
   - Filename format: Client_EpisodeTopic_Clip01_30s.mp4
9) **Delivery**:
   - Share via Drive/Dropbox link
   - Include a short posting note: title, suggested caption, 3 hashtags, CTA

## Quality checklist (must pass)
- First 1.5 seconds has a clear hook line
- Captions are readable on phone; no cutoff
- Audio is clear; speaker volume consistent
- Clip is understandable without the full episode
- No obvious “dead air” >0.5s

## Revision policy (for sample)
- 1 free revision: hook line OR caption styling OR trimming (not full re-edit)
- Turnaround: ≤24 hours after revision request

## SLA
- Start within 2 hours of receiving the link (during business hours)
- Delivery within 24 hours
