  - Change requested: [e.g., “Replace caption line with: …”]
  - Notes: [e.g., “Keep same style; remove ‘um’ here.”]

Revision scope (keeps the 48-hour pace)
Included (1 round):
- Caption wording fixes, minor timing adjustments
- Hook swap from provided selects (if we already pulled alternates)
- Removing a brief filler word (“um”, “you know”) when it doesn’t change meaning
- Minor framing/zoom tweaks

Not included (would require re-quoting / new order):
- Creating net-new clips beyond the ordered count
- Major narrative re-editing (rebuilding the story from scratch)
- New b-roll sourcing that changes the concept direction

Revision timing
- One consolidated revision round delivered within 24 hours after we receive your complete notes.
- If notes come in piecemeal, turnaround extends because we batch revisions to stay efficient.

If you’re happy
