INTERNAL GATING RULE (ADD TO YOUR OPERATIONS / SOP)
Rule name: “No Intake = No Start”
Policy text (internal):
1) Do not begin editing until the client has submitted the Clip Intake Form.
2) If a client insists on starting immediately, require a written waiver via email: “Proceed without intake; I accept risk of brand term/caption style mismatches.”
3) Store the intake response link in the job tracker and paste key constraints at the top of the editor brief: brand terms, banned words, platforms, caption style.
4) Any revision request that contradicts their intake response is treated as a change request (paid), unless it’s correcting our error.

Operational benefit: reduces caption/brand mistakes, prevents circular revisions, and creates a clear paper trail for refund disputes.
