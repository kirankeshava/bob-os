## 3) 24-hour Sample-Clip Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

**Goal:** Deliver 2 sample vertical clips in ≤24 hours from receiving link/files. Optimize for speed + “wow” factor.

### A) Intake (5 minutes)
1) Confirm receipt and set expectation: “You’ll have 2 sample clips in 24 hours. Reply with any words to avoid + your preferred tone (punchy/calm/teacher).”
2) Collect required inputs:
   - YouTube link or file
   - Target platform priority (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
   - CTA (follow, book call, download lead magnet)
   - Any brand elements (logo, colors, fonts) — optional
3) Save assets to folder structure:
   - /ClientName/EpisodeDate/raw
   - /ClientName/EpisodeDate/exports
   - /ClientName/EpisodeDate/thumbnails (optional)

### B) Clip selection (20–30 minutes)
Pick 2 moments with:
- Clear standalone context in first 1–2 seconds
- Strong “pattern break” or contrarian line
- Minimal cross-talk
- Length target: 20–35s (one can be 10–18s if it’s a punchy hot take)

**Hook rules:**
- Start at the strongest sentence (not the lead-in)
- Remove filler, long breaths, and dead space
- If needed, add a 3–6 word on-screen hook title

### C) Edit + formatting checklist (45–90 minutes)
- 9:16 canvas, 1080×1920
- Speaker framed center/upper third
- Captions burned-in:
  - 2 lines max
  - Highlight key words (optional)
  - Large enough for mobile
- Audio:
  - Normalize; remove harsh peaks
  - Light noise reduction if needed
- Visual pacing:
  - Cut pauses > 250–400ms when it doesn’t harm meaning
  - Add subtle zoom/cut every ~1.5–3 seconds (optional)
- Add minimal branding:
  - Handle or show name (bottom corner)
  - Optional end card 1s with CTA

### D) Export specs (5–10 minutes)
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Frame rate: match source (usually 30fps)
- Bitrate: ~8–16 Mbps
- Filename convention:
  - Client_Episode_Clip01_HookPhrase.mp4
  - Client_Episode_Clip02_HookPhrase.mp4

### E) Delivery (5 minutes)
Send email to: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to (or prospect’s email if provided) with:
- 2 attached clips or a share link (Drive/Dropbox)
- 1–2 suggested captions per clip (copy/paste)
- Suggested posting notes (best platform, CTA)
- Close:
  - “If you want, I can turn each episode into 10–20 clips weekly. Want pricing for a clip pack?”

### F) Revision policy (samples)
- 1 quick adjustment per clip (captions style, trim start/end, framing).
- No heavy re-concepting unless they move to a paid pack.

### G) Quality bar (before sending)
- Captions have no obvious errors in first 3 seconds
- Hook hits immediately (no ‘so today we’re going to…’)
- Audio is clear on phone speakers
- Clip is understandable without full-episode context
