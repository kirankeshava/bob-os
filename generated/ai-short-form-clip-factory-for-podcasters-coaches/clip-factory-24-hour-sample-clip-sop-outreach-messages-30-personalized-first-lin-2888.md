## 1) 24-Hour “Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (DFY-first)

### Goal
Convert a warm lead into a paid DFY package by delivering 1 high-quality vertical clip within 24 hours that proves: hook quality, captions readability, pacing, and platform formatting.

### Offer (what the prospect gets)
- 1 vertical clip (9:16) from their existing YouTube/podcast episode
- Burned-in captions (clean, high-contrast, mobile-safe)
- Hook options (2 variants) in the first 1–2 seconds
- Title + post caption suggestion + 3 hashtag sets (optional)
- Delivery: MP4 + optional SRT

### Scope boundaries (to prevent time sinks)
- Clip length: 20–45 seconds (unless they request up to 60)
- Revisions: 1 revision round included (captions timing + hook text). No content re-editing unless clearly an error.
- Source: 1 link only (YouTube/Drive). If multiple, ask them to choose one.

### Production steps (internal)
1. **Source pull:** Download or stream the segment; confirm audio quality.
2. **Segment selection:** Find 1 moment with: clear pain/insight, emotional punch, specific outcome, or contrarian take.
3. **Hook writing:** Draft 2 hook overlays:
   - Hook A: curiosity/contrarian (“Most people get X wrong…”) 
   - Hook B: outcome-driven (“Do this to get Y in 7 days…”) 
4. **Edit:** Tighten pauses, remove filler, add subtle zooms/cuts every 1–2 seconds.
5. **Captions:** Word-accurate, 2 lines max, large font, highlight keywords.
6. **Safe zones:** Keep text within mobile safe margins; avoid bottom UI overlap.
7. **Export:** 1080x1920, 30fps, high bitrate; filename: `Client_Episode_Topic_Clip1.mp4`.
8. **QC checklist:**
   - First 2 seconds compelling?
   - Captions readable on phone?
   - No cut-off text?
   - Audio levels consistent?
