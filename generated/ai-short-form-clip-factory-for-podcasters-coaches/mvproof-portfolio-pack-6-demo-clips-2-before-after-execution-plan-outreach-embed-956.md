B) Source Selection (license-safe and fast)
Pick 1 permission-friendly longform source per niche (3 total). Criteria:

• Topics evergreen (no news-specific claims).

C) Batch Production Specs (so all clips look consistent)
Video:
• 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps.
• Safe margins: keep captions above bottom UI area; keep face centered.
• Punch-in zooms: 105–115% on emphasis words; cut every 1.5–3.0 seconds.
Captions:
• Burned-in, high-contrast, 2-line max, big font, keywords highlighted.
• Add 1 on-screen header line for the hook (first 1.5–2.0s).
B-roll cues:
• For MVProof, we can simulate b-roll cues with on-screen callouts (e.g., “B-ROLL: property exterior” in small top corner) if we want to demonstrate direction without sourcing footage.
Audio:
• Normalize loudness target approx -14 LUFS integrated (or platform-safe normal).
• Remove long pauses; keep pacing tight.
Branding:
• Small corner tag: “Clip Factory SAMPLE”
• End card 1.5s max: “Want this for your podcast? agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”
