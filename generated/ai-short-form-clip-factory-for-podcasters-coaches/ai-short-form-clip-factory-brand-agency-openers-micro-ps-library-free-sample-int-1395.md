B) MICRO-PS ROTATION LIBRARY (append under proposals/DMs/emails)

Pick ONE per message. Keep it short.

1) “PS: You get the hook, burned-in captions, and vertical formatting—ready to post.”
2) “PS: We’ll match your on-screen caption style (font/size/colors) if you share one reference Reel.”
3) “PS: Turnaround is 24h for the sample; ongoing packages are usually 48h per batch.”
4) “PS: If the sample hits, we can batch 10–30 clips/month so you never fall behind.”
5) “PS: We pull clips that keep context—no chopped-up sentences or weird cuts.”
6) “PS: We include suggested titles + first-line hooks for TikTok/Reels/Shorts.”
7) “PS: You can approve clips via simple ‘keep / tweak / cut’—no long edit threads.”
8) “PS: If you already have an editor, we can just deliver timestamps + captions to speed them up.”
9) “PS: We can lean more ‘educational’ or more ‘story/controversy’ depending on your brand.”
10) “PS: We add b-roll cues so the clip feels intentional, not just talking-head.”
11) “PS: We can deliver in CapCut or .mp4—your preference.”
12) “PS: If your content is sensitive (medical/legal), we’ll keep it compliant and on-message.”
