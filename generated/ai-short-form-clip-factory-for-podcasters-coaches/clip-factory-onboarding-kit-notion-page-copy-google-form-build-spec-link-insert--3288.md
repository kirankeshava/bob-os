## 1) Overview
We turn your long-form podcast/video into ready-to-post vertical clips optimized for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. Each clip is delivered with:
- **Final MP4 (vertical)**
- **Captions file (SRT)**
- **B-roll cue sheet** (what to overlay + where)
- **Posting suggestions** (caption first line + CTA + hashtags)

## 2) What we need from you (Intake)
To avoid delays/revisions, please complete the intake form and share your source file/link. We confirm:
- Platforms (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Tone + target audience
- CTA + links/handles
- Brand constraints (colors, fonts, safe words, banned words)
- Content boundaries (topics to avoid)

## 3) Our QA Standards (what we check before you see anything)
### A) Safe zones + framing
- No cropped faces (forehead/chin/eyes) and no cut-off microphones.
- Subject positioned within **vertical safe zones** (UI overlays won’t cover key text/face).
- No critical text in bottom ~15% (caption/CTA overlays).

### B) Captions accuracy + readability
- Captions match spoken audio (names/terms checked).
- Line breaks optimized (2 lines max where possible).
- Punctuation for clarity; filler words removed only if it improves readability.
- Profanity policy follows your intake instructions.

### C) Audio leveling
- Speech consistently audible, minimal pumping.
- Noise reduced without robotic artifacts.
- Peaks controlled to avoid harshness; no clipping.

### D) Editing quality
- Strong **hook** within first 1–2 seconds.
- Jump cuts feel intentional; no awkward mid-word cuts.
- Dead air removed; pacing matches platform norms.

### E) Hook clarity + story arc
- Clear “why keep watching” immediately.
- Clip delivers one coherent idea (no context missing).

### F) Export + platform formatting
- Vertical 9:16 exports.
- MP4 H.264, 1080x1920, high bitrate (platform-safe).
- Filename includes platform + clip number.

## 4) Review Flow (fast, simple, timestamped)
We send a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave **timestamp comments**.
**How to request revisions:**
1) Open the review link
2) Pause at the exact moment
3) Leave a comment describing the change (e.g., “00:07 caption should say ‘client acquisition’ not ‘customer acquisition’”).

## 5) Revision Window (24 hours)
To keep turnaround fast and predictable:
- You have **24 hours** from delivery to request revisions.
- We confirm receipt of revisions and deliver updates as quickly as possible.

## 6) What counts as a free revision vs. a change request
### Free revisions (included)
- Typos/caption mismatches
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, cut timing)
- Small crop/framing fixes (face safe zone)
- Audio leveling adjustments
- Swapping a hook text overlay when the meaning stays the same

### Change requests (out of scope)
- Changing the clip’s topic/angle after approval
- Adding new branding packages/assets not provided initially
- Rebuilding multiple clips due to new directions not in intake
- Extra formats beyond agreed platforms

If a request is out of scope, we’ll propose the smallest viable adjustment first.

## 7) Delivery Pack (what you receive)
You’ll receive a folder containing:
1) **/01_Final_MP4/** (ready-to-post vertical files)
2) **/02_Captions_SRT/** (matching SRT per clip)
3) **/03_Broll_Cue_Sheets/** (per clip notes)
4) **/04_Posting_Suggestions/** (caption first line + hashtags + CTA)
5) **/05_Review_Link/** (a text file with the review URL)
