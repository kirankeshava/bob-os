C) RENDER-READY CHECKLIST (NO PAID TOOLS)
Export specs:
- Canvas: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- FPS: 30
- Bitrate: 12–20 Mbps
- Audio: -14 LUFS integrated (target), limiter at -1 dBTP
- Safe margins: keep text inside 10% from edges; avoid bottom 20% (platform UI)

Caption style rules:
- Burned-in captions, 2 lines max, 3–5 words per line.
- Emphasis: highlight only 1–3 keywords per sentence.
- Always caption the hook in the first 1.5 seconds.

Pacing rules:
- Remove dead air >200ms when possible.
- Cut on breaths; add punch-in on numbers/claims.
- Pattern interrupt every 3–5 seconds: zoom, b-roll cue, on-screen number badge, or keyword highlight.

B-roll cue overlay format:
- Small text tag: “B-ROLL: …” at top-left, 70–80% opacity, doesn’t block captions.
