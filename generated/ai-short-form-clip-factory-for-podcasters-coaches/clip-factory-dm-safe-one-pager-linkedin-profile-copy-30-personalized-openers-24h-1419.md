## 4) 24-hour sample-clip fulfillment SOP (internal)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 high-quality sample clip within 24 hours to reduce risk and close a weekly/monthly DFY package.

### A) Intake (timebox: 10 minutes)
Collect via email to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to:
1) Link/file to the episode (YouTube/Drive).
2) Preferred platforms (TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
3) Brand notes: colors, font preference (or “choose for me”), logo optional.
4) Hook style: (a) curiosity, (b) contrarian, (c) list, (d) story.
5) CTA: follow, comment keyword, book call, download lead magnet.
6) Any “do not clip” topics.

### B) Segment selection (timebox: 20 minutes)
Pick 1 segment that:
- has a clear payoff within 20–45 seconds
- contains a strong opinion, lesson, or story
- doesn’t require heavy context
Log timestamps and a 1-sentence rationale.

### C) Edit spec (timebox: 60–90 minutes)
- Format: 9:16, 1080×1920.
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast, safe margins, remove filler words lightly.
- Hook: add 1–2 on-screen hook options in first 1.5 seconds.
- Pacing: remove dead air, add jump cuts if needed.
- Optional: add subtle b-roll cue notes (not required to render b-roll for sample).

### D) QA checklist (timebox: 10 minutes)
- Captions accurate, no typos.
- Safe margins respected (no text under UI).
- Audio normalized; no clipping.
- Filename convention: {Client}_{Episode}_{Clip01}_{Length}.mp4

### E) Delivery (timebox: 10 minutes)
Send an email with:
- Drive/Dropbox link to the MP4.
- 3 title/caption suggestions.
- Recommended posting note (best platform + CTA).
- One question to close: “Want me to produce X clips/week in this style?”
