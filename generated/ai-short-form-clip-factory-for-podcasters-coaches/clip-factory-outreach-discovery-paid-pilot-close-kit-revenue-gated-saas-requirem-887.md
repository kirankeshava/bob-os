## 1) Target lead list spec (who to contact today)
Prioritize people who already publish long-form but lack consistent short-form.

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Podcasters (video-first)** publishing 1–4 episodes/month (YouTube + Spotify), 30–120 minutes.
- **Coaches/consultants** posting webinars, live trainings, or Zoom recordings.
- **Small creator brands** (1–10 employees) with a marketing assistant but no video editor.

### Qualification signals
- Has **recent long videos** (last 30 days) and/or backlog of episodes.
- Has **inconsistent Reels/TikTok cadence** (<3/week) OR captions are weak.
- Mentions “repurposing,” “content team,” “editor,” or “social clips.”
- Obvious monetization (course, coaching, sponsorships, newsletter, paid community).

### Where to find them fast
- YouTube: search “podcast” + niche keyword, filter by upload date (past month).
- Spotify/Apple charts by category; cross-check YouTube.
- LinkedIn: creators posting podcast clips; founders promoting episodes.
- Instagram: coaches with long captions linking to YT/podcast; lives; webinars.

## 4) Discovery call script (12–20 minutes)
**Goal:** diagnose workflow + quantify pain + confirm willingness-to-pay; then pitch DFY pilot.

### Opening (1 min)
- “Thanks for the time. I’m Bob. Quick context: I help creators turn long-form into short-form clips that are ready to post.”
- “If it makes sense, I’ll suggest a small pilot. If not, I’ll still share what I’d clip from your content.”

### Current workflow (4–5 min)
1) “How many long videos/podcasts do you publish per month? Where?”
2) “How many short clips do you post per week today?”
3) “Who does the clipping/editing right now (you, VA, editor)?”
4) “What’s the biggest bottleneck—finding moments, writing hooks, captions, editing time, or posting consistency?”

### Outcomes + constraints (4–5 min)
5) “What would success look like in 30 days? Followers? Leads? Sales calls? Course revenue?”
6) “Any brand rules—fonts/colors, profanity, pacing, camera framing?”
7) “Do you want b-roll cues and on-screen text style, or just clean captions?”

### Willingness-to-pay / pricing (3–4 min)
8) “If someone delivered 20–30 ready-to-post clips/month reliably, what would that be worth?”
9) “Would you prefer: (A) done-for-you weekly package, or (B) software you run yourself?”
10) “At $99–$299/mo for software later, which features are must-have for you?” (see section 6)

### Close to DFY pilot (2–4 min)
- “Based on what you said, the fastest path is a DFY pilot so you get output immediately. I can deliver {{X}} clips/week from your long-form, captions included, optimized for vertical.”

## 5) DFY paid pilot offer + close script (convert now)
### Offer framing
“Instead of building tools first, we’ll prove ROI with a 2-week pilot. You’ll get consistent short-form output without hiring an editor.”

### Pilot packages (say this verbally)
**Pilot A (Starter):** 10 clips over 2 weeks (5/week), captions + basic hook text.
**Pilot B (Growth):** 20 clips over 2 weeks (10/week), captions + 3 hook options per clip + b-roll cues.

### Pricing guidance (leave numbers flexible)
- Anchor: “Most clients land between **$500–$1,500** for a 2-week pilot depending on volume and complexity.”
- Ask: “Which volume is closer to what you need—5/week or 10/week?”

### Close language
“If you’re in, the next step is simple: you send one YouTube link (or upload), your brand notes, and we start this week. I’ll email a short recap and we can lock the pilot.”

**Objection handling (fast):**
- *‘We already have an editor’* → “Perfect—this often complements them. We can focus on hooks/captions and consistent cadence, or become overflow when they’re backed up.”
- *‘Not sure it will perform’* → “That’s exactly why we pilot. We’ll test 10–20 clips, track retention and saves, then double down on formats that hit.”
- *‘Too busy’* → “Lowest-lift: just send a link and 2 examples of clips you like. We’ll handle the rest.”

## 6) SaaS feature validation checklist (what they’d pay $99–$299/mo for)
Ask them to rate each: **Must / Nice / Don’t care** and “Would you pay +$50/mo for this?”
1) Auto-transcribe + **word-accurate captions** with speaker punctuation.
2) **Highlight detection** (auto selects best moments) vs manual clip selection.
3) **Template library** by niche (podcast, coaching, real estate, fitness, SaaS founder).
4) **Hook generator** (3–5 hook options) aligned to their offer.
5) Brand kit: fonts/colors/logo + reusable lower-thirds.
6) **Diarization** (Speaker 1/2 labels) and multi-speaker caption styling.
7) Auto b-roll cues + stock suggestions.
8) Multi-platform export presets (Reels/TikTok/Shorts) + safe margins.
9) Team workflow (review links, approve/reject, comments).
10) “Upload/link → highlights → export” speed expectations (minutes vs hours) and acceptable quality tradeoffs.
