### How to review (best practice)
Please review in this order to avoid slow feedback loops:
1) **Hook & topic** (first 1–2 seconds): Is the promise/curiosity strong?
2) **Cut tightness**: Does it feel fast and engaging (no dead air)?
3) **Captions**: Are they readable and aligned with your voice?
4) **Brand fit**: Do the fonts/colors/positioning match your preference?
5) **Platform readiness**: Is the framing safe for mobile (no cut-off text)?

### What counts as a “revision” vs. “new creative”
To protect the 48-hour speed, revisions are focused changes to delivered clips.

**Included revisions (fast):**
- Caption text fixes (typos, wording)
- Caption placement adjustments (higher/lower)
- Color/font swap within the preset pack
- Tightening the start/end by a few seconds
- Removing filler words or a short section
- Replacing a b-roll cue with a different cue (if available)

**Not included as revisions (treated as new clip requests):**
- Changing the clip topic to a totally different moment
- Full restructuring (different storyline) beyond small trims
- Adding new source footage not included in the original intake
- Major style overhaul (new design system beyond chosen preset)

### How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to the delivery email and use this format for each clip:

**Clip name:** (example: EP12_C03_Hook-Myth_V1)
**Timestamp:** (00:00–00:12)
**Request:** (Example: “Replace caption line 2 with: ‘Most agents miss this one step…’ and move captions slightly higher.”)
**Priority:** Must-have / Nice-to-have
