Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (DFY Clip Factory)

Where to review
• You’ll receive either:
  1) A Google Drive/Dropbox folder with MP4s + a “Clip Notes” doc, and/or
  2) A review link (where you can leave time-stamped comments).

What “done” looks like
• 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for Shorts/Reels/TikTok.
• Captions burned-in, platform-safe framing, hook-forward pacing, and clean audio.

How to give feedback (required format)
To keep the 48-hour turnaround fast, please send feedback using this exact format:
• Clip filename: (example) 2026-05-14_Client_Ep12_C03_v1.mp4
• Timestamp(s): 00:02–00:05, 00:14
• Change request: “Replace caption line,” “Remove word,” “Zoom out 10%,” “Swap hook text,” etc.
• If the change is stylistic: reference a clip example you liked (from this batch) and say “Make others match this.”

What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are for polishing the same clip concept:
• Caption corrections (typos, punctuation, speaker attribution)
• Minor timing tweaks (tighten start/end, pause trimming)
• Reframing adjustments (safe margins, face centered, zoom level)
• Subtitle styling tweaks within the chosen preset (size, position, emphasis)
• Audio leveling / removing short filler words if it doesn’t change meaning

What counts as a new request (not included in revisions)
These are new scope because they require re-editing the concept:
• “Pick different moments” / new highlights
• “Make 10 more clips” / new deliverables
• Major rewrite of the hook concept or new narrative structure
• Adding custom motion graphics beyond the preset
• Extensive b-roll sourcing beyond what was already cued

Revision limits + SLA
• One revision round is included for the batch.
• You have 48 hours after delivery to send revision notes.
• Turnaround on revisions: within 24 hours after we receive correctly formatted notes.

Fast approval tip
If you like 80% of the clips, approve those immediately and only request revisions on the remainder. This keeps your posting schedule moving.
