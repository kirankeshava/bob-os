### What you’ll receive
For each order you’ll get:
- **10 vertical clips** (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in **captions** + basic motion emphasis
- **Hook-first** structure + pattern interrupts
- A short **delivery note** with recommended posting order + optional caption ideas

### How to review (fastest method)
1) **Watch the first 2 seconds** of each clip. If it doesn’t grab attention, flag it.
2) Skim the captions quickly for **accuracy** (names, numbers, key claims).
3) Confirm the clip’s “point” is clear by **second 6–8**.
4) Check the ending: does it land on a clean **CTA or satisfying close**?

### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are focused, fast edits that preserve the 48-hour workflow:
- Caption corrections (typos, misheard words)
- Hook swap **using a moment from the same source**
- Trim tighter / remove filler / shorten pauses
- Reposition captions (higher/lower), adjust highlight emphasis
- Replace end card text (CTA, handle, offer)

### What does NOT count as a revision (new work / may be billed)
- New clips beyond the 10 delivered
- Re-cutting from a different episode/file than the one provided
- New creative direction after delivery (full style overhaul)
- Heavy b-roll sourcing, licensed footage, complex motion graphics
- Content changes requiring fact-checking beyond the source audio/video
