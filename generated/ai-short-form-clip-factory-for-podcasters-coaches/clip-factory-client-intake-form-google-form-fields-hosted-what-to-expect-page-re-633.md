Section: Our QA standards (so you know what’s being checked)
“Before anything is sent, we QC:
• Captions: high accuracy, correct names/terms, minimal drift
• Framing: no cropped faces, safe zones respected for TikTok/Reels UI
• Audio: balanced loudness, no clipping/peaking, dialogue clarity
• Hook clarity: strong first 1–2 seconds, no slow intros
• Jump cuts: clean pacing without awkward mid-word cuts
• Export settings: 1080x1920, H.264 MP4, platform-safe bitrate”

Section: Review process (timestamp comments)
“1) You’ll receive a private review link.
2) Leave comments with timestamps (example: “00:07 change caption to …”).
3) Please consolidate feedback into one pass when possible.
4) Revision window: 24 hours from delivery of the review link (extensions available if requested in writing before the window ends).”

Section: Revision boundaries (what’s included)
“Free revisions (included):
• Fixing caption errors/misspellings
• Audio level tweaks (if clipping/too quiet)
• Minor reframes to avoid cropped faces
• Swapping a hook line if it changes wording but keeps the same moment
• Removing accidental filler or awkward cut points

Paid changes (out of scope):
• Changing the chosen clip moments after approval
• Re-editing due to new brand rules not provided in intake
• Adding new footage, complex motion graphics, or new editing style references after delivery
• Additional versions per platform beyond the agreed scope
If something is unclear, email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to and we’ll quote before doing extra work.”

Section: How to get the fastest turnaround
“• Complete the Intake Form fully (brand terms, banned words, caption style)
• Provide 1–3 example clips you like
• Respond with consolidated feedback in the 24-hour window
This prevents back-and-forth and keeps your output consistent.”

FAQ section:

3) INTERNAL GATING RULE — “NO INTAKE = NO EDIT”
========================
Rule (paste into internal SOP / task board):
“Do not start clipping/editing until one of the following is true:
(1) Client Intake Form submitted (link stored in the project folder), OR
(2) Client explicitly waives intake in writing via email (copy/paste waiver into project notes).
If intake is missing, send the ‘Intake Required’ email and pause work. This prevents caption/brand mistakes and protects refund risk.”

Enforcement checklist (internal):
• Intake link stored in /01_Admin/Intake_Link.txt
• Banned words captured
• Platform targets selected
• Caption style selected
• Pronunciation notes captured (or “none”)

This package is designed to reduce churn by preventing preventable errors (caption drift, brand terms, compliance issues) and by setting clear expectations for review and revision timing before production begins.
