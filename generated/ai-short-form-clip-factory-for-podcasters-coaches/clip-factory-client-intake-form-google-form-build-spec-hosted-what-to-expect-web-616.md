Section: What you receive (Delivery Pack)
For each order, you’ll receive a Delivery Pack containing:
1) Final vertical MP4 clips (ready to post)
2) Captions file(s) (.SRT) for each clip
3) B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggested overlays/cutaways)
4) Posting suggestions: first-line caption options + hashtag set + recommended posting notes

Section: Review flow (timestamp comments)
1) We share a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube—whichever we agree on)
2) You leave feedback as timestamped comments (example: “00:12 change caption”)
3) We implement revisions and deliver finals

How to leave great feedback:
- Reference timestamps whenever possible
- Be specific (“remove this sentence”, “change hook to X”, “caption style: emphasis”)
- If you want a different direction (new hook angle), say so explicitly—see revision policy below

Section: Revision window (24 hours)
You have 24 hours from the time we send the review link to request revisions included with your order.
After 24 hours, we can still help—changes may be treated as a new request depending on scope.

Section: Quality standards (what we check before you see it)
- No cropped faces / safe-zone framing for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Captions match spoken audio (names/terms verified when provided)
- Audio leveled (no clipping; consistent loudness)
- Hook clarity in the first 1–2 seconds
- Clean jump cuts and pacing; no awkward dead air
- Platform-ready exports

Section: What counts as a free revision vs. a paid change
Included revisions (free within 24 hours):
- Typos or misheard captions
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, jump cut smoothing)
- Safe-zone/framing fixes
- Small hook wording adjustment without changing the clip’s core angle

Typically paid changes (scope change):
- New clip selection / different moments from the source
- Complete re-cut to a new angle or different narrative
- Reformatting for a new platform after delivery (e.g., square + vertical + horizontal)
- Adding new assets not provided (logos, new b-roll library requests, complex motion graphics)

If you’re unsure, email us and we’ll classify it quickly: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
