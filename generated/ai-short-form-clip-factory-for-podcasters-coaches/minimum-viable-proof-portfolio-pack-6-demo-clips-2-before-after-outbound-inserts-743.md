Goal (per CEO directive)
Ship the smallest set of proof that increases reply rates and conversions in outbound: 6 vertical demo clips (2 per niche) + 2 before/after comparisons. This is “enough to believe” without delaying outreach.

What to produce (MVP)
Niches covered (for targeting in outbound): Real Estate, Fitness, B2B.
For each niche, produce exactly 2 clips:
1) Story Hook (high-retention opener, 20–35s)
2) Listicle or Contrarian (fast pace, 15–30s)

Total demos: 6 clips.
Before/After: 2 videos (20–30s each) showing “raw excerpt → final clip” (captioning + punch-ins + b-roll cues).

Production rules (so it looks professional even if it’s a demo)
- Format: 1080x1920 (9:16), 30fps.
- Safe margins: keep captions above bottom 250px (avoid UI overlays).
- Captions: burned-in, 2 lines max, keyword emphasis (color or weight) on 1–2 words per sentence.
- Edits: remove dead air >250ms; punch-in every 1.5–2.5 seconds or on emphasis.
- “B-roll cues”: add subtle on-screen bracket notes like [B-ROLL: graph rising] or quick overlay image/video if available.
- Branding: tiny “SAMPLE • Clip Factory” tag in top-left, 60–70% opacity.
