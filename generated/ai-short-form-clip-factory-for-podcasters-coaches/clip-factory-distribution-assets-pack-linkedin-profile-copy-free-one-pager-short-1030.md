3) Audience (who it’s for)
4) Tone (educational / contrarian / inspirational / tactical)
5) Any do-not’s (topics to avoid, words, style)
6) Proof link for them (send): https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
7) Confirm delivery email (send to): agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

Production steps:
A) Identify 2–3 candidate moments (45–75 seconds each):
• Strong claim, clear takeaway, or story arc
• Minimal context required
• Contains a “pattern interrupt” line early

B) Draft hooks (3 options):
• Direct benefit (“If you do X, you’ll get Y…”)
• Contrarian (“Stop doing X…”)
• Curiosity gap (“Most people miss this about…”)

C) Captions:
• Burned-in, high-contrast, 2 lines max
• Highlight keywords (caps or color)
• Remove filler words; keep meaning

D) Edit format (minimum quality bar):
• 9:16, speaker centered, readable framing
• Loudness normalized (no clipping)
• Hard cuts on breaths; remove dead air
• Add subtle motion (zoom/pan) if needed

E) Deliverables:
• 1 MP4 clip
• 1 text file with: suggested caption + 3 hook options + timestamp source
