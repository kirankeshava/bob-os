Scope of the free sample
- Deliverable: 1 vertical (9:16) MP4 clip (20–45s) with burned-in captions + hook text + minimal polish.
- Optional (if time): 1 alternate hook line as a second version.
- Promise: delivered within 24 hours of receiving the link + brand notes.

Intake (what you need from the prospect)
Send this exact checklist once they agree:
1) Source link: YouTube / Spotify video / Drive link (or timestamped segment)
2) Target platform: TikTok, IG Reels, or YouTube Shorts
3) Their audience: “Who is this for?” (1 sentence)
4) Tone: bold/controversial vs educational vs inspiring
5) CTA: follow, comment keyword, book call, download lead magnet
6) Brand constraints: words to avoid, preferred terms, colors/fonts if any
7) Caption style preference: single-line / 2-line / karaoke highlight

Internal workflow (24-hour clock)
T+0–1 hr: Pick a segment
- Listen at 1.5–2x speed.
- Choose a 20–45s segment with a clear setup + payoff.
- Priority signals: strong claim, surprising stat, clear lesson, emotional moment, actionable tip.

T+1–2 hr: Draft hook + caption plan
- Write 3 hook options (5–9 words each). Pick the best.
- Create a one-sentence “clip promise” (what viewer gets).
- Decide on on-screen text beats: Hook (0–2s), Reinforce (3–8s), Payoff (last 3–5s).

T+2–6 hr: Produce the clip (tool-agnostic)
- Format: 1080x1920, 30fps preferred, H.264 MP4.
- Composition: speaker centered; ensure subtitles won’t cover mouth.
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast, readable on mobile.
- Add pattern interrupts every ~2–4 seconds when possible: jump cut, zoom, b-roll cue, on-screen text change.
- Add light music only if it does not compete with voice.

T+6–8 hr: QA pass (must be clean)
Use this checklist:
1) Hook lands in first 1.5 seconds (no long intro)
2) Captions are accurate (names/terms correct) and not cut off
3) Safe margins: text not too low for TikTok/IG UI
4) Audio is clear; no clipping; volume consistent
5) Clip ends with a point or CTA (no awkward trailing)
6) Export plays on mobile; file size reasonable

T+8–24 hr: Deliver + close
- Deliver via Drive link (view + download enabled) or directly attached if small enough.
- Immediately ask for: (a) 1–10 rating, (b) what to change, (c) whether they want 8/20/40 clips per month DFY.
