## 2) Our QA standards (how we avoid refunds)
We run a pre-delivery checklist on every clip.

### A) Safe zones + framing
- Faces are not cropped awkwardly (forehead/chin not clipped).
- Text overlays stay inside platform **safe areas** (avoid edges where UI covers).
- Subject is centered for vertical viewing; reframing used when needed.

### B) Captions accuracy + readability
- Captions are checked against audible speech for major errors.
- Speaker changes are formatted clearly (when applicable).
- Readability rules:
  - High contrast (white with stroke / background)
  - 2 lines max whenever possible
  - Punctuation used to improve comprehension

### C) Audio leveling
- Loudness is normalized for phone viewing.
- Peaks are controlled (no sudden blasting).
- No distracting hum/clicks left unaddressed when easy to clean.

### D) Hook clarity + pacing
- Each clip has a clear opening hook in the first ~1–2 seconds.
- Dead air is removed; pacing is tight.
- Jump cuts are clean (no jarring visual/audio discontinuities).

### E) Visual rhythm + b-roll cues
- B-roll cues are included when they increase clarity/retention.
- On-screen emphasis (keywords, numbers, bold moments) is used sparingly.

## 4) Revision policy (24-hour window)
To keep turnaround fast and pricing fair:

- You have **24 hours** from delivery of the review link (or Delivery Pack) to request revisions.
- Revision requests must be delivered as timestamped notes (preferred) or a short list referencing clip filenames.

### What counts as a FREE revision
We fix issues that are part of the agreed deliverable, including:
- Caption typos / mishears
- Minor timing adjustments to captions
- Small crop/safe-zone fixes
- Audio leveling tweaks
- Removing obvious filler words or tightening a cut (within the same chosen segment)
- Correcting formatting to match the requested platform specs

### What is a PAID change (scope change)
These requests change the assignment rather than fix errors:
- “Pick totally different moments from the episode” (new clip selection)
- “Change the hook concept/angle” after approval
- “Add new branded motion graphics package” not originally included
- “Rewrite content / add new VO / new subtitles style system across all clips”
- Large re-edits that require re-cutting multiple sections end-to-end

If it’s a paid change, we’ll quote it clearly before work begins.
