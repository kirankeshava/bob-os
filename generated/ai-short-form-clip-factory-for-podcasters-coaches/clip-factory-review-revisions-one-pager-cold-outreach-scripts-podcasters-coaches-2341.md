What to check (in order)

2) Captions
   - Are captions accurate (names/terms correct)?
   - Is readability strong (size/contrast/placement)?
3) Flow & pacing
   - Are there pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds (zoom/cut/b-roll cue/text emphasis)?
   - Does it feel tight (no long pauses)?
4) Brand & CTA
   - Does the end card match your handle/CTA?
   - Any words/topics to avoid?

How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Please reply to the delivery email with:
- Clip file name(s): (example: CLIP-03_YourShow_Ep12_Hook-Calendars)
- Timestamp(s): (example: 00:02–00:05)
- Change type: (choose one)
  A) Caption correction (wording/spelling)
  B) Hook swap (use alt line / start 0.5s later)
  C) Cut/tighten (remove pause, shorten answer)
  D) Visual note (caption position, highlight color)
  E) End card/CTA (handle, phrase)
- Exact requested text (if applicable)

Revision policy (speed-protecting)
- Included: 1 revision round per batch of 10 clips (up to 10 total change requests).
- Definition of “change request”: one targeted change with timestamps (e.g., fix 1 caption line, tighten 1 pause, swap 1 hook start).
- Not included (counts as new scope): rewriting scripts, changing the entire creative direction, adding new b-roll sourcing, or requesting brand-new clips from new segments.
- Turnaround: revisions returned within 24 hours after we receive timestamped notes.

Pro tip to get the best outcome
