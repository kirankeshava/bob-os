## 3) 20‑Minute Discovery Call Script (designed to close DFY + capture SaaS needs)
**Goal:** close a pilot OR at minimum capture paid-signal requirements.

1) **Context (2 min)**
- “How are you currently producing short-form from long episodes?”
- “What’s your weekly cadence and main platforms?”

2) **Pain + cost (5 min)**
- “What’s the most annoying part: finding highlights, writing hooks, captions, editing, posting?”
- “How much time/week does it take?”
- “Have you hired an editor/agency before? What did it cost and what broke?”

3) **Quality bar (5 min)**
- “Show me 1–2 shorts you liked (yours or others). What do you like about them?”
- “What’s your caption style: clean, bold, word-by-word karaoke, emojis, highlighted keywords?”

4) **Pilot close (5 min)**
- “We can run a 1-week pilot: you send 1 episode link, we deliver {8/15/30} clips with hooks + captions + b-roll cues.”
- “Which package fits your output goals this month?”
- “If we could deliver the first batch by {date}, would you want to start?”

5) **SaaS validation (3 min)**
- “We’re turning this into a self-serve tool later. Which would you pay for monthly?” (Use scoreboard below.)

## 4) Willingness-to-Pay (WTP) Feature Scoreboard (10-call rollup)
**How to use:** After each call, fill one row. Summarize totals after 10 calls to decide SaaS tiering and build-vs-buy.

### 4.1 Feature list to score (tick all that prospect wants)
**Core pipeline (v0 candidate):**
- F1 Upload/YouTube link ingest
- F2 Auto transcription
- F3 Auto highlight detection (timestamps)
- F4 Clip editor: choose segments + trim
- F5 Captions (burned-in) + style templates
- F6 Export 9:16 (plus 1:1 / 16:9 variants)

**Premium signals (subscription drivers):**
- P1 Speaker diarization (multi-speaker labels)
- P2 Brand presets (fonts/colors/logo/lower thirds)
- P3 Hook generator in your brand voice
- P4 Niche templates (podcast, coaching, real estate, fitness, etc.)
- P5 B-roll cues / stock suggestions
- P6 Auto face tracking + smart crop
- P7 Multi-platform safe zones + subtitle positioning
- P8 Team collaboration (client approvals/comments)
- P9 Auto posting/scheduling

## 5) MVP PRD Update (strict v0 scope, still gated)
### 5.1 MVP user story (v0)
“As a podcaster/coach, I paste a YouTube link or upload a file → the system transcribes → proposes highlight segments → I select a template → I export 9:16 clips with burned-in captions.”

### 5.2 In-scope for v0 (only if validated + after $5k DFY)
- Link ingest (YouTube) + upload
- Transcription via API
- Basic highlight detection (top N segments) with manual adjust
- 2–3 caption styles
- Export 9:16 mp4, downloadable

### 5.3 Out-of-scope until paid demand proves it
- Auto posting/scheduling
- Full collaborative review system
- Advanced motion graphics pack
- Unlimited custom branding without templates

### 5.4 Acceptance criteria (v0)
- User can submit a link/upload and receive highlights in < X minutes (set X from paid pilot expectations)
- User can export at least 3 clips from a 30–60 min episode
- Captions are legible, timed correctly, and not off by more than ~250ms on average
- Exports conform to 1080x1920 and play correctly on mobile

## 6) Build-vs-Buy Decision Trigger (post-interviews)
After 10 interviews, decide based on scoreboard:
- If **rendering reliability + speed** is top dealbreaker: prefer managed rendering (buy) first.
- If **brand presets + templates** are primary WTP drivers: invest in template system early.
- If **diarization** appears in >40% of $199+ “Yes” prospects: prioritize diarization.

## 7) 2‑Week Build Plan (gated; do not start until $5k DFY)
**Sprint goal:** a working v0 for 3–5 pilot customers.
- Days 1–2: finalize requirements from scoreboard; lock templates and acceptance criteria.
- Days 3–5: link/upload + storage + transcription.
- Days 6–8: highlight detection + segment picker.
- Days 9–11: rendering pipeline + caption styles.
- Days 12–14: QA with pilot episodes, fix edge cases, ship.

**Stop condition:** If pilot customers won’t pay monthly at $99+ for the v0 experience, pause and continue DFY.
