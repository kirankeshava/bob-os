C) 3 BEFORE/AFTER COMPARISONS (RAW → FINAL)
============================

- Right: FINAL (captions, zooms, b-roll cues, hook)
- Label corners: “RAW” and “FINAL”
- Duration: 15–25s each (use the same excerpt on both sides)
- Add a 1-line headline top: “What we do in 60 minutes”

Format B (Alternative): Sequential
- 0:00–0:08 RAW
- 0:08–0:20 FINAL
- Add “watch the upgrade” arrow animation between sections
