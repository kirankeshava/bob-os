1) RE — STORY HOOK: “The open house mistake that costs you offers”
Length: 35–45s
VO/Script (spoken or on-screen text if no VO):

- Line 2 (2–7s): “Most sellers think more flyers = more offers. It’s not.”
- Line 3 (7–16s): “The real killer: buyers can’t picture themselves living there in the first 10 seconds.”
- Line 4 (16–28s): “Fix it with 3 things: clear entry (no clutter), one ‘wow’ scent/lighting moment, and a single guided path through the home.”
- Line 5 (28–38s): “Make it easy to feel the lifestyle—not the square footage.”

- “Quiet open house?”

- Entryway clutter vs clean entry; warm lamp; cookie/candle; simple arrows over floorplan.
