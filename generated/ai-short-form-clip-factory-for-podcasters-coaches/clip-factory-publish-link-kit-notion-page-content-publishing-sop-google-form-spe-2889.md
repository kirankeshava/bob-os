## 1) Notion Page (Publish-Ready) — “Clip Factory — What to Expect”

**Page title:** Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)

**Hero block (top of page):**
**Clip Factory** converts your long-form video/podcast into ready-to-post vertical clips (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) with hooks, captions, b-roll cues, and platform-friendly formatting.

- Official page: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Support: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

**Section A — What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack):**
For each batch you’ll receive a “Delivery Pack” folder containing:
1) **Final vertical MP4 clips** (ready to post)
2) **Caption files (SRT)** for each clip (optional upload to platforms that support it)
3) **B-roll / overlay cue sheet** (timestamps + suggestions)
4) **Posting suggestions** per clip:
   - 1st line caption (hook)
   - suggested caption body
   - hashtags (light + niche)
   - CTA suggestion (comment prompt or link direction)

**Section B — Quality standards (what we check before you see it):**
We run a final QA pass on every clip:
- **Safe zones & framing:** faces not cropped; key text stays inside TikTok/Reels safe areas.
- **Captions:** accurate to spoken words; punctuation for readability; no repeated/garbled lines.
- **Audio:** consistent loudness; reduced background noise; no clipping/distortion.
- **Hook clarity:** first 1–2 seconds clearly communicates “why watch.”
- **Pacing:** clean jump cuts; remove dead air; keep intent and meaning intact.
- **Branding:** correct spelling of names/handles; consistent style (if provided).
- **Export settings:** 1080×1920 vertical, 30fps (unless specified), high bitrate, AAC audio.

**Section C — Review flow (how to request changes):**
1) You’ll receive a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) for timestamp comments.
2) Please leave feedback as **timestamped notes** (example: “00:07 change caption line 2”).
3) If you prefer email, send feedback in this format:
   - Clip filename
   - Timestamp(s)
   - What to change (exact wording if it’s caption text)

**Section D — Revision window & turnaround:**
- **Revision window:** 24 hours after delivery/review link is sent.
- **Turnaround:** revisions are typically completed within 24 hours of receiving consolidated notes.
- Best practice: send **one consolidated list** per clip to avoid delay.

**Section E — What counts as a free revision vs. a change request:**
**Free revisions (included):**
- Caption fixes (typos, missed words, punctuation readability)
- Minor timing adjustments (caption sync, tiny trim)
- Audio level balancing within the same clip
- Safe-zone/framing fixes (no face cropped, reposition captions)
- Fixing anything that violates the agreed specs

**Change requests (out of scope):**
- Choosing completely different moments from the long-form
- Rewriting hooks/CTA beyond minor tweaks
- New creative direction/style after approval
- Adding new branded elements not provided before editing
- Additional clips beyond the agreed count

If a request is a change request, we’ll reply with options (what we can do + new ETA). During Week 1 we can still accommodate small changes at no cost when feasible, but we label it clearly to prevent timeline risk.

**Section F — What we need from you (Intake):**
Please complete the intake form (link provided in onboarding email). It covers:
- Target platforms (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Tone (educational, punchy, calm, etc.)
- CTA + handle/links
- Banned words/topics
- Caption style preferences
- Examples of clips you like
