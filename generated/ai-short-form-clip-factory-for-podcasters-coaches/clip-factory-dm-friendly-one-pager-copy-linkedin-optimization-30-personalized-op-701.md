## 4) 24-Hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 ready-to-post vertical clip in ≤24 hours to maximize conversion to a weekly package.

### A) Intake (0–15 minutes)
1) Confirm receipt of link (YouTube/Drive) and ask 3 questions:
   - Target platform: TikTok / Reels / Shorts
   - Audience + CTA (follow, book call, download, comment)
   - Style preference: “clean/subtle” vs “bold/fast” captions
2) Ask for brand assets (optional): logo, colors, font preference, handle.
3) Log in CRM: date/time, link, niche, CTA, promised delivery deadline.

### B) Content Selection (15–45 minutes)
1) Skim transcript/chapters (or listen at 1.5–2x) for:
   - strong hook line in first 1–2 seconds
   - a single clear payoff (one idea per clip)
   - emotion/contrast: mistake → fix, myth → truth, before → after
2) Select 45–75 seconds of raw material (aim final 20–45s unless the content demands longer).

### C) Edit + Captions (45–180 minutes)
1) Crop to 9:16 with subject centered (safe margins for UI overlays).
2) Add burned-in captions:
   - 2 lines max
   - high contrast, readable
   - highlight 1–3 keywords (consistent style)
3) Add a hook text line if needed (first 1–2 seconds):
   - “Stop doing X” / “If you’re struggling with Y…” / “3 mistakes with Z”
4) Light cleanup:
   - remove dead air
   - normalize volume
   - subtle zoom cuts if needed

### D) Export Settings (10 minutes)
- 1080x1920 (9:16), H.264, 10–16 Mbps, AAC audio 320 kbps.
- Filename: {ClientName}_{Topic}_{Platform}_{Date}.mp4

### E) Delivery (5–15 minutes)
1) Deliver via Drive link + short instructions:
   - recommended caption copy (1–2 options)
   - hashtags (5–10 niche-specific)
   - posting suggestion (best time window)
2) Ask a single conversion question:
   “Want me to do this as a weekly batch? Typical clients do 8–30 clips/week depending on volume.”
3) Include proof link + contact:
   - Official site: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
   - Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### F) Quality Checklist (before sending)
- Captions synced and typo-free
- First 2 seconds have a clear hook
- No cut-off words at edit points
- Safe margins respected (no text under UI)
- Audio intelligible on phone speakers

### G) After-delivery follow-up (next day)
Send: “Any tweaks to match your brand voice? If you like it, I can lock the style and deliver {X} clips/week consistently.”
