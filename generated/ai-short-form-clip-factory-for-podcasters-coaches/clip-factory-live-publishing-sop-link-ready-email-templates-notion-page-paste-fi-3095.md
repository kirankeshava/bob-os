# Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)

**Official site:** https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

**Contact:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

## The Goal
We turn your long-form video/podcast into **ready-to-post vertical clips** with:
- Strong hooks (first 1–2 seconds)
- Clean on-screen captions
- Tight pacing and jump cuts
- Platform-friendly framing (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
- Optional b-roll cues + posting suggestions

## What We Need From You (Before Editing Starts)
Please complete the intake form (we’ll send a link). We typically need:
- Your source file link (video/audio)
- Where you’re posting (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts)
- Brand tone + any banned words/topics
- Your handle(s), CTA, and any link you want included
- Examples of clips you like (optional but helpful)

## Our QA Standard (What We Check Before You See Anything)
We run a final quality pass on every clip:

### 1) Framing & Safe Zones
- Subject stays centered and readable on mobile
- No cropped faces/foreheads/chins during key moments
- Captions stay within safe zones (not blocked by UI)
- Any inserted b-roll does not hide important facial expression moments

### 2) Captions (Accuracy + Readability)
- Captions match the spoken words (no major omissions)
- Names/brands/terms spelled correctly (based on intake)
- Punctuation and line breaks optimized for fast reading
- No “caption drift” (timing stays aligned with speech)

### 3) Audio Quality
- Dialogue is clear and consistent
- No harsh clipping, peaking, or sudden level jumps
- Loudness normalized so it’s comfortable on phone speakers
- Background noise reduced where possible without robotic artifacts

### 4) Hook & Clarity
- First line/hook is clear and compelling
- Clip has a single main idea (no confusion)
- If context is missing, we add a short intro caption or re-cut for clarity

### 5) Pacing & Jump Cuts
- Dead air removed
- Jump cuts feel intentional (not distracting)
- Visual changes support retention (subtle zooms/cutaways if needed)

### 6) Export & Platform Compliance
- Vertical format (9:16)
- MP4 format suitable for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- No black bars unless explicitly requested
- File naming consistent so you can post fast

## Review Flow (How You Give Feedback)
We send a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube). Please leave feedback as:
- **Timestamped comments** (e.g., “00:12 change caption wording”, “00:27 remove ‘um’”)
- Clear, single-action requests whenever possible

### Revision Window
- **24-hour revision window** starts when we send the review link.
- If we don’t receive notes within the window, we assume approval and proceed to final delivery.

## Delivery Pack (What You Receive)
You’ll receive a folder containing:
1) **Final MP4 clips** (ready to post)
2) **SRT caption files** (one per clip)
3) **B-roll cue sheet** (timestamps + suggested overlays/cutaways)
4) **Posting suggestions** per clip:
   - Suggested first line (caption)
   - Suggested hashtags (starter set)
   - Posting note (e.g., “pin comment CTA”, “use trending audio optional”)

## Revision & Change Policy (To Protect Deadlines)
We’re fast and flexible, but we separate **fixes** from **scope changes**.

### Free Revisions (Included)
Free revisions cover:
- Caption typos or mis-hears
- Caption timing/line-break readability adjustments
- Minor audio leveling fixes
- Minor framing/safe-zone fixes
- Removing obvious filler words (“um”, “uh”) if it doesn’t change meaning

### Paid Changes (Out of Scope)
(If/when paid plans apply; during the free launch, we’ll confirm feasibility case-by-case.)
Out-of-scope requests include:
- Changing the clip concept/angle after delivery (“different hook”, “new story arc”)
- Large restructuring or swapping to a different part of the episode
- New branding packages, heavy motion graphics, or custom templates
- Multiple rounds of revisions beyond the included window

If you request a change that materially increases time, we’ll message you with options before proceeding.

## How to Get the Fastest Turnaround
- Submit the intake form completely
- Provide 2–3 example clips you like (optional)
- Give timestamped notes in one pass (batch feedback)
- Confirm platform + CTA early
