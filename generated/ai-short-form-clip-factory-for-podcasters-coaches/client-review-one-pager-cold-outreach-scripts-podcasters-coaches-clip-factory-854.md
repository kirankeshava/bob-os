Title: How to Review Your Clips (and Request Revisions)

Thanks for trusting Clip Factory. To keep turnaround fast and consistent, please review using the checklist below and send revision requests in the same format.

Where to review
1) Watch each clip in full-screen on your phone (sound ON) to confirm pacing, captions, and safe-zone placement.
2) If you’re reviewing on desktop, also do a quick “mobile check” before requesting changes.

What “Approved” means
A clip is approved when:
- The first 1–2 seconds feel scroll-stopping (clear hook / curiosity / bold claim).
- Captions are readable on mobile (no cutoffs, good contrast, no covering the speaker’s mouth).
- The message is accurate and aligned with your brand.
- The end has a clear CTA (follow / comment / book / download) where applicable.

What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are limited to edits that do not change the core selection or require rebuilding the clip:
- Caption corrections (typos, punctuation, a few words)
- Minor timing tweaks (tighten pauses, adjust cut by a second or two)
- Replace CTA text (end-card copy)
- Swap subtitle style within the agreed preset (e.g., position slightly higher/lower)

What is NOT included (requires a new clip or add-on)
To protect the 48-hour delivery promise, these are out of scope for included revisions:
- New clip selection (different segment / different topic)
- Major restructuring (re-cutting the story, new hook concept, multiple new b-roll sequences)
- Adding new assets not provided during intake (logos, brand packs, product screenshots)
- Compliance/legal rewrites that fundamentally alter the spoken message
