**Clip Factory — How to Review Your Clips (and Request Revisions)**

Thanks for working with us. To keep the **48-hour delivery pace** and make revisions fast, please review clips using the steps below.

**Where your clips live**
- You’ll receive either: (1) a folder link (Google Drive/Dropbox) or (2) a review link (Frame.io / similar) with all exports.
- Each clip filename includes its **hook keyword + timestamp + version** so it’s easy to reference.

**What to look for (quick review checklist)**
1) **Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds):** Is it attention-grabbing and easy to understand?
2) **Captions:** Are there typos, missing words, or timing issues?
3) **Pacing:** Does it feel tight (no dead air)? Are pattern interrupts too frequent/not enough?
4) **Framing:** Is the speaker centered and readable (safe margins for TikTok/IG)?
5) **Brand fit:** Does the tone match your audience (no overly aggressive edits if you’re more professional)?

**How to request revisions (fastest method)**
Reply to the delivery email (or comment in the review tool) using this exact format:
- **Clip file name:** 
- **Timestamp (mm:ss):** 
- **Change requested:** (e.g., “Replace caption word ‘X’ with ‘Y’”, “Cut 0.5s pause”, “Swap hook text overlay”, “Remove b-roll cue”) 
- **Priority:** Must-fix / Nice-to-have

**Revision policy (so we can stay fast)**
- Includes **1 revision round** for the batch, limited to:
  - Caption text fixes, timing tweaks
  - Small trims (remove filler, tighten pacing)
  - Hook text overlay tweaks
  - Minor b-roll cue adjustments
- Out of scope for the included revision round:
  - Changing the core clip selection strategy after delivery (new selects)
  - Full re-edits with new creative direction across all clips
  - Custom motion graphics packages beyond the agreed style preset

**Revision deadline**
