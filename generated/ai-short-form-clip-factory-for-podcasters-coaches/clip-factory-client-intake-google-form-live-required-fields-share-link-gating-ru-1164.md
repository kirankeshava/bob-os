FIELDS (with required settings)
1) Contact & Project
- Your name (Short answer, Required)
- Email for delivery/review (Short answer, Required)
- Brand / channel name (Short answer, Required)
- Primary platform(s) (Checkboxes: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, Other; Required)
- Clip volume requested (Multiple choice: 5, 10, 20, Other; Required)
- Deadline / desired delivery date (Date, Required)

2) Source Media
- Link to long-form video/podcast file (Short answer, Required)
- If multiple files, paste all links + any timestamps of best sections (Paragraph, Optional)

3) Audience & Offer
- Who is your target audience? (Paragraph, Required)
- What action should viewers take? (Multiple choice: Follow, Comment, Visit link, Book call, Buy product, Other; Required)
- If ‘Other’, specify CTA (Short answer, Optional)

4) Hook + Style Preferences
- Hook style (Checkboxes: Contrarian, List/steps, Question, Bold claim, Story, Educational, Other; Required)
- Pace preference (Multiple choice: Fast cuts, Medium, Minimal cuts; Required)
- On-screen text density (Multiple choice: Heavy, Balanced, Light; Required)
- Caption styling reference link(s) (Short answer, Optional)

5) Captions & Language
- Caption format (Multiple choice: Word-by-word karaoke, Sentence-based, Hybrid; Required)
- Profanity policy (Multiple choice: Allow, Bleep, Replace with symbols, None; Required)
- Terms that must be spelled exactly (Paragraph, Optional)
- Banned words/phrases (Paragraph, Optional)
- Names/terms with pronunciation notes (Paragraph, Optional)

6) Brand Safety / Compliance
- Topics to avoid (Paragraph, Optional)
- Required disclaimer text (e.g., not financial advice) (Paragraph, Optional)

7) Visuals
- Face framing priority (Multiple choice: Keep full face centered, Keep eyes in upper third, Creator is not always on screen; Required)
- Preferred b-roll style (Checkboxes: Stock-style, Screen recordings, Product shots, Minimal b-roll, Other; Optional)
- Brand colors / fonts (Paragraph, Optional)

8) Review + Revisions Acknowledgement (Required)
- Checkbox (Required): “I understand I’ll receive a review link for timestamped comments, and revisions are available for 24 hours after delivery of the review/finals as defined in the revision policy.”

No work starts until the intake form is submitted. If the client wants to start immediately, they must explicitly waive missing info in writing (email/message), and any resulting changes become paid change requests (not free revisions).
