B) EXPORT SPECS (use for all renders)
- Format: 9:16 vertical
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- FPS: 30
- Length target: 25–45s (max 60s)
- Captions: burned-in, large, high contrast; 2–4 words per line; highlight 1 keyword per sentence (brand color optional)
- Safe margins: keep text inside central 80% (avoid UI overlays)
- Audio: normalize to -14 LUFS target; remove long pauses
- Visual style: punch-in zooms every 2–4 seconds; cut on meaning changes; add simple emoji/underline emphasis sparingly
