### A) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch, you’ll receive a folder containing:
1. **Final vertical MP4 clips** (ready to upload)
2. **Caption files (SRT)** for each clip
3. **B-Roll / Visual cue sheet** (timestamps with suggested overlays, cutaways, screen recordings, inserts)
4. **Posting suggestions** for each clip:
   - Suggested **first-line caption** (hook)
   - Suggested **hashtags** (platform-appropriate)
   - Suggested **CTA** (follow, comment keyword, link-in-bio prompt)

---

### B) QA standards (how we prevent mistakes)
Before anything is delivered, we run a consistent QA pass:

**1) Safe zones + framing**
- Subject remains in-frame throughout; no cutoffs of chin/forehead.
- No cropped faces in key moments.
- Proper vertical framing (9:16) with platform-safe margins (avoid UI overlays).

**2) Captions accuracy**
- Captions match spoken words with high accuracy.
- Correct spelling for names, brands, and key terms (from your intake form).
- Captions timed to speech; no long delays or “laggy” lines.

**3) Audio leveling**
- Dialogue is clear and consistently leveled.
- Background noise reduced; harsh sibilance limited.
- No peaking/clipping.

**4) Jump cuts + pacing**
- Cuts feel intentional (no awkward mid-word chops).
- Minimal dead air.
- Retains meaning while increasing speed/clarity.

**5) Hook clarity**
- First 1–2 seconds contain a clear hook (text + edit choice).
- Clip has a single main takeaway.

**6) Export settings**
- Format: MP4 (H.264), 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Delivery is compatible with TikTok / Reels / Shorts.
- No black bars unless explicitly requested.

---

### C) Review flow (fast and simple)
We send a **review link** (preferred: Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) with **timestamped commenting** enabled.

**How to request revisions:**
- Leave comments with timestamps (e.g., “00:12 — caption typo: change ‘their’ to ‘there’”).
- If you want an alternate hook or different cut, describe the goal (e.g., “Make hook more direct for coaches; keep the key quote at 00:20”).

**Revision window:**
- You have **24 hours** after we send the review link to request revisions.
- Requests received after the window may be scheduled as a paid change (see Revision Policy below).

### E) Revision policy (what’s free vs paid)
We aim for zero surprises. Here’s the policy that keeps delivery fast and fair.

**Free revisions (included):**
- Caption typos / punctuation / timing fixes
- Minor audio level adjustments
- Small reframes to fix safe-zone/cropping issues
- Fixes for anything that doesn’t match your submitted intake requirements
- Small timing trims (e.g., remove 1–2 seconds of dead air)

**Paid changes (scope changes):**
- New creative direction after delivery (e.g., “make it a totally different angle”)
- Swapping to different clip selections not in the original request
- Major restructuring (new narrative order, new story arc)
- Additional clips beyond the agreed count
- Requests submitted after the 24-hour revision window

If something is unclear, email us: **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** and we’ll reply with the fastest path.
