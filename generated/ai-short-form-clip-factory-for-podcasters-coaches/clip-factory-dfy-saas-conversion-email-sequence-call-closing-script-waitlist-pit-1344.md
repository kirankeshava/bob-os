B) DISCOVERY CALL → PAID PILOT CLOSING SCRIPT (10–12 minutes)
Goal: close DFY now; capture SaaS requirements; do NOT promise build timelines; preserve $5k gate.

1) Set frame (30 sec)
“Thanks for the time. In 10 minutes we’ll confirm your goals, what ‘good clips’ means for you, and if it’s a fit I’ll propose a paid pilot so you can judge quality with real output. Sound good?”

2) Qualify content + cadence (2 min)
- “What’s your content format (podcast/interview/coaching calls)? Length per episode?”
- “How often do you publish long-form?”
- “Where do you want clips posted (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) and who posts them?”

3) Define success (2 min)
- “What would make clips a win in the next 30 days—leads, followers, booked calls, watch time?”
- “Any examples of clips you love (send links after)?”

4) Workflow constraints (2 min)
- “Do you need speaker labels (host/guest)?”
- “Brand requirements: fonts, colors, caption style, safe words, disclaimers?”
- “Do you want b-roll cues or should we keep it punch-zoom + captions?”

5) Offer the paid pilot (2 min)
“Here’s what I recommend: a Week 1 paid pilot batch. You send 1 episode link (or upload), we deliver {{X}} vertical clips with burned-in captions, hook variants, and platform-ready exports. Turnaround: {{time}}. If you love it, we continue weekly. If not, you keep the whole batch.”

6) Close + logistics (1–2 min)
- “Do you want {{8}} clips/week or {{15}} clips/week to start?”
- “If we start today, can you send the link within 24 hours?”
- “Where should we deliver (Google Drive/Dropbox)?”

7) SaaS signal capture (60 sec, after close or if they hesitate)
“I’m also validating a DIY tool for teams that want to generate clips faster. If it existed today, what would you pay monthly for it—$99, $199, or $299—assuming it reliably finds highlights and exports vertical captions? What feature would be non-negotiable?”
