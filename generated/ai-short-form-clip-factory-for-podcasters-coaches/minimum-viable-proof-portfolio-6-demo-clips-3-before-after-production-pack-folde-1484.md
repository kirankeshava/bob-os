2) FITNESS — 2 clips
FT-01 Listicle (30–45s)
- Hook template: “3 reasons your fat loss stalled (and what to do today).”
- Structure: #1 nutrition, #2 steps/NEAT, #3 sleep/stress.
- Edit beats: number pop animations; cut on each list item; add b-roll of grocery cart, walking, sleep.

FT-02 Testimonial/Result Framing (25–40s)
- Hook template: “A client lost X in Y weeks—here’s the boring part that worked.”
- Edit beats: on-screen ‘Result → Method’; emphasize ‘boring part’; add progress photo placeholder (blurred) or generic gym b-roll.

3) B2B — 2 clips
B2B-01 Storytelling Hook (25–45s)
- Hook template: “We were stuck at [metric] until we changed one thing in our outreach.”
- Edit beats: underline the metric; add ‘Before/After’ overlay; b-roll of inbox/CRM.

B2B-02 Contrarian Take (25–45s)
- Hook template: “Cold email isn’t dead—your offer is.”
- Edit beats: big headline first 0.5s; cut to supporting proof; add 2 quick ‘proof bullets’ overlays.

C) BEFORE/AFTER (3 exports)
BA-01 Captions + Cuts Before/After (20–30s)
- Format: split screen. Left = raw excerpt (no captions). Right = finished (captions + punch-ins).
- Label overlays: “RAW” and “FINAL”.

BA-02 Hook Rewrite Before/After (20–30s)
- Format: sequential. First 10s = raw opening. Next 10–15s = final with a stronger hook line as text overlay.
- Overlay: “Hook upgrade + pacing + captions”.

BA-03 B-roll Cues Before/After (20–30s)
- Format: sequential. Show raw talking head, then final with 2–3 b-roll inserts + on-screen cue text like “(B-ROLL: city skyline)” briefly appearing.
