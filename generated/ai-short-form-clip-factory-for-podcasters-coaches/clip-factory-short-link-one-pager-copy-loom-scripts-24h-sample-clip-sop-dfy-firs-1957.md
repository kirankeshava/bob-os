## 3) 24-Hour “Free Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

**Objective:** Deliver a high-quality sample clip within 24 hours of receiving a link, maximizing conversion into a weekly DFY package.

### Inputs required (ask in one message)
- Episode link (YouTube/Drive) + permission to download
- Target platform (Reels/TikTok/Shorts)
- Audience + desired CTA (follow, book call, newsletter)
- Brand notes (font preference/colors if any; otherwise use clean default)

### Step-by-step production
1) **Select segment (10–45 seconds)**
   - Criteria: strong opening line, clear claim, emotional/contrarian angle, or tactical value.
   - Avoid long setups; first 1.5 seconds must hook.

2) **Write 3 hook options**
   - Hook A: contrarian/curiosity
   - Hook B: outcome-driven
   - Hook C: “mistake” framing

3) **Captioning rules**
   - Large, high-contrast, safe margins for UI overlays.
   - 6–10 words per line max; break on meaning.
   - Emphasize 1–2 keywords per sentence.

4) **B-roll cues (even if not fully executed)**
   - Add time-coded notes: “Overlay screenshot of X” / “Cut to example visual” / “Add headline text.”
   - If no b-roll available, use kinetic text + punch-in zooms.

5) **Export spec**
   - 1080x1920 (9:16), 30fps.
   - Loudness normalized for mobile.
   - Filename: [Client]_[Episode]_[Topic]_[Platform].mp4

6) **Delivery email template**
Subject: Your sample clip is ready (vertical + captions)
