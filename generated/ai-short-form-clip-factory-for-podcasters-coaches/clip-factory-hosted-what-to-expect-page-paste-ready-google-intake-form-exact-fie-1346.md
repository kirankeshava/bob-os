## 1) What You’ll Receive (Delivery Pack)
For each order, you receive a **Delivery Pack** containing:
1. **Final vertical MP4 clips** (ready to post)
2. **Caption file for each clip** (SRT)
3. **B-roll / on-screen cue sheet** (timecoded suggestions)
4. **Posting suggestions** for each clip:
   - recommended first-line caption (hook)
   - suggested hashtags (platform-specific)
   - recommended posting notes (CTA, pinned comment idea)

---

## 2) Your Quick Intake (Required Before We Start)
To avoid wasted edits and missed preferences, we ask you to complete a short intake form before production starts. It takes ~3 minutes.

What we collect: platform targets, brand terms, words to avoid, caption style, hooks you like, and any compliance notes.

**Intake is required before work begins** (unless you explicitly waive it in writing).

---

## 3) Review Flow (Timestamped Feedback)
We will send a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can:
- watch each clip
- leave **timestamped comments**
- confirm hooks/captions and any small fixes

### How to Leave Feedback
Please leave feedback as timestamped notes, e.g.:
- “00:03 – caption should say ‘client acquisition’ not ‘customer acquisition’”
- “00:08 – cut 0.3s earlier to remove the ‘um’”
- “00:12 – reduce caption size slightly”

This is the fastest way to finalize without back-and-forth.

---

## 4) Revisions Window (24 Hours)
You have **24 hours from the time we send the review link** to request revisions included in your package.

If we don’t receive revision notes within 24 hours, we treat the clips as **approved** and proceed to final delivery.

---

## 5) What Counts as Included Revisions vs. Paid Changes
### Included revisions (free, within the 24-hour window)
- Caption typo fixes / punctuation / light rewording for accuracy
- Timing adjustments for captions (sync)
- Minor audio leveling adjustments
- Minor jump-cut tweaks (tighten a pause, remove filler word)
- Safe framing fixes (face not cropped; subtitle safe zone)
- Swap a b-roll suggestion if it conflicts with your brand/banned topics

### Paid changes (scope change)
- Changing the clip selection / swapping to entirely different moments after review
- Rewriting hooks/captions to a new angle that changes the message/intent
- New clip requests not included in the order
- New brand style direction after edits already started (fonts/colors/caption style overhaul)
- Significant restructuring (new storyline, different CTA strategy, new format)

If something is a paid change, we’ll tell you first and quote options.

---

## 6) Quality Standards We Check Before You See Anything
Every clip goes through a final QC pass:
- **No cropped faces** + platform-safe framing (Reels/TikTok safe zones)
- **Captions match spoken words** (accuracy + timing)
- **Audio is leveled** (no clipping/distortion; voice clarity)
- **Hook clarity** (first 1–2 seconds are compelling)
- **Clean jump cuts** (no distracting glitches)
- **Export settings** correct for vertical platforms

## Internal Rule (Ops Gate): No-Work-Without-Intake
**Policy:** Do not begin clip selection, captioning, or edits until the client submits the intake form.

**Exception:** If the client refuses/doesn’t have time, they must reply in writing: “Proceed without intake; I accept that revisions may be limited to typos and technical fixes.” File that message in the job folder.

**Why this exists:** The top refund triggers are mismatched caption style, banned terms, platform format misses, and ‘wrong moments’ selection. Intake reduces these before production starts, protecting delivery speed and reputation.
