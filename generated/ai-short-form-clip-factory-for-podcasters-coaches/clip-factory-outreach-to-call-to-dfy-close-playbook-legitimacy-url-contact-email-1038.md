## 1) Prospecting targets (fastest paths)
Use these channels to find podcasters/coaches who already create long-form content and need consistent Reels/TikTok output.

### A. Where to find prospects (free)
1) **YouTube (podcasts + coaches):** search queries:
- “podcast episode #” + (coach | founder | real estate | fitness | marketing)
- “full podcast” + “clips” (look for channels with long episodes but inconsistent shorts)
- “live coaching call” / “webinar replay” (long-form assets, usually no repurposing)

2) **Apple Podcasts / Spotify:** pick categories with high monetization:
- Business, Entrepreneurship, Marketing, Health & Fitness, Real Estate
Then locate the host on LinkedIn/IG via show notes.

3) **LinkedIn:** search for:
- Title: “Coach”, “Founder”, “Agency Owner”, “Podcast Host”
- Keywords: “podcast”, “newsletter”, “webinar”, “YouTube”
Filter for creators posting video but not consistently posting shorts.

4) **Instagram/TikTok:** look for creators with:
- 5k–200k followers, long captions, obvious offers, and sporadic video cadence

### B. Prospect qualification checklist (choose those most likely to pay)
- Has **at least 2 long-form pieces/month** (podcast, YouTube, webinars)
- Is selling something (course/coaching/agency) and has a clear CTA
- Existing shorts are **inconsistent** or **low quality** (bad hooks, weak captions)
- They mention “team”, “editor”, “content calendar” (budget signal)

## 3) Discovery call script (20 minutes) — sell DFY + capture SaaS signal
**Goal:** Convert to a paid DFY pilot now; collect structured MVP requirements (without building).

### 0:00–2:00 — Set agenda
“Thanks {{Name}}. In 20 minutes I want to (1) understand your content + growth goals, (2) show how we’d turn one episode into clips, and if it’s a fit (3) lock a 2-week pilot so you get clips shipping next week. Sound good?”

### 2:00–7:00 — Current workflow + pain
1) “What’s your current cadence for long-form? (weekly, biweekly)”
2) “How are you producing shorts today? Who edits?”
3) “What’s the biggest bottleneck: finding moments, writing hooks, captions, speed, or consistency?”
4) “Where do you post: TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts, LinkedIn?”

### 7:00–12:00 — Offer + process (DFY)
“Here’s how Clip Factory works:
- You send a link/upload
- We identify 8–12 highlight moments
- We add a hook, burned-in captions, and b-roll cue notes
- Export in 9:16, platform-safe settings
- You approve in a simple list; we deliver ready-to-post files.”

**Fit check:** “If you had 12 strong clips every week for the next month, what would that do for leads/sales?”

### 12:00–16:00 — Close a 2-week paid pilot
“Best next step is a **2-week pilot** so you can judge quality + consistency.
- Deliverable: **24 clips total** (12/week)
- Includes hooks + captions + b-roll cues
- Turnaround: first batch in 3–4 business days after you send the link(s)

If we start this week, which episode(s) would you want clipped first?”

(If price comes up, anchor to outcome and simplicity.)
“Pricing depends on volume, but the pilot is designed to be simple and fast. If we agree on clip volume and turnaround, are you comfortable starting with the pilot?”
