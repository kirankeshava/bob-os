1) Before we start (Required Intake)
To avoid missed brand terms, wrong caption style, and avoidable revisions, we require a quick intake form before production begins:
Client Intake Form: https://forms.gle/CLIPFACTORY-IN-TAKE-LINK

You’ll be asked for:
- Links to your source video(s)/podcast episode(s)
- Your target platforms (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
- Preferred caption style (clean / bold / word-by-word emphasis)
- Brand terms (spellings, names, products) + banned words
- Tone preferences (direct, educational, humorous, etc.)
- Example creators you like (optional)

2) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each clip set, you’ll receive a structured Delivery Pack containing:
A) Final vertical MP4(s) — ready to post
B) Caption files (SRT) — one per clip
C) B-roll cue sheet — timecodes + suggested overlays/cutaways
D) Posting suggestions — per clip:
   - 1–2 recommended first-line captions
   - Suggested hashtags (platform-appropriate)
   - Notes on ideal posting time / CTA (when relevant)

3) Review flow (timestamp comments)
We send a Review Link (unlisted) so you can leave timestamped comments.
How to review efficiently:
- Watch each clip once without stopping
- Second pass: leave timestamped notes (e.g., “00:07 change ‘their’ to ‘there’”)
- Group requests per clip to reduce back-and-forth

4) Revision window (24 hours)
You have 24 hours from the time we send the Review Link to request revisions included in your package.
If we don’t hear back within 24 hours, we’ll assume approval and deliver finals.

5) What counts as a free revision vs. paid change
Included (free) revisions — within the 24h window:
- Caption corrections (typos, punctuation, missed words)
- Minor timing adjustments to captions
- Small framing fixes (if faces are cropped/unsafe)
- Audio leveling fixes (if inconsistent vs. other clips)
- Removing a single phrase you flagged as “banned” IF it was provided in intake
- Minor hook tightening (same core idea)

Not included (paid change requests):
- Changing clip selection direction after delivery (new timestamps / new angles)
- New versions for different audiences (e.g., “make it for realtors instead”)
- Rewriting significant portions of captions to be a different script
- Adding brand-new b-roll research beyond the cue sheet scope
- Additional aspect ratios or reformatting beyond agreed platforms
- Requests submitted after the 24-hour window

If something is objectively our mistake (e.g., we misspelled a brand name you provided, or cropped your face), we fix it regardless of the window.

6) Technical standards (what we QC)
Every clip is checked for:
- Safe zones: no critical text/face in UI-covered areas
- No cropped faces or awkward headroom
- Caption accuracy and timing
- Audio: no peaking/clipping, consistent loudness across clips
- Clean jump cuts (no jarring mid-word cuts)
- Hook clarity in the first 1–2 seconds
- Export settings appropriate for vertical platforms (high-quality MP4)
