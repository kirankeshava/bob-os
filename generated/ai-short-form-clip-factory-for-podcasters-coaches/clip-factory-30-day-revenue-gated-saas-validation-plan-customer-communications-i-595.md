# Clip Factory — 30-Day Revenue-Gated SaaS Validation (No Build Until $5k DFY)

## North Star
Collect **$5,000 in DFY clip-package revenue** first. Only then greenlight any SaaS MVP build (link/upload → highlights → captions export). Until that threshold, every customer interaction must do two things:
1) Convert into DFY revenue now (clips delivered manually/with lightweight tooling), and
2) Capture paid-signal requirements for a future $99–$299/mo subscription.

---

## 30-Day Plan (Distribution → Revenue → Product Signal)

### Week 1: Recruit + Book Calls (10 total)
**Goal:** 10 discovery calls booked with podcasters/coaches + 2 paid pilots closed.
- Target list: podcasters (5k–100k subs), coaches with weekly longform, small creator agencies, B2B founders who podcast.
- Channels: email, LinkedIn DM, IG/Twitter DM.
- Offer: “Free Clip Audit” + “Paid Pilot option (fast turnaround)”.

**KPIs:**
- 60 outbound messages → 10 booked calls (16% booking)
- 10 calls → 2 paid pilots (20% close)

### Week 2: Run Calls + Close Paid Pilots
**Goal:** Close enough DFY to approach $5k (e.g., 5 clients at $1k, or 10 at $500).
- Use the call to qualify, then present a paid pilot deposit.
- Immediately send recap + invoice instructions (handled by sales ops). 

### Week 3: Deliver DFY + Collect Proof + Upsell Signal
**Goal:** Deliver results fast; gather testimonials and “what would you pay monthly for?” answers.
- Track what takes time: transcript cleanup, selecting hooks, captions style, b-roll notes.
- Note dealbreakers: turnaround, accuracy, brand style, compliance.

### Week 4: Aggregate Feature Demand + Lock MVP Spec (Still No Build Unless $5k Hit)
**Goal:** Convert interview + DFY data into:
- Top 5 subscription features customers will pay for
- Pricing band confidence ($99 vs $149 vs $299)
- Build-vs-buy decision finalization once revenue gate is met
