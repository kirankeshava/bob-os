## 1) Your feedback deadline (protects the SLA)
**Feedback window:** Please send notes **within 6 hours** of receiving the review link.

- If feedback arrives within 6 hours: we can usually still keep the 48-hour delivery promise.
- If feedback arrives later: we’ll still revise, but the final delivery time may shift.

---

## 2) The only feedback format we can process fast
Please give notes **per clip** using this structure:

**CLIP NAME:** (exact filename we provided)  
**TIMESTAMP:** (e.g., 00:03–00:06)  
**ACTION:** Keep / Cut / Change  
**NOTE:** (one sentence, specific)

### Examples
- **CLIP NAME:** RE_2026-04-09_E12_C03_HomePriceDrops_v1.mp4  
  **TIMESTAMP:** 00:00–00:02  
  **ACTION:** Change  
  **NOTE:** Replace hook text with “3 signs your listing is overpriced”

- **CLIP NAME:** Fit_2026-04-09_E07_C06_ProteinMistake_v1.mp4  
  **TIMESTAMP:** 00:12–00:15  
  **ACTION:** Cut  
  **NOTE:** Remove this sentence (too technical)

- **CLIP NAME:** B2B_2026-04-09_E03_C02_PricingAnchors_v1.mp4  
  **TIMESTAMP:** 00:00–end  
  **ACTION:** Keep  
  **NOTE:** Approved as-is

If it’s easier, you can also leave **timestamped comments inside Frame.io** (preferred). If you’re using Google Drive, put timestamps in your email.

## 3) What counts as “in-scope” revisions (fast)
Included revisions are limited to **one revision round** with edits like:
- Hook text tweak (same idea, better wording)
- Removing a sentence / tightening pacing
- Caption typo fixes
- Swapping CTA end card text (e.g., “Book a call” → “DM ‘PLAN’”)
- Minor b-roll cue adjustment (swap one overlay for another)

---

## 4) What causes delays (we can do it, but timeline may shift)
These are usually **out-of-scope** or SLA-impacting:
- Changing the entire clip topic/angle after the cut is approved
- Requesting multiple alternative versions of the same clip
- Heavy audio repair (severe noise/clipping) or missing source files
- New brand kit after we’ve already templated captions
- Compliance rewrites that require legal review (medical/financial claims)

If you need any of the above, email us at **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** and we’ll confirm scope + timing.
