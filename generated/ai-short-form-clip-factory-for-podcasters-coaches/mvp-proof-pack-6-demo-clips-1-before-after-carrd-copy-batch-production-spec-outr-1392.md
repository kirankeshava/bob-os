## Batch Production Spec (do this once; apply to all clips)
**Canvas / export**
- 1080x1920 (9:16), 30fps.
- Safe margins: keep captions and key face content within center 80% height; avoid bottom 15% (UI overlays).
- Loudness target: ~-14 LUFS integrated (or normalize to platform standard). Avoid clipping.

**Caption style**
- Burned-in captions, 2 lines max, high contrast.
- Highlight 1–3 keywords per sentence (color emphasis).
- Punctuation for pace. Add line breaks every 3–6 words.

**Framing / cuts**
- Auto-reframe on speaker, center face.
- Remove dead air >250ms where possible.
- Zoom cadence: subtle 105–115% push every 2–4 beats (don’t overdo).

**B-roll cues (lightweight but visible)**
- Use on-screen cue text only (no need to source b-roll for MVP):
  - Example overlay: “[B-ROLL: house walkthrough]” or “[B-ROLL: gym set / form close-up]”.
- Place cue at the moment the speaker mentions it; keep 0.8–1.2s.
