## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Clip Factory Review Guide (10 clips / 48-hour delivery)**

Thanks for working with us. To keep turnaround fast, here’s the fastest way to review and request revisions.

**What you’ll receive**
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + hook-first pacing + pattern interrupts
- Optional: b-roll cue notes (if included in your package)

**How to review (10–15 minutes total)**
1) **Watch each clip once without pausing** (judge “Would I watch this?”).
2) Watch a second time and only note:
   - Hook clarity in the first **1.5 seconds**
   - Any caption errors (names, numbers, acronyms)
   - Pacing (dead air > 0.3–0.5s)
   - Brand/style (font, color, caption position)
3) For any clip you like, simply reply **“Approved”** (no extra notes needed).

**What counts as a revision (included)**
- Caption corrections (typos, punctuation, speaker name, numbers)
- Minor timing trims (tighten intro/outro, remove filler)
- Subtitle style adjustments (within the chosen preset)
- Swap the first line hook text (keeping same clip segment)

**What does NOT count as a revision (new work / may require a new order)**
- Re-cutting the clip from a different timestamp/topic
- New creative direction after delivery (new style, new branding kit)
- Adding new b-roll not previously requested / not provided
- Rebuilding multiple clips due to strategy change

**Revision request format (required to keep SLA)**
Reply with:
- **Clip file name:** (example: `CLIP_03_TikTok_v1.mp4`)
- **Timecode:** (example: `0:02–0:05`)
- **Change needed:** (example: “Fix caption: ‘CRM’ not ‘cream’”)
- **Exact replacement text (if applicable):**

**Revision SLA**
- 1 revision round included if requested within **48 hours of delivery**.
- We return revisions within **24 hours** (typical same-day).
