============================
E) WHAT TO LOOK FOR (to decide $99–$299/mo features)
============================
Green-light signals (strong):
- They already pay an editor or tool and want to replace it.
- They mention “brand consistency” + “approval workflow” + “time savings” in the same sentence.
- They ask for diarization, templates, and batch export because they’re posting weekly.

Red flags (weak):
- They only want 1–2 clips/month.
- They won’t provide source files or won’t commit to a cadence.
- They treat clips as “nice to have.”

Use the scoring section to pick the top 3 subscription-worthy features; everything else stays DFY or backlog until paid demand is proven.
