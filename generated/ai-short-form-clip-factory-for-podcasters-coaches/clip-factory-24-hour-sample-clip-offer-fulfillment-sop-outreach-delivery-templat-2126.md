A) 24-hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (internal)
Goal: deliver 1 ready-to-post 9:16 clip with captions + hook text + posting suggestion in <24 hours from getting the source link.

1) Intake (5 minutes)
Collect (via email/DM):
- Source link (YouTube, Spotify video, Riverside, Drive, etc.)
- Target platform (TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- Audience (who they sell to)
- Desired CTA (follow, comment keyword, book call)
- Brand notes (tone: direct/educational/hype; any banned words)

2) Segment selection (15–25 minutes)
- Skim transcript or playback at 1.5–2x.
- Pick 30–45 seconds with:
  - A clear “pain → insight → payoff” arc
  - A strong opinion or surprising claim within first 1–2 seconds
  - Minimal context dependency
- Write 3 hook options (on-screen opening line) and choose the best one.

3) Edit assembly (35–60 minutes)
Produce a vertical 9:16 composition:
- Layout: speaker center; if horizontal video, crop to face/upper torso.
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast; 2–4 words highlighted per line.
- Pacing: remove dead air; tighten pauses; jump cuts OK.
- B-roll cues (optional text notes): 2–3 moments where simple stock/B-roll could enhance clarity.
- Branding: keep light for sample (avoid heavy brand work unless provided).

4) Quality checklist (10 minutes)
- First second has a hook (visual and/or caption).
- Captions accurate (names/terms corrected).
- Audio intelligible on phone speakers.
- Safe margins for UI (top/bottom).
- Export settings:
  - 1080x1920 (9:16)
  - H.264 MP4
  - 30fps (or match source)
  - Loudness normalized (no clipping)

5) Delivery (5 minutes)
Send:
- The MP4 file link (Drive/Dropbox) OR attach if small.
- Suggested caption (1–2 lines).
- Suggested post time/day (simple recommendation).
- Ask 2 quick questions to validate subscription features (see section D).
