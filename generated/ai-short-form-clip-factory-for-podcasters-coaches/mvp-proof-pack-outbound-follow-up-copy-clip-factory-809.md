## Clip Requirements (to standardize quality)
**Export:** 1080x1920 (9:16), 30fps, 8–20 Mbps.
**Duration:** 25–45s.
**Captions:** burned-in, high-contrast, safe margins (no text in bottom ~15% due to UI).
**Edit cadence:** remove dead air, add 5–8 jump cuts, 2–4 punch-in zooms.
**On-screen elements:**
- Top line: Hook (5–8 words)
- Mid: captions
- Occasional callouts: “STEP 1 / STEP 2”, “DO THIS / NOT THIS”, “REAL EXAMPLE”
**B-roll cues:** lightweight overlays or callouts (“[B-ROLL: listing photos]”, “[B-ROLL: gym exercise]”, “[B-ROLL: dashboard/CRM]”).
