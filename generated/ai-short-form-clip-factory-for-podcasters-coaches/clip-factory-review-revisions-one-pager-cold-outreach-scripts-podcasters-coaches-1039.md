## How to review in under 10 minutes
**Step 1 — Watch at 1.25x** and only decide: *Keep / Revise / Replace*.

**Step 2 — Use this simple checklist** for each clip:
- Hook grabs attention in the **first 1.5 seconds**
- Captions are readable on mobile (no face coverage, good contrast)
- The “point” lands by ~7–12 seconds (no long warm-up)
- Any CTAs match your brand voice

**Step 3 — Mark revisions using this format (copy/paste)**
Send your feedback to **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** like this:

**Clip filename:** CLIENT_EP05_C03_v1.mp4  
**Request type:** Revise (or Replace)  
**Timecode:** 00:00–00:02  
**Change:** Replace hook text with: “3 mistakes killing your leads”  
**Notes:** Keep the same segment; just update on-screen hook + first caption line.

### What counts as a “Revision” vs “Replace”
**Revision (included):**
- change on-screen hook text
- caption wording tweaks (not a full rewrite of every line)
- remove/add 1–2 seconds at start/end
- swap CTA end-card text
- minor layout changes (caption position, emphasis styling)

**Replace (may require a new select):**
- choose a totally different moment/topic
- add a new segment not in the original selects
- restructure the clip’s narrative significantly
