## The 3 Rules (follow these and we ship on time)
1) **Give feedback in one batch** (not drip messages). One consolidated pass = fastest revisions.
2) **Comment on the timeline** (Frame.io) or **timestamp** your notes (00:00–00:05). “The hook is weak” is slow; “Replace hook line at 00:00–00:02 with X” is fast.
3) **Approve 70–80%** and only revise what matters. Speed beats perfection for short-form.

## What “Good Feedback” Looks Like (copy/paste)
- **Hook:** “Change first 1.5s to: ‘If you’re a realtor in 2026, do this…’”
- **Captions:** “Increase caption size ~10%, keep highlighted words to max 2 per line.”
- **B-roll:** “At 00:06 add quick overlay: Zillow listing screenshot (blur address).”
- **CTA:** “End card should say: ‘DM “LISTING” for the script’.”

## Where to Leave Notes
### Option A (Preferred): Frame.io
- We’ll invite you to a Frame.io review folder.
- **Click the video → pause at the moment → type comment**.
- If you want a specific edit: include the **timestamp range** and the replacement text.

### Option B: Google Drive + Timestamps
- Open the clip in Drive.
- Send feedback as: **Clip Name + timestamp + instruction**.
  - Example: “CLIP03 — 00:02–00:04 replace subtitle text with: ‘3 mistakes killing your reach’.”

## Feedback Deadline (to protect the 48-hour promise)
- **Standard workflow:** You send source + brand assets → we deliver first pass within 48 hours.
- **Revisions:** Please return revision notes **within 12 hours** of delivery.
- If feedback arrives after 12 hours, we still complete revisions ASAP, but **delivery timing may extend** (because we keep the production queue moving).

## What Counts as a Revision (and what doesn’t)
**Included:**
- Hook line swap (same clip)
- Caption style tweaks (size/position/highlight)
- Minor trimming (±2 seconds)
- Replace a b-roll overlay or on-screen text

**Not included (new work):**
- Selecting brand new moments not in the delivered 10 clips
- Recutting into a different narrative or adding new graphics packages
- Platform strategy changes mid-batch (e.g., switching niche/offer)
