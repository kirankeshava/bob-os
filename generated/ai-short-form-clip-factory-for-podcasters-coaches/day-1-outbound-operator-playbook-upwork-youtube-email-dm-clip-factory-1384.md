A) Morning Block (90 minutes): Upwork proposals (6–8)
1) Find jobs with keywords: “podcast clips”, “short form editor”, “repurpose long form”, “reels/tiktok”, “captions”, “video podcast”.
2) For each job, capture in CRM: date, platform=Upwork, job URL, client name, budget, required deliverables, personalization hook (their show/topic), status=Proposed, next action=Follow up in 48h.
3) Proposal template (paste + customize):
Subject/Opening: “I can turn your latest episode into 10 ready-to-post vertical clips in 48 hours.”
Body: “Hey [Name] — I watched your [episode/title] and the segment at ~[timestamp/topic] is a perfect hook. I run Clip Factory: we convert long podcasts/videos into vertical TikTok/Reels/Shorts with hooks, word-accurate captions, safe-zone formatting, and b-roll cues.
Offer: I’ll deliver 10 clips in 48 hours from your latest episode. To prove fit, I’ll make 1 free 30–60s sample clip from your existing YouTube link today.
Quick questions: 1) Which platform is priority (Reels/TikTok/Shorts)? 2) Do you prefer burned-in captions or SRT too? 3) Any brand font/colors?
Legitimacy: Website: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
If you share your latest episode link, I’ll send the sample via email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to.”
Close: “Want me to cut the first sample from [their link]?”

B) Midday Block (60 minutes): YouTube value comments (20 total; 2 per creator on 10 creators)
Rule: comments must be real value, not spam. Structure:
Comment #1 (insight): “Great point about [specific topic]. One thing I’ve seen work is [actionable tip]. The way you framed [quote/idea] at ~[timestamp] was especially strong.”
Comment #2 (clip idea): “Clip idea: the 20–35s section starting at ~[timestamp] is a perfect ‘hook → payoff’ segment for Shorts/Reels. A quick on-screen headline like ‘[hook headline]’ would crush.”
Log each comment in CRM: platform=YouTube, video URL, comment permalink (if available), status=Commented, next action=DM/email within 2 hours.

C) Afternoon Block (60–90 minutes): Follow-up email/DM (20 messages)
Send to the same 10 creators you commented on (prefer email from YouTube “About”; otherwise IG DM/TikTok DM).

Body: “Hey [Name] — I just watched your episode ‘[Title]’. The segment about [topic] at ~[timestamp] is already a great hook.

Template 2 (coach/offer angle):
“Loved your point on [pain/desire]. Quick win: I can package 10 vertical clips from your latest training in 48 hours so you have 2 weeks of daily posts. Want me to cut 1 sample from [link]?”

Template 3 (lightweight):
“Want a free sample? I’ll cut one 30–60s vertical clip from your latest YouTube and send it today. Website: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3 | Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

D) CRM Logging Rules (non-negotiable)
Statuses: New → Commented → Messaged → Replied → Call Booked → Won/Lost.
Every touch gets: date/time, channel, exact message used, personalization hook, next action date.
Follow-up schedule:
- If no reply: T+2 days: “bump + I can still send the sample.”
- T+5 days: “I cut 3 hook options for your next episode—want them?”
- T+10 days: final: “Close loop—should I stop reaching out?”

Notes to avoid platform issues:
