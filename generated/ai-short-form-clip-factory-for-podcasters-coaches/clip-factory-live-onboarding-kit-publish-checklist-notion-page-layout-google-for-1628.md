## A) Notion page: exact structure (copy/paste headings)
**Page title:** Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery & Revisions)

### 1) Welcome
We turn your long-form video/podcast into ready-to-post vertical clips (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) with strong hooks, accurate captions, clean edits, and platform-safe formatting.

If you need anything, email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to  
You can also reference our site here: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

### 2) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each delivery you’ll receive a folder containing:
- **Final clips (MP4)** — vertical, ready to post
- **Captions file (SRT)** — one per clip
- **B-roll / visual cue sheet (PDF or Google Doc)** — timestamps + suggested overlays/cutaways
- **Posting suggestions (Doc)** — caption first line, CTA, hashtag set, and platform notes

### 3) Quality standards (our internal QA)
We check every clip for:
- **Hook clarity:** the first 1–2 seconds states a clear curiosity gap or outcome
- **Captions accuracy:** spelling, punctuation, correct names/terms; no dropped words
- **Readable captions:** safe-zone placement; high contrast; consistent style
- **Framing:** no cropped faces; headroom appropriate; subject centered for vertical
- **Audio:** consistent loudness, no clipping, reduced noise, clear voice
- **Pacing:** clean jump cuts; no dead air; no awkward mid-word cuts
- **Platform fit:** TikTok/Reels/Shorts friendly length and aspect ratio

### 4) Review flow (how to request revisions)
We provide a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) that supports **timestamp comments**.

**How to leave feedback (required format):**
- Comment at the exact timestamp (e.g., 00:12)
- State the change clearly (e.g., “Caption should be ‘CRM’ not ‘CEM’”)
- If you want a different hook, provide the new hook text (or choose from 2 options we propose)

### 5) Revision window
- You have **24 hours** from delivery to request revisions.
- Revisions requested after the window may be scheduled as a paid update (see section 6).

### 6) What counts as free revision vs paid change
**Free revisions (included):**
- Caption corrections (spelling/accuracy)
- Minor timing adjustments (caption sync, small trim)
- Audio fixes (leveling, noise reduction tweaks)
- Safe-zone fixes (caption repositioning, avoid cropped faces)
- Minor visual fixes (color balance, small zoom/position)

**Paid changes (scope change):**
- New creative direction (different hook angle/topic)
- Adding new footage/assets not provided at intake
- Major restructuring (new story arc, new selections across the long-form)
- Brand/style overhaul after approval
- Revisions requested after 24h window

### 7) Best results: what we need from you (Intake)
Please complete the intake form before we start. It ensures:
- correct platform formatting
- correct tone/brand constraints
- banned words/topics are respected
- accurate handles/links/CTA
