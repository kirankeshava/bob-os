Page title:
“What to Expect (Clip Factory) — Review + Delivery Process”

Page body (paste verbatim):

“Welcome to Clip Factory. This page explains our review process, what you’ll receive, and how revisions work so you can post consistently without surprises.

Contact
Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
Business page: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

Step 1 — Intake (Required)
Before we begin editing, please complete the Client Intake Form (you’ll receive the link in your onboarding email). This ensures captions, spelling, brand terms, and hook style match your preferences.

Step 2 — First Cut Review Link
You’ll receive a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave timestamped comments. Please:
1) Watch each clip once without pausing (overall flow)
2) Re-watch and add timestamp notes for any needed changes
3) Prioritize ‘must-fix’ issues (names, meaning, cropping, offensive words)

Step 3 — Revision Window (24 Hours)
To keep turnaround fast, we include a 24-hour revision window starting when the review link is delivered. If we don’t receive notes within that window, we’ll treat the clips as approved and proceed to final delivery.

What You Receive (Delivery Pack)
For each clip, you’ll receive:
- Final vertical MP4 (ready to post)
- Captions file (.SRT)
- B-roll cue sheet (timestamp + suggestion list)
- Posting suggestions: first-line caption options + hashtag set

Quality Standards We Check Before Delivery
- Captions match the spoken words and preserve meaning
- No cropped faces / awkward framing (face-safe zones)
- Audio is clean and leveled (no clipping, no harsh peaks)
- Hook is clear within the first 1–2 seconds
- Pacing is tight (clean jump cuts, no dead air)
- Export is platform-ready (vertical 1080×1920, readable text)

Revisions Policy (Simple)
Included (free) revisions cover mistakes relative to your intake (spelling, timing drift, meaning errors, cropping issues, or missed brand terms). New creative direction after approval (new hooks, new structure, swapping topics, new clip selection, or additional clips) is treated as a paid change request.

Our goal is simple: you get a consistent pipeline of clips you can post immediately, with a review step that’s fast and painless.”

3) INTERNAL RULE — INTAKE GATE (ADD TO SOP)
Policy (paste into internal SOP):
“No work starts until the client intake form is submitted and reviewed. If a client insists on starting immediately, they must explicitly waive intake in writing (email) and accept that caption spelling/style errors caused by missing brand terms are not eligible for free revisions. Editors must confirm ‘INTAKE COMPLETE’ before clip selection begins.”

Operational note:
- This gate prevents 80% of refund-triggering issues: wrong brand spelling, wrong CTA, prohibited topics, and misaligned caption tone.
