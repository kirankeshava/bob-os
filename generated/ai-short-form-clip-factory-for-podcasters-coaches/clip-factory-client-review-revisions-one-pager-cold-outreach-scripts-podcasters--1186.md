### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **light edits** that keep the clip’s core concept:
- Adjust hook wording (same idea)
- Tighten start/end by a few seconds
- Caption fixes (typos, casing, emphasis)
- On-screen text tweaks
- Swap CTA end card text

### What does NOT count as a revision (new work)
These requests require a new clip slot or a separate add-on:
- “Pick totally different moments” / new highlights
- Major restructuring (new narrative)
- Adding custom motion graphics beyond preset style
- Heavy manual rotoscoping or advanced VFX

### Revision limits + timelines (protects 48-hour SLA)
- **Round 1 (included):** Up to **10 clip-level requests** (one per clip) if you submit within **48 hours of delivery**.
- **Round 2 (included):** Up to **5 additional clip-level requests** if submitted within **24 hours after Round 1 is delivered**.
- Requests submitted after these windows may be scheduled into the next production queue.

### Approval standard (what “done” looks like)
A clip is “approved” if:
- Hook lands in the first ~1.5 seconds
- Captions are readable on mobile (no face obstruction)
- No obvious jump cuts, clipping audio, or broken captions
- CTA and/or handle is present where requested
