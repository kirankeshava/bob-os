REAL ESTATE
Clip RE-01 (Story Hook)
- Hook text (on-screen): “This ONE mistake costs buyers $10k+…”
- EDL (edit beats):
  0.0–2.0s: Start mid-sentence for pattern interrupt; add punch-in 110%.
  2.0–10s: Keep speaker; add 2–3 quick zoom cuts; emphasize “mistake”.
  10–25s: Add b-roll cues (houses, inspection, paperwork) as 0.5–1.0s overlays while audio continues.
  25–40s: Bring back facecam; add “Do this instead:” header.
- B-roll cue list: “home inspection”, “closing documents”, “agent showing home”, “calculator/money”.
- Caption callouts: highlight “inspection”, “contingency”, “closing costs”.

Clip RE-02 (Listicle)

  35–45s: recap text card: “Save this for your next showing.”
- B-roll cues: “mortgage pre-approval”, “neighborhood map”, “budget spreadsheet”.
