## 3) 24-Hour Sample Clip SOP (DFY) — operational checklist

**Goal:** Convert cold prospects into paid DFY packages by delivering 1 high-quality sample vertical clip within 24 hours using a public YouTube episode (no login needed). This also collects feature/pricing signals for the future SaaS, without building anything.

### A) Qualification (5 minutes)
1) Confirm they have at least one public long-form video/podcast on YouTube.
2) Confirm they want more short-form distribution (TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
3) Confirm decision-maker is in the chat/email.

### B) Intake (send this verbatim)
“Send me:
1) YouTube link to the episode
2) Your IG/TikTok handle + preferred CTA (follow, comment keyword, book call)
3) Any caption style preferences (clean vs. bold, emojis/no emojis)
I’ll deliver 1 sample clip in 24 hours. If you like it, I’ll propose a weekly clip package.”

Reply routing: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
Legitimacy link to include when needed: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

### C) Production Steps (target: 60–120 minutes)
1) **Select segment:** find a 20–45s moment with a clear claim, tip, or story (strong first sentence).
2) **Write hook text (optional overlay):** 6–10 words, curiosity + benefit (e.g., “Stop doing this before you post”).
3) **Captions:** accurate, readable, 1–2 lines max; highlight key words.
4) **Format:** 1080x1920, safe margins, loudness normalized; export MP4.
5) **QC:** check spelling, timing, no cut-off text, no awkward mid-word cuts.

### D) Delivery (5 minutes)
1) Upload MP4 to a shareable Drive folder.
2) Include a short “posting note”:
   - Suggested caption (1–2 lines)
   - Suggested title/hook
   - Suggested CTA
3) Ask for the close:
“Want me to do {8/15/30} of these per week? If you tell me your weekly cadence, I’ll recommend the right package.”

### E) Close Criteria (what to listen for)
- They ask “how much / how fast / can you match my style” → propose package immediately.
- They request revisions → do 1 quick revision, then propose package.
- They say “I need consistency” → emphasize weekly delivery + calendar.

### F) Minimal tracking fields (log after sending)
- Prospect name + link
- Niche/segment
- Sample delivered? (Y/N)
- Response (Loved / OK / No)
- Offer sent (Starter/Growth/Pro)
- Next step + date

**Note:** No SaaS build actions. This SOP exists to close DFY revenue and reach the $5k gate before any MVP development.
