## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips + Request Revisions

**Where to view**
You’ll receive a delivery email with either:
- A folder link (Drive/Dropbox) containing final .mp4 files, plus notes, OR
- A review link (if we use a review tool) with time-stamped commenting enabled.

**What “done” looks like (acceptance criteria)**
Each clip should:
1) Be formatted vertical (9:16) and platform-safe (no cut-off captions).
2) Start strong: a clear hook within the first ~1.5 seconds.
3) Have readable captions synced to speech (no long lag or missing key words).
4) Maintain pacing with pattern interrupts approximately every 2–3 seconds (zoom/cut/b-roll cue/graphic emphasis).
5) End with a clean CTA (follow/comment/DM/link prompt) aligned to your goal.

**How to give feedback (fastest method)**
Please reply with **timestamped notes** using this format for each clip:
- **Clip name:** (example: `EP12_C03_IG_v1.mp4`)
- **Timestamp:** (example: `00:04–00:06`)
- **Change type:** (choose one)
  - Caption text tweak
  - Caption styling tweak (size/position/emphasis)
  - Hook tweak (reorder first line / swap first sentence)
  - Cut/pacing tweak (trim dead air / tighten pauses)
  - B-roll cue tweak (change/omit cue)
  - CTA tweak
- **Exact instruction:** (example: “Replace ‘prospecting’ with ‘lead gen’ and bold ‘3 calls/day’.”)

**What counts as a revision (included vs. out-of-scope)**
Included revisions are **light edits** that keep the same core segment:
- Caption wording fixes, minor styling adjustments
- Small trims for pacing (tighten pauses, remove filler)
- Swap the hook line to another sentence **from the same selected segment**
- Adjust b-roll cues (add/remove/replace) without changing the segment

Out-of-scope (would be a new clip / new scope in paid phase):
- Replacing the clip with a completely different moment from the long-form
- New creative direction for all clips after delivery (new style pack)
- Heavy VFX, complex motion graphics, custom illustrated assets

**Revision policy (Week 1 free launch)**
- **One (1) revision round included** for up to **10 clips**.
- Please consolidate all feedback into a single response when possible.
- Turnaround for revision round: **24 hours** after we receive clear timestamped notes.

**If you want us to pick winners**
If you’re unsure which clips will perform best, reply: “**Choose Top 3**” and we’ll nominate the 3 strongest clips for immediate posting, with suggested captions/hashtags.
