## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for working with Clip Factory.** Here’s the fastest way to review your clips and request edits so we can keep everything inside the 48-hour delivery cadence.

**Where to review**
- You’ll receive either (a) a folder link with MP4 files, or (b) a review link where you can comment on each clip.
- If you need us to use a specific tool (Drive/Dropbox/Frame-style comments), tell us during intake.

**What you will receive (typical delivery package)**
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) ready for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions (matching your chosen style preset)
- Hook-first structure (first 1–1.5 seconds)
- Light motion/pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds (where appropriate)
- Optional: a text file with titles + suggested post captions/hashtags (if requested)

**How to review quickly (recommended)**
1) Watch each clip once without pausing (gut-check: “Would I keep watching?”)
2) Rewatch and pause only when something is clearly off
3) Leave **timestamped notes** in this exact format:
   - **Clip name:** (example) EP12_C01_IG_v1
   - **Timestamp:** 00:04–00:07
   - **Change requested:** “Replace caption line 2 with: ‘…’” or “Remove this sentence”
   - **Reason (optional):** “Brand/compliance/tone”

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Included revisions are meant for **polish**, not re-cutting the entire narrative:
- Caption fixes (typos, timing, line breaks)
- Minor trims (remove a sentence, tighten dead air)
- Replace CTA line / on-screen text
- Swap between provided preset styles (Real Estate / Fitness / B2B) if requested
- Small audio leveling tweaks

**What does NOT count as an included revision (scope change)**
These requests are still doable, but they reset timelines and are treated as a new cut/request:
- “Pick entirely different moments from the episode” (new selects)
- “Make all 10 clips longer/shorter with a new structure”
- “Add custom motion graphics packages or heavy manual keyframing”
- “Source and license stock footage/music” (we use your assets or platform-safe options)

**Revision limits + turnaround**
- **One (1) revision round included** if requested within **48 hours** of delivery.
- We turn included revisions around within **24 hours** after we receive clear timestamped notes.
- If you send multiple batches of notes across multiple days, we’ll consolidate into the next available revision window.

**Approval rule (prevents endless back-and-forth)**
If we don’t hear back within **48 hours** after delivery, we’ll mark the batch as approved and archived—so we can keep your next turnaround fast.
