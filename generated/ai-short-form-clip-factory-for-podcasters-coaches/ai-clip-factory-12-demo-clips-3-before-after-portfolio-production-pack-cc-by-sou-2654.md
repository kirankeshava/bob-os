B) EXPORT + EDIT SETTINGS (CONSISTENT LOOK)
- Format: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- FPS: match source (30 if unsure)
- Captions: burned-in, 2-line max, 4–6 words per line, highlight keywords (yellow) and rest white
- Safe margins: keep text inside ~10% padding
- Style: light zoom cuts every 1.0–1.5s, remove silences, add subtle whoosh on jump cuts
- B-roll cues: add 1–2 b-roll inserts per 15 seconds (stock, screenshots, icons, or zoom-in on relevant visual)
