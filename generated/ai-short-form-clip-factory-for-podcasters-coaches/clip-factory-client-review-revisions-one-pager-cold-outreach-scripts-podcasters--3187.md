Legitimacy link (about us / workflow reference): https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

What’s included in delivery
1) 10 vertical clips (9:16), ready to post (TikTok/IG Reels/YouTube Shorts compatible)
2) Burned-in captions (readable safe-zone placement)
3) Hook-forward edits (first 1–1.5s is the “grab”)
4) Light b-roll cues / on-screen emphasis where it improves retention
5) A Clip List with: title, 1-sentence caption idea, hashtags suggestions (optional), and source timestamps

How to review efficiently (10–15 minutes total)
Step 1 — Scan the Clip List first
- Read the titles + the first 2–3 words of each clip. This often tells you if the selection matches your brand.
Step 2 — Watch at 1.25x
- You’re reviewing pacing and clarity. The edit should feel tight, not perfect.
Step 3 — Grade each clip quickly
Use one of these tags per clip:
- APPROVE (good to post)
- MINOR TWEAK (captions/wording/trim 0.5–2 seconds)
- SWAP (replace clip with a different moment — include the timestamp)

What counts as a revision (included)
To protect speed, revisions are limited to “quick-turn” changes that don’t require re-editing the whole video:
Included revisions (fast):
- Caption text corrections (misspelling, names, jargon)
- Caption styling adjustments within the chosen preset (size/position/safe-zone)
- Trim start/end (up to ~3 seconds each side)
- Swap hook line if it’s in the same 20–40s segment
- Mute/remove a phrase for compliance (if possible without harming coherence)
Not included (would be a new order / new batch):
- Full re-storyboarding a clip, major b-roll rebuild, new music direction
- Rebuilding 10 clips because style preference changed after delivery
- Requests that require sourcing licensed assets or paid libraries

Exactly how to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to the delivery email with this structure:
Subject: Revisions — [ClientName] — [Episode/Date]

Clip: [CLIP_ID] (example: CL01)
Tag: MINOR TWEAK / SWAP
Request: (one sentence)
Timecode: (if applicable)
Notes: (optional)

Examples
- Clip: CL03 | Tag: MINOR TWEAK | Request: Fix caption “SaaS” (currently “sass”) | Notes: Keep same style.
- Clip: CL07 | Tag: SWAP | Request: Replace with moment where I say “Here’s the framework…” | Timecode: 12:14–12:45

Turnaround & limits (SLA)
- Standard: 10 clips delivered within 48 hours of receiving (a) source file/link and (b) completed intake.
- Revisions: 1 revision round included if requested within 48 hours of delivery.
- Revision turnaround: typically 24 hours for MINOR TWEAKS; SWAPs may take up to 48 hours depending on source length.

Approval rule (to keep momentum)
If we don’t hear back within 72 hours, we’ll mark the batch as approved and close the project so we can keep turnaround fast for everyone.
