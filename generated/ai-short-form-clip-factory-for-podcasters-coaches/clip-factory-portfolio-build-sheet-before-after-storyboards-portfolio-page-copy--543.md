### Required standards
- **License Type**: CC BY / CC0 / creator-granted permission / public domain. Avoid “Standard YouTube License” unless explicit creator permission is documented.
- **Export Spec**: 1080x1920, 30fps, safe margins, burned-in captions.
- **Caption readability**: 2 lines max, large font, high contrast, highlight 1–3 keywords per sentence.
- **Loudness**: normalize to ~-14 LUFS integrated for social.
- **Branding**: subtle “Clip Factory — SAMPLE” watermark bottom-left.

## Caption Template (consistent look)
- Font: bold sans (e.g., Montserrat/Arial Black)
- Size: large enough to read on phone (approx 7–9% of frame height)
- Stroke/shadow: black 4–6px stroke OR shadow 60–70% opacity
- Keyword highlight: 1–2 words in yellow per line; avoid highlighting every word
- Placement: lower third, above UI-safe area

# Before/After Comparisons — 3 Storyboard Templates

Goal: show the delta between raw excerpt and “Clip Factory” final (captions, pacing, zooms, b-roll cues). These should be short and unmistakable.

## Before/After #1 (Sequential: Raw → Final)
**Total length**: 25–35s
1) 0:00–0:02 — Slate
- On-screen: “BEFORE / RAW EXCERPT”
- Small text: “No captions • No pacing • No framing”

2) 0:02–0:12 — Raw excerpt
- Show unedited 16:9 crop inside 9:16 frame with black bars or blurred background.
- No captions.

3) 0:12–0:14 — Transition
- On-screen: “AFTER / CLIP FACTORY VERSION”

4) 0:14–0:30 — Final excerpt
- Full 9:16 reframed.
- Burned-in captions with keyword highlights.
- 2–3 zoom cuts and 1 b-roll overlay cue.

5) 0:30–0:33 — End card (standard)

## Before/After #2 (Split-Screen)
**Total length**: 20–30s
- Left: “RAW” (tiny label top-left)
- Right: “FINAL” (tiny label top-right)
- Left shows flat framing/no captions; right shows reframed, captions, zooms.
- Add callouts appearing on the FINAL side:
  - “Hook rewrite”
  - “Caption emphasis”
  - “Pacing + jump cuts”
  - “B-roll cue overlay”

## Before/After #3 (Checklist Overlay)
**Total length**: 25–35s
- Start with raw excerpt for 6–8s.
- Then final excerpt begins, and a checklist animates on the side:
  - ✅ Hook tightened
  - ✅ Captions added
  - ✅ Keywords emphasized
  - ✅ Zoom cuts
  - ✅ B-roll cues
  - ✅ Platform formatting
- End card (standard).
