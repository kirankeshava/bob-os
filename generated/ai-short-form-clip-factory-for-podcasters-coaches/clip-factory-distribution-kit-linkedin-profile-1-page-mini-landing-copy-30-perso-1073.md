## 4) 24-hour Sample Clip fulfillment SOP (DFY)
**Goal:** Deliver 1 high-quality vertical sample clip within 24 hours of receiving assets (or set expectation if capacity is limited). Convert to a paid weekly package.

### Inputs required (minimum)
- Link to episode/video (YouTube, Spotify video, Drive, etc.)
- Preferred clip style reference (1–2 links) OR “clean captions, minimal effects”
- Brand basics (optional for sample): colors, font preference, logo
- CTA preference (follow, subscribe, book call) and handle(s)

### Step-by-step process
1) **Confirm scope (5 min):** Reply to client confirming: 1 sample clip, 30–60s, 9:16, burned-in captions. Provide delivery time and ask for any must-include topic.
2) **Find the moment (15–25 min):** Skim for: strong claim, story turning point, numbered framework, contrarian take, or a clean FAQ answer. Target 35–55 seconds.
3) **Edit (30–60 min):**
   - Convert to 9:16 (center/auto reframe)
   - Tighten dead air, remove filler, add jump cuts
   - Add hook text in first 1–2 seconds (big, readable)
   - Burn captions (high contrast, 2–3 lines max, highlight keywords)
4) **QC checklist (10 min):**
   - No words cut off; safe margins for TikTok/Reels UI
   - Captions accurate and timed well
   - Audio normalized; no clipping
   - Hook readable on phone
5) **Deliver (5 min):** Send MP4 + suggested caption copy (1–2 options) + 3 hashtag sets (optional) via email.
