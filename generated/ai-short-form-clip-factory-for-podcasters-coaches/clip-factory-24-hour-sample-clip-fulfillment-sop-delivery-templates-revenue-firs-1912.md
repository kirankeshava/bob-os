## Goal
Deliver a **free sample vertical clip** (9:16) within **24 hours** that feels ready-to-post (hook, captions, pacing), then convert the prospect into a paid DFY package. This SOP is designed to be fast, repeatable, and low-risk (clear scope + limited revisions).

## Scope of the Free Sample (Non-Negotiable)
**Included (1 clip):**
- 1 vertical clip (9:16), **20–45 seconds** (max 60 seconds only if it’s exceptionally strong)
- Burned-in captions (word-highlight optional if easy)
- Simple hook text (top line) + basic CTA at end (optional)
- One (1) music bed if it fits (royalty-free / platform-safe) or none
- Basic punch-ins/zoom cuts, silence removal, pacing

**Not included (sample):**
- Multi-clip batches
- Complex motion graphics packages
- Manual b-roll sourcing that takes >10 minutes (use quick stock only if readily available)
- Custom brand kit buildout (we can approximate with neutral style)

**Revision policy (sample):**
- 1 minor revision max (caption typo, small trim). Major creative changes require paid package.

## Intake (0–15 minutes)
### What to request (send prospect the intake message below)

## Clip Selection (15–35 minutes)
Pick **one** segment using this ranking:
1) Clear contrarian hook or strong promise in first 1–2 seconds
2) A complete idea with a payoff (not a mid-thought)
3) Minimal context required
4) Contains a “teachable moment” or strong emotion

If no timestamps provided:
- Skim transcript/auto captions quickly.
- Look for: “here’s the mistake…”, “the reason you’re not…”, “do this instead…”, “I used to think…”, “the framework is…”

**Target structure:**
- 0–2s: Hook
- 2–35s: Value / story
- Last 2–5s: Light CTA (“Follow for more”, “Want 10 of these/week?”)

---

## Edit Checklist (35 minutes–3.5 hours)
### Format
- Canvas: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Safe margins: keep text away from bottom UI (TikTok/Reels)

### Pacing
- Remove dead air >250ms where possible
- Add punch-in zoom cuts every 2–4 seconds if it improves retention
- Normalize audio to consistent loudness

### Captions
- Burn-in captions with high contrast
- 2 lines max on screen
- Break lines at natural phrase boundaries
- Highlight keywords if easy (optional)

### Hook treatment
- Add **top hook text** (5–9 words) if the audio hook is not instantly clear
Examples:
- “Stop Doing This In Sales Calls”
- “This Is Why Your Podcast Doesn’t Grow”
- “3 Tweaks That Tripled My Leads”

### Light b-roll cues (optional)
If you add b-roll quickly, keep it minimal:
- 1–3 inserts max
- Only if it clarifies the point or adds pattern interrupt

---

## Quality Control (10 minutes)
Before exporting, verify:
- No caption typos / mis-timed captions
- Hook is readable on mobile
- Audio is clear (no clipping)
- No awkward cut mid-word
- Clip ends cleanly (no trailing silence)

## Internal Notes: Risk Controls

- If prospect asks for multiple clips “as a sample,” respond: “Happy to—my sample is 1 clip. If you want 6–12, I can do that as the starter package.”
- Track time spent. If sample regularly exceeds 2.5 hours, tighten scope: require timestamps or reduce b-roll.
