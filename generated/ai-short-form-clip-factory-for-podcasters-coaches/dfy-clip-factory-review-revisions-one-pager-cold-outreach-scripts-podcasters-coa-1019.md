How to review (10–15 minutes)
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) On the second pass, only note “must-fix” issues (see below).
3) Approve or request revisions using the exact format under “Revision Request Format.”

What counts as a “must-fix” (covered by revisions)
- Wrong words / incorrect captions
- Awkward cut that changes meaning
- Missing key word at the start (hook clarity)
- Captions hard to read (placement/contrast)
- Export issues (black bars, wrong size, audio desync)

What does NOT count as revision (to protect speed)
- Rewriting the script, changing the core point of the clip
- Rebuilding clips from entirely different sections than the selected moments
- Changing brand direction after delivery (new fonts/colors/layout)

Revision policy (simple)
- Included: 1 revision round per batch of 10 clips.
- Scope: Up to 2 small edits per clip (caption fixes, micro-trims, CTA text swap).
- Timing: Revision requests submitted within 48 hours of delivery. We return revisions within 24 hours.
