**Thanks for reviewing your clips. Here’s the fastest way to approve (or request edits) so we can keep the 48-hour turnaround.**

### What you’ll receive
- **10 vertical clips** optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + hook-first pacing
- Platform-safe exports (1080x1920)
- A simple delivery note with any context (e.g., “best for IG,” “strong CTA,” “use as ad test”)

### How to review (10–15 minutes total)
1) **Watch each clip once without pausing** (gut check: would you keep watching?)
2) Watch again and confirm:
   - Hook lands in the first ~1–1.5 seconds
   - Captions are readable on mobile (not covering face)
   - No obvious mis-captions on key terms/names
   - The clip ends with a clear takeaway or CTA
3) Mark each clip as:
   - **APPROVE**
   - **REVISION** (provide exact notes using the format below)

### Revision policy (speed-first)
- Includes **1 revision round** for the batch, covering:
  - Caption fixes (names/terms), light trimming, removing a sentence, tightening the hook, swapping an on-screen line, adjusting caption placement.
- Out of scope for the included revision round:
  - Rebuilding the clip concept from scratch, new clip selections, major re-edits across multiple clips, new motion graphics packages.
  - (We can quote these if needed, but we optimize for fast delivery.)
