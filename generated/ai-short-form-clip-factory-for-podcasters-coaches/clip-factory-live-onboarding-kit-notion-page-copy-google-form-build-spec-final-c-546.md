## 2) Our QA standards (what we check before you ever see it)
We run QC so you don’t have to. Every clip is checked for:

### A) Framing & composition
- No cropped faces (forehead/chin safe) and no cut-off hands during key gestures
- Subject stays centered with consistent headroom
- Safe margins for TikTok/Reels UI (avoid placing key text near edges)
- Any zooms/reframes are smooth (no “pumping”)

### B) Captions accuracy & readability
- Captions match spoken words (names/brands checked where possible)
- Punctuation and line breaks for readability (no walls of text)
- Highlighting/keywords used consistently (if style applies)
- No banned words/phrases per your brand rules (if provided)

### C) Audio quality & leveling
- Dialogue is clear and intelligible throughout
- Music does not overpower voice
- Sudden loudness spikes removed; breaths/plosives managed
- Consistent perceived volume across all delivered clips

### D) Editing rhythm (jump cuts & pacing)
- Cuts feel intentional (no awkward mid-word cuts)
- Dead air removed unless used for comedic/impact timing
- B-roll/overlays support the message instead of distracting

### E) Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds)
- Each clip starts strong (question, contrarian claim, bold result, or curiosity)
- No long preamble; we get to the “point” quickly

### F) Platform formatting
- Vertical aspect ratio (9:16)
- Text placement is platform-safe
- Export settings chosen for crisp text and social upload reliability

## 3) Review flow (fast + timestamped)
To keep turnaround tight and avoid messy back-and-forth, we use timestamped feedback.

### Step 1 — You receive a review link
We’ll send a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can comment with timestamps.

### Step 2 — Leave timestamped comments
Please comment like:
- `00:07 — change “$10k” to “$8k”`
- `00:12 — remove the “um”`
- `00:18 — swap caption color to white`

### Step 3 — Revision window
You have **24 hours** from delivery of the review link or Delivery Pack (whichever is provided first) to request revisions covered under the “Free Revisions” policy below. This keeps production moving and protects your deadlines.

## 4) Revisions policy (what’s free vs paid)
We’re happy to revise—this policy keeps scope clear.

### Free revisions (included)
These are fixes/adjustments to match the agreed direction:
- Caption typo fixes, punctuation tweaks, minor wording corrections
- Timing tweaks (caption sync, micro-trims)
- Small audio leveling adjustments
- Reframe fixes (if a face is cropped, or text is unsafe)
- Remove a filler word / awkward pause
- Minor hook tweak (within the same concept/topic)
- Fixes for anything that missed our QA checklist

### Paid changes (out of scope)
These are changes that expand scope or alter the brief:
- New clip selection requests (e.g., “pick different moments”) after delivery
- Major re-edit / restructure (new narrative arc)
- Brand style changes after approval (new font package, new layout system)
- Adding new assets not provided originally (new b-roll sourcing, new motion package)
- Additional platforms/ratios not in the original order
- Any request after the 24-hour revision window

If a request is out-of-scope, we’ll reply with a clear quote and options.
