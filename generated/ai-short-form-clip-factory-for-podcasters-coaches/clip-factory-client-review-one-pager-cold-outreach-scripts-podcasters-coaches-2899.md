## What counts as a revision (and what doesn’t)
**Included (fast):** caption text fixes, small timing trims, replacing the first line, swapping CTA end card text, removing a sentence.

**Not included in the free workflow (slow):** changing the topic of a clip entirely (new clip selection), heavy b-roll sourcing, adding complex motion graphics.

## Approval rules (to keep turnaround fast)
- If you reply **“Approved”**, we consider the batch final.
- If we don’t hear back within **3 days**, we assume the batch is approved so we can keep production moving.
