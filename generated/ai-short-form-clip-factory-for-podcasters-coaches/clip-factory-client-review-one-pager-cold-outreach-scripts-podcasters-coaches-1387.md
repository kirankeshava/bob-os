What you’ll receive
1) 10 ready-to-post vertical clips (9:16)
2) Captions (burned-in) with consistent styling
3) Hook-first edits (first 1–1.5 seconds)
4) Platform exports for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
5) A “Clip Notes” summary (timestamps + why each clip was chosen)

How to review (the fastest way)
Step 1 — Watch each clip once without pausing.
Step 2 — Rewatch only the clips you’re unsure about and note:
- Clip file name (example: EP12_C03_V1)
- Timestamp (example: 00:04–00:09)
- What to change (one sentence)

What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are quick changes that do not require re-cutting the entire clip:
- Caption corrections (typos, punctuation, missed words)
- Small trimming (tighten start/end by a few seconds)
- Swap CTA end-card text (same duration)
- Minor sound leveling
- Replace b-roll cue with another cue (if available)

What does NOT count as a revision (requires a new clip)
To protect the 48-hour turnaround, these are treated as a new clip request:
- Changing the clip’s core topic/angle (new “moment”)
- Rebuilding the clip around a different section of the episode
- Major restructuring across multiple timestamps
- Extensive rebranding changes after style approval (fonts/colors/layout)

How to request revisions (copy/paste this format)

1) Clip: [FILE NAME]
   - Timestamp: [00:00–00:00]
   - Change: [exact request]

2) Clip: [FILE NAME]
   - Timestamp: [00:00–00:00]
   - Change: [exact request]

Revision SLA
- Standard: 1 revision round included per batch (up to 3 clips touched)
- Turnaround: 24 hours from receiving your consolidated notes

Approval tip (to move fast)
If 7–8 clips are good, approve them immediately and send revision notes only for the remaining 2–3 clips. You can post while we revise.
