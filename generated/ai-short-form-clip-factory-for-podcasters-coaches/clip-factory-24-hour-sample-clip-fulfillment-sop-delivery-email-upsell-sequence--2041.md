3) Internal Workflow (24-hour timeline)
T+0–1 hr: Validate source access
- Confirm link works, audio is clear, and there’s at least one “clip-worthy” segment.
- If poor audio: ask for alternate recording or suggest we do “audio cleanup add-on” for paid package later.

T+1–3 hr: Find highlight segment
Selection criteria (choose one segment):
- Self-contained idea in 20–45s
- Clear payoff (tip, framework, hot take, story beat)
- Strong opening line or easily re-ordered to create one
- Minimal names/inside references

T+3–6 hr: Assemble cut
- Start with the most compelling statement (or re-order to create hook)
- Remove pauses, filler, stumbles
- Add jump cuts or subtle zooms to maintain pace
- Keep on-screen speaker framed center; avoid tiny headroom

T+6–10 hr: Captions + formatting
Caption rules:
- 2–3 lines max; 5–8 words per line target
- Highlight 1–2 keywords per sentence (color or weight)
- Use punctuation for rhythm; remove ums
- If a sentence is long: split into two beats

Hook packaging rules:
- First frame should visually match motion; no dead air
- Add a short header line if needed (e.g., “Most coaches do this wrong”)
- End with micro-CTA (e.g., “Full episode: …” or “Comment ‘CLIP’ for the template”)

T+10–20 hr: QC + export
QC checklist:
- Audio not clipping; volume consistent
- Captions match speech; no typos
- Safe margins: captions not cut off on TikTok/Reels UI
- File plays on mobile; <200MB

T+20–24 hr: Deliver + upsell
- Upload to Google Drive folder (structure below)
- Send delivery email + upsell sequence
- Log notes: what they liked, what they requested, SaaS signals (templates, diarization, hooks)
