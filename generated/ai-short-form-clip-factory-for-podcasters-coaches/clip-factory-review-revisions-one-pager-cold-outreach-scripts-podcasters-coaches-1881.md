## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Copy-Paste)

**Clip Factory — Review & Revisions Guide (48-hour delivery)**

To keep turnaround fast and consistent, please review your clips using the checklist below and send revision requests in the requested format.

**Where to review**
- You’ll receive: (1) a folder with MP4s + (2) a “Clip Notes” document containing titles, hook text, and timestamps.
- If we included review links, you can leave comments directly on the link; otherwise reply by email with timestamps.

**What “good” looks like (quick checklist)**
1) **Hook lands immediately**: the first 1–1.5 seconds clearly signals what the clip is about.
2) **Captions are readable**: no cut-off text, high contrast, and correct punctuation.
3) **Pacing feels modern**: pattern interrupts / zooms / b-roll cues every ~2–3 seconds (when applicable).
4) **Audio is clean**: no harsh peaks, no dead air at start/end.
5) **Safe framing**: speaker is centered for vertical; nothing important in the bottom UI zone.
6) **Clear CTA** (if requested): short end-card or on-screen CTA matches your offer and platform.

**Revision policy (to protect speed)**
- **Included:** 1 revision round per batch of 10 clips (minor edits).
- **Minor edits include:** caption corrections, trimming 1–3 seconds, replacing a b-roll cue, adjusting on-screen text, swapping CTA line.
- **Not included as “minor” (may require a new cut):** selecting entirely different moments, rewriting the hook from scratch for multiple clips, changing the style preset after delivery, adding new branded assets after kickoff.

**How to request revisions (copy/paste this format)**
Subject: Revisions — [ClientName] — [EpisodeName] — Batch [#]

For each clip needing changes:
- Clip filename: (example: ACME_E12_C03_TikTok_v1.mp4)
- Timestamp(s): (00:03–00:06)
- Change requested: (example: “Replace ‘$10k’ with ‘ten thousand’ in captions; shorten pause after first sentence.”)
- Priority: (Must-have / Nice-to-have)

**Revision turnaround**
- We ship revisions **within 24 hours** after receiving all revision notes in the format above.
