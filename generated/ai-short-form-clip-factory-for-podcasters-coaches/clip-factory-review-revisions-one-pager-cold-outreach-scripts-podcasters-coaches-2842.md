1) CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW & REQUEST REVISIONS (FAST)
Title: “How to Review Your Clips & Request Revisions (2–5 minutes)”

Goal
Help you approve clips quickly so you can post consistently without getting stuck in long feedback cycles.

What you’ll receive
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) formatted for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions (readable on mobile)
- Hook-first pacing (first 1–1.5 seconds)
- A short note doc listing each clip’s core takeaway + any b-roll cues used

How to review (fast)
Step 1 — Watch on your phone
These are mobile-first. Please watch in-app or on your phone if possible.

Step 2 — Use the “2-pass” method
Pass A (speed): Watch all clips quickly and mark:
- KEEP (good to post)
- TWEAK (small changes)
- SWAP (wrong moment; pick another)

Pass B (precision): For TWEAK/SWAP clips, note the exact timestamp + what to change.

What counts as a “revision” (covered)
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, minor wording)
- Caption styling tweaks (size up/down, higher/lower, emphasis words)
- Trim adjustments (tighten start/end, remove a pause)
- Swap hook line (use next sentence as the hook)
- Audio leveling within the clip

What does NOT count as a revision (new scope)
- Rebuilding a clip from a totally different section not previously selected
- Adding custom motion graphics packages beyond the preset style
- Heavy manual rotoscoping or custom b-roll sourcing beyond simple cues
(If you need these, we can do it after the free week as an add-on.)

Revision limits & turnaround
