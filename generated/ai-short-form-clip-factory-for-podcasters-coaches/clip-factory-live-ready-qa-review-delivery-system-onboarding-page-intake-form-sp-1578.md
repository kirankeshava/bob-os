## 1) What you’ll get (Delivery Pack)
For each batch we deliver a **Delivery Pack** containing:
1. **Final MP4 vertical clips** (ready-to-post)
2. **Caption files (.SRT)** per clip
3. **B-roll cue sheet** (timestamps + suggestions)
4. **Posting suggestions** (first-line caption + hashtags + CTA ideas)

**Typical formats:** 9:16 for TikTok / Reels / Shorts (safe-zone compliant). If you want 1:1 or 16:9 versions, request during intake.

## 2) Our quality bar (QA checklist summary)
We run a final QC pass before delivery to reduce revisions and avoid platform issues.

### Visual + framing
- No cropped faces (eyes/forehead not cut off; subject kept in center-safe area)
- Subtitles and on-screen text stay within TikTok/Reels safe zones
- Clean jump cuts (no awkward half-words; natural breath points)
- B-roll overlays don’t hide key facial expressions or important text

### Captions
- Captions match spoken audio (names/brands spelled correctly when provided)
- Good punctuation and line breaks for fast reading
- Highlight/keyword emphasis used sparingly (readability > gimmicks)

### Audio
- Speech is clear and consistent across cuts
- Loudness is leveled (no sudden spikes, no quiet sections)
- Background noise reduced where possible without artifacts

### Hook + clarity
- First 1–2 seconds includes a clear hook (question, tension, bold claim, or outcome)
- Clip has a complete idea (no “what are they talking about?” endings)
- On-screen text supports the main point (not filler)

### Export settings (baseline)
- Resolution: **1080×1920**
- Codec: **H.264** (MP4)
- Frame rate: matches source where possible
- Bitrate: high enough to avoid caption shimmer/blocking

## 3) Review process (timestamp comments)
We send a review link (choose one):
- **Frame.io** (best for timestamp comments)
- **Vimeo** review link
- **Unlisted YouTube** (timestamp notes in email)

**How to request revisions:**
- Leave timestamped comments (e.g., “00:07 replace caption wording”)
- Or email: a numbered list with timestamps

## 4) Revision window (24 hours)
To keep schedules tight, the **revision window is 24 hours** from delivery.
- If we don’t receive revision notes within 24 hours, the delivery is considered approved.
- If you need more time, reply within the window and request an extension.

## 5) What counts as free revision vs. paid change
### Free revisions (included)
- Typos or misheard words in captions
- Fixing safe-zone issues (text too low/high; cropped faces)
- Audio leveling fixes (if clearly inconsistent)
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, jump cut smoothing)
- Small on-screen text corrections (names/handles you provided)

### Paid changes (scope change)
- New clip selections / new angles after delivery (“different 30 seconds instead”)
- New brand direction after work starts (new style, fonts, colors, templates)
- New CTA strategy or offer pivot requiring re-cuts across multiple clips
- Major content rewrite of captions not driven by accuracy (voice/tone overhaul)
- Additional aspect ratios or platform variants not requested during intake

**If a request is paid:** we’ll quote first and only proceed after you confirm.

# Internal: Go/No-Go QC Gate (Run Before Any Delivery Email)
**Goal:** prevent refund triggers (missing files, wrong formatting, captions wrong, broken links).

## A) File-level QC (each clip)
1. Play from start to finish at 1.0x (no skipping)
2. Hook present in first 1–2s; no dead air
3. Face not cropped; subtitles in safe zone
4. Captions: scan for obvious mishears, names, profanity, banned words (from intake)
5. Audio: check intro, mid, end for leveling consistency
6. Export: confirm 1080×1920 MP4 H.264; file plays on phone

## B) Delivery Pack completeness
- MP4(s): present and named correctly
- SRT(s): included for each MP4, filename matches
- B-roll cue sheet: included (Google Doc/PDF/Markdown)
- Posting suggestions: included (Doc)

## C) Link + permissions check (critical)
- Drive folder: “Anyone with the link can view” (or client email whitelisted)
- Review link works in incognito
- All assets open without requesting access
