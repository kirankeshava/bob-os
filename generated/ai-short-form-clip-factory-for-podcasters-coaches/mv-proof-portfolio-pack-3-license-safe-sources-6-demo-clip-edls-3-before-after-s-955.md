C) 6 DEMO CLIP EDLs (2 per niche)
==================================================

1) REAL ESTATE — Storytelling Hook (RE-01)
- Goal: emotional hook + practical takeaway
- Hook on-screen (0.0–1.2s): “This is why buyers keep losing right now…”
- Caption style: bold keyword highlights: “losing”, “right now”, “one fix”
- EDL:
  • 0.0–0.8s: cold open on the strongest sentence (trim intro)
  • 0.8–3.5s: punch-in 110% + add 2 quick b-roll flashes (house exterior, offer sheet)
  • 3.5–12s: keep A-roll; add jump cuts removing pauses
  • 12–22s: overlay 3-word takeaway captions: “Speed wins”, “Clean terms”, “Clear proof”
  • 22–end: micro-summary + CTA line
- B-roll cues: listing photos, “Under Contract” stamp, simple chart arrow up

2) REAL ESTATE — Listicle (RE-02)
- Goal: fast tips, highly saveable
- Hook (0.0–1.0s): “3 things to do before you tour a home.”
- Structure:
  • 0–2s: hook + big “3 THINGS” text
  • 2–16s: Tip #1 with b-roll cue
  • 16–32s: Tip #2
  • 32–48s: Tip #3
  • 48–end: “Want weekly clips like this?” + website/email
- B-roll cues: calendar, checklist, mortgage pre-approval doc

3) FITNESS — Contrarian Take (FIT-01)
- Goal: pattern interrupt + authority
- Hook: “Stop doing this ‘fat loss’ habit. It backfires.”
- EDL:
  • 0–1.5s: contrarian claim, large red “STOP” overlay
  • 1.5–8s: explain the mistake; zoom cut on key word
  • 8–20s: replace with better approach (2 bullet overlays)
  • 20–end: quick recap + CTA line
- B-roll cues: scale, late-night snacking, gym clip, meal prep

4) FITNESS — Story Hook (FIT-02)
- Goal: relatable mini-story -> lesson
- Hook: “I trained 5 days a week and still stalled… until I fixed this.”
- EDL:
  • 0–2s: hook + quick montage b-roll
  • 2–14s: the ‘problem’ moment (caption: “I was doing everything right…”) 
  • 14–28s: the fix (caption: “Progress = stimulus + recovery”) 
  • 28–end: ‘do this today’ action step + CTA
- B-roll cues: sleep, steps counter, protein meal, rest day

5) B2B — Listicle (B2B-01)
- Goal: practical ops/marketing tips
- Hook: “If your pipeline is slow, fix these 3 things first.”
- EDL:
  • 0–2s: hook + animated “3 FIXES”
  • 2–15s: Fix #1 (tight, one sentence)
  • 15–30s: Fix #2
  • 30–45s: Fix #3
  • 45–end: “Need 20 clips/month from your podcast?” + website/email
- B-roll cues: CRM screen blur, calendar blocks, email subject lines

6) B2B — Testimonial-style (B2B-02)
- Goal: simulate client result proof (even if it’s from the source speaker)
- Hook: “We changed one message and replies doubled.”
- EDL:
  • 0–1.2s: hook
  • 1.2–10s: ‘before’ framing subtitle: “Before: lots of calls, few yeses”
  • 10–26s: ‘after’ framing subtitle: “After: clearer offer, higher intent”
  • 26–end: takeaway + CTA line
- B-roll cues: inbox replies, simple ‘before/after’ bar chart, meeting booked screen
