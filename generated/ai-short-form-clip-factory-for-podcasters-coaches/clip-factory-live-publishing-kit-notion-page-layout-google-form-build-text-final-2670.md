### A) What we deliver (Delivery Pack)
You’ll receive a delivery folder with:
1) **Final MP4 clips (vertical)** — ready to upload
2) **Caption files (.SRT)** — one per clip
3) **B-roll Cue Sheet (Google Doc or PDF)** — timecoded suggestions and on-screen notes
4) **Posting Suggestions** — first-line caption options + hashtags + recommended platform

---

### B) QA standards (what we check before you ever see it)
We run QC on every clip before review:
- **Safe zones / framing:** faces not cropped; key text kept inside TikTok/Reels safe areas
- **Caption accuracy:** corrected spelling, names, brand terms; punctuation for readability
- **Caption timing:** aligned to speech; no long gaps; no rapid unreadable bursts
- **Hook clarity:** first 1–2 seconds communicates the “reason to watch”
- **Jump cuts:** clean transitions; no awkward mid-word cuts; pacing matches the platform
- **Audio leveling:** consistent loudness; no clipping; noise reduction without artifacts
- **Platform formatting:** correct resolution, aspect ratio, bitrate, and file naming

---

### C) Review flow (timestamp comments)
**How you review:**
- You’ll get a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / Unlisted YouTube) where you can leave **timestamped comments**.
- Please place feedback directly on the moment it applies (example: “00:07 — remove filler word” or “00:12 — caption typo: ‘their’ not ‘there’”).

**Revision window:**
- **24 hours** from delivery of the review link to submit revision notes.

---

### D) Revisions (what’s included vs scope change)
**Free revisions (included):**
- Caption typos/mistiming
- Minor trimming for pacing
- Audio level tweaks
- Safe-zone/framing fixes
- Small on-screen text adjustments

**Scope changes (not included):**
- Changing the clip concept/topic selection after delivery
- Re-cutting the clip into a different angle/narrative
- Replacing clips with different source sections not originally requested
- New branding kit or new styling direction after work has started

If a request is a scope change, we’ll confirm it in writing and propose an updated delivery plan.
