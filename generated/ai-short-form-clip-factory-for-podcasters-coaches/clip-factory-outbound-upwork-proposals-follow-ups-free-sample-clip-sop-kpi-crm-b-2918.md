=== D) FREE SAMPLE CLIP FULFILLMENT SOP (FAST) ===
Goal: deliver a credible sample in <45 minutes.

1) Intake (2 minutes)
Ask for: (a) YouTube link, (b) target platform (Reels/TikTok/Shorts), (c) preferred caption style (bold vs clean), (d) any “do not mention” topics.

2) Pick the clip moment (5 minutes)
Choose 30–60 sec with: clear claim, emotional spike, contrarian take, or quick framework. Note start/end timestamp.

3) Create hook + caption plan (5 minutes)
Write 2 hook options:
• Hook A: “Most people think X, but…”
• Hook B: “If you’re doing X, stop—do this instead…”
Add 1 CTA line for end-screen: “Follow for more” / “Link in bio” / “Comment ‘CLIP’.”

4) Produce (25–30 minutes)
Export vertical 1080x1920.
Captions: 3–8 words per line, highlight 1–2 keywords.
Safe zones: keep face/text centered; avoid bottom 250px and top 200px.

5) Deliver (2 minutes)
Send link + ask 1 question:
“Want captions more bold (Hormozi-style) or cleaner/minimal? If you like this, I can batch 10 clips from the same episode in 48 hours.”
