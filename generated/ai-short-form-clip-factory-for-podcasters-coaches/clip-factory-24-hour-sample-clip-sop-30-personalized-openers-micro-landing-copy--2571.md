Purpose: deliver 1 high-quality vertical short within 24 hours of receiving a link, to convert prospects into a paid clip package.

**Promise to prospect:** “Send me one episode link and I’ll deliver 1 ready-to-post 9:16 clip (hook + captions + export) in 24 hours. If you like it, we can turn it into 12–30 clips/month.”

### 0) Required inputs (ask for these immediately)
Send from: **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to**
Ask prospect to reply with:
1) Long-form source: YouTube link / podcast video link / Drive link
2) Target platform: TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts (pick 1)
3) Audience: “Who is this for?” (1 sentence)
4) CTA: follow, newsletter, book call, etc.
5) Brand constraints: words to avoid, any disclaimers
6) Preferred caption style: (A) clean, (B) bold w/ emphasis, (C) karaoke highlight

### 1) Select the clip candidate (15 minutes)
Criteria:
- Strong first 2 seconds (contrarian claim, surprising stat, clear promise)
- Self-contained payoff within 25–45 seconds
- Minimal context required
Process:
- Skim/transcript scan for: “The real reason…”, “Most people get this wrong…”, “Here’s the framework…”, numbers, steps
- Pick **one** segment with a clear takeaway
- Record start/end timestamps

### 2) Edit spec (deliverable definition)
Output requirements:
- Format: 9:16 vertical (1080x1920), H.264 MP4, 30fps if possible
- Duration: 20–45s (max 60s)
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast, safe margins, line breaks every ~3–6 words
- Hook text: on-screen headline for first 1–2 seconds
- Basic cleanup: normalize audio, remove dead air >0.4s if it helps pacing
- Optional: subtle zoom/cut every ~2–3 seconds if talking head

### 3) Captions workflow (fast)
- Generate transcript (any tool you already use).
- Manually correct names/numbers (top priority).
- Apply emphasis styling: highlight 1–2 keywords per sentence max.

### 4) Export + QC checklist (10 minutes)
QC checklist:
- Captions not covering face (or centered if face moves)
- No typos in first 2 lines
- Audio not peaking / not too quiet
- Hook visible immediately
- First frame not blank
