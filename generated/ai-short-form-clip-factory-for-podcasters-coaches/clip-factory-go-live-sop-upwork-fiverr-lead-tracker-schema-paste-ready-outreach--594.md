## 2) Lead Tracker Schema (CSV columns) + Qualification Rules
Create a Google Sheet/CSV with columns:
1. Lead_Name
2. Brand_or_Show
3. Niche
4. Platform_Found (YouTube/Spotify/Apple/LinkedIn/IG)
5. Profile_URL
6. Email
7. IG_Handle
8. LinkedIn_URL
9. Est_Audience_Size
10. Longform_Frequency (weekly/biweekly/monthly)
11. Clip_Velocity (none/some/consistent)
12. Pain_Signal (no shorts, inconsistent, low views, no captions, etc.)
13. Offer_Fit (Starter/Growth/Pro)
14. Channel_First_Touch (Email/IG/LI/Upwork/Fiverr)
15. Date_Sent
16. Message_Variant (A/B/C)
17. Reply_Status (No reply / Interested / Not now / Referred)
18. Next_Followup_Date
19. Call_Booked (Y/N)
20. Checkout_Rail (Upwork/Fiverr)
21. Order_Value
22. Notes

**Qualification rules (fast):**
- Must have 20+ min longform episodes or coaching videos.
- Must publish at least 2x/month.
- Must have either: (a) no shorts at all, or (b) inconsistent shorts, or (c) shorts without captions/hooks.
- If they already post daily shorts with pro editing, deprioritize.
