INTERNAL START-WORK GATE (ADD TO SOP)
Rule: No editing starts until the intake form is submitted and reviewed.
Exception: Client may waive intake only if they explicitly confirm in writing (email/message) that: (1) captions style is "default", (2) no banned words, (3) no special brand spellings, (4) platform target is TikTok/Reels standard.
Enforcement: If intake is missing, send the Onboarding email + intake link and pause the order clock. Log the pause reason in the order tracker as “Waiting on Intake — Client”.

This gate prevents the top refund causes: wrong tone, wrong captions style, missed brand terms, and platform mismatch.
