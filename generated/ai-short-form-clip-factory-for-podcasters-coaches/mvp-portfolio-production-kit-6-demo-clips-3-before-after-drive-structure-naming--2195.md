D) Before/After comparisons (3 videos total):
Make 3 comparison videos—one per niche—using the exact same excerpt as a finished demo.
Format option 1 (fastest): Sequential
- 0:00–0:02 Title card: “BEFORE (raw excerpt)”
- 0:02–0:12 raw excerpt (no captions, no zooms)
- 0:12–0:14 Title card: “AFTER (Clip Factory edit)”
- 0:14–end final clip (captions, zoom cuts, b-roll cues)
Format option 2 (higher signal): Split-screen
- Left = raw, Right = final; add labels “RAW” / “FINAL”
- Keep under 25–35s.

E) Editing spec (consistent look across all samples):
Canvas/export:
- 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps, H.264 mp4, 10–16 Mbps.

Captions (burned in):
- Style: bold, high-contrast, 2 lines max.
- Placement: lower third, centered.
- Highlight: 1–3 keywords per line in yellow.
- Timing: phrase-level (not word-by-word) unless tool makes word-by-word easy.

Hook treatment:
- First 1.0s: big hook text (6–10 words) + quick zoom-in.
- Cut any greetings; start mid-thought.

Cuts/zooms:
- Add a cut or punch-in every 1.5–3.0 seconds.
- Use subtle motion blur if available (optional).

B-roll cues (simple, not heavy):
- Use on-screen callouts like: “[B-ROLL: house listing photos]” or insert 1–2 relevant overlays.
- If no b-roll available, use stock-like stills or icons (keep it license-safe).

Branding:
- Tiny corner tag: “Sample — AI Clip Factory” (top-left), 60–70% opacity.
