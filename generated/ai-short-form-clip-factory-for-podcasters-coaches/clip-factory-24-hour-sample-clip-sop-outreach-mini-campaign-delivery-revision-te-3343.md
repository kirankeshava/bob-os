A) Offer Definition (what the prospect gets)

C) Fulfillment SOP (Operator steps)
Step 1 — Log job (5 min)
- Create a row in CRM/job tracker:
  - Prospect name, handle, email, link, received time, due time (24h), niche, CTA
- Save source link and any brand notes.

Step 2 — Find 3 candidate moments (20–30 min)
Selection criteria:
- Clear “problem → insight → payoff” in <45s
- Strong first sentence (or can be trimmed to start with the punchline)
- Minimal context required
- Emotional edge: surprising, contrarian, tactical, or story beat

Pick the best 1 moment + keep 2 backups.

Step 3 — Edit assembly (30–60 min)
- 9:16 crop (speaker centered; cut to active speaker)
- Remove dead air; tighten pauses
- Add subtle punch-in/punch-out cuts at emphasis points
- Audio cleanup: normalize loudness, reduce harshness if needed

Step 4 — Captions + hook formatting (30–45 min)
- Captions burned in, 2–3 lines max, large readable font
- Highlight 1–3 keywords per line (color or bold style)
- Ensure captions avoid covering mouth area when possible
- Add an opening title (optional): 4–8 words that matches the hook
- Add end card text (optional): “Follow for more” / “Comment ‘CLIP’ for the guide”

Step 5 — QC checklist (10 min)
- Spelling and punctuation correct
- Captions synced (no lag)
- No black bars, correct 1080x1920 export
- Hook lands within first 2 seconds
- Volume consistent; no clipping
- CTA visible in last 1–2 seconds

Step 6 — Export + package (5–10 min)
Deliverables:
- Final.mp4
- Suggested post caption (1–3 sentences)
- 5 hashtags (niche + format + outcome)

Step 7 — Delivery email (template below)
- Send within 24 hours.
- Ask one closing question to convert to a paid package.
