## 1) Platform compliance rules (simple)
### Upwork
- **Before a contract exists:** do **not** push off-platform calls or file sharing. Keep everything inside Upwork chat and Upwork Project Catalog / proposals.
- You may reference a website as **portfolio/legitimacy** if Upwork allows it in profile/portfolio fields. Avoid language like “pay off Upwork” or “book outside.”
- **After a contract exists:** you can share intake links, Drive uploads, Calendly scheduling (still best practice: keep key decisions and deliverables posted in Upwork).

### Fiverr
- **Before an order exists:** avoid directing to external checkout/booking. Use Fiverr order page/packages.
- You can usually include a portfolio link, but keep wording informational (“portfolio / examples”) not a hard CTA.
- **After an order exists:** you can share the intake form and asset upload instructions.
