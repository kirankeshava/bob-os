## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

**What you’ll receive**
- **10 vertical clips** (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Each clip includes **captions**, a **strong hook**, and **light pattern interrupts** (zoom/cut/b-roll cue moments)
- Delivery includes either: (a) download links to MP4s, and/or (b) a shared folder with all assets

**How to review (fastest method)**
1) Watch all clips once without pausing.
2) On the second pass, flag only what materially impacts performance: hook, clarity, pacing, captions, framing.
3) Approve clips in batches (e.g., “Clips 1–7 approved”).

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Included revisions are changes that keep the clip concept the same:
- Caption typo fixes, small timing fixes
- Adjust caption position/size for readability
- Trim 0.3–2.0 seconds for pacing
- Swap a b-roll cue or remove a distracting moment
- Replace the first line/hook text *without changing the clip’s topic*

**What is NOT a revision (scope change)**
These requests require a new clip selection/edit:
- Choosing a completely different moment from the episode
- Rewriting the clip angle to target a different audience
- Adding heavy motion graphics or complex custom animation
