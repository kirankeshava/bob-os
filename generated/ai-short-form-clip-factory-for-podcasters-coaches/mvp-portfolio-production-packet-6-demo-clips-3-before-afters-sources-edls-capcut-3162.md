## 3) CapCut (free) editing SOP (fast, consistent)
**Project settings**
1. New project → set canvas **9:16 (1080x1920)**.
2. Import longform → cut the chosen 30–60s excerpt.

**Core style (applies to all clips)**
- Jump cuts: remove pauses/breaths; keep pace.
- Zoom: 105–120% punch-in on emphasis lines (1–2 per clip).
- Captions: auto-captions → bold white text, black shadow/outline; max 2 lines.
- Safe margins: keep captions above bottom UI area (TikTok safe zone).
- Add tiny footer tag: “SAMPLE — AI Clip Factory” (bottom-left).

**B-roll / overlays**
- Use free stock clips (Pexels/Pixabay) or CapCut library.
- Insert as 0.3–0.8s cutaways or picture-in-picture.

**Audio**
- Normalize voice to consistent loudness; reduce background noise if needed.
- Optional low music bed at -25 to -30 dB.

**Export**
- 1080x1920, 30fps, H.264, high bitrate.
- Filename convention (important):
  - `RE-D1_openhouse-mistake_45s_v1.mp4`
  - `RE-D2_stop-staging_40s_v1.mp4`
  - `RE-BA_before-after_openhouse_25s_v1.mp4`
  - similarly for FT and B2B.
