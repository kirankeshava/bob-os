Title: How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Hit the 48-Hour Delivery)

This project runs on speed. The fastest way to get exactly what you want—without slowing delivery—is to give feedback in a consistent format.

Where to leave feedback
1) Frame.io (preferred): Leave comments directly on the clip timeline.
2) Google Drive: Reply to the delivery email with Clip IDs + timestamps.
3) Email: Works fine—use the template below.

What we need from you (the 4-part feedback format)
For every change request, please include:
1) Clip ID: (example: CLIP-03)
2) Timestamp: (example: 00:01.2–00:03.0)
3) Change Type: choose one
   - HOOK: first line / first 1–2 seconds
   - CAPTIONS: wording, punctuation, highlight color, emoji removal
   - CUT: tighten, remove a sentence, jump cut
   - B-ROLL: add/replace/remove visual overlay
   - ON-SCREEN TEXT: headline, lower third, labels
   - AUDIO: volume, noise, censor a word
   - END CARD / CTA: final line, handle, offer, URL
4) Priority:
   - MUST (required for approval)
   - NICE (only if time allows within SLA)

Examples (good feedback)
- CLIP-02 | 00:00.0–00:01.4 | HOOK | MUST: Replace opening line with “3 mistakes first-time buyers make…”
- CLIP-07 | 00:05.2–00:06.6 | CAPTIONS | NICE: Highlight the words “30 days” in the accent color.
- CLIP-10 | 00:12.0–00:14.0 | CUT | MUST: Remove the sentence about “my old agency” (too off-topic).

What counts as a Revision (included)
Revisions are small, targeted edits to clips we already produced, such as:
- Swapping the hook line while keeping the same highlight
- Minor caption wording/typos/highlighting changes
- Tightening a section (remove 1–2 lines)
- Changing b-roll overlay for an existing cue
- Adjusting end-card CTA text/handle

What counts as New Scope (may affect timeline)
These requests are considered new work and can impact the 48-hour SLA:
- “Make 10 totally new clips” or replace multiple clips with new picks
- New creative direction after delivery (different style, fonts, color system)
- Adding new footage not provided during intake
- Major restructuring (new narrative, new topic angle across many clips)

Timing: when feedback is due
To stay inside the 48-hour window:
- Feedback deadline: within 12 hours of receiving the first delivery.
- If we don’t get feedback by then, we will assume “approved” and close the batch to protect the SLA.
