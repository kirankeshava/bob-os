Contact email for all feedback: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

What you’ll receive
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + safe margins
- Hook-forward edits (hook in first ~1.5s) and pattern interrupts
- A delivery note with suggested posting order + 1-line posting prompts (optional)

How to review (fastest method)
1) Watch each clip once without pausing to judge “Would I stop scrolling?”
2) Re-watch and note ONLY the moments that feel off: start point, pacing, caption errors, or a sentence you want removed.
3) Send revision requests in the format below (copy/paste).

What counts as a revision (included)
- Trimming start/end, removing a line, tightening pauses
- Caption text corrections (typos, speaker attribution if present)
- Minor caption styling tweaks within the chosen preset (size/position within safe margins)
- Swapping CTA end-card text (same design template)

What counts as a new edit (not included in revision rounds)
- Changing the clip concept (new highlight) after delivery
- Full re-style to a different niche preset after work is completed
- Adding new b-roll research beyond simple cues
- Rebuilding more than ~30% of clips due to late strategy changes

SLA + revisions policy (client-friendly)
- Delivery: 10 clips within 48 hours after (a) source files are received and (b) style preset + goals are confirmed.
- Included revisions: 1 revision round covering up to 10 clips (batch feedback preferred).
- Turnaround for revisions: 24 hours after we receive clearly formatted notes.
- If feedback is sent as multiple small messages over time, the revision clock starts when the final consolidated note arrives.

Best practices to get clips you’ll actually post
- Pick ONE primary goal per batch: followers OR leads OR authority.
- Tell us 3 “red flag” topics/phrases to avoid (compliance/brand safety).
- If you have a preferred caption style, send 1 reference Reel/TikTok link.
