## 1) DFY Fulfillment SOP (24–48h turnaround)
**Goal:** Deliver a small batch of ready-to-post vertical clips that look native to TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts and feel on-brand, while capturing product signals (hooks, templates, caption style) for the later SaaS.

### Inputs to request (copy/paste to client)
Send your assets here: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
1) Long-form link (YouTube/Spotify video/Drive link) + timestamps if you have them
2) Your target audience (1 sentence) + your offer/CTA (1 sentence)
3) Brand notes: colors, font preference, emoji/no-emoji, caption style (clean vs energetic)
4) Examples you like (2–3 links)
5) Must-avoid topics/words (optional)

### Tooling (free/owned-first)
- **Sourcing:** YouTube download (where permitted) or client-provided file
- **Transcription:** Descript trial / YouTube transcript / built-in platform transcript; fallback: manual + selective captions
- **Editing:** CapCut Desktop (free) or DaVinci Resolve (free)
- **Captions:** CapCut auto-captions + manual corrections (critical)
- **B-roll cues:** Simple on-screen text cues + stock suggestions (no paid stock in Week 1)

### Production steps
1) **Find 8–12 candidate moments** (30–90s each) using:
   - strong claim → proof → takeaway
   - story beats (conflict, surprise, lesson)
   - numbered frameworks (3 steps, 5 mistakes)
2) **Select 5–10 final clips** (per package) with tight hooks in first 1.5 seconds.
3) **Vertical framing (9:16):** speaker centered, eyes near top third; crop punches during emphasis.
4) **Captions:** 2 lines max, high contrast, correct jargon/names; highlight 1–3 keywords per sentence.
5) **Hook overlay (optional):** 3–7 words, benefit-driven.
6) **CTA tail (optional):** “Follow for X”, “Comment ‘CLIP’ for template”, or client’s offer.
7) **Export formats:**
   - 1080x1920, H.264, 30fps, 12–20 Mbps
   - filename convention: `Client_Topic_Clip01_45s.mp4`
8) **Delivery:** Google Drive folder + a simple index doc.

### QC checklist (do not skip)
- Captions accurate (names/brands), no repeated words
- First frame hook readable (no tiny text)
- No dead air > 0.3s at start
- Audio normalized, no clipping
- Safe margins for TikTok/Reels UI (keep text mid-screen)
- Each clip has a clear point + ends cleanly

### Delivery package structure
- `/Exports` (final mp4)
- `/Captions` (SRT + plain text)
- `Clip Index` (Google Doc): hook, timestamp source, suggested caption, suggested hashtags, posting notes
