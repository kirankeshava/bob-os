B) 20-MIN DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (WITH BUILT-IN CLOSE)
Purpose: diagnose workflow, quantify value, and offer a paid DFY pilot. Also capture SaaS requirements (without building).

1) Open (2 min)
“Thanks {{Name}}. Quick agenda: understand your current content workflow, what ‘consistent posting’ would look like, then I’ll suggest a pilot. If it makes sense, we can start this week.”

2) Current workflow + pain (5 min)
• “How often do you publish long-form? Where?”
• “How many short clips do you post per week today?”
• “What’s the bottleneck: time, editing skill, captions, ideation, consistency?”
• “What’s your current process from episode → clip? (tools, people, turnaround)”
• “What would a win look like 30 days from now?”

3) Content specifics (4 min)
• “Any non-negotiables: fonts, colors, safe areas, speaker framing?”
• “Do you want burned-in captions always?”
• “Do you prefer: (A) ‘talking head’ only, (B) b-roll overlays, (C) meme-style cuts?”
• “Any compliance constraints? (medical/finance claims)”

4) Value + willingness-to-pay (4 min)
• “If you got 10 high-quality clips per episode, what would that be worth vs your time?”
• “If we handled this end-to-end weekly, what budget range is realistic?”
   Offer anchors: “Some clients are at $99–$299/mo for DIY tooling, but for done-for-you weekly delivery it’s usually higher. Where do you land?”
• “What would make you cancel after week one?”

5) Offer a DFY pilot (3 min)
“Based on what you said, here’s what I recommend: a 7-day pilot.
• You send 1 episode link/file.
• We deliver {{X}} vertical clips (9:16), each with: hook options + burned-in captions + title + export settings for Reels/TikTok/Shorts.
• Turnaround: {{48–72h}}.

To start, we do a deposit of ${{Deposit}}. If you like the pilot, we roll into a weekly cadence.”
