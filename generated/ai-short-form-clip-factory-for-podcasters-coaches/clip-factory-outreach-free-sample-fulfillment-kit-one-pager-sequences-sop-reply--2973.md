========================
C) FREE SAMPLE CLIP — 15-MIN FULFILLMENT SOP (Week 1)
========================
Goal: Deliver 1 impressive vertical clip in <24 hours, minimal back-and-forth, optimized to convert to DFY package.

Step 0 — Intake (2 minutes)
Ask for:
- Link to source video (YouTube/Drive)
- Where they post (TikTok/IG/Shorts)
- Their CTA (follow/comment/book call/lead magnet)
- Any “do not include” topics
- Optional: 1 example clip style they like

Step 1 — Pick the moment (5 minutes)
Choose a segment that:
- Starts with tension/curiosity (“Most people get this wrong…”) or a strong opinion
- Is self-contained (no heavy context)
- Has a clear payoff in <35 seconds
- Maps to the CTA (for coaches: problem → insight → next step)

Step 2 — Produce the clip (6 minutes)
- Crop to 9:16, subject centered, safe zones respected
- Add burned-in captions (2 lines max, high contrast)
- Add on-screen headline (5–9 words)
- Add 2–3 hook options as text notes in the delivery message
- Add b-roll cue notes (not necessarily implemented): “insert screenshot of X”, “cut to tweet”, “zoom at keyword”

Step 3 — Deliver (2 minutes)
Send:
- MP4 file (or link) + recommended caption text + hashtags (optional)
- 2–3 alternative hook lines
- Suggested posting note: “post as Reel + pin comment: {CTA}”

Step 4 — Soft upsell (30 seconds)
Message:
“If you like this direction, I can batch 10–20 clips per episode and deliver 2–3x/week. Want me to propose a weekly clip cadence based on how often you publish?”
