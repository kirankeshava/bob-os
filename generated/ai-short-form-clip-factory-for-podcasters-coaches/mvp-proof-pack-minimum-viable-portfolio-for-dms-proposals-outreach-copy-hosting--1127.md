Goal: create the smallest proof set that increases reply/close rates during outbound, without waiting for the full 12-demo build. This MVP is designed to be embedded and shared within 24–48 hours.

MVP deliverables (minimum viable proof):
A) 6 vertical demo clips (1080x1920, 25–45s each)
- Real Estate: 2 clips (Story hook + Listicle)
- Fitness: 2 clips (Contrarian take + Listicle)
- B2B: 2 clips (Story hook + Testimonial-style “results narrative”)
Why these: They demonstrate the core buying triggers—hooks, pacing, captions, structure, and platform formatting—without needing the full 4 styles per niche.

B) 2 before/after comparisons (15–25s each)
- Before/After #1: raw excerpt (no captions, no zooms) → final clip (burned captions, punchy hook line, zoom cuts, b-roll cues)
- Before/After #2: raw excerpt → final clip with heavier structure (headline, 3 beats, CTA)
Format options:
1) Sequential: 3–5s “BEFORE” then 10–15s “AFTER”
2) Split-screen: left BEFORE, right AFTER with labels at top
On-screen labels (exact text):
- “BEFORE (Raw Podcast Excerpt)”
- “AFTER (Ready-to-Post Short)” 

MVP selection rules (license + safety):
- Use Creative Commons (CC BY) or CC0 sources where reuse is permitted, or content with explicit creator permission.
- Keep the demo clips clearly labeled as “SAMPLE/DEMO – editing showcase” in descriptions.
- Store license proof alongside the source URL (screenshot or page note) in the same Drive folder.

Export specs (consistent across all MVP clips):
- 9:16 1080x1920, 30fps
- Loudness target: ~-14 LUFS integrated (platform-safe)
- Burned-in captions with emphasized keywords (1–3 words highlighted per sentence)
- Safe margins: keep text inside ~10% padding; avoid bottom 15% where UI overlays appear
- Add subtle progress bar or section headers only if it improves clarity (optional)
