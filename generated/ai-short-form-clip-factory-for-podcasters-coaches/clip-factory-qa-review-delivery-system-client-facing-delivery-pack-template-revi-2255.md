## 2) QA standards (what we check before you see it)
We run a final QA pass on every clip:

### Framing / safe zones
- Subject’s face is **not cropped** (forehead/chin safe)
- Composition respects **TikTok/Reels UI safe zones** (top/bottom overlays)
- Text/captions never cover critical visual elements
- If dynamic reframing is used, it remains stable (no distracting drift)

### Captions
- **Accurate transcription** (names/brands corrected)
- Clean readability (line breaks, punctuation, no caption “walls”)
- Highlighting/emphasis (if used) supports comprehension, not noise
- Profanity/banned words handled per client rules

### Audio
- Consistent loudness across clips (no big jumps)
- Speech is clear (de-noised where needed; no metallic artifacts)
- Music/SFX never overpowers voice
- No clipping/distortion

### Editing rhythm
- Strong opening 1–2 seconds (hook clarity)
- Jump cuts are clean (no awkward half-words)
- Visual variety: b-roll cues or on-screen emphasis when helpful
- No dead air unless intentionally used for comedic/impact timing

### Export / formatting
- Vertical 9:16 deliverable
- High quality encode suitable for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- No watermarks
- Filename conventions are consistent (easy to post)

## 3) Review flow (fast + timestamped)
We provide a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) or a Drive folder with preview files.

### How to request changes
- Leave **timestamped comments** (e.g., “00:07 change caption wording”) 
- Group comments by clip name (if multiple clips)

### Revision window (important)
- You have **24 hours** from delivery to request revisions.
- After 24 hours, the delivery is considered accepted (this protects turnaround speed for all clients).

## 4) Revision policy (what’s free vs. out of scope)
### Free revisions (within 24 hours)
Included when the request is consistent with the original brief:
- Caption fixes (typos, misheard words)
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, cut timing)
- Minor framing fixes (face/headroom adjustments)
- Audio leveling tweaks
- Remove/add a small overlay element (if aligned with original style)

### Not included (scope change / would be paid later)
These are considered **new direction** and are not included as free revisions:
- Changing the clip selection strategy (different moments) after delivery
- Major style change (new fonts, full redesign, different editing vibe)
- Adding new assets not provided (logos, brand packages) after edit started
- Rebuilding clips due to new constraints introduced late (new banned words, new CTA, new offers)
- Recutting from different source episodes/videos

If a request is out-of-scope, we’ll reply with the fastest option (e.g., “We can do that as a change request next batch”).

Before editing starts, we’ll ask you to fill out a short intake form so clips match your brand and platform goals.
