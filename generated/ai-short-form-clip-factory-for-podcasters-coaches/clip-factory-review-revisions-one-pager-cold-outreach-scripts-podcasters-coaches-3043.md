### Step-by-step review (10–15 minutes total)
1) **Watch each clip once without pausing** (simulate a real viewer).
2) On the second pass, quickly confirm:
   - **Hook lands in first ~1.5s** (first line is curiosity, benefit, or contrarian).
   - Captions are readable on mobile (not covered by UI; high contrast).
   - No obvious cut errors (jump cuts are fine; broken sentences are not).
   - Names/brands spelled correctly.
3) Pick **your top 3 clips** and tell us which platform they’re for (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts). We’ll prioritize those if any changes are requested.

### What counts as a revision (included)
To protect speed and consistency, revisions are meant for **accuracy + clarity**, not full creative reworks.

**Included (per clip):**
- Fix typos, miscaptioned words, name corrections
- Tighten the first line/hook text overlay (keep same clip)
- Swap CTA end card text (same template)
- Small trim adjustments (remove 1–3 seconds, tighten pauses)
- Adjust caption placement to avoid UI overlap

**Not included (requires new clip / new scope):**
- Rebuilding clip concept from scratch
- Changing style preset across all clips after delivery
- Adding extensive b-roll sourcing beyond cues
- Pulling entirely different segments (new highlights)
