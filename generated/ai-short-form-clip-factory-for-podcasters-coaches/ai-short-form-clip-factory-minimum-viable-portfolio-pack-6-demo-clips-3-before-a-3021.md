- FPS: 30
- Loudness target: ~ -14 LUFS integrated (or “Auto normalize” in CapCut)
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast, 2-line max, keywords highlighted
- Visual rhythm: punch-in every 1.5–2.5 seconds, plus occasional jump cuts
- B-roll: optional overlays (stock-style cues only; do not embed copyrighted footage)

### REAL ESTATE
#### RE-01 — Storytelling Hook (30–45s)
Goal: stop-scroll with an “I made this mistake” narrative.
1) Open (0–2s): hard cut mid-sentence + big on-screen hook
   - On-screen: “I almost overpaid by $27,000…”
   - Edit: punch-in + whoosh
2) Setup (2–10s): explain the scenario
   - Captions: “The listing looked perfect… until I saw ONE thing.”
3) Reveal (10–28s): the ‘one thing’ (inspection, comps, HOA, rate buydown, etc.)
   - Captions with highlights: “COMPS”, “HOA”, “INSPECTION”
   - B-roll cue: quick overlay of “comps grid” / “inspection checklist” (generic)
4) Takeaway (28–40s): rule of thumb
   - Captions: “Rule: never decide until you check ___.”
