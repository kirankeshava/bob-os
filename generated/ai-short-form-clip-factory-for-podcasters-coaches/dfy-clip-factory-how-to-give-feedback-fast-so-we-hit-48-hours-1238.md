Why this matters
Our promise is 10 vertical clips delivered within 48 hours. The #1 thing that breaks the timeline is slow or unclear feedback. Use this guide so revisions are fast and we keep your momentum.

Where to leave feedback
1) Use the review links we provide (Frame.io or Google Drive).
2) Leave comments directly on the timeline/timecode when possible.
3) If you must email, include timestamps in this format: [CLIP NAME] 00:12–00:18.

Your feedback deadline (to protect the 48-hour SLA)
- Round 1 feedback window: within 12 hours of receiving the first batch.
- Round 2 feedback window (if needed): within 6 hours of receiving revisions.
If feedback arrives after these windows, delivery may slide (we’ll tell you immediately).

How to write “good” feedback (fastest to implement)
Use this 3-part format:
A) What to change (caption / hook / cut / b-roll / pacing / end card)
B) Where to change it (timestamp)
C) What you want instead (exact words, or an example)

Examples (copy/paste)
- Captions: “Clip 03 00:05–00:08: change caption to ‘Most agents price this wrong—here’s the fix.’ Keep it 2 lines max.”
- Hook: “Clip 07: replace first 1.5s with the moment at 12:44 where I say ‘Stop doing this…’ Start there.”
- Pacing: “Clip 02: remove the pause at 00:14–00:16 and tighten to keep energy.”
- B-roll: “Clip 05 00:09: insert simple b-roll cue: ‘[B-ROLL: chart going up]’ or a relevant stock shot.”
- Compliance: “Clip 09: remove ‘guaranteed’ and replace with ‘typically’.”

What NOT to do (creates delays)
- “Make it better” without specifics.
- Multiple conflicting opinions in separate messages.
- Requests that change the clip’s core angle after selection (that becomes a new clip).

What’s included vs. out of scope (to keep turnaround predictable)
Included (within standard revisions):
- Caption wording tweaks, minor timing adjustments, replacing a b-roll cue, swapping the end card CTA, small trims.
Out of scope (requires a new clip or new batch):
- Changing the main topic/angle, requesting a different moment from the episode after approval, full re-style to a new brand kit mid-batch.

Revision policy (speed-first)
- 1 revision round included per clip (bundled feedback preferred).
- Additional rounds are possible but may affect SLA; we’ll confirm timing before proceeding.

Best practice: consolidate feedback
Send one consolidated feedback message per batch with:
- Clip name
- Timestamp
- Exact change
This is the fastest path to final delivery.

If you’re unsure, reply with ONE of these
- “Approve all.”
- “Approve except Clip 04 and Clip 08—see notes.”
