1) Before We Start (Intake Required)
To prevent off-brand captions, wrong framing, and missed CTAs, we require a completed intake form before editing begins. If you want us to proceed without intake, you can waive it in writing (note: this reduces accuracy guarantees).

2) Our QA Standards (What We Check)
Every clip is checked for:
- Safe framing: no cropped faces, no cut-off text, and platform-safe vertical framing.
- Captions: correct wording (or approved edits), correct spelling for names/brands, clean line breaks, and on-screen readability.
- Audio: consistent loudness, no clipping, reduced background noise, and balanced voice levels.
- Hook clarity: first 1–2 seconds communicate the point; no slow lead-ins.
- Jump cuts & pacing: removed dead air, no distracting stutters, and natural flow.
- Export settings: vertical format, social-ready bitrate, and clean playback.

3) Review Flow (Fast + Timestamped)
You will receive a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave timestamp comments.
How to request changes:
- Leave comments directly on the video at the exact timecode (e.g., 00:12 “Change caption wording”, 00:27 “Tighten pause”).
- Consolidate feedback when possible to speed up revisions.

Revision window:
- You have 24 hours from the time we send the review link to request included revisions.

4) What You Receive (Delivery Pack)
Final delivery includes:
- Final MP4 clip(s) ready to post
- SRT caption file(s)
- B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggested overlays)
- Posting suggestions: first-line caption + hashtags (platform-specific)

5) Revision Rules (What’s Free vs Paid)
Included (free) revisions within the 24-hour window:
- Caption typos, punctuation, timing adjustments
- Minor reframing to prevent cropped faces or improve composition
- Audio level fixes (peaks, dips) and light cleanup
- Hook tweak(s) that keep the same meaning and clip concept

Paid changes (out of scope) typically include:
- Changing the clip concept, pulling different sections, or “make it a totally different clip”
- New versions for different audiences/offers beyond what was agreed
- Major rewriting that changes meaning or adds new messaging
- Additional clips beyond the purchased quantity

If you’re unsure, email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with your request and we’ll confirm whether it’s included or quoted.
