CLIENT-FACING ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW & REQUEST REVISIONS
====================================================
Title: How to Review Your Clips (and Request Revisions Fast)

Thanks for trusting Clip Factory. To keep the 48-hour turnaround, please review using the steps below and send revisions in the requested format.

Where you’ll receive clips
- You’ll receive either: (a) a folder link (Drive/Dropbox) OR (b) an unlisted review link.
- Each clip filename includes a clip number and short title so it’s easy to reference.

What “done” means (our quality bar)
Each clip ships ready-to-post, including:
1) Hook in the first 1–1.5 seconds
2) Burned-in captions (high contrast, mobile safe)
3) 9:16 framing, platform-safe margins
4) Light pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds (zoom/cut/b-roll cue)
5) Clear CTA (follow/comment/DM or episode CTA)

How to review quickly (recommended workflow)
1) Watch all clips once at 1.25x speed.
2) Star your top 5 immediately.
3) On the second pass, only note fixes that materially impact performance:
   - Wrong words in captions
   - Cropping issues / face cut off
   - Missed hook (opening line doesn’t grab)
   - Brand/style mismatch (colors, font, caption placement)

Revision policy (to protect speed)
- Included: 1 revision round per clip set (up to 10 clips) if requested within 48 hours of delivery.
- What counts as a revision: caption corrections, trimming, reframing, hook swap from provided selects, adjusting caption style to chosen preset.
- Not included (treated as new work): requesting totally new moments not in the delivered set, changing niche preset after delivery, or re-editing the episode with a different strategy.
