CREATIVE SPECS — 6 MVP DEMO CLIPS (2 PER NICHE)
Global formatting:
- 9:16, 1080×1920, 30fps
- Loudness target: ~-14 LUFS integrated (platform-safe)
- Captions: bold, high-contrast, 2-line max, keywords highlighted
- Safe margins: keep text within central 80% width and above bottom UI
- Pace: remove filler >200ms, add punch-ins every 1.5–3.0 seconds
- Hook window: first 1.0–1.5 seconds must contain the premise

REAL ESTATE
RE_S1 (Storytelling hook):
- Hook template: “I almost lost $___ on a deal because of ONE line in the contract…”
- Structure (35–55s): Hook → 1-sentence context → the mistake → the fix → takeaway
- Captions: emphasize $ amount + “ONE line” + “fix”
- Visual: moderate punch-ins, show relevant on-screen graphic cue: “Contract line → check this”

RE_C1 (Contrarian take):
- Hook template: “Stop telling buyers to do THIS — it’s costing them offers.”
- Structure (25–45s): Hook → common advice → why it fails → what to do instead
- Captions: highlight “Stop” + “costing offers” + “do this instead”
- B-roll cues: offer sheet / ‘multiple offers’ headline screenshot cue (generic)

FITNESS
FIT_S1 (Storytelling hook):
- Hook template: “I trained 6 days a week and got WORSE results… until I changed this.”
- Structure (30–50s): Hook → what you did → what went wrong → change → result
- Captions: emphasize “got WORSE” + “changed this”
- Visual: punch-ins, quick cutaways to generic gym b-roll cue (no branding)

FIT_C1 (Contrarian take):
- Hook template: “If you’re doing endless cardio for fat loss, you’re making it harder.”
- Structure (25–45s): Hook → myth → mechanism → alternative plan (2 steps)
- Captions: emphasize “making it harder” + “2 steps”
- On-screen: simple 2-bullet overlay for the alternative

B2B / CONSULTING
B2B_S1 (Storytelling hook):
- Hook template: “We were stuck at $___/mo… then we changed ONE thing in our offer.”
- Structure (30–55s): Hook → old offer → change → why it worked → takeaway
- Captions: emphasize revenue figure + “ONE thing”
- B-roll cues: simple overlay ‘Before/After offer’ (text-only)

B2B_C1 (Contrarian take):
- Hook template: “Cold outreach isn’t dead — your MESSAGE is.”
- Structure (25–45s): Hook → why people fail → 1 concrete fix → example line
- Captions: emphasize “MESSAGE” + include the example line as on-screen text
