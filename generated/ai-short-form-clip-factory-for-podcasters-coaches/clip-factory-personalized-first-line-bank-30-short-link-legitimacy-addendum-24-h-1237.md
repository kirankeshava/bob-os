Goal: deliver 1–2 finished vertical clips within 24 hours of receiving the episode link + brand basics, to de-risk purchase and close DFY packages.

1) Intake (Target: <10 minutes)
Ask for:
- Episode link (YouTube, Drive, podcast video) + timestamp highlights (if any)
- Primary platform (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts)
- Brand handles + preferred CTA (follow, book call, download lead magnet)
- Caption style preference (word-by-word, clean sentences, karaoke highlight)
- Do’s/don’ts (no profanity, avoid certain topics)
Send them to: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

2) Clip Candidate Selection (Target: 20–30 minutes)
- Skim for 3 candidate moments:
  a) Contrarian statement / bold claim
  b) Step-by-step framework
  c) Story with before/after or lesson
- Choose 1 primary (25–45s) and optional 1 secondary (10–25s).
- Write 3 hook options (first on-screen line) that match their voice.

3) Edit Spec (Target: 60–90 minutes)
- Format: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps.
- Crop: face-centered; avoid cutting off chin/forehead; keep safe margins.
- Captions: burned in; 2 lines max; keyword emphasis (color or weight).
- Add b-roll cues (if not inserting b-roll): bracketed notes like [B-ROLL: calendar], [B-ROLL: testimonial], [SCREEN: 3 steps].
- Add subtle jump cuts to remove dead air.

4) Export + Delivery (Target: 10 minutes)
Deliverables:
- MP4 (final)
- Plain-text caption copy (with hashtags optional)
- Title/hook used + 2 alternatives
Send via email with subject:
