C) INTERNAL GATING RULES — “No-Intake, No-Edit”
Rule:
“Do not start editing until the client submits the Intake Form OR explicitly waives intake in writing (email/DM screenshot saved to the project folder).”

Why:
“Most refunds come from mismatch in hook/caption style, banned words, brand terms, or platform expectations. Intake prevents this.”

Exception path (rush):
“If a client wants rush turnaround and refuses intake, reply:
‘We can start immediately if you confirm in writing: (1) preferred platforms, (2) banned words, (3) caption style (clean/high-energy/minimal), (4) examples (optional). Without this, revisions may be paid.’
Save confirmation to project folder.”

Internal checklist before sending any review link:
- Intake submitted and reviewed
- Banned words list highlighted for editor
- Brand terms list pasted into caption QA notes
- Platform targets confirmed (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Delivery deadline written in task title

This system is designed to minimize avoidable revisions, protect reputation, and reduce refund risk while keeping turnaround fast.
