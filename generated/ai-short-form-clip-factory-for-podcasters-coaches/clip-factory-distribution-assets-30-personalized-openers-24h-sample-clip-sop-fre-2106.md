## 4) 24-Hour “Sample Clip Offer” Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

**Goal:** deliver 1–3 sample clips within 24 hours of receiving a usable link, to convert the free trial into a paid DFY package.

### Step 0 — Confirm intake (5 minutes)
Reply template:
“Got it—thanks. I’ll pull highlights and send 3 clips within 24–48h. If you have any must-include moments (timestamps) or a preferred CTA (follow/DM/book), send them here. Site for reference: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3”

### Step 1 — Pick clip candidates (20–30 minutes)
- Watch at 1.5–2x speed.
- Mark 6–10 candidate moments.
- Filter to 3 that have: (a) clear hook in first 1–2 seconds, (b) single idea, (c) payoff within 45 seconds.

### Step 2 — Write hook + caption plan (15 minutes)
For each clip:
- Hook A (curiosity/contrarian)
- Hook B (pain/benefit)
- Suggested on-screen title (3–7 words)
- End CTA (follow for X / DM ‘Y’ / book link)

### Step 3 — Edit specs (per clip)
- Format: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Keep pace tight (remove dead air >0.3–0.5s)
- Caption style: 2 lines max, high contrast, readable line breaks
- Emphasis: highlight 1–3 keywords per sentence (caps or color)
- Safe zones: keep text away from bottom UI region

### Step 4 — Add b-roll cues (simple, fast)
- Insert notes in a text file or in the delivery email:
  - “Add b-roll: stock ‘busy calendar’ at 0:06–0:08”
  - “Zoom-in/punch at 0:12 on ‘most people get this wrong’”

### Step 5 — QC checklist (5 minutes)
- Captions match audio
- No cut-off words
- First frame is visually clean
- Audio level consistent
- Clip length 20–45s unless intentionally longer
