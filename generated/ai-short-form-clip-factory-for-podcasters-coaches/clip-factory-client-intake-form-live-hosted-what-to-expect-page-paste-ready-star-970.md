“We run a QC pass on every clip:
• Captions match the spoken words (no drift)
• No cropped faces; safe-zone aware framing
• Audio leveled for speech; no harsh peaks/clipping
• Clean jump cuts; no distracting flashes
• Hook clarity in the first 1–2 seconds
• Platform-friendly export settings (vertical 9:16, readable captions)”

“Free revisions (our mistake vs your intake/specs):

• Cropping/safe-zone issues (cut-off faces/text)

• Wrong platform formatting/export settings

Paid changes (new direction):
• New clip selection (different moments/timestamps)
• New style after approval (different hook approach, caption style overhaul)
• Additional variants beyond what was ordered
• Major rewrites not tied to an error
