====================
A) GOOGLE FORM SETUP
====================
Form title:
“Clip Factory — Client Intake (Required Before Editing Starts)”

Form description (paste):
“Thanks for choosing Clip Factory. This quick intake ensures your clips match your brand, captions style, and platform requirements. We begin editing after this form is submitted (unless you explicitly waive this in writing). If you have questions, email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to. Business site: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3”

Collect email addresses: ON
Allow response editing: OFF (keep clean specs)
Required questions: mark all “Required” except where noted.

Sections + Questions (recommended structure):

SECTION 1 — Contact + Project Basics
1) Your name (Short answer)
2) Best email for delivery + review links (Short answer)
3) Brand / channel name (Short answer)
4) Links to your TikTok / IG / YouTube Shorts (Short answer)
5) Primary platform(s) for these clips (Checkboxes): TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, Other
6) Target audience in one sentence (Short answer)

SECTION 2 — Source Content
7) Source link(s) to long-form video/podcast (Paragraph)
   - Instruction text: “Paste YouTube link, Drive link, or upload link. If multiple episodes, list each on a new line.”
8) Episode title(s) (Short answer or Paragraph)
9) Time zones / deadline constraints (Short answer)

SECTION 3 — Clip Direction
10) Desired # of clips from this episode (Multiple choice): 3, 5, 8, 10, Other
11) Preferred clip length range (Multiple choice): 15–25s, 25–40s, 40–60s, 60–90s
12) Energy/editing style (Multiple choice): Clean & minimal, Medium (jump cuts + emphasis), High-energy (fast cuts + more captions)
13) Hook style (Checkboxes): Bold claim, Question, Counterintuitive, Story tease, Statistic, Other
14) Example creators you like (Optional) (Paragraph)

SECTION 4 — Captions + Brand Rules
15) Caption style (Multiple choice):
   - Word-for-word (verbatim)
   - Cleaned up (remove filler, keep meaning)
   - Condensed (tight summary captions)
16) Profanity handling (Multiple choice): Leave as-is, Bleep/mute, Replace words (e.g., “f—”)
17) Words/phrases to always use (Required) (Paragraph)
18) Words/phrases to avoid (Required) (Paragraph)
19) Mandatory disclaimer text (Optional) (Paragraph)

SECTION 5 — Visual Preferences
20) On-screen layout preference (Multiple choice):
   - Speaker full-screen (9:16)
   - Split-screen (speaker + b-roll)
   - Auto (you decide)
21) Face cropping sensitivity (Multiple choice):
   - Must keep full face at all times
   - Keep face mostly safe; minor tight shots OK
   - No preference
22) Brand colors (Optional) (Short answer)
23) Brand font (Optional) (Short answer)
24) Logo/watermark (Multiple choice): Yes (I will provide), No

SECTION 6 — CTA + Posting
25) Primary call-to-action (Multiple choice): Follow, Comment, DM, Book a call, Visit link in bio, Other
26) Link to include (Optional) (Short answer)
27) Hashtags: Provide your own vs. “suggest a set” (Multiple choice)

SECTION 7 — Approval + Start Rule
28) Confirmation checkbox (Required):
“I confirm these specs are correct. I understand editing starts after this form is submitted unless I waive intake in writing.”

====================
B) INTERNAL GATING RULE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
====================
Rule name: “Intake-First Production Gate”

Policy text (paste into internal SOP):
“No Clip Factory edit begins until the client submits the Intake Form OR explicitly waives the intake in writing (email reply is acceptable). If intake is missing, we do not open timelines, generate captions, or export drafts. This prevents brand-term mistakes, caption-style mismatches, and avoidable revision cycles.”

Exception handling:
- If the client needs same-day turnaround and cannot complete intake, send a waiver email: “Reply ‘I waive the intake form and approve you to proceed with best judgment’.” Save the reply in the project folder.
