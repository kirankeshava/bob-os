**Thanks — your clips are ready.** To keep the 48-hour turnaround fast and painless, please review and request revisions using the simple format below.

  2) A “CLIP LIST” document (titles + timestamps + suggested captions/hashtags)

**What to check (in this order)**

3) **Pacing:** Any moment that feels slow or confusing?
4) **Safe framing:** Face not cropped awkwardly; key visuals inside safe margins.
5) **CTA:** Clear and matches your desired outcome (follow, comment, book call, etc.).

Reply to this email (or paste into your message) with:

- **Timecode:** (example: 00:07–00:11)
- **Change requested:** (example: “Remove the word ‘uh’ and tighten the pause”)

- **What’s out of scope:** Changing the entire topic/angle, requesting new clips outside the original 10, major re-edit from scratch, new branding pack after approval.
