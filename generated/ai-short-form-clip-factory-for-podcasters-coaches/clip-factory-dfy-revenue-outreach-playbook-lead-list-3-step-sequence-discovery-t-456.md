## 15-minute discovery-to-close call script (DFY sale + SaaS validation)
**0:00–2:00 Context**
- “What’s your current content cadence (long-form + short-form)?”
- “What’s the bottleneck—time, editing skill, or ideas?”

**2:00–6:00 Outcomes + constraints**
- “If this worked perfectly, how many clips/week would you post?”
- “Any brand constraints (font/colors, pacing, profanity, compliance)?”
- “Where do clips fail today—hooks, captions, or pacing?”

**6:00–10:00 Pitch DFY**
- “We take one long video and deliver 8–15 vertical clips with hooks + burned-in captions + platform formatting in 48–72h. AI-assisted, human QC.”
- “We can start with a test batch from your latest episode.”

**10:00–12:00 Close next step**
- “If I send 3 timestamped clip ideas today, can you reply with the episode link and we’ll start?”
- Confirm deliverables: number of clips, turnaround, revision policy.

**12:00–15:00 SaaS validation (only after DFY fit)**
- “If there were a self-serve tool that did link→highlights→captions export, what would you pay monthly?” (anchor: $99 / $199 / $299)
- “Which features matter enough to pay more?”
  - Speaker detection/diarization
  - Brand template packs
  - Hook rewriting in your voice
  - Auto b-roll cue suggestions
  - Team collaboration/client approvals
- “What would make you cancel?”

Log their answers verbatim.
