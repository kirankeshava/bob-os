B) 6 DEMO CLIPS (2 per niche) — READY-TO-EDIT SCRIPTS/EDL
==============================
Export settings (all clips): 1080x1920, 30fps, 8–20 Mbps, burned-in captions, safe margins, bold keywords highlighted.
Caption style: 2 lines max, 4–7 words/line, key words in yellow, rest white. Add subtle background shadow.

REAL ESTATE CLIP #1 (Story Hook)
- Length: 35–45s
- Hook (0–2s, on-screen): “If you bought a home in 2021… watch this.”
- Edit beats:
  1) Start mid-sentence on a surprising stat (hard cut).
  2) Add 120% zoom punch-in on the key number.
  3) Add b-roll cue overlay text (not actual b-roll required for demo): “B-ROLL: house keys / open house / mortgage rate chart”.
- On-screen caption script (split into beats):
  • “If you bought in 2021… watch this.”
  • “Your payment didn’t change.”
  • “But your options did.”
  • “Here’s what I’d do in 30 days…”
- CTA (last 2s): “Want 12 clips/month like this? Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

REAL ESTATE CLIP #2 (Listicle)
- Length: 25–35s
- Hook: “3 things buyers forget to ask (and it costs them).”
- Structure: count-up with on-screen counter (1/3, 2/3, 3/3)
- Caption script:
  • “3 questions buyers forget.”
  • “1) What’s the true monthly cost?”
  • “2) What repairs were ‘cosmetic’?”
  • “3) What’s the neighborhood plan?”
  • “Save this before you tour.”
- B-roll cues overlay: “B-ROLL: inspection photo / HOA letter / street view”.

FITNESS CLIP #1 (Contrarian Take)
- Length: 30–45s
- Hook: “Stop doing more cardio if fat loss is your goal.”
- Edit beats: fast jump cuts every 1–2 sentences; add ‘record scratch’ SFX at hook; punch-in on “STOP”.
- Caption script:
  • “Stop doing MORE cardio.”
  • “If fat loss is the goal…”
  • “You need: protein + steps + strength.”
  • “Cardio is the tool, not the plan.”
- B-roll cues: “B-ROLL: treadmill close-up / meal prep / dumbbells”.
- CTA: “I turn long podcasts into daily Reels. Proof: (site link in bio)”.

FITNESS CLIP #2 (Testimonial framing)
- Length: 30–40s
- Hook: “My client stalled for 6 weeks—here’s the 1 change.”
- Caption script:
  • “Stalled 6 weeks.”
  • “We changed ONE thing:”
  • “We tracked sleep like training.”
  • “Then weight started moving.”
- Edit beats: add on-screen “CLIENT WIN” badge; blur any names if present; add b-roll cue text.
