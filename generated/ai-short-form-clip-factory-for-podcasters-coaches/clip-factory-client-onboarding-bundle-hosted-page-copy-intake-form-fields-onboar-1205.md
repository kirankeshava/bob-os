## What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each order, you’ll receive a **Delivery Pack** containing:
1) **Final vertical MP4 clips** (ready to post)
2) **Caption file for each clip** (.SRT)
3) **B-roll / on-screen cue sheet** (what to overlay, where, and why)
4) **Posting suggestions** per clip (first-line caption + hashtags + recommended platform)

## Our workflow (fast + clear)
### Step 1 — Intake (required)
Before we start editing, please complete the **Clip Preferences Intake Form** (link provided in your onboarding email). This prevents rework and ensures we match your brand voice.

**Work begins after intake is submitted.** If you need us to start immediately without intake, please reply explicitly confirming you’re **waiving intake** and we will use best judgment.

### Step 2 — First cut + review link
We’ll deliver a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube). You can leave timestamp comments (e.g., “0:12 change caption wording” / “0:18 tighten pause”).

### Step 3 — Revisions (24-hour window)
You have **24 hours from the time we send the review link** to submit revision notes.
- Please consolidate notes into **one message** when possible.
- Timestamp comments are preferred (fastest turnaround).

### Step 4 — Final delivery
We deliver the final **Delivery Pack** with:
- Final MP4s
- SRT captions
- Cue sheet
- Posting suggestions

## Quality standards we check before delivery
Every clip is QC’d for:
- **No cropped faces** (safe-zone framing for Reels/TikTok)
- **Readable captions** (timed accurately, no major word drift)
- **Audio leveling** (voice clarity, no clipping/peaks)
- **Hook clarity** in the first 1–2 seconds
- **Platform formatting** (vertical export settings, bitrate, resolution)

## What counts as a free revision vs. paid change
### Free revisions (included, within the 24-hour window)
- Caption typo fixes or minor rewording that keeps the same meaning
- Timing tweaks (caption timing, cut tightening)
- Minor framing adjustments (recenter, safe-zone fixes)
- Basic audio tweaks (leveling, noise reduction adjustments)
- Small b-roll/cue swaps that keep the same concept

### Paid changes (scope change)
- New creative direction (“make them all funnier / more aggressive / different style” after seeing cuts)
- Re-cutting from different sections not included in the original selected moments
- Adding new brand packages, complex motion graphics, or new templates not agreed
- Multiple rounds of revisions beyond the included window
- Revisions requested **after the 24-hour review window**

If something is our mistake (e.g., missed instruction from your intake), we fix it immediately.
