## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips & Request Revisions

**Who this is for:** Customers receiving their first batch of vertical clips.

**Where to reach us:**
- Website (legitimacy link): https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### What you’ll receive
For each batch, you’ll receive:
1) **10 vertical clips** (9:16) ready for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
2) **Captions burned in** (plus optional .srt if requested)
3) **Hook-first edits** + pattern interrupts
4) **A delivery note** with clip titles + suggested captions/hashtags (if included in your package)

### How to review (fast + consistent)
Please review each clip in this order:
1) **First 2 seconds:** Does it immediately grab attention? (Hook lands quickly; no slow intro)
2) **Audio:** Clear voice, no jarring cuts, music not overpowering
3) **Captions:** Readable on mobile, not blocking faces, no obvious typos
4) **Pacing:** No dead air; pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds (zoom/cut/b-roll/text emphasis)
5) **End:** Clear payoff or CTA (follow/subscribe/book/click link)

### How to request revisions (the exact format)
To keep turnaround fast, send revision requests as **timestamped notes**.

Copy/paste this format (one clip per block):

**CLIP FILE NAME:** (example) JRE_2026-04-09_EP12_C03_v1_TT.mp4
- **00:00–00:02:** Replace opening text with: “Most people do THIS wrong…”
- **00:06:** Remove filler word “like” (tighten cut)
- **00:10–00:13:** Add b-roll cue: “client before/after”
- **Caption fix:** “their” not “there”

If you don’t have timestamps, describe the moment clearly (e.g., “right after the phrase ‘when I started coaching…’”).

### Revision policy (built for speed)
**Included:** Up to **1 revision round** per batch (covers small edits: text tweaks, trim, caption corrections, b-roll swap, censor request).

**Not included (counts as a new request/batch):**
- Changing the topic/angle of a clip after delivery (new highlight selection)
- Rebuilding a clip from a different section of the episode
- Major rebrand or style overhaul after approval of preset

### Revision turnaround SLA
- If you reply with timestamped notes within **24 hours**, we’ll typically turn revisions in **24 hours** (often faster).
- If feedback arrives later, we’ll schedule it into the next production slot.

### Quick approvals
If you like a clip, replying with **“Approved”** is enough—no extra steps.
