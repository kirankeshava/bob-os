## Batch Production SOP (free tools)
**Tools (free):** CapCut Desktop *or* DaVinci Resolve. Captions via CapCut auto-captions *or* Whisper (local) then import SRT.

### Standard settings (consistency matters more than fancy)
- **Canvas:** 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps
- **Safe margins:** keep captions and hook text inside 10% inset
- **Audio:** -14 LUFS integrated target, limiter -1dB true peak
- **Captions style:** Bold, high-contrast; 2 lines max; 3–5 words highlighted per sentence.

### Edit formula per clip (30–45s)
1) **Hook (0–2s):** big on-screen text + jump cut.
2) **Context (2–8s):** speaker line that frames payoff.
3) **Value (8–35s):** 2–4 beats; remove pauses; add 2–3 pattern interrupts (zoom/punch-in, b-roll overlay, kinetic text emphasis).
4) **CTA (last 2s):** “Want clips like this? → [website] | email: [email]”.

### B-roll cue treatment (for demos)
If you don’t have licensed b-roll, **use cue callouts** (clean and honest):
- Small label top-left: “B-ROLL CUE: [‘home exterior’, ‘gym workout’, ‘dashboard KPI’]”
- Optional: 1–2 stock clips from CC0 sources only.

### File naming convention
`Niche_Style_SourceID_Clip01_1080x1920.mp4`
Examples:
- `RealEstate_Story_SourceA_Clip01_1080x1920.mp4`
- `B2B_Contrarian_SourceC_Clip02_1080x1920.mp4`
