## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for using Clip Factory.** Here’s the fastest way to review and request revisions so we can keep turnaround within the 48-hour SLA.

### What you received
You’ll receive:
1) **10 vertical clips** (MP4) per episode/video (or the quantity agreed).
2) **A Clip List** (Google Doc / Notion-style text in email) with:
   - Clip filename
   - Working title + hook
   - Source timestamp range (where it came from)
   - Notes (b-roll cues, on-screen text intent, CTA)

### How to review (fastest method)
**Step 1 — Watch at 1.25x speed.** You’re evaluating:
- Does the hook make sense in the first ~1–1.5 seconds?
- Are captions readable on mobile?
- Does the clip stand alone without extra context?
- Any brand/compliance issues?

**Step 2 — Approve in batches.** Reply with:
- **APPROVED:** Clip_01, Clip_02, Clip_04…
- **REVISE:** Clip_03, Clip_05…

### How to request revisions (required format)
To keep revisions quick, please use this exact template for each clip you want changed:

**Clip filename:** (example: CF_Jones_E12_Clip03_v1.mp4)
**Timestamp in the clip:** (example: 00:07–00:10)
**Change type (choose one):** 
- Caption text
- Caption style/position
- Remove/replace a line
- Tighten pacing (cut 0.5–2s)
- Replace hook (use alt line)
- CTA/end card change
- Blur/mute/redact
**Your exact instruction:** (example: “Change caption from ‘double your leads’ to ‘increase qualified leads’ and move captions 10% higher.”)

### What counts as a revision (and what doesn’t)
**Included revisions (within policy):**
- Text/caption corrections
- Minor pacing trims
- CTA/end card tweaks
- Small style adjustments within the same preset

**Not included (treated as new work / next batch):**
- Re-cutting the entire clip around a new idea
- Major restructuring (new story arc)
- New b-roll sourcing beyond simple cues
- Rebranding to a new style preset mid-batch

### Timing
- Please send revision requests **within 48 hours of delivery**.
- We turn standard revisions in **24 hours** (often faster) if requests follow the template above.
