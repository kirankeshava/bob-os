## 1) The 48-hour feedback timeline (timeboxed)
We build speed into the process. Feedback needs to match it.

**T+0 (Delivery):** You receive a review link (Frame.io or Google Drive).  
**Within 12 hours:** Mark “Approve” or leave comments on any clips you want adjusted.  
**Within 24 hours:** We deliver **Revision Round #1**.  
**Within 36 hours:** If needed, final tweaks for **Revision Round #2 (minor only)**.  
**By 48 hours:** Final package is delivered.

If feedback arrives later than the windows above, we’ll still help, but the delivery clock may shift.

---

## 2) Where to leave feedback (so we don’t miss anything)
**Best:** Leave comments directly on the clip at the exact timestamp in the review tool.  
**OK:** Reply by email with: Clip Name + timestamp + instruction.  
**Avoid:** “Make it better” without specifics (creates delays).

---

## 3) Use this comment taxonomy (copy/paste)
To keep revisions fast, start each note with one of these tags:

- **KEEP:** Confirm a section is good and should remain.
- **CUT:** Remove a word/line/section. (Include timestamp)
- **SWAP:** Replace with another moment. (Tell us the timestamp to use)
- **CAPTION:** Fix wording, punctuation, or highlight emphasis.
- **B-ROLL:** Add/replace supporting visuals. (Describe what you want)
- **CENSOR:** Blur/bleep a word/name/logo. (Timestamp + what to censor)
- **CTA:** Change the end card call-to-action.

**Example (great):**  
“**CUT** 00:07–00:09 (remove the pause). **CAPTION** change ‘their’ to ‘there’. **CTA** end card: ‘DM ME “PLAN” for details’.”

**Example (too vague):**  
“Clip 3 isn’t hitting. Please improve.”

---

## 4) Decide with this simple rubric (10 seconds per clip)
For each clip, choose ONE:

1. **APPROVE** (ready to post)
2. **MINOR EDITS** (captions/words, 1–2 quick tweaks)
3. **MAJOR CHANGE** (new hook or different highlight)

If you mark **Major Change**, please include the **replacement timestamp** you want us to use (or tell us the goal and we’ll propose an alternate moment).

---

## 5) What counts as a revision (so scope stays predictable)
### Included (fast)
- Caption corrections, punctuation, emoji removal/addition
- Hook trimming (tightening first 1–2 seconds)
- Censor/bleep/blur requests
- Small pacing changes (remove pauses)
- CTA end card text change
- B-roll swap cues (if simple)

### Not included (new work; may require a new order)
- Rebuilding the entire set around a new topic
- Full rebrand (new fonts/colors/layout) after clips are already produced
- New footage sourcing beyond simple stock cues
- Additional clips beyond the purchased batch

---

## 6) The fastest way to request a “new hook”
If a hook isn’t strong enough, pick one option:

**Option A (fastest):** Provide a replacement hook line you want on-screen (1 sentence).  
**Option B:** Provide a timestamp from the long-form where you say the best hook.  
**Option C:** Tell us the goal (e.g., “book calls,” “sell program,” “get follows”) and we’ll propose 2 alternate hooks—choose 1.

---

## 7) Approval = delivery-ready files
When you mark a clip **Approved**, we treat it as ready to export in the final format set (TikTok / IG Reels / YT Shorts presets). Any change after approval may fall outside the 48-hour window.

---

## 8) If you’re busy: the “Just ship it” option
If you don’t have time to review, reply: **“Ship as-is”** and we’ll finalize and deliver the full pack. This is the fastest route to consistent posting.
