D) SAMPLE-CLIP FULFILLMENT SOP (30–60s)
Goal: Turn any prospect’s existing long video into a compelling 30–60s vertical sample fast.

1) Pick the moment:
- Identify a self-contained “problem → insight” segment.
- Prefer strong opinion, contrarian take, or actionable list.
- Target 35–55 seconds for best completion.

2) Hook rewrite (first 2 seconds):
- Add a 6–10 word hook as on-screen text mirroring the line they say (or a tighter paraphrase).
- Examples: “This is why your Reels aren’t converting” / “Stop doing this in your podcast intro”.

3) Captions:
- Burn in high-contrast captions.
- Emphasize 2–4 keywords in a second color.
- Keep max 2 lines, large font for mobile.

4) Formatting:
- 9:16 vertical, safe-zone margins (avoid bottom UI + right-side icons).
- Speaker centered; use punch-ins (110–125%) at emphasis points.

5) Finishing touches:
- Add subtle whoosh/cut SFX only if it fits.
- Keep music low or none.
- Export H.264 MP4, 1080x1920.
