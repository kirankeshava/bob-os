Section: 1) What You’ll Receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll receive a “Delivery Pack” for each batch/order. Standard contents:

A) Final Clips (MP4)
- One MP4 per clip, vertical format, ready to post.

B) Captions File (SRT)
- One .srt file per clip (matching file name) so you can upload native captions if desired.

C) B-Roll / Visual Cue Sheet (PDF or Google Doc)
- Timestamped notes: where b-roll, on-screen text, screenshots, zooms, emojis, or overlays are recommended.

D) Posting Suggestions (Doc)
- For each clip: suggested first-line caption, 5–15 hashtags (platform-appropriate), and optional CTA.

---
Section: 2) Our QA Standards (What We Check Before Delivery)
We run QA on every clip to reduce revisions and prevent issues that hurt performance.

A) Safe Zones / Cropping
- Faces and key on-screen text remain inside “safe zones” for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.
- No accidental cropping of foreheads/chins; no UI overlays blocking text.

B) Captions Accuracy
- Captions match spoken words (no meaning-changing errors).
- Speaker labels only if needed; punctuation added for readability.
- Profanity handling matches your preference (bleep, mask, or verbatim).

C) Hook Clarity (First 1–2 seconds)
- The opening is clear and attention-grabbing.
- On-screen hook text (if used) matches the spoken promise.
- No slow intros or dead air.

D) Jump Cuts & Pacing
- Cuts feel natural; no distracting stutters or abrupt mid-word cuts.
- Removes filler where appropriate while preserving meaning.

E) Audio Leveling
- Dialogue is consistently audible.
- No clipping, harsh sibilance, or sudden volume jumps.
- Background noise reduced where possible without artifacts.

F) Visual Quality
- No accidental blur or incorrect crop.
- Consistent framing; no jittery zooms.
- Optional light color correction if source needs it.

G) Platform Formatting
- Vertical resolution consistent with 9:16.
- Exports compatible with TikTok/IG/YouTube.

---
Section: 3) Review Process (Fast, Timestamped Feedback)
We use timestamped comments to keep revisions fast and precise.

Step 1 — You receive a review link
- We share a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave comments with timestamps.

Step 2 — Leave feedback with timestamps
- Please comment like: “00:07 change ‘your’ to ‘you’re’” or “00:12 remove word ‘basically’”.
- If you prefer email feedback, include timestamps in the email.

Step 3 — Revision window
- Standard revision window is 24 hours from delivery of the review link.
- If we don’t hear back within 24 hours, we assume approval and move the project to completed status.

---
Section: 4) Revisions Policy (What’s Free vs What’s a Paid Change)
We want you thrilled with the clips while protecting timelines and scope.

Free revisions (included)
- Caption typos or mis-hear corrections.
- Small timing tweaks (caption timing, cut timing, remove brief filler).
- Minor framing/safe-zone corrections.
- Small audio leveling adjustments.
- Replacing a b-roll moment with a similar alternative (same idea, similar effort).

Paid changes (scope change)
- Changing the clip selection (new timestamps/topics after we already edited).
- New creative direction after delivery (different style, new template, new pacing rules).
- Adding significant new elements: heavy motion graphics, custom animation, complex meme edits.
- Re-edit from scratch due to new instructions not provided at intake.
- Additional versions per platform beyond the agreed package.

If a request is paid:
- We will quote the change clearly before starting.
