C) THE 6 DEMO CLIPS (2 per niche)

REAL ESTATE CLIP 1 — Story Hook (30–40s)
Goal: demonstrate hook, pacing, zoom cuts, and clean captions.
Hook on-screen (0–2s): “This is why buyers keep getting rejected in 2026…”
Edit beats:
- 0–1s: pattern interrupt (quick zoom-in + whoosh)
- 1–10s: presenter sets the scenario (3–4 quick jump cuts)
- 10–30s: 3-point explanation, each point gets a big keyword highlight
B-roll cues: overlay quick cutaways: “offer sheet”, “house exterior”, “email inbox”, “calendar”.
CTA (final 2s): “Want your market updates turned into daily clips?”

REAL ESTATE CLIP 2 — Listicle (25–35s)
Hook (0–2s): “3 things buyers must do before touring homes.”
Structure:
- Title card 0–1s
- Point 1 (1–10s): caption “#1 Get pre-approved (not pre-qualified)”
- Point 2 (10–20s): caption “#2 Know your walk-away number”
- Point 3 (20–30s): caption “#3 Ask for the seller timeline”
B-roll cues: quick overlays for each point (bank doc / calculator / calendar).
CTA: “DM ‘CLIPS’ to see a weekly clip plan.”

FITNESS CLIP 1 — Contrarian Take (25–40s)
Hook: “Stop doing more cardio if fat loss has stalled.”
Edit beats:
- 0–3s: hard cut + big caption emphasis on “STOP”
- 3–15s: explain the common mistake (too much cardio + low recovery)
- 15–35s: give the better lever (protein target + steps + strength progression)
B-roll cues: treadmill → protein meal → dumbbell set.
CTA: “Want your long workouts/podcasts clipped daily?”

FITNESS CLIP 2 — Listicle (25–35s)
Hook: “The 4-question checklist before you change your diet.”
Points:
1) “Am I sleeping 7+ hours?”
2) “Did I hit protein 5/7 days?”
3) “Am I walking 7–10k steps?”
4) “Did I lift with progressive overload?”
Editing: each point appears with a subtle sound pop + quick zoom.
CTA: “I’ll turn one episode into 10 clips. Free samples: email above.”

B2B CLIP 1 — Testimonial-Style (30–45s)
If the source is an interview/podcast: pick a segment where the speaker references results or a clear outcome.
Hook: “We didn’t need more leads. We needed a better offer.”
On-screen structure:
- 0–2s: hook
- 2–10s: problem statement (caption: “Problem: busy, inconsistent pipeline”)
- 10–30s: change (caption: “Change: narrow ICP + stronger promise”)
- 30–40s: outcome (caption: “Result: higher reply rate + faster closes”)
B-roll cues: simple icons/overlays (pipeline chart, calendar, inbox).
CTA: “Want your podcast turned into client-getting clips?”

B2B CLIP 2 — Story Hook (25–40s)
Hook: “If your content ‘educates’ but doesn’t sell… this is why.”
Beats:
- 0–8s: identify the symptom (views, no calls)
- 8–25s: explain the cause (no clear POV, no CTA, too broad)
- 25–38s: fix (one-liner positioning + specific CTA)
Caption emphasis words: “POV”, “ICP”, “CTA”.
CTA: “Get 6 sample clips: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3”.
