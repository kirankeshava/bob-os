### What you received
You should have:
1) **10 vertical clips** (9:16) ready for TikTok / Reels / Shorts  
2) **A delivery folder** with consistent filenames  
3) **A notes file** (or message) listing each clip’s hook + topic + recommended post caption (optional)

### How to review (fast)
Please review in this order:
1) **Hook (first 1–2 seconds):** Would you stop scrolling?  
2) **Captions:** Are they readable on mobile and matching the audio?  
3) **Pacing:** Does something change every ~2–3 seconds (zoom, cut, b-roll cue, on-screen emphasis)?  
4) **Brand fit:** Does it match your style preset (Real Estate / Fitness / B2B or your custom notes)?

### What counts as a revision (included)
Revisions are meant for **polish and correctness**, not re-editing from scratch.
Included revisions (per clip):
- Caption fixes (typos, timing, readability)
- Small trim adjustments (tighten start/end)
- Replace a hook line with an alternate line from the same segment
- Swap b-roll cue text (keep same duration)
- Adjust on-screen emphasis (highlight different words)

### What is NOT included (new scope)
These are considered new work and may require an additional order:
- Choosing entirely different moments (new timestamps / new source segment)
- Full style overhaul (new fonts/colors/layout not in the agreed preset)
- Heavy content rewriting that changes the clip’s message
- Adding new external b-roll assets that require sourcing/licensing

### Exactly how to request revisions (required format)
Reply with a list in this format (copy/paste):
- **Clip filename:** (example: CF_JSmith_E05_C03_v1.mp4)  
  **Change needed:** (example: captions start 0.3s too late; trim first 0.5s; replace on-screen word emphasis at 00:06)  
  **Timestamp(s):** (example: 00:00–00:02, 00:06–00:08)

### Revision limits & timeline
- **1 revision round included** (up to **5 clips** in that round).  
- Turnaround for the revision round: **within 24 hours** of receiving your revision notes.
