### A) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch of clips you’ll get:
1) **Final MP4 clips** (vertical, platform-ready)
2) **Captions file** for each clip (**.SRT**)
3) **B-roll cue sheet** (timestamped suggestions + on-screen text notes)
4) **Posting suggestions** per clip:
   - Suggested first line (caption hook)
   - Suggested hashtags (light + niche)
   - CTA suggestion (follow / comment / link in bio)

### B) Our QA standards (what we check before you ever see a draft)
We run a consistent checklist to protect your brand and reduce back-and-forth:

**1. Framing & safe zones**
- Faces are not cropped (forehead/chin/cheeks preserved).
- Subject is centered with platform-safe margins (avoid UI overlays on TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
- On-screen text stays inside safe zones and remains readable.

**2. Captions accuracy**
- Captions match spoken words (names/terms corrected).
- Punctuation used for readability.
- No offensive/unsafe mis-transcriptions.
- Line breaks optimized for mobile.

**3. Audio quality & leveling**
- Speech is clear and consistent clip-to-clip.
- No clipping/distortion; background noise reduced where possible.
- Loudness is normalized so clips don’t feel “quiet” vs other posts.

**4. Edit pacing (jump cuts, dead air, hook clarity)**
- Dead air removed.
- Jump cuts are clean and not disorienting.
- Strong hook in the first 1–2 seconds.
- Clip has a clear point + payoff.

**5. Visual polish**
- Subtitles don’t block key visuals.
- B-roll cues align with the narrative (if included).
- No awkward freeze frames.

**6. Export settings (platform-ready)**
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1080×1920 preferred
- Frame rate: matches source when possible (typically 30fps)

### C) Review process (timestamp comments)
We deliver **review links** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) so you can leave timestamped feedback.

**How to request changes**
- Leave comments at exact timestamps (e.g., 00:07 “caption typo”; 00:12 “trim 0.5s pause”).
- Group feedback into one pass when possible to speed turnaround.

### D) Revision window and turnaround
- You have a **24-hour revision window** after we send the review link.
- We aim to complete eligible revisions quickly after receiving consolidated feedback.

### E) Revision policy (what’s free vs what changes scope)
**Free revisions (included)**
- Caption corrections/typos
- Minor timing tweaks (tighten pauses, small trims)
- Replace a word/line of on-screen text
- Fix safe-zone/framing issues (e.g., cropped face)
- Minor audio leveling adjustments

**Out-of-scope / paid changes (handled as a new request later)**
- Changing the clip topic/angle after delivery (“make this about X instead”)
- New clips not in the original batch
- Re-edit from scratch based on a new creative direction
- Heavy rewriting of hooks across all clips after approval
- New branding package after work is completed (new fonts/colors/layout)

If you’re unsure, email us at agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with a short description and we’ll classify it fast.
