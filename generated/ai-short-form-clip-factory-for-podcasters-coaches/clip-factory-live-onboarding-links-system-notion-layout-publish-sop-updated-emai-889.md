Section 1 — What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll receive a Delivery Pack folder containing:
1) Final MP4 clips (vertical)
2) Captions file for each clip (.SRT)
3) B-roll cue sheet (what to overlay + where)
4) Posting suggestions (first-line caption options + hashtag set)

Section 2 — Our QA standards (what we check before you see it)
We run QC on every clip:
• Safe zones: on-screen text and captions stay clear of UI overlays (TikTok/Reels)
• Captions accuracy: names/terms checked; punctuation and line breaks readable
• Audio leveling: consistent loudness; no clipping; background noise reduced
• Jump cuts and pacing: removes dead air; keeps natural flow
• Hook clarity: first 1–2 seconds communicate why to keep watching
• Framing: no cropped faces; speaker stays centered; key gestures visible
• Export settings: 1080×1920, H.264 MP4, 30fps (unless source requires), AAC audio

Section 3 — Review flow (how to request revisions)
We send a review link (Frame.io/Vimeo/YouTube unlisted) with timestamp comments enabled.
How to comment:
1) Watch and pause at the moment you want changed
2) Leave a single comment per issue with a clear instruction
3) If referencing captions, paste the corrected text

Revision window:
You have 24 hours from delivery of the review link to request free revisions (within scope).

Section 4 — Revision policy (what’s free vs paid)
Free revisions (within scope):
• Caption fixes/typos, minor timing adjustments
• Minor reframes/crop tweaks to avoid face cutoffs
• Small audio level tweaks
• Swapping a b-roll overlay per the cue sheet (if provided/available)

Paid changes (out of scope):
• New creative direction, new hook angle, or different topic selection
• Additional clips beyond the order quantity
• Rewriting scripts or creating new narratives from scratch
• New brand kit changes after work starts (fonts/colors/styles) requiring rework
• Major re-edit due to new instructions not provided at intake

Section 5 — What we need from you (Intake)
Please complete our intake form before editing begins. It captures:
• Target platforms (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
• Tone (educational, punchy, calm, etc.)
• CTA + link/handle
• Banned words/topics
• Brand preferences (captions style, emojis yes/no)

Section 6 — Delivery timeline & communication
• We confirm intake and start editing once requirements are clear.
• You’ll receive a review link first (if included) or final pack directly (per order terms).
• We respond to revision notes quickly within the 24-hour window.
