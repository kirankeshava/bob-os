## 1) Before we start (Client Intake + Pre-Flight)
To avoid rework and missed expectations, we confirm these items before editing begins:
- Target platforms: TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Tone: educational, direct, hype, calm, etc.
- Your handle(s), URL/CTA, and any “must-include” line
- Words/topics to avoid (compliance, personal brand, sponsor rules)
- Caption style: verbatim vs lightly cleaned (recommended)
- Branding: logo yes/no, colors, fonts (if any)

If anything is missing, we’ll email the same day to confirm.

---

## 2) Our QA standard (what we check on every clip)
**Hook & clarity**
- Strong first 1–2 seconds (no dead air)
- Clip has one clear idea and a clear payoff
- Removes filler words when it improves pace (without changing meaning)

**Framing & safe zones**
- No cropped faces/hands; subject stays centered
- On-screen text stays inside safe zones (not under UI buttons)
- Any b-roll overlays do not block key visual information

**Captions**
- Captions match spoken words; names/terms corrected
- Readable size/contrast; line breaks make sense
- No obvious punctuation errors; numbers standardized

**Audio**
- Clean, consistent loudness; no clipping or pumping
- Dialogue prioritized; music (if used) stays underneath

**Edits**
- Jump cuts are intentional (no jarring micro-cuts)
- No accidental repeated words/frames
- Ending is clean (no cut mid-word)

**Export / platform formatting**
- Vertical 9:16 export
- Clean MP4 delivery + captions file (SRT)
- Versioned filenames so nothing gets overwritten

---

## 3) Review flow (timestamp comments)
We send a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) so you can leave **timestamped comments**.

**How to request changes (best practice):**
- Comment directly on the video at the exact timestamp
- Be specific: “0:07 remove pause” / “0:12 caption typo” / “0:15 swap b-roll cue”

**Revision window:** 24 hours from delivery of the review link (unless otherwise agreed).

---

## 4) What you receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll get a folder with:
1. **Final MP4s** (ready to post)
2. **SRT caption files** (per clip)
3. **B-roll cue sheet** (timestamped suggestions used or recommended)
4. **Posting suggestions** (per clip):
   - First-line caption (hook)
   - 5–12 suggested hashtags (platform-appropriate)
   - Recommended post time window (if requested)

---

## 5) Revisions & change requests (triage rules)
### Free revisions (within the revision window)
Included when it’s correcting what was intended or meeting spec:
- Caption typos/misheard words
- Minor timing tweaks (trim head/tail, tighten pause)
- Safe-zone/framing fixes (cropped face, text too low)
- Audio leveling fixes
- Simple on-screen text corrections (names, handle, CTA line)

### Paid changes (scope changes)
Not included because they change direction after work is delivered:
- New hook direction or rewriting the narrative angle
- Swapping to entirely different clip selections from the long-form
- Adding new graphics package/brand kit not provided initially
- Major restructuring (new script-style overlays, heavy motion graphics)
- Additional deliverables not in the original order (extra clips, extra aspect ratios)

If a request is a paid change, we’ll quote it first and wait for your approval.
