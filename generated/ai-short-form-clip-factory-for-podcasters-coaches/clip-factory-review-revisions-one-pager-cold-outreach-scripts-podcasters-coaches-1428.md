## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (48-hour Clip Factory)

**What you’ll receive**
- 10 ready-to-post vertical clips (9:16) with burned-in captions, hook-first pacing, and platform-safe formatting.
- A delivery note that includes: clip list, filenames, any context notes (e.g., b-roll cues), and where to download.

**Where to review**
1) Download the clips (or open the provided review link, if included).
2) Watch each clip *twice*:
   - First pass: “Would I stop scrolling?”
   - Second pass: check captions/readability + brand fit.

**Approval standard (what “done” means)**
A clip is considered approved when:
- The hook is clear in the first ~1.5 seconds.
- Captions are readable on mobile (no covering key on-screen elements).
- Audio is clean enough for social (no major distortion).
- The clip matches the chosen style preset (fonts/colors/layout) and includes a clear end CTA.

**How to request revisions (fastest way)**
Reply to the delivery email and use this exact format for each clip:
- **Clip filename:** (example: EP12_C03_TheRealReasonLeadsGhostYou_v1.mp4)
- **Timestamp(s):** (example: 00:04–00:07)
- **Change request:** (example: “Replace ‘ghost you’ with ‘stop responding’ in captions.”)
- **Optional:** Paste your preferred wording.

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Included (within policy below):
- Caption text fixes (misheard words), punctuation tweaks
- Caption positioning changes (higher/lower), minor emphasis changes
- Swap the hook line text overlay (keep same underlying moment)
- Tighten pacing with small trims (remove dead air)
- Update CTA end card text (same design)

Not included (treated as a new request / scope change):
- Changing the 10 selected moments after delivery (new selects)
- Full re-styling to a different preset after production
- Adding new footage/assets not provided at intake
- Re-editing to match a different creator’s style reference after delivery

**Revision policy (protects the 48-hour turnaround)**
- **1 revision round included** if requested within **48 hours of delivery**.
- Revision requests must include **filenames + timestamps**.
- We return revisions within **24 hours** for clear, timestamped notes.

**Need a second round?**
- We can do it, but it becomes a paid add-on or a new order depending on the request size.
