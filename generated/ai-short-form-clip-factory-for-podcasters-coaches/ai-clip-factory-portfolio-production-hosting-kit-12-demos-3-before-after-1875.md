GOAL

A) PRODUCTION SPEC (USE FOR EVERY CLIP)
1) Export format
- 9:16 vertical, 1080x1920, 30fps
- 25–55 seconds (aim 35–45s), keep pace tight
- Burned-in captions (always on)

2) Caption style
- Top-centered or upper-third; 2 lines max
- Highlight keywords (1 color accent)
- Remove filler words, keep meaning
- Add speaker labels only if more than one voice

3) Editing cadence
- Jump cut every 1.0–2.5 seconds (remove dead air)
- Add micro-zooms (105–120%) on emphasis beats
- Pattern interrupt every 6–10 seconds (zoom, b-roll, on-screen text punch)

4) B-roll cue language (use on-screen notes or cutaways)
- Use quick b-roll overlays (0.5–1.5s) matching nouns (e.g., “open house”, “macros”, “pipeline”)
- Insert on key phrases only; don’t drown the talkhead

5) End frame
- 1.5–2 seconds: “Sample clip by AI Clip Factory”
- Small line: “Get clips: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”
