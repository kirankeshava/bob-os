# 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips + Request Revisions

**Clip Factory — Review & Revisions Guide (for 10 clips / 48-hour delivery)**

Thanks for working with us. To keep turnaround fast and avoid long back-and-forth, please use this review format.

**What you’ll receive**
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) ready to post
- Burned-in captions (on-screen)
- Hook-forward edits (hook in first ~1.5s, pattern interrupts every ~2–3s)
- Platform-ready exports (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts)

**Where to review**
- You’ll get a single delivery email with either a review link (preferred) or a folder link that contains:
  - Final MP4s
  - A “CLIP LIST” document with titles + timestamps + suggested captions/hashtags

**How to review efficiently (10–15 minutes total)**
1) Watch clips at 1.25x speed.
2) Mark only *objective* fixes first (names, numbers, misspellings).
3) Then mark *style* feedback in batches (e.g., “Captions slightly higher on all clips”).

**Revision policy (built for speed)**
- **Included:** 1 revision round per clip (up to 10 clips).
- **Timeline:** Revisions returned within **24 hours** after we receive your notes.
- **What counts as a revision (included):**
  - Caption text corrections
  - Caption timing adjustments
  - Hook swap from provided options / minor trimming
  - Small layout changes (caption position, safe margins)
  - Replacing end-card CTA text
- **What counts as a new clip (not included in revision round):**
  - Changing the source section entirely to a different segment
  - New topic angle that requires a different select
  - Full style rebrand (new fonts/colors/look across the batch)

- **Timestamp:** (example: 00:04–00:09)
