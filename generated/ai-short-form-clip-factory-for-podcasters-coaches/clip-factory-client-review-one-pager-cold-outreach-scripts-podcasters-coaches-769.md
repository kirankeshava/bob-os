## 1) Client-Facing One-Pager: How to Review Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

**Clip Factory Review Guide (2–7 minutes)**

Thanks for trusting us with your short-form.

**What you’re receiving**
- Up to **10 vertical clips** (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Each clip includes: **hook-first framing**, **captions**, **light pattern interrupts**, and **a clear CTA end-beat** (when appropriate)

**Where to review**
- You’ll receive either:
  - a shared review link (preferred), or
  - a folder containing MP4s + a “Notes” doc

**How to review efficiently (recommended order)**
1) **Watch first 2 seconds**: Does it grab attention immediately? (Hook clarity)
2) Watch **without sound** for 5 seconds: Do captions carry the meaning?
3) Watch the **last 2 seconds**: Is there a clean ending (no awkward cut) and CTA if requested?

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Included revisions are **light adjustments** that don’t change the selected moment:
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, minor phrasing)
- Small timing tweaks (±0.5–1.5s trims)
- Caption position changes (top/mid/bottom safe area)
- Volume normalization tweaks
- Swap CTA text on end card
- Blur/censor request (simple)

**What is NOT included as a revision (new work)**
These requests require a new clip or additional scope:
- “Pick a different moment from the episode” (new highlight selection)
- Major restructuring (adding new sections, changing the narrative arc)
- Complex motion graphics / heavy VFX
- Rebuilding multiple clips based on new strategy after delivery

**Revision window + SLA**
- Please send revision requests within **7 days** of delivery.
- We handle included revisions in **1 business day** (best effort).
