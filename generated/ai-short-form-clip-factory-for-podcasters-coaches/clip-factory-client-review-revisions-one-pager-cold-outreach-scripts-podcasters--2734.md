### What you’ll receive
You’ll get a delivery email from **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** with:
1) A link to the delivered clips (download folder or review link)
2) A “Clip List” (titles/hooks + timestamps + filenames)

### Your 3-step review process (10–15 minutes)
**Step 1 — Watch with sound ON (first pass):**
- Do the first 1–2 seconds pull attention?
- Is the topic accurate and not misleading?
- Does it match your brand tone?

**Step 2 — Captions + framing (second pass):**
- Are captions readable on mobile?
- Any obvious misspellings of names/brands?
- Is the speaker centered / not cut off?

**Step 3 — Pick your “winners” (third pass):**
Reply with:
- Your top 3 clips to post first
- Any clips to avoid (and why)

### What counts as a revision (included)
We include **one revision round** per batch (up to **10 clips**) if requested within the revision window.
Included revision types:
- Caption typo fixes (names, key terms)
- Hook text tweak (short replacement line)
- Caption position adjustment (higher/lower)
- Trimming up to ~2 seconds at start/end
- Swapping the CTA end-card text (same template)

### What is not included (to protect speed)
Not included in the free/standard batch:
- Re-cutting entirely different segments (new selects)
- Changing the overall style preset after delivery
- Heavy b-roll sourcing/placement across all clips
- Complex motion graphics beyond the template

If you want a different direction, request **Batch v2** and we’ll re-run selects with updated rules.

### How to request revisions (copy/paste this format)
Reply to the delivery email with:
1) **Clip filename:**
2) **Timecode:** (e.g., 00:03–00:07)
3) **Issue type:** (caption / hook / trim / framing / CTA)
4) **Exact change requested:**
5) **Priority:** Must-fix / Nice-to-have

Example:
- Clip: EP12_C03_HowToCloseDeals_TK_v1.mp4
- Timecode: 00:00–00:02
- Issue: Hook text
- Change: Replace “Stop losing deals” with “Stop losing buyers at the last minute”
- Priority: Must-fix

### Revision window + turnaround
- **Revision window:** 48 hours from delivery
- **Revision turnaround:** Typically within 24 hours (best effort)
