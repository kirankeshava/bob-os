Title: How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Hit 48 Hours)

Thanks for working with DFY Clip Factory.
To deliver 10 clips in 48 hours, feedback has to be simple, specific, and fast. Here’s the system.

Where you’ll review
- We’ll send a review link (Frame.io or a Google Drive folder).
- You’ll see Clip 01–10 with titles and timestamps.

Your review window (critical)
- Please respond within 12–24 hours of receiving drafts.
- If feedback arrives after 24 hours, final delivery may slip because we batch edits in a fixed production block.

How to leave feedback (best format)
Copy/paste this template for each clip:
Clip #: (e.g., Clip 03)
Decision: APPROVE / MINOR CHANGES
Change requests (bullet points):
- [00:02] Replace “X” with “Y” in captions
- [00:08] Cut 0.5s before the pause
- End card CTA: change to “DM ‘START’”

What counts as a ‘minor change’ (included)
- Caption text fixes, spelling, emphasis highlights
- Small trims (generally <3 seconds)
- Swap hook text overlay
- Adjust caption placement slightly (safe area)

What counts as a ‘major change’ (not included in the included revision round)
- Choosing entirely different moments from the long-form
- Rewriting the clip narrative or adding new talking points
- Large restructuring (multiple cut points across the clip)
- Adding custom motion graphics beyond the selected preset

If you want different moments
Reply with timestamps from the source like:
- “Use 12:40–13:15 and 27:05–27:35 instead of Clip 06.”
(We can scope it as a new order or paid add-on depending on volume.)
