## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for reviewing your clips.** To keep the **48-hour turnaround** fast, please use the process below.

### What you received
You’ll receive:
- **10 vertical clips** (9:16) ready for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- A folder with **final exports** + (optional) **no-captions** versions
- A simple **Clip List** (titles + timestamps + notes)

### How to review (fastest method)
Please review in this order:
1) **Hook (first 1–2 seconds):** Would you stop scrolling?
2) **Captions:** Readable on mobile? Any missed words?
3) **Pacing:** Any dead air? Too fast?
4) **Context:** Does the clip make sense without the full episode?
5) **CTA:** Does the ending feel natural and on-brand?

### How to request revisions (required format)
Reply to this email with your revision requests in this exact format so we can execute quickly:

**Clip File Name:** (example: CF_ACME_EP012_C03_v1.mp4)
- **Timestamp:** 00:00–00:03
  - **Change:** Replace hook text to: “…”
- **Timestamp:** 00:12–00:18
  - **Change:** Remove 1 sentence / tighten pacing
- **Timestamp:** 00:22
  - **Change:** Caption correction: “their” → “there”

If you want a **different moment** entirely, please provide:
- The **episode timestamp range** (example: 14:20–16:10)
- A 1–2 sentence note on the angle (example: “make it about the #1 mistake new buyers make”)

### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **edit-level changes** that keep the same selected moment:
- Caption corrections
- Hook text swaps
- Removing dead air, tightening a sentence
- Minor b-roll cue swaps
- CTA/end-card text update

### What counts as a new clip (not a revision)
These requests are considered a **new selection** (we can do them, but it changes scope):
- “Use a different part of the episode” with no timestamps provided
- “Make 10 totally different clips”
- New creative direction or new brand style after delivery

### SLA for revisions
- **1 revision round included** (up to 10 clips in the batch)
- Revisions returned within **24 hours** after you send structured notes
