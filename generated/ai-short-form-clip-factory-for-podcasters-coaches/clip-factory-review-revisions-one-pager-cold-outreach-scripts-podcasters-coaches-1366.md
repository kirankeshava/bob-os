### What you’re receiving
For each order, you’ll receive:
- **10 ready-to-post vertical clips** (9:16) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- **Burned-in captions** (high-contrast, platform-safe margins)
- **Hook-first pacing** (first 1–1.5 seconds)
- **Pattern interrupts** every ~2–3 seconds (punch-ins, b-roll cues, emphasis words, etc.)
- Optional: A simple **delivery notes doc** with timestamps and suggested post captions/hashtags

### How to review (fast + clear)
1) **Watch each clip once without pausing** (does it keep attention?)
2) Watch again and check:
   - Hook lands immediately (no slow intros)
   - Captions are accurate and readable
   - No awkward cuts mid-sentence
   - Any on-screen text matches what’s said
3) If you’re happy: reply **“Approved”** and we’re done.

### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **small, targeted changes** that don’t require a full re-edit, such as:
- Caption fixes (typos, mis-heard words)
- Slight timing adjustments (start/end trim)
- Swap a b-roll cue or remove a distracting overlay
- Change CTA end-card text (same template)
- Minor re-frame adjustments (face centered / safe margins)

### What does NOT count as an included revision
To protect the 48-hour delivery promise, these are treated as a **new request / paid add-on** (we’ll quote first):
- Changing the topic/angle after delivery (“make it more about X”)
- Rebuilding the clip around a different section of the episode
- Major style overhaul (new fonts/colors/layout not in agreed preset)
- New clips beyond the included quantity

### Revision limits + turnaround
- **1 revision round** included for the batch
- Please send revisions within **48 hours of delivery**
- We return the revised batch within **24 hours** after receiving a clear revision list
