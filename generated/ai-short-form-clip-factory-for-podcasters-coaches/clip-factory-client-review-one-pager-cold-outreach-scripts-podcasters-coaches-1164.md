What you’ll receive
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + hook-forward pacing
- A delivery note with: (a) clip titles, (b) any context, (c) recommended posting order

How to review (fastest method)
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) On the second watch, only note issues that impact performance or brand safety:
   - Wrong words in captions that change meaning
   - Awkward cuts that remove context
   - Incorrect names/terms
   - Anything that conflicts with brand or compliance rules
3) If you want a “style change” (fonts/colors/layout), choose ONE clip as the reference and say: “Make the rest match Clip 03.”

Revision policy (to protect 48-hour speed)
- Included: 1 revision round per order (up to 10 clips).
- Revision window: Please send feedback within 48 hours of delivery; after that, turnaround may shift based on queue.
- What counts as a revision (included):
  - Caption fixes (typos/mis-hears) and minor timing adjustments
  - Replace 1–2 words on-screen text, adjust emoji usage, small layout shift
  - Swap a clip’s start/end by up to ~2 seconds if it improves clarity
- What counts as a new request (not included):
  - New clip ideas, new selects from the long-form, or major restructuring
  - New brand package/theme after delivery
  - Adding new b-roll sourcing beyond simple cues
