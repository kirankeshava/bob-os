To avoid confusion, please send revisions in this exact structure:
- **Clip Name:** (example: `EP12_C02_Hook-HowToCloseMoreDeals_v1`)

- **Change Needed:** (example: “Replace the word ‘prospect’ with ‘buyer’ in captions”)
- **Priority:** Must-fix / Nice-to-have

**What counts as a revision (included)**
- Caption typo fixes / punctuation / minor timing adjustments
- Slight hook tightening (trim 0–2 seconds)
- Reframing the on-screen headline text (keeping same clip topic)
- Audio leveling tweaks

**What counts as a new clip request (not included in revisions)**
- Swapping to a different segment/topic entirely
- Adding new b-roll you didn’t provide or changing the whole visual style direction
- Requesting additional clips beyond the 10 delivered
