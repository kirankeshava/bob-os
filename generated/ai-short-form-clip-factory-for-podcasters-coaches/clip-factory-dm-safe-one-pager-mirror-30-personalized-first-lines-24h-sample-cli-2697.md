## 3) 24-Hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (tight execution, fast turnaround)

Goal: Deliver a credible ‘sample clip’ output in <24h that makes the prospect say “yes—do this for my whole show.”

### A. Intake (15 minutes)
Ask for (email to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to):
1) Episode link (YouTube/Spotify) or file
2) Who the content is for (target audience)
3) Their offer/CTA (newsletter, consult, program)
4) 2 caption styles they like (send links)
5) Any banned words / compliance notes
6) Preferred tone: direct, friendly, contrarian, story-first
Log in CRM: date received, deadline, niche, CTA, link.

### B. Select candidate moments (30–45 minutes)
1) Skim transcript (auto or manual quick scan) for:
- Contrarian take (“Most people get this wrong…”) 
- Clear list (“3 steps…”) 
- Simple rule (“If you only do one thing…”) 
- Story turning point (“Then I realized…”) 
2) Pick **1 primary segment** (20–45s) + **1 backup segment**.
3) Note: start/end timestamps.

### C. Hook options (20 minutes)
Write **5 hook lines** aligned to the clip content:
- 2 curiosity hooks
- 2 authority hooks
- 1 contrarian hook
Also write **1 CTA line** that matches their business goal.

### D. Captions + on-screen text plan (30 minutes)
1) Caption rules:
- 1–2 lines max on screen
- Punchy punctuation
- Highlight 1–2 keywords per sentence (caps or color note)
2) Provide a simple on-screen text plan:
- Opening 1–2s: main hook text
- Mid-clip: 1 reinforcement phrase
- End: CTA text

### E. Deliverable package (send via email)
Subject: “Sample clip plan for [Show/Name] — timestamps + hooks”
Include:
1) Timestamped segment (start/end)
2) 5 hook options
3) Caption style notes + sample caption text (first 10–15 seconds)
4) CTA text
5) Next-step ask: “Want 5 clips from this episode by [date]?”

### F. Upsell script (copy/paste)
“If this sample direction matches your voice, I can turn this episode into **5 ready-to-post vertical clips** (captions + hooks + exports). Want to start with the Starter pack? Just reply ‘Starter’ and send your top 1–2 goals (followers vs leads).”

### Quality checklist (before sending)
- Segment is understandable with no extra context
- First 1.5 seconds has a clear hook angle
- Captions are readable and not too dense
- CTA matches their offer and doesn’t feel spammy
