## 4) 24‑Hour “Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (DFY, no SaaS build)

**Goal:** deliver 2 sample vertical clips within 24 hours of receiving a link, proving quality and speeding conversion.

### Step 0 — Intake (10 minutes)
Ask for (via email to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to):
- Video link (YouTube/Drive) + permission to download
- Target platform(s): TikTok / IG Reels / Shorts
- Brand references: 1–2 creators they like
- Any banned words/topics + preferred CTA
- On-screen style: minimal / bold / karaoke captions

### Step 1 — Source + Select Highlights (30–45 minutes)
- Skim the episode at 1.5–2x.
- Mark 4–6 candidate timestamps with:
  - clear “hook” moment in first 1–2 seconds
  - single idea per clip
  - emotional spike (surprise, contrarian take, story, result)
- Choose best 2 candidates based on: clarity, novelty, shareability.

### Step 2 — Edit Vertical (45–90 minutes)
- Set canvas 9:16, keep speaker centered.
- Add quick jump cuts, remove dead air.
- Add safe-zone padding for platform UI.
- Add light background music only if it won’t conflict with platform licensing; otherwise skip.

### Step 3 — Captions + Hook Text (30–45 minutes)
- Generate transcript (tool of choice).
- Clean punctuation; remove filler where needed.
- Style captions: 2 lines max, high contrast, highlight keywords.
- Add **1 on-screen hook line** at start (e.g., “Most coaches price wrong because…”).

### Step 4 — B‑roll Cues (10–15 minutes)
- Add on-screen notes (non-rendered or as a sidecar doc) for 2–4 b-roll moments:
  - screenshot suggestion
  - simple overlay keyword
  - quick cutaway idea

### Step 5 — Export + Delivery (10 minutes)
Deliver:
- 2 MP4 files (9:16)
- Captions burned-in
- A short text block per clip:
  - suggested caption copy
  - 5–10 hashtags
  - posting note (ideal first comment / CTA)

Send via email with subject: “Your 2 sample clips (24h) — Clip Factory”
Include:
- Official site link: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Next step CTA: “Want me to do this weekly? Reply ‘WEEKLY’ and I’ll propose a clip pack.”

### Quality checklist (must pass)
- Hook visible in first second
- Captions readable on mobile
- Audio normalized; no clipping
- No awkward mid-word cuts
- 9:16 safe zones respected
