D) CapCut Desktop SOP (fast render)
Project settings
• Canvas: 9:16 (1080x1920)
• FPS: 30
• Audio: -14 to -10 LUFS feel; keep voice clear.

Template
1) Import source, cut the selected excerpt to target length.
2) Reframe for vertical: keep eyes on upper third; use auto-reframe then manual tweaks.
3) Hook card (0–2s)
   • Solid background or blurred frame
   • Big text (6–10 words max)
4) Captions
   • Style: bold sans; white text w/ black shadow
   • Highlight 1–2 keywords per line (yellow)
   • Line length: 3–7 words; max 2 lines
5) Pace / cuts
   • Jump cut every 1.5–2.5s
   • Punch-in 105–115% on emphasis words
6) B-roll cues
   • Don’t add heavy b-roll if short on time—add cue overlays: “B-ROLL: …” in small uppercase in top-left for 1–2 seconds.
7) Light polish
   • Noise reduction if needed
   • Subtle background music at -28 to -32 dB (optional)
8) End CTA (last 2s)
   • “Want clips like this weekly? Email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

Export
