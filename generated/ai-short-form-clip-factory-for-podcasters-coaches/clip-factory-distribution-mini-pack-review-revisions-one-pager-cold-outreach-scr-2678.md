## 1) Client One-Pager — How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (24h Window)

**Welcome!** Your short-form clips are ready for review. To keep our **48-hour delivery** promise and maintain fast iteration, we use a simple review system.

### What you’ll receive
- **10 vertical clips** (9:16) optimized for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in **captions** and a strong **hook** in the first ~1.5 seconds
- Light **pattern interrupts** (visual emphasis / cut pacing) every ~2–3 seconds
- Optional **b-roll cues** noted when relevant (if requested)

### How to review (fastest method)
Please review clips in this order:
1) **Hook + first 3 seconds**: Would you stop scrolling?
2) **Caption readability**: Easy to read on mobile? No covering faces?
3) **Pacing**: Any dead air or confusing jump?
4) **Claim safety**: Any phrases that could be risky or non-compliant for your niche?
5) **CTA fit**: Does the ending line match what you want viewers to do?

### What counts as a revision
Included revisions are meant to improve clarity and correctness without re-editing the entire concept.
**Included (OK):**
- Fixing caption errors (typos, punctuation, wrong words)
- Tightening trims (remove a pause, shorten an intro)
- Adjusting caption position/size for readability
- Swapping the title line / hook text overlay (if it matches the same clip)
- Removing a sentence that creates compliance risk

**Not included (new work request):**
- Re-cutting new clips from different timestamps (new selects)
- Changing the entire style direction after delivery (new template)
- Adding heavy motion graphics, complex b-roll sourcing, or new music direction not agreed upfront

### Revision window + policy
- **Revision request window:** within **24 hours** of delivery
- **Rounds included:** **1 revision round** (consolidated)
- **Turnaround for revisions:** typically **24 hours** after we receive your consolidated list
