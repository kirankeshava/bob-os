## 1) Lead sourcing spec (30 leads in 60–90 minutes, no paid tools)
### Target profiles
- Podcasters (video-first preferred) posting 1–4 long episodes/month but inconsistent short-form
- Coaches/consultants publishing webinars, lives, or YouTube videos (10–60 min)
- Small agencies managing 3–20 creators

### Where to find them (free)
1) YouTube
- Search: “podcast episode” + niche (e.g., “podcast episode real estate”, “podcast episode fitness coach”)
- Filter: Upload date (This month), Duration (20+ minutes)
- Prioritize channels with: 1k–100k subs; videos with decent views but minimal Shorts output

2) Spotify/Apple Podcasts directories
- Find shows with recent episodes; google the show name + “YouTube” or “contact”

3) LinkedIn
- Search titles: “Podcast Host”, “Founder”, “Coach”, “Consultant”, “Agency Owner”
- Look for people posting video clips already (signals they value distribution)

### What to capture per lead (simple sheet)
- Name
- Role (podcaster/coach/agency)
- Link to latest long-form episode
- Current short-form output (Y/N, where)
- Contact method (email / LinkedIn)
- One personalization hook (e.g., “Episode with X guest”, “great 0:42 story about …”)

## 3) 15-minute discovery call agenda (to close DFY)
Goal: qualify fast, propose a pilot, collect constraints to inform later SaaS (without building now).

1) Current workflow (2 min)
- Where does long-form live? (YouTube, Riverside, Drive)
- Where do they post short-form today? How often?

2) Pain + urgency (3 min)
- What’s stopping consistent output? (time, editing skill, hiring cost)
- Any upcoming launches/events that make this urgent?

3) Output definition (4 min)
- Target: clips/week, duration (15–45s), tone (edu/story), caption style
- Brand constraints: fonts/colors, forbidden words, CTA preferences

4) Close to paid pilot (4 min)
Offer: “I’ll deliver {{X}} clips in {{Y}} days; if you like them, we roll into weekly cadence.”

5) SaaS validation questions (2 min; capture only)
- If this were self-serve at $99–$299/mo, what would it need to do to replace a human editor?
- Which is most valuable: diarization/speaker detection, brand voice hooks, niche templates, auto b-roll cues, auto posting?
