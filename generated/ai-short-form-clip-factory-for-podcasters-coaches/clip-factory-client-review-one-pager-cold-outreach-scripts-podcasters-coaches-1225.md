### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are meant to fix or adjust the delivered clips **without changing the clip strategy**:
- Caption corrections (spelling, punctuation, mis-transcribes)
- Minor trims/tightening (timing/pacing)
- Volume leveling / remove filler words if obvious
- Blur/redaction requests
- Swap to an alternate moment **from the same selected segment**

### What counts as a new request (not included)
These are new scope items and may require a new batch/order:
- “Pick totally different moments” across the episode (new highlight selection)
- Rebuilding all clips into a different style pack after delivery
- Adding heavy motion graphics or custom animations beyond our template packs
