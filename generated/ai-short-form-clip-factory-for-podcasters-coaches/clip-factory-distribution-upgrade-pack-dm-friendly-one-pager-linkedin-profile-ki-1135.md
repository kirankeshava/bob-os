# 4) 24-Hour “Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

## Goal
Deliver a fast, tangible win within 24 hours of payment (or as a paid trial) to increase trust, reduce refunds, and accelerate referrals.

## Inputs needed (email to client)
Send to: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
Ask for:
1) YouTube link or Drive file (source)
2) Target platform(s): TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
3) Brand notes: fonts/colors (optional), examples they like (2 links)
4) Audience + offer/CTA (1 sentence)
5) Any “must include” segment timestamps (optional)

## Process (internal)
1) **Select moments (30–45 min):**
   - Find 5–8 candidate moments with a clear claim, list, or story.
   - Choose 1–2 “sure thing” clips (20–45s each) for the sample.
2) **Hook packaging (15 min):**
   - Write 2 hook variants per clip (first 1–2 seconds).
3) **Edit (60–90 min):**
   - 9:16 framing, jump cuts, remove dead air.
   - Burned-in captions, highlight keywords.
4) **QC (15 min):**
   - Check captions accuracy, safe margins, audio levels.
5) **Delivery (10 min):**
   - Provide MP4 + a text file containing hooks, caption, and posting suggestion.

## Deliverables
- 1–2 vertical clips (MP4), 9:16, platform-safe
- Captions burned-in
- 2 hook options per clip
- Suggested title + first-line caption for posting
