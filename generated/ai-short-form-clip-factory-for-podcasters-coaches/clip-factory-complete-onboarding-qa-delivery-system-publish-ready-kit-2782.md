Page Title: Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)

Intro
Welcome to Clip Factory. We turn your long-form video/podcast content into ready-to-post vertical clips optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Official links:
- Website: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Support: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

What we deliver (your “Delivery Pack”)
For each batch/order, you receive:
1) Final vertical MP4 clips (platform-safe exports)
2) Captions file(s): .SRT for each clip
3) B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggested overlays/cutaways)
4) Posting suggestions per clip (first-line caption + CTA + hashtags)

Quality standards (our QA checklist)
We run QC on every clip before delivery:
- Safe zones respected (no key text/face in UI-covered regions)
- Captions accuracy + readability (timed properly, minimal typos, no broken lines)
- Audio leveling (voice forward, no clipping, consistent loudness)
- Framing & cropping (no cut-off faces/chins; subject centered for vertical)
- Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds should create curiosity/benefit)
- Jump cuts are intentional (no awkward mid-word cuts; pacing feels natural)
- Visual clarity (no accidental blur; b-roll cues match the spoken point)
- Platform formatting (9:16, correct bitrate, no black bars unless requested)

Review process (how to request changes)
1) We send a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) or a Google Drive folder.
2) Please leave timestamped comments (example: “00:07 change caption wording”).
3) Revision window: 24 hours from delivery. This keeps production fast and prevents delays.

Revision policy (what’s included vs. out-of-scope)
Included (free) revisions within 24 hours:
- Typos or caption timing fixes
- Minor caption rewording without changing the meaning
- Small trim adjustments (tighten start/end by a few seconds)
- Minor audio balance tweaks (voice up/down slightly)
- Safe-zone / cropping fixes (if any UI overlap or face crop issues)

Out-of-scope changes (handled as a new request after the free-trial/week-1 window):
- Changing the hook concept or overall angle after delivery
- Re-editing to include new sections not in the original source
- New branding package (fonts/colors/logo rules) after editing has started
- Replacing music/style with a different creative direction
- Major restructuring (new storyline) or additional clips beyond the agreed count

How to get help
If anything is unclear, reply to your delivery email or contact: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

Form title: Clip Factory — Client Intake (Short-Form Clips)
Form description:
Use this form to tell us your goals, platforms, and preferences so your clips are consistent and on-brand. If you have urgent constraints (banned words, compliance requirements), include them here.

Required fields (set to Required unless noted):
1) Contact email (Short answer)
2) Your name / brand name (Short answer)
3) Primary platform(s) (Checkboxes): TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Other (short answer)
4) Link to source content (Long answer) — “Paste Google Drive/Dropbox/YouTube link(s) to the long video/podcast.”
5) Source type (Multiple choice): Video podcast, Audio podcast, Webinar/Zoom, Coaching content, Other
6) Goal for this batch (Multiple choice): Growth/viral reach, Leads/book calls, Course sales, Newsletter signups, Brand authority
7) Target audience (Long answer)
8) Tone (Checkboxes): High-energy, Calm/authoritative, Funny, Direct, Storytelling, Educational, Other
9) Call-to-action preference (Multiple choice): Follow, Comment keyword, DM me, Link in bio, Book a call, Other
10) Handle(s) + link(s) to include (Long answer) (Optional)
11) On-screen text style (Multiple choice): Clean/minimal, Bold/viral, Subtle, Match my existing style (link below)
12) Brand references (Long answer): “Link examples of your past clips or creators you want to emulate (3–5 links).”
13) Words/topics to avoid (Long answer) (Optional)
14) Must-include phrase(s) (Long answer) (Optional)
15) Clip length preference (Multiple choice): 15–30s, 30–45s, 45–60s, 60–90s
16) Caption style (Multiple choice): Burned-in captions, Minimal captions, No burned-in (SRT only)
17) B-roll preference (Multiple choice): Minimal, Moderate, Heavy, Only if it helps clarity
18) Deadline/launch date (Date) (Optional)
19) Anything else we should know? (Long answer) (Optional)
