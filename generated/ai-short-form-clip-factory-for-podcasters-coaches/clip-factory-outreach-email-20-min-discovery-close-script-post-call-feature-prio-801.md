## 2) 20-Min Discovery Call Script (validate + close DFY; SaaS still gated)

**Goal:** Learn workflow + quantify willingness-to-pay for subscription features ($99–$299/mo), and convert to a DFY pilot today. Do *not* promise a SaaS build date; explicitly frame product as revenue-gated and customer-driven.

### 0:00–2:00 — Setup
- “Thanks for taking the time. Quick context: I run Clip Factory — we produce short-form vertical clips from long videos/podcasts. I’m also validating what to automate later, but we’re focused on delivering results now.”
- “If it’s useful, I can propose a small paid pilot at the end. Sound good?”

### 2:00–7:00 — Current workflow + pain
1) “Where do your long-form videos/podcasts live? (YouTube, Riverside, Zoom, Drive)”
2) “How many short clips do you post per week today?”
3) “What’s hardest right now: finding highlights, editing, captions, writing hooks, posting consistency, or something else?”
4) “How long does it take you (or an editor) to make one good clip end-to-end?”
5) “What’s your current spend per month on editing/clips (if any)?”

### 7:00–12:00 — Output definition (what ‘good’ means)
6) “What’s a ‘win’ for you from clips? Leads, booked calls, views, email signups?”
7) “Do you have examples of clips you like (your own or someone else’s)? What specifically works?”
8) “Any must-follow brand rules? Fonts/colors, logo placement, profanity filters, on-screen framing?”
9) “Do you want b-roll cues (e.g., ‘cut to screenshot’, ‘show tweet’, ‘overlay headline’)?”

### 12:00–16:00 — Validate SaaS features + WTP (subscription signal)
Preface: “Later, we may offer a self-serve tool, but only if it’s clearly worth paying for. I want to sanity-check what you’d actually pay for.”

Ask each feature with a 1–5 rating and a pricing anchor:
- **Auto highlight detection** (find best 30–60s moments automatically)
- **Captions + dynamic word highlighting**
- **Hook generator** (first 1–2 seconds + on-screen headline variants)
- **Speaker diarization** (label speakers, improve caption accuracy)
- **Brand templates** (your fonts/colors/layouts saved)
- **Niche templates** (coach vs agency vs podcast formats)
- **Export presets** (TikTok/Reels/Shorts, safe zones, length variants)

WTP questions:
- “If this reliably saved you {{X}} hours/week and improved consistency, would you pay **$99/mo**?” (Y/N)
- “What would need to be true for **$199/mo** to be a no-brainer?”
- “At **$299/mo**, what features become mandatory?”
- “Any dealbreakers? (watermarks, slow render time, inaccurate captions, no brand control, etc.)”

### 16:00–20:00 — Close into DFY pilot (revenue now)
- “Based on what you said, the fastest path is: we do a small **done-for-you pilot** so you can judge quality and results quickly.”

**Pilot offer (choose one):**
A) **Starter Pilot:** 5 clips from 1 episode/video delivered in 72 hours.
B) **Growth Pilot:** 10 clips/week + 2 hook variants each, delivered weekly.

**Close questions:**
- “If I can deliver {{pilot}} with your brand style and captions, would you want to start this week?”
- “What’s the best episode/video to start with? Can you send a YouTube link or upload?”
- “Who needs to approve? Just you?”

**Next-step email to send immediately after call:**
- Request: link/file, brand assets (logo/fonts/colors), 3 example clips they like, CTA, and posting cadence.
- Include legitimacy URL + reply-to email.

**Important boundary (SaaS gate):**
- “Re: the self-serve product — we’re intentionally not building it until we’ve delivered enough pilots to be sure it solves the right problem. Your answers today directly shape the MVP.”
