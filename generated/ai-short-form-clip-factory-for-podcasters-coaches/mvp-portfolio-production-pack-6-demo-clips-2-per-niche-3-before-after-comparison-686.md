B) EDITING SPEC (applies to all 6 clips)
Format
- Canvas: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps.
- Safe margins: keep captions above bottom 15% and below top 10%.

Caption style (burned-in)
- Large, high-contrast, 2-line max, keyword highlight color (e.g., yellow) on 1–2 words per line.
- Auto-captions corrected for names/numbers; include punctuation for readability.

Motion + cuts
- Add a cut every 1.5–3.0 seconds (jump cuts) where natural.
- Subtle punch-in/punch-out zoom (5–10%) on emphasis phrases.
- Add 2–3 b-roll cues per clip (stock footage suggestion text overlays are acceptable if not inserting actual b-roll yet).
