## 2) Quality standards (our QA checklist)
We run a final QC pass before you ever see a clip.

### A) Safe zones + framing
- No cropped faces/foreheads/chins; the speaker’s eyes remain comfortably within frame.
- Vertical-friendly composition (subject centered or rule-of-thirds).
- On-screen text stays inside platform-safe zones (avoids UI overlays on TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
- Any zoom/crop is intentional (emphasis) and does not degrade clarity.

### B) Captions accuracy + readability
- Captions match the spoken words (no meaning drift).
- Correct names/brands/terms (from your intake).
- Punctuation improves clarity without changing intent.
- Line breaks are readable; no overly long lines.
- Captions do not cover critical visuals (faces, key props, on-screen demos).

### C) Audio leveling + cleanliness
- Dialogue is clearly audible on mobile speakers.
- Loudness is consistent clip-to-clip (no sudden volume jumps).
- Background noise reduced where needed; no distracting artifacts.
- No clipped peaks or distortion.

### D) Hook clarity + pacing
- First 1–2 seconds contain a clear hook (curiosity, bold claim, problem/solution, or surprise).
- Cuts remove dead air and filler while preserving natural rhythm.
- The clip resolves cleanly (no awkward cut mid-word).

### E) Jump cuts + visual continuity
- Jump cuts are smooth (micro zoom, angle shift, b-roll, or intentional cut timing).
- No jarring flashes, glitches, or accidental frame drops.
- B-roll is contextually relevant and not misleading.

### F) Platform formatting + export settings
- Aspect ratio: **9:16 vertical**
- Resolution: **1080x1920** (unless otherwise requested)
- Codec: **H.264**
- File type: **.mp4**
- Frame rate: matches source where possible (commonly 30fps)
- No watermarks unless requested.

## 3) Review flow (fast + timestamped)
We use a review link system that supports **timestamp comments**.

**Preferred (free options):**
- Frame.io (free tier, if available)
- Vimeo review link
- Unlisted YouTube (comments referencing timestamps)

**How to leave feedback (required format)**
- Use timestamped comments like: `00:07 - change caption to “…”, 00:13 - remove “um”, 00:21 - swap b-roll to something about X`
- Group feedback per clip, and keep it consolidated when possible.

## 4) Revision policy (24-hour window)
To keep turnaround fast and protect consistency, we use a clear revision window.

**Revision window:** **24 hours** from when review/delivery links are sent.

### Free revisions (included)
These are fixes to meet the agreed scope:
- Caption typos/mis-hears
- Minor caption timing alignment
- Small framing/safe-zone adjustments
- Audio leveling tweaks
- Removing a tiny filler word/pause (within reason)
- Replacing b-roll with a similar option when the original doesn’t fit

### Out-of-scope changes (not included)
These are new directions after work is completed:
- Changing the hook angle/theme for the clip
- Major re-cut/restructure (new storyline)
- Replacing clips chosen from the long-form with completely different moments (after selection approved)
- Brand/visual style changes not provided at intake (fonts/colors/logo/overlays)
- Additional deliverables not listed in the Delivery Pack

If you request out-of-scope changes, we’ll reply with options (often: do nothing, small compromise, or schedule as a new request). During Week 1 (free launch), we will clearly label these as “out-of-scope” and propose the simplest workaround.

## 5) Turnaround expectations
- Typical turnaround depends on batch size and source length.
- We confirm an estimated delivery date after intake (and after we have source links/files).
