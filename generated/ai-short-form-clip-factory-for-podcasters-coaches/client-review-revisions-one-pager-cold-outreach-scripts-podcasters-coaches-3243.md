CLIENT ONE-PAGER — HOW TO REVIEW YOUR CLIPS + REQUEST REVISIONS (FAST)

Purpose: Help you review quickly so we can keep the 48-hour delivery promise.

What you’ll receive
1) A folder/link with 10 vertical clips (9:16) ready for TikTok / Reels / Shorts.
2) Captions burned-in (with our agreed style preset), plus optional .SRT if requested.
3) A “Clip Notes” doc listing:
   - Clip filenames
   - Source timestamps (where it came from)
   - Hook text used
   - Any b-roll cue notes (if applicable)

How to review (10–15 minutes total)
Step 1 — Watch in feed mode
- Watch on your phone if possible. Vertical clips are designed for mobile.
- Keep sound ON for at least the first 2 seconds of each clip (hook performance).

Step 2 — Mark each clip: APPROVE / REVISE / SWAP
- APPROVE: Good to post as-is.
- REVISE: Keep the same moment, but adjust captions/hook/trim.
- SWAP: Replace this clip with a different moment from the same episode.

Step 3 — Use this exact revision format (copy/paste)
Clip filename: (example) CLIP-03_GrowthMistake_TikTok_v1.mp4
Status: REVISE or SWAP
Request type (choose one):
A) Trim
- “Cut the first 0.4s; end 1.0s earlier”
B) Captions
- “Change first line to: ‘Most founders do THIS wrong…’ and increase highlight on ‘THIS’”
C) Hook
- “Replace opening text with a curiosity hook; keep the same content”
D) CTA
- “End card: ‘DM ME “PLAN” for the template’”
E) Swap moment
- “Swap with a moment about pricing. Timestamp range: 12:40–14:05”

What counts as a revision (fast)
- Caption wording tweaks
- Emojis/keywords/highlights adjustments
- Small timing changes (tighten start/end, remove pauses)
- CTA/end card text changes

What is NOT a revision (new scope)
- Re-editing all 10 clips from scratch with a new style after approval
- Creating new custom motion graphics packages
- Changing brand kit mid-project (fonts/colors) after work begins
- Pulling from additional episodes not provided at intake

Turnaround for revisions
- 1 revision round included (up to 10 notes total across the batch) delivered within 24 hours of receiving your consolidated notes.
- Additional revisions (later): handled after the free launch period (week 1), or scheduled as a new batch.
