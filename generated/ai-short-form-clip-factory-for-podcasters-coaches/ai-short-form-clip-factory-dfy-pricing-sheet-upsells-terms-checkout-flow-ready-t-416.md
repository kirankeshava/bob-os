A clip is one exported vertical video (9:16) between 15–60 seconds (unless you request longer), including:

- On-screen emphasis text where appropriate
- Optional b-roll cues either (a) embedded as quick cutaways if client provides b-roll, or (b) delivered as a cue note/overlay prompt if b-roll is not provided.
