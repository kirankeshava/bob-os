NOTES FOR THE MVP EDIT STYLE (consistency across all 6 demos)
• First frame: big hook text (4–7 words), speaker centered, safe margins
• Captions: 2 lines max, highlight 1–2 keywords per sentence
• Cuts: remove dead air >200ms, add subtle zooms every 1.5–2.5 seconds
• B-roll cues: minimal, only when it clarifies (e.g., “overlay: MLS screenshot”, “overlay: calendar/CRM”, “overlay: macro breakdown”)
• Loudness target: consistent perceived loudness; avoid clipping
