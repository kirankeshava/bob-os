1) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For every batch/order, you’ll receive a folder containing:
1. Final MP4 clips (vertical, platform-ready)
2. Captions file(s): .SRT for each clip (matching the MP4 filename)
3. B-roll / on-screen cue sheet (timestamps + suggestions)
4. Posting suggestions per clip:
   • First-line caption (hook line)
   • 8–15 hashtags (platform-appropriate)
   • Suggested posting note (CTA, pinned comment idea, where to place link/handle)

2) Our quality standard (QA checklist summary)
We run a final QA pass on every clip before delivery:
• Framing & safe zones: no cropped faces; key text stays inside safe zones; center-weighted composition for TikTok/Reels/Shorts.
• Hook clarity: first 1–2 seconds clearly communicates the “reason to watch.”
• Captions accuracy: correct wording, punctuation, speaker attribution when needed; no repeated/garbled lines.
• Caption readability: high contrast, large enough on mobile, non-blocking placement.
• Audio leveling: consistent loudness across clips; no clipping/distortion; background noise minimized.
• Jump cuts: intentional, not jarring; no “dead air” at start/end.
• Platform formatting: 9:16 export, crisp text, no accidental letterboxing.

3) Review process (timestamp comments)
We deliver a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave timestamped comments.
How to leave feedback:
• Please comment directly on the review link at the exact timestamp (e.g., “00:12 change caption to…”).
• Consolidate feedback in one pass when possible.
• If you prefer, you can also email timestamps to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to.

4) Revision window (protects speed + quality)
• You have a 24-hour revision window starting when we send the review link.
• If we do not receive revision notes within 24 hours, we will mark the batch as approved and proceed to finalize/deliver.
• If you anticipate being unavailable, tell us in advance and we can schedule a different review window.

5) What counts as a free revision vs. paid change
Free revisions (included):
• Caption fixes (typos, misheard words) within the source audio
• Minor timing tweaks to captions
• Small framing adjustments to avoid cropped faces or improve safe-zone compliance
• Audio leveling tweaks (if it’s inconsistent)
• Swapping a b-roll suggestion that doesn’t fit the context

Paid changes (scope change):
• Changing the clip selection/theme after cutting (e.g., “pick totally different moments”)
• Rewriting hooks/CTAs based on a new direction not provided at intake
• Adding new branded motion graphics beyond the agreed package
• Changing aspect ratio/package requirements after delivery (e.g., request 1:1 + 16:9 versions)
• Requests that require re-editing from scratch (new structure, different narrative)

If a request is paid, we’ll reply with: (a) why it’s out of scope, (b) the cheapest option to achieve it, and (c) a turnaround estimate.

6) How to get the fastest results (client checklist)
To minimize revisions, please provide at intake:
• Primary platform(s): TikTok / Reels / Shorts
• Your target audience + tone (e.g., punchy, educational, contrarian)
• Your CTA (follow, book call, newsletter, etc.)
• Any banned words/topics, compliance constraints, or brand terms
• Handle/tag + link preference
