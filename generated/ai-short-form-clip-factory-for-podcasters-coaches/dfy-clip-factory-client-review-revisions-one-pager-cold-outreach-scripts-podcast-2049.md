## What you’ll receive
- **10 vertical clips** (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts-ready)
- Each clip includes: **hook-first structure**, **burned-in captions**, and **b-roll cues** (when applicable)
- A delivery note with **clip titles**, **source timestamps**, and **recommended posting caption/CTA** (optional)

## Review checklist (fast)
For each clip, please check:
1) **Hook lands in first 1.5s** (clear benefit, surprise, or contrarian angle)
2) **Captions are readable** (no cut-off, good contrast, low typo rate)
3) **Audio clarity** (no harsh pops; volume consistent)
4) **Framing** (subject centered; safe margins for UI overlays)
5) **Brand fit** (tone and phrasing align with you)
6) **CTA** (if included) is accurate and compliant

## How to request revisions (the format that gets fastest turnaround)
Reply to the delivery email and include:
- **Clip filename** (example: `CLIP_03_TikTok_1080x1920_v1.mp4`)
- **Timestamp(s)** (example: `00:04–00:07`)
- **Exact change request** (choose one):
  - Caption text change: “Replace ‘X’ with ‘Y’”
  - Caption timing: “Shift caption earlier by ~0.5s”
  - Hook swap: “Use the next sentence as the first line”
  - Remove word/phrase for compliance: “Remove ‘guarantee’ at 00:05”
  - Crop/framing: “Zoom out 10%”

**Best practice:** bundle all notes for a clip into **one message** to avoid delays.

## What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **execution fixes** that don’t change the core edit direction:
- Caption typos, small wording tweaks
- Minor caption timing adjustments
- Minor crop/framing changes
- Audio leveling tweaks
- Removing/bleeping a word for compliance

## What is NOT included as a revision (would be a new clip request)
These are “new direction” requests:
- “Choose a totally different moment from the episode”
- “Make it a different topic angle”
- “Re-edit the clip into a new storyline”

If you want swaps, we can do **clip replacements** using your unused highlights (when available) during the free trial period.

## Turnaround + approval timing
- Please send revision notes within **24 hours of delivery**.
- We aim to return included revisions within **24 hours**.
- If we don’t hear back in **48 hours**, we’ll mark the batch as **approved** so we can keep your cadence moving.
