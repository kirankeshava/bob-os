## 4) 24‑Hour “Sample Clip Offer” Fulfillment SOP (internal)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 free sample vertical clip within 24 hours to maximize close rate for Starter/Growth packages.

**Tools (free/low cost options):**
- Source: YouTube link / Google Drive
- Notes: Google Doc
- Delivery: Google Drive folder link
- Comms: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

**Step 0 — Intake (5 minutes)**
Ask for:
- Episode link (YouTube/Drive)
- Target platform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Niche + audience (1 sentence)
- Preferred tone (direct/educational/story)
- Any caption style preferences (big/high-contrast/minimal)

**Step 1 — Find 3 candidate moments (20 minutes)**
- Skim for: strong claim, contrarian insight, numbered steps, emotional story, clear takeaway.
- Select 3 time ranges (15–45s).

**Step 2 — Choose 1 moment + write hooks (10 minutes)**
- Pick the most “stops the scroll” moment.
- Write 2 hook variants:
  - Hook A: curiosity/contrarian
  - Hook B: promise/result

**Step 3 — Edit (30–60 minutes)**
- Format: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Pacing: remove dead air, add jump cuts
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast, 2 lines max, highlight key words
- Add b‑roll cues as on-screen notes if needed (e.g., “[Overlay: screenshot of …]”)

**Step 4 — Export + QA (10 minutes)**
- Export MP4, 15–45 seconds
- Check: readable captions, no cutoff faces, audio clear, safe margins.

**Step 5 — Deliver + close (5 minutes)**
Email subject: “Your sample clip is ready (24h) — want 8–20 more?”
Body:
- Drive link
- 2 hook options included
- CTA: “If you want a batch this week: Starter ($299/8 clips) or Growth ($599/20 clips). Reply ‘STARTER’ or ‘GROWTH’ and I’ll send the intake + next steps.”

**Quality bar (acceptance):**
- Delivered within 24 hours
- Captions readable on mobile at arm’s length
- First 1.5 seconds has a clear hook
- No awkward pauses; clean start/end
