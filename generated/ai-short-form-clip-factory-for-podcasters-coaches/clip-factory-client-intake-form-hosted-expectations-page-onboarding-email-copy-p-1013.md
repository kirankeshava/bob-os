Page title: Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery)

Intro:
Welcome to Clip Factory. We turn long-form video/podcast content into ready-to-post vertical clips with hooks, captions, b-roll cues, and platform-friendly formatting.

Legitimacy + support:

Step 1 — Intake (required to start)
Before we begin, please complete the Client Intake Form. This locks in your caption style, hook preferences, banned words, platforms, and CTA.
Important: We start production after intake submission (unless you explicitly waive intake in writing).

Step 2 — Editing + QA standards (what we check)
Every clip is checked for:
- No cropped faces / heads cut off; subject stays in safe zones for TikTok/Reels/Shorts UI
- Caption accuracy (names, numbers, keywords) and readable pacing
- Audio leveling (voice clear, no clipping/peaking, balanced loudness)
- Hook clarity in the first 1–2 seconds
- Clean jump cuts (no awkward mid-word cuts; remove dead air)
- Export settings appropriate for vertical platforms (1080x1920, H.264, 30fps unless source requires otherwise)

Step 3 — Review flow (timestamp comments)
You will receive a review link or a review folder.
How to request changes:
1) Use timestamped notes (preferred). Example: “00:07 change ‘5%’ to ‘15%’” or “00:12 remove this sentence.”
2) If you can’t timestamp, describe the exact line on screen.
3) Consolidate feedback into one message when possible.

Revision window:
You have 24 hours from the time we send the review link/finals to request revisions included in your package. After 24 hours, changes move into a new paid revision round (unless the issue is clearly our error).

Step 4 — Delivery Pack (what you receive)
You receive a Delivery Pack folder containing:
- Final MP4(s) (ready-to-post vertical)
- SRT caption file(s)
- B-roll cue sheet (if requested)
- Posting suggestions per clip:
  - First-line caption
  - 5–15 hashtags (platform-appropriate)
  - Optional CTA suggestions

Step 5 — What counts as a free revision vs paid change
Included (free revisions):
- Typos or caption mishears that don’t match the audio
- Incorrect on-screen text (names, numbers) when you provided the correct info
- Cropping/safe-zone mistakes (cutting off faces, UI overlap)
- Audio issues introduced during editing (peaking, noise added, wrong track)
- Timing tweaks to fix readability or abrupt cuts

Billable changes (paid):
- Changing clip selection/topic direction after approval
- Adding new clips beyond the agreed quantity
- Rewriting hooks/CTAs due to new preferences not provided in intake
- Adding heavy motion graphics/styles not agreed upfront
- Brand repositioning or new banned-words lists after delivery

If you’re unsure, email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with the clip name + timestamps and we’ll classify it within 1 business day.
