## A) 100-Lead Sourcing SOP (Podcasters + Coaches)
### ICP filters (use to qualify quickly)
- Has long-form video/podcast episodes (YouTube, Spotify, Apple, Riverside uploads)
- Posts at least weekly (or has a backlog)
- Clear monetization: coaching, course, consulting, SaaS, agency, info-product
- Low-to-mid production team signals: inconsistent shorts, few clips, or basic captions

### Where to source (free)
1) YouTube (primary)
- Search queries:
  - "podcast" + "episode" + "coach"
  - "(niche) podcast" (e.g., "real estate podcast", "fitness podcast", "marketing podcast")
  - "live coaching" + "episode"
  - "founder podcast" + "episode"
- Filter: Upload date = last month; Duration = 20+ minutes.
- Qualify: Channel has 10+ long videos OR a podcast playlist.

2) Apple Podcasts / Spotify directories
- Search niche categories and grab shows that mention coaching/consulting.
- Find show website/IG/LinkedIn via show notes.

3) LinkedIn
- Search: "podcast host" AND (coach OR founder OR consultant)
- Look for recent episode posts with low repurposing.

4) Instagram / TikTok
- Search hashtags: #podcastclips #podcastmarketing #businesspodcast #coachlife
- Identify accounts posting occasional clips (inconsistent = opportunity).

### Lead capture rules
Capture 100 leads in batches of 25.
For each lead, collect:
- Name
- Brand/show
- Platform link (YouTube/Podcast)
- Contact method (email/IG/LinkedIn)
- Proof of pain (e.g., “only 1 clip in last 14 days”, “no captions”, “horizontal only”)
- Last episode link (for sample)
- Offer fit score (1–5)

### Fit scoring (1–5)
5 = long-form weekly + sells coaching + poor/inconsistent shorts
3 = long-form exists but inconsistent posting or unclear monetization
1 = no long-form assets or no business outcome
