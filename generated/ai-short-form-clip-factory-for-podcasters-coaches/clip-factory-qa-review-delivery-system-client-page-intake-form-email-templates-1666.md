## 1) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch of clips we deliver a folder containing:

2. **Caption files (SRT)** — one per clip, matching the final MP4.

4. **Posting suggestions** — recommended first-line caption + CTA + hashtags by platform.

## 2) Our QA checklist (what we check before you ever see it)
### A) Framing & “safe zones”
- Video is **9:16 (1080×1920)**.
- Subject’s face is not cropped; headroom is consistent; no “forehead cut.”
- Captions and key on-screen text avoid typical UI overlays:
  - Keep primary text **center-safe** (avoid bottom ~15% and extreme edges).
  - Avoid placing critical text where TikTok/Reels buttons typically cover.

### B) Captions accuracy & readability
- Captions match spoken words (names/brands corrected).
- No hallucinated words or missing phrases; punctuation is sensible.
- Readability rules:
  - Max ~2 lines on screen at once (when possible).
  - High-contrast text (stroke/shadow) for bright backgrounds.
  - Words appear on time; no long lag behind speaker.

### C) Audio leveling
- Speech is clearly intelligible; no clipped peaks.
- Volume is consistent across clips.
- If music is used: it sits under voice (no competition).
- No distracting background noise; basic cleanup applied where needed.

### D) Editing quality (jump cuts, pacing, hook)
- First 1–2 seconds contain a **clear hook** (question, contrarian statement, result, or tension).
- Cuts feel intentional; no jarring mid-word cuts.
- Dead air removed; pacing matches platform norms.
- Clip has a “point” (setup → payoff) and ends cleanly.

### E) On-screen elements
- Any emphasized words, callouts, or subtitles are spelled correctly.
- If you provided branding (colors, handle, CTA), it is applied consistently.
- No copyrighted logos/music added unless explicitly requested by client.

### F) Export settings (baseline)
- Format: **MP4 (H.264)**
- Resolution: **1080×1920**
- FPS: **30** (unless source is clearly different and needs matching)
- Audio: **AAC, 48kHz**
- Bitrate: high enough to keep captions/text crisp (avoid blocky compression).

## 3) Review flow (fast, timestamped, low-friction)
We send a **review link** (Frame.io free, Vimeo review, or unlisted YouTube) so you can leave **timestamp comments**.

### How to leave feedback
- Please comment **at the exact timestamp** (e.g., “00:07 change ‘coach’ to ‘consultant’”).
- If you have multiple notes, list them per timestamp.
- Avoid vague feedback like “make it pop”—tell us what to change (hook wording, caption style, cropping, etc.).

### Revision window
- **24 hours** from delivery of the review link.
- We start revisions once we receive consolidated notes.

## 4) Revisions & scope (refund protection + speed)
### Free revisions (included)
Free revisions cover mistakes or mismatches relative to your intake:
- Caption typos, missed words, incorrect names.
- Cropping/frame issues (cropped face, bad safe-zone placement).
- Audio level inconsistencies.
- Small pacing tweaks that do not change the clip’s concept.
- Export issues (wrong format, wrong aspect ratio).

### Paid changes (out of scope)
(Week 1 note: we may still do these for free as a goodwill gesture, but we track them as *scope changes*.)
- Changing the **topic/angle** of the clip after delivery.
- Re-selecting entirely new moments from the long-form (new clip picks).
- Adding heavy motion graphics beyond the agreed style.
- Major re-write of hook/structure across many clips.
- Brand overhaul after you previously approved style.

### If something is urgent
Email **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** with:
- Project name
- Clip filename
- Timestamp + requested change
- Deadline (if any)
