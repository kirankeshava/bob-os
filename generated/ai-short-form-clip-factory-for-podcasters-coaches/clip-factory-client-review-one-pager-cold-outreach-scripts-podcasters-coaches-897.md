Send revisions to **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** using:

- Clip_09 — Timestamp: 00:12–00:15 — Change: [b-roll suggestion: “…”]

**Global change (optional):**
- Captions: [bigger/smaller] / [move higher/lower] / [different keyword highlight color]
- CTA: [replace with: “…”]

## What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **small edits** that keep the original clip concept:
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, small rewording)
- Hook line swap (1–2 lines)
- Move/trim a clip by a few seconds
- Replace b-roll cue(s) or remove a cue
- Adjust caption position, highlight color, emoji usage (if any), or emphasis

## What is considered a new clip (not a revision)
These usually require a new clip slot or an add-on:
- Changing the **topic/angle** of the clip completely
- Asking us to create **additional clips** beyond the 10 delivered
- Recutting from a different section not included in the clip

## Revision SLA
- **1 revision round included** (up to 10 clips) if requested within **48 hours of delivery**.
- Revisions delivered within **24 hours** after we receive your feedback in the format above.

## If you want us to “pick for you”
Reply with: **“Approved — ship as-is”** or **“Approved with global change: [single change]”**.
