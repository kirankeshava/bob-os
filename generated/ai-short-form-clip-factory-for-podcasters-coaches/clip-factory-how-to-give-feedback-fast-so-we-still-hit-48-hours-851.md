How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Still Hit 48 Hours)

Thanks for working with Clip Factory. Our promise is delivery of 10 ready-to-post vertical clips within 48 hours of receiving your source file(s) + brand assets. The fastest way to hit that deadline is clear, structured feedback.

Where to leave feedback
1) Preferred: Frame.io comments (time-stamped)
2) Backup: Google Drive video comments (time-stamped)
3) Fallback: Email reply to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with timestamps

The only feedback format we can act on (copy/paste)
For each requested change, please include:
- Clip ID: (example: CF_ACME_E12_C03)
- Timestamp: (example: 00:07–00:10)
- Category: Hook / Captions / Cut / B-roll / On-screen text / CTA / Audio
- Action: exactly what you want changed
- Optional: replacement wording (if copy changes)

Example
Clip ID: CF_ACME_E12_C03
Timestamp: 00:00–00:02
Category: Hook
Action: Replace the first line of on-screen text with: “3 mistakes first-time buyers make” and start from the sentence “Most people…” (remove the first phrase).

What counts as an “in-scope” revision (fast)
Included revisions (1 round):
- Caption typo fixes, punctuation, minor phrasing
- Tightening/shortening within the same selected moment
- Swapping the hook text overlay while keeping the same clip segment
- Adjusting b-roll cues within our existing library options
- Small repositioning of captions or safe-zone adjustments

What is out-of-scope (slows delivery)
These are treated as new work and may require a new order or extended timeline:
- Re-picking entirely new moments for multiple clips (new highlight selection)
- Changing the brand style after editing begins (new fonts/colors/template)
- Heavy audio repair beyond basic cleanup
- Adding custom motion graphics beyond the preset style pack

Timing rules to protect the 48-hour SLA
- Feedback window: Please send revision notes within 24 hours of receiving the first delivery.
- If we don’t receive feedback within 24 hours, we will assume approval and close the delivery as “Final v1.” (You can still request changes later, but it may be scheduled as a new revision cycle.)

How we label versions
- V1 = initial delivery
- V1R1 = revised version after your first (included) revision round
- V2+ = additional revision cycles (if needed)

What to do if you’re busy (quick approval options)
If you don’t have time to review everything, reply with ONE of these:
A) “Approved — post-ready”
B) “Approve all except clips: C02, C07 (notes coming)”
C) “Hold — do not post yet, need compliance review (ETA: ___)”

Compliance / claims reminder
If your industry requires compliance review (medical, financial, legal, real estate claims, etc.), please flag it in your feedback and provide the exact wording you prefer. We can edit quickly, but we can’t guess compliance boundaries.
