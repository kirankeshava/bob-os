C) WTP SURVEY (ASK VERBATIM; CAPTURE NUMERIC)

Use 1–5 scales + explicit dollars.
1) Volume: “How many clips/week would you realistically post if editing friction went away?” (number)
2) Pain ranking: “Rank your top 3 pains: (a) finding highlights (b) hook writing (c) captions (d) editing time (e) review/approval (f) brand consistency (g) posting/scheduling.”
3) Must-have outputs: “Which outputs do you require? (9:16 render, SRT captions, burned-in captions, headline/hook, b-roll suggestions, hashtags, platform variants).”
4) Review workflow: “Do you need approval links and comments (yes/no)? How many stakeholders?”
5) Diarization: “Do you need speaker labels and per-speaker caption styling? (never / sometimes / always).”
6) Brand voice hooks: “If a tool generated 10 hook options in your tone, how valuable is that (1–5)?”
7) Niche templates: “Would you pay extra for templates tailored to your niche (fitness, real estate, SaaS, therapy, business coaching) (yes/no)?”
8) Integration needs: “Where should clips land automatically? (Drive/Dropbox, Frame.io, Slack, Notion, Zapier).”
9) Price anchoring: “If this saved you 6–10 hours/week and increased posting consistency, what would you pay per month? Choose one: $49 / $99 / $149 / $299 / $499+.”
10) Dealbreaker: “What would make you cancel in month 1?”
