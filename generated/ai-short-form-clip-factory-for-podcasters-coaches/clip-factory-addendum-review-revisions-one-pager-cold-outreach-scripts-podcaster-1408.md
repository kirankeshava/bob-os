CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW & REQUEST REVISIONS (FAST)

Thanks for choosing Clip Factory. To keep the 48-hour turnaround intact and make feedback easy, please review your clips using the steps below.

Where your clips arrive
• You’ll receive a delivery email from agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with:
  1) A download link (or folder link) containing all MP4s
  2) A “Clip List” summary (titles + timestamps + suggested captions/hashtags)
  3) Any notes about hooks/CTAs and platform versions
• You can verify our website here (for internal/vendor approval):
  https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

How to review (recommended)
1) Watch each clip twice:
   • Pass 1 (gut check): Is this the right moment? Is it compelling?
   • Pass 2 (details): Captions accuracy, pacing, CTA, framing, and any brand terms.
2) Check captions on mute. If it reads cleanly without audio, it will perform better.
3) Confirm the first 1.5 seconds: hook is clear and the “point” is obvious.
4) Look for pacing: something changes every ~2–3 seconds (zoom, cut, b-roll cue, text emphasis).

What counts as a revision (included)
We include 1 revision round for the batch (up to 10 clips) within 7 days of delivery.
Included revisions:
• Caption corrections (typos, names, terminology)
• Small timing trims (tighten dead air, remove a phrase, shorten intro)
• On-screen text edits (hook wording, headline emphasis)
• CTA swap (e.g., “Book a call” → “Download the guide”)
• Minor framing tweaks (reposition captions, safe area adjustments)

What is NOT included (new work)
These require a new request/quote because they change the scope/time materially:
• Re-selecting completely different moments for multiple clips
• Adding brand-new b-roll that must be sourced/licensed
• Rebuilding the style into a different niche preset after delivery
• Multiple iterative rounds beyond the included revision round

How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to the delivery email with the following format to keep turnaround fast:
Subject: Revisions — [Brand] — [Episode/Recording Name]

For each clip:
• File name: (example) ACME_EP12_C03_v1_IG.mp4
• Timecode: 00:00–00:07 (or “entire clip”)
• Change request (1 sentence):
• Exact replacement text (if applicable):

Example:
• File name: ACME_EP12_C03_v1_TT.mp4
• Timecode: 00:02–00:05
• Change request: Replace “CRM” with “client management system” and remove filler word.
• Replacement text: “Client management system”

Revision turnaround
• Most revision rounds ship within 24 hours (often faster) if feedback is sent in the format above.
