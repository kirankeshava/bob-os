B) 6 CLIP PULLS (2 PER NICHE) — STYLES + EDIT DIRECTIONS
Export specs for ALL:
- 1080x1920 (9:16), 30–60s
- Burned-in captions (large, high-contrast)
- Safe margins: keep text centered and above UI zones
- Add subtle punch-zooms every 1.5–2.5s on emphasis words
- Add b-roll overlays when the speaker references an example (house images for RE, exercise form shots for fitness, dashboards/meetings for B2B)
- End card (last 1.0–1.5s): “Sample clip by AI Clip Factory” + website + email

REAL ESTATE (RE)
RE Clip #1 — Storytelling hook (30–45s)
- Timestamp target: a segment where the speaker tells a short story about a buyer/seller mistake or a market surprise.
- Hook (on-screen, first 1.5s): “They almost lost the house over THIS…”
- Captions: emphasize words like “inspection”, “appraisal”, “rate lock”, “deadline”.
- Edit beats:
  1) Cold open on the punchline phrase (start mid-sentence if needed)
  2) Quick cut back 0.5–1s to set context
  3) 2–3 punch-zooms on key terms
- B-roll cues:
  - When “inspection/appraisal” mentioned: overlay inspection checklist / clipboard
  - When “rates” mentioned: overlay simple rate chart
- CTA text (last 3s): “Want 3–5 clips/week from your podcast? → agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

RE Clip #2 — Contrarian take (35–60s)
- Timestamp target: segment where they challenge common advice (e.g., “don’t wait for rates”, “open houses aren’t for selling”).
- Hook: “Unpopular opinion: most ‘home buying tips’ are wrong.”
- Caption styling: highlight “Unpopular opinion” in a contrasting color.
- Edit beats:
  - Add 0.2s jump cuts to remove breaths
  - Insert a 0.5s pattern interrupt: bold text card “STOP DOING THIS”
- B-roll cues:
  - Overlay ‘myth’ stamp when stating the common advice
  - Overlay ‘truth’ stamp when stating the real recommendation
