Title: How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Hit the 48-Hour Turnaround)

Thanks for working with Clip Factory. To keep delivery on schedule, we need fast, clear feedback. This one-pager shows exactly how to review clips and respond so we can ship your 10 clips within 48 hours.

Our “fast feedback” rules (please follow these)
1) Use timestamps only
- Good: “Clip 03 @ 00:04—remove ‘um’ and tighten pause.”
- Not good: “Clip 3 feels off.” (We can’t action this quickly.)

2) One consolidated message
- Send ONE message with all notes (email reply or Frame.io comments). Multiple threads slow the queue and risk missing the 48-hour window.

3) Must-fix vs Nice-to-have
- Label each note:
  - MUST-FIX (required for publish): factual error, compliance risk, wrong name/title, offensive content, broken captions, incorrect framing.
  - NICE-TO-HAVE (only if time permits): alternative hook, different emoji, new b-roll idea, slightly different pacing.

4) Keep it bounded
- Max 10 total notes across all clips per revision round.
- If you want a larger creative re-direction (new editing style, new tone, new hook strategy across all clips), that’s a new scope/episode.

5) Decide on the “house style” quickly
If you’re not sure, tell us ONE of these:
- “Prioritize maximum retention (faster cuts, stronger hooks).”
- “Prioritize clarity (slower pacing, fewer cuts).”
- “Prioritize authority (clean, minimal, fewer effects).”
We’ll apply it consistently across the batch.

What we need from you (checklist)
- Confirm platform: TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts (or all).
- Confirm caption preference: larger/louder vs clean/minimal.
- Provide any do-not-use words/topics (brand or compliance).
- Provide correct spelling of names + brand terms.

Response timeline (to protect the SLA)
- Feedback window: Please reply within 12 hours of receiving the review link.
- If we don’t hear back: We will proceed with “silent approval” and deliver the current version to stay inside 48 hours.
- If feedback arrives after the window: We’ll still incorporate it, but the 48-hour turnaround may shift.
