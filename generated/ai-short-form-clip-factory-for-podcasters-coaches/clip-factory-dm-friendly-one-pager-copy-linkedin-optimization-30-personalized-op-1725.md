- Audience + what they sell

- Any words/topics to avoid
- Brand references (2 accounts they like)

Reply template (same day):
“Got it — I’ll send 1 sample vertical clip within 24h. If you like it, I’ll propose a weekly package (10/20/30 clips) based on your posting goals. Official page: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3”

### 2) Clip selection (20–40 minutes)
Pick a segment that:
- Has a clear promise or contrarian hook in first 1–2 seconds
- Contains one complete idea in 15–45 seconds
- Ends with a takeaway or CTA

Write a hook option set (choose 2):
- “Most people get X wrong. Do this instead.”
- “If you’re struggling with X, this is why.”
- “Here’s the fastest way to X (without Y).”

### 3) Captioning rules (10–25 minutes)
- 2 lines max on screen
- Break on meaning, not on word count
- Emphasize 1–3 keywords per screen
- Keep safe margins for TikTok UI

### 4) Export rules (10–20 minutes)
- 1080x1920
- Loudness normalized (avoid peaks)
- Burned-in captions + clean font
- Filename: CLIENT_topic_platform_v1.mp4

### 5) Delivery (5 minutes)
Email subject: “Your sample clip (vertical + captions) — 24h turnaround”
Body:
- Link to download
- 2 alternate hook lines (text)
- 2 suggested captions for posting
- Ask: “Want 10 like this per week or 20?”
- Include official page + contact email again

### 6) Close (same thread)
If positive reply, send 2 options:
- “Starter: 10 clips/week”
- “Growth: 20 clips/week”
Ask for: posting cadence, turnaround needs, and preferred topics next week.

**Success metric:** Sample delivered <24h; conversion ask sent immediately; track outcome in CRM (Sample sent / Won / Lost + reason).
