A) 24-HOUR “SAMPLE CLIP SPRINT” SOP (DFY)
Goal: deliver 1 finished vertical clip (9:16) within 24 hours of receiving a link + minimal brand notes. Purpose is to win trust and convert to a paid package.

1) Qualify + Confirm (0–10 minutes)
- Confirm source: YouTube link / Google Drive / Riverside / Zoom file.
- Confirm platform priority: TikTok, Reels, Shorts (default: Reels+TikTok safe).
- Confirm topic/ICP: “Who is this for?” (1 sentence).
- Confirm CTA: follow, comment keyword, book call, download lead magnet.
- Confirm style: “clean captions” vs “high-energy jump cuts” (default: clean).

2) Intake Minimums (send in one message)
Ask for:
- Link to the episode/video + timestamp range if they have a favorite moment (optional)
- Name/pronunciation + 3 keywords they want on-screen (optional)
- Brand colors + font preference (optional; default to white text with subtle shadow)
- Their handle + URL/CTA

3) Clip Selection (10–25 minutes)
Pick ONE segment that:
- Has a clear hook in first 1–2 seconds (problem, contrarian take, shocking stat, question)
- Is 20–45 seconds (ok 15–60)
- Has minimal cross-talk
- Has a single idea (no context needed)
Write a hook overlay variant if the speaker’s first sentence isn’t a hook.

4) Edit Steps (45–120 minutes)
- Create 9:16 sequence (1080x1920, 30fps).
- Reframe speaker (center, eyes on top third). If 2 speakers, use alternating crops or split-screen.
- Remove dead air >250ms when safe; keep natural cadence.
- Add hard-burned captions (large, high contrast, 2 lines max). Highlight 1–3 keywords.
- Light cleanup: normalize audio (-14 LUFS target), mild noise reduction, limiter.
- Add minimal motion: 2–4% scale/punch-ins on emphasis.
- Optional b-roll cue (only if it truly clarifies): 1–2 quick overlays max.

5) QC Checklist (10 minutes)
- First frame/hook: does it make sense with no context?
- Captions: 99% accurate, no awkward line breaks, no covering face.
- Safe zones: captions not cut off on IG/TikTok.
- Audio: no clipping, consistent volume.
- Branding: handle/CTA present last 2–3 seconds (or pinned in caption suggestion).

6) Deliverable Package
Send:
- MP4 vertical (H.264, 15–20 Mbps)
- Caption text (SRT or plain text)
- 2 suggested post captions + 5 hashtags
- Optional: recommended posting time window (based on their audience if known)

7) Delivery Message (copy)
Subject/DM: Your sample clip is ready
“Made a first vertical cut from your episode. Here’s the MP4 + captions. If you want, I can produce 10–20 like this per month and keep the style consistent. Want me to propose a 2-week starter package based on your posting goals?”

8) Conversion Path (Upsell)
If they like it, offer:
- Starter: 10 clips / 7 days
- Growth: 20 clips / 14 days
- Partner: 40 clips / month
(Keep pricing handled by sales owner; this SOP is operational.)
