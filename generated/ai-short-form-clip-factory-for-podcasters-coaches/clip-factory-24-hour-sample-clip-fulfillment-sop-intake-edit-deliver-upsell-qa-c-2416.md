## 24-Hour Workflow (Time-Boxed)
### Step 1 — Confirm receipt (5 minutes)
Send a confirmation with ETA + one clarifying question if needed.

### Step 2 — Find 2–3 candidate moments (20–30 minutes)
Criteria:
- Strong emotion or clear “aha”
- Specificity (numbers, frameworks, mistakes, contrarian take)
- Can stand alone without deep context
- Ideally 20–45 seconds for TikTok/Reels; up to 60 seconds acceptable

### Step 3 — Select hook + structure (10 minutes)
Pick one moment and shape into:
- Hook (first 1–2 seconds): bold claim/question
- Proof/value (middle): the insight
- Close (last 2–5 seconds): CTA or “follow for X”

### Step 4 — Edit vertical 9:16 (45–75 minutes)
- Canvas: 1080x1920
- Safe margins: keep captions and key visuals away from bottom UI
- Layout options:
  A) Speaker full-frame + subtle punch-ins every 2–4 seconds
  B) Speaker top + relevant b-roll lower third (optional)
  C) Split: speaker + keyword/bullet callouts
- Add quick cuts to remove dead air (no awkward pauses)

### Step 5 — Captions + emphasis (30–45 minutes)
- Generate auto captions then **manually correct** proper nouns, jargon, and numbers.
- Style:
  - 1–2 lines max
  - Highlight 1–3 keywords per sentence (color or bold)
  - Keep reading speed comfortable (no walls of text)

### Step 6 — Add “b-roll cues” (10 minutes)
If not adding b-roll directly, add on-screen cues or a notes file:
- Example: “[B-ROLL: stock footage of busy gym]”
- Keep it minimal; only where it increases clarity or retention.

### Step 7 — Export + naming (5 minutes)
Export settings:
- MP4 (H.264)
- 1080x1920
- 30fps
- High bitrate (8–16 Mbps)
File name convention:
- ClientName_EpisodeTopic_Clip01_30s.mp4

### Step 8 — QA checklist (10 minutes)
Run the checklist below before delivery.

### Step 9 — Deliver + ask 3 feedback questions (5 minutes)
Deliver via Drive link and ask for a fast yes/no + one improvement.

## QA Checklist (Must Pass)
- [ ] First 1–2 seconds makes sense without context
- [ ] Captions are accurate (names, numbers, key terms)
- [ ] Captions are within safe margins (not blocked by UI)
- [ ] Audio levels consistent; no sudden spikes
- [ ] No long pauses; pacing feels modern
- [ ] Visual framing is centered; face not cropped awkwardly
- [ ] CTA (if included) is readable and brief
- [ ] Export is 1080x1920, MP4, plays on mobile

## Revision Policy (Prevents Scope Creep)
- Sample clip: 1 light revision (caption fixes + 1 hook tweak).
- Paid packages: 1 revision round per clip bundle unless otherwise agreed.
- Major changes (new segment, different topic, full re-cut) = treated as a new clip.

## Upsell Path (After Delivery)

- Ask what frequency they want (3/wk, daily, etc.)
- Offer a starter package with clear turnaround
- Ask what would make this “hands-off” for them (this feeds SaaS roadmap)
