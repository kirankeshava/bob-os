## MVP Clip List (6 total)
Each clip should be **20–35 seconds**. Keep captions large with safe margins.

### Real Estate (2 clips)
**RE-01 — Storytelling hook**
- Hook style: “I almost lost $___ on my first deal—here’s what saved it.”
- Edit: 1.1x–1.2x speed, remove pauses, 3–5 jump cuts.
- Captions: highlight money/time words (e.g., “$10k”, “48 hours”).
- B-roll cues (on-screen small text, bottom-right): “B‑ROLL: house exterior”, “overlay: contract closeup”.
- Filename: `RE-01_story_hook_1080x1920.mp4`

**RE-02 — Contrarian take**
- Hook style: “Stop staging your listings like THIS. Do this instead.”
- Edit: punch-in zoom on “Stop” + 1 hard pattern break (sound/zoom).
- Captions: emphasize negations (“STOP”, “DON’T”).
- B-roll cues: “B‑ROLL: messy vs staged room”, “overlay: checklist”.
- Filename: `RE-02_contrarian_1080x1920.mp4`

### Fitness (2 clips)
**FT-01 — Listicle**
- Hook style: “3 mistakes keeping your fat loss stuck (and the fix).”
- Structure: #1 #2 #3 with on-screen counter.
- Edit: cut on each number reveal; add subtle whoosh between points.
- Captions: emphasize numbers and the fix phrase.
- B-roll cues: “B‑ROLL: grocery aisle”, “overlay: macro chart”.
- Filename: `FT-01_listicle_1080x1920.mp4`

**FT-02 — Testimonial-style (creator talking about client result)**
- Hook style: “My client didn’t change workouts—only THIS—and lost ___.”
- Edit: insert 1–2 screenshot placeholders as b-roll cues.
- Captions: emphasize result metric (lbs/inches/time).
- B-roll cues: “B‑ROLL: progress photo blur”, “overlay: client check-in text”.
- Filename: `FT-02_testimonial_1080x1920.mp4`

### B2B (2 clips)
**B2B-01 — Story hook**
- Hook style: “We fixed churn with one onboarding email—here’s the template.”
- Edit: add quick overlay of an email layout (as cue).
- Captions: emphasize “one email”, “churn”, “template”.
- B-roll cues: “overlay: email wireframe”, “B‑ROLL: dashboard metric”.
- Filename: `B2B-01_story_hook_1080x1920.mp4`

**B2B-02 — Contrarian / POV**
- Hook style: “Cold email doesn’t work when you personalize like this.”
- Structure: wrong way → right way (two-part contrast).
- Edit: split-screen moment or quick title cards “WRONG / RIGHT”.
- Captions: emphasize “WRONG” “RIGHT”.
- B-roll cues: “overlay: subject line examples”, “B‑ROLL: inbox view”.
- Filename: `B2B-02_contrarian_1080x1920.mp4`
