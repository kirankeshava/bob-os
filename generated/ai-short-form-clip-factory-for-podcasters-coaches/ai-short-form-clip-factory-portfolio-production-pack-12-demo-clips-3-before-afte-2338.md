B) GLOBAL STYLE GUIDE (apply to all 12 clips)
Format: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps, loudness normalized, burned-in captions.
Safe margins: keep text within center 80% width; avoid bottom 15% (UI overlays).
Caption style: 2 lines max, 5–7 words per line, highlight 1–2 keywords in a contrasting color.
Pacing: cut every 1.0–2.5s (either jump cut, punch-in, or b-roll overlay).
B-roll cue language: [BROLL: …] indicates overlay suggestion; do not require licensed footage—use free stock (Pexels/Pixabay) or on-screen keywords.
