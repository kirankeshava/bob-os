  What you want instead: Fix the word “their” (currently shows “there”) and remove the comma.
- Clip File Name: EP12_C07_PricingMindset_v1_IG
  Timecode(s): 00:00–00:02
  Issue Type: hook
  What you want instead: Replace the first on-screen hook with “You’re undercharging and here’s why.”

What counts as a revision (included)
- Caption corrections (typos, punctuation, missed words)
- Minor timing tweaks (tighten dead air, shift cut by ~0.5–2 seconds)
- Swap hook text overlay (keeping the same underlying audio)
- Adjust caption position/safe margins for platform UI
- Replace CTA end card text (within existing template)

What is out of scope (requires a new request / next batch)
- Creating entirely new clips beyond the 10 delivered
- Rebuilding clips around different segments not previously selected
- Heavy motion graphics, custom animation packages, complex b-roll sourcing
- Major narrative restructuring (e.g., “make this a different point”)

Turnaround + limits (to protect speed)
