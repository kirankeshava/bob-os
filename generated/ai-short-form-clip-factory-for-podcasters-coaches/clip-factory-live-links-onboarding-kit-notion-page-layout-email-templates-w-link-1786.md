1) What you will receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch, we deliver a folder containing:
• Final clips (MP4) — vertical 9:16, platform-ready
• Captions file (SRT) — matches final video timing
• B-roll / on-screen cue sheet (Google Doc or PDF) — timecodes + suggestions
• Posting suggestions — first-line caption options + CTA + hashtag set(s)

2) Our QA standards (what we check before you see anything)
Framing & composition
• Safe zones: critical text and faces stay within TikTok/IG UI safe areas
• No cropped faces (forehead/chin cut-off). Subject stays centered during key moments
• Clean jump cuts: no distracting mid-word cuts, no flash frames

Captions
• Captions match the spoken words (names/brands corrected)
• Punctuation used for clarity; line breaks readable on mobile
• Caption placement avoids UI overlays and the speaker’s mouth/face

Audio
• Voice is clear and consistent across cuts
• No clipping/distortion; background noise minimized
• Music (if used) never competes with speech; consistent loudness

Hook clarity
• First 1–2 seconds includes a clear hook (question, bold claim, contrarian statement, or payoff)
• Clip has a single point; removes tangents and dead air

Export settings
• MP4 (H.264), 1080x1920 (9:16), 30 fps (unless source requires otherwise)
• Clean text rendering; no crushed blacks/over-sharpening

3) Review flow (fast + simple)
We send a review link (Frame.io, Vimeo, or unlisted YouTube).
How to leave feedback:
• Use timestamp comments (e.g., “00:12 change caption wording”)
• Group feedback per clip so we can resolve quickly

Revision window
• You have 24 hours from delivery of the review link to request revisions.
• If we don’t hear back in 24 hours, we assume approval and finalize/close the batch.

4) What counts as a free revision vs a change request
Free revisions (included)
• Caption fixes (typos, mis-hears, punctuation)
• Small timing adjustments (tighten pauses, micro cut tweaks)
• Minor reframes to prevent cropped faces / improve safe-zone compliance
• Audio leveling tweaks (voice a bit louder/softer)

Change requests (out of scope)
• New clip selection direction after clips are already cut (e.g., “use different moments entirely”)
• Major creative pivot (new style, new hook strategy, new brand voice)
• Adding new footage/assets not provided during intake
• Expanding scope beyond the agreed number of clips

If you’re unsure: email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with the timestamp notes and we’ll confirm what’s included before doing extra work.

5) How to get the best results (client responsibilities)
To avoid delays and revisions, please provide:
• Your target platform(s): TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
• Your handle(s) + preferred CTA link
• Words/topics to avoid + any compliance constraints
• Branding preferences: fonts/colors (if any) and whether you want emojis
