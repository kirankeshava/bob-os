====================
A) 30-DAY A/B TEST PLAN (Direct vs Friendly)
====================
Goal: Increase reply rate, sample-clip acceptance rate, and closes while keeping the CTA identical:
“Send your latest link and we’ll return 1 sample clip in 24h.”

North Star Metrics (track weekly):
1) Reply Rate = replies / messages sent
2) Link Rate = links received / replies
3) Sample Acceptance = “yes, make more” / sample delivered
4) Close Rate = paid clients / links received
5) Speed-to-First-Response = average hours from first message to reply

Test Design (simple + fast):
- Week 1: Use DIRECT tone for all outreach to podcasters + coaches.
- Week 2: Use FRIENDLY tone for all outreach to podcasters + coaches.
- Week 3: Keep the winning tone per niche (podcast vs coach). If unclear, split 50/50.
- Week 4: Add one variable ONLY: either “free sample framing” (value-first) OR “deliverable list” (clarity-first). Keep tone constant.

Rules to avoid muddy data:
- One niche at a time: tag every lead as PODCAST / COACH / BRAND.
- One tone at a time (unless you intentionally split 50/50).
- Keep CTA identical across all variants.
- Do not change pricing language mid-week.

Recommended Volume Targets (minimum):
- 10 new outreaches/day (Mon–Fri) = ~200/month.
- Aim for 20+ links received per month (enough to see what’s working).

Weekly Review (15 minutes every Friday):
- Which niche had higher reply rate?
- Which tone got more links?
- Which opening line patterns got “Sure—here’s the link” fastest?
- Write down the top 3 objections you saw and add/upgrade your canned responses.

- Sample Delivered Date

- Objection (Editor / AI quality / Price / Timing / Other)

- Deal Value ($)
- Notes (1–2 lines)

Statuses (dropdown):
- New → Messaged → Replied → Link Received → Sample Delivered → Follow-Up → Closed Won / Closed Lost / Nurture

SLA (speed rule):
