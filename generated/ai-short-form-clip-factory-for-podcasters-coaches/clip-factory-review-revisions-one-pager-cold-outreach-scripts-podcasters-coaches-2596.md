# Clip Factory — How to Review Clips & Request Revisions (Client One-Pager)

**What you’ll receive**
- **10 vertical clips** (TikTok/Reels/Shorts-ready) with **burned-in captions**, hook-first pacing, and basic formatting.
- A **delivery folder** containing:
  - `/EXPORTS/` final MP4s
  - `/NOTES/` a short doc with clip titles + source timestamps + posting suggestions

**How to review (fastest method)**
1) Watch all clips once at **1.25x speed**.
2) For each clip, decide: **Approve** or **Needs changes**.
3) If “Needs changes,” choose from the revision menu below and send your request in the exact format provided.

**Revision menu (what’s included)**
Included in the free launch trial (Week 1): **1 revision round per clip** for up to **3 clips** (to keep the 48-hour turnaround intact). Choose:
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, emphasis, profanity masking)
- Hook swap (use an alternate opening line from the same segment)
- Remove/trim filler words, tighten pacing
- Reframe (move subject left/right/up/down), safe margins for UI
- Add/adjust on-screen text callout (1–2 callouts)
- CTA end-card text swap

**Not included (requires a new request / longer timeline)**
- Full re-edit from a completely different section not in the selects
- Heavy VFX/motion graphics packages
- Licensed music sourcing (we can use platform-safe audio when you post)
