Section (H2): What You’ll Receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll receive a folder containing:
- Final MP4 clip(s) (vertical 9:16)
- Caption file(s) .SRT (per clip)
- B-roll Cue Sheet (timecoded suggestions)
- Posting Suggestions (first line caption + hashtags + notes)

Section (H2): Our QA Standards (What we check before delivery)
Video / framing:
- 9:16 framing, no cropped faces, safe margins for TikTok/Reels UI
- Speaker remains centered; reframes occur before the subject moves out of frame
- No accidental overlays, glitches, or pixelation

Captions:
- Captions match spoken words (names/brands verified where possible)
- No distracting typos; punctuation for readability
- Correct timing (no “late captions” or captions lingering after speech ends)

Audio:
- Voice is clear and consistent (no sudden level jumps)
- No harsh clipping/distortion
- Background noise minimized without “robotic” artifacts

Editing rhythm:
- Hook is clear in first 1–2 seconds
- Jump cuts feel natural; no awkward mid-word cuts
- Pacing matches platform norms (short pauses trimmed)

Export:
- MP4 (H.264), 1080x1920 preferred, 30fps (or source-matched), high bitrate
- File names follow the naming convention (see below)

Section (H2): Review Process (Timestamp Comments)
1) We send you a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube).
2) Please leave feedback using timestamped comments (e.g., “00:12 change caption” or “00:21 tighten pause”).
3) Consolidate feedback in one pass when possible to avoid delays.

Section (H2): Revision Window
- You have 24 hours from delivery of the review link to request free revisions covered under our policy.
- After 24 hours, changes may be treated as a new request depending on scope and scheduling.

Section (H2): What Counts as a Free Revision vs. Out of Scope
Free revisions (included):
- Caption typo fixes / misheard words
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, small trim)
- Minor reframing adjustments to keep faces safe
- Audio leveling consistency fixes

Out of scope (not included as free revision):
- Changing the hook/topic after delivery
- Rewriting captions for a new angle/voice after approval
- Adding new assets not provided initially (logos, product shots, new b-roll libraries)
- Major structural re-edit (different storyline, new clip selection)

Section (H2): How to Get Fast Turnaround on Revisions
- Use timestamp notes.
- Be specific: “Replace caption text at 00:08 with: ‘…’ ”
- Note platform preference if different (TikTok vs Reels style).
