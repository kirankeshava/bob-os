## 1) Client One-Pager — How to Review & Request Revisions (Clip Factory)

**Welcome!** To keep delivery fast and quality consistent, please review your clips using the steps below. This helps us turn revision requests around quickly and protects the 48-hour timeline.

**Official contact + legitimacy**
- Website proof: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Support email (send all requests here): agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### What you’ll receive
For each order (typically 10 clips):
1) **MP4 vertical clips** (9:16)
2) **A “Clip Notes” text file** (or message) that includes:
   - Original source timestamps
   - Hook/angle intention (why we chose it)
   - Any b-roll cues added
   - Suggested caption/description + 3–5 hashtags

### The fastest way to review (10–15 minutes total)
1) **Watch on your phone** (the way your audience will see it).
2) First pass: mark each clip as one of:
   - **APPROVE** (ready to post)
   - **MINOR FIX** (caption tweak, word correction, timing)
   - **REPLACE** (not on-brand, wrong angle, or you’d never post it)
3) Second pass: for any MINOR FIX / REPLACE, note the exact issue (see revision format below).

### Quality checks you should focus on (don’t overthink)
- **Hook clarity in first 1–1.5s**: Do you immediately know what it’s about?
- **Captions readability**: Not covering mouth too much; high contrast; no long lines.
- **Pacing**: No dead air; pattern interrupt every ~2–3 seconds (zoom/cut/b-roll cue).
- **Brand safety**: No claims you can’t make; no sensitive info.

### Revision policy (built for speed)
- **Included revisions:** Up to **1 revision round per clip** for **caption fixes, timing tweaks, punch-ins/zoom timing, removing filler words, and swapping b-roll cues**.
- **Not included (counts as replacement/new clip):** Changing the clip’s core idea/angle, requesting a different segment/topic, or rewriting the entire narrative. If you want this, mark the clip as **REPLACE** and tell us the direction.
- **Deadline to request revisions:** Within **48 hours of delivery** (so we can keep your queue moving).

### Exactly how to request revisions (copy/paste template)
Send one email to **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** with this format:

**Subject:** Revisions — [Your Brand] — [Episode/Source Name]

**Clip 01 — APPROVE / MINOR FIX / REPLACE**
- Timecode (if applicable): 00:00–00:18
- Issue: (e.g., “Caption misspells ‘Keller Williams’”, “Remove ‘um’ at 00:04”, “Hook needs to be more direct”)
- Exact change request: (1–2 bullets)

**Clip 02 — …**

If you want a replacement clip, add:
- Replacement direction: (e.g., “More tactical, avoid mindset”, “Must mention ‘free consultation’”, “Choose a segment about warm leads”)

### Approval and posting
When all clips are marked **APPROVE**, reply with:
- Which platform you’re posting to first (TikTok / IG / YT Shorts)
- Any CTA link/offer you want in the description

We’ll respond confirming final files and any updated exports.
