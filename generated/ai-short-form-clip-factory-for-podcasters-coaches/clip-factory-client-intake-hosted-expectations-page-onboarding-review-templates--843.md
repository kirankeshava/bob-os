SECTION (H2): Our Review Flow (Fast + Clear)
Step list:
1) We send a private review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube).
2) You leave timestamped comments (e.g., “00:07 change caption” or “00:14 trim pause”).
3) We apply revisions and deliver final files + folder pack.

Review tips:
- One comment per change, tied to a timestamp.
- If you want a different hook direction, provide 1–2 example hooks you like.

SECTION (H2): Revisions — What’s Included
Included free (within the revision window):
- Caption fixes (typos, missing words, punctuation, casing)
- Timing fixes (caption drift, early/late subtitles)
- Safe zone/framing fixes (cropped faces, cut-off text)
- Audio leveling tweaks (voice too quiet/loud, light cleanup)
- Minor trims to remove “um/uh” or dead air

Typically paid changes (quoted before work):
- New creative direction after approval (e.g., “change all hooks to a different angle”)
- Major re-edit/re-cut using different sections of the episode not previously requested
- Heavy sound repair (severe noise/echo) beyond basic cleanup
- New brand package implementation after clips are already produced
- Additional clips beyond the agreed quantity

SECTION (H2): Revision Window + Delivery SLA
- Revision window: 24 hours after the review link is sent (unless otherwise agreed in writing).
- After the window, additional changes may be scheduled/quoted as a new request.

SECTION (H2): Technical Standards (Quick)
- Export: MP4 (H.264), 1080x1920, 30fps (unless source requires otherwise)
- Audio: voice-centered mix, no clipping/peaking
- Composition: faces and key text kept inside safe zones (no cropped foreheads/chins)

4) INTERNAL GATING RULE (ADD TO SOP / JOB TICKET)
