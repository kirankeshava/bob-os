- Pacing (pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds)

**How to request revisions (fastest format)**
Reply to the delivery email and paste:
1) **Clip ID(s):** (e.g., `C03, C07`)
2) **Timestamp(s):** (e.g., `00:02–00:05`)
3) **Change request:** (pick one)
   - Text change (exact new wording)
   - Remove a line / shorten captions
   - Swap hook (we’ll choose alt from the same segment)
   - Blur/bleep a word
   - Tighten pacing (fewer pauses)
   - Adjust framing/zoom
4) **Priority:** must-fix vs nice-to-have

**Revision policy (to protect speed)**
- Includes **1 revision round** covering up to **10 clips**.

- **Revision turnaround:** typically **24 hours** after you send revision notes in the format above.
