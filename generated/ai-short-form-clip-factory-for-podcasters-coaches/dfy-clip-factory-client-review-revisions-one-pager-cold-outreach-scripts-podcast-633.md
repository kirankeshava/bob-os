## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Fast)

**Thanks for your order — here’s the fastest way to review and request revisions so we can keep the 48-hour pace.**

**What you’ll receive**
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + hook-focused opening
- Light b-roll cues (when applicable) and on-screen text emphasis
- A delivery note that includes: clip titles, suggested post copy ideas (optional), and any caveats (audio/noise, low-res sections, etc.)

**Where to review**
- You’ll receive either a review link (preferred) or a downloadable folder.
- If you need a different method, reply and tell us your preferred platform.

**How to leave feedback (required format)**
To keep revisions quick, please send feedback in this exact structure:
1) **Clip Name:** (example: `CLIP-03_The-Real-Reason-Youre-Not-Closing`)  
2) **Timestamp:** (example: `00:02–00:05`)  
3) **Change Type:** choose one: `Captions` / `Cut` / `On-screen text` / `CTA` / `B-roll` / `Format` / `Audio`  
4) **Exact instruction:** (example: “Replace ‘buyers’ with ‘first-time buyers’ and keep the highlight color.”)

**Examples of good feedback**
- “CLIP-02 00:00–00:02 — Cut 0.5s earlier so it starts on ‘Here’s the mistake…’”
- “CLIP-07 00:04–00:06 — Captions: fix spelling of ‘diarization’ → ‘diarization’.”
- “CLIP-10 end card — Replace CTA line with: ‘DM ME ‘PLAN’ for the template.’”

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Included revisions are **small, specific changes** to the delivered clips, such as:
- Caption typo fixes, minor timing shifts, removing filler words
- Swapping hook text, CTA text, or replacing 1–2 words on-screen
- Slightly tighter cut (a few seconds), audio leveling tweaks
- Moving caption position up/down slightly to avoid UI

**What does NOT count as a revision (new work)**
These are treated as a new request because they change the creative direction or require re-cutting:
- “Redo all clips with a different style/preset”
- “Find a completely different set of highlights”
- “Add extensive b-roll sourcing for every clip”
- “Change brand kit after editing starts”

If you want any of the above, we can quote a fast add-on.

**Revision policy + timelines (to protect speed)**
- **1 revision round included** for the batch (up to **10 notes total** across the 10 clips).
- Please send revision notes within **48 hours of delivery**.
- We turn revision requests around within **24 hours** once we receive notes in the required format.

**Approval = permission to post**
When you reply with “Approved” (or no notes within 48 hours), we assume clips are approved to publish.
