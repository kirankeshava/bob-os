## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (24h)

**Clip Factory — Review & Revisions Guide (Fast + Simple)**

Thanks for working with Clip Factory. To keep the **48-hour delivery** promise and move fast, please review and request revisions using the format below.

**Official website (for reference):** https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3  
**Support / delivery email:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### What you’ll receive
For each clip (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts-ready):
- Vertical 9:16 video export (1080×1920)
- Burned-in captions (brand style)
- Hook-first edit with pattern interrupts (every ~2–3 seconds)
- Optional end card CTA (follow/subscribe/lead magnet)
- A short “clip notes” line (what this clip is about + suggested caption idea)

### Review window (important)
- **Please respond within 24 hours** of delivery.
- If we don’t hear back within 24 hours, the order is treated as **approved** so production stays on schedule.

### Included revisions (what’s “in scope”)
**1 revision round included** covering up to:
- Caption text corrections (typos, small wording fixes)
- Caption timing tweaks (minor)
- Removing or swapping 1–2 seconds at the start/end
- Adjusting on-screen headline/hook text
- Changing CTA end card text

**Out of scope (counts as a new request / new batch):**
- New clip topics not present in the delivered selects
- Major re-cut with a totally different angle/story
- Changing brand style direction after delivery (new fonts/colors/layout)
- Additional platforms or new export specs not requested in intake

### How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to the delivery email with:
1) **Clip IDs** to revise (example: CF-ACME-EP12-CLIP03)
2) For each clip, specify one of:
   - **A) Text change** (exact new text)
   - **B) Timing change** (timestamp range, e.g., 00:07–00:10)
   - **C) Remove segment** (timestamp range)
   - **D) CTA change** (exact CTA text)

**Example revision request (perfect):**
- CF-ACME-EP12-CLIP03
  - A) Change caption line at 00:04 from “clients” to “customers”
  - D) End card: “Get the checklist → link in bio”
- CF-ACME-EP12-CLIP07
  - B) Captions are 0.3s late from 00:12–00:18 (please tighten)

### Fastest feedback method
If you’re unsure what to change, just reply with:
- **“Approve all”** OR
- **“Revise clips: 03 and 07”** and a sentence per clip.
