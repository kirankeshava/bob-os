## 2) Our Quality Bar (QA Checklist Highlights)
Every clip is QC’d before it hits your review link.

### A) Framing / Safe Zones (vertical-first)
- Export is **9:16** (1080x1920)
- Subject face is not cropped (forehead/chin safe)
- Text stays inside safe zones (not blocked by UI):
  - Avoid bottom ~20% for TikTok/IG UI overlap
  - Keep critical text away from edges
- No accidental zoom artifacts or blurred upscales

### B) Captions (accuracy + readability)
- Captions match spoken words (no meaning changes)
- Proper names/brands corrected when obvious from context
- Line breaks are readable (no “word salad” across 3 lines)
- Punctuation used to enhance comprehension and pacing
- No banned words if you request filtering

### C) Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds)
- Opens with a clear hook (question, contrarian statement, payoff)
- First on-screen text supports the hook (not generic)
- No slow intros or dead air at the start

### D) Edits / pacing
- Tight cuts; remove filler words where it improves flow
- Jump cuts feel natural (no distracting flashes)
- Avoid cutting mid-word or mid-breath when possible

### E) Audio leveling
- Speech is clear and consistent across cuts
- No clipping/distortion
- Background noise reduced when needed
- Loudness is consistent clip-to-clip (no sudden volume jumps)

### F) B-roll cues (if used)
- B-roll supports the spoken line (not random)
- No copyrighted visuals unless client-provided or clearly licensed
- Timing notes are included in the cue sheet

### G) Export / technical
- MP4 (H.264), 1080x1920, 30fps (unless specified)
- Audio AAC 48kHz (standard)
- Filename matches naming convention (see Delivery Pack)

## 3) Review Flow (Timestamp Comments)
You’ll receive a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave **timestamped comments**.

**How to request changes:**
1. Click the exact timestamp
2. Write what to change (e.g., “00:07: change caption to ‘…’”)
3. If you want a tone change, give a reference (example clip or short description)

**Revision window:** **24 hours from delivery of the review link**. This keeps production moving and ensures fast turnaround for you.

---

## 4) What You Receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll get a single folder containing:

**A) Final MP4s**  
- Platform-ready vertical videos

**B) Caption files (SRT)**  
- One SRT per clip (matching filename)

**C) B-roll cue sheet (if applicable)**  
- Timestamped cues so you (or another editor) can recreate/extend

**D) Posting suggestions**  
- Recommended first-line caption (the “caption hook”)
- Suggested hashtags + CTA wording

---

## 5) Revision Policy (Issue Triage)
We want you happy, but we also keep scope clear.

### Free revisions (included)
- Caption typos / mishears
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, small cut timing)
- Safe-zone fixes (text too low, face cropped)
- Audio leveling adjustments
- Remove a small section that violates your stated constraints

### Not free / considered a new request (post-trial or paid change later)
- Changing the clip’s concept after delivery (“different moment entirely”)
- New creative direction not stated in intake (new style, new templates, new pacing)
- Adding new assets not provided earlier (logos, brand kits, new b-roll sourcing)
- Major re-edit of multiple clips due to late preference changes

**If something is our mistake:** we fix it fast, free.
