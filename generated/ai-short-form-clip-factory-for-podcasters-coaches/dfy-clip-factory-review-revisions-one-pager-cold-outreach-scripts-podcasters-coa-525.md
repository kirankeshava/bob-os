Title: How to Review Your Clips (and Request Revisions Fast)

What you’ll receive
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) optimized for TikTok + IG Reels + YouTube Shorts.
- Burned-in captions (on-video).
- Clean file naming + a simple “Clip Notes” doc with timestamps and suggested titles/hashtags.

How to review (fastest workflow)
1) Watch all clips once without pausing.
2) On the second pass, flag only what blocks posting (hooks, incorrect captions, wrong names/claims, awkward cut, brand mismatch).
3) Approve the rest. Consistency beats perfection for short-form.

What counts as a revision (included)
Included (up to 1 revision round per clip, max 3 clips per order unless otherwise agreed):
- Fix caption typos / misheard words
- Tighten first 1–2 seconds hook timing
- Remove/replace a line that’s off-brand
- Adjust caption placement (up/down) for face coverage
- Replace CTA end-card text

Not included (new work / new scope)
- Re-cutting from a different section of the episode (new highlight)
- Changing the overall style preset after delivery
- Adding new assets not provided at intake (logos, b-roll packs, music)
- Multiple iterative rounds across many clips
