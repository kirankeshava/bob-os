## 4) Before/After Video Format (so it’s consistent)

**Export:** 1080×1920, 30fps, 20–45 seconds.

**Structure (sequential, simplest for viewers):**
- 0:00–0:02 Title card: `BEFORE (raw excerpt)`
- 0:02–0:08 Raw excerpt (no captions, minimal edits)
- 0:08–0:10 Title card: `AFTER (finished clip)`
- 0:10–end Finished version (captions + zoom cuts + b-roll cues)

**On-screen text rules:**
- Keep captions 2 lines max
- Highlight 1–3 key words per sentence
- Add a pattern interrupt every ~2 seconds (zoom, cut, b-roll, kinetic emphasis)
