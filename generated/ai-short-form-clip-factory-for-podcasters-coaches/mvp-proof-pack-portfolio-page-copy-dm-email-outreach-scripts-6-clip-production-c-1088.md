## MVP Clip Production Checklist (6 clips + 2 before/after)
Use this to render consistently and fast (batch production).

### Global export specs (all clips)
- Aspect: **9:16**
- Resolution: **1080×1920**
- Frame rate: match source (typically 30fps)
- Length targets: **20–45s** (aim 30–35s)
- Loudness: target approx **-14 LUFS integrated** (platform-safe)
- Safe margins: keep captions inside **~10%** margins; avoid bottom UI region

### Caption style (consistent across all)
- Burned-in captions, 2 lines max, high contrast
- Emphasis: highlight 1–3 keywords per sentence (color or bold)
- No long paragraphs; break on phrase boundaries every 1–2 seconds

### Editing cadence
- Remove dead air and filler
- Add subtle punch-ins every 2–4 seconds
- Pattern interrupts: quick zoom, b-roll overlay, or on-screen word callout

### Clip set (6 total)
**Real Estate (2):**
1) Story/insight hook: “Here’s why homes aren’t moving like 2021…”
   - Hook in first 1.0s, quick context, 1 takeaway
   - B-roll cues: listing photos, neighborhood shots, rate chart

2) Contrarian: “Stop doing open houses like this…”
   - Myth → reality → action step
   - B-roll cues: open house sign, showing footage, checklist overlay

**Fitness (2):**
3) Listicle: “3 reasons your fat loss stalled…”
   - Rapid 1-2-3 structure, big on-screen numbers
   - B-roll cues: meal prep, steps counter, gym clips

4) Coaching moment: “If you can’t stick to workouts, do this…”
   - Empathy line → tiny habit prescription → CTA
   - B-roll cues: calendar, 10-min workout montage

**B2B (2):**
5) Contrarian: “More leads won’t fix your pipeline…”
   - Problem framing → surprising point → metric to track
   - B-roll cues: CRM screen blur, pipeline chart, KPI overlay

6) Testimonial-style: “We changed one thing and got X result…”
   - Before → change → after
   - B-roll cues: dashboards, customer logo blur, win screen
