D) CAPCUT BUILD SPECS (standardize look)
Project:
- Canvas: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Safe margins: keep all text within center 80% width; avoid bottom 15% (UI overlays)
Editing rules:
- Remove dead air > 0.25–0.4s
- Add punch-in zooms every 2–4 seconds (110–130%) alternating left/right slight pan
- Add 1 b-roll cutaway per 8–12 seconds (stock, screenshots, or simple graphics)
Captions:
- Burned-in captions ON
- Style: bold, high-contrast (white text + black shadow/stroke)
- Highlight 1–3 keywords per line (yellow)
- Max 7–9 words per line; 2 lines max
Audio:
- Normalize voice to consistent loudness
- Optional low bed music at 6–10% (if license-safe)
Exports:
- MP4, H.264
- 1080x1920, 30fps
- Bitrate: 10–16 Mbps
