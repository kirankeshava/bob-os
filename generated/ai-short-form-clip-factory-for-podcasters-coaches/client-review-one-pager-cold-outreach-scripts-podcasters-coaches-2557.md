## What you’ll receive
- **10 vertical clips** (9:16) ready for TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- Burned-in captions + safe margins
- File naming that matches the clip title + a short “posting note”
- Optional: timestamp list from the source episode

## How to review in 7 minutes (fast)
1) **Watch the first 2 seconds** of each clip. Ask: “Would I stop scrolling?”  
2) **Check caption readability** (big enough, not cut off, synced to speech).  
3) **Confirm brand safety**: no banned claims, no private info, no off-brand language.  
4) **Check the ending**: clear close + CTA or clean stop (no awkward cutoff).  
5) **Pick winners**: choose 3–5 clips you want to post first.

## What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **small edits** that keep the same clip selection:
- Caption fixes (typos, timing tweaks)
- Hook text change (same first sentence, different phrasing)
- Remove a word/line that feels off
- Adjust b-roll cue / emoji / emphasis
- Trim 0.5–2.0 seconds for pacing

## What counts as a new request (not a revision)
These are effectively new work (we can queue them for the next batch):
- Choosing **different timestamps** / swapping to new moments
- Creating **additional clips** beyond the 10 delivered
- Changing the entire style pack (fonts/colors/layout) after approval
- Heavy content rewrites or new narrative assembly

## Revision policy (speed-protecting)
- **1 revision round** included for the batch.
- Request revisions **within 48 hours of delivery**.
- Turnaround on approved revision requests: **24 hours**.

## The fastest way to request revisions (copy/paste)
Reply with **one line per clip** using this format:

- **Clip File Name:** (example: EP12_C03_TheRealReasonYourAdsFail_v1.mp4)  
  **Change:** (example: Replace hook text with “Most ads fail for one reason…”; remove the word “cheap”; trim last 1.0s)  
  **Priority:** Must-fix / Nice-to-have

If you’re unsure, just tell us **your top 3 clips** and what felt “off” (hook, captions, pacing, or CTA). We’ll propose the minimal edits.
