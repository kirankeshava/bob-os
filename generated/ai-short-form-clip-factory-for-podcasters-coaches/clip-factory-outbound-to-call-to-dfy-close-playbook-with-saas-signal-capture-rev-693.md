C) Discovery Call Script (20 minutes)
Objective: (1) confirm pain + volume, (2) close a DFY pilot, (3) capture SaaS signals (WTP, must-haves) WITHOUT promising a product timeline.

0–2 min: Set frame
- “If it’s a fit, I’ll propose a simple pilot for clips. Separately, we’re building internal automation based on paying clients—your answers help, but no commitments today.”

2–8 min: Diagnose
- “Where do your leads/sales come from today?”
- “How often are you posting Reels/TikTok/Shorts?”
- “What’s your biggest bottleneck—time, editing cost, ideation, consistency?”
- “Do you have an editor already? What’s missing?”
- “What content formats perform best for you (teaching, story, hot take, Q&A)?”

8–12 min: Process + assets
- “Where is the source content (YouTube, Riverside, Zoom, Drive)?”
- “Do you want captions burned in? Brand colors? Specific fonts?”
- “Any compliance constraints (medical/financial claims)?”

12–17 min: Pitch DFY pilot (two options)
- Option A (Starter): 8 clips/week from 1 episode, 9:16, burned captions, basic hook text.
- Option B (Growth): 12–20 clips/week + 2 hook variations per clip + b-roll cues.
Close question:
- “If we can deliver {X} clips/week with consistent style, would you want to start with a 2-week pilot?”

17–20 min: SaaS signal capture (quick quantitative)
- “If you could upload a link and get highlight options + captions automatically (still with human QA), would that be worth $99–$299/mo to you?”
- “Which is most valuable: (1) speaker detection/diarization, (2) brand voice hook suggestions, (3) niche templates, (4) one-click export presets for TikTok/Reels/Shorts, (5) team approvals?”
- “What would be a dealbreaker?”
