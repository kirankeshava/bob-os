Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

Thanks for trusting Clip Factory. To keep the 48-hour turnaround and make revisions painless, please review using the steps below.

1) What you received
You’ll receive:
- A folder or download link with 10 vertical clips (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts-ready)
- A “Clip List” document (or notes) with:
  - Clip filenames
  - Source timestamps
  - Hook notes (what to expect)
  - Any b-roll cues used

2) The fastest way to review
A. Watch each clip once for “message” (is the point clear?)
B. Watch again for “polish” (captions, pacing, framing)
C. Decide: APPROVE or REVISE

3) What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are small changes that don’t require re-cutting the concept:
- Caption fixes (typos, timing tweaks, emphasis words)
- Minor pacing trims (within the same selected segment)
- Safe-area adjustments (caption position, scale, crop)
- Replacing/toning down b-roll overlays
- Swapping CTA end card text (same style)

4) What is a re-cut (not included, but available)
These requests change the clip’s “idea” or require new selects:
- Changing the chosen segment to a different topic
- Changing the hook concept entirely
- Combining multiple distant parts of the episode into one
- Making a new clip that wasn’t part of the original 10
If you want re-cuts, reply with “RE-CUT” and we’ll quote options.

5) Exactly how to request revisions (copy/paste format)
