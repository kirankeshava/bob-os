---

## 1) Feedback deadline (to keep 48h)
- **Round 1 feedback window:** within **12 hours** of receiving the review link.
- If feedback arrives after the window, the delivery date may shift to the next production block.

---

## 2) The “good feedback” formula (use this every time)
For each clip, give notes in this format:
1. **Clip ID** (example: `E07_C03_IG`)  
2. **Timestamp** (example: `00:04–00:07`)  
3. **Change type** (choose one): Hook / Captions / Cut / B-roll cue / CTA / Branding / Audio  
4. **Exact instruction** (one sentence)

### Examples
- **Good:** `E07_C03_IG | 00:01–00:03 | Hook | Replace first line with “Stop doing this before you list your house.”`  
- **Good:** `E07_C08_TT | 00:12 | Captions | Correct spelling: “pre-approval” (add hyphen).`  
- **Not usable:** “Make it more engaging.” (We need where + what.)

---

## 3) Approvals: fastest way to finish
If you like a clip, reply with one of these:
- **APPROVE** (no changes)  
- **APPROVE with minor** (typos/captions only)  
- **REVISION** (specific change requests with timestamps)

If you approve **8/10** quickly, we can finalize the full set faster while revising the remaining 2.

---

## 4) What counts as a “revision” (and what doesn’t)
### Included revisions (fast)
- Caption spelling/grammar fixes
- Tightening a sentence on-screen
- Swapping hook text while keeping the same segment
- Replacing CTA end card text (same template)
- Muting a word / bleeping profanity

### Not included (new work request)
- Choosing a totally different moment from the episode (“use a different segment”) 
- Changing the style preset after production (fonts/colors/layout overhaul)
- Re-cutting multiple clips because the strategy changed (new angle, new audience)
- Adding new brand kit assets after the fact (logo pack, new color system)

If you want “new work,” we’ll confirm scope and timeline before proceeding.

---

## 5) One-round revision policy (speed-first)
To protect the 48-hour promise:
- **1 revision round included** for the batch (up to **10 clips**).
- Revisions must be delivered as **one consolidated message** (single list of Clip IDs + timestamps).
- Additional rounds are possible, but timeline and scope may change.

---

## 6) “Pattern interrupt” rule (avoid conflicting notes)
We intentionally add quick changes every **2–3 seconds** (jump cuts, zooms, b-roll cues, on-screen emphasis) to keep retention.
- If you prefer “calmer edits,” say so once and we’ll apply it consistently.
- Otherwise, avoid notes like “too many cuts” on one clip and “more dynamic” on another—pick one direction.

---

## 7) Platform decisions (choose once)
When giving feedback, confirm your priority platform order:
1) TikTok 2) IG Reels 3) YouTube Shorts

We can optimize for one primary platform per batch (best results). If you need all three equally, tell us upfront.

---

## 8) Compliance & sensitivity flags
If your industry has compliance needs (medical, finance, real estate disclaimers), send your required language before editing. Otherwise, add feedback as:
- `E07_C02 | 00:00 | Compliance | Add disclaimer line: “Results vary; not financial advice.”`
