## 3) 24-Hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 high-quality sample clip within 24 hours of receiving link/assets, to increase close rate for weekly/monthly DFY packages.

### Inputs required (client sends via email to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to)
- 1 YouTube link or file (or timestamped section)
- Platform priority: TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- Topic + target viewer (who is this for?)
- Brand notes (optional): font preference, color, any examples they like
- CTA preference: follow / comment keyword / book call

### Process (time-boxed)
1) **Source review (10–15 min):**
   - Skim for 3 candidate moments (15–45s) with clear hook potential.
   - Pick the strongest based on: immediate tension, clear promise, strong one-liner, or actionable steps.
2) **Hook options (5 min):**
   - Write 3 hook overlays (<= 8 words) and choose best.
   - Example patterns: “Stop doing X”, “The real reason X fails”, “3 steps to X”, “I wish I knew this earlier”.
3) **Edit + format (35–60 min):**
   - 9:16 canvas.
   - Tight cuts (remove dead air, keep energy).
   - Add burned-in captions (high contrast, safe margins).
   - Add light emphasis styling (keyword highlight max 1–2 per sentence).
   - Audio leveling to consistent loudness.
4) **Quality checklist (5 min):**
   - Captions readable on mobile.
   - No words cut off; safe area respected.
   - First 1.5 seconds includes hook text.
   - Export settings: H.264 MP4, 1080x1920, 30fps.
5) **Delivery (5 min):**
   - Send MP4 + 2 alternative hook text options.
   - Include suggested caption + 5 hashtags.
