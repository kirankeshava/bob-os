Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (DFY Clip Factory)

Thanks for working with Clip Factory. To keep the 48-hour turnaround fast and consistent, please review using the process below.

Where to review
- We’ll deliver a folder link containing: (a) final MP4 clips, (b) a “Clip Notes” doc (hooks/CTAs/intent), and (c) a “Review & Revisions” section.
- Business legitimacy URL (for your records): https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Questions/support: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

What “approved” means
A clip is approved when:
- The first ~1.5 seconds include a clear hook (or the strongest available moment).
- Captions are readable (no blocking faces; safe margins; no major typos).
- Audio is clean and levels are consistent.
- The clip matches your selected style preset (colors/fonts/layout).

How to leave feedback (required format)
To request changes, reply with a single message using this exact structure:
- Clip filename: (example) CF_JOE-ROGAN_EP2187_C03_V1.mp4
- Timestamp: (example) 00:07–00:11
- Change requested: (example) “Replace the word ‘insane’ with ‘wild’ in captions”
- Priority: Must-fix / Nice-to-have

Revision rules (so we can keep delivery fast)
Included revisions per batch:
- 1 revision round per clip (up to 10 clips) included.
- “Revision” = caption corrections, small trims (±2 seconds), caption repositioning, CTA text swap, minor b-roll swap.

Not included (counts as new work)
- Changing the selected topic/angle after delivery (new highlight selection).
- Rebuilding clips from different timestamps not previously selected.
- New creative direction or new branding pack after editing is complete.

Timing
- If you send revision notes within 24 hours of delivery, we aim to turn revisions around within 24 hours.
- If revision notes arrive later, we’ll schedule them into the next available production window.

Fast approval tip
- If you like a clip, simply reply: “Approved: [clip filenames]”.
