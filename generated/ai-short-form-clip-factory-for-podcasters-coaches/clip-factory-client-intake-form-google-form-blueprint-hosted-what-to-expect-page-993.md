Section 1 — What you receive (Delivery Pack)
“For each clip, you’ll receive:
1) Final vertical MP4 (9:16) — ready to post
2) Caption file (.SRT)
3) B-roll cue sheet (what to overlay + when)
4) Posting suggestions: first-line caption options + hashtag starter set + best-practice notes for your platform(s)”

Section 2 — Our QA standards (how we avoid refunds)
“Before anything is delivered, we check:
- Safe zones: subtitles and key text avoid platform UI overlays
- No cropped faces: we preserve eyes/mouth in-frame; reframe when needed
- Captions accuracy: correct spelling of names/brand terms; minimal drift
- Hook clarity: first 1–2 seconds are understandable with/without sound
- Audio leveling: voice-forward; no clipping; music under dialogue
- Jump cuts: intentional pacing; no awkward mid-word cuts
- Export settings: clean 1080x1920 output optimized for TikTok/Reels/Shorts”

Section 3 — Review flow (timestamped comments)
“1) We send an unlisted review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube).
2) Leave feedback using timestamp comments (example: “00:07 change caption to…”, “00:12 remove filler”).
3) We apply revisions and send finals + the full Delivery Pack.”

Section 4 — Revision window
“You have 24 hours after receiving the review link to request revisions included in the order. After 24 hours, additional changes may be billed or scheduled into the next batch (depending on workload).”

Section 5 — What counts as a free revision vs a paid change
“Free revisions (included):
- Caption corrections (misspellings, punctuation, minor wording fixes)
- Minor timing adjustments to captions
- Small reframes to avoid cropping faces or improve composition
- Audio tweaks (leveling, removing a short noise, reducing music)
- Swapping the hook line if it uses the same clip segment

Paid changes (scope change):
- Choosing entirely different moments/segments than originally clipped
- New creative direction after approval (different style, new template)
- Adding new on-screen graphics beyond standard captions
- Re-cutting multiple clips due to new preferences not provided in intake
- Additional clips beyond the order quantity”
