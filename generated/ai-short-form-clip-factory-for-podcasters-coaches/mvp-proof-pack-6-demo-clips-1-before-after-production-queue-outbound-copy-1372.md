MVP Deliverables (Ship First)
A) 6 Demo Clips (1080x1920, 30–45s each, burned-in captions)
Each niche gets:
1) Story Hook clip (high retention, emotional/relatable opener)
2) Contrarian Take clip (pattern interrupt, strong POV)

Niche 1 — Real Estate
1. RE-Story Hook: “The open house mistake that kills offers…”
   Edit notes: cold open in first 0.7s; captions 3–5 words per line; zoom-in every 2–3 seconds; b-roll cues: ‘overcrowded open house’, ‘inspection report’, ‘offer sheet’.
2. RE-Contrarian: “Stop staging your home like HGTV…”
   Edit notes: punchy jump cuts; emphasize keywords in yellow (STOP, staging, buyer); b-roll cues: ‘empty rooms’, ‘neutral decor’, ‘before/after staging’.

Niche 2 — Fitness
3. FIT-Story Hook: “I trained for 10 years—this is what actually changed my body…”
   Edit notes: remove pauses; add subtle whoosh transitions; b-roll cues: ‘meal prep’, ‘sleep tracker’, ‘progress photos’.
4. FIT-Contrarian: “If you’re doing HIIT 6 days/week, you’re sabotaging fat loss…”
   Edit notes: fast cadence; on-screen ‘myth vs truth’ cards; b-roll cues: ‘treadmill sprint’, ‘rest day walk’, ‘heart rate graph’.

Niche 3 — B2B
5. B2B-Story Hook: “We doubled pipeline with one change in our discovery call…”
   Edit notes: add callout text for the ‘one change’; b-roll cues: ‘CRM pipeline’, ‘calendar invite’, ‘call recording waveform’.
6. B2B-Contrarian: “Stop chasing leads. Fix your offer clarity first.”
   Edit notes: bold captions; underline ‘offer clarity’; b-roll cues: ‘landing page headline’, ‘pricing page’, ‘proposal doc’.

B) 1 Before/After Comparison (45–60s)
Format: sequential (cleanest) OR split-screen.
Sequence (sequential recommended):
- 0–3s: Title card “RAW → FINAL (Captioning + Cuts + B-roll cues)”
- 3–18s: RAW excerpt (label top-left: RAW) minimal/no captions, uncut audio
- 18–60s: FINAL version (label top-left: FINAL) with burned-in captions, jump cuts, zooms, b-roll cue overlays (small, bottom-right)
Use any one of the MVP clips (pick the most impressive transformation—usually B2B or Fitness).

Batch Production Checklist (No paid tools required)
1) Source selection: choose 1 longform per niche with reuse-friendly license (Creative Commons/CC0/creator-permitted). Save license proof screenshot/URL.
2) Clip extraction: pull the two segments per niche (30–45s) and normalize audio (-14 LUFS target).
3) Vertical layout: 9:16, safe margins for TikTok/Reels, speaker centered, add subtle blurred background if needed.
4) Captions: high-contrast, 2-line max, highlight 1–2 keywords per sentence; ensure no words covered by UI.
5) Retention edits: remove dead air; zoom/punch-in every 2–3 seconds; add pattern interrupt at ~12–15s.
6) B-roll cues: overlay small bracketed cues like “[B-ROLL: CRM pipeline]” bottom-right (portfolio viewers love seeing intent).
7) Export: 1080x1920, H.264, 12–20 Mbps, AAC audio.
