## Discovery Call Script (20 minutes) → DFY Close
**Setup (1 min)**
“Thanks for the time. I’ll ask a few quick questions about your current content workflow, then I’ll share what we do and—if it’s a fit—what a 7-day pilot looks like. Sound good?”

**1) Context & goals (4 min)**
1. “Where do your best leads/sales come from today?”
2. “What’s the #1 goal for short-form right now—followers, email leads, booked calls, course sales?”
3. “How many long-form assets do you publish per week/month (podcast, YouTube, webinars)?”

**2) Current workflow & pain (5 min)**
4. “Do you currently repurpose into Reels/TikTok/Shorts? If yes, what’s the process?”
5. “What’s the most annoying part—finding highlights, editing, captions, hooks, posting consistency?”
6. “What’s your current cadence of short-form (per week) and what would you *like* it to be?”
7. “Have you hired an editor before? What worked / what failed?”

**3) Content & brand constraints (4 min)**
8. “Any hard rules? (words to avoid, tone, captions style, profanity, disclaimers)”
9. “Do you have a brand kit? fonts/colors? or should we match your current style?”
10. “Platforms you care about most: TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn?”

**4) Qualify budget + urgency (3 min)**
11. “If we could reliably ship 12 high-quality clips/week from your long-form, what would that be worth monthly?”
12. “Have you budgeted for editing/repurposing before? Rough range?”
13. “If we delivered the first batch within 72 hours, would you want to start this week or next?”

**5) Pitch (2 min)**
“Here’s what Clip Factory does: you send a YouTube link or upload a file, we pull the best highlight moments, add high-retention hooks, clean captions, and deliver ready-to-post 9:16 clips—optimized per platform. You can see the overview here (legitimacy page):
https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

We’re starting with a DFY pilot so you get results immediately—no tooling learning curve.”

**6) Close: paid pilot (1–2 min)**
“Best next step is a 7-day pilot: 12 clips delivered from your latest episode, first batch in 48–72 hours. It’s $750 for the week (or $1,500/month for 30 clips if you want to start monthly). If you’re in, I’ll send a simple intake checklist and you can reply to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with the episode link and any brand notes.”

**If price pushback**
- “Totally fair—what budget would make this a no-brainer?”
- Offer smaller pilot: “We can do a mini-pilot: 6 clips for $400 to prove quality and retention.”

**If they want SaaS instead of DFY**
“We are building a lightweight self-serve version, but we’re gating it behind client results first. I can put you in the founding group: you’ll get priority access, and your workflow will shape the MVP. Are you open to a paid pilot now so you get clips this week, and we bake your needs into the product?”
