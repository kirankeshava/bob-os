What counts as a “clip” (scope definition)
A clip is one exported vertical video file derived from your long-form content, with:
- A clear hook/open loop
- Captions (burned-in)
- Basic reframing/cropping for 9:16
- Light cleanup (silence trimming, minor pacing)
Not included by default: heavy motion graphics, deep sound design, full episode editing, long-form editing, complex VFX.
