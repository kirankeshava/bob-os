## 3) 24-hour Sample Clip Sprint — Fulfillment SOP

**SOP objective:** Deliver 2 high-quality vertical clips in <24 hours from receiving assets, so closing DFY packages becomes easy.

### Intake checklist (must-have to start)
Ask the client for:
- 1 link: YouTube, Drive, Riverside, or podcast video file
- Target platform(s): TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- Tone: “educational”, “high-energy”, “calm/premium”, etc.
- CTA preference: “follow”, “download lead magnet”, “book a call”, “comment keyword”
- Any banned words/topics + pronunciation notes

**Send intake replies to:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### Step-by-step workflow
1) **Download/source media**
   - Confirm video length, audio clarity, speaker count.
2) **Transcribe quickly (manual + AI-assisted)**
   - Produce rough transcript; mark 5–10 candidate moments.
3) **Select 2 moments using retention heuristics**
   - Pick moments with: (a) conflict/contrarian hook, (b) “framework/list”, (c) story with payoff, or (d) strong tactical tip.
4) **Edit vertical framing**
   - 9:16 canvas; center face; dynamic reframes as needed.
   - Add jump cuts to remove dead air; target tight pacing.
5) **Captioning rules (burned-in)**
   - 2 lines max; 3–5 words highlighted max per line.
   - Keep captions above UI safe zones; large enough for mobile.
   - Correct names/brands.
6) **Hook frame (first 0.5–1.0s)**
   - Add bold hook text. Create 5 hook variants to test.
7) **B-roll cues (lightweight)**
   - If you don’t insert b-roll, at least provide 3–5 cues with timestamps.
8) **Export specs**
   - 1080x1920, H.264, 30 fps, ~10–20 Mbps.
   - Filename: {Client}_{Episode}_{ClipA/B}_{Date}.mp4
9) **Delivery**
   - Upload to Drive/Dropbox; send the “proof package” email.
10) **Close loop**
   - Ask one question: “Want 8, 12, or 20 clips/week from each episode?”

### Acceptance criteria (Sample Clip Sprint)
- Delivered within 24 hours of receiving link + requirements
- 2 clips in 9:16 with burned-in captions
- At least 5 hook variants provided
- No obvious caption errors (names/terms)
- Clear ask to move into weekly DFY package
