## 3) DFY “24-Hour Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (internal execution)

### Goal
Deliver **one high-quality vertical clip** in ≤24 hours to reduce risk for the prospect and convert to a paid package. (If you choose to make samples paid-only, the SOP still applies after payment.)

### Inputs required (collect in one message)
- Source link (YouTube) or raw file
- Target platform(s): Reels / TikTok / Shorts
- Audience + offer (1 sentence)
- Style preference (pick 1): 
  - Clean minimal captions
  - Bold/high-contrast “viral” captions
  - Podcast-subtitle style
- Any “do not include” topics/words
- CTA (follow, subscribe, book call, download lead magnet)

### Step-by-step production process
1) **Select moment (10–15 min):**
   - Find a segment with (a) a contrarian take, (b) a crisp framework, (c) a mistake-to-fix, or (d) a strong story beat.
   - Target length 20–45 seconds.

2) **Hook shaping (10 min):**
   - Create 3 hook options. Examples:
     - “Most [niche] get this wrong…”
     - “If you’re doing X, stop. Do this instead.”
     - “Here’s the 10-second test to know if…”

3) **Captioning rules (20–30 min):**
   - Burned-in captions, 2 lines max, avoid covering faces.
   - Correct punctuation, remove filler words where it improves readability.
   - Highlight 1–3 key words per sentence (color or weight) but don’t overdo.

4) **9:16 formatting rules (10–15 min):**
   - Canvas: 1080×1920.
   - Safe zones: keep text within central ~80% height; avoid bottom UI.
   - If source is 16:9: center on speaker; gentle motion/punch-in as needed.

5) **Audio cleanup (5–10 min):**
   - Normalize audio to consistent loudness.
   - Light noise reduction if needed.

6) **Export & delivery (5 min):**
   - Export .mp4, 1080×1920, high bitrate.
   - Deliver via share link + attach hooks in text.

### Deliverable package (what client receives)
- 1 vertical .mp4 clip
- 3 hook/title options
- Suggested caption/description (1–2 sentences)
- Optional: 3 hashtag clusters (if requested)

### QA checklist (must pass before sending)
- Captions readable on mobile, not covering face
- No typos, no awkward line breaks
- First 1–2 seconds are compelling
- Clip ends cleanly (no mid-word cut)
- Audio is not peaking/distorted

### Conversion CTA (what you ask for after delivery)
“Want me to turn the rest of this episode into **10–20 clips** so you can post daily? If you tell me your posting goal (3x/week or daily), I’ll recommend the right package.”
