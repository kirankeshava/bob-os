Goal (per CEO directive): create the minimum viable portfolio proof that increases reply/close rates while outbound runs. Instead of waiting for 12 demos + 3 before/after, we publish an MVP set that still demonstrates quality, consistency, and niche relevance.

MVP Deliverables
A) 6 vertical demo clips (2 per niche: Real Estate, Fitness, B2B)
- Style coverage (per niche):
  1) Storytelling hook clip (fast hook + narrative cutdown)
  2) Listicle/steps clip (structured, numbered captions)
Why these two: they’re the most common winning short-form formats and quickest to demonstrate editing/caption quality.

B) 2 before/after comparisons
- Format: sequential (Raw Excerpt 4–6s → Final Clip 12–18s) OR split-screen.
- On-screen labels:
  - RAW (no captions, no punch-ins)
  - FINAL (hook rewrite, captions, zoom cuts, b-roll cues)

Standard Look & Export Specs (apply to all clips)
- Canvas: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30fps.
- Captions: burned-in, high-contrast (white text + subtle shadow), 2 lines max, safe margins for TikTok UI.
- Highlighting: 1–3 keywords per sentence in accent color.
- Editing cadence: remove dead air; punch-in every 1.5–2.5s on emphasis; quick J-cuts where natural.
- Loudness target: consistent spoken audio; no clipping; light limiter.
- B-roll cues: shown as subtle on-screen callouts (e.g., “[B-ROLL: listing exterior]”) or executed if available.
- End card (optional, 1s): “SAMPLE CLIP — Clip Factory”
