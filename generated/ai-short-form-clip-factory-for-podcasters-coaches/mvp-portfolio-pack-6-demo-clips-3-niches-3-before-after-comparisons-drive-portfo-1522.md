Niche A — Real Estate (1 source → 2 clips)
CLIP RE-1 (Storytelling Hook) — 35–45s
- Hook (0–2s, big text): “The #1 mistake first-time buyers make…”
- Spoken line to find in source: a cautionary anecdote about buyers focusing on rate/payment instead of inspection/terms.
- Edit beats:
  1) Cold open on the most emotional sentence (start mid-sentence).
  2) 0.3–0.5s jump cuts every sentence.
  3) Subtle punch-in (105–115%) on key words: “inspection”, “earnest money”, “terms”.
- Captions:
  - Style: bold, 2-line max, highlight keywords in yellow.
  - Punctuation for pace (… and dashes).
- B-roll cues (overlay 20–30% opacity or picture-in-picture):
  - “Home inspection report”, “house keys”, “closing table”.
- CTA (last 2s, small lower-third): “Want 10 clips/month like this? Link in portfolio + email.”

CLIP RE-2 (Contrarian Take) — 30–40s
- Hook text: “Stop obsessing over the interest rate.”
- Core point: rate matters, but purchase price + terms + timeline often matter more.
- Edit beats:
  - Add a fast “record scratch” SFX at hook.
  - Insert 3 on-screen bullets timed to speech:
    1) “Price > Rate (sometimes)”
    2) “Terms win deals”
    3) “Speed = leverage”
- B-roll cues: mortgage calculator, offer letter, “Sold” sign.
- CTA: “DM ‘CLIPS’ — I’ll send a sample pack.”

- Captions: kinetic captions + large number overlays.
- Edit beats:
  - Cut on the beat (use a consistent whoosh between cues).
  - Add a quick zoom on each cue word.
- B-roll cues: squat demo, side profile, feet stance, barbell close-ups.
- CTA: “Save this—want daily clips from your podcast? Email me.”

- B-roll cues: gym floor, timer, notebook, plates being loaded.
