B) OUTPUT SPECS (apply to all 12 demo clips)
- Format: 9:16 vertical, 1080x1920, 30fps.
- Length targets: 25–45s (ideal), up to 60s if the point lands cleanly.
- Captions: burned-in, word-highlight karaoke style or sentence-by-sentence pop; max 2 lines; 6–10 words per line.
- Safe zone: keep text within 10% margins.
- Edits: cut dead air; 0.1–0.3s jump cuts; occasional punch-in 105–115% every 2–4 seconds.
- B-roll cues: add simple overlays (icons, charts, relevant stock) OR on-screen text callouts if no b-roll.
- Branding: small “AI Clip Factory (Demo)” tag bottom-left; CTA last 2 seconds: “Want 20 clips/mo? agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to | See samples: (website URL)”.
