Title: How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Still Hit the 48‑Hour Delivery)

Thanks for trusting Clip Factory. We can keep your “10 clips in 48 hours” timeline only if feedback is fast, specific, and timecoded. Please use this guide.

Where you’ll review
- You’ll receive a review link (Frame.io or Google Drive) from us.
- Reply to the delivery email or comment in the review tool (preferred). If you email feedback, include timecodes.

Feedback deadline (protects the 48‑hour SLA)
- You have 24 hours from delivery to request revisions for this batch.
- If we don’t receive revision notes within 24 hours, the batch is considered accepted and we’ll close it out.

What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are small edits to clips we already delivered:
1) Captions: fix typos, punctuation, casing, name/term spelling
2) Timing: tighten pauses, adjust clip start/end by a few seconds
3) Visuals: swap a b‑roll moment, adjust caption position within the same style
4) Hook tweaks: minor text hook rewrite using the same moment
5) Safe cuts: remove a sentence for compliance or sensitivity

What does NOT count as a revision (new work)
These are new requests and may require a new batch or an add‑on:
- “Find 10 different moments” or “Replace most clips with new ones”
- New style direction after delivery (new fonts/colors/layouts beyond the chosen preset)
- New platform strategy (e.g., changing from TikTok to LinkedIn + YouTube longform cutdowns)
- Adding new footage/graphics not provided during intake

The fastest way to send revision notes (copy/paste template)
Paste this into email or comments:

Batch: [Client Name] — [Episode Title] — [Delivery Date]
Priority: MUST FIX (needed) / NICE TO HAVE (optional)

Clip 01 — [Filename]
- Timecode: 00:00–00:02
  Change: Replace on-screen hook text with: “________”
- Timecode: 00:08
  Change: Caption typo: “_____” → “_____”

Clip 02 — [Filename]
- Timecode: 00:12–00:15
  Change: Remove sentence: “________”

(Repeat)

How to prioritize (so we hit the timeline)
- Mark each note as MUST FIX or NICE TO HAVE.
- If you send 20+ notes, we’ll address MUST FIX items first within the revision window.

If you need us to choose for you
If you’re not sure what to change, send ONE sentence about the goal (e.g., “make it punchier and more premium”) and pick the top 3 clips you care about most. We’ll optimize those first.
