4) GUARANTEE + TERMS (Paste into website / proposals / platform FAQs)
============================
Turnaround SLA:
- Starter: 10 clips delivered within 48 hours after we receive (a) usable source file/link and (b) your brand notes.
- Growth/Pro/Retainer: first batch delivered within 72 hours; remaining clips delivered on a rolling schedule defined at kickoff.

Revisions:
- Starter: 1 revision round.
- Growth/Pro: 2 revision rounds per delivery period (week or sprint).
- Retainer: 1 revision round per weekly batch.
Revision rounds cover: caption corrections, minor timing trims, swapping a hook line, removing a sentence for compliance.
Not included: re-cutting an entirely new set of moments because strategy changed after delivery, or major rebrands mid-sprint.

What counts as a “clip”:
- A finished vertical video export (9:16), typically 15–60 seconds unless otherwise requested.
- Includes burned-in captions + on-screen hook text.
- One clip = one exported file.

Client responsibilities (required inputs):
- Provide source video/audio via downloadable link or file.
- Confirm any words/names that need special spelling.
- Confirm brand basics (colors/fonts if you have them) OR we use a clean default caption style.

Content rights:
- You retain full rights to your content.
- We may not redistribute your source or clips without written permission.

Refund policy:
- If we miss the stated turnaround SLA by more than 24 hours (without a client-caused delay), you can request a prorated refund for the delayed portion OR equivalent make-good clips.
- No refunds after delivery of the first batch if work matches the agreed scope and technical requirements.

Platform compliance:
- You are responsible for ensuring your content complies with platform rules, disclosure requirements, and any music/licensing restrictions. We can avoid copyrighted music by default.
