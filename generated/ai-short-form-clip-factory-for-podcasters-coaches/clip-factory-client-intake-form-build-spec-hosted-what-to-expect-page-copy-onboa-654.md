Page title:
“Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery)”

Hero / intro:
“Clip Factory turns long videos/podcasts into ready-to-post vertical clips with hooks, captions, b-roll cues, and platform-friendly formatting.

Business site: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
Support: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

Section: Before we start (required)
“To protect quality and avoid revisions, we begin editing after you submit our Intake Form (unless you waive it in writing). The intake captures caption style, banned words, platform targets, and examples so we can match your brand from the first draft.”

Section: Our QA promise (what we check)
“Every clip is checked for:
- Safe framing (no cropped faces, readable subtitles, mobile-first)
- Caption accuracy + timing (no meaning drift)
- Audio leveling (voice clear, no peaking/distortion)
- Hook clarity in the first 1–2 seconds
- Clean jump cuts (no awkward gaps, consistent pacing)
- Platform formatting (9:16, export settings, safe zones)
- Delivery completeness (MP4 + SRT + cue sheet + posting suggestions)”

Section: Review flow (timestamp comments)
“1) You receive a review link (with timestamp commenting).
2) Leave notes using timestamps (example: “0:07 change ‘clients’ to ‘students’”).
3) We apply revisions and send final files.

Revision window: 24 hours from when the review link is sent. This keeps turnaround fast and prevents delays.”

Section: What you receive (Delivery Pack)
“Your delivery folder includes:
1) Final MP4 clips (ready to post)
2) Caption files (SRT) for each clip
3) B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggestions)
4) Posting suggestions per clip:
   - First-line caption (copy/paste)
   - Hashtags (platform-appropriate)
   - Recommended posting notes (optional CTA variations)”

Section: Revisions — what’s free vs paid
“Free revisions (within the 24h window):
- Caption fixes (accuracy, punctuation, minor timing)
- Small hook/copy tweaks that don’t change the clip’s core segment
- Minor crop/framing adjustments
- Audio leveling adjustments

Paid changes (scope change):
- Switching to a different segment/topic after drafts are delivered
- Major rewrite of the hook direction or brand style after intake approval
- New clips beyond the ordered quantity
- Adding new brand requirements not provided in intake (banned words, legal disclaimers, etc.)

If you’re unsure, send a note—we’ll classify it clearly before doing work.”

Section: Contact
“For any questions, email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to.

We aim for fast, clear communication and predictable delivery.”
