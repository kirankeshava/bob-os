CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW YOUR CLIPS + REQUEST REVISIONS

Purpose: Help you review fast, approve confidently, and request changes in a way that preserves the 48-hour delivery speed.

Where you’ll receive your clips
- You’ll receive a delivery email from: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
- We’ll include a downloadable folder link and/or a review link.
- Our website for reference/legitimacy: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

What we deliver (standard)
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) ready to post
- Burned-in captions (consistent style)
- Hook-forward edits (hook in first ~1.5s)
- Platform-safe exports for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- A “Clip List” note that includes: source timestamps + suggested titles/hooks

How to review in 10 minutes (recommended)
Step 1 — First-pass scan (3 minutes)
- Watch the first 2 seconds of each clip only.
- Ask: “Would I stop scrolling for this?”
- Mark Yes/No.

Step 2 — Keepers pass (5 minutes)
- Watch only the “Yes” clips fully.
- Check: captions readability, pacing, and ending CTA.

Step 3 — Notes pass (2 minutes)
- For any clip needing changes, request revisions in the format below.

Revision request format (copy/paste)
Subject line: Revisions — [ClientName] — [EpisodeName/Date]

For each clip:
- Clip filename: (example) CLIP_03_Hook-Disagree_IG-REELS_v1.mp4
- Timecode(s): 00:04–00:07
- Change requested (pick one):
  1) Caption fix (typo/wording)
  2) Caption style tweak (size/position)
  3) Remove a phrase / tighten by X seconds
  4) Swap hook (use alt line from same segment)
  5) Add/remove b-roll cue
- Exact instruction: “Change ‘their’ to ‘there’ in captions at 00:05” OR “Trim the pause between 00:06–00:08”

What counts as a “revision” (and what doesn’t)
Included revisions (fast)
- Caption typos or minor readability improvements
- Small trims/tightening within the same selected segment
- One hook-line swap from the same 30–90s moment
- CTA text swap at end card

Not included (requires new scope)
- New clip selection from a different part of the episode
- New style/look beyond the agreed preset
- Rebuilding multiple clips from scratch due to strategy pivot

Revision limits + turnaround
- Standard: 1 revision round included, up to 3 clips per batch.
- Turnaround for included revisions: 24–48 hours depending on request volume.

How to avoid revisions (best practices)
- Share 2–3 example clips you love (links are fine)
- Tell us your “no-go” list (topics/words/claims)
- Tell us your #1 CTA (follow, lead magnet, book call)
