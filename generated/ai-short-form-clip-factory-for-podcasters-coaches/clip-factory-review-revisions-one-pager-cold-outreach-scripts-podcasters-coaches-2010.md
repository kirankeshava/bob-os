## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for trusting Clip Factory.** To keep our **48-hour delivery** fast and consistent, here’s the simplest way to review your clips and request changes.

### What you’ll receive
You’ll get **10 ready-to-post vertical clips** (9:16) plus:
- Burned-in captions (matching your chosen style preset)
- Hook-first pacing (first 1–1.5s)
- Platform-safe exports (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts)
- A short notes file listing: clip titles + timestamps + suggested caption/hashtags (if requested)

### How to review (fastest workflow)
1) **Watch each clip once without pausing** (gut-check: “Would I keep watching?”).
2) Watch a second time and check only these 5 items:
   - Hook clarity in first 1–1.5s
   - Captions readable on mobile (no face/mouth overlap)
   - No obvious jump cuts / audio pops
   - CTA or ending feels clean (doesn’t cut mid-word)
   - Any names/claims are accurate

### What counts as a revision (included)
We include **1 revision round** per batch (up to 10 clips) as long as the requests are consolidated.

Included revisions (examples):
- Caption timing/typos, emphasis, line breaks
- Swap hook text overlay / first sentence framing
- Trim 0.5–2s for pacing
- Replace/remove b-roll cue segment
- Blur a section or remove a sensitive line

Not included in the included revision round:
- New topic selection from the full episode (net-new clips)
- Full style change after the batch is produced (new fonts/colors/layout)
- Heavy re-editing that changes the narrative (we can do it, but it becomes a new clip)
