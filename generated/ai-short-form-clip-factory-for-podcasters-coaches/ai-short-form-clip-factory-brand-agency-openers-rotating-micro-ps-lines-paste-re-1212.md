“Saw you’re actively running Meta ads (Ad Library). If you’re repurposing the same creative angles, we can cut fresh short-form from your longer demos/reviews to test new hooks weekly—captions + b-roll cues included.”

3) SaaS (founder-led content)
“Your founder posts have great insight, but they’re ‘too long for the feed.’ We convert long videos/podcasts into ready-to-post vertical clips with a strong hook + clean captions so you can ship consistent Reels/TikToks without editing time.”

4) SaaS (webinar / YouTube signal)
“Noticed you’ve got webinars/YouTube long-form—those are a goldmine for short clips. We extract the most ‘stop-scrolling’ moments, add on-screen captions, and format for Reels/TikTok/Shorts so your team can post consistently.”

5) Local service (trust + testimonials)
“Your reviews are solid—people just need to *see* the proof faster. We turn long testimonials/FAQs into quick vertical clips with bold captions + b-roll cues (before/after, shots of the team, location) that are ready to post.”

6) Local service (seasonal push)
“If you’re heading into a busy season, short-form is the fastest way to stay top-of-mind. We’ll turn one longer video (or a few phone clips) into multiple vertical posts with hooks/captions—so you can keep posting without hiring an editor.”

7) Agency/SMMA (offer positioning)
“Agencies sit on a ton of client calls/case studies that never get repurposed. We convert those long recordings into polished vertical clips (hook + captions + b-roll cues) your clients can approve quickly and you can deliver as an add-on.”

8) Agency (white-label angle)
“Quick idea: we can be your white-label clip factory. You send long-form (podcast, Zoom, webinar), we send back ready-to-post verticals with captions + b-roll cues + platform formatting—fast turnaround, consistent output.”

9) Ecom/brand (UGC library)
“If you’ve got UGC sitting in Drive, it’s usually ‘long and messy’ but full of money moments. We find the best 15–45s segments, write the hook, add captions, and cue b-roll so it feels native to TikTok/Reels.”

10) B2B services (authority clips)
“Your expertise is clear—but it’s hard to consume in long form. We pull the strongest ‘opinion + proof’ moments into vertical clips with captions and pacing tuned for Reels/TikTok, so you can publish consistently without editing overhead.”

B) 12 ROTATING MICRO-PS LINES (CREDIBILITY BOOSTERS)
(Use ONE line under your message/proposal before the CTA. Rotate to avoid repetition.)

PS1: “Turnaround is fast: we return 1 sample clip in 24h so you can judge quality before committing.”

PS2: “You’ll get hook options + caption styling + b-roll cues (so your team can shoot/overlay quickly).”

PS3: “We format per platform (Reels/TikTok/Shorts): safe margins, caption sizing, pacing, and export settings.”

PS4: “Brand voice matters—tell us the vibe (punchy vs premium vs educational) and we’ll match it.”

PS5: “If you already have an editor, we slot in as overflow: we deliver a clean cut + captions + notes so they can approve/post faster.”

PS6: “We can build a repeatable ‘clip rubric’ from your best-performing posts (hooks, pacing, caption style) and apply it every week.”

PS7: “We include b-roll suggestions by timestamp (what to show, where to zoom, when to cut) to increase retention.”

PS8: “No lock-in—judge us on the sample and only continue if it’s a clear upgrade.”

PS9: “For compliance/brand safety: we avoid risky claims, and we can add on-screen disclaimers if your niche needs it.”

PS10: “We aim for ‘native’ edits—tight pacing, clean captions, and hooks that don’t sound like generic AI.”

PS11: “If you want, we can deliver 2 caption styles (minimal vs bold) so you can A/B quickly.”

PS12: “We can batch: one long recording → multiple clips → a simple posting checklist so your team stays consistent.”
