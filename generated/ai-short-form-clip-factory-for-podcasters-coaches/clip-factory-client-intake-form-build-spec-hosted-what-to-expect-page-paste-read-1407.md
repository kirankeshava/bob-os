### 1) Before We Start (Intake Required)
To protect quality and avoid back-and-forth, we start work after you submit the intake form with:
- Platform targets (TikTok/Reels/Shorts/etc.)
- Caption style + examples
- Brand terms + banned words
- Framing preference (face safe zones)
- Source video link(s)

If you want to waive intake, you can do so in writing. If you waive intake, we will still deliver best-effort, but revisions may be limited to objective fixes.

### 2) What You’ll Receive (Delivery Pack)
For each clip you receive:
- Final vertical MP4 (ready to post)
- Captions file (.SRT)
- B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggestions)
- Posting suggestions (first-line caption + hashtags + platform notes)

### 3) Quality Standards (What We Check)
Every clip is QC’d for:
- Hook clarity in the first 1–2 seconds
- Captions accuracy (meaning + names + numbers)
- Clean framing (no cropped faces, text inside safe zones)
- Audio leveling (speech consistent, no clipping)
- Pacing (no dead air; clean jump cuts)
- Platform-friendly formatting (vertical, readable text size, consistent look)

### 4) Review Flow (Timestamped Notes)
You’ll get a review link (unlisted) where you can leave timestamped comments.
How to review efficiently:
1) Watch once without pausing.
2) Rewatch and leave notes with timestamps for any change.
3) Group changes into: captions fixes, wording swaps, pacing/cuts, on-screen text, or branding.

### 5) Revision Window
We include a 24-hour revision window starting when the review link is delivered.
- Revisions requested within the window are prioritized.
- Notes received after 24 hours may be scheduled as a new task.

### 6) What Counts as a Free Revision vs a Paid Change
Free revisions (included):
- Caption corrections (misheard words, wrong names, missing words)
- Cropping/safe zone fixes (face chopped, text too low/high)
- Audio fixes (peaking, inconsistent leveling)
- Minor timing fixes to match the same script/meaning
- Simple brand consistency fixes (color/font/logo placement) if provided in intake

Paid changes (scope change):
- Changing the chosen clip moments/topics after we already clipped
- Rewriting the hook or script direction beyond minor edits
- New caption style after delivery (e.g., switching to word-by-word)
- Adding new assets not provided (logos, new brand kit creation)
- Additional versions for different platforms beyond the original scope

If you’re unsure, send your request—we’ll classify it clearly before doing extra work.
