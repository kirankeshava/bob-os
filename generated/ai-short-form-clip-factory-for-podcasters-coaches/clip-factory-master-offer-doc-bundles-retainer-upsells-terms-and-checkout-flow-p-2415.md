## What you get (the “Content System”)
We turn long-form video/podcasts into **ready-to-post vertical clips** designed to win attention:
- Hook-first openers (pattern interrupts + curiosity)
- Burned-in captions (mobile-friendly, high contrast)
- Platform formatting (9:16, safe margins)
- Delivery in organized folders + filenames you can post immediately

**A “clip” is:** 1 vertical video, typically **15–60s** (can be up to 90s), from your source content, with captions + basic motion/zoom/punch-ins.

## Revisions policy
- A “revision” means: text/caption corrections, trimming a few seconds, replacing a clip moment, minor caption styling adjustments.
- Not included: entirely new clip selection strategy after approval, re-editing the whole episode, or rewriting the creative direction from scratch.
