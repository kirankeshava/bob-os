Purpose
This guide makes reviews fast so we can hit the “10 clips in 48 hours” promise. You’ll receive a batch of vertical clips plus a simple review checklist. You can approve quickly, or request revisions in a structured way.

What you’ll receive (typical delivery)
1) 10 vertical clips (MP4) formatted for TikTok / Reels / Shorts
2) A short “Clip List” doc (or message) with:
   - Clip filenames
   - Working title/hook
   - Source timestamp range (e.g., 12:44–13:31)
   - Notes (b-roll cues, on-screen text intent)

How to review (5–10 minutes total)
For each clip, answer these 5 yes/no questions:
1) Is the hook accurate and compelling in the first 1–2 seconds?
2) Are captions readable on mobile (size, contrast, no cutoff)?
3) Is the clip on-brand (tone, no awkward cut, no missing context)?
4) Does it end cleanly (no abrupt cutoff) with a clear CTA or “loop”?
5) Any compliance issues (claims, regulated language, sensitive topics)?

How to request revisions (fastest format)
Reply with ONE message using this template (copy/paste):
Subject/Message: Revisions for [Episode/Batch Name]
Clip filename: [e.g., EP12_COACH_Clip03_v1.mp4]
Timecode: [e.g., 00:04–00:07]
Change type (choose one): Hook / Captions / Cut / B-roll cue / CTA end card / Other
Exact request: [one sentence]
Optional: reference example link (TikTok/Reel) if you want a specific vibe.

Revision rules (to protect speed)
- Included: 1 revision round per batch (up to 10 clips).
- “Revision” means adjusting hook text, caption wording, minor timing, CTA end card, or swapping b-roll cue instructions.
- Not included in the free trial: choosing an entirely new set of 10 moments after delivery, full re-edits of every clip, or creating net-new clips beyond the 10 promised.

Approval & posting
When you approve, reply “Approved” (or “Approved with notes”) and we’ll mark the batch complete. You can post immediately. If you want us to recommend posting captions/hashtags, ask and we’ll include a lightweight suggested caption per clip next batch.
