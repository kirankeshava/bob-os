## 1) Client One-Pager — How to Review Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Clip Factory Review Guide (10 clips / 48-hour delivery)**

Thanks again for the order. To keep turnaround fast and revisions clean, please review clips using the steps below.

**What you received**
- 10 vertical clips (9:16) formatted for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions
- Platform-safe audio levels
- File naming that matches the provided “Clip List”

**How to review (fastest method)**
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) On the second pass, pause only to note specific timecodes.
3) Approve in batches if possible (e.g., “Clips 1–7 approved; revisions needed on 8–10”).

**What counts as a revision (included)**
Please request revisions using **timecodes** and one clear instruction per change.
- Caption fixes (spelling, punctuation, incorrect word)
- Caption timing adjustments (a line appears too early/late)
- Hook tweak (first sentence re-cut, remove a few words, swap first 1–2 seconds)
- Remove/replace a single visual moment (trim 0.5–2.0 seconds)
- CTA text change on end card

**What is not included (but available on request)**
These are effectively new edits and may require a new order/add-on:
- Rebuilding the clip concept (new angle / different point)
- Changing the clip to a different section of the episode entirely
- Full style redesign (new fonts/colors/layout) after delivery
- Heavy b-roll sourcing/licensing beyond simple cues

**Revision policy (to protect 48-hour speed)**
- **1 revision round included** for the batch of 10 clips.
- Please submit **all revision requests in one message** (batch feedback) within **72 hours** of delivery.
- We turn the revision round in **24 hours** after receiving your consolidated notes.

**How to submit revision notes (copy this format)**
- Clip Name: (e.g., EP12_C03_TikTok_v1)
  - 00:01–00:02: tighten first pause / start 0.3s later
  - 00:07: caption word should be “clients” not “client”
  - End card: change CTA to “DM ‘CLIPS’ for the workflow”
