Section 1 — Overview
We turn long-form podcast/video content into ready-to-post vertical clips (TikTok / Reels / Shorts). Each clip is designed to maximize retention: strong hook, readable captions, clean audio, and platform-safe framing.

Section 2 — What You Will Receive (Delivery Pack)
For each order, you receive a “Delivery Pack” folder containing:
1) Final MP4 clips (vertical)
2) Caption file for each clip (.SRT)
3) B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggestions)
4) Posting suggestions per clip (first-line caption + hashtags + optional CTA)

Section 3 — QA Standards (What We Check Before You See Anything)
Video + Framing
- 9:16 vertical format; subject centered; no cropped faces/hands when avoidable.
- Safe zones respected: captions and key visuals do not sit under platform UI areas.
- No distracting zooms; jump cuts are intentional and clean.

Captions
- Captions match spoken words (high accuracy; names/brands double-checked).
- Punctuation and line breaks improve readability.
- Keyword emphasis used sparingly (not “shouty”).

Audio
- Speech is clear and consistent; no sudden volume jumps.
- Background noise reduced without warbling artifacts.
- Music (if used) is low enough to keep speech intelligible.

Hook + Story
- First 1–2 seconds clearly state the “why watch” (hook).
- Clip has a clear point and ends cleanly (no awkward cutoff).

Export + Platform Compatibility
- MP4/H.264 export; plays cleanly on mobile.
- No black bars; no stretched aspect ratio.

Section 4 — Review Flow (Timestamp Comments)
We use a review link that supports timestamped feedback (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube).
How to request changes:
1) Watch the clip.
2) Leave comments with timestamps (example: “00:07 change ‘their’ to ‘there’”).
3) Group feedback per clip if possible.

Section 5 — Revision Window
Included revisions must be requested within 24 hours of delivery.
Why: this keeps turnaround fast and protects your content calendar.

Section 6 — What Counts as a Free Revision vs a Paid Change
Free revisions (included within the 24h window):
- Caption typos/wording fixes to match audio
- Minor timing tweaks to captions
- Small audio leveling adjustments
- Minor crop/reframe to avoid cropped face/UI overlap
- Replacing a b-roll suggestion with a similar alternative

Paid changes (new scope):
- Changing the clip selection (different sections of the long video)
- New hooks/rewrites due to preference changes after delivery
- Brand/visual style overhaul introduced after work started
- Major restructuring (new narrative) or adding new source files

If something is clearly our mistake (missed requirement from your intake), we fix it promptly.

Section 7 — How to Get the Best Results (Client Responsibilities)
To maximize quality and minimize back-and-forth:
- Provide your target platforms (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
- Provide your handle(s), CTA, and any “banned words”
- Share brand notes (fonts/colors/emoji usage preferences) if relevant
- Tell us if you prefer aggressive jump cuts vs more natural pacing
