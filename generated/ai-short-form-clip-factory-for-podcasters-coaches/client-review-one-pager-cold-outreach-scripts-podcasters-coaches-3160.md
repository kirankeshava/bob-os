## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips & Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for reviewing your clips from AI Short-Form Clip Factory.** To keep everything fast and hit the 48-hour workflow, please use the format below.

### What to Review (in this order)
1) **Hook strength (first 1–2 seconds):** Would *your audience* stop scrolling?
2) **Caption readability:** Are captions easy to read on a phone? Any typos?
3) **Pacing:** No long dead air; pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds.
4) **Accuracy:** Any misheard words/names?
5) **Brand fit:** Does the tone match your style (bold vs clean, energetic vs calm)?
6) **CTA + end frame:** Is the call-to-action correct and appropriate?

### Revision Policy (fast + clean)
- **Included (free):** 1 revision round per clip, limited to **caption fixes, timing trims, hook swap from provided timestamps, CTA text swap, and small layout tweaks** (font size/position within preset).
- **Not included in the free revision round:** full re-edits, new clip topic selection from scratch, new b-roll research sourcing, or changing the overall style preset after delivery.
- **Turnaround:** revision requests submitted within **48 hours of delivery** are returned within **24 hours**.

### How to Request Revisions (required format)
Reply with one bullet per clip:
- **Clip Filename:** (example: `ACME_EP12_C03_HowToCloseDeals_TT_v1.mp4`)
- **Timecode:** (example: `00:03–00:07`)
- **Change Requested:** (example: “Change caption to: ‘Most agents lose deals in the follow-up.’”)
- **Optional:** any brand words to avoid / must-use phrases

### If You Want More Than 10 Clips
Tell us your next episode link/file and whether you want: 
- **10 clips / week**, **20 clips / week**, or **daily posting pack**. We’ll confirm turnaround and batching.
