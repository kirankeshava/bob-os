## 1) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each clip, you receive:
1. **Final MP4 (vertical, ready to post)**
2. **Captions file (.SRT)** matching the final MP4
3. **B-roll / on-screen cue sheet** (timestamps + suggested inserts)
4. **Posting suggestions** per clip:
   - Suggested first-line caption (hook)
   - CTA suggestion
   - Hashtags (optional, platform-appropriate)

We deliver everything in one organized folder with consistent naming.

---

## 2) Our QA standard (what we check before you see it)
We run QC on every clip so you don’t lose time on avoidable fixes:

### Framing & safe zones
- No cropped faces (forehead/chin not unintentionally cut)
- Subject stays inside **platform safe zones** (top/bottom UI areas)
- Any punch-ins/reframes preserve natural composition

### Captions accuracy & readability
- Captions match spoken audio (names/terms checked)
- Line breaks are readable; no overloaded lines
- On-screen text avoids UI overlays

### Audio
- Clear voice; no clipping or harsh peaks
- Consistent loudness across clips from the same episode
- Background noise reduced without “robotic” artifacts

### Edits & pacing
- Hook clarity in first 1–2 seconds
- Jump cuts feel intentional (no awkward micro-pauses)
- No missing context caused by over-trimming

### Export / formatting
- Vertical format optimized for TikTok/IG Reels/YouTube Shorts
- Crisp text rendering; no blurry captions
- Standard-friendly MP4 output

---

## 3) Review flow (timestamp comments)
We provide a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave **timestamped** comments.

### How to review fast
- Watch clips once straight through.
- Then rewatch and leave **timestamped notes** (e.g., “00:07 change caption to …”).
- Group feedback per clip, not scattered across emails.

### Revision window
- **24-hour revision window** starts when we send the review link.
- Revisions are turned around as fast as possible; timing depends on volume and complexity.

If you miss the revision window, we can still help—see revision policy below.

---

## 4) Revisions policy (what’s free vs paid)
We aim to protect speed and quality while keeping scope clear.

### Included (free) revisions
Free revisions cover fixing anything that violates the approved specs or our QA standard, such as:
- Caption typos/misheard words
- Minor timing adjustments to captions
- Small reframing fixes (face cropped, unsafe zones)
- Audio leveling fix if inconsistent within the clip set
- Removing a filler word/pause when it doesn’t change meaning

### Not included (paid changes)
These are considered new scope and may require an additional fee:
- Changing the clip selection/topic after drafts are delivered (“make it about a different part of the episode”)
- Major rewrite of hooks/captions to change tone/strategy (beyond small tweaks)
- Adding new clips beyond the original order
- New branding package after delivery (new fonts/colors/logo rules)
- Significant b-roll sourcing or custom motion design beyond the original agreement

### If something is our fault
If we missed a clear requirement you provided in the intake, we fix it quickly.
