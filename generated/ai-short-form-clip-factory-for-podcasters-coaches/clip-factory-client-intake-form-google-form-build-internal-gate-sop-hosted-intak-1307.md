# 2) Internal Gate SOP (must-follow rule to protect reputation)

## Rule: No intake = no work
**We do not start editing until we have (a) a completed Intake Form OR (b) an explicit written waiver from the client** (email message: “Proceed without intake; use your best judgement.”). This is non-negotiable for new clients.

## Why
Most refunds and 1-star reviews come from mismatched expectations: hook tone, caption style, banned words, and framing preferences. The intake captures these up front and reduces rework.

## Workflow
1) **Order received** → send onboarding email with:
   - legitimacy URL: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
   - support: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
   - Intake Form link (once created)
   - ‘What to Expect’ page link (once published)
2) **Wait state:**
   - If Intake not submitted within **12 hours**, send one reminder.
   - If still not submitted within **24 hours**, message: “We can start as soon as intake is complete; delivery timeline begins when we receive it.”
3) **Once intake received** → project kickoff:
   - Confirm platform targets + clip count
   - Confirm banned words + framing preference
   - Confirm CTA preference
4) **If client waives intake** → reply confirming:
   - “Proceeding without intake; first cut will follow our default style. Revisions outside technical fixes may be billable.”

## Defaults (used only if intake is waived)
- Platform: TikTok/Reels/Shorts-friendly 9:16
- Captions: clean, high-readability, 2 lines max, emphasis highlights
- Hooks: direct + curiosity hybrid
- Framing: protect face/eyes; avoid aggressive crops
- CTA: “Follow for more” (light)
