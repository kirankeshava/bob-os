# B) Demo Clip Templates (6) — Fill-in-the-blank scripts + edit beats
Use any permission-friendly footage (Creative Commons) OR record original talking-head. Each template yields a 25–45s clip.

## Real Estate Demo 1 (Story Hook)
**Hook (0–2s, big text):** “This is why your listing isn’t getting offers.”
**Line (2–8s):** Identify one common failure (photos, pricing, first 3 seconds of walkthrough).
**Payoff (8–25s):** 2-step fix.
**CTA (last 3s):** “Want 10 clips/week from your tours? Email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”
**Captions:** Highlight words: “isn’t getting offers”, “first 3 seconds”, “2-step fix”.
**Edit cadence:** Cut every 1.5–2.5s. Add 1 punch-in zoom on the payoff.
**B-roll cues (on-screen notes or placeholders):** [B-ROLL: exterior shot], [B-ROLL: pricing graph], [B-ROLL: before/after photo].

## Real Estate Demo 2 (Contrarian Take)
**Hook text:** “Stop saying ‘open concept’.”
**Body:** Explain why generic descriptors don’t sell; offer 3 specific phrasing swaps.
**Structure:** Problem → why it fails → 3 swaps.
**Captions:** Emphasize “Stop saying”, “generic”, “3 swaps”.
**Edits:** 3 quick pattern interrupts (emoji-free): subtle whoosh + zoom.

## Fitness Demo 1 (Listicle)
**Hook text:** “3 mistakes keeping you from losing fat.”
**Body:** Mistake #1 (8s), #2 (8s), #3 (8s).
**CTA:** “Want this turned into daily clips? agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”
**Captions:** Big numbered callouts (1/2/3). Keywords: “protein”, “steps”, “sleep”.
**Edits:** Hard cuts at each number. Add progress bar at bottom.

## Fitness Demo 2 (Testimonial/Case)
**Hook text:** “Client down 12lbs without ‘more cardio’.”
**Body:** 1 sentence context → 2 changes made → result.
**Safety:** Avoid medical claims; keep to general fitness results, “individual results vary”.
**Captions:** Emphasize “12lbs”, “2 changes”.
**Edits:** Overlay simple ‘before/after’ text card (not body photos unless permitted).

## B2B Demo 1 (Story Hook)
**Hook text:** “We wasted $8k/month until we fixed THIS.”
**Body:** What was happening → what was changed → measurable outcome.
**Captions:** Emphasize “$8k/month”, “fixed”, “outcome”.
**Edits:** Add on-screen ‘Lesson’ label at the payoff.

## B2B Demo 2 (Contrarian Take)
**Hook text:** “More leads won’t fix your pipeline.”
**Body:** Explain bottleneck (speed-to-lead, qualification, follow-up). Give 2 actions.
**Captions:** Emphasize “won’t fix”, “bottleneck”, “2 actions”.
**Edits:** Add quick cutaway card: “Pipeline ≠ Leads”.
