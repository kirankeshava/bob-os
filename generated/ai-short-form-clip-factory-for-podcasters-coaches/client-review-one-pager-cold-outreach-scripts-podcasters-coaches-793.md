What you’re receiving

- Hook-first pacing + pattern interrupts
- Exported in platform-safe format (9:16, high bitrate)

How to review (fastest method)
1) Watch the clips in order.
2) Mark any clip as:
   - APPROVE (good to post)
   - REVISE (needs a specific change)
   - REJECT (do not use; replace if replacement is included in your package)
3) For each REVISE clip, give 1–2 specific edits only (keep turnaround fast).

How to request revisions (required format)
Reply to the delivery email and paste this block:

REVISION REQUESTS
Clip File Name:
Timecode (00:00–00:00):
Change requested:
Priority: Must-have / Nice-to-have

Examples of good revision requests
- “Clip RE_01… Timecode 00:03–00:07: Remove the word ‘basically’ from captions; keep meaning same.”
- “Clip FIT_04… Timecode 00:00–00:02: Swap hook line to: ‘If you’re doing THIS, your squat will stall.’”
- “Clip B2B_07… Timecode 00:10–00:13: Add b-roll cue: ‘overlay dashboard screenshot’.”

Examples of slow/unclear requests (avoid)
- “Make it punchier.” (What exactly should change?)
- “I don’t like it.” (Which part: hook, captions, pacing, framing?)

Revision policy (designed to protect speed)
- 1 revision round included per batch of 10 clips.
- Revisions cover: caption fixes, small trims, hook text swap, mild pacing changes, b-roll cue swaps.
- Not included (new scope): new style direction, full re-edit, new topic/storyline, new footage sourcing beyond simple stock, multiple brand refreshes.
- If you send revisions in the required format within 24 hours of delivery, we prioritize turnaround.

Posting note
If a clip is “Approved,” you can post as-is. If you want platform-specific caption copy/hashtags, request it and we’ll add it as a separate add-on.
