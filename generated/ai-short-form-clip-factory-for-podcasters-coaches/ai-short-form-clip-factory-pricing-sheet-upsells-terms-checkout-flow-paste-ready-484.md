WHAT COUNTS AS A “CLIP” (to avoid confusion)
A clip is a single exported vertical video (1080x1920) that includes:

Each clip is one deliverable regardless of minor variant requests. If you want multiple variants (different hooks, different CTAs, different framing), that’s billed as additional clips or an upsell.
