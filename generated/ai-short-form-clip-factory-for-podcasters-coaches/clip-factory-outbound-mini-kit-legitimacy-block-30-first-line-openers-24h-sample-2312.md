## 3) 24-Hour Sample Clip Offer — Fulfillment SOP (DFY revenue path)

### Goal
Deliver **1 free, ready-to-post 9:16 vertical clip** within **24 hours** of receiving a link, to prove quality and convert to a DFY package. This is the fastest path to revenue (and to the $5k gate before SaaS work).

### Scope of the free sample
- 1 vertical clip (9:16), **15–35 seconds**
- Burned-in captions (clean, high-contrast)
- Hook text overlay (first 1–2 seconds)
- Basic cutdowns (remove dead air, tighten pacing)
- Optional: simple b-roll cue notes (if not adding b-roll in sample)

### Intake (send immediately when prospect says “yes”)
Ask for:
1) Source: YouTube link / podcast video link / raw file link
2) Best episode to clip (or “pick the best moment for me”)
3) Target platform: TikTok / Reels / Shorts (default: Reels)
4) Brand preferences: font style (clean/bold), colors (if any), and any words to avoid
5) Desired CTA: “Follow for more”, “DM ‘X’”, or none
6) Any existing high-performing clip (so we mimic style)

Include legitimacy block:

- Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### Production steps (internal)
1) **Select moment (10 minutes):**
   - Look for: contrarian hook, story, list (“3 mistakes”), clear teaching moment, emotional beat.
   - Aim for a cold open that starts mid-sentence if needed.
2) **Transcribe + identify hook line (10 minutes):**
   - Pull a hook phrase for on-screen text.
3) **Edit (30–60 minutes):**
   - Hard cut dead air, tighten pauses, remove tangents.
   - Add subtle zoom/cuts every 1.5–3 seconds if video allows.
4) **Captions (15–25 minutes):**
   - Max 1–2 lines on screen; highlight keywords.
   - Avoid covering face; keep safe margins.
5) **Export specs (5 minutes):**
   - 1080x1920, H.264, 30fps, AAC audio.
   - Filename: [Creator]_[Episode]_[Topic]_9x16_sample.mp4
6) **QA checklist (5 minutes):**
   - Hook visible in first 2 seconds
   - Captions readable on mobile
   - No clipped audio, no desync
   - Ends cleanly (no abrupt cutoff)

### Delivery
Send:
- The .mp4 (Drive link)
- Suggested caption text (1–2 options)
- 3–5 hashtags tailored to niche
- Posting note: “Post natively, don’t reuse TikTok watermark for Reels.”

### Conversion sequence (immediately after delivery)
Send a short close message:
- “If you want, I can do **X clips/week** with the same style so you stay consistent. Want me to propose a pack based on your posting goals?”
- Offer two DFY choices (light + standard), and ask a binary question: “Want 8/week or 16/week?”

### What to log in CRM
- Prospect name + handle + niche
- Source link
- Delivered timestamp (must be <24h)
- Their reaction (1–2 quotes)
- Next step: “Send packages” / “Book close call” / “No response”

### Guardrails
- Do not mention building SaaS; keep focus on DFY outcomes.
- If they ask for automation: “We’re validating demand; right now we deliver the same output DFY, and subscription automation comes after initial client volume.”
