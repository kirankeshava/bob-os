Welcome to Clip Factory. This page explains exactly how we turn your long-form video/podcast into ready-to-post vertical clips—plus how review, delivery, and revisions work.

1) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch/order we deliver a “Delivery Pack” folder containing:
A) Final MP4 clips (vertical)
- Ready to post on TikTok/Reels/Shorts (platform-specific specs noted below)
- Clean jump cuts and pacing
- Hook-focused opening

B) Caption file for each clip (SRT)
- Timecoded subtitles aligned to spoken audio
- Clean punctuation and line breaks for readability

C) B-Roll / On-screen cue sheet (per clip)
- Timestamped prompts like “Insert screenshot,” “Add product shot,” “Add zoom,” “Add emoji/arrow,” etc.
- Notes for emphasis moments (quotes, stats, punchlines)

D) Posting suggestions (per clip)
- Suggested first-line caption (hook line)
- Suggested CTA (comment keyword, follow, link in bio, etc.)
- Hashtag set (starter set; you can edit to match your niche)

2) Our QA standards (how we prevent preventable issues)
We run a consistent QA checklist before anything is sent:
Framing & Safe Zones
- No cropped faces (eyes/mouth not cut off)
- Text stays inside safe zones (not covered by UI on TikTok/IG)
- Subject remains centered; no accidental off-screen movement

Captions Accuracy & Style
- Captions match the audio (names/terms checked)
- No distracting typos; consistent style (case, punctuation)
- Lines are short and readable (no giant paragraph blocks)

Audio
- Audio is clear and intelligible
- No harsh peaks/clipping; levels are consistent across clips
- Background noise reduced when possible without artifacts

Edits
- Strong hook in first 1–2 seconds (pattern interrupt or clear promise)
- Jump cuts feel natural (no awkward mid-word chops)
- Dead air removed; pacing optimized for retention

Export / Platform Compliance
- Format: Vertical 9:16
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Codec: H.264 (MP4)
- Audio: AAC
- File names are consistent and easy to match to notes

3) Review flow (simple + timestamped)
We send a review link where you can comment with timestamps.
Supported free options:
- Frame.io review link (preferred)
- Vimeo review link
- Unlisted YouTube link

How to request changes:
- Leave comments directly on the review link with timestamps
  Example: “00:07 change ‘customers’ to ‘clients’ in captions”
- If you prefer email, paste: Clip name + timestamp + requested change.

4) Revision policy (protects speed + quality)
Revision Window: 24 hours
- You have 24 hours from delivery to submit revision notes.
- After 24 hours, we treat new requests as a new round (handled as scheduling allows).

What counts as FREE revisions (within 24 hours)
- Caption fixes (typos, misheard words)
- Minor timing adjustments to captions
- Small punch-in/out trim (1–2 seconds) to tighten pacing
- Safe-zone text adjustments (if platform UI overlaps)
- Minor audio leveling adjustments

What counts as a change request (out of scope)
These are not “fixes”—they change the creative direction or require re-editing:
- Changing the clip topic/angle after delivery
- Rebuilding multiple clips from scratch from new timestamps
- Adding brand-new assets not provided originally (logos, b-roll libraries, etc.)
- New CTA strategy across the whole batch

If a request is out-of-scope, we’ll clearly label it as a “Change Request” and suggest the fastest option (either a new batch or a new round).

5) What we need from you (fastest way to great clips)
Please provide:
- Your target platforms (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts)
- Your tone (high-energy, calm authority, comedic, etc.)
- Any banned words/topics
- Your handle(s) and preferred CTA
- Any must-include phrases (course name, offer name, etc.)
