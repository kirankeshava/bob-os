**Contact:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

## 1) What you’ll receive (standard delivery)
You’ll receive a folder/link containing:
- **10 vertical clips** (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts-ready)
- **Burned-in captions** (brand style preset)
- **Hook-first structure** (hook within first ~1–1.5s)
- **Notes file** (optional): clip titles + source timestamps + suggested post text

## 2) The fastest way to review (10 minutes)
Please review in this order:
1. **Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds):** Would you stop scrolling?
2. **Caption readability:** Easy to read on mobile? No blocked faces?
3. **Pacing:** Any dead air? Any awkward jump cuts?
4. **Meaning:** Did we preserve the original intent (no misleading edit)?
5. **Brand safety:** No sensitive claims, private info, or off-brand language.

## 3) What counts as a revision (covered)
Revisions are meant for **fixes**, not new creative direction. Covered revisions include:
- Caption typos, punctuation, missing words
- Timing tweaks (captions slightly earlier/later)
- Removing a sentence or tightening a pause
- Replacing a shot with an adjacent moment (same topic)
- Small reframes (safe crop/positioning)

## 4) What does NOT count as a revision (new scope)
These requests usually require a new clip/round:
- “Change the clip topic entirely” or choose a different section
- Rewriting the narrative to a different angle
- New style direction after approving a preset
- Heavy b-roll sourcing beyond cue notes (unless previously agreed)

## 5) Revision policy (SLA-protecting)
- **Included:** 1 revision round for up to **5 clips** (within 7 days of delivery).
- **Turnaround for revisions:** typically **24 hours** after we receive a complete revision request.
- **If you need more:** request a second revision round; we’ll confirm scope and timing.
