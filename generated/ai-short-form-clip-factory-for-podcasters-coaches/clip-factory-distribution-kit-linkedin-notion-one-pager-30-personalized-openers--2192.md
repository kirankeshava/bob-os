## Production Steps (internal checklist)
1) Pull best 60–120 seconds candidate moments (aim for 20–45s final)
2) Choose 1 moment with: clear pain → insight → payoff
3) Edit to 9:16:
   - Center speaker, remove dead air
   - Add light jump cuts (pacing)
4) Captions:
   - Burned-in, high contrast, 2 lines max
   - Emphasize 3–6 keywords (bold/color)
5) Hook text:
   - Add a hook line in first 1 second (top text)
6) Audio cleanup:
   - Normalize levels, remove harsh noise if present
7) Export:
   - MP4 1080x1920
   - Optional: SRT file
