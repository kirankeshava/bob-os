## 1) Lead Sourcing Rubric (Free / Fast)
### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
**Primary:** Podcasters + coaches who already publish long-form weekly but inconsistent short-form.
**Strong signals:**
- 1+ episodes/week (or 2+/month with backlog)
- Active Instagram/TikTok/YouTube Shorts but irregular cadence
- Talks-head + advice format (easy to clip)
- Has offers (course/coaching/consulting) = direct ROI

### Exclusions (low conversion)
- Pure entertainment podcasts with heavy copyrighted media
- Creators demanding daily edits but unwilling to pay
- Channels with no long-form source content

### Quick qualification score (0–10)
- Long-form frequency (0–3)
- Existing short-form presence (0–2)
- Clear monetization offer (0–3)
- On-camera clarity/audio quality (0–2)
**Target:** 7+.

### Where to source (no paid tools)
**YouTube search queries:**
- "podcast" + "episode" + "coach" + "#" (filter last month)
- "business coach" "podcast" "episode"
- "real estate coach" "podcast"
- "fitness coach" "podcast"
- "agency owner" "podcast"

**Spotify/Apple Podcasts directories:**
- Browse categories: Business, Health, Education
- Look for shows with recent episodes + host is also service provider.

**LinkedIn search (manual):**
- Title contains: Coach, Founder, Consultant, Agency Owner + keyword "podcast"
- Filter by "Posted content" in last 30 days.

**Instagram/TikTok search:**
- Hashtags: #businesspodcast #coachpodcast #founderpodcast
- Accounts with long captions + talking head videos.

### Minimum lead list fields (copy/paste into a sheet)
- Name
- Role (podcaster/coach)
- Niche
- Platform + URL (YT/IG/LI)
- Email/DM link
- Score (0–10)
- Recent episode URL (source content)
- Note: what they sell (offer)

## 2) Discovery → Paid Pilot Call Script (20 minutes)
**Positioning:** “We turn your long episodes into ready-to-post vertical clips with hooks/captions and a consistent weekly cadence—without you hiring an editor.”

### Pre-call setup (2 minutes)
- Pull one recent episode.
- Note 2 potential clip moments (timestamps if possible).
- Prepare a simple outcome: “We can deliver X clips/week that point to your offer.”

### Opening (Minute 0–2)
“Thanks for jumping on. I’m Bob. I run Clip Factory (here’s our page if you want to verify: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3). 

In 20 minutes I want to understand your current content workflow, what ‘winning’ looks like for short-form, and if it makes sense to run a small paid pilot where we deliver clips weekly. Sound good?”

### Context + pain (Minute 2–7)
1) “Where do clips fit in your growth strategy today—lead gen, authority, or sales?”
2) “What’s your current clip workflow? Who edits? How long does it take?”
3) “What’s been the biggest blocker to posting consistently?”
4) “How many short clips would you *ideally* post per week if it was easy?”

### Value + ROI anchoring (Minute 7–10)
“Quick benchmark: most creators we talk to don’t need 100 clips—they need **consistent 3–7 clips/week** that are formatted correctly and map to a clear CTA.

What matters most for you:
- views/reach,
- DMs/leads,
- or clicks to an offer?”

### Workflow requirements (Minute 10–14)
5) “Do you prefer clips that are: educational tips, contrarian takes, personal stories, or Q&A?”
6) “Any non-negotiables? (brand fonts/colors, censored words, on-screen captions style)”
7) “Do you want b-roll prompts and on-screen hook rewrites, or just clean captions?”
8) “Do you need speaker labeling (diarization) when there are guests?”

### The Paid Pilot Offer (Minute 14–18)
“Here’s what I recommend: a **2-week paid pilot** so you can evaluate quality and cadence without committing long-term.

**Pilot deliverable (example):**
- 10 clips over 2 weeks (5/week)
- 9:16 format, burned-in captions, platform-safe framing
- Hook options + suggested titles
- B-roll cues (optional)

**Process:** you send a YouTube link or upload the file, we deliver a folder of ready-to-post videos.

If this is useful, the next step is simple: reply to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to with your best episode link + your branding notes, and we’ll start the pilot.”

**Pricing close (choose one):**
A) “For the pilot, we usually start at **$___** depending on volume and complexity. If we hit your quality bar, we can roll into a monthly plan.”
B) “If we could reliably deliver 3–7 clips/week that match your brand, what monthly budget would that replace—an editor, VA time, or your own time?”

### Transition to SaaS validation (Minute 18–20, keep brief)
“I’m also validating a self-serve version later (not available yet). If it existed, what would you pay monthly for something that turns an episode link into highlight clips with captions?
- $99/mo
- $199/mo
- $299/mo
And what feature would make it a ‘must-have’?”

Close:
“I’ll email a quick recap and the next step. If you send the link today to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to, we can start immediately.”
