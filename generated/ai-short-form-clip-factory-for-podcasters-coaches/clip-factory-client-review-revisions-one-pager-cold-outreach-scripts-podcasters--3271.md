1) CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW CLIPS + REQUEST REVISIONS
Title: How to Review Your Clips (Fast) + Request Revisions

Goal: Approve 10 clips quickly with minimal back-and-forth so we can keep the 48-hour delivery pace.

What you’ll receive
- 10 vertical clips (TikTok/Reels/Shorts-ready)
- Burned-in captions + hook-first edit
- A “Clip Notes” summary (what the clip is about + suggested caption/CTA)
- Optional: b-roll cues included as on-screen notes or as a short edit note (based on your package)

How to review in under 10 minutes
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) On the second watch, pause only if something is clearly wrong (mis-caption, awkward cut, brand risk).
3) Decide one of three statuses per clip:
   - APPROVE
   - REVISE (minor changes)
   - REPLACE (swap for a different moment)

Revision policy (built for speed)
- Included: 1 revision round per clip (minor edits only) within the 7-day free trial.
- Turnaround: revisions delivered within 24 hours of receiving a complete revision request.
- “Minor edits” include:
  - Fix a caption word/line break
  - Remove a sentence or tighten a pause
  - Adjust caption position/safe area
  - Swap CTA text (same CTA style)
  - Replace the hook line from our suggested options (if provided)
- Not included in the free trial (requires a new request later):
  - Full style redesign (new fonts/colors/layout)
  - Rebuilding the entire clip narrative from scratch
  - Sourcing/licensing paid b-roll or paid music

Exactly how to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to the delivery email with this template:

Subject: Revisions — [ClientName] — [Episode/VideoName]

Clip 01 — [APPROVE / REVISE / REPLACE]
Timestamp (if revise/replace): [00:00–00:00]
Change request: [e.g., “Caption line 2 should say ‘cash flow’ not ‘cashflows’”]

Clip 02 — [APPROVE / REVISE / REPLACE]
Timestamp:
Change request:

(Repeat…)

If you want a replacement clip
- Tell us the replacement angle you want (pick one):
  - More contrarian
  - More tactical/how-to
  - More emotional/story
  - More authority/credibility
- Optional: send 1–2 timestamps you already like.

Common approval checklist (what “good” looks like)
- Hook in the first 1.5 seconds
- Clear captions, readable on mobile, inside safe areas
- No brand/compliance red flags
- Ends with a clear CTA or curiosity loop

Need help choosing clips?
Reply “Pick for me” and tell us your goal (leads, authority, followers). We’ll recommend the best 5 to post first.
