Title: Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)

Welcome — this page explains exactly how Clip Factory works, what “done” means, and how to review clips quickly.

Official site (legitimacy / portfolio): https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
Support: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

1) What you will receive (Delivery Pack)
For each order, you’ll receive a delivery folder containing:
- Final MP4 clips (vertical, ready to post)
- Captions file for each clip (.SRT)
- B-roll cue sheet (timestamps + suggested overlays/cutaways)
- Posting suggestions per clip:
  - Recommended first-line caption (hook)
  - Suggested hashtags (platform-appropriate)
  - CTA suggestion (follow, comment, book call, etc.)

2) Quality standards we check (QA)
Before delivery, every clip is checked for:
- Hook clarity: first 1–2 seconds clearly communicates “why watch”
- Captions accuracy: correct words, punctuation, speaker labels if needed
- Caption readability: safe margins, high contrast, no blocking faces
- Framing: no cropped faces/chins; subject centered for vertical
- Audio: consistent loudness, no clipping, reduced background noise
- Jump cuts: clean pacing; no awkward mid-word cuts
- Brand safety: removes filler/off-topic tangents; avoids sensitive moments unless requested
- Platform compliance: formatting suitable for TikTok/IG Reels/YouTube Shorts

3) Review process (fast + timestamped)
To keep revisions fast and avoid back-and-forth, please review using timestamped comments.
- You’ll get a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) OR a folder with MP4s.
- Leave comments as:
  - Clip name
  - Timestamp (e.g., 00:07)
  - What to change (be specific)

Example: “Clip_03 — 00:07: change caption ‘there’ to ‘their’.”

4) Revision window
- You have 24 hours after delivery to request revisions included in the order.
- If we don’t hear back within 24 hours, we consider the delivery approved so we can keep turnaround fast for everyone.

5) What counts as a free revision vs a paid change
Included (free, within 24h):
- Caption typo fixes
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, small cut adjustment)
- Safe-zone adjustments (move captions up/down, avoid covering face)
- Basic audio level corrections (if something slipped)
- Export fix (wrong resolution/aspect ratio)

Usually paid (scope change):
- Changing clip selection criteria (new hooks/topics after delivery)
- Adding new clips beyond the agreed number
- Major re-edit requests (new narrative arc, major restructuring)
- New branding package added late (fonts/colors/overlays not provided at intake)
- Revisions requested after the 24h window

If something is our mistake (e.g., wrong aspect ratio, broken file), we fix it regardless.

6) How to get the best results (2-minute checklist)
Before we start, please confirm:
- Target platform(s): TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- Your handle(s) + preferred CTA link
- Any banned words or topics
- Tone preference: punchy / calm / authoritative / comedic
- Any brand assets: logo, font, colors (optional)
