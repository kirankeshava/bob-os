## 1) What we deliver (Delivery Pack)
For each clip batch, you receive a **Delivery Pack** containing:

1. **Final vertical MP4(s)** (ready to post)
2. **Caption file(s) (.SRT)** matching the final MP4 timecode

4. **Posting suggestions**

## 2) Our QA standards (what we check before you ever see it)
We run QA on every clip to protect your brand and avoid rework.

### A) Safe zones + framing (no cropped faces)
- Subject’s eyes/face stay clear of platform UI overlays (captions, buttons, usernames)
- No “forehead cut” or chin cut unless stylistically intentional
- If the source is widescreen, crop/zoom is stabilized and does not feel jittery

### B) Captions accuracy + readability
- Captions match spoken words (no meaning-changing errors)
- Proper nouns fixed when provided (names, brand terms, product names)
- Clean line breaks and pacing (no walls of text)
- High contrast styling for legibility (including on busy backgrounds)

### C) Audio quality + leveling
- Speech is clear and prioritized
- Loudness is consistent across clips in the same batch
- No clipping/distortion; background noise reduced when possible

### D) Jump cuts + pacing
- Cuts feel intentional (no awkward mid-word cuts)
- Dead air removed without making speech unnatural
- Visual variety is used strategically (zoom, b-roll cues, on-screen text)

### E) Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds)
- Clear hook or curiosity opener
- On-screen text supports the hook
- The viewer understands what they’ll get by watching

### F) Export settings (platform-ready)
- Vertical format: **1080×1920**
- H.264 MP4 (standard)
- Crisp captions (not blurred from over-compression)
- Clip length matches the intended platform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)

## 4) Revisions & issue triage (what’s free vs. a new scope)
We want you happy and posting consistently. The key is separating **fixes** (our responsibility) from **new creative direction** (scope change).

### Free revisions (included)
These are fixes to meet the agreed spec:
- Caption typos / misheard words
- Minor timing tweaks to captions
- Safe-zone/framing fixes (cropped faces, UI overlap)
- Audio level corrections if inconsistent or unintelligible
- Small hook text adjustments that don’t change the core angle
- Export/format errors (wrong resolution, broken file, missing SRT)

### Not included (scope change)
These are treated as a **new request** (handled after the included revisions are complete):
- Changing the selected moments/topics after editing (e.g., “use a different section of the podcast”)
- Rewriting the clip angle entirely (new hook concept, new story structure)
- Major style change after delivery (new caption style, new pacing approach) not specified upfront
- New branding package introduced after work began (fonts/colors/logos)
- Additional clips beyond the agreed count

If a request falls into scope change, we’ll clearly label it as such and offer the fastest path to get it done.
