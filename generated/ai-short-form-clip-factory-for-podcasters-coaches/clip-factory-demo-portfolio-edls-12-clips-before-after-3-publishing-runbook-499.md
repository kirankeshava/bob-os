Below is a paste-ready production package to generate the portfolio quickly. It assumes any editor tool (CapCut/Descript/Premiere) and exports 9:16 vertical 1080x1920 with burned-in captions.

GLOBAL STYLE (apply to all clips)
- Aspect/Export: 1080x1920, 30fps, 10–45s length target (platform-friendly).
- Safe margins: keep captions inside center 80% width, bottom 25% height.
- Caption style: bold white, black stroke 6–8px, 2 lines max, keyword highlight in yellow (#FFD400). Add subtle pop-in (0.08s) on emphasized words.
- Cuts: remove >250ms dead air. Add punch-in zooms 105–115% on emphasis words (1–2 per sentence).
- B-roll cues: show as small top-left label “B-ROLL: …” for demo clarity (or overlay brief b-roll footage if available).
- CTA end-card (last 1.0s): “Want clips like this? 24hr turnarounds. https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/3 | agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”

REAL ESTATE (4 clips)

RE-1 Storytelling Hook (25–35s)
- Structure:
  1) Hook (0–3s): “This is why your open house isn’t working…”
  2) Personal story beat (3–18s): narrator describes a real scenario (low turnout / wrong buyers / no follow-up).
  3) Lesson (18–30s): 2 actionable changes.
  4) CTA (final 1s end-card).
- Edit beats:
  - Add hard cut after hook.
  - Punch-in on “isn’t working”, “one change”, “follow-up”.
  - Insert 2 quick b-roll flashes (0.6–0.8s) during lesson.
- B-roll cues:
  - “B-ROLL: empty open house sign-in sheet”
  - “B-ROLL: agent texting leads / CRM follow-up screen”

RE-2 Listicle (20–30s)
- Hook (0–2s): “3 things that sell a listing faster (that nobody does).”
- List format (2–26s): #1, #2, #3 as on-screen kinetic text.
- Edit beats:
  - Use on-screen counters with soundless pop.
  - Cut tightly between list items; 0.2s gap max.
- B-roll cues:
  - “B-ROLL: bright living room wide shot”
  - “B-ROLL: before/after declutter”
  - “B-ROLL: drone exterior (2s)”

RE-3 Contrarian Take (15–25s)
- Hook: “Stop telling buyers ‘rates are coming down.’ It’s hurting your deal.”
- Point: explain why certainty > speculation; give alternate phrasing.
- Edit beats:
  - Add on-screen “CONTRARIAN:” label for first 2s.
  - Emphasize alternate phrasing with larger captions.
- B-roll cues:
  - “B-ROLL: mortgage rate chart blur”
  - “B-ROLL: handshake closing table”

RE-4 Testimonial Style (20–35s)
- Hook: “We got 7 offers in 4 days—here’s what we changed.”
- Format: quote-driven; overlay “Client result” + “What we did” lower-thirds.
- Edit beats:
  - Add subtle camera shake micro effect (optional) to feel UGC.
  - Insert 2 proof overlays (offer count, days on market).
- B-roll cues:
  - “B-ROLL: offer emails stack visual”
  - “B-ROLL: staging details closeups”
