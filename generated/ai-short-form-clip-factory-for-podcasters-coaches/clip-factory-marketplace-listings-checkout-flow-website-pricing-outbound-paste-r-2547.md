## 4) Guarantee + Terms (Client-Facing)

### Turnaround SLA
- **Starter:** 10 clips delivered within **48 hours** after we receive (a) source file/link and (b) brand notes.
- **Growth:** weekly delivery by **end of week** (or agreed schedule).
- **Pro:** delivered across **14 days** (or agreed sprint plan).

### Revisions
- Revisions are **batch-based** (submit notes in one message). 
- Starter includes **1 batch revision**. Growth includes **1 batch/week**. Pro includes **2 batch/sprint**.
- Revisions cover: captions timing, minor trims, hook swap, safe area fixes, minor audio tweaks.
- Revisions do **not** cover: changing the clip concept for every clip after delivery, full re-edits of all clips, or adding new source content.

### What counts as a “clip”
A clip is a **9:16 vertical video** typically **15–60 seconds** (unless otherwise requested), cut from your source content, with:
- a hook-forward opening,
- captions (burned-in) and optionally SRT,
- basic audio cleanup,
- export ready for TikTok/Reels/Shorts.

### Source content requirements
You provide:
- the long-form file (or link) + permission to download,
- any required brand assets (logo, font preference, colors),
- any “do-not-use” topics/timecodes.

### Content rights
- You keep full rights to your original content.
- We produce edits derived from your content for your use.
- We may not post your content publicly without written permission.

### Refund policy
- Because this is a digital, made-to-order service, **completed deliverables are non-refundable**.
- If we miss the SLA by more than **48 hours** (without an agreed extension), you may request:
  - a **pro-rated refund** for undelivered clips, or
  - equivalent replacement clips.

### Platform compliance
We avoid intentionally misleading edits. You are responsible for final posting decisions and any platform/account policy impacts.
