### B) Quality standards (QA checklist — what we check on every clip)
**Visual / framing**
- Subject’s face is not cropped (forehead/chin safe), no important text/graphics cut off.
- Safe zones respected for TikTok/Reels/Shorts UI overlays (caption area + right-side UI).
- No distracting jump in framing; reframe is intentional and consistent.

**Captions**
- Captions match spoken audio (names, brands, numbers, and jargon checked).
- Readability: line breaks are natural, no overcrowding, high contrast.
- Punctuation used for clarity; no awkward mid-word wraps.

**Audio**
- Loudness is consistent across the full clip (no sudden spikes/drops).
- Speech is clearly audible; background noise reduced if needed.
- No clipped peaks; no harsh sibilance.

**Edit & storytelling**
- Clear hook in first 1–2 seconds.
- Cuts are tight; dead air removed.
- On-screen text supports the main point; no conflicting messages.
- Any b-roll cue aligns with what’s being said (no misleading visuals).

**Export & platform formatting**
- Vertical 9:16 output.
- Clean MP4 export, social-friendly bitrate, crisp text.
- File naming matches delivery convention.

### C) Review flow (timestamp comments)
You’ll receive a single review link/folder containing all draft clips.

**How to request changes**
- Leave **timestamped comments** (e.g., “00:07 replace caption text”, “00:12 remove word”, “00:18 swap b-roll”).
- Consolidate feedback in one pass when possible.

**Revision window**
- Revisions are available for **24 hours** after the draft delivery link is sent.
- If we don’t receive notes within 24 hours, the project is considered approved and will be finalized/archived.

---

### D) What you receive (Delivery Pack)
You’ll get a structured folder with:
1) **Final MP4s** (ready to post)
2) **SRT caption files** (one per clip)
3) **B-roll cue sheet** (per clip: timestamp + recommended visuals)
4) **Posting suggestions** (per clip: first-line caption + hashtags + recommended platform notes)

---

### E) Revisions: what’s free vs paid (scope rules)
**Included (free) revisions** — within 24 hours:
- Caption corrections (accuracy/typos)
- Minor timing tweaks (caption timing, slight trim)
- Small framing adjustments (avoid cropped face, safe-zone fixes)
- Audio leveling fixes (if inconsistent)
- Swap a b-roll suggestion (if provided options in the cue sheet)

**Paid changes (scope change)** — quoted before work begins:
- Changing clip selection/angles after drafts are delivered (new moments, new story)
- New creative direction or new style guide after editing started
- Additional versions (multiple hooks, multiple caption styles) not originally included
- Major restructuring or rewriting captions for a different message/offer
- Requests after the 24-hour revision window

If something is our mistake (e.g., cropped face, missed safe zone, wrong caption text), we fix it.

---

### F) Quick tips to get the best results
- Share your best-performing past clips (links) so we can match tone.
- Provide correct spelling for names, brands, and any technical terms.
- Tell us what not to include (banned words, sensitive topics, competitor names).
