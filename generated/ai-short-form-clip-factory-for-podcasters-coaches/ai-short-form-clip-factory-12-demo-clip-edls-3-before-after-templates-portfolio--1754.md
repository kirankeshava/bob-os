C) CAPCUT STYLE BASELINE (use on every demo)
- Canvas: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Safe margins: keep captions above bottom UI area
- Captions: bold, high contrast (white text + black shadow), highlight 1–3 keywords in yellow
- Pattern: hook (0–2s) → proof/value (2–20s) → payoff (20–45s) → CTA (last 2s)
- Cuts: remove breaths, add subtle zoom (105–115%) on emphasis words
- B-roll: insert 2–4 quick cutaways max (0.6–1.2s each) using stock or simple on-screen text cards if no b-roll
- Sound: light whoosh on 2–3 transitions only
- End card (2s): “Want this for your podcast? agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to”
