## 4) 24-Hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (DFY)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 sample vertical clip within 24 hours of receiving a YouTube link (or file), demonstrating hook selection, captions, and platform formatting.

**Inputs to request (send as a single email):**
1) Episode link (YouTube) + best timestamps (optional)
2) Target platform (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
3) Audience (who is this for?)
4) Brand preferences (font/colors) + 1–2 reference creators (optional)
5) CTA (follow, comment, book call, download lead magnet)

**Process (time-boxed):**
1) **Ingest (10 min):** Pull audio/video, note title/topic, skim transcript (auto or YouTube captions).
2) **Select highlight (20 min):** Choose 1 moment with: clear point, emotion, contrast, or step-by-step framework. Target 20–45 sec.
3) **Hook + structure (15 min):** Write 2 hook options (first 1–2 seconds). Ensure payoff by 10–15 sec.
4) **Captioning (30 min):** Generate clean captions; remove filler; add emphasis words; keep 1–2 lines max; safe margins.
5) **Edit + pacing (45–60 min):** Jump cuts, remove dead air, add subtle zooms (optional), add b-roll cues as notes if not inserting.
6) **Export (10 min):** 1080x1920, H.264, 30fps, loudness normalized, filename: Client_Episode_Topic_V1.mp4
7) **QA (10 min):** Check readability on phone (safe zone), no caption overlap, hook hits fast.

**Deliverables (what you send):**
- 1x MP4 vertical clip (ready-to-post)
- 1x message with: suggested caption text + 3 title options + 5 hashtags (optional)
- 1x note: “Here are 2 additional moments I’d clip next” (to upsell package)
