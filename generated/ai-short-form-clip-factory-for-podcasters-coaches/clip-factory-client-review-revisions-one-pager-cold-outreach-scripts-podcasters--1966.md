## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for working with Clip Factory.** Here’s the fastest way to review your 10 vertical clips and request changes while keeping the 48-hour pace.

### What you’ll receive
- **10 ready-to-post vertical clips** (9:16) with burned-in captions
- **Platform exports** for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts (as requested)
- A **Delivery Notes** doc with: clip titles, hooks used, and any recommended post captions

### How to review (takes ~10 minutes)
1) Watch all clips once, **no pausing**, and mark your **top 3**.
2) Re-watch the remaining clips and note any **must-fix issues** (see checklist below).
3) Submit revision requests in the **exact format** below so we can execute quickly.

### What counts as a “revision” (included)
Included revisions are **small edits** that don’t change the core clip selection:
- Caption fixes (spelling, punctuation, speaker label)
- Timing tweaks (start/end trim, remove a pause)
- Minor repositioning (caption placement, safe-zone adjustments)
- Swap a hook line within the **same moment** (if the moment supports it)
- Adjust CTA end-card text (same template)

### What is a “new cut” (not included in revisions)
These requests require re-selecting content and are treated as a new clip:
- “Use a totally different moment from the episode”
- “Make this clip about another topic”
- “Replace the entire middle section”

### Revision request format (copy/paste)
For each clip, please send:
- **Clip filename:** (example: CF_ACME_EP12_C03_v1)
- **Timestamp(s):** (example: 00:07–00:11)
- **Change requested:** (example: “Remove ‘um’ + tighten pause”)
- **Optional:** desired wording for captions or end-card CTA

### Fast QA checklist (what to look for)
- Hook lands in first **0–1.5 seconds**
- No cut-off words at clip start
- Captions are accurate and readable (mobile)
- No important face/gesture covered by captions
- Audio is clear and consistent
- End-card CTA matches your brand voice
