# SOP: “Sample Clip in 24 Hours” (DFY Fulfillment)
**Goal:** Deliver 1 high-quality vertical clip within 24 hours of receiving assets, then upsell into a DFY package (and lightly validate SaaS demand).

## 0) Definition of Done (DoD)
A delivery is “done” when the client receives:
1) **One 9:16 MP4** (1080x1920) with burned-in captions.
2) **Caption file** (SRT) + **posting caption text** (for TikTok/IG).
3) **Hook options** (2–3) included in the message.
4) **B-roll cues list** (timecoded suggestions) OR confirmation that no b-roll was used.
5) Clear revision policy and next steps.

## 1) Intake (client supplies)
Collect via email reply or a simple form:
- Source: YouTube link / Google Drive link / Dropbox link (or raw upload)
- Target platform(s): TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts
- Handle(s) + niche + audience
- Brand basics: fonts/colors (optional), logo (optional)
- “Do/Don’t”: words to avoid, tone, emoji/no-emoji, profanity policy
- Offer/CTA: what should viewers do?
- 1–2 example creators they like

**If anything missing:** proceed with defaults: clean captions, no profanity, neutral tone, CTA = “Follow for more”.

## 2) Source prep
- Download/obtain the highest quality version available.
- Confirm audio quality; if poor, apply light noise reduction (don’t over-process).
- Identify 1–2 candidate segments (20–45s each) with: strong claim, emotion, clear lesson, contrarian point, or story beat.

## 3) Transcript + hook selection
- Transcribe the candidate segment(s).
- Select the best 20–45s based on:
  - Clear first 1.5 seconds
  - One idea per clip
  - A “payoff” within 10–20 seconds
- Write **2–3 hook text options** (on-screen title or opening caption line). Keep hooks short and specific.

## 4) Captions style rules (default)
- Burned-in captions, high contrast (white text with black stroke).
- 2 lines max, 5–9 words per line where possible.
- Punch in emphasis for key words (ALL CAPS or highlight color) sparingly.
- Remove filler words only if it improves readability without changing meaning.

## 5) 9:16 layout + safe zones
- Create vertical canvas 1080x1920.
- Keep primary subject in center safe area.
- Leave space for platform UI (avoid bottom ~15% and right side overlays).
- If face cam is small/wide shot: crop with gentle pan/zoom to keep attention.

## 6) B-roll cues (lightweight)
Provide timecoded suggestions like:
- 00:02–00:05: “Overlay screenshot of X”
- 00:08–00:10: “Cutaway to stock clip: busy office / gym / house tour”
If you don’t add b-roll in the sample clip, still provide cues to demonstrate the workflow value.

## 7) Export package
Deliverables to include:
- MP4 (H.264), 1080x1920, 30fps (or match source), audio AAC.
- SRT file.
- Posting caption text (1–2 variants) with 5–12 hashtags max relevant to niche.

## 8) QA checklist (5 minutes)
- Captions: no typos, readable on mobile, not covering face.
- Audio: no clipping; voice clear.
- First 2 seconds: strong, no dead air.
- Ends cleanly (no awkward cutoff).

## 9) Delivery + revision policy
**Delivery channel:** email to the client.
**Revision policy for sample:** 1 minor revision (caption wording/timing, crop, hook text). No major content changes unless the wrong segment was chosen.
**Turnaround:** within 24 hours of receiving assets.

## 10) Upsell path (DFY packages)
After sample delivery, offer:
- Starter: 12 clips/mo
- Growth: 30 clips/mo
- Daily: 60 clips/mo
(Exact pricing can be sent on request; aim to book a call and close.)

## 11) SaaS validation (without building)
Ask 3 questions in the delivery email:
1) “If you could upload a link and get 10 ready-to-post clips automatically, what would you need it to do?”
2) “Would you pay $99–$299/mo for that if the quality matched this sample?”
3) “Which matters most: speaker diarization, brand templates, auto-hook rewrites, b-roll suggestions, or scheduling?”
