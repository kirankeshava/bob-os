# 8) 12-Min Discovery-to-Close Call Script (DFY-first)

## Goal
Close a starter DFY batch (or weekly batch) AND capture 3 SaaS signals (features + WTP), without promising software delivery yet.

## Opener (30 seconds)
“Thanks for the time. I’ll ask 5 quick questions about your content + goals, then I’ll tell you exactly what I’d clip and what a weekly workflow would look like. If it’s a fit, we’ll pick a starter batch and I’ll send an intake email.”

## Qualifying Questions (5 minutes)
1) What are you posting today (TikTok/Reels/Shorts) and how often?
2) What content source is most consistent (podcast, Zoom coaching, YouTube, webinars)?
3) What’s your main CTA right now (book call, newsletter, course, sponsor)?
4) What’s been the bottleneck—finding moments, editing time, captions quality, or posting consistency?
5) Do you have an editor today? If yes, what’s missing?

## Diagnose + Mini Pitch (3 minutes)
“Based on that, I’d turn each episode into {{X}} clips. We’ll handle: highlight selection, hooks, mobile-readable captions, 9:16 exports, plus b-roll cues so it doesn’t feel like a talking head.”

## Close (2 minutes)
“Want to start with a low-risk batch? If you send 1 episode link, we’ll deliver {{X}} ready-to-post clips in {{TAT}}. If you like the results, we roll into a weekly pipeline.”

## SaaS Signals (2 minutes; do NOT overfocus)
“Quick question—if there were a simple tool that let you paste a YouTube link, auto-detect highlights, apply your caption style, and export 9:16 clips… would that be useful, and what would you pay monthly if it saved you hours?”
- Price anchor options: $99/mo, $199/mo, $299/mo
- Ask: “Which one feels fair, and what feature would make it a ‘no-brainer’?”

## Next Steps (30 seconds)
“I’ll email the intake checklist to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to. Once I have the link + brand notes, we start.”
