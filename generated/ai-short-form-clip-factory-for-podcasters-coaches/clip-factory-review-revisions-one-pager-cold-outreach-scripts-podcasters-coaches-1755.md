CLIP FACTORY — HOW TO REVIEW YOUR CLIPS + REQUEST REVISIONS (CLIENT ONE-PAGER)

Where to find your clips
You’ll receive either (1) a Google Drive/Dropbox folder link, or (2) a Frame.io review link (when available), plus a simple “Clip List” with titles and notes.

What “done” looks like (our standard)
Each clip is:
- Vertical 9:16, platform-safe framing
- Hook in the first ~1.5 seconds
- Captions burned-in and readable on mobile
- Pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds (zoom/cut/b-roll cue/onscreen text)
- Clean audio and no dead air
- Exported for TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts (or your requested platforms)

How to review fast (recommended 10-minute method)
1) Watch each clip once without stopping.
2) If it’s a “yes,” mark it Approved.
3) If it’s a “maybe,” leave ONE clear note describing what to change.
4) If it’s a “no,” tell us why (hook, clarity, pacing, caption errors, brand mismatch).

How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Please send revisions in this exact format to reduce turnaround time:
- Clip filename:
- Timestamp (mm:ss):
- Change request (one sentence):
- Optional: replacement text (if captions/onscreen text):

Good revision examples
- Clip filename: EP12_C03_IG_v1.mp4 | Timestamp: 00:04 | Change: Remove the word “crushing” from captions; replace with “improving.”
- Clip filename: EP12_C07_TT_v1.mp4 | Timestamp: 00:00–00:02 | Change: Start 0.5s later so the hook begins on the punchline.
- Clip filename: EP12_C01_YS_v1.mp4 | Timestamp: 00:08 | Change: Add b-roll cue: “show listing exterior” for 1.5s.

What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are limited to:
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, timing)
- Small trims (start/end), tightening pauses
- Swap hook line from the same section
- Reframe/safe area adjustments
- Color/caption style tweaks within the chosen preset
- Add/remove b-roll cues (notes), not sourcing footage unless already provided

What does NOT count as a revision (out of scope)
- New clip strategy from scratch (new topic direction)
- Re-cutting the clip from entirely different segments not already selected
- Brand-new style/preset after production starts
- Sourcing paid b-roll/music

Turnaround (SLA)
- First delivery: within 48 hours of receiving the required assets.
- Revisions: 1 round included, delivered within 24 hours of receiving consolidated notes.
