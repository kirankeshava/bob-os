What counts as INCLUDED (in-scope)
- Caption text corrections (typos, wording tweaks)
- Caption timing adjustments

- Minor cut tightening (remove a pause, remove a filler phrase)
- Volume normalization tweaks
- Safe area adjustments (move captions up/down for UI)

- Rebuilding the clip with a different narrative/angle from scratch
- New b-roll research beyond the provided cues
- Re-formatting to a totally new style preset after delivery
- Adding licensed music/assets that require purchase
