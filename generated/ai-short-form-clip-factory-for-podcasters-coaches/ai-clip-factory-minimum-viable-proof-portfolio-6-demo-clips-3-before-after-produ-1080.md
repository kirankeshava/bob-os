B) The 6 demo clips (2 per niche) — styles + specs
Global specs for all exports:
- Format: Vertical 9:16, 1080x1920
- Duration: 25–45s target (never >60s for the portfolio)
- Captions: burned-in, high contrast (white text + dark stroke), 2 lines max, keyword highlight color
- Edits: remove dead air, add subtle zoom cuts every 1.5–3.0 seconds, add whoosh/pop SFX lightly, keep audio clean
- On-screen top label: “SAMPLE CLIP — AI Clip Factory” (small, not intrusive)
- End card (last 1.0s): Website + email

- Hook pattern: “If you’re buying this year, stop doing X—do Y.”
- Visual: tight crop on face, quick punch-in on key words
- B-roll cues: generic neighborhood shots, home exterior, mortgage/contract close-ups (stock/CC)

C) Before/After comparisons (3 videos)
Goal: show transformation in <20 seconds.

BA-1 (Sequential): Raw excerpt (5–7s) → Final (8–12s)

- Labels: “RAW (no edits)” then “FINAL (captions + cuts + b-roll cues)”

BA-2 (Split-screen): Raw vs Final simultaneously (15–20s)
- Left: raw (no captions)
