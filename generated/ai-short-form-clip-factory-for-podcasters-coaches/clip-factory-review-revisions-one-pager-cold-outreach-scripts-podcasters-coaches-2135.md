## Client One-Pager: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Where your clips arrive:** You’ll receive a delivery email from **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to** with a folder link and a short “Clip List” (titles + notes).

### 1) Fast Review (10–15 minutes total)
Please review each clip in this order:
1. **Hook (first 1.5 seconds):** Does it start instantly (no dead air) and make you want to keep watching?
2. **Captions:** Readable on mobile, no major errors, highlights emphasize key words.
3. **Pacing:** Cuts every ~2–3 seconds (or intentional punch-ins), no long pauses.
4. **Meaning:** Clip makes sense without needing the full episode.
5. **Brand fit:** Tone matches your style; no sensitive claims.

### 2) What Counts as a Revision (Included)
Included revisions are for:
- Caption corrections (typos, names, missed words)
- Hook swap (alternate first line/cut) using the **same segment**
- Minor timing/pacing tweaks
- CTA/end-card text change

Not included (new request / new clip):
- Choosing a completely different segment after delivery
- Adding new footage/assets that weren’t provided at intake
- Major creative re-concepting of multiple clips

### 3) How to Request Revisions (Copy this format)
Reply to the delivery email and paste this block:

**Episode:** [Episode Name]
**Clip File Name:** [e.g., EP01_C03_TikTok_v1.mp4]
**Timecode or exact moment:** [e.g., 00:02–00:07]
**Change requested:** [e.g., Replace opening with: “If you’re buying a home in 2026, do this first…”]
**Priority:** [Must-fix / Nice-to-have]

### 4) Revision Timing
- **Round 1:** Included if requested within **48 hours** of delivery.
- **Turnaround:** Typically **24 hours** for revision round 1.
- **Max rounds included:** **1 round** per batch (keeps speed + consistency).

### 5) Approval = We Move Faster Next Time
If you reply with **“Approved”** (or list only caption typos), we’ll lock the style and get even faster on your next batch.
