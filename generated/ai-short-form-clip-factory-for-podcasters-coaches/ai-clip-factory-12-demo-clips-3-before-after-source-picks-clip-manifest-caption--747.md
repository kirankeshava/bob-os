B) PORTFOLIO OUTPUTS (WHAT TO RENDER)
- Total: 12 demo clips (9:16) + 3 before/after comparison videos (9:16)
- Length targets:
  - Demos: 25–45s each (fast, punchy)
  - Before/after: 20–35s each (raw 8–12s + final 12–20s)
- Export: 1080×1920, 30fps, H.264, 12–20 Mbps
- Burned-in captions: yes

C) CAPTION STYLE RULES (CONSISTENT LOOK)
- Font: bold sans (CapCut defaults OK)
- Size: large enough for mobile (approx 60–75 on CapCut)
- Placement: lower third, avoid UI safe areas
- Highlight: keyword color (1 color only) + occasional underline for emphasis
- Pace: 2–6 words per caption line; change on sentence beats

D) 12 DEMO CLIP MANIFEST (4 styles per niche)
For each niche, pull moments that match these structures. Replace bracketed words with the niche-specific content from the source.

REAL ESTATE (RE)
RE-01 Storytelling Hook (30–40s)
- Hook on-screen: “I almost lost $[X] on this house… here’s why.”
- Captions (sequence):
  1) “I almost lost $[X] on this house…”
  2) “Not because of the price.”
  3) “Because of ONE line in the inspection.”
  4) “Here’s what I check every time now:”
  5) “#1 [thing], #2 [thing], #3 [thing]”
- Edit beats: punch-in on “ONE line”; add zoom + whoosh on list reveal
- B-roll cues: inspection report close-up; “under contract” graphic; neighborhood exterior
- CTA: “Want 3 clips/week from your listings? See samples: [website URL]”

RE-02 Listicle (25–35s)
- Hook: “3 mistakes first-time buyers make in 2026”
- Captions:
  1) “3 mistakes first-time buyers make”
  2) “Mistake #1: [generic]”
  3) “Mistake #2: [generic]”
  4) “Mistake #3: [generic]”
  5) “Save this before you tour homes.”
- Edits: number pop animations; quick jump cuts every 1.5–2.5s
- B-roll: house tour shots; mortgage calculator; checklist overlay

RE-03 Contrarian Take (30–45s)
- Hook: “Stop doing open houses like this.”
- Captions:
  1) “Stop doing open houses like this.”
  2) “You’re optimizing for foot traffic…”
  3) “…not serious buyers.”
  4) “Do THIS instead:”
  5) “[1–2 step alternative]”
- Edits: hard cut after hook; “WRONG/RIGHT” labels; bleep for emphasis (optional)
- B-roll: sign-in sheet; CRM screenshot; calendar blocks

RE-04 Testimonial (25–40s)
- Hook: “They told me selling would take 90 days…”
- Captions:
  1) “They told me selling would take 90 days…”
  2) “We got offers in [X] days.”
  3) “Because we changed 2 things:”
  4) “1) [thing]  2) [thing]”
  5) “If you’re listing soon, DM me.”
- Edits: add review stars; subtle background music; emphasize numbers in yellow
