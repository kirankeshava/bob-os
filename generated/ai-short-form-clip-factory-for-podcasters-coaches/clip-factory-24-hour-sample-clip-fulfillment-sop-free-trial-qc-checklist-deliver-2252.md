## Definition of Done (DoD)
A delivered package containing:
1) **1 vertical 9:16 clip** (20–45s unless otherwise requested) with burned-in captions, hook text, and light b-roll cues.
2) **Caption copy** (platform-ready) + 8–15 hashtag suggestions.
3) **Posting notes**: best hook line, suggested title, CTA.
4) Delivery message sent + upsell question asked.

Target SLA: **<24 hours** from receiving usable source link/assets.

## Intake (5 minutes)
### What to ask for (copy/paste)
Send this immediately when they say yes:
- Source video link (YouTube, Drive, Dropbox) OR upload
- Your target platform (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
- Your audience (1 sentence)
- 1–2 example creators you like (optional)
- Any words to avoid (optional)
- Your preferred CTA (follow, comment keyword, book call, download)

### Acceptable source requirements
- Clear voice (no heavy background noise)
- Speaker visible or clean audio
- Minimum 3 minutes long (so we can find a moment)

If unusable, request an alternate link immediately.

---

## Clip Selection Criteria (10–15 minutes)
Pick one moment that meets **2+** of the following:
- Strong opinion / contrarian take
- Specific actionable step (“Do X, then Y”)
- Story w/ tension + resolution
- “Mistake most people make…”
- Measurable claim or result

**Avoid** long intros, housekeeping, sponsor reads, inside jokes.

### Hook formula options (choose 1)
- “Stop doing ___ if you want ___.”
- “The fastest way to ___ is ___.”
- “I wasted ___ years until I learned this…”
- “If I had to start over, I’d do this first…”

## Edit Spec (Operator Checklist)
### Format
- Canvas: **1080x1920 (9:16)**
- FPS: 30
- Audio: AAC 48k
- Loudness target: ~ -14 LUFS (or “consistent + not peaking” if not measuring)

### Visual layout (simple, repeatable)
- Speaker centered (crop/pan as needed)
- Subtle background blur if necessary
- Top hook text (optional but recommended)
- Captions: bottom 20–25% safe area, high contrast

### Captions
- Burned-in, word-accurate, punchy line breaks
- Highlight key words (brand color if known, otherwise yellow/white)
- Remove filler words only if it improves readability (don’t change meaning)

### B-roll cues (lightweight)
Add 1–3 quick cues as on-screen notes or quick cutaways (optional):
- Stock-style concepts (e.g., “calendar”, “invoice”, “gym”, “charts”)
- Simple zooms/cuts on emphasis

### Branding
If no brand kit provided:
- Use clean default: white captions + subtle shadow, yellow highlight
- No logos unless provided

## QC Checklist (3–5 minutes)
Before delivery, verify:
- [ ] First 1.5 seconds has a clear hook (spoken or text)
- [ ] Captions match audio (no glaring errors)
- [ ] No captions under UI elements (TikTok/Reels safe area)
- [ ] No abrupt cut at end; end on a complete phrase
- [ ] Audio not clipping; voice clear
- [ ] Filename follows naming convention

### Naming convention
`ClientName_Platform_Topic_YYYY-MM-DD_v1.mp4`
Example: `AcmeCoach_Reels_MindsetHook_2026-04-09_v1.mp4`

## Upsell (same day, 1–2 questions)

### Upsell Question Options

### If they say yes (close)
“Perfect—if you send the next episode link, I can start immediately and have the first batch back in 48 hours. Do you prefer TikTok-first or Reels-first formatting?”
