## 2) 24‑Hour “Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (DFY, zero spend)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 high-quality vertical clip fast to convert prospect into a paid package.

### A. Intake (5 minutes)
Ask for (copy/paste checklist):
- Episode link (YouTube/Drive)
- Best segments/timecodes (if they have them)
- Target audience + offer
- Tone: punchy/educational/story
- Caption style preference (minimal vs word-by-word)
- CTA (follow, comment, lead magnet, book call)

Route all intake via email: **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to**
Reference legitimacy link once in the thread: **https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3**

### B. Select highlight (15–25 minutes)
- Skim 10–20 minutes around likely high-signal areas (intro, story, objection, framework, hot take).
- Choose **1 segment** of **20–45 seconds** with:
  - clear claim or story
  - minimal context required
  - one crisp takeaway
- Write **3 hook options** (on-screen text) using their language.

### C. Edit spec (60–90 minutes)
- Format: **1080×1920**, safe margins, center framing.
- Captions: burned-in, high-contrast, 2 lines max, highlight key words.
- Audio cleanup: normalize/limit (avoid clipping).
- Add subtle punch-ins/cuts every 1–2 seconds when possible.
- Add **b-roll cue notes** (not mandatory b-roll insertion): e.g., “Add quick overlay of calendar when saying ‘every week’.”

### D. Delivery (5–10 minutes)
Deliver via Google Drive/Dropbox link (client-provided is fine). Include:
- Final MP4
- Optional SRT
- A short message:
  - “Here are 3 alternate hooks”
  - “Here are 2 more timestamps that would make great clips if you want a batch”

### E. Conversion follow-up (send immediately after delivery)
Email script:
Subject: Your sample clip is ready
Body:
“Just finished the sample clip: [link].
If you want, I can turn this episode into **10–20 clips** in the same style. Two other strong timestamps: [00:00] and [00:00].
Reply ‘batch’ and I’ll send the fastest plan to get your next 7 days of content scheduled.”

### F. Revision policy (keep tight)
- 1 revision included for sample (caption wording + hook + trimming)
- Turnaround: within 24 hours
