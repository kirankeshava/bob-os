DAY-1 RUNBOOK (repeat daily)
Goal: 6–8 Upwork proposals/day + 25–35 targeted creator touchpoints/day (comments + DM/email), logged in CRM.

Block A — Prospecting + Prep (30 minutes)
1) Upwork: pull 15 fresh job posts using searches like “podcast clips”, “repurpose video into reels”, “short form editor”, “TikTok captions podcast”. Filter to clients with payment verified and some spend if possible.
2) Pick 8 jobs max: choose the ones that mention podcast, YouTube longform, Reels/TikTok, captions, or weekly volume.
3) For each job, fill 1 CRM row: Job URL, client name, pain, turnaround expectations, your offer angle, proposal variant to use.

Block B — Send Proposals (60 minutes)
Send 6–8 proposals. Each proposal must include:
- A one-line personalization (mirror their wording)
- The core offer: “10 ready-to-post vertical clips in 48 hours”
- Deliverables: hooks, captions, safe-zone formatting, b-roll cues
- Free 30–60s sample clip offer from their existing content
- Proof link + email: website URL above and agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

Block C — YouTube Value Comments + DM/Email (60 minutes)
For 10 creators/day:
1) Identify 2 recent long episodes (last 30 days).
2) Leave 1 value comment per episode (not spam; reference a timestamp/insight).
3) Immediately DM/email: reference the episode + offer the free sample.

Block D — Follow-ups (30 minutes)
- Follow up on yesterday’s proposals and DMs with a short “I can cut a sample today if you send the link” message.
- Update CRM status: Sent → Follow-up 1 → Replied → Booked → Won/Lost.
