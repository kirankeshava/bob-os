8) “If we could wave a wand—what would success look like in 30 days?”

“Based on that, here’s what we do: we take your long-form and turn it into **ready-to-post vertical clips** with (1) hook-first opening, (2) clean captions, (3) b-roll cues, and (4) platform-specific formatting. The goal is consistent output without you spending hours in an editor or managing freelancers.”

“We start with a proof step: **send your latest link and we’ll return 1 sample clip in 24h.** If it hits your quality bar, we batch weekly so you get predictable posting.”

Reply:
“Totally. Most editors are great at cutting; the gap is usually volume + hooks + speed. We can either support your editor (give them hooks/cut list/captioning style) or take clipping off their plate so they can focus on long-form. Want the sample clip to compare output and turnaround?”

Reply:
“Fair. We don’t just press a button and ship. AI helps us find moments and speed captions, but the deliverable is curated: hook tightened, captions cleaned, pacing adjusted, safe margins checked. The 24h sample is specifically so you can judge quality before committing.”

Objection: “Price is high.”

“Understood. The way to think about it is cost per posted clip and consistency. If we deliver [X] clips/week, you’re buying a predictable publishing machine. Also, you don’t need to decide today—let’s do the 24h sample first, then choose a smaller weekly batch that fits.”

14:00–15:00 — CLOSE
