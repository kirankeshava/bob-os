DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (12–20 MINUTES)
Purpose: qualify + sell DFY pilot + capture requirements for SaaS (revenue-gated).

1) Context (2 min)
- “What are you posting today across TikTok/Reels/Shorts?”
- “How are clips made right now (you, editor, VA, agency)?”

2) Pain + stakes (4 min)
- “What breaks in the current workflow?” (time, cost, quality, inconsistency)
- “If you had consistent clips, what would that unlock?” (leads, sales, brand)

3) Content inventory (3 min)
- “How many long videos/podcasts per week?”
- “Where are they hosted (YouTube, Riverside, Google Drive)?”
- “Any brand rules (fonts/colors), banned words, compliance constraints?”

4) Definition of ‘good clip’ (4 min)
- “What clips have performed best for you? Why?”
- “Do you want: educational, controversial, story-based, or hard CTA?”
- “Captions: word-by-word karaoke, 2-line chunks, or minimal?”

5) Paid pilot close (3–5 min)
- “I can do a pilot pack this week: {{OptionA}} clips from 1 episode delivered in {{X}} days. If you like it, we convert to weekly.”
