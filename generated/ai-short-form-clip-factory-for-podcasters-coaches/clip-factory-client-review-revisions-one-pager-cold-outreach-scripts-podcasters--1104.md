## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

**Purpose:** Help you review quickly so we can keep the **48-hour delivery** promise and ship publish-ready clips with minimal back-and-forth.

**Where your clips arrive**
- You’ll receive a delivery email from **agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to**
- Proof/legitimacy link: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3
- Delivery includes: (1) download link(s), (2) a “Clip Notes” sheet, (3) filenames that match the notes.

**What “done” means**
Each clip is delivered as a vertical short optimized for TikTok/Reels/Shorts with:
- A clear hook in the first ~1.5 seconds
- Dynamic captions (readable on mobile)
- Light pattern interrupts (cuts/zooms/b-roll cue callouts) every ~2–3 seconds
- Clean audio + safe margins + platform-ready export

**How to review (fastest method)**
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) Rewatch and only flag *objective* issues (typos, wrong captions, awkward cut, incorrect name/claim).
3) Only then give preference feedback (tone, pacing, caption style).

**How to request revisions (copy/paste format)**
Reply to the delivery email using this exact format for speed:
- **Clip filename:** (example) `CLIENT_E07_C03_2026-04-09_v1.mp4`
- **Timestamp:** (example) `00:07–00:10`
- **Change request:** (be specific) “Replace caption line with: ‘…’” / “Remove the pause” / “Swap b-roll cue: use ‘calendar/meeting’ instead of ‘gym’”
- **Severity:** Must-fix / Nice-to-have

**Revision policy (to protect turnaround)**
- Includes **1 revision round** covering up to **10 clips** (bundled feedback preferred).
- Revisions are for: caption corrections, trimming, minor pacing, b-roll cue swaps, CTA text swaps.
- Out of scope (new order): changing the selected moments entirely, rewriting the story arc, heavy motion graphics, new scripting/recording.

**Deadlines for revisions**
- Please send revision notes within **48 hours of delivery**.
- If feedback arrives after 48 hours, revisions are scheduled in the next production slot.

**Best practices (to get clips that perform)**
- Tell us your #1 goal: leads, followers, or webinar/offer clicks.
- Share 2–3 examples of clips you love (links).
- If you have compliance constraints (finance/health/legal), send required disclaimers in advance.
