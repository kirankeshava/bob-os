## Clip spec (applies to all 6)
**Aspect/size:** 9:16, **1080x1920**
**Duration target:** 20–35s
**Captions:** burned-in, high contrast, 2 lines max, highlight 1–2 keywords per sentence
**Safe margins:** keep text inside central 80% (avoid UI overlays)
**Audio:** normalize to ~-14 LUFS (or “consistent & not peaking” if using free tools)
**Branding:** small “Clip Factory (Sample)” watermark top-left OR end-card label to avoid confusion

### Editing beats (simple but “pro”)
- Cut dead air aggressively (no pauses >0.25–0.35s)
- Subtle zoom/punch every 2–4 seconds (or on emphasis words)
- Insert 1–2 b-roll cues as on-screen text (e.g., “[B-ROLL: house exterior]”)
- On-screen hook text in first 1 second
