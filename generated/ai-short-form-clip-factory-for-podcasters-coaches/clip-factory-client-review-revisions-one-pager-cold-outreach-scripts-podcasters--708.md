## 1) What you’ll receive
You will get:
- 10 ready-to-post vertical clips (TikTok/IG Reels/YouTube Shorts specs)
- Burned-in captions (readable, platform-safe)
- Hook-first pacing + pattern interrupts (so clips don’t “feel like a podcast excerpt”)
- A delivery note with: clip titles, timestamps, and suggested post captions/hashtags (if included in your package)

## 2) How to review (fastest method)
**Step A — Watch each clip once without pausing** (as if you’re scrolling). If it holds attention, it’s working.

**Step B — Then re-watch and check only these 5 items:**
1. **Hook clarity in first 1–1.5 seconds** (Does it start strong? No long intro?)
2. **Caption accuracy** (Names, numbers, key claims)
3. **Pacing** (No dead air; quick cuts when needed)
4. **Brand fit** (Tone, words that match your audience)
5. **Platform safety** (No copyrighted music unless you provided it)

## 3) How to request revisions (copy/paste template)
Please send revision requests in *one* email to keep turnaround fast.

**Email to:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

**Subject:** Revisions — [ClientName] — [Episode/Video Name] — Round 1

**In the email, paste this block once per clip:**
- Clip filename: (example: `C01_Fitness_CutCravings_2026-04-09_v1.mp4`)
- Timecode(s): (example: `00:07–00:10`)
- Change type (pick one): **Captions / Trim / Hook / CTA / Visual / Audio**
- Exact request: (example: “Change ‘15’ to ‘50’ in captions; trim 0.5s before the hook; replace CTA with ‘DM ME “PLAN”’.”)
- Priority: **Must-fix** or **Nice-to-have**

## 4) What counts as a revision (and what doesn’t)
**Included revisions (typical):**
- Caption corrections (words, numbers, names)
- Small trims (tighten intro/outro, remove filler)
- Minor CTA text changes
- Swapping to another style preset you selected (Real Estate / Fitness / B2B)

**Not included (requires a new clip or new scope):**
- Rebuilding from a different topic section not in the selected timestamps
- Full script rewrite / new narrative structure from scratch
- Heavy VFX, custom motion design, or sourcing large amounts of external b-roll

## 5) Revision turnaround
- **Round 1 revisions:** delivered within **24 hours** of receiving your consolidated revision email.
- Additional rounds may affect the schedule depending on volume.

## 6) Approval = ready to post
Once you reply **“Approved”**, we consider clips final and ready for posting. If you want us to generate suggested post copy for each platform, ask and we’ll provide it if included in your package.
