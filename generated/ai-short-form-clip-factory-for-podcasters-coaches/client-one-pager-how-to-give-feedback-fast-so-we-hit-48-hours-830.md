How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Hit 48 Hours)

Thanks for trusting Clip Factory. Our promise is speed + consistency: we deliver 10 ready-to-post vertical clips within 48 hours of receiving (1) your source file/link and (2) brand/style inputs.

This page explains exactly how to give feedback so revisions are quick and we stay inside the 48-hour window.

Where to leave feedback (pick ONE)
1) Frame.io review link (preferred)
   • Leave comments directly on the timeline at the exact second/frame.
   • Example: “00:07 — change caption to: ‘Stop doing this in 2026…’”

2) Google Drive / Dropbox folder
   • If we deliver as MP4 files, reply with a timestamp list.
   • Example format:
     - Clip 03 (filename: …): 00:02–00:05 remove ‘um’
     - Clip 07: swap hook line, use alt hook #2 from notes

What ‘good feedback’ looks like (fastest to execute)
• Timestamp + precise instruction + replacement text (if applicable)
  - “00:00–00:01 hook text should be: ‘If I only had 30 days to sell a house…’”
  - “00:12 cut 0.5s of silence”
  - “00:18 add b-roll cue: ‘MLS listing screenshot’”

• Priority-based feedback (so we don’t churn)
  - “Must change (required): …”
  - “Nice to have (if time): …”

What slows revisions down (avoid if possible)
• Vague notes like “make it more engaging” without timestamps or copy.
• Multiple feedback channels (e.g., some notes in DM, some in email, some in comments).
• New creative direction after delivery (new style, new niche, new CTA) without calling it a new batch.

Our revision rules (so expectations are clear)
• Included: 1 revision round per batch of 10 clips.
• Revision scope: copy tweaks, caption fixes, minor trims, hook swap using existing transcript, b-roll cue adjustments, safe/brand compliance edits.
• Not included in the revision round: choosing an entirely different set of highlights, brand-new design system, re-editing from scratch, heavy audio restoration, or adding net-new footage (can be quoted as a new batch).

Feedback deadline to keep the 48-hour cadence
• To stay on schedule, send revision notes within 24 hours of receiving the first delivery.
• If feedback arrives after 24 hours, we still revise—delivery timing may shift based on the queue.

Quick checklist before you hit send
1) Did you include timestamps?
2) Did you include exact replacement text (if changing words on screen)?
3) Did you mark MUST vs NICE-TO-HAVE?
4) Did you keep all feedback in one place (Frame.io or one email reply)?
