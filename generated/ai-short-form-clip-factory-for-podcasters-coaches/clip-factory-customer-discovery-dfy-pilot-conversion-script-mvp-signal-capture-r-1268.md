2) 20-minute call run-of-show (script)
0:00–2:00 Set frame
- “Thanks for the time. In 20 minutes I want to (1) understand how you currently create clips and what ‘good’ looks like, (2) show a simple way we produce ready-to-post vertical clips, and (3) if it’s a fit, propose a short paid pilot so you can judge results quickly. Sound good?”

2:00–7:00 Diagnose current workflow (pain + baseline metrics)
Ask, then quantify.
- “Where does your long-form content live? (YouTube, Riverside, Zoom, podcasts, etc.)”
- “How often do you publish long-form? Minutes per episode?”
- “How many short clips do you post per week right now?”
- “What’s the bottleneck: finding highlights, editing, captions, approvals, posting, or consistency?”
- “Who does it today (you, VA, editor, agency) and what does it cost in time or dollars?”
- “What does a ‘winning clip’ mean for you—views, leads, booked calls, course sales?”
- “Any hard requirements? (brand font/colors, on-screen positioning, burn-in captions style, profanity removal, platform constraints)”
