1) CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW & REQUEST REVISIONS
==============================
Title: How to Review Your Clips (and Request Revisions in 5 Minutes)

Where to review
- You’ll receive a delivery email from us with a download link (or folder link) plus a “Clip List” that includes each clip’s title + hook + timestamp source.
- If we shared a review link, you can leave time-stamped comments directly on the video.

What “good” looks like (quick checklist)
For each clip, check these 6 things:
1) Hook speed: Does the first 1–1.5 seconds start with a bold statement/question (no slow intros)?
2) Readability: Captions are large enough on mobile, not cut off by UI, and easy to scan.
3) Accuracy: No obvious caption errors, names spelled right, key numbers correct.
4) Flow: Cuts feel intentional; no awkward mid-word cuts; pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds.
5) Branding: Colors/fonts match the chosen style; CTA end card present (if included).
6) Platform fit: Vertical 9:16; safe margins respected; duration appropriate for Shorts/Reels/TikTok.

How to request revisions (fastest method)
Send ONE message/email containing:
A) Clip name(s): (example: “CLIP_03_HomePriceMistake_v1”) 
B) Exact timestamp(s): (example: “00:07–00:10”) 
C) What to change: (example: “Replace ‘$50k’ with ‘$15k’ and tighten the pause before the punchline.”)
D) Priority: Must-fix vs Nice-to-have

Revision boundaries (so we stay fast)
- Included revisions: caption fixes, minor trim/timing tweaks, headline/hook text swap, CTA text change, small layout adjustments.
- Not included (treated as a new request): rewriting the clip concept from scratch, changing the niche preset style entirely after delivery, sourcing new b-roll libraries, or requesting 10 new clips from different sections of the episode.

Turnaround on revisions
- Minor revisions: delivered within 24 hours of receiving your consolidated notes.
- If notes arrive in multiple messages, we start the clock after the final consolidated list is received.
