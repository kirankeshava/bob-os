Title: How to Give Feedback Fast (So We Still Hit 48 Hours)

This guide ensures we can deliver your 10 vertical clips within 48 hours. It tells you exactly how to review and what feedback is most helpful.

Where you’ll review
- We’ll send a review link (Frame.io if available, otherwise Google Drive).
- Each clip will be clearly labeled so you can comment on the exact moment you want changed.

Your review deadline (important)
- Round 1 feedback is due within 12 hours of receiving the first delivery.
- If feedback arrives after 12 hours, delivery may shift because the edit queue is time-boxed to protect other client deadlines.

How to leave feedback (best format)
Please use timestamped notes. Copy/paste this format:
- Clip name: [CLIP ID]
- Timestamp: 0:00–0:02
- Change: Replace hook text with “___”
- Reason (optional): Too aggressive / not on-brand

What we can change in revisions (included)
Each order includes 1 revision round across the 10 clips (bounded scope). Examples:
- Hook text wording tweaks (keep same idea)
- Caption corrections (names, acronyms, punctuation)
- Swapping a b-roll cue or replacing a stock shot
- Removing a sentence or tightening a pause
- Slight reframing / safe-area adjustments

What is out of scope (will require a new order)
To keep turnaround fast, these are not included in the standard revision:
- Re-cutting the entire clip with a different topic/angle
- Changing the style system (fonts/colors/layout) after approval
- Adding complex motion graphics, custom animation, or heavy VFX
- Creating new scripts, new recordings, or new voiceover

Approval rules (fastest path)
- If you like a clip, respond with “APPROVED” (no extra notes).
- If you want a clip removed from delivery, say “KILL CLIP [ID]” (we won’t replace it unless requested).

If you don’t respond
- If we don’t receive feedback by the deadline, we treat the delivered set as approved and close the order to maintain the 48-hour SLA.
