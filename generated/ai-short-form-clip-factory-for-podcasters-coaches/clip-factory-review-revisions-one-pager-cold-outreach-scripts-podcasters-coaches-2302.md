## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (48-Hour Clip Factory)

**Welcome!** To keep delivery fast and painless, please use the process below to review your clips and request revisions.

**What you’ll receive**
- 10 vertical clips formatted for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions (on-screen)
- Hook-first pacing + pattern interrupts
- A delivery note with clip titles + a short “why this works” summary per clip

**How to review (10–15 minutes total)**
1) **Watch each clip once without stopping** (this matches how viewers experience Shorts).
2) Re-watch only the clips you want to adjust and note:
   - **Timestamp** (e.g., 00:07–00:10)
   - What to change (one sentence)
   - If you have a preference: “More aggressive hook” / “Less jump cuts” / “Bigger captions” etc.
3) Decide per clip: **Approve / Revise / Swap**.

**The fastest revision format (copy/paste template)**
- Clip Name:
- Status: Approve / Revise / Swap
- Revision request (be specific):
- Timestamp(s):
- Notes (optional):

**Examples of good revision requests**
- “Clip 03: remove the pause at 00:02–00:03 and start on the word ‘Here’s the trick…’”
- “Clip 07: captions are covering my hands—move captions 10% higher.”
- “Clip 02: change opening hook text to ‘Most people do THIS wrong…’”

**What counts as a revision (included)**
- Caption adjustments (wording, size, position)
- Hook text tweaks
- Tightening dead air / pacing
- Minor cut changes within the same selected segment
- CTA end-card text changes

**What counts as a new clip (not a revision)**
- Choosing an entirely different section from the full episode because you don’t like the topic/angle
- Major re-storyboarding (new structure, new narrative arc)

**Revision policy (to protect the 48-hour workflow)**
- **Included:** 1 revision round across up to **10 clips** if requested in one consolidated message.
- **Deadline:** please send revision notes within **48 hours of delivery**.
- **Consolidation rule:** multiple scattered revision emails/messages may be treated as a second revision round.
