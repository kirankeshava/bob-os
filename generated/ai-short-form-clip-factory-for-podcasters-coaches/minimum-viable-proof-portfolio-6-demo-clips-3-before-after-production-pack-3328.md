B) CLIP PRODUCTION SPECS (applies to all 6)
Format: 9:16, 1080x1920, 30–45s, 24–30fps.
Captions: Burned-in, high-contrast (white text w/ black stroke), 2 lines max, highlight keywords (yellow).
Editing: Jump cuts every 1.5–3.0s, subtle punch-in 105–115% on emphasis, remove filler words.
B-roll cues: Add on-screen bracket cues like [B-ROLL: …] for 0.8–1.2s overlays.
