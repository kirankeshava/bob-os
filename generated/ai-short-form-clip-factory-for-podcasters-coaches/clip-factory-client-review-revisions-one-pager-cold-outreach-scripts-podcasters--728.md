## 1) Client-Facing One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Clip Factory)

**Thanks for working with Clip Factory.** To keep the **48-hour delivery + fast turnaround** promise, we use a simple review process.

### What you’ll receive
You’ll get a delivery message that includes:
- A link to your clips (download folder)
- A list of clip filenames
- A short note per clip (hook/angle + optional suggested caption)
- Optional: timestamp notes that map to the source episode

### How to review efficiently (takes ~10–15 minutes)
1) **Watch each clip once** without stopping.
2) Watch again and only note changes that materially improve performance:
   - Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds)
   - Caption readability (typos, timing, line breaks)
   - Any compliance/brand-sensitive phrasing
   - Incorrect speaker attribution (rare)

### How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Please reply with **timestamps** and **specific instructions**.

**Revision Request Template**
- Project/Episode:
- Clip filename:
- Timestamp (mm:ss–mm:ss):
- Change requested:
- Priority: Must-fix / Nice-to-have

**Examples**
- Clip: `EP12_C03_HowToLoseFat_IG_v1.mp4`
  - 00:02–00:04: Change caption from “loose” → “lose”
  - 00:14–00:18: Remove “guaranteed” wording (compliance)

### What counts as a revision vs. a new clip
**Included revisions** (within policy):
- Caption typos, timing, readability tweaks
- Minor hook swap within the same selected segment
- Small framing/position adjustments (safe margins)
- Remove/bleep a word, adjust on-screen emphasis

**New clip request** (not a revision):
- “Choose a totally different moment” from the episode
- “Make 10 more clips” or “Change the strategy/angle for all clips”

### Turnaround + limits
- **Standard SLA:** revision results delivered within **24 hours** after you send clear timestamp-based notes.
- **Included:** **1 revision round** covering up to **10 delivered clips**.
- Additional revision rounds or net-new clip requests can be quoted quickly.

### Fastest way to approve
Reply with either:
- **APPROVED** (nothing else needed), or
- Your revision template notes (timestamps + instructions)
