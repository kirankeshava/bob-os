**Title:** Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)

### Welcome
Thanks for working with Clip Factory. We convert your long-form video/podcast into ready-to-post vertical clips with strong hooks, accurate captions, and platform-specific formatting.

For legitimacy and contact details:  
Website: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3  
Email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch/order you will receive a folder containing:
1. **Final vertical MP4 clips** (platform-ready)
2. **SRT captions** (one per clip)
3. **B-roll / cut cue sheet** (what we changed + suggestions + timestamps)
4. **Posting suggestions** per clip: hook/first line caption + CTA + hashtags

### Our quality bar (quick QA summary)
Before delivery, every clip is checked for:
- **Safe zones / framing:** faces not cropped; key text inside TikTok/IG safe areas; no UI overlays blocking captions.
- **Caption accuracy:** correct words + punctuation; correct speaker intent; proper line breaks; profanity handled per your preferences.
- **Audio:** clear voice, consistent loudness, no harsh peaks, minimal background noise.
- **Hook clarity:** first 1–2 seconds clearly communicates the payoff.
- **Pacing:** tight jump cuts; remove dead air; keep momentum.
- **Export settings:** vertical 9:16, high quality MP4/H.264, platform-compatible specs.

### Review process (timestamp comments)
We send a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave timestamped comments.
- Please consolidate notes into **one round** if possible.
- For each requested change, include: **clip name + timestamp + what to change**.

### Revision window (24 hours)
You have **24 hours from delivery** to request revisions included in scope. After 24 hours, we may treat requests as a new batch depending on complexity.

### What counts as a free revision vs. paid change
**Included (free) revisions**
- Caption fixes (typos, small wording corrections)
- Minor timing tweaks (caption sync, cut timing micro-adjustments)
- Small framing fixes (reposition subject, minor zoom/pan adjustments)
- Audio leveling fixes (minor EQ/compression adjustments)
- Swapping a hook line from options already present in your source

**Out of scope (paid change / new batch)**
- New creative direction after delivery (different angle, different story)
- Changing the clip selection strategy (new timestamps / new clips)
- Adding new assets not provided initially (logos, photos, b-roll libraries)
- Heavy re-editing (rebuilding pacing, replacing multiple sections)
- Brand-new formats (turning into carousel, long YouTube edit, etc.)

If a request is borderline, we’ll reply with: **(a)** what we can do free, **(b)** what would be a paid change, and **(c)** the fastest path to completion.

### How to get the best results
- Send clean source audio if possible.
- Tell us: target platform(s), tone, CTA, any banned words/phrases, and your @handles.
- If you have brand fonts/colors, include them upfront.
