### A) How the process works (simple)
1) **You submit your long-form video/podcast + preferences** (via our Intake Form).  
2) We produce **ready-to-post vertical clips** with hooks, captions, and formatting for your target platforms.  
3) You review via a **timestamp-comment link** (Frame.io/Vimeo/Unlisted YouTube).  
4) You get **one revision round** inside the revision window (see below).  
5) We deliver a complete **Delivery Pack** (MP4 + SRT + cue sheet + posting suggestions).

### B) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack contents)
For each batch/order, you’ll receive:
- **Final MP4 files (vertical)** — ready to post
- **Captions file per clip (SRT)** — editable, uploadable to platforms
- **B-roll / on-screen cue sheet** — what we used or recommend (time-coded)
- **Posting suggestions** — first-line caption options + CTA ideas + hashtag starters

### C) QA standards we follow (what we check before you see it)
We run every clip through this QA list:

**1) Framing & safe zones**
- Subject is centered with **no cropped face** (forehead/chin not unintentionally cut)
- Key visuals stay inside platform-safe areas (avoid UI overlays)
- No critical text near edges; subtitles remain readable

**2) Captions accuracy & readability**
- Captions match spoken words with high accuracy
- Correct punctuation for clarity; no “wall of text”
- Consistent style (case, emphasis) and readable size/contrast
- Names/brands/terms checked against your intake form

**3) Audio & loudness**
- Dialogue is clear; music never masks speech
- No clipping/distortion; consistent volume across clips
- Noise reduction applied when needed (without robotic artifacts)

**4) Hook clarity & pacing**
- Strong first 1–2 seconds (hook or tension)
- Tight pacing; dead air removed
- Jump cuts are clean (no jarring micro-glitches)

**5) Visual polish**
- No distracting flashes/frames
- Clean transitions; b-roll cues match the topic
- Brand elements included only if requested

**6) Export & platform formatting**
- Vertical format: **1080×1920**
- H.264 MP4 (high compatibility)
- Crisp text/captions; no compression blur

### D) Review flow (how to request revisions)
We’ll send you a review link (Frame.io/Vimeo/YouTube unlisted) where you can:
- Leave **timestamped comments** (preferred)
- Or reply by email with **clip filename + exact timestamp + change request**

**Best practice:** Consolidate comments into one pass so we can turn around revisions faster.

### E) Revision policy (protects timeline + prevents scope creep)
**Revision window:** **24 hours** from when we send the review link.

**Included for free (within the window):**
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, minor timing)
- Small trims/pacing adjustments
- Swap a hook line if it’s the same idea and doesn’t require a full restructure
- Minor audio leveling fixes
- Small safe-zone/framing adjustments

**Not included (treated as a change request):**
- Changing the clip selection criteria after delivery (e.g., “make these totally different moments”)
- Major restructure: new storyline, new concept, extensive re-edit
- Brand/tone direction changes not provided in intake (e.g., new style guide after edits)
- Requests requiring new sourcing/research beyond basic b-roll cues

If you’re unsure, send the request and we’ll classify it clearly.

### F) What we need from you (to avoid delays)
- Your long-form video link/file access
- Target platform(s): TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- Your preferred tone: educational / high-energy / calm / contrarian
- Any banned words/topics
- Your handle(s), CTA link, and any required on-screen text
