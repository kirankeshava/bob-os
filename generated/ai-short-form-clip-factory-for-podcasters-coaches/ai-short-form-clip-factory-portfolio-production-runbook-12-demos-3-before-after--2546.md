D) The 4 Styles per Niche (template)
1) Storytelling Hook: open with curiosity (“I didn’t believe this until…”). Use 3-beat arc: setup → tension → takeaway.
2) Listicle: “3 mistakes / 5 rules / 7 signs”. Use large numeral overlays; fast cuts.
3) Contrarian Take: “Stop doing X” / “Unpopular opinion: Y”. Use pattern interrupt at 0–2s.
4) Testimonial/Case Study: “Client went from A → B”. Use proof framing, show timeline, end with CTA.
