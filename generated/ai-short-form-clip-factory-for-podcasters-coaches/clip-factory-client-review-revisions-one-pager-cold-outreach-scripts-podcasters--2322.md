## 1) Client One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Paste into Delivery Email)

**Thanks for reviewing your clips.** To keep our **48-hour turnaround** fast and predictable, here’s the simplest way to approve or request changes.

### What you’re receiving
You’ll receive **10 ready-to-post vertical clips** (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts formatting) with:
- Strong hook + pacing
- On-screen captions
- Light pattern interrupts (zoom/cut/b-roll cues)
- Platform-safe export settings

### What to check (fast approval checklist)
For each clip, please check:
1) **Hook clarity (first 1–2 seconds):** Does it start strong and make sense?
2) **Caption accuracy:** Any incorrect words/names/numbers?
3) **Pacing:** Does it feel too slow or cut too aggressively?
4) **Brand fit:** Tone, no off-brand phrasing, appropriate emphasis.
5) **CTA (if included):** Correct handle/URL + matches your offer.

### How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to this email and list revisions like this:
- **Clip filename:** CF_[Client]_[Episode]_[Clip##]_v1
- **Timestamp:** 00:03–00:07
- **Change requested:** (e.g., “Replace ‘$50K’ with ‘$15K’ in captions”, “Trim the pause”, “Swap CTA end card to ‘Book a Call’”)

If you don’t have timestamps, you can also say: “At the sentence where I say ‘…’”.

### What counts as a revision (within scope)
Included revisions focus on:
- Caption corrections
- Minor trimming/tightening
- Swapping an end card/CTA
- Small reframes (cropping/positioning)

### What is out of scope (requires a new clip request)
To protect speed, the following are considered **new work**:
- Changing the selected topic/angle entirely
- Rebuilding a clip from a different section of the episode
- Heavy re-editing, adding custom motion graphics, or sourcing extensive b-roll

### SLA for revisions
- **1 revision round included** for the batch.
- Revisions returned within **24 hours** after receiving your timestamped notes.
