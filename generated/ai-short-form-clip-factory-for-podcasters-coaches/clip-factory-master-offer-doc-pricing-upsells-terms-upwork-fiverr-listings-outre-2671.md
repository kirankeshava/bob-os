### Revisions
- Starter: **1 round**
- Growth/Pro: **2 rounds**
- A “revision” = changes to selected clips (captions wording, trims, hook overlay, minor pacing). New clip requests or new topic pulls count as new work.

### What counts as a “clip”
A clip is one exported vertical video (9:16) up to ~60 seconds unless otherwise specified, containing:
- A clear hook (first 1–2 seconds)
- Captions (burned in)
- Clean cuts for retention

### Inputs required
Client provides at least one of:
- Downloadable video file, or
- A direct link with permission to download (Drive/Dropbox/YouTube unlisted)
Plus:
- Brand notes (tone, banned words, emoji policy, preferred caption style) OR we use our default clean style.

### Content rights
- Client confirms they own/operate the content and have rights to repurpose it.
- Once paid (or once platform escrow releases), client owns delivered clip exports.

### Refunds
- If we miss the agreed turnaround **by more than 48 hours** with all inputs received, client may request either (a) a **partial refund** for the delayed portion or (b) equivalent additional clips as a make-good.
- No refunds after delivery of final exports, except where required by marketplace policy.

We do not guarantee platform approval/virality. We deliver edits designed for retention and platform best practices.
