H) REVISION TRIAGE RULES (paste into Notion + proposals)
Free revisions (within 24h window):
- Caption typos/accuracy; punctuation; speaker labels
- Minor timing (±1–2 sec), jump cut smoothing, removing filler words
- Minor reframes to keep face in safe zone; small crop/zoom adjustments
- Audio leveling, noise reduction tweaks, de-esser tweaks
- Bleeping a missed profanity if client flagged profanity rules in intake

Paid changes (require approval/quote):
- New clip selection direction after drafts are delivered
- New hook/CTA direction not in intake, or new brand voice requirements
- Major restructure (new narrative order, inserting new sections, replacing core sections)
- New deliverable formats (additional aspect ratios, new platform versions, extra languages)
- Client provides new source footage after editing started

Refund protection rule (internal): If there is a clear missed requirement from the intake form or a clear technical defect (audio clipping, captions materially wrong, faces cropped), fix it immediately as free revision regardless of window.
