## 4) 24-HOUR SAMPLE CLIP OFFER — FULFILLMENT SOP (ZERO-TO-DELIVERY)

**Goal:** Deliver 1 impressive vertical clip within 24 hours to convert the prospect into a paid DFY package.

**Inputs required (ask via email):**
- Source: YouTube link / Drive link / raw file
- Target platform: TikTok or Reels (default: both)
- Topic focus: 1–2 themes they want to push
- Any brand notes: font preference, colors, examples they like (optional)

**Process (time-boxed):**
1) **Ingest (5 min):** Download or open link, confirm audio quality, note speaker count, note episode title.
2) **Find candidate moments (20 min):** Skim at 1.5–2x; select 2–3 potential highlight timestamps.
3) **Pick 1 best moment (5 min):** Choose the one with: clear payoff + concrete takeaway + strong first 2 seconds.
4) **Hook pass (10 min):** Write 3 hook options (on-screen text). Pick the strongest.
5) **Edit (30–60 min):**
   - Cut to 20–45s (aim 25–35s)
   - Add jump cuts to remove dead air
   - Add subtle zooms every 3–5 seconds if appropriate
   - Ensure it works without sound (captions must be readable)
6) **Captions (20 min):** Burned-in captions; correct names/terms; keep 2 lines max; highlight keywords.
7) **B-roll/cue notes (10 min):** Add 2–4 simple cues in a text note (e.g., “overlay screenshot of X here”).
8) **Export (10 min):** 1080x1920 MP4, 30fps, high bitrate; file name: Brand_Episode_Topic_01.mp4.
9) **QA checklist (5 min):**
   - First frame has hook text
   - Captions are accurate and not covering face
   - Audio levels consistent
   - No awkward cut at start/end
   - Ends with clean final sentence (no trailing breath)
10) **Delivery email (5 min):**
   - Attach/Drive link
   - Include 2 alternative hook lines
   - Include suggested caption + CTA
   - Ask: “Want 10 more like this per week?”
