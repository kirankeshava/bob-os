- 10 vertical clips (MP4) optimized for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
- Burned-in captions + hook formatting
- A delivery note with suggested post copy + recommended hashtags (optional)

**How to review (fastest method)**
1) Watch each clip once without pausing.
2) On the second pass, note only: (a) timestamp, (b) what’s wrong, (c) what you want instead.
3) Send feedback using the format below (copy/paste). This is the fastest way to hit our 48-hour promise.

**Feedback format (required)**
- Clip name: `CLIP_##_Title_v1.mp4`
- Timestamp(s): `00:03–00:07`
- Issue: (caption wrong / pacing / framing / b-roll cue / hook line / CTA)
- Change request: “Replace caption with: …” or “Trim 0.5s before this line”

**What counts as an included revision (within scope)**
- Caption text fixes (spelling, accuracy, minor rewording)
- Timing tweaks (tighten intro, remove pauses, adjust cut points)
- Reframe adjustments (safe margins, face tracking, cropping)
- CTA/end-card text swap (same style preset)

**Out of scope (requires a new order or extra time)**
- Changing the style preset after delivery (fonts/colors/layout)
- Rebuilding clip selection strategy (new hooks/topics) after the 10 clips are delivered
- Adding new source footage that wasn’t provided at intake

**Revision window & turnaround**

- Included: **1 revision round** for up to **3 clips** (most clients only need small fixes).
- Turnaround: typically **24 hours** for in-scope revisions.
