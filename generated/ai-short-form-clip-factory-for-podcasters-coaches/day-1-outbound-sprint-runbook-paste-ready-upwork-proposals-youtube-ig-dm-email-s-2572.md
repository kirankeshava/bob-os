============================
DAY-1 RUNBOOK (2.5–3 hours)
============================
Goal today: 6–8 Upwork proposals + 30–40 creator touches (comments/DMs/emails) + log everything.

A) SETUP (10 min)
1) Open CRM sheet.
2) Create two tabs:
   - Send Queue (Today): Lead | Channel | Link | Personalization Hook (Episode + timestamp) | Message Type | Status | Next Action Date
   - Follow-up Queue: Lead | Last Touch Date | Last Touch Type | Response | Next Action | Notes
3) Time blocks:
   - Block 1 (45 min): Upwork proposals (6–8)
   - Block 2 (60 min): YouTube comments (20 videos; 2 per creator)
   - Block 3 (45 min): DMs/emails (10–20)
   - Block 4 (15 min): Logging + schedule follow-ups

B) YOUTUBE MICRO-PERSONALIZATION WORKFLOW (repeat per creator)
1) Find two recent long episodes.
2) Pull ONE specific timestamp moment (e.g., “3:42 when you explain X”).
3) Leave 2 comments:
   - Comment 1: insight + timestamp
   - Comment 2: repurposing angle suggestion (hook + clip idea)
4) Then DM/email:
   - Subject/first line references that timestamp.
   - Offer: “I’ll cut 1 free 30–60s sample vertical clip with captions + hook.”

C) FOLLOW-UP RULES
- Upwork: Follow up 48 hours after proposal if no response.
- DMs/emails: Follow up after 48 hours with “I made a sample / want it?” OR “Want me to make the sample from [link]?”
