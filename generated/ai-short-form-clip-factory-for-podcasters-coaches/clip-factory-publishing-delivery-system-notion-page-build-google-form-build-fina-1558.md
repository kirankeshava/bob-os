- Export: 1080x1920, H.264, high bitrate, crisp text, correct aspect ratio

H2: What counts as a free revision vs. a paid change
Free revisions (examples):
- Caption typo/wording mismatch vs audio
- Minor timing tweaks (captions on/off a beat)
- Small crop/position adjustments to fix cut-off faces or safe-zone issues
- Fixing an editor mistake against your intake instructions

Paid changes (examples):
- New creative direction after delivery (different hook angle, different story)
- Swapping to new source footage not originally provided
- Adding net-new clips beyond scope
- Extensive re-cut/re-script because the target platform/audience changed

H2: How to request revisions (fastest method)
Bullets:
- Reply with timestamped notes OR comment directly on the review link
- Group notes per clip
- Confirm whether you want “minimal fixes” or “creative rework”
