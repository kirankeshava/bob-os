E) LOGGING SOP (NON-NEGOTIABLE DAILY CADENCE)
Tool: simple sheet/CSV with columns: Date | Lead Name | Segment (YT/IG/TikTok/Upwork) | URL | Contact Method | Personalization (episode + timestamp) | Message Type (comment/DM/email/proposal) | Status (Sent/Opened/Replied/Booked/Won/Lost) | Next Action Date | Notes

Daily blocks:
- Morning (60–90 min): send all new messages (8 Upwork proposals + 30 creator outreaches).
- Immediately after sending: log each touch + set Next Action Date.
- Afternoon (30 min): follow-ups due today (T+2 and T+5) + reply handling.

Counting rules for KPI dashboard:
- “Connection attempt” = DM sent OR email sent OR Upwork proposal submitted (count separately if needed, but keep a single total).
- “Proposal” = Upwork proposal OR explicit priced offer via DM/email.
- “Reply” = any response (positive/neutral/negative).
- “Meeting” = scheduled call OR request to send sample OR asked for pricing (track separately).

Follow-up schedule:
- T+2: short bump + repeat free sample offer.
- T+5: new angle (different timestamp insight) + ask a binary question (e.g., ‘Want 10 clips/48h or just 1 sample first?’).

Close trigger:
- Once they say “yes / interested” → ask for link + preferred style (captions, pacing, platform) and confirm delivery SLA (48 hours for 10 clips).
