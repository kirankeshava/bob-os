**Reply / Support Email:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

### What you’ll receive
For each batch, you’ll get:
- **10 vertical clips** (ready to post) in **9:16**
- **Burned-in captions** + hook styling + light pattern interrupts
- **Platform-ready exports** (TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts)
- A **Delivery Notes** doc with: clip titles, timestamps from the original, and suggested post copy/CTA (optional)

### How to review (fastest method)
Please review in this order:
1) **Hook (first 1–2 seconds):** does it grab attention instantly?
2) **Caption readability:** can you read it on mobile without pausing?
3) **Cut tightness:** no dead air, no long breaths, no rambling starts
4) **Brand fit:** tone, “you” language, and any sensitive claims/compliance

### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are **small, fast edits** that don’t change the clip selection:
- Adjust captions (spelling, punctuation, emphasis, timing)
- Swap hook text overlay (same clip)
- Remove/add a bleep, tighten pauses, remove filler words
- Adjust caption placement to avoid covering faces or on-screen text
- Minor crop/reframe to keep speaker centered

### What is NOT included as a revision (scope change)
These are treated as a **new request** (we can quote/slot it next batch):
- “Pick a different moment” (new highlight selection)
- Rebuilding the clip with a different angle/storyline
- Adding custom motion graphics packages beyond the preset
- Heavy manual b-roll sourcing/stock footage selection (unless included in your tier)

### How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Send **one email** with all edits consolidated:
Subject: **Revisions — [ClientName] — [EpisodeName] — Batch [#]**

Body:
- Clip filename: **[exact filename]**
- Timecode in the clip: **00:00–00:00**
- Change request: **[exact text to replace / what to remove / what to move]**
- Priority: **Must / Nice-to-have**

Example:
- Clip filename: **ACME_POD_E12_C03_HowToStopOverthinking_v1.mp4**
- Timecode: **00:01–00:04**
- Change: Replace hook text with: “Stop overthinking in 10 seconds”
