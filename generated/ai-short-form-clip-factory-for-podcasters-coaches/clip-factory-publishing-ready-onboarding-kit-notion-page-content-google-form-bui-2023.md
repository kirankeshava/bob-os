2) Our Clip QA Standards (What We Check Before You See Anything)
We run a final QC pass on every clip to avoid preventable revisions.

A. Framing + Safe Zones
- 9:16 vertical framing, subject centered.
- No cropped faces: eyes/forehead/chin remain in-frame throughout.
- Platform safe zones respected:
  - Keep captions and key on-screen text away from bottom UI areas.
  - Keep key elements away from right-side UI overlays.
- No awkward zoom/crop artifacts.

B. Captions (Accuracy + Readability)
- Captions match spoken words (no meaning-changing errors).
- Punctuation for clarity; remove filler words only if it improves clarity and doesn’t change intent.
- Line breaks for fast reading; avoid long single-line walls of text.
- Proper nouns checked (names/brands/locations) as best as possible.

C. Hook + Clarity
- First 1–2 seconds contain a clear hook (curiosity, outcome, contrarian statement, or strong claim).
- Clip can be understood without external context.
- No dead air at the start; remove long pauses.

D. Pacing + Jump Cuts
- Cuts feel natural; no jarring micro-cuts that reduce trust.
- Remove obvious verbal stumbles if it improves flow.
- Maintain continuity (no mid-word cuts; no chopped breaths unless intentional).

E. Audio
- Spoken voice is consistently audible.
- Leveling: avoid large volume swings.
- Reduce harshness/sibilance where possible.
- No clipping/distortion.

F. Visual Enhancements (When Used)
- B-roll cues support the point and don’t distract.
- On-screen keywords emphasize meaning (not clutter).
- Brand colors/fonts applied only if provided/approved.

G. Export + Platform Formatting
- MP4 delivery.
- 1080x1920 (9:16).
- H.264 (standard social-friendly encoding).
- Crisp text rendering; no excessive compression.

3) Review Process (How You Approve Fast)
We provide a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) that supports timestamp comments.

How to request changes:
- Leave comments at exact timestamps (e.g., “00:07 – swap caption wording” / “00:12 – remove pause”).
- Group feedback into one message when possible to avoid delays.

Revision window:
- 24 hours from delivery of the review link.
- If you miss the window, we’ll still help, but turnaround may be longer depending on queue.

4) What You Receive (Delivery Pack)
You receive a Delivery Pack folder containing:

/01_FINAL_MP4
- Final vertical clips in MP4

/02_CAPTIONS_SRT
- Matching .SRT caption files for each clip

/03_BROLL_CUE_SHEET
- A cue sheet listing timestamps + suggested b-roll/visual overlays

/04_POSTING_SUGGESTIONS
- For each clip: recommended first-line caption, CTA suggestion, and hashtags

5) Revision Policy (To Protect Quality + Speed)
Free revisions (within 24 hours) include:
- Caption typo fixes or minor phrasing edits
- Timing nudges for captions
- Small framing adjustments to prevent face cropping
- Minor audio leveling tweaks
- Swapping a b-roll suggestion or removing a distracting overlay

Not free (scope changes) include:
- Changing the hook concept entirely after delivery
- Requesting additional clips beyond the agreed quantity
- Recutting from a different part of the source not included in the original selection
- Full style change (new font package/branding system) not provided up front
- New deliverables requested after approval (e.g., extra aspect ratios, long-form edits)

If something is objectively broken (cropped face, wrong audio segment, corrupted export), we fix it ASAP.

6) How to Get the Best Results
- Send your best episodes (clear audio matters).
- Tell us your target audience + desired CTA.
- Share 1–2 example clips you love (links are fine).
