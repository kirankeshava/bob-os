## Real Estate (4)
### RE-Story-01 (Storytelling hook)
- Hook (spoken/on-screen): “I’ve seen buyers lose the house before they even tour it—here’s why.”
- Caption beats:
  1) “Buyers lose homes BEFORE the tour…”
  2) “The mistake: waiting to verify ___”
  3) “Fix this in 10 minutes:”
- Edit beats: cold open on hook → hard cut to consequence → 3-step fix.
- B-roll cues: frantic email/phone screen, calendar booking, “pre-approval” doc overlay.
- CTA: “If you want 3–5 clips/week like this, see: [website] / email [email].”

### RE-List-02 (Listicle)
- Hook: “3 things I’d check before making an offer in 2026.”
- Captions: “3 Offer Checks” → #1 comps → #2 inspection risk → #3 financing timeline.
- Edit: quick numbered pops; zoom on each number.
- B-roll: neighborhood shots, inspection checklist, mortgage rate chart.
- CTA: end card.

### RE-Contrarian-03 (Contrarian take)
- Hook: “Skipping the open house can be smarter—and most people don’t realize it.”
- Captions: “Hot take: Skip the open house” → “Why:” → “Do this instead:”
- Edit: pause + subtitle emphasis on “smarter”; cut to alternative.
- B-roll: crowded open house, private showing calendar, agent text overlay.
- CTA: end card.

### RE-Testimonial-04 (Testimonial style)
- Hook: “One client thought they needed 20% down… they didn’t.”
- Captions: “Client win” → “Belief: need 20%” → “Reality: options” → “Result:”
- Edit: show ‘before belief’ → ‘new info’ → ‘outcome’.
- B-roll: simple before/after text cards, house keys, “approved” stamp.
- CTA: end card.
