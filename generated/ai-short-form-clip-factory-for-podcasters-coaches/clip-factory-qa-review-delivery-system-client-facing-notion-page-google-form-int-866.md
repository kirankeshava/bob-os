Section 1 — The Workflow (Simple)
1) Intake (you tell us goals + platforms + style)
2) Production (we cut clips, add captions, hooks, b-roll cues)
3) Review (you leave timestamped notes)
4) Delivery (final MP4s + SRT + cue sheets + posting suggestions)

Section 2 — What You’ll Receive (Delivery Pack)
For each clip we deliver:
- Final MP4 (vertical)
- Captions file (.SRT)
- B-roll Cue Sheet (Google Doc or PDF)
- Posting Suggestions (first-line caption options + hashtags + CTA)

Section 3 — QA Standards (What We Check Before You See Anything)
We run every clip through a final QC pass:
A) Framing & Safe Zones
- Subject’s face stays fully visible (no cropped forehead/chin)
- Platform-safe margins respected (no key text under UI elements)
- No accidental zooms/crops that cut out speakers or on-screen text

B) Captions Accuracy
- Captions match spoken words (no meaning changes)
- Punctuation and capitalization are clean and readable
- Names/brands/terms spelled correctly (per your intake form)

C) Audio
- Speech is clear and consistent (no sudden volume jumps)
- Noise reduction applied where needed without “robotic” artifacts
- Music (if used) never overpowers voice

D) Editing & Pacing
- Strong hook in the first 1–2 seconds
- Jump cuts feel intentional (no awkward stutters or dead air)
- Clip ends cleanly (no mid-word cutoffs)

E) Export/Format
- Vertical export optimized for Reels/TikTok/Shorts
- File naming matches the delivery pack standard

Section 4 — Review Process (Timestamp Comments)
We provide a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) or a Google Drive preview.

How to give feedback (best practice):
- Leave timestamped notes (e.g., “00:08 replace word ‘X’ with ‘Y’”)
- Keep feedback consolidated in one message when possible
- If you want a structural change, say the goal (e.g., “make it more high-energy”)

Revision Window:
- Includes 1 revision round per batch (unless your package states otherwise)
- Revision window is 24 hours from the time we send the review link
- If we don’t receive notes within 24 hours, we treat the batch as approved and proceed to final delivery

Section 5 — Revisions: What’s Included vs Paid Changes
Included (free) revisions (within the revision window):
- Caption fixes (missed words, punctuation, spelling)
- Minor timing tweaks (tighten pauses, align captions)
- Minor reframing adjustments to fix cropping/safe-zone issues
- Small audio leveling corrections
- Swap a b-roll moment for another option already in the cue list

Paid changes (scope change) examples:
- New creative direction after delivery (different style, different hook strategy)
- Major restructuring (new storyline, new ordering, new theme)
- Recutting using different timestamps or different source sections not in the original selection
- Adding new branding packages or motion graphics not agreed in intake
- Multiple revision rounds beyond the included round

If something is our mistake (QA miss), we fix it fast at no cost.
If it’s a scope change, we’ll quote a price before doing the extra work.

Section 6 — How to Avoid Delays (Fastest Path)
To keep turnaround fast, please provide:
- Platform targets (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Your brand terms + banned words
- Your handle(s) and CTA link
- Any required on-screen text style or examples you like
