B) 3 Loom script outlines (send as a 60–120s video)
All Looms end with: “If you want, I’ll do a free sample clip from your latest episode. Details here: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3 — or just reply to agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to.”

Loom #1 — Personalized Audit (90s)
1) 10s: Who you are + why you’re messaging (mention their show/name).
2) 20s: What’s already working (specific episode, guest, strong moments).
3) 30s: Gap: inconsistent shorts cadence / no pattern of hooks / captions hard to read.
4) 20s: What you’d do: “Turn each episode into 8–15 vertical clips with 3 hook options, burned-in captions, and platform-safe formatting.”
5) 10s CTA: free sample clip + ask where to pull long-form from.

Loom #2 — Sample Clip Teardown (120s)
1) Show their existing clip (or a timestamp in a long episode).
2) Explain 3 improvements:
   - Hook rewrite (first 1.5s)
   - Caption style (keywords, pace, line breaks)
   - B-roll cue idea (screenshots, headline overlay)
3) Close: “Want me to produce 3 like this from the latest episode as a sample?”

Loom #3 — Mini Case-Study Pitch (60–90s)
1) Frame: “Creators who post 3–5 shorts/week win distribution.”
2) Your mechanism: “We extract highlights + hooks + captions + export ready-to-post 9:16.”
3) Offer: “Pilot: 10 clips in 72 hours from one episode.”
4) CTA: link + email.
