1) CLIENT ONE‑PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW CLIPS + REQUEST REVISIONS
====================
Title: How to Review Your Clips (Fast) + Request Revisions

You’ll receive: 10 ready-to-post vertical clips delivered within 48 hours (unless otherwise agreed). To keep turnaround fast, please review using the steps below.

Where to review
- You’ll get a delivery email with either:
  (A) a share link to a review folder, or
  (B) a zip + a simple index (clip list).
- If you need anything resent, email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
- Proof of business / reference link: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

How to review (best practice)
1) Watch each clip once with sound ON (hook + pacing).
2) Watch again with sound OFF (caption readability + framing).
3) Check the first 1.5 seconds: does it “grab” immediately?
4) Check captions: accurate, not covering face, readable on mobile.
5) Check CTA/end card: correct handle, offer, and branding.

What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are meant for “make it correct,” not a full creative re-cut.
Included (typical):
- Caption fixes (typos, missed words, punctuation)
- Minor timing tweaks (caption sync, tightening dead air)
- Cropping/framing adjustments (keep speaker centered)
- Swap hook text overlay (if meaning stays the same)
- Replace CTA/end card text/handle
- Remove a specific phrase or bleep a word

Not included (would be a new clip / new order)
- Rebuilding the storyline from scratch
- Changing the clip topic/angle to a different segment not used
- New b-roll sourcing beyond simple cues already provided
- Major re-edit requiring new selects across the full episode

How to request revisions (required format)
Reply to the delivery email and paste this template:

Subject: Revisions – [ClientName] – [EpisodeName/Date]

Clip ID(s): (example: 03, 07)
For each clip:
- Clip ID:
- Timestamp (mm:ss – mm:ss):
- Issue type (choose one): Caption / Hook text / Framing / Audio / CTA / Other
- Exact change requested (one sentence):

Example:
- Clip ID: 03
- Timestamp: 00:07–00:10
- Issue type: Caption
- Exact change: Change “their” to “there” and remove the comma after “actually”.

Revision SLA
- 1 revision round is included for up to 10 clips.
- Turnaround: 24 hours after we receive your revision request in the required format.
- If feedback is delivered across multiple messages without timestamps, turnaround may reset to when we receive the consolidated request.

Approval tip
If you like a clip but want to test a different hook, request ONE alternate hook per batch (we’ll prioritize speed). If you request alternates for many clips, that becomes a new creative pass.
