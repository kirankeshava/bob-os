1) CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW CLIPS + REQUEST REVISIONS
==============================
Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

Where to review
- You’ll receive a delivery email with a folder link and/or review link.
- Each clip is named consistently so you can approve quickly.

What “done” looks like (quick approval criteria)
Approve a clip if:
1) The hook lands within the first 1.5 seconds.
2) Captions are readable on mobile (no cut-off, clean contrast).
3) The main point is understandable with sound OFF.
4) The clip ends cleanly and matches your brand tone.

How to review in under 10 minutes
Step 1: Watch all clips once on your phone (sound off).
Step 2: Star/mark your favorites for immediate posting.
Step 3: For any clip needing a change, leave ONE consolidated note per clip.

How to request revisions (required format)
To keep the 48-hour workflow fast, please send revision notes in this exact format:
- Clip Name: (example: EP12_CLIP03_Hook-Referral_MW_v1)
- Timestamp: (example: 00:07–00:12)
- Change type (choose one):
  A) Caption text fix
  B) Caption styling adjustment (size/position)
  C) Trim/cut adjustment
  D) Swap to an alternate moment (provide timestamp range)
  E) CTA/end-card text change
- Your exact instruction (one sentence):
  Example: “Replace caption line with: ‘Your referrals are a system, not luck.’”

What counts as an included revision
Included (fast fixes):
- Caption typos, misheard words, punctuation
- Minor caption layout changes (position up/down, slightly larger/smaller)
- Small trims (tighten pauses, remove a phrase)
- CTA/end card text changes

What counts as a scope change (not included)
- Changing the topic/angle after delivery (new hook direction)
- Requesting entirely new clips outside the 10 delivered
- Heavy content restructuring or multiple rounds of subjective changes

Revision policy (simple)
- Included: 1 revision round per delivered batch (up to 10 clips).
- Turnaround: revisions returned within 24 hours of receiving consolidated notes.
- To avoid delays: send all feedback in one message using the format above.
