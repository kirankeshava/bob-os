## MVP Production Checklist (no paid tools)

### A) Select 6 clips (from existing 12-clip matrix)
Pick **2 per niche**:
- 1x Story hook (30–45s)
- 1x Contrarian take (20–35s)

Lock in the master sheet:
- Source URL + license proof note
- Start/end timestamps
- Hook text (first on-screen line)
- 3–5 caption emphasis keywords
- 2–3 b-roll cues max (keep simple)

### B) Edit settings (consistent across all MVP samples)
- Canvas: **1080x1920 (9:16)**
- FPS: 30
- Audio loudness: target ~ -14 LUFS (platform-safe)
- Captions: bold, high-contrast, safe margins, 2–3 lines max
- Cuts: remove silences >250ms, add subtle punch-in every 2–4 seconds
- Add on-screen label: **“SAMPLE EDIT”** small in corner

### C) Before/After (2 exports)
Option 1 (sequential):
- 0–2s title card: “BEFORE (raw excerpt)”
- 2–20s raw
- 20–22s title card: “AFTER (final clip)”
- 22–45s final

Option 2 (split-screen):
- Left: raw, Right: final (captions/zooms/b-roll cues visible)
- Add top label: BEFORE | AFTER
