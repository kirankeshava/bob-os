- CTA matches your goal (follow, comment, download, book a call)

4) What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions (up to 1 revision round):
- Caption text corrections, punctuation, emphasis
- On-screen hook/CTA text tweaks
- Cropping/positioning adjustments (speaker framing)
- Minor b-roll swap or removal
- Removing a line for compliance/brand reasons

Not included (treated as new scope):
- Rewriting the clip concept to a different takeaway
- Changing to a different timestamp segment from the source
- Major brand redesign (new style system/fonts/colors)
- Adding new assets not provided during intake
