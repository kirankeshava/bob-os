C) 24-HOUR SAMPLE-CLIP FULFILLMENT SOP (DFY, WEEK-1 FREE)
====================================================
Goal: Deliver 1 posting-ready vertical clip within 24 hours of receiving a link, and convert to a paid weekly package (later), while collecting SaaS signal data.

TOOLS (FREE / EXISTING):
• Source: YouTube link or uploaded file
• Basic editing: CapCut desktop (free) or DaVinci Resolve (free)
• Captions: CapCut auto-captions (free) or YouTube transcript + manual cleanup
• Delivery: Google Drive/Dropbox link (free tier) or direct file share

INTAKE (copy/paste message):
“Send: (1) link to the episode/video, (2) your ideal audience, (3) your CTA (follow/DM/book call), (4) any style notes (bold captions / clean captions / emojis yes-no), (5) examples of shorts you like.”

PROCESS CHECKLIST (do in order):
1) Pick 1 highlight with: clear problem, punchy opinion, or actionable list.
2) Define clip length target: 25–45 seconds (default), unless the highlight needs 45–60.
3) Create 2 hook options:
   • Hook A: curiosity (“Most people get X wrong…”) 
   • Hook B: outcome (“Do this to get Y in Z days…”) 
4) Edit vertical 9:16:
   • Center speaker, add subtle punch-ins every 2–4 seconds
   • Add safe margins for platform UI
   • Ensure audio is clean and consistent volume
5) Captions:
   • Burned-in, high contrast
   • 2–4 words per line, avoid covering mouth
   • Highlight keywords (color or weight) if possible
6) Add simple end card (last 1–2 seconds): handle + CTA.
7) Export settings (default): 1080x1920, 30fps, high bitrate.
8) QA:
   • Watch once with sound, once without sound
   • Check caption errors, jump cuts, and pacing

DELIVERY MESSAGE (copy/paste):
“Here’s your sample clip (ready to post): [link].
If you want, I can also cut 10–20/week from your backlog and format them specifically for Reels/TikTok/Shorts. If you tell me your weekly posting goal + your niche, I’ll recommend the best package.”

UPSELL QUESTIONS (to capture SaaS validation signals):
Ask after delivery:
1) “What matters more: (A) finding the best moments, (B) captions that look premium, or (C) posting consistency?”
2) “Would you pay monthly for: auto-highlights + templates + export-ready captions? If yes, what range feels fair: $99 / $199 / $299?”
3) “Do you need brand voice hooks, diarization (speaker labels), or niche templates?”

DEFINITION OF DONE:
• 1 vertical clip delivered + captions readable + hook tested + CTA included
• Turnaround ≤ 24 hours from receiving link
• At least 3 data points captured: preferred clip style, posting goal, and willingness-to-pay range
