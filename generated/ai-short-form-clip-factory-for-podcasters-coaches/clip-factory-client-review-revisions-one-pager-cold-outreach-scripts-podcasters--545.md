Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Clip Factory)

Thanks for trusting Clip Factory with your shorts. To keep the 48-hour turnaround fast and consistent, here’s the simplest way to review and request revisions.

Where to review
1) You’ll receive a download link (or folder link) containing:
   - Final MP4s (vertical 9:16)
   - Optional captions file (SRT) if requested
   - A “Clip Notes” doc (timestamps, hook notes, b-roll cues)
2) Watch each clip once all the way through on your phone if possible (that’s the real viewing context).

What “done” looks like (our quality targets)
- Hook lands immediately (first 0–1.5s)
- Captions are readable on mobile (safe margins, consistent style)
- Cuts keep pace (pattern interrupt every ~2–3s where appropriate)
- Clear point + payoff within the clip
- CTA (if included) appears at the end and does not block key visuals

How to request revisions (fastest format)
Reply with one message using this template (copy/paste):
Subject/Clip Name:
Timecode (mm:ss–mm:ss):
Change requested:
Priority: Must-have / Nice-to-have

Examples
- Clip: EP12_Clip03
  Timecode: 00:04–00:07
  Change: Replace “7 days” with “14 days” in captions
  Priority: Must-have

- Clip: EP12_Clip08
  Timecode: 00:00–00:02
  Change: Use Hook Option B from notes (less aggressive)
  Priority: Nice-to-have

What counts as a revision (included)
- Caption text fixes (typos, minor rewording)
- Caption timing tweaks
- Minor trim adjustments (tighten intro/outro, remove a sentence)
- Swap hook line if we provided options
- Small on-screen text/CTA adjustments

What counts as a new clip (not included as a revision)
- Changing the clip topic entirely
- Building a completely new highlight from a different section
- Adding new brand elements we didn’t receive at intake
- Major re-edit: changing structure, pacing style, or adding lots of new b-roll beyond the original plan

Revision policy (to protect speed)
- 1 revision round included for the delivered batch.
- Please send consolidated notes for all clips in one message.
- We aim to turn revisions around within 24 hours once we have complete notes.
