## 3) 20-Min Discovery → DFY Close Call Script (with SaaS gate)
**Goal:** Book/close a paid DFY pilot now + capture product requirements. Do NOT promise a SaaS launch date; software is gated behind validated demand.

### (0:00–2:00) Set context
“Thanks for making time. In 20 minutes I want to understand your workflow, what’s blocking consistent short-form, and if it’s a fit I’ll outline a quick DFY pilot we can start this week. Sound good?”

### (2:00–7:00) Current state + pain
1) “What’s your content stack? (podcast, YouTube, coaching calls, webinars)”
2) “How often do you want to post short-form ideally?”
3) “What happens today after you publish a long episode?”
4) “What’s the biggest bottleneck—finding highlights, editing time, captions, hooks, approvals, branding?”
5) “Do you have an editor now? What do you like/dislike about that setup?”

### (7:00–12:00) Output definition
6) “What’s a ‘good clip’ for you—education, controversy, story, tactical steps?”
7) “Any brand constraints—fonts, colors, caption style, pace, b-roll preferences?”
8) “Do you want hooks written in your voice, or just highlight selection + captions?”
9) “Where do you post—Reels, TikTok, Shorts—and do you need platform-specific variants?”

### (12:00–15:00) Willingness-to-pay + decision
10) “If this reliably got you {{X}} clips/week, what would that be worth monthly?”
11) “Would you prefer DFY (done-for-you) or a software workflow you can run yourself?”
12) “If it were software, which matters more: speed, accuracy, brand templates, or auto-picking highlights?”

**SaaS gating line (use verbatim):**
“We are validating what creators will pay for before we build software. Right now we’re delivering this DFY so you get results immediately, and your feedback shapes what becomes a $99–$299/mo product later.”

### (15:00–19:00) DFY pilot pitch (simple)
“Based on what you said, I recommend a 1-week pilot:
- You send 1 long video/episode link or file
- We deliver {{3/5/7}} vertical clips (9:16) with burned-in captions + SRT + hook options
- 1 revision round on caption styling/wording
If you like it, we roll into a weekly cadence.”

**Close question:**
“Do you want to start with the pilot and measure results for 7 days?”

### (19:00–20:00) Next steps
- Confirm content source + brand assets + turnaround time
- Confirm deliverables count
- Confirm point of contact and posting schedule
- Send recap via email: agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to
