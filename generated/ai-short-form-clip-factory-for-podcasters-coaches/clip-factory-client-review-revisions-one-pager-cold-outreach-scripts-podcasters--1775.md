## 1) Client-Facing One-Pager: How to Review & Request Revisions (Copy/Paste)

**Thanks for reviewing your clips — here’s the fastest way to give feedback so we can turn revisions around quickly.**

### What you’ll receive
- **10 vertical clips** (TikTok/IG Reels/YT Shorts-ready)
- Burned-in captions + hook-led opening
- Minor b-roll cues (when applicable)
- A simple text file / notes with any timestamps and recommendations

### How to review (10 minutes total)
1) **Watch each clip once without pausing** (gut-check the hook + pacing).
2) Rewatch only the ones you want changed and note:
   - **Clip filename**
   - **Timestamp** (example: 00:03–00:06)
   - **What to change** (choose from the menu below)
3) Reply with your revisions in the format at the bottom.

### What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions are changes that keep the core clip selection the same:
- Caption fixes (typos, punctuation, word swaps)
- Caption styling tweaks (size/position within the chosen style preset)
- Small trim/timing adjustments (tighten pauses, cut filler)
- Hook text adjustment (on-screen headline)
- CTA text change (end-card wording)
- Audio leveling (minor)

### What is not a revision (out of scope for 48-hour delivery)
These are effectively new work and can be queued for the next batch:
- Replacing the clip with a totally different moment
- Rebuilding the clip around a different angle/story
- New motion graphics package beyond the preset
- Heavy b-roll sourcing or licensed footage requests

### Revision limits + turnaround
- **Up to 1 revision round included** (batch-level)
- **Turnaround:** typically **24 hours** from receiving clearly formatted notes
- If you have more changes after Round 1, we’ll roll them into the **next batch** to keep speed high.

**Timestamp:** [00:00–00:00]
**Change:** [e.g., “Replace caption text with: …” / “Trim 0.5s pause” / “Move captions slightly higher”]
