### What you’ll receive
For each order (standard = 10 clips), you’ll receive:
1) **10 vertical videos (9:16)** ready for TikTok / IG Reels / YouTube Shorts
2) **Captions burned-in** (your chosen style preset)

4) **Optional notes** (recommended caption text, post title ideas, or suggested hashtags if requested)

### How to review (fastest process)
Please review in this order:
1) **Watch first 2 seconds**: Is the hook strong and on-topic?
2) **Watch with sound OFF**: Do captions remain readable and accurate?
3) **Scan pacing**: Any dead air longer than ~0.3–0.5 seconds? Any repeated idea?
4) **Brand check**: Is framing/caption position aligned with your preference?

### What counts as a revision (included)
Each clip includes **one revision pass** covering:
- Caption corrections (misheard words, punctuation)
- Minor timing tweaks (cut a pause, tighten intro)
- Safe reframe/face-tracking adjustments
- Swap to another **approved style preset** (Real Estate / Fitness / B2B) if requested
- CTA text tweak on end card

### What is out-of-scope (not included in the free 7-day trial workflow)
To protect 48-hour delivery speed, these are treated as new work:
- Rewriting the clip’s premise (changing the “point”)
- Creating custom motion graphics beyond the preset pack
- Extensive b-roll sourcing/licensing, custom stock selection per clip
- Multi-person story reconstruction (re-ordering the narrative across multiple timestamps)

### How to request revisions (copy/paste format)
Reply to the delivery email with:
- **Clip name** (example: CLIP05_TaxStrategy_HookA_v1.mp4)
- **Timestamp(s)** (example: 00:03–00:06)
- **Change request** (example: “Replace ‘deductions’ with ‘write-offs’ in captions; tighten pause at 00:04.”)
- **Severity**: Must-fix / Nice-to-have

### Review deadline (so we can keep the SLA)

- If we don’t hear back in 48 hours, we’ll mark the batch as **approved** and close the order to keep production moving.
