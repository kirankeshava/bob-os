## 4) Terms + Guarantee (Client-Friendly)

### What counts as a “clip”
A clip is **1 exported vertical video** (typically 15–60 seconds; longer by request) containing:
- Hook-focused cut from your provided source
- On-screen captions (burned-in)
- Basic formatting for TikTok/Reels/Shorts

### Turnaround times
- Starter: **48 hours** after we receive usable source files + intake answers
- Growth: weekly delivery (first batch within **4 business days**)
- Pro: delivered within **14 days** (with mid-point batch updates)

### Revisions
- Starter: **1 revision round** (timing/captions/trim tweaks)
- Growth/Pro: **2 revision rounds** per batch
Revisions do not include “new creative direction for all clips.” If the request changes the brief substantially, we’ll quote it as a new scope.

### Client responsibilities (to avoid delays)
You provide:
- Source files/links (Google Drive/Dropbox/YouTube unlisted okay)
- Your name, handle, brand notes, and any “do-not-say” list
- Confirmation of preferred style (clean/pro, high-energy, educational, etc.)
If intake is incomplete or the source is unusable (missing audio, corrupted files), delivery dates shift.

### Rights
You retain full rights to your original content. We grant you rights to use the edited deliverables for your channels/ads. We may reference anonymized results (views, retention) unless you request confidentiality in writing.

### Refund policy
- If we **miss the stated turnaround** by more than 48 hours (excluding client delays), you may choose: **10% refund** or **20% extra clips** added to the order.
- If deliverables are not produced at all: **full refund**.
- If work is delivered consistent with the agreed scope, refunds are not provided for “performance” (views/virality), since platforms vary.

### Platform payment rules (important)
- If the lead originated on Upwork or Fiverr, payment must follow that platform’s rules. We will not request off-platform payment when it violates terms.
- Direct invoicing is used only when the client is sourced off-platform or when the platform explicitly permits it.
