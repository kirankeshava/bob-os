C) “Raw vs Final B-roll Cues”: 8s raw talking head → 12s final with on-screen b-roll prompts (e.g., [B-ROLL: neighborhood aerial], [B-ROLL: gym close-up], [B-ROLL: dashboard/CRM]) + subtle SFX callouts.
