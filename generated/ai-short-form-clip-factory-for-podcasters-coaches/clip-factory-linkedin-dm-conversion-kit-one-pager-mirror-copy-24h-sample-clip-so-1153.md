## 6) 24-Hour Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (internal)
Goal: deliver a high-quality sample within 24 hours of receiving the link, to maximize closes.

### Inputs (collect via DM/email)
1) Link to source video (YouTube, Drive, Riverside, etc.)
2) Target platform: TikTok / IG Reels / YT Shorts
3) CTA (follow, book call, download lead magnet, comment keyword)
4) Style references (2–3 clips they like) OR “default clean”
5) Any forbidden topics/phrases

### Process (repeatable)
1) Download or access source video.
2) Identify 1 clip candidate (20–45s) with:
   - clear payoff
   - emotional spike or contrarian insight
   - minimal context required
3) Write 3 hook options (choose strongest):
   - Contrarian: “Most [niche] get this wrong…”
   - Specific outcome: “How to [result] in [time]…”
   - Pattern interrupt: “Stop doing [common thing]…”
4) Generate captions (clean, large, 2-line max). Correct key terms/names.
5) Add b-roll cues (simple on-screen prompts like “(show screenshot of X)” or “(cut to example)”).
6) Export:
   - 1080x1920 (9:16)
   - burned-in captions
   - safe margins for UI
7) QA checklist:
   - Hook starts within first 0.5–1.0s
   - Captions readable on phone
   - No dead air > 0.3s
   - Audio levels consistent
   - CTA included (final 2–3s or pinned-comment suggestion)
8) Deliver:
   - 1 MP4 file
   - Text note: hook used + suggested caption text + suggested posting time (optional)
