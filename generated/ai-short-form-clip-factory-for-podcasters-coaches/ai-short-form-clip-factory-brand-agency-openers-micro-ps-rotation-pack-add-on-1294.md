B) 12 MICRO-PS LINES (ROTATE FOR CREDIBILITY)
=========================

How to use:
- Add ONE PS under your main message (don’t stack).
- Choose based on the lead’s biggest concern: speed, quality, brand safety, editing load, consistency, or platform formatting.

1) Speed / responsiveness
“PS: Turnaround is 24h for the sample; ongoing batches can be delivered in 48–72h depending on volume.”

2) Quality control (human-in-the-loop)
“PS: We use AI to accelerate, but every clip is human-checked for pacing, captions, and hook clarity before delivery.”

3) Captions reliability
“PS: Captions are burned-in, styled for mobile readability, and we can match your brand font/colors if you have them.”

4) Hook + structure
“PS: Each clip includes a written hook + suggested title so you’re not guessing what to post or how to frame it.”

5) B-roll cues
“PS: We add b-roll / cutaway cues (where to insert screenshots, product shots, headlines) to keep retention high.”

6) Platform formatting
“PS: We format for vertical first (safe margins, subtitle placement) and can output variations for Reels/TikTok/Shorts.”

7) Consistency / volume
“PS: Most clients start with 8–20 clips/month so posting stays consistent without hiring a full-time editor.”

8) Minimizing your workload
“PS: You can literally just send the link + the goal (sales/authority/recruiting) — we handle the rest.”

9) Editor-friendly (if they already have one)
“PS: If you already have an editor, we can deliver a clip pack with timestamps + hook options so they can execute faster.”

10) Brand safety / compliance
“PS: We can follow a do-not-say list and keep claims compliant (especially for health/finance niches).”

11) Proof mindset
“PS: No pressure to commit — the 1 free sample clip is to prove the output quality and style match.”

12) Legitimacy / where to reply
