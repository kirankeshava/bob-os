# Clip Factory — What to Expect (Client Onboarding Page)

**Official site (legitimacy & info):** https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3  
**Support / delivery contact:** agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to

## 1) What you’ll receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch/order you’ll get a **Delivery Pack** folder containing:
1. **Final MP4 clips** (vertical, ready-to-post)
2. **Caption files (.SRT)** for each clip
3. **B-roll cue sheet** (timestamps + what to overlay)
4. **Posting suggestions** per clip:
   - Suggested first-line caption (hook)
   - Suggested caption body
   - Hashtag set (platform-specific)
   - Optional CTA line

## 2) Our QA standards (how we avoid rework)
We run a final QA pass on every clip to ensure:
- **Safe zones:** text/captions stay within platform-safe margins; nothing critical is covered by UI.
- **Captions accuracy:** spelling and meaning match the audio; punctuation and line breaks improve readability.
- **Audio leveling:** voice is clearly audible, consistent loudness across clips, no peaking/distortion.
- **Framing:** no cropped faces; speaker stays centered; zooms are smooth and intentional.
- **Hook clarity:** first 1–2 seconds are attention-grabbing (clear statement/question/result).
- **Jump cuts:** cuts feel natural (no mid-word cuts); pacing fits short-form consumption.
- **Export settings:** correct resolution/aspect ratio; high-quality bitrate; no black bars.

## 3) Review process (simple + timestamped)
You’ll receive a **review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can leave **timestamped comments**.

**How to request changes:**
- Please comment directly on the timeline with:
  1) timestamp,
  2) what to change,
  3) what you want instead (example text if applicable).

## 4) Revision window + turnaround
- **Revision window:** 24 hours after we send the review link.
- **Turnaround for included revisions:** typically 24 hours after receiving clear timestamped notes (depending on volume).

If we don’t receive revision notes within the 24-hour window, the order is considered **accepted and finalized**.

## 5) What counts as a free revision vs. paid change
### Included (free) revisions
These are changes that correct mistakes or match agreed requirements:
- Caption typos or misheard words
- Minor timing adjustments (caption sync / cut timing)
- Fixing cropped faces or unsafe-zone issues
- Audio leveling fixes (too loud/quiet, peaking)
- Formatting corrections to match the requested platform specs

### Paid changes (scope changes)
These are requests that change direction or add new work:
- Changing the selected clip moments (new segments) after delivery
- Rewriting hooks/captions beyond small tweaks (new angles/strategy)
- Adding new b-roll concepts not in the original cues
- Brand/style changes after you approved a different style (fonts, colors, pacing)
- Additional versions for new platforms/aspect ratios not originally requested

If something is a paid change, we’ll reply with a clear quote and options before any work begins.

## 6) How to get the best results (quick tips)
To help us nail the clips with minimal revisions, please share:
- Your **target platform(s)** (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Your **audience** + desired tone (direct, friendly, high-energy, etc.)
- Any **banned words/topics**
- Your **CTA** (follow, comment, book a call, download, etc.)
- Brand assets (logo, font preference, colors) if needed
