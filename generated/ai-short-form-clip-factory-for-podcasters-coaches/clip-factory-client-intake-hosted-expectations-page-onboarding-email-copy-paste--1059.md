### A) How the review works (fast + timestamped)
1) **We send a review link** (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) with your draft clips.
2) **You leave timestamp comments** directly on the video (e.g., “00:07 caption typo”, “00:12 crop left”).
3) **We apply included revisions** and deliver finals.

**Revision window:** You have **24 hours** after the review link is sent to request included revisions. If you need more time, email agent_bob_replit+clip-factory@agentmail.to before the 24 hours ends.

### B) What we QA before anything goes to you
We run a final QC pass on every clip:
- **Safe zones:** subtitles and key text kept in center-safe; no UI-covered text
- **No cropped faces:** framing avoids cutting off forehead/chin; speaker stays readable
- **Captions accuracy:** correct words, names, punctuation; readable line breaks
- **Audio leveling:** voice clarity prioritized; no peaking/clipping; consistent loudness
- **Hook clarity:** first 1–2 seconds communicate the reason to keep watching
- **Jump cuts:** clean transitions; no awkward mid-word cuts
- **Export settings:** vertical 9:16 MP4, high quality, platform-friendly bitrate

### C) What you receive (Delivery Pack)
For each batch, you get a folder with:
1) **Final MP4s** (ready to post)
2) **SRT caption files** (one per clip)
3) **B-roll cue sheet** (timestamps + suggested overlays)
4) **Posting suggestions** (first-line caption + hashtag set per clip)

### D) What counts as an included revision vs paid change
**Included (free) revisions** within 24 hours:
- Caption typo/wording corrections for accuracy
- Subtitle positioning/safe area fixes
- Minor crop/framing adjustments
- Light audio tweaks (gain/noise reduction)
- Remove filler words or tighten pacing within the same selected segment

**Paid changes (scope change):**
- Picking entirely new moments/clips not in the draft set
- New creative direction (different hook style, new caption theme, new brand look)
- Major rewrites of captions for tone/voice (beyond accuracy)
- Adding new assets you didn’t provide (logos, brand kits) after work begins
