## Discovery → close call script (20 minutes, designed to close a DFY pilot)
1) Frame (1 min)
“Goal: understand your current clip workflow, show you what we deliver, and if it’s a fit we’ll lock a 2-week pilot.”

2) Context (5 min)
- What content do you publish weekly? (episodes, trainings)
- Where do clips break down today? (time, editor, consistency)
- Volume target: how many clips/week would actually move the needle?

3) Quality bar (4 min)
- Show me 1–2 examples you like (or describe).
- Any non-negotiables? (brand fonts, colors, profanity rules, topic sensitivity)

4) Turnaround + workflow (3 min)
- How fast do you need first batch after publishing?
- Do you want approvals, or is “ship it” okay?

5) Paid pilot pitch (4 min)
“For the pilot, you send 1–2 long videos. We deliver {{8–15}} ready-to-post vertical clips/week with hooks + captions. Two-week pilot so you can judge quality + cadence. If it works, we roll into monthly.”

6) Close (3 min)
- “Which is closer to what you want: 8 clips/week or 15 clips/week?”
- “If we can hit your style and turnaround, are you ready to start this week?”
- Next steps: confirm deliverables, collect asset links, send invoice (handled outside this document).

Important: if they ask about SaaS self-serve, say:
“We’re validating the workflow with DFY clients first. The self-serve version is planned, but not something I’ll sell until it’s proven.”
