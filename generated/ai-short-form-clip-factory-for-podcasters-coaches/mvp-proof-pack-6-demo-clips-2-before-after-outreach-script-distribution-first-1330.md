MVP CLIP MATRIX (COVERS ALL 4 STYLES)
Use 1 license-safe longform source per niche, producing 2 clips per source.

1) REAL ESTATE (2 clips)
- Clip RE-1 (Storytelling Hook): “The mistake that costs buyers $___ in the first 10 minutes”
  Edit: fast cold open, 1.2–1.4x pacing, punch-in every 2–3 sec, captions w/ 2–3 word highlights.
  B-roll cues: [ON SCREEN] “Insert: listing photo / neighborhood street / rate chart.”
- Clip RE-2 (Listicle): “3 things to check before you tour a home”
  Edit: list counter 1/2/3, whoosh SFX optional, hard cuts on each point.
  B-roll cues: “inspection checklist / HVAC / roof / comps screenshot.”

2) FITNESS (2 clips)
- Clip FT-1 (Contrarian Take): “Stop doing X if your goal is Y”
  Edit: strong opener, quick jump cuts, emphasize ‘STOP’ and ‘INSTEAD’ in captions.
  B-roll cues: exercise demo close-ups / form overlay arrows.
- Clip FT-2 (Testimonial/Results Framing): “Client result in 30 days (what actually worked)”
  Edit: before/after text card, then 3-step breakdown.
  B-roll cues: progress photo blur boxes, meal prep, workout clips.

3) B2B (2 clips)
- Clip B2B-1 (Storytelling Hook): “We almost lost the deal—here’s the 10-sec fix”
  Edit: cold open w/ stakes, remove pauses, add on-screen ‘Problem → Fix → Result’ strip.
  B-roll cues: CRM pipeline screenshot, calendar view, doc signing.
- Clip B2B-2 (Contrarian or Listicle): “Don’t hire for X—do this instead” OR “3 onboarding steps that cut churn”
  Edit: kinetic captions, 1 idea per sentence, pattern interrupt every 4–6 sec.
  B-roll cues: dashboard charts, Slack/Notion visuals, call recording waveforms.
