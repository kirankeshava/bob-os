## B) 24-Hour “Sample Clip” Fulfillment SOP (internal)
**Goal:** Deliver 1 finished vertical clip in ≤24 hours from receiving the source link/assets, then upsell to a DFY package.

### 1) Intake (0–15 minutes)
**Trigger:** Client paid or agreed to sample.

Send the **Intake Request** (copy below) asking for:
- Source: YouTube link / Drive link / podcast episode link
- Target platform: TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts (or all)
- Brand notes: colors/fonts (optional), preferred caption style, any do-not-say words
- Audience + CTA: “follow for ___”, “book a call”, “download lead magnet”
- 2 examples they like (optional)

Log in CRM:
- Client name, email, link received time, due time (+24h), niche, offer, CTA.

### 2) Find the moment (15–45 minutes)
Pick **one** segment with:
- Clear takeaway or contrarian statement
- Emotional spike (laugh, surprise, strong opinion)
- Minimal context required

Target length:
- 20–35s for TikTok/Reels
- Up to 45s for YT Shorts if pacing is strong

Write:
- Hook line (first 1–2 seconds)
- Caption emphasis words (3–6 key words to highlight)
- Optional on-screen title (5–8 words)

### 3) Edit + format (45–180 minutes)
**Specs:**
- 9:16, 1080x1920
- Loudness normalized (don’t distort)
- Captions burned-in, readable, high contrast
- Remove dead air; add jump cuts on breaths where natural

**Template rules (keep simple):**
- Safe margins for platform UI
- Captions bottom-middle, not too low
- Speaker framing: face in top 2/3 when possible

Optional enhancements (only if they help clarity):
- Subtle zooms
- 1–2 b-roll cutaways max
- On-screen keyword emphasis

### 4) QA checklist (10 minutes)
- Hook hits immediately (no intro music, no “today we’re going to…”) 
- Captions: no typos, not covering face, timing accurate
- End: clear payoff or CTA; doesn’t cut mid-word
- File name: `CLIENT_topic_platform_YYYY-MM-DD.mp4`

### 5) Delivery (10 minutes)
Deliver via Drive/Dropbox link (view + download). Include:
- Final MP4
- Suggested caption text (copy/paste)
- 3 hashtag options (lightly researched, not spam)

### 6) Upsell (5 minutes)
Immediately ask for next step:
- “Want 10–20 clips from your next episode each week?”
- Offer DFY package with turnaround + weekly cadence
