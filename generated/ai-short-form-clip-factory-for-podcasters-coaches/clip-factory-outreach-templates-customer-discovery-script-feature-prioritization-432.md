B) CUSTOMER DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (20 minutes)
Goal: Confirm the painful job-to-be-done, current workflow cost, and which features justify $99–$299/mo. Do NOT pitch building. Offer DFY sample and learn.

0) Setup (1 min)
- “Thanks — quick context: I’m validating a clip workflow for creators who want consistent Reels/TikToks without hiring an editor full-time. I’ll ask a few questions, then I’ll summarize what I heard and what I think would be worth paying for. Cool?”

1) Their content + goals (3 min)
- “What are you selling or trying to grow right now (course, coaching, leads, audience)?”
- “Where do short-form clips matter most for you (TikTok, Reels, Shorts, LinkedIn)?”
- “How many long episodes do you publish per week/month?”

2) Current repurposing workflow (4 min)
- “Walk me through your process from episode to short clips.”
- “Who does the work today (you, VA, editor, agency)?”
- “How many clips do you actually publish per week?”
- “What does it cost you (time + money)?”

3) Pain + failure points (4 min)
- “Where does it break down most often? (finding moments, writing hooks, captions, editing time, consistency, approvals, brand style)”
- “What have you tried that didn’t work?”

4) Reaction to the proposed MVP flow (4 min)
Present in 20 seconds:
- “Imagine: paste a YouTube link or upload → it transcribes → finds highlight segments → you choose a template → it exports 9:16 with burned-in captions + a few hook options + b-roll suggestions.”

Ask:
- “What part of that is most valuable?”
- “What part do you not trust / would need to control?”
- “What would make you say ‘this is a must-have’?”

5) Willingness-to-pay + packaging (4 min)
- “If this reliably saved you {{X}} hours and produced publish-ready clips, what would that be worth monthly?” (let them answer first)
- Then anchor ranges: “Would $99/mo feel like a no-brainer, maybe, or no? How about $199? $299?”
- “What billing model fits you better: per month with clip limits, or pay-per-use per export?”
- “What outcome would you need to see in the first week to keep paying?”

6) Feature validation (final 2 min)
Pick 3–5 and ask which are worth paying for:
- Diarization / speaker labeling
- Brand kit (fonts/colors/caption style) + reusable templates
- ‘Brand voice’ hook generator trained on your content
- Niche templates (podcast, real estate, fitness, B2B, etc.)
- Auto b-roll suggestions + timestamps
- Auto-publish/scheduler
- Team workflow (approvals, comments)
- Analytics (hook retention score / predicted watch time)

Close:
- “Can I follow up with either (a) a free sample clip concept, or (b) a paid done-for-you batch if you want clips immediately?”
- Get permission to use quotes + ask for 1 referral.

Decision thresholds (recommended)
