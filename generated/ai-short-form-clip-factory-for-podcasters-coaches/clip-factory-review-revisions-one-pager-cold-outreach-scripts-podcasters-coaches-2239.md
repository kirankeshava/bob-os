1) CLIENT ONE-PAGER: HOW TO REVIEW & REQUEST REVISIONS
==============================
Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

Delivery format you’ll receive (typical):
- A folder or Drive link containing:
  1) Final exports (9:16 vertical)
  2) A “Clip Index” document (titles, hooks, timestamps, suggested captions/hashtags, platform notes)
  3) Optional: project files (only if requested)

Step 1 — Review in the right order (takes ~10–15 minutes)
1) Watch clip #1–#10 straight through (don’t pause).
2) On the second pass, pause only to note timecodes.
3) Check the “Clip Index” for hook/title suggestions and platform notes.

Step 2 — What counts as a revision (included)
Included revisions focus on:
- Caption accuracy (typos, missed words)
- Tightening starts/ends (remove dead air, better cut point)
- Hook alternatives using existing spoken content
- On-screen text tweaks (wording, emphasis)
- Minor pacing improvements (trim 0.25–1.0s beats)

Not included (would be a new request / next batch):
- Rewriting the entire concept to a different angle unrelated to the selected moment
- Adding brand-new external b-roll sourcing beyond simple cues
- Major re-edit across multiple minutes of the original episode
- Re-cutting all 10 clips into a new style direction after approval
