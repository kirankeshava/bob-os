CLIENT ONE-PAGER (Paste into Notion/GDoc or include in delivery email)

Title: How to Review Your Clips + Request Revisions (Fast)

Where to review
1) You’ll receive either:
   • A share link to a review page (preferred), OR
   • A Google Drive folder with MP4s + a “Clip List” doc.
2) Each clip is labeled with a Clip ID (example: CLIP-03) and a short title.

What “done” looks like (our quality bar)
• Hook starts immediately (first 0–1.5s) with a clear curiosity/benefit statement.
• Captions are readable on mobile (safe margins, high contrast).
• Pacing stays tight (pattern interrupts every ~2–3 seconds via zoom/cut/b-roll cue).
• Ends with a simple CTA (follow/comment/DM/subscribe) when appropriate.

How to review quickly (recommended)
Step 1 — First pass (10–15 minutes total)
• Watch each clip once on your phone.
• Mark: KEEP / MINOR EDIT / DROP.

Step 2 — Leave revisions in this exact format (fastest turnaround)
Copy/paste per clip:
• Clip ID: CLIP-__
• Timestamp(s): 00:__–00:__
• Change needed (choose one):
  1) Captions: fix words / punctuation / remove line
  2) Hook: swap first line to: “_____”
  3) Remove segment: cut 00:__–00:__
  4) Add b-roll cue: “_____” over 00:__–00:__
  5) Branding: change CTA to: “_____”

Revision limits (to protect speed)
• Included: 1 revision round on up to 3 clips (caption fixes + small trims + hook line tweaks).
• Not included (counts as new work): re-cutting the entire clip theme, new clip selection from scratch, new style redesign, or sourcing/licensing external b-roll.

Deadlines
• Please send revision notes within 48 hours of delivery.
• We return revisions within 24 hours (typical) after receiving clear timestamped notes.
