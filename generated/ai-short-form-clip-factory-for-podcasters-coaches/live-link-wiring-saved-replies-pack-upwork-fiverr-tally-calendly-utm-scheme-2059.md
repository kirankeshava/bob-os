LINK PLACEMENT RULES (to reduce platform issues)
Upwork:
- Safest: Put the external proof link in “Portfolio” or “Other experiences” as a reference (not as a direct off-platform ordering instruction).
- Avoid: “Pay me off Upwork” or “Order via my website.” Always keep ordering on Upwork.
- Allowed intent: Use Calendly/Tally as a ‘pre-project fit check’ and ‘requirements intake’ while stating the contract remains on Upwork.
Fiverr:
- Safest: Put intake link in FAQ (“Where do I send assets?”) and in Requirements (“After ordering, you’ll receive an intake link…”).
- Avoid: Asking them to message/email files before ordering. Phrase as “After you place the order…” to keep it compliant.
