D) 24-Hour Free Sample Clip Fulfillment SOP (to convert replies into paid DFY)
1) Intake (5 minutes):
Ask for: (a) link to episode/video, (b) target platform (TikTok/Reels/Shorts), (c) audience outcome (leads/calls/course), (d) any banned words/topics, (e) preferred tone (calm/direct/high-energy).

2) Select moment (10 minutes):
Pick 20–45 seconds with: clear claim, emotional contrast, or actionable steps. Favor moments with natural pauses.

3) Write 3 hook options (10 minutes):
- Contrarian: “Stop doing X. Do this instead.”
- Specific outcome: “In 30 days, X happens if you do this.”
- Curiosity gap: “The mistake no one notices about X…”
Choose the best one and keep the other two as alternates in your delivery email.

4) Captioning spec (10 minutes):
- Burned-in captions, 2 lines max, emphasize 1–3 keywords per sentence.
- Add bracket notes for b-roll cues (e.g., [b-roll: calendar], [on-screen text: 3 steps]).
