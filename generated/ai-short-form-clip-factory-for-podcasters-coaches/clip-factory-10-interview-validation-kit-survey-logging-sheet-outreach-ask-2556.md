B) Post-Call Logging + Feature Prioritization Sheet (Google Sheet-ready)
Create a Google Sheet with these columns (one row per interview):
1. Date
2. Name
3. Role (podcaster/coach/etc.)
4. Niche
5. Audience size (approx)
6. Long-form frequency/month
7. Current short-form frequency/week
8. Current solution (self/freelancer/in-house/none)
9. Biggest pain (verbatim quote)
10. Time spent per clip today (mins)
11. Cost per clip today ($)
12. Desired output volume/month
13. Must-have features (comma list)
14. Nice-to-have features
15. Deal-breakers
16. WTP bracket (49/99/149/299/500+)
17. Preference (DFY / self-serve / hybrid)
18. Integration needs (Drive, Dropbox, YouTube, Riverside, Descript, etc.)
19. Brand needs (fonts/colors/caption style)
20. Urgency (1–5)
21. Buying authority? (Y/N)
22. Next step booked? (Y/N)
23. Notes

Simple scoring rubric (add as a note at top of sheet):
- Demand score per feature = (# of interviews listing it as must-have) + 0.5*(# listing as nice-to-have)
- Revenue pull = average WTP of people mentioning it as must-have
- Complexity (H/M/L) = your estimate
Prioritize features that have high Demand + high Revenue pull + low/medium Complexity.
