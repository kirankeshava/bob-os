# Clip Factory — What to Expect (QA, Review, Delivery, Revisions)
**Official site (for legitimacy):** https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/3

## 1) Overview
You share a long-form video/podcast. We turn it into ready-to-post vertical clips optimized for TikTok/IG Reels/YouTube Shorts.
Each clip includes:
- Hook-first cut (fast start, no dead air)
- Burned-in captions (readable, platform-safe)
- Exported MP4s sized for vertical
- Separate caption file (SRT)
- B-roll cue sheet + notes (optional overlays/cutaways)
- Posting suggestions (caption starter line + hashtag set)

## 2) What we need from you (Intake)
Please complete the **Clip Factory Intake Form** (link goes here once created) and provide:
- Source link/file (YouTube, Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
- Target platforms (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
- Your handle(s) and preferred CTA
- Tone (clean/professional, bold, comedic, etc.)
- Brand constraints (fonts/colors if any, forbidden words/topics)
- Examples of clips you like (optional)

### Pre-flight confirmation (we do this before editing)
We confirm:
- We can access the source file + permissions are correct
- Platform targets + aspect ratio are correct
- Any “do not include” topics/phrases are recorded
- Your CTA/handles are correct
- Any logo/brand assets are received (if applicable)

## 3) Our QA standards (what we check before you ever see it)
### A) Framing & safe zones
- Subject’s face is never cropped awkwardly (forehead/chin/eyes safe)
- Text stays within platform-safe areas (not blocked by UI)
- Visual composition is centered for 9:16; no accidental zoom artifacts

### B) Captions accuracy & readability
- Spelling and punctuation checked; names/brands spelled correctly
- Captions match spoken audio (no meaning changes)
- Line breaks readable on mobile; no walls of text
- Caption style consistent across the pack (font size, highlight rules)

### C) Audio leveling
- Loudness normalized for mobile listening
- No clipping, harsh sibilance, or sudden volume jumps
- Music (if used) sits under voice; never competes

### D) Hook clarity & pacing
- First 1–2 seconds contain a clear hook (curiosity, pain point, contrarian, or promise)
- Dead air removed; tight jump cuts that still feel natural
- Clip ends with a clear point, CTA, or loopable finish

### E) Platform compliance basics
- No banned words slurs; no obvious ToS violations
- Avoids misleading claims where possible; we can’t guarantee platform approval

## 4) Review flow (fast, timestamped)
We provide a review link (Frame.io / Vimeo / unlisted YouTube) where you can comment with timestamps.
**How to request edits:**
1. Open the review link
2. Leave a comment at the exact moment you want changed
3. Be specific: “Change caption wording,” “Trim 0:03–0:05,” “Remove this sentence,” “Add CTA at end,” etc.

### Revision window
- **24 hours** from delivery of the review link.
- If we don’t receive revision notes within 24 hours, we consider the delivery accepted and archive the project.

## 5) Delivery pack (what you receive)
You receive a single folder containing:
- **/01_FINAL_MP4/** → final vertical MP4 files
- **/02_CAPTIONS_SRT/** → matching SRT files
- **/03_BROLL_CUES/** → b-roll cue sheet (timestamped suggestions)
- **/04_POSTING_SUGGESTIONS/** → caption starters + hashtag sets per clip
- **README.txt** → quick instructions + revision deadline (if applicable)

### File naming convention
`CLIENTNAME_Clip##_Platform_9x16_v01.mp4`  
Example: `AcmeCo_Clip03_Reels_9x16_v01.mp4`

## 6) Revision & issue triage rules (avoid scope confusion)
### Free revisions (included)
Free revisions cover mistakes or reasonable polish changes:
- Caption typos or misheard words
- Minor timing tweaks (tighten/extend by a second or two)
- Small framing adjustments (recenter, reduce crop)
- Audio leveling fixes
- Replace a b-roll cue if it’s clearly mismatched

### Not free (scope change / new work)
These are treated as a new request (handled after initial delivery):
- Changing the clip selection strategy (new moments, new angles)
- Adding new styles/brand packages after editing started
- Major script rewrites or changing the message of the clip
- Re-editing the pack for an additional platform spec not in intake
- Multiple rounds beyond the single revision window

### If something is truly broken
If files don’t play, links are inaccessible, or captions are corrupt: we fix immediately regardless of window.
