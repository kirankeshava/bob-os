24-HOUR SAMPLE CLIP FULFILLMENT SOP (internal)

Goal: Deliver 1 high-quality vertical clip within 24 hours of payment to maximize conversion into a larger DFY package.

Inputs required (from client):
1) Source link (YouTube/Drive) + timestamp suggestions (optional)
2) Brand notes: preferred caption style (word-by-word vs sentence), font/colors (optional)
3) Target platform priority: TikTok / Reels / Shorts
4) CTA preference: “Follow for more”, “Link in bio”, “DM me”, etc.

Workflow (time-boxed):
1) Source ingestion (10 min)
• Download or reference source from link.
• Confirm audio is clear; note any loudness issues.

2) Segment selection (20–30 min)
• Find 2–3 candidate segments (15–35 seconds) with:
  - Clear payoff
  - Minimal context needed
  - A strong first sentence (or can be reframed with hook text)
• Pick best candidate.

3) Edit + pacing (30–45 min)
• Cut dead air, tangents, repeated words.
• Add tight jump cuts to maintain momentum.
• Frame for 9:16: speaker centered, room for captions.

4) Captions (25–35 min)
• Create burned-in captions with high contrast.
• Emphasize 2–4 key words (bold/color) where appropriate.
• Ensure safe margins for platform UI.

5) Hook text + b-roll cue (10–15 min)
• Add 1 hook line at top (e.g., “Most people get this wrong:”)
• Add 1 optional b-roll cue note in delivery message (e.g., “Overlay screenshot at 0:07”).

6) QC checklist (10 min)
• No typos in captions
• Audio levels consistent
• First 1–2 seconds are compelling
• Export settings correct: 1080x1920, H.264, 30fps (or match source), AAC audio
• Filename: ClientName_Topic_Clip1_9x16.mp4
