## 2) 20-minute discovery call script (designed to close DFY)

### Goal
Close a paid DFY pilot (fast revenue) AND capture SaaS requirements. (No SaaS build until $5k DFY collected.)

### Agenda (say this up front)
“Quick agenda: (1) your current clip workflow, (2) what ‘good’ looks like, (3) I’ll propose a 7–14 day pilot, (4) if it fits we’ll start this week.”

### Questions (10 minutes)
1) What’s the content source? (podcast video, Zoom, YouTube, Riverside, etc.)
2) How often do you publish long-form? How many clips/week do you want?
3) What’s your current editing process? Who does it and what does it cost?
4) What makes a clip perform for you? (hooks, cuts, captions style, b-roll, memes, etc.)
5) Any non-negotiables? (brand fonts/colors, profanity rules, topics to avoid)
6) Platforms: TikTok/Reels/Shorts—any differences in style?
7) Turnaround expectations? (24–48h vs weekly batch)
8) What would make this a clear win in 2 weeks? (views, saves, leads, consult calls)

### Diagnose + mirror (2 minutes)
Repeat back pain/goal: “So you’re publishing {{X}} long-form/week, want {{Y}} clips/week, but editing is {{slow/expensive/inconsistent}}. You want {{style}} and faster turnaround.”

### Offer the DFY pilot (5 minutes)
Option A (starter pilot): “7-day pilot: **10 clips** from 1–2 episodes. Includes: hook options, burned-in captions, 9:16 format, safe margins, and a delivery folder organized by platform. Turnaround: 48–72h.”
Option B (weekly): “Ongoing weekly: **12–20 clips/week** with a consistent caption template and a monthly style refresh.”

Pricing language (pick one):
- “Pilot is **$___** (one-time). If you like it, we roll into weekly.”
- “If you want to move fast, we can start with a **deposit today** and deliver first batch in 72h.”

### Close (3 minutes)
“Do you want to start with the pilot or go straight to weekly?”
If hesitation:
- “What would you need to see in the first batch to feel confident?”
- “Is the blocker budget, timing, or style fit?”

### Next steps checklist (send immediately after)
- 1–2 episode links/files
- brand notes (colors/fonts, examples you like)
- platform priority
- CTA preferences (follow, comment, book call)
