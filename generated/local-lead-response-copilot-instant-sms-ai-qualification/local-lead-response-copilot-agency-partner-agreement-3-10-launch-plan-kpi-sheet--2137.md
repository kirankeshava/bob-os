# 3×10 Accounts in 30 Days — Agency Partner Launch Plan

**Goal:** 30 active client accounts in 30 days by closing 3 cohorts of 10 accounts.  
**Core pitch:** “We contact leads in under 60 seconds via SMS, qualify them automatically, and route booked appointments—so you stop paying for leads you never reach.”  
**Proof link:** https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4  
**Support:** agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to

## Offer Structure (Simple)
- **7-day free trial** (Week 1 policy) + setup included.
- **Retail:** $499–$999/mo per location.
- **Close trigger:** “If we can’t reduce response time and improve contact rate, cancel anytime.”

## Week-by-Week Execution

### Week 1 (Days 1–7): Build Pipeline + Close Cohort #1 (10 accounts)
**Daily targets (Mon–Fri):**
- 30 outbound touches/day (email + LinkedIn + calls)
- 5 warm follow-ups/day
- 2 booked demos/day

**Who to target (fastest closes):**
- Home services with high intent: plumbing, HVAC, roofing, water damage, pest control, garage doors, electricians.
- Agencies already running lead gen / ads for these niches.

**Sales angle:** speed-to-lead. Show a simple math example:
- 100 leads/mo × $25 CPL = $2,500 spend.
- If slow response loses 30% of leads, that’s ~30 leads wasted.
- If 20% of those could book, that’s 6 jobs recovered.
- With $500 average profit/job = $3,000 recovered monthly.
- Service cost $499–$999 is justified.

**Delivery promise:** “Live in 48 hours once intake is complete.”

### Week 2 (Days 8–14): Deliver Wins + Close Cohort #2 (10 accounts)
- Pull Week 1 KPI snapshot for each client (response time + contact rate).
- Ask for 1 referral per client (“Know another contractor buying leads?”).
- Run a “missed lead audit”: ask prospects how long it takes to respond; demo the instant SMS flow.

### Week 3 (Days 15–21): Standardize + Close Cohort #3 (10 accounts)
- Productize onboarding: same intake checklist, same 5-question qualifier, same routing.
- Publish 1 simple case snippet (“median response time cut from X minutes to <1 minute”).
- Push volume tier: “Hit 10 accounts → wholesale drops to $349/account.”

### Week 4 (Days 22–30): Lock Retention + Expand Within Accounts
- Add second location upsells and after-hours coverage.
- Add “no-response rescue” path: second text + call prompt.
- Offer quarterly review: script tweaks based on KPI drop-offs.

## KPI Targets to Manage the Rollout
- **Median first response time:** < 60 seconds
- **Contact rate (two-way):** 35–60% depending on niche/lead quality
- **Qualified rate:** 20–50% depending on qualifiers
- **Booked rate (of qualified):** 25–60% depending on booking method

# Monthly KPI Reporting — Google Sheets Template (Tabs + Columns)

Create a Google Sheet with these tabs. Copy/paste the columns exactly.

## Tab 1: Executive Summary (Monthly)
Columns:
- Month
- Client Name
- Total Leads
- Median First Response Time (sec)
- Contact Rate (%)
- Qualified Rate (%)
- Booked Rate (%)
- Notes / Changes This Month

## Tab 2: Lead Status Breakdown (Monthly)
Columns:
- Month
- Client Name
- Lead Status (New / Contacted / Two-way Contact / Qualified / Not Qualified / Booked / No Response / Bad Number / Duplicate)
- Count
- % of Total

## Tab 3: Funnel Metrics (Monthly)
Columns:
- Month
- Client Name
- Leads Received
- Leads Contacted (attempted)
- Two-way Conversations
- Qualified
- Booked
- Showed (optional)
- Closed/Won (optional)

Formulas (example):
- Contact Rate = Two-way Conversations / Leads Received
- Qualified Rate = Qualified / Leads Received
- Booked Rate = Booked / Qualified

## Tab 4: Response Time (Lead-Level)
Columns:
- Lead ID
- Client Name
- Lead Source (FB / Web / Call / Other)
- Timestamp Lead Received
- Timestamp First SMS Sent
- Seconds to First SMS
- Timestamp First Lead Reply
- Seconds to First Reply (optional)
- Outcome (Qualified/Booked/etc.)

## Tab 5: Notes / Experiments Log
Columns:
- Date
- Client Name
- Change Made (copy, questions, routing, hours)
- Hypothesis
- KPI Impact (before/after)
