=== 6) PARTNER ONBOARDING CHECKLIST (reduce implementation friction) ===
Send this to the agency + client immediately after close.

A) Client contact + permissions
- Primary owner name + mobile number
- Best email for notifications
- Confirm consent language on forms/ads (SMS opt-in)

B) Lead sources (what to connect)
- Website form provider (Webflow/WP/Typeform/Jotform/etc.) + access details
- Facebook Lead Ads access (Business Manager) if applicable
- Any other sources (Google LSA, CallRail, Zapier, etc.)

C) Routing + calendar
- Calendar link (Calendly / Google Calendar / Acuity) and booking rules
- Business hours + after-hours behavior (auto-book vs. message + next-day follow-up)
- Who gets notified (SMS/email) for qualified leads

D) Qualification questions (2–4 max)
Pick from:
- “What service do you need?”
- “What zip code is the property in?”
- “How soon do you want this done?” (today/this week/this month)
- “Is this residential or commercial?”
- “What’s a rough budget range?” (optional)

E) Lead statuses (for reporting)
Define 4–6 statuses:
- New lead
- Contacted
- Qualified
- Not qualified
- Booked
- No response

F) Success metrics to report monthly
- Median first-response time (goal: < 60 seconds)
- Contact rate (% who reply)
- Qualified rate (% of total leads)
- Booked rate (% of total leads)
- Status breakdown counts

G) Go-live checklist
- Test lead submitted (confirm SMS fires)
- Confirm qualification flow and handoff
- Confirm booking works
- Confirm notifications + tracking
