## 6) “First 3 Paid Pilots” Scoping + Scheduling Checklist
Use this to qualify quickly and move to payment + onboarding.

**Pilot is a fit if:**
- They generate at least ~10 leads/week from forms, calls, or FB lead ads (or they have a high ticket where 1 saved lead matters).
- They can accept booked calls/appointments OR have a dispatcher.
- They agree to SMS compliance (only text leads who opted in or requested service).

**On the demo call, confirm:**
1) Lead sources: website form (which platform), FB Lead Ads, Google LSA, etc.
2) Current response time + who responds.
3) Booking destination: Calendly / Housecall Pro / Jobber / ServiceTitan / Google Calendar / dispatcher.
4) Hours + after-hours behavior (book later vs “emergency” routing).
5) Service area rules (zip codes/cities) + out-of-area message.
6) 3–5 qualification questions (use niche packs) + disqualifiers.
7) Internal notifications: who gets notified, by SMS/email, and escalation rules.
8) Success metric: baseline contact rate + target lift.

**Close + next steps (end of call):**
- “If we can launch this in <24 hours and you get faster contact + more booked jobs, are you ready to start the pilot today?”
- Confirm pricing: $499 setup + $499/mo (or $0 setup annual).
- Send: payment request + intake form + schedule 30-min onboarding.

**After payment, collect within 2 hours:**
- Intake form completed
- Booking link access or calendar permissions
- Test lead submitted + confirm texts received

**Go-live definition (<24h):**
- Lead source connected (webhook/FB)
- SMS flow live (greeting, qualification, booking/routing)
- Notifications working
- 1–2 test leads pass end-to-end
