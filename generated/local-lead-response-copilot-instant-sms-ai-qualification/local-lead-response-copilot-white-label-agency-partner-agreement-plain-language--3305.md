1) WHAT WE PROVIDE (THE SERVICE)
Provider supplies a white‑labelable “Lead Response Copilot” system that (a) instantly responds to new leads by SMS, (b) asks short qualification questions, (c) routes qualified leads to booking/calls, and (d) tracks outcomes for reporting. The Service is intended for local, high‑intent lead sources (forms, landing pages, FB/Google lead forms, etc.).

2) WHO OWNS THE CLIENT RELATIONSHIP
Agency owns the client relationship and billing relationship with its end clients (“Client Accounts”). Provider supplies the Service to Agency for each Client Account at wholesale pricing.

3) PRICING (WHOLESALE) + VOLUME DISCOUNTS
Agency pays Provider wholesale per Client Account, per month (or per billing period). Suggested wholesale ranges:
- Standard: $399/account/month
- Starter tier option (limited): $299/account/month
- Pro tier option (priority + advanced routing): $499/account/month

Volume discounts (applied to ACTIVE accounts, same billing date):
- 1–3 accounts: base wholesale price
- 4–9 accounts: 10% off wholesale
- 10–19 accounts: 15% off wholesale
- 20+ accounts: 20% off wholesale

Minimum commitment (for priority support and discount eligibility):
- Agency agrees to maintain a minimum of 10 active Client Accounts for 90 days (“Minimum Commitment”) to access priority support and volume pricing.
If the minimum is not met, pricing reverts to base wholesale and priority support may be removed until the minimum is restored.

4) AGENCY RETAIL PRICING (YOUR MARGIN)
Agency is free to set retail pricing. Recommended retail range to end clients:
- $499–$999/account/month
This creates typical margin of ~$200–$500/account/month depending on tier and volume.

Included:

- Basic monthly performance reporting: median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate, and lead status breakdown
- Ongoing maintenance for normal usage

Not included unless agreed in writing:
