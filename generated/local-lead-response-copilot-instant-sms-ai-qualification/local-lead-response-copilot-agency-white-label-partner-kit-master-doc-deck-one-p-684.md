C) AGENCY IMPLEMENTATION SOP (DONE‑WITH‑YOU)
========================
Goal: Launch each client in 48–72 hours after intake.

1) SALES HANDOFF (FROM AGENCY TO IMPLEMENTATION)
Collect:
- Business name, primary contact, phone, email
- Service area (cities/ZIPs), business hours, after-hours policy
- Job types served + exclusions (e.g., no warranty work)
- Ideal customer + minimum job size (if any)
- Booking preference: calendar booking link OR “call us” handoff
- Team routing: who gets notified + escalation rules
- Lead sources: website form, FB lead ads, landing pages, CRM

2) SCRIPT + QUALIFICATION DESIGN (30–45 MIN)
Create the conversation flow:
- First message (sent instantly):
  “Hi {{first_name}} — thanks for reaching out to {{business}}. Quick question so we can help fast: what service do you need?”
- Qualification questions (2–4):
  a) Service needed (picklist + free text)
  b) Location/ZIP (confirm in service area)
  c) Urgency (today/this week/just researching)
  d) Optional: budget range or job size threshold
  e) Optional: preferred appointment windows
- Pass/fail rules:
  - Out of service area → mark Unqualified + polite close
  - Not offered service → Unqualified
  - Qualified → proceed to booking/handoff

3) TECH INTEGRATION CHECKLIST
- Confirm lead source capture method (webhook/CRM/form integration)
- Map fields: name, phone, email, service, message, location
- Configure:
  - Immediate SMS trigger
  - Retry logic (e.g., 2 nudges if no response)
  - Staff notifications (SMS/email)
  - Booking link insertion (if applicable)
  - Status tagging (New/Contacted/Qualified/Booked/Unqualified/Lost)

4) QA TEST (20–30 MIN)
Run test leads:
- In-area qualified lead → confirm booking/handoff + correct status
- Out-of-area lead → confirm graceful close + Unqualified
- No response → confirm nudges + eventual “we’ll try again” message
- After-hours lead → confirm after-hours script and next-day follow-up

5) LAUNCH
- Turn on live triggers
- Notify agency + client
- Set expectations: first 7 days are tuning; weekly review of transcripts + outcomes

6) OPTIMIZATION (WEEK 1 AND MONTHLY)
- Review transcripts for friction points
- Update qualification wording
- Tighten booking prompts
- Add FAQs / objections handling
- Ensure response time stays <60 seconds median

- The top 3–5 services you want to book
