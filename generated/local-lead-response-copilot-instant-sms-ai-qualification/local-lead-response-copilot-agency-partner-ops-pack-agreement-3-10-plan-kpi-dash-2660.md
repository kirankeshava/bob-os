## 2) 30‑Day “3×10 Accounts” Agency Plan (to hit revenue fast)

### Goal
Enroll **3 agency partners** who each activate **10 client accounts** within 30–45 days.

### Wholesale vs Retail (the math)
Recommended agency pricing to clients: **$499–$999/account/month**
- If Partner sells at **$799** and pays LLRC **$399** (Tier B), Partner gross margin ≈ **$400/account/month**.
- With **10 accounts**, Partner gross margin ≈ **$4,000/month** per agency (before its costs).

LLRC revenue target from partners:
- 30 accounts at $349–$399 wholesale ⇒ **~$10.5k–$12k MRR** from 3 partners (base). Scale by adding partners and pushing Tier C/D.

### Target agency types (fastest closes)
1) Home services lead-gen agencies (plumbing/HVAC/roofing)
2) Local PPC agencies running Google LSAs / search
3) FB ads agencies for high-intent lead forms
4) Appointment-based local businesses (med spa, dental implants, legal intake)

### Offer positioning partners can repeat
- “**Instant speed-to-lead**: respond in <60 seconds, not 30 minutes.”
- “**Qualify + book automatically** so your client stops missing calls.”
- “**Reporting included**: contact/qualified/booked rates and median response time.”

### Weekly execution plan (Partner + LLRC)

**Week 1 (Partner recruitment + first pilot client):**
- Partner list building: 50 agencies/day (LinkedIn + local FB groups + Google “{city} PPC agency”).
- Outreach volume: 30/day cold email + 20/day LinkedIn DMs.
- Target: 10 discovery calls booked, 3 partner trials started.
- Close lever: “Bring one client, we launch in 48 hours, you keep the margin.”

**Week 2 (Activate the first 3–5 accounts):**
- Each partner chooses 3 existing clients with high lead volume.
- Implement in 48 hours using SOP.
- Collect early KPI wins: response time < 60 sec; contact rate improvement.
- Case study snippet: “Before/After response time + booked calls.”

**Week 3 (Scale to 10 accounts per partner):**
- Use the case study + ROI math to upsell the rest of their base.
- Partner runs a client webinar: “Stop missing leads: instant SMS + booking.”
- Target: 5 additional accounts activated.

**Week 4 (Retention + referral flywheel):**
- Monthly KPI report sent to every client.
- Optimize qualification questions.
- Ask for referrals: 1 referral per client, with 30-day discount (Partner-controlled).

### KPIs for the 30-day sprint
- Partner pipeline: contacted, replied, calls booked, partners onboarded
- Activation: accounts live, time-to-live, first lead processed
- Performance: response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate

## 3) Monthly KPI Dashboard Template (Google Sheets Structure)

Create a Google Sheet with these tabs.

### Tab A — SETTINGS
Columns:
- Client Name
- Timezone
- Booking Link
- Lead Sources (FB form / website form / CRM)
- Reporting Period Start
- Reporting Period End

### Tab B — LEADS (raw log import)
Columns (one row per lead):
- Lead ID
- Lead Date/Time (timestamp)
- Source
- Name
- Phone
- Email
- First Response Time (timestamp)
- **Response Time (seconds)** = `(First Response Time - Lead Date/Time) * 86400`
- Status (No Response / Contacted / Qualified / Not Qualified / Booked / Lost)
- Qualified? (Y/N)
- Booked? (Y/N)
- Notes

### Tab C — KPI SUMMARY (monthly)
Metrics (with example formulas):
- Total leads = `COUNTA(LEADS!A:A)-1`
- **Median response time (sec)** = `MEDIAN(LEADS!I:I)`
- **Median response time (min)** = `MEDIAN(LEADS!I:I)/60`
- **Contact rate** = `COUNTIF(LEADS!J:J,"Contacted") / Total leads`
  - (Or define “Contacted” as any lead that replied; keep consistent.)
- **Qualified rate** = `COUNTIF(LEADS!K:K,"Y") / Total leads`
- **Booked rate** = `COUNTIF(LEADS!L:L,"Y") / Total leads`

Add a simple scorecard:
- Green: median < 60 sec
- Yellow: 60–180 sec
- Red: > 180 sec

### Tab D — STATUS BREAKDOWN
Pivot-style table:
- Rows: Status
- Values: Count of Lead ID
- Optional: % of total

### Metric Definitions (include at top of KPI SUMMARY)
- **Response time:** time from lead submission to first outbound SMS.
- **Contacted:** lead responded at least once or answered a question.
- **Qualified:** lead meets criteria (service area, job type, budget/urgency).
- **Booked:** appointment/call scheduled.
