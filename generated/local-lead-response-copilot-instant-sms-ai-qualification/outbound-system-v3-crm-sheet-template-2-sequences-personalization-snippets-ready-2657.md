1) GOOGLE SHEETS CRM / TRACKING TEMPLATE
Create a Google Sheet with 3 tabs: (A) Leads, (B) Touches, (C) Replies & Demos.

(A) TAB: Leads (one row per company)
Columns:
- Lead_ID (e.g., A001)
- Segment (Agency | Operator)
- Niche (HVAC/Plumbing/Roofing/Pest/Water Damage/Med Spa | Agency-FB Lead Gen)
- Company_Name
- Website_URL
- Source_URL (Clutch/UpCity/Google Maps/LinkedIn/Upwork)
- City
- State/Region
- Decision_Maker_Name
- Decision_Maker_Title (Owner, Founder, Growth, Ops)
- Email
- Phone (optional)
- LinkedIn_Profile_URL
- Contact_Path (Direct email | Contact form | LinkedIn only)
- Signal (e.g., “FB lead ads”, “Book now”, “24/7”, “after-hours”, “multi-location”)
- Personalization_Line (1 sentence)
- Status (Not Started | Sent-1 | Sent-2 | Replied | Demo Booked | Closed/Won | Closed/Lost | Do Not Contact)
- Next_Action_Date
- Last_Touch_Date
- Notes

(B) TAB: Touches (one row per touch)
Columns:
- Touch_ID
- Date
- Lead_ID
- Channel (Email | LinkedIn | SMS)
- Step (1 | 2)
- Subject/Template_Name
- Message_Sent (paste the exact message)
- Outcome (No response | Bounce | Reply-positive | Reply-neutral | Reply-negative | Unsubscribe)
- Next_Step

(C) TAB: Replies & Demos
Columns:
- Lead_ID
- Reply_Date
- Reply_Category (Interested | Not now | Price | Already have system | Wrong person)
- Key Objection / Quote
- Demo_Date_Time
- Demo_Result (No-show | Qualified | Disqualified | Pilot Started)
- Pilot Start Date
- Pilot Outcome
