## A) Enrichment SOP (to make targets send-ready)
Goal: For each prospect, produce:
1) **Decision-maker name** (Owner/Founder/Head of Growth/Marketing Director)
2) **Direct email** (preferred) OR verified contact path (contact form + LinkedIn)
3) **Verification confidence** (High/Med/Low)
4) **1-line personalization note** (specific, non-creepy)

### Steps per target (3–6 minutes each)
1. **Open source page** (Clutch/UpCity/Google Maps/Upwork/LinkedIn company page).
2. **Find decision-maker**
   - Agency: Owner/Founder, Head of Paid, Head of Growth.
   - Operator: Owner/GM/Marketing Manager/Office Manager.
3. **Find email**
   - Check website header/footer/contact page.
   - If none: look for “team” / “about” page.
   - If still none: use contact form + LinkedIn profile URL.
4. **Create personalization line (1 sentence)**
   Use something they explicitly advertise:
   - “Saw you run FB Lead Ads for HVAC/roofing…”
   - “Noticed you offer ‘24/7 emergency’ water damage response…”
   - “Saw you mention ‘same-day estimates’…”
5. **Log in CRM columns**
   - First name, last name, company, niche, city/state
   - Email OR contact form URL
   - LinkedIn URL (person)
   - Personalization line
   - Confidence score (High if direct email + name; Med if named person + form; Low if generic inbox only)

### Confidence scoring
- **High:** named person + direct email
- **Med:** named person + contact form/LinkedIn
- **Low:** only generic email (info@) or only company page
