C) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY + CLIENT OPS)
========================
SOP GOAL: Launch each client account in < 1 hour and prove lift within 7–14 days.

1) PRE-LAUNCH CHECKLIST (AGENCY)
Collect from client:
- Business name + service category
- Service area (cities/ZIPs)
- Business hours + after-hours instructions
- Primary phone number for escalations
- Booking method:
  a) Calendar link (preferred) OR
  b) “Call now” routing to office
- Qualification criteria (3–6 questions):
  Examples:
  - “What service do you need?”
  - “What’s your ZIP code?”
  - “Is this urgent or can it wait a few days?”
  - “Do you own the property?”
  - “What’s your estimated budget range?” (optional)
- Disqualifiers:
  - Out of service area
  - Not offered services
  - Price shoppers below minimum
- Brand voice:
  - Friendly + direct + local
  - Spanish (yes/no)

2) MESSAGE FLOW (DEFAULT)
Message 1 (instant):
“Hi {{first_name}} — this is {{business_name}}. Thanks for reaching out. What service do you need help with?”

Message 2 (qualify):
“Got it. What ZIP code is the job in?”

Message 3 (timing):
“When are you looking to get this done? (Today / This week / Just pricing)”

Message 4 (booking):
“Perfect — want to grab a time now? Here’s the booking link: {{booking_link}}”
OR
“Perfect — can you take a quick call now? Reply YES and we’ll call you.”

No-response follow-ups (example cadence):
- +10 min: “Just checking in — still need help with {{service}}?”
- +2 hrs: “We can usually get you scheduled quickly. What ZIP is the job in?”
- Next day: “Last check — would you like to book a time or should we close this out?”

3) QA TEST (BEFORE GO-LIVE)
- Submit a test lead
- Confirm:
  - Response time under 60 seconds
  - Questions are correct order
  - Booking link works
  - Lead status is tagged correctly

4) GO-LIVE
- Turn on for the client’s lead sources
- Notify client team: “Leads will be pre-qualified; check booked/qualified queue twice daily.”

5) WEEK-1 OPTIMIZATION
- Review first 20–50 leads
- Adjust:
  - Question wording (reduce friction)
  - Disqualifiers (avoid false negatives)
  - Follow-up cadence if too aggressive/too light

6) MONTHLY REPORTING (AGENCY DELIVERABLE)
- Send KPI report with:
  - Response time
  - Contact/qualified/booked rates
  - Status breakdown
  - 2–3 insights + next month tweaks
