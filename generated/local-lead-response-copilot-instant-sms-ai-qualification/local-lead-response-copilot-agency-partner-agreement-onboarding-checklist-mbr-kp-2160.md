## Agency Onboarding Checklist + 7-Day Launch Plan (Run This Without Founder Help)
Use this per **Client Account**.

### A) Intake Checklist (Day 0)
**Client basics**
- Business name, niche, service area(s)
- Business hours + after-hours handling preference
- Primary contact(s) + escalation contact

**Lead sources (choose all)**
- Website forms (URL + form tool)
- Facebook/IG Lead Ads
- Google LSA / PPC landing page
- Other (CRM lead stage, call tracking tool)

**Routing destination**
- Booking link (Calendly/GoHighLevel/Housecall Pro/Jobber, etc.)
- Or “call now” routing (phone number + rules)
- Or “manual follow-up” (CRM task + internal notification)

**Qualification rules**
- Top 3–5 qualifying questions (keep short)
- Disqualifiers (e.g., outside service area, wrong job type)
- “Qualified” definition (what must be true to book)

**Brand voice**
- Friendly vs. direct tone
- Must-use phrases, prohibited phrases

**Compliance**
- Confirm lead consent language exists on forms/ads
- Confirm opt-out keywords handling (STOP)

### B) Build Checklist (Days 1–2)
- Connect lead source → response trigger
- Build message flow: greeting → 2–4 questions → route/CTA
- Configure hours logic: instant response vs. next-business-day message
- Configure lead tagging/statuses:
  - New
  - Contacted
  - Engaged
  - Qualified
  - Booked
  - Unqualified
  - No response
- Configure notifications to Agency/Client (email/slack/CRM task)

### C) QA Checklist (Day 3)
Run **10 test leads** end-to-end.
- Test within business hours
- Test outside business hours
- Test invalid service area
- Test “price shopper” response
- Confirm booking link works
- Confirm lead status updates correctly

### D) Go-Live Checklist (Day 4)
- Turn on for live leads
- Monitor first **20** leads closely
- Make 1–2 message tweaks max (avoid constant changes)

### E) Optimize (Days 5–7)
- Review KPIs:
  - Median response time
  - Contact rate
  - Qualified rate
  - Booked rate
- Tighten qualifiers if too many low-quality bookings
- Loosen qualifiers if contact rate is high but booked is low
- Add 1 reminder follow-up message if needed (e.g., 15–30 min later)

### Client-Facing “What You’ll Notice” Script (Agency can paste)
“Once this is live, every new lead gets an instant text, answers a couple quick questions, and either books immediately or gets flagged for follow-up. You’ll see leads organized by status (New/Engaged/Qualified/Booked/etc.). We’ll review performance monthly and tune the questions to increase booked jobs.”
