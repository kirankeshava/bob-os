3) PRIORITIZATION RUBRIC (FIRST 50)
Score each lead 0–2 per factor; prioritize scores 7–10 first.
- Fit (home services / local high-intent OR agency serving them)
- Speed-to-lead pain signal (mentions FB lead ads, “request a quote”, after-hours, many reviews but slow intake)
- Clear decision-maker path (owner listed, LinkedIn found, direct email pattern)
- Proof of spend/volume (agency: case studies; operator: multiple locations, active ads cues, strong GMB)
- Simple CTA match (books calls / estimates / appointments)

4) PERSONALIZATION WORKFLOW (60 SECONDS)

- Recent case study line (agency) OR service + city (operator)
- Lead capture mechanism observed (FB lead ads, “Get quote” form, chat widget)
- One operational pain trigger (after-hours, weekend, missed calls)
Then write: “Noticed X — quick question about Y?”
