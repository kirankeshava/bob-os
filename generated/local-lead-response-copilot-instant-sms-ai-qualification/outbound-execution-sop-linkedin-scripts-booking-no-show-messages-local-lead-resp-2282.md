A) 7-Day Outbound SOP (Email + LinkedIn)
Goal: 200 total outbound emails + 200 LinkedIn touches (connection or message), 20 replies, 10 demos.

Day 0 (today): Deliverability + Warmup
1) Pick 10–20 “friendly-ish” targets (smaller agencies/operators with public emails).
2) Send 1:1 emails (no links in 50% of them). Keep under 120 words. Ask a simple question.
3) Reply to any replies within 10 minutes.

Day 1–4: Ramp
- Day 1: 50 emails total (30 agencies / 20 operators). Manual personalization line required.
- Day 2: 50 emails (new prospects). Send follow-up #1 to Day-1 non-responders.
- Day 3: 50 emails (new). Follow-up #1 to Day-2 non-responders.
- Day 4: 50 emails (new). Follow-up #2 (final) to Day-1 non-responders.

Day 5–7: Close + Book
- Prioritize replying fast, booking demos, and moving “interested” to a specific time.
- Recycle positive responders into referrals (“anyone else running FB lead gen for home services?”).

- Agency: “Saw you run FB lead gen for {vertical} and mention {offer} on your site/Clutch.”
