• Routes outcomes (qualified, unqualified, no response)
• Supports booking a call/appointment (by routing to booking link and/or notifying the team)

AGENCY PARTNER ONBOARDING CHECKLIST (COPY/PASTE)
Goal: launch a client account in 48 hours with zero founder involvement.

A) Client Qualification (before you sell)
□ Client receives 15+ inbound leads/month OR high ticket value (e.g., $1,500+ jobs)
□ Client answers calls/texts within business hours
□ Client has a clear service area + top 3 services
□ Client agrees to fast follow-up + appointment setting

B) Required Intake (collect from client)
1) Business basics
□ Business name
□ Website URL
□ Business hours
□ Service area (cities/zip codes)
□ Primary services (top 3)
□ Offer statement (what happens after booking?)

2) Lead sources (choose)
□ Web form (URL + form tool)
□ Facebook Lead Ads (page + access method)
□ Google LSA / GBP form integration (describe)
□ Other webhook source

3) Contact + routing
□ Primary contact name + role
□ Phone number(s) that should receive notifications
□ Email(s) for notifications
□ Booking link (Calendly/Housecall Pro/ServiceTitan/etc.) OR preferred booking method

4) SMS compliance
□ Confirm client has opt-in language on forms/ads (yes/no)
□ Provide opt-in text snippet used (paste)
□ Confirm STOP/HELP handling expectations

5) Qualification logic
□ 3–5 qualifying questions to ask leads (choose from templates)
□ Disqualifiers (e.g., “not in service area”, “job too small”, etc.)
□ “Qualified lead” definition (what must be true?)

6) Brand voice
□ Tone: friendly / professional / direct
□ Use emojis? (yes/no)
□ Sign-off name (e.g., “—Mike at ABC Plumbing”)

C) Copilot Setup Steps (agency internal)
□ Create new client account
□ Connect lead source (form/FB/webhook)
□ Configure immediate first text (speed-to-lead)
□ Configure qualification flow (Q1–Q5) + branching
□ Configure booking handoff (booking link + reminders)
□ Configure internal notifications (qualified/booked/no response)
□ QA test with 3 sample leads (qualified, unqualified, no response)
□ Launch

D) Launch Day Checklist
□ Confirm client can answer/respond
□ Confirm appointment calendar works
□ Confirm notifications received
□ Confirm lead status labeling works

E) Weekly Optimization Checklist (first 30 days)
□ Review KPIs: response time, contact, qualified, booked
□ Identify drop-off question(s)
□ Update script to reduce friction
□ Confirm client follow-up cadence is being executed
