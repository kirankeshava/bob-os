## 1) 30-Minute Paid Pilot Onboarding Call Script (Run-of-Show)
**Goal (state up front):** “In 30 minutes we’ll capture the exact inputs needed to go live in <24 hours: lead source hookup, your business hours/service area, 3–5 qualifying questions, booking path, and who gets notified. At the end, we’ll confirm launch time and success criteria.”

### Minute 0–3: Context + Outcome
1. “What service do you most want booked from inbound leads right now?”
2. “What happens today when a lead comes in (response time, who answers, what’s the usual delay)?”
3. Confirm target lead type: emergency vs estimate vs routine.

### Minute 3–10: Lead Source + Routing
4. “Where do leads come from? (Website form, Google LSA, FB Lead Ads, Google Ads landing page, etc.)”
5. “Do you have access to the form builder/CRM? If yes, which: Webflow/Wix/WordPress/Elementor/HubSpot/GoHighLevel/ServiceTitan/Jobber/Housecall Pro?”
6. “Where should qualified leads be routed?” Options:
   - Book directly on calendar
   - Transfer to office via notification + call
   - Create CRM opportunity
7. **Recipients:** “Who gets notified (names + phone numbers + emails)? Any backup if no response?”

### Minute 10–18: Business Rules
8. **Business hours:** “What days/hours do you answer the phone? Any lunch/after-hours policy?”
9. **Service area:** “Zip codes/cities you serve, and any areas you exclude?”
10. **Services list:** “List top 3–5 services you want to prioritize.”
11. **After-hours behavior:** “If lead comes in after hours, should we (A) collect info + set expectation for morning follow-up, or (B) offer next-day booking slots?”

### Minute 18–25: Qualification Questions + Booking Path
12. “We’ll ask 3–5 short questions max. What are the deal-breakers?”
   - Location in service area
   - Service type
   - Urgency/timeframe
   - Property type (residential/commercial)
   - Budget/approval authority (optional)
13. Confirm booking link:
   - “Do you have Calendly / Acuity / GoHighLevel calendar / ServiceTitan booking?”
   - If not: “Do you want calls booked or just ‘ready-to-call’ leads?”

### Minute 25–28: Compliance + Consent
14. “Does your form/FB lead ad already include SMS consent language? If not, we’ll provide a compliant line.”
15. “Do you want opt-out language in every thread? (Recommended: ‘Reply STOP to opt out.’)”

### Minute 28–30: Close + Launch Plan
16. Confirm deliverables: “Today we captured everything needed. Next: you’ll receive the intake form to confirm details in writing. We’ll connect the lead source, test end-to-end, then go live.”
17. **Define success metrics for week 1:** speed-to-lead, contact rate, booked calls.
18. **Payment + scheduling:** “To start DFY setup, we take payment and schedule a 15-minute ‘go-live test’ tomorrow. We charge $499 setup + $499/mo, or $0 setup if you prefer annual.”

### Section F — Routing + Notifications
- Who gets SMS notifications (name + phone)
- Who gets email notifications (email)
- Backup recipient rules
- If office doesn’t respond within X minutes, what should happen?

### Section G — Compliance
- Confirm you have consent to text new leads (checkbox)
- Paste your existing consent language OR request our suggested language
- Acknowledge opt-out handling (STOP)

## 3) DFY Setup Checklist (Internal, <24 Hours)
**Target SLA:** Go-live within 24 hours of payment + completed intake.

1. Confirm payment received + pilot start date.
2. Validate lead source hookup path:
   - Website form → webhook/Zapier/Make/GHL/CRM
   - FB Lead Ads → native integration/Zapier/Make
3. Create customer configuration record:
   - Hours, service area, services
   - Qualification questions + allowed answers
   - Booking link + fallback CTA
4. Configure SMS flow:
   - First message within 10–30 seconds
   - 3–5 question sequence
   - Handoff behavior (booked vs qualified-not-booked vs unqualified)
5. Configure routing rules:
   - Who gets notified
   - Escalation if no response
6. Compliance:
   - Include opt-out language
   - Verify consent language exists on form/ad
7. Test end-to-end:
   - Submit test lead
   - Confirm instant SMS received
   - Run full qualification
   - Confirm booking/notifications fired
8. Go-live confirmation:
   - Send customer ‘Live’ email + what to watch
9. Post-launch monitoring (first 24–72h):
   - Check message delivery
   - Review qualification answers
   - Adjust questions to improve booking
