1) GOOGLE SHEETS CRM / TRACKING (create 4 tabs)

TAB A — Leads (one row per prospect)
Columns:
- Lead_ID (e.g., A001)
- Segment (Agency | Operator)
- Company
- Niche (HVAC/Plumbing/Roofing/Pest/WaterDamage/MedSpa/Agency)
- City
- State
- Website_URL
- Source_URL (Clutch/UpCity/LinkedIn/Maps/Upwork/FB group link)
- Decision_Maker_Name
- Decision_Maker_Role (Owner/Founder/Head of Growth/Marketing Manager)
- Email
- LinkedIn_URL (person)
- Company_LinkedIn_URL
- Phone (only if public + opted-in context)
- Contact_Path (Email | Contact form | LinkedIn DM)
- Personalization_Line (1 sentence)
- Status (Not Sent | Sent #1 | Sent #2 | Replied | Demo Booked | Not a Fit | No Response)
- Last_Touch_Date
- Next_Touch_Date
- Notes

TAB B — Touches (one row per touch)
Columns:
- Lead_ID
- Date
- Channel (Email | LinkedIn | SMS)
- Step (1/2)
- Template (Agency S1 / Operator S1 / etc.)
- Subject
- Outcome (Sent/Delivered/Bounced/Replied/Booked)
- Notes

TAB C — Replies
Columns:
- Lead_ID
- Date
- Reply_Type (Interested | Not now | Not a fit | Referral | Objection)
- Key Details
- Next Step

TAB D — Demos
Columns:
- Lead_ID
- Company
- Demo_Date
- Outcome (Show/No-show)
- Next Action

Daily KPI fields (top of any tab):
- Emails sent today
- Replies today
- Reply rate (7-day rolling)
- Demos booked (7-day rolling)
