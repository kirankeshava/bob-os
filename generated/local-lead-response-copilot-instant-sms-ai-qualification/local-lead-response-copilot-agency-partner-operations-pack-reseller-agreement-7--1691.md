## 2) 7‑Day Agency Partner Onboarding Checklist (Per New Client Account)
**Goal:** go live in ≤7 days with a measurable speed-to-lead improvement.

### Day 0 (Intake received)
**Partner submits:**
- Client business name + service category (e.g., HVAC, roofing, plumbing)
- Service area (cities/ZIPs)
- Business hours + after-hours rules
- Lead sources: website form(s), FB Lead Ads, call tracking, etc.
- Booking destination: calendar link / scheduling tool / “call now” routing
- Qualification outcomes: (A) Booked, (B) Qualified-not-booked, (C) Unqualified, (D) No response
- Disqualifiers (example): out of area, wrong job type, insurance not accepted

**Required copy assets:**
- Brand name to use in SMS
- “Offer hook” (e.g., “Same-day estimates”)
- 1–2 trust lines (licensed/insured, years in business, financing)

### Day 1–2 (Build)
Provider builds:
- SMS opener (instant)
- AI qualification questions (3–5 max)
- Routing logic (book, request address, request photos, or mark unqualified)
- Lead status tags for reporting

### Day 3 (Partner review)
Partner validates:
- Tone (friendly + direct)
- Qualification criteria
- Business hours behavior
- Disqualifier handling

### Day 4 (QA)
Go-live checklist:
- Test lead submitted from each source
- Verify first SMS sends within target window
- Verify question flow completion
- Verify booking/routing works
- Verify lead status tagging works

### Day 5 (Launch)
- Turn on live routing
- Set expectations with client: first 7 days are baseline + tuning

### Day 6–7 (Tune)
- Review early KPI snapshot: response time + contact/qualified/booked
- Adjust question order to reduce drop-off
- Tighten disqualifiers (reduce wasted human follow-up)

**Go-live definition:** at least 10 live leads processed with correct status tagging and a working booked/hand-off path.

## 4) Monthly KPI Reporting Template (Paste into Google Sheets)
Create a Google Sheet with **3 tabs**:

### Tab A: Executive Summary (per client)
Columns:
- Month
- Client Name
- Leads Received
- **Median Response Time (sec)**
- **Contact Rate** = Contacted / Leads
- **Qualified Rate** = Qualified / Contacted
- **Booked Rate** = Booked / Qualified
- Booked Appointments (#)
- Notes / Key Changes Made

Definitions:
- **Contacted:** lead replied OR engaged in at least 1 qualification step
- **Qualified:** met criteria (service + area + intent)
- **Booked:** appointment scheduled OR call connected (define per client)

### Tab B: Lead Status Breakdown
Rows = statuses, columns = counts:
- New Lead
- Contacted
- No Response
- Qualified
- Unqualified (Out of Area)
- Unqualified (Wrong Service)
- Unqualified (Low Intent)
- Booked
- Lost / Chose Competitor

### Tab C: Response Time Log (optional)
Columns:
- Lead ID
- Timestamp Lead Received
- Timestamp First SMS Sent
- **Response Time (sec)** = First SMS - Lead Received
- Source (Form/FB/etc.)

**Monthly email summary template (agency → client):**
Subject: [Month] Speed-to-Lead + Booking Report — [Client]
Body: 
- Median first response time: [X] seconds (goal: under 60 sec)
- Contact rate: [X]%
- Qualified rate: [X]%
- Booked rate: [X]%
- Total booked: [#]
Top insight: [one sentence]
Next month improvements: [2 bullets]
