## 4) Pricing & billing (Partner → Client)
Partner sets its own retail pricing. Recommended retail: **$499–$999 per account/month**.

## 5) What’s included in the Service
- Instant SMS response to new leads (from form/CRM/webhook/lead source)

- Routing outcomes: book call/appointment, connect to sales rep, or tag/disposition lead
- Monthly KPI reporting metrics (see template)

## 6) What’s not included (unless separately agreed)
- Ad management, lead generation, landing page build, or creative
- CRM purchase/fees, phone numbers, SMS usage fees (if applicable), or third-party tools
- Sales team training beyond the provided scripts/SOP

**Partner provides (per Client Account):**
- Business name, service area(s), services offered, hours
- Booking link or desired booking method
- Lead sources (forms/FB leads/CRM) and how leads arrive (webhook/Zapier/etc.)
- Compliance confirmation: permission to text leads and required disclosures
- At least 10–20 sample leads (or test submissions) for QA

**We provide:**
- Configuration of qualification flow and routing rules
- Test + QA checklist and launch support
- Baseline KPI report structure

- Standard support: email support via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Target first response: **1 business day** (Priority Support: same-day target)
- Incident handling: if message delivery or routing fails, we prioritize restore and provide a post-incident summary.

## 9) Data handling & confidentiality
- Partner and Client Accounts are responsible for providing leads in compliance with applicable laws.
- We treat lead data as confidential and use it only to operate the Service.
- Partner agrees to keep any credentials, webhooks, and configuration details confidential.

## 10) Compliance (TCPA / consent)
Partner warrants that its Client Accounts only text leads that have given appropriate consent (e.g., form checkbox language, existing business relationship where applicable). Partner is responsible for Client compliance language and disclosures.

## 11) Results disclaimer

## 12) Cancellation
