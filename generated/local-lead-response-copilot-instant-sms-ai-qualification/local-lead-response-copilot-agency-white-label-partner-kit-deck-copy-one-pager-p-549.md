D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY-READY)
==============================

Goal: go live in 48–72 hours after intake with measurable speed-to-lead improvements.

SOP OVERVIEW
Phase 1 — Intake (Day 0)
1) Collect client details (agency gathers):
   • Business name, location(s), service area zips
   • Services offered + “not offered” exclusions
   • Business hours + after-hours rules
   • Booking preference: call booking vs appointment booking
   • Calendar link or scheduling rules (if booking)
   • Lead sources: website form, FB lead ads, landing pages, call tracking forms
   • CRM access (optional) or notification recipients
   • Compliance note: confirm SMS consent language on forms
2) Agency sends intake to: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to

Phase 2 — Build (Day 1)
3) Configure lead capture → SMS trigger:
   • Connect form/lead source to instant SMS workflow
4) Configure AI qualification script (3–6 questions):
   • Q1: What service do you need?
   • Q2: What’s your ZIP code?
   • Q3: When do you need this? (today/this week/this month)
   • Q4: Property type (residential/commercial)
   • Q5: Job size / quick details
   • Optional: budget range (only if appropriate)
5) Routing rules:
   • If qualified + within hours → booking link or call scheduling
   • If qualified + after hours → set expectation + schedule next available
   • If not qualified (out of area / wrong service) → polite decline + referral (optional)

Phase 3 — QA (Day 2)
6) Run test leads (agency + client):
   • Confirm SMS arrives under 60 seconds
   • Confirm qualification path works
   • Confirm booking handoff works
   • Confirm notifications/logging work
7) Approve scripts:
   • Tone: short, friendly, direct
   • Ensure opt-out language present where required: “Reply STOP to opt out.”

Phase 4 — Launch (Day 3)
8) Turn on for all lead sources.
9) Train dispatcher/sales:
   • How to handle “hot lead” notifications
   • Follow-up SLAs

Phase 5 — Optimize (Week 2+)
10) Weekly review (first month):
   • Identify drop-off question(s)
   • Shorten script if contact rate low
   • Adjust booking/handoff if booked rate low
11) Monthly KPI report to agency (template below).

CLIENT REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST (SEND THIS TO CLIENT)
• Confirm lead sources + where SMS consent appears
• Provide business hours + after-hours handling rules
• Provide booking link OR confirm “call now” handoff process
• Provide service area + exclusions
• Provide 2–3 preferred message tones (friendly/professional/urgent)
