# 7-Day Agency Partner Launch Checklist (Get First 3 Clients Live)
**Objective:** In 7 days, sign + launch 3 End Client accounts so you have proof, KPIs, and a repeatable process.

## Day 0 (Prep: 60–90 minutes)
1) Pick 1 vertical to start (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, etc.).
2) Decide your retail price: $499 (basic) / $799 (standard) / $999 (premium).
3) Create your offer blurb: “Instant text-back + qualification + booked appointments in minutes.”
4) Create a simple client intake doc (or Google Form).

## Day 1 (Prospecting)
1) Identify 30 targets (past clients, warm leads, local businesses running ads).
2) Send your outreach (use the mini-sequence below).
3) Book 5–8 short calls (15 minutes).

## Day 2 (Sales calls)
1) Run discovery: lead volume, current response time, booking workflow.
2) Close with a 7-day free launch (Week 1 free policy) + KPI baseline.
3) Collect intake immediately (same call or right after).

## Day 3 (Implementation: Intake completion)
For each End Client, collect:
- Business name, service area, hours
- Main offer + top 3 jobs
- Ideal lead qualification questions (3–6 max)
- Booking link or call routing number
- Lead sources: website form, FB lead form, CRM
- Escalation: when to notify human + who receives it

## Day 4 (Build + copy approval)
1) Submit intake to Provider at: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
2) Approve the message script:
- First message (speed-to-lead)
- 3–6 questions
- Qualification thresholds
- Booking handoff message

## Day 5 (QA testing)
1) Run 5 test leads:
- New lead after hours
- New lead during hours
- In-service-area vs out-of-area
- Low intent response
- High intent response
2) Validate lead status tagging + booking flow.

## Day 6 (Launch)
1) Turn on for live leads.
2) Monitor first 20 conversations.
3) Apply quick tweaks: wording, question order, escalation timing.

## Day 7 (Report + upsell)
1) Deliver first KPI snapshot:
- Median response time
- Contact rate
- Qualified rate
- Booked rate
- Lead status breakdown
2) Ask for referral + add-on upsell (missed-call textback, review requests, reactivation).
