D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY ONBOARDING + CLIENT ONBOARDING)
====================================================

SOP GOAL
Launch in 72 hours with measurable improvements in response time and contact rate.

ROLES
• Agency Owner/AM: owns client communication + billing
• Implementation Lead (you/us): config, scripts, integrations
• Client Point of Contact: approves wording + booking rules

STEP 1 — PRE-SALE QUALIFICATION (AGENCY)
Collect:
• Lead sources (FB lead ads, website form, Google LSA, etc.)
• Service area + zip codes
• Business hours + after-hours rules
• Services offered (list)
• Booking preference: call now vs appointment scheduling
• Team size (who receives notifications)

STEP 2 — CLIENT ONBOARDING INTAKE (15 minutes)
Ask client:
1) Primary service(s) to qualify for
2) Top 3 disqualifiers (out of area, minimum job size, service not offered)
3) Preferred booking method (Calendly/booking link, call transfer, “we’ll call you”) 
4) Tone/branding (friendly, direct, premium)
5) Compliance notes (do not message outside hours, etc.)

STEP 3 — SCRIPT SETUP (COPY BLOCK)
Default opening (editable):
“Hi {{first_name}} — it’s {{company}}. Got your request a moment ago. Quick question so we can help: what service do you need?”

Suggested qualification questions (choose 2–5 max):
• “What’s the address or zip code for the job?”
• “Is this urgent (today/this week/not sure)?”
• “What best describes the issue? (A/B/C)”
• “Is this residential or commercial?”
• “What time works best for a call/visit? (morning/afternoon/evening)”

Booking prompt:
“Perfect—want to grab the soonest time? Here’s our schedule: {{booking_link}}”

STEP 4 — INTEGRATIONS + ROUTING
• Connect lead intake (form/FB) to SMS trigger
• Configure notification recipients (SMS/email)
• Set business-hours logic:
  - In-hours: full qualification + booking
  - After-hours: confirm receipt + capture urgency + schedule follow-up

STEP 5 — QA TEST (REQUIRED)
Run 5 test leads:
• Confirm first message sent instantly
• Confirm qualification sequence works
• Confirm lead status updates
• Confirm booking link works
• Confirm notifications delivered

STEP 6 — GO LIVE CHECKLIST
• Client approves final wording
• Confirm opt-out language available if needed (“Reply STOP to opt out”)
• Confirm escalation path for “angry / spam / wrong number”

STEP 7 — WEEK 1 OPTIMIZATION
• Review transcripts and drop-off points
• Reduce questions if completion is low
• Adjust wording for clarity

STEP 8 — MONTHLY REPORTING + QBR (AGENCY-LED)
• Send KPI report
• Recommend script tweaks and booking CTA improvements

TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK HITS
• Low reply rate: shorten first message; remove extra questions; add social proof line “We can usually quote same day.”
• High disqualify rate: refine targeting; add location question first.
• Many “call me” requests: shift CTA to “Want us to call you now or book a time?”
