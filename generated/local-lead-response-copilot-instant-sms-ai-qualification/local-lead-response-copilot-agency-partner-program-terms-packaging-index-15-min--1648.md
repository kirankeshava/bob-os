## 1) Parties + Roles
- **“Provider”** = Local Lead Response Copilot (the fulfillment + platform).  
- **“Partner”** = the agency/reseller managing the client relationship.  
- **“Client”** = Partner’s end customer using the system.

Partner owns the client relationship, billing to the Client, and first-line support. Provider supplies the configured lead-response workflow and provides second-line support to Partner.

## 2) What’s Included (Per Client Account)
Each active Client account includes:
- Lead intake connection (form/webhook/FB lead flow where available)
- Instant SMS response to new leads
- AI-driven qualification (short Q&A)
- Routing/booking handoff (book call/appointment or send to team)
- Monthly KPI reporting export (median response time, contact, qualified, booked, lead status breakdown)

## 3) Pricing to Partner + Resale Guidance
**Partner pays Provider:** $299–$499 per Client account/month (based on tier/volume).  
**Partner charges Client:** recommended $499–$999 per Client account/month.

### Volume discount tiers (to Partner)
- **1–2 accounts:** $499/account/mo
- **3–9 accounts:** $399/account/mo
- **10–24 accounts:** $349/account/mo
- **25+ accounts:** $299/account/mo

### Minimum commitment (for priority support)
- **Priority Support eligibility:** minimum **10 active accounts** (can be ramped within 30 days) and at least **30-day commitment** per account.
- Provider may pause priority routing if active accounts drop below 10.

## 4) Payment Terms (Partner → Provider)
- Billing occurs monthly in arrears or in advance (Partner chooses) once accounts are activated.
- **Week 1 policy (free launch):** initial onboarding can be offered free; no paid tools required to start.
- If payment is late, Provider may pause outbound messaging/automation until brought current.

## 5) Implementation Responsibilities
**Partner provides (required):**
- Client business name + service area
- Lead sources (forms, landing pages, FB Lead Ads) and access/credentials as needed
- Phone number to receive booking notifications (or team inbox)
- Booking link or scheduling rules (optional)
- Offer context: what counts as a “qualified lead,” hours of operation, and disqualifiers

**Provider provides:**
- Workflow setup, qualification logic, messaging copy, routing rules
- QA test with Partner
- Go-live checklist and KPI template

## 6) Support + SLA
- **Partner = first-line support** to Client (billing questions, basic edits, client training).
- **Provider = second-line support** (system issues, workflow debugging, integration fixes).

**Target response times (business days):**
- Standard: respond within 1–2 business days
- Priority (10+ active accounts): respond within 4 business hours

## 7) Data Handling + Privacy
- Client/lead data is used only to operate the workflow and reporting.
- Partner is responsible for obtaining Client’s consent for SMS outreach and for any required disclosures.
- Partner agrees not to upload or process sensitive categories unless explicitly approved (e.g., medical/financial info).

## 8) Messaging/Compliance (Partner Obligations)
Partner ensures:
- Leads have opted in (or the lead source provides compliant consent) for SMS.
- Required opt-out language is included where appropriate.
- No spam, no purchased lists for outbound SMS by default.

Provider may suspend accounts that generate excessive complaints or violate carrier policies.

## 9) White‑Label + Representation
- Partner may present the service under Partner’s brand.
- Partner will not misrepresent capabilities (e.g., “guaranteed booked jobs”).
- Partner will use the legitimacy URL above when needed to reassure Client during onboarding.

## 10) Cancellation + Offboarding
- Partner can cancel an account with 7 days notice before renewal.
- Provider will export KPI summary and status breakdown for the final period upon request.

## 11) KPI Definitions (for consistent reporting)
- **Median response time:** median seconds/minutes from lead created → first outbound SMS.
- **Contact rate:** % of leads that reply at least once.
- **Qualified rate:** % of total leads marked Qualified.
- **Booked rate:** % of total leads that book a call/appointment (or explicitly request scheduling).
- **Lead status breakdown:** New / Contacted / Unresponsive / Qualified / Not Qualified / Booked / No‑Show (optional).

# “Sell This in 15 Minutes” — Partner Sales Script (With Objections)

**Goal:** Close a monthly retainer by focusing on *speed-to-lead* and simple KPIs. Keep it tight and measurable.

## 1) Opener (30 seconds)
“Most local businesses don’t lose leads because their ads are bad—they lose them because they reply too slowly. This is a Lead Response Copilot: the moment a lead comes in, it texts them instantly, asks 2–4 quick questions, and either books them or routes them to your team. Here’s the live product page for credibility: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4.”

## 2) Diagnose (3 minutes)
Ask:
1) “Where do your leads come from—FB Lead Ads, website forms, Google LSAs, other?”
2) “What’s your current average response time to new leads?”
3) “What happens after a lead comes in—who calls/texts, and how fast?”
4) “What % of leads do you think you’re actually reaching?”

Confirm pain:
“Got it. If response time is more than a couple minutes, you’re donating leads to faster competitors—especially in home services.”

## 3) The Promise (2 minutes, KPI-based)
“We improve one thing: **median response time** goes from ‘whenever someone notices’ to **near-instant**. That lift typically increases:
- **Contact rate** (more leads reply)
- **Qualified rate** (fewer tire-kickers waste your time)
- **Booked rate** (more appointments set)

We track this monthly so you can see impact: median response time, contact, qualified, booked, and a lead status breakdown.”

## 4) How It Works (3 minutes)
“Step-by-step:
1) Lead comes in → instant text within seconds
2) AI asks short qualifying questions (job type, location, timeframe, budget range)
3) If qualified → book a call/appointment or notify your team
4) If unresponsive → gentle follow-ups and status tagging

You get a clean status dashboard and a monthly KPI report.”

## 5) Offer + Pricing (2 minutes)
“For a single location, it’s **$___/month**. That includes setup, the qualification flow, booking/routing, and monthly reporting.

Most clients choose **$499–$999/mo** depending on lead volume and how aggressive they want follow-ups. If one extra booked job covers the monthly fee, it’s a no-brainer.”

## 6) Close (2 minutes)
“Do you want this to start with your **highest-intent lead source** first (FB/form) and then expand once we see the KPIs? If yes, we can launch in 24–72 hours after we get access + your qualification rules.”

## 7) Objection Handling
**Objection: “We already text leads.”**
“Totally. The difference is consistency and measurement. This responds instantly 100% of the time, qualifies automatically, and reports median response time + booked rate so you can prove ROI.”

**Objection: “Will this annoy leads?”**
“It’s short and service-focused—2–4 questions max. We optimize to raise contact rate while keeping opt-outs low. Also, you can control follow-up timing and stop rules.”

**Objection: “We don’t have time to set this up.”**
“We do the build. You just answer a short intake: services, areas, what counts as qualified, and where bookings should go.”

**Objection: “How do I know it’s real?”**
“Here’s the live product page: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4. And you can reach our partner support at agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to.”

**Objection: “What results can you guarantee?”**
“No guarantees on revenue—but we *do* guarantee measurable operational KPIs: response time, contact rate, qualified rate, and booked rate. If those don’t move, you’ll see it plainly in the report and we’ll adjust messaging/qualification.”

## 8) Next Step (30 seconds)
“Great—next step is a 20-minute launch call. I’ll send a simple intake checklist and we’ll be live in a few days.”
