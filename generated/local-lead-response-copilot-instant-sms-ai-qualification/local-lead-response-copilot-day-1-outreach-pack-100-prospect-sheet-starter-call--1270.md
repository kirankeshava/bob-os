H) FIRST-3 PAID PILOT SCOPING CHECKLIST (USE ON DEMO)
====================
To ensure <24h go-live, confirm:
1) Lead source type: website form (which tool), FB lead form, Google LSA, call tracking, etc.
2) Where lead data can be sent: webhook/Zapier/Make/email parse/API.

5) Qualification questions (3–5) and disqualifiers.
6) Booking destination (Calendly/Housecall Pro/ServiceTitan/etc.) and what counts as “booked”.
7) Routing recipients (owner, dispatcher) + escalation if no response.
8) Compliance: consent language present on form/ads; opt-out supported.
9) Success metric baseline: current response time + booked jobs/week.

If all 9 are “yes/known”, schedule onboarding immediately and send intake link right away.
