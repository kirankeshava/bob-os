C) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (Agency → Client onboarding in <60 minutes)
============================================================

SOP: Local Lead Response Copilot — Client Implementation (Under 60 Minutes)
Owner: Agency Ops / Account Manager
Goal: Go live fast. First KPI is “median first response time.”

Pre-req checklist (before call)
1) Confirm client niche + service area
2) Confirm intake method(s):
   • Website form provider (Webflow, WordPress, Wix, etc.)
   • Facebook Lead Ads
   • CRM (HubSpot, GoHighLevel, etc.)
3) Confirm booking method:
   • Calendly / Acuity / native scheduling tool
   • Or “callback request” to office
4) Confirm client hours + after-hours handling

Step 1 — Kickoff call (10–15 minutes)
Collect:
• Business name
• Main phone number (for internal notifications)
• Service ZIP codes / cities
• Business hours
• Appointment types (e.g., “Estimate”, “Service Call”)
• Disqualifiers (e.g., “No rentals”, “No commercial”, “No jobs under $X”)
• Desired next step: Book directly vs. request a call

Step 2 — Configure qualification script (10–15 minutes)
Use a 3–6 question flow.
Must-have questions (most niches):
1) Service needed
2) ZIP/city
3) Timeline (ASAP/this week/later)
4) Property type (res/com)
Optional:
• Budget range
• Job size
• Photo upload link (optional)
Disqualification logic:
• Out-of-area → politely decline + close status “Not a Fit (Area)”
• Wrong service → “Not a Fit (Service)”
• Low minimum job size → “Not a Fit (Job Size)”

Step 3 — Connect lead source (15–20 minutes)
Choose one path:
A) Website forms: add webhook / integration to send lead data (name/phone/service) into Copilot.
B) FB Lead Ads: connect via native integration/Zapier-like flow.
C) CRM: route new lead event into Copilot.

Test: submit one lead and confirm SMS sent within 15 seconds.

Step 4 — Booking + routing (10 minutes)
If booking:
• Insert booking link after qualification when intent is high
• Confirm confirmation message + reminders
If routing to human:
• Send internal SMS/email notification including:
  - Lead name
  - Service requested
  - Timeline
  - Status (Qualified)
  - Suggested next action

Step 5 — Lead statuses + definitions (5 minutes)
Standard statuses:
• New
• Contacted
• No Response
• Qualified
• Not a Fit
• Booked
• Closed Won (optional if integrated)
• Closed Lost (optional)

Step 6 — Go live + monitoring (first 72 hours)
Day 1:
• Verify 5+ real leads are being texted correctly
• Check for false disqualifications
Day 3:
• Review conversation transcripts
• Tighten the script: reduce friction, remove extra questions

Step 7 — Monthly reporting cadence
On the 1st business day each month:
• Export prior month KPI report
• Send to client with 3 bullets:
  1) What improved
  2) What’s stuck
  3) Next month optimization
