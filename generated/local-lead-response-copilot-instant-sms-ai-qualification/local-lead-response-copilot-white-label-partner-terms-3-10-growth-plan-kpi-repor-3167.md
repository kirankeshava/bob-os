## The math agencies use to sell ROI (simple)
Assume:
- 100 leads/mo

Incremental revenue = 3.75 × $800 = **$3,000/mo**  
Cost = $499–$999/mo → strong ROI.

# KPI Reporting SOP + Monthly Template (Partner/Client-Facing)

## Reporting cadence
- Send on the 1st–3rd business day each month.
- Include month-over-month deltas and a short action plan.

## Definitions (so everyone reports the same)
1) **Median Response Time (seconds):** median time from lead created → first SMS sent.
2) **Contact Rate:** % of leads that replied at least once OR were reached (agency chooses one definition; be consistent).
   - Formula: contacted leads / total leads.
3) **Qualified Rate:** % of total leads that meet qualification criteria.
   - Formula: qualified leads / total leads.
4) **Booked Rate:** % of total leads that booked an appointment/call.
   - Formula: booked leads / total leads.
5) **Lead Status Breakdown:** counts by status so the client sees where leads go.

## Suggested statuses
- New (received)

- Contacted (replied)
