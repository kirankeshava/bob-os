### The problem (why this matters)
Local businesses lose the “best” leads because:
- Response times are slow (minutes → hours)
- Staff miss after-hours leads
- Leads go cold or book with the first responder
- Manual follow-up is inconsistent

### The KPI promise
**Target: <60 seconds speed-to-lead** for every inbound form/FB lead.

### How it works (simple flow)
1) **Lead comes in** (website form / FB Lead Ad / landing page / call tracking form)
2) **Instant SMS** goes out to the lead (and optional email)
3) **AI qualification** asks 3–5 questions (service needed, urgency, zip code, budget range, preferred time)
4) **Booking**: routes to Calendly / Google Calendar / your scheduler OR alerts your team to call
5) **CRM notes**: all answers + intent score logged to your CRM / Google Sheet

- Audit + optimization checklist (speed-to-lead, question set, booking rate)
- Simple reporting: response time, conversations started, qualified leads, booked appointments

- **Lead sources**: Web forms (Typeform, Jotform, Webflow, WordPress), FB Lead Ads (via Zapier/Make), Google Sheets
- **Scheduling**: Calendly, Google Calendar
- **CRMs**: HighLevel (GHL), HubSpot, Pipedrive, Sheets
