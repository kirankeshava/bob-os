## D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (Agency → Client → Build → Launch)

### 1) Pre-Sale Checklist (Agency)
Confirm client has:
- Consistent lead flow (forms/FB leads/calls)
- Booking destination (calendar link or office scheduler)
- Service area + service list
- Clear “who we serve / who we don’t” rules

### 2) Client Intake (Agency collects; send to us)
Required fields:
- Business name, location(s), timezone
- Services offered (top 5) + exclusions
- Service area ZIPs/cities
- Hours of operation
- Booking method: calendar link or “request appointment” handoff
- Lead sources: website form URL, FB lead form, CRM/webhook details
- Routing contacts: phone(s), email(s)
- Brand voice: friendly/professional, emojis yes/no

### 3) Script Setup (We draft; agency/client approves)
- First message (instant)
- 3–6 qualifier questions (service, urgency, location, timeline, budget optional)
- Qualification rules (what counts as qualified)
- Booking/handoff messages
- No-response follow-ups (e.g., +5 min, +2 hrs, next day)

### 4) Build & QA (We execute)
- Configure lead capture → SMS trigger
- Configure qualification flow
- Configure tags/status outcomes
- Configure appointment link or handoff rules
- QA with test leads (min 10 tests)

### 5) Launch
- Turn on live lead sources
- Monitor first 24–48 hours
- Fix edge cases (wrong service area, odd lead formats)

### 6) Optimization (Monthly)
- Review KPIs
- Tighten question order to increase replies
- Adjust follow-up timing
- Update qualification rules based on close data

### 7) Support Rules (Simple)
- Submit requests via: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Include: account name, issue, screenshots, example lead timestamp
- Priority support: 3+ accounts + 60-day commitment

## F) PARTNER TERMS & OPERATING GUIDELINES (Plain Language, Non-Legal)

**1) White-Label Use**  
Partners may present the service under their agency brand. Do not represent that you “built” the underlying system if asked directly; position it as your **delivery stack**.

**2) Commitments & Billing**  
Priority support requires a minimum of 3 active accounts and a 60-day commitment. Accounts are billed per active location/account per month based on the wholesale tier.

**3) Support & SLA Expectations**  
We handle build, QA, and ongoing workflow edits. Standard support is best-effort; priority support receives same/next business-day responses for most issues.

**4) Data Handling**  
Lead data is used only to operate the service. Partners should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive info. If a client requests deletion/offboarding, partner notifies us and we remove access and retain only minimal logs required for operations.

**5) Client Communication**  
Partner is the primary client-facing party. We can join calls when necessary, but the kit is designed so you can sell and onboard without us.

**6) Offboarding**  
If a client churns, partner notifies us to disable triggers and stop messaging. Any client-facing assets (scripts, templates) remain usable by partner, but the system access ends.

**7) Compliance Note (Practical)**  
Partners should ensure their clients’ lead forms/ads include consent language for SMS follow-up. We can provide sample consent language upon request via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to.
