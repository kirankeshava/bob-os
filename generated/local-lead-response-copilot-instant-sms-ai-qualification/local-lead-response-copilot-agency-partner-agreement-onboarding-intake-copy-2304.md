If minimum commitment is not met, Provider may move Partner to Standard pricing until volume is reached.

5) BILLING + PAYMENT TERMS
- Provider invoices Agency Partner monthly in advance for active accounts.
- An “active account” means an account with an enabled phone number + at least one connected lead source (form/FB lead ads/webhook) OR in-production routing.
- Agency Partner is responsible for collecting payment from Clients.
- During Week 1 launch policy (if applicable), onboarding/services may be free to end Clients as agreed; wholesale billing still follows this section unless Provider explicitly grants a temporary waiver in writing.

Provider responsibilities:

Agency Partner responsibilities:
- Collect Client intake (see intake checklist below).
- Ensure Client has consent-compliant lead collection (opt-in language where required) and appropriate privacy policy.
- Provide/coordinate access to lead sources (forms/CRM/FB leads/webhooks).
- Provide the booking workflow destination (calendar link, CRM appointment, or call scheduling rules).
- Handle Client training and first-line support.

7) SUPPORT + SLA
Channels: email (agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to).

Target response times:
- Priority support partners: 24 hours on business days.
- Standard: 48 hours on business days.
Escalations: system outage, lead delivery failure, or message-sending failure should be marked URGENT in the subject line.

8) DATA + PRIVACY
- Agency Partner and Client data may include names, phone numbers, lead source metadata, and conversation transcripts.
- Provider uses data only to operate, maintain, and improve the service and to generate performance reporting.
- Agency Partner is responsible for ensuring Clients have the right to share lead data and have provided any required disclosures/consents.

9) COMPLIANCE + ACCEPTABLE USE
- No spam, cold SMS blasts, or purchased lead lists.
- Use only with leads who requested contact (e.g., web form, inbound call request, FB lead form) or where consent is obtained.
- No illegal services, harassment, or deceptive content.
Provider may suspend accounts that violate acceptable use to protect deliverability and platform integrity.

10) REPORTING + KPI DEFINITIONS
Provider supplies a monthly KPI summary and/or exports. KPI definitions:
- Median Response Time: median time from lead created → first outbound SMS.
- Contact Rate: % of leads that reply or answer call (per your chosen definition; define once and keep consistent).
- Qualified Rate: % of leads that meet qualification criteria.
- Booked Rate: % of leads that book an appointment/call.
- Lead Status Breakdown: counts by status (New, Contacted, Qualified, Unqualified, Booked, No Response, Lost, Other).

11) PERFORMANCE DISCLAIMER
Provider improves speed-to-lead and qualification consistency; results vary based on lead quality, offer, pricing, service area, and follow-up process. No guarantees of revenue or booked jobs.

12) TERM + TERMINATION
- Month-to-month unless otherwise agreed.
- Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice.
- Upon termination, Provider will disable sending/automation for affected accounts after the notice period.

13) IP / MATERIALS
- Provider retains ownership of the platform, workflows, and templates.
- Agency Partner may use the reseller materials to sell and service Clients.

14) NON-CIRCUMVENT (OPTIONAL, OPERATIONAL)
