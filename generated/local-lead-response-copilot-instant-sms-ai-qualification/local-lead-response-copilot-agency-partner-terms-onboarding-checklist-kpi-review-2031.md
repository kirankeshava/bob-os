Partner Onboarding Checklist (Launch a New Client in <48 Hours)

Goal: Go from “yes” to live Copilot with measurable response-time improvement in 1–2 days.

A) Intake (Agency collects from client) — 20 minutes
1) Business basics
□ Business name
□ Service area (cities/zip codes)
□ Business hours + after-hours handling
□ Primary phone number(s) + caller ID preference
□ Booking link OR preferred booking method (call transfer, calendar, CRM)

2) Lead sources (choose all)
□ Website form (URL + field list)
□ Facebook Lead Ads (page access / webhook plan)
□ Google LSA / other (describe)
□ Third-party lead vendor (confirm opt-in)

3) Qualification rules
□ “Good lead” definition (job types, min ticket, location, urgency)
□ Disqualifiers (outside area, wrong job type, too small)
□ Questions to ask (3–6 max)

4) Routing
□ If qualified: book link OR notify/schedule call
□ If not qualified: polite closeout + optional referral
□ Notification recipients (emails/phones)

B) Build (Copilot config) — 60–120 minutes
□ Connect lead source → Copilot intake
□ Create conversation flow (greeting + 3–6 questions)
□ Configure lead status tags:
   - New
   - Contacted
   - Responded
   - Qualified
   - Not Qualified
   - Booked
   - No Show (optional)
   - Unreachable
□ Configure handoff:
   - booking URL OR call routing OR “text-to-call” prompt
□ Configure fail-safes:
   - after-hours message
   - stop words: STOP/UNSUBSCRIBE handling

C) QA (Agency + Client) — 30 minutes
□ Test 3 leads end-to-end (during business hours)
□ Confirm first response time < 60 seconds
□ Confirm questions match the client’s tone and rules
□ Confirm qualified lead triggers booking/notification
□ Confirm STOP compliance behavior

D) Go-Live — 10 minutes
□ Turn on for all lead sources
□ Notify client team: “What happens when a lead comes in” (simple SOP)
□ Set expectation: reply/call within 5 minutes for best conversion

E) Week-1 Optimization — 30 minutes
□ Review KPIs at day 3 and day 7
□ Tighten qualification questions (reduce friction)
□ Adjust routing/booking messaging to lift booked rate

4) Status Definitions (so reporting is consistent)
• Contacted: Copilot sent the first SMS
• Responded: Lead replied at least once
• Qualified: Lead meets service area + job type + basic criteria
• Not Qualified: Disqualified by rules or self-reported mismatch
• Booked: Appointment scheduled or call confirmed
