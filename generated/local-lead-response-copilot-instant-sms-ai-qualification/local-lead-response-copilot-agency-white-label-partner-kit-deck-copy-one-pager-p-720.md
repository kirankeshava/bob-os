### Slide 3 — The Promise (what this fixes)
**We respond instantly, qualify in under 2 minutes, and book the next step automatically.**
- New lead comes in → **instant SMS**
- Short AI-driven questions → **qualified/not qualified**
- If qualified → **book a call/appointment** OR hand off warm lead
- If no response → **automated follow-up sequence**

Outcome: **more contacted leads, more qualified conversations, more booked jobs**.

### Slide 4 — How it Works (simple flow)
**1) Trigger:** Form submit / FB Lead Ad / webhook / CRM lead created  
**2) Instant SMS:** “Hey {Name}, it’s {Company}. Quick question…”  
**3) Qualification:** 3–6 questions (job type, urgency, location, budget range, etc.)  
**4) Routing:**
- Qualified → booking link / calendar / dispatcher
- Not qualified → polite disqualify + optional nurture
- No response → follow-up cadence

## D) Implementation SOP (Agency-Ready)

### Objective
Get a new client live in **≤ 5 business days** with measurable KPI reporting from week one.

### Roles
- **Agency Owner/AM:** sells, owns client relationship, collects intake
- **Our team (Copilot):** builds workflows, QA, supports, provides KPI format

### Step 1 — Pre-Sale Checklist (Agency)
Confirm:
- Client has a consistent lead source (forms/FB/CRM)
- Client can handle booked calls/appointments
- Client agrees to respond to warm transfers within SLA (recommended < 5 min)

### Step 2 — Intake (Agency → Client → Us)
Collect and email to: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to

**Client Intake Form (copy/paste questions):**
1) Business name:
2) Main service categories (top 3):
3) Service area (cities/zip codes):
4) Business hours:
5) Ideal customer (1–2 sentences):
6) Disqualifiers (out of area, job size, etc.):
7) Preferred next step:
   - Book appointment link (URL) OR
   - Call dispatcher at (phone) OR
   - “Collect info then we call them”
8) Required qualification questions (choose 3–6):
   - What service do you need?
   - When do you want it done?
   - What’s your address/zip code?
   - Owner/decision maker?
   - Any photos/notes?
9) Tone/brand voice (friendly/professional/direct):
10) Compliance note: include opt-out language “Reply STOP to opt out.” (default)

### Step 3 — Build (Our team)
- Configure lead trigger (form/FB/webhook/CRM)
- Draft SMS opener + follow-ups
- Implement qualification path + status tags
- Configure booking/routing logic

### Step 4 — QA (Agency + Us)
Test with 3 scenarios:
1) Qualified + books
2) Qualified + doesn’t book (follow-up triggers)
3) Not qualified (polite exit)

Validate:
- SMS arrives instantly
- Questions make sense for niche
- Status labeling correct
- Booking link works on mobile

### Step 5 — Go-Live
- Turn on for production lead source
- Confirm first 5 live leads are correctly tagged

### Step 6 — Week-1 Optimization
- Review early KPIs
- Adjust question count (aim: 3–6 questions)
- Adjust follow-up timing and messaging

### Escalation Rules
- If response time > 60 seconds median: check lead trigger/integration
- If contact rate < 35%: adjust opener + timing + follow-up cadence
- If qualified rate too low/high: refine disqualifiers/questions
