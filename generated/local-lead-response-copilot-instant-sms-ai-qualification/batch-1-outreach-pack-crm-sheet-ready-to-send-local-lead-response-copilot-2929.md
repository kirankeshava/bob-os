====================
F) CRM / TRACKING SHEET (Google Sheets layout)
====================
Create a Google Sheet with 3 tabs: Leads, Touches, Dashboard.

TAB 1: Leads (one row per prospect)
Columns:
- Lead_ID
- Segment (Agency/Operator)
- Company
- Website
- Source_URL
- Location
- Niche
- Decision_Maker_Name
- Role
- Email
- Phone
- LinkedIn_URL
- Contact_Path (Direct email / Contact form / LinkedIn)
- Personal_Line
- Status (New / Warmup Sent / Step1 Sent / Step2 Sent / Replied / Demo Booked / Not Fit / No Response)
- Last_Touch_Date
- Next_Step_Date
- Notes

TAB 2: Touches (one row per touch)
Columns:
- Lead_ID
- Date
- Channel (Email/LinkedIn/SMS)
- Step (Warmup/1/2)
- Subject_or_Message
- Outcome (Sent/Bounced/Opened/Clicked/Replied)
- Reply_Summary

TAB 3: Dashboard
Metrics (simple formulas):
- Total Leads
- Total Sent (count Touches outcome=Sent)
- Reply Rate (unique Lead_ID with Replied / Total Sent)
- Demo Booked (count Leads status=Demo Booked)
- Demo Rate (Demo Booked / Total Sent)
- By Segment (pivot: Segment vs Replied/Demo Booked)
