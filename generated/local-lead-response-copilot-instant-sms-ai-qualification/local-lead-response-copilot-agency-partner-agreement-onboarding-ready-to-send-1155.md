## 1) What you’re reselling
Provider supplies a white‑labelable “Instant SMS + AI Qualification” workflow that:
- Responds instantly to new leads (forms / ads / web inquiries)
- Asks short qualifying questions via SMS
- Routes outcomes (qualified / not qualified / no response)
- Books calls/appointments or pushes qualified leads to the client/Agency

Agency Partner may resell the service to their clients (“End Clients”) under Agency Partner branding.

## 2) White‑label + positioning
- Agency Partner may brand the service as their own (white label). Provider branding can be removed from end-client facing materials.
- Agency Partner is responsible for their own client sales claims. Provider supplies recommended messaging and KPI expectations.

## 3) Pricing, margins, and volume tiers (B2B wholesale)
Agency Partner pays Provider per End Client account, monthly in advance.

**Standard wholesale tiers (choose one):**
- **Tier A (1–2 accounts):** $499/account/month
- **Tier B (3–9 accounts):** $399/account/month
- **Tier C (10–24 accounts):** $349/account/month
- **Tier D (25+ accounts):** $299/account/month

**Recommended agency resale pricing (MSRP):** $499–$999/account/month depending on niche and booking value.

### Minimum commitment for priority support
To receive **Priority Partner Support**, Agency Partner commits to either:
- **10 active accounts**, or
- **$3,490/month minimum** (equivalent to 10 accounts at Tier C)

If Agency Partner falls below the minimum for two consecutive billing cycles, support shifts to Standard Support until the minimum is restored.

## 4) Billing + payment terms
- Provider invoices monthly in advance. Net due on receipt unless otherwise agreed.
- Late payments may pause provisioning/support until resolved.
- Agency Partner may pass through any telecom/message costs to clients if desired; otherwise include in their retail pricing. (Provider will specify what’s included for the current plan.)

## 5) Onboarding + implementation responsibilities
**Provider responsibilities:**
- Configure the workflow per account based on intake
- Provide test scripts and QA checklist
- Troubleshoot initial routing and qualification flows

**Agency Partner responsibilities:**
- Collect End Client intake data (numbers, hours, qualification rules, booking link, lead sources)
- Ensure End Client has consent-compliant lead capture language and opt-in where required
- Provide access/credentials needed for integrations (or coordinate with End Client)
- Act as first-line support to End Clients (recommended)

## 6) Support levels + response times
**Standard Support:**
- Email support via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Target response: 1–2 business days

**Priority Partner Support (10+ accounts or minimum commitment):**
- Target response: within 1 business day
- Implementation queue priority

## 7) KPI reporting expectations (what “good” looks like)
Agency Partner agrees to track and share (monthly, per account):
- **Median response time** (goal: under 60 seconds)
- **Contact rate** (% of leads who reply)
- **Qualified rate** (% of leads who meet criteria)
- **Booked rate** (% of leads booked)
- **Lead status breakdown** (new / contacted / qualified / booked / lost / no response)

Provider will supply a reporting template that Agency Partner can copy per account.

## 8) Compliance + texting consent
Agency Partner is responsible for ensuring End Clients collect leads with appropriate consent and comply with local laws and carrier policies (TCPA/CTIA where applicable). Provider can share suggested opt-in language, but Agency Partner/End Client remains responsible for legal compliance.

## 9) Data handling + confidentiality
- Provider will access lead/contact data solely to deliver the service.
- Agency Partner and Provider agree to keep each other’s non-public business information confidential.
- If an End Client requests deletion of data, Agency Partner will coordinate with Provider.

## 10) Uptime and limitations
Provider aims for high reliability but does not guarantee uninterrupted service. Provider is not liable for lost revenue, missed jobs, or consequential damages arising from downtime, carrier issues, client phone issues, or misconfigured intake.

## 11) Cancellation + account changes
- Agency Partner may cancel an account with **30 days notice** prior to renewal.
- Provider may suspend accounts for non-payment.
- Plan/tier changes apply at the next billing cycle unless otherwise agreed.

## 12) Relationship
Agency Partner is an independent contractor and reseller. This agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship.
