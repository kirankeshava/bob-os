## 4) Implementation SOP (agency view)
### A) Intake (per client)
Collect:
- Business name + hours + service area (zips/cities)
- Primary services offered
- Lead sources (form URL, FB lead form, funnel)
- Booking preference (call vs appointment)
- Routing rules (who gets alerted, when)
- Disqualifiers (out of area, wrong service, spam)

### B) Build (internal)
- Connect lead source → instant SMS trigger
- Configure qualification question flow (3–6 questions max)
- Configure booking/alert routing
- Set fallback behavior (no response after X minutes)

### C) QA checklist
- Test lead capture (real submission)
- Confirm SMS arrives <60 seconds
- Confirm qualification flow works end-to-end
- Confirm alerts/booking links work

### D) Launch
- Turn on production
- Monitor first 20 leads closely
- Adjust wording + routing rules based on outcomes

### E) Optimize (weekly, first 30 days)
- Improve question order for higher completion
- Tighten disqualifiers to reduce noise
- Add “hot lead” escalation for urgent jobs
