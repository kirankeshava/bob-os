D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY DELIVERY PLAYBOOK)
============================================================

PURPOSE
Launch Local Lead Response Copilot quickly and consistently across client accounts.

ROLES
• Agency AM (Account Manager): client intake + approvals
• Build Ops (us): configure flows, test, go-live
• Client Office: receives hot leads, takes calls, attends booked appointments

TIMELINE TARGET
48–72 hours from complete intake → go-live.

STEP 1 — Client intake (agency)
Collect:
1) Business name, timezone, service area (cities/zips)
2) Services offered (top 5), disqualifiers (what they don’t do)
3) Target job types + minimum budget (if any)
4) Booking preference:
   a) Schedule link, OR
   b) Call-now routing to office
5) Lead sources:
   • Website form (provider, URL)
   • Facebook lead ads (if used)
   • CRM/webhook (if used)
6) Office hours + after-hours behavior
7) Primary contact numbers (where hot leads should go)

STEP 2 — Define the qualification script (agency + us)
Default question set (edit per niche):
• “What type of service do you need?”
• “Where is the job located (zip or city)?”
• “How soon do you need help?”
• “Is this residential or commercial?”
• “Anything important we should know before we schedule?”
Rules:
• Keep to 3–6 questions max
• Ask only what changes whether they are a fit
• Always end with a booking or handoff

STEP 3 — Configure lead routing + statuses (us)
Statuses to track:
• New
• Contacted (2-way conversation started)
• Qualified
• Booked
• Not Qualified
• No Response
Routing examples:
• Qualified + urgent → notify office immediately
• Qualified + normal → offer booking link
• Not qualified → polite close + tag

STEP 4 — QA test (agency)
Test scenarios:
• New lead during business hours
• New lead after hours
• Qualified path to booking
• Not-qualified path
• No-response follow-up behavior
Approve:
• Message tone/brand
• Questions
• Handoff notifications

STEP 5 — Go-live (us)
• Turn on automation for specified lead sources
• Monitor first 24 hours
• Provide agency with “Day 1 summary” (counts + any issues)

STEP 6 — 7-day optimization (agency + us)
Review:
• Response time
• Drop-off question (where prospects stop replying)
• Qualification strictness (too tight/too loose)
Adjust:
• Shorten questions
• Change ordering
• Add/adjust booking CTA

STEP 7 — Monthly reporting + retention (agency)
Send KPI report (template below) with:
• 1 insight
• 1 optimization shipped
• 1 recommended next step
