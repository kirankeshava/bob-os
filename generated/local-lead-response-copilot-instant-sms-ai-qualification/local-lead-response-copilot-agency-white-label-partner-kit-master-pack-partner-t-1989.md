4) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY-READY)
============================================================
Title: Agency SOP — Launch a Client in Local Lead Response Copilot (60–90 minutes)
Support: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
Reference site: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4

Phase 0 — Pre-Qualify (5 minutes)
Goal: Confirm the client is a good fit.
- High-intent leads (quote requests, “call now”, service inquiries)
- 20+ leads/month recommended (lower can still work for high ticket)
- Willing to include SMS consent language on forms/ads

Phase 1 — Intake (15 minutes)
Collect:
1) Business details: name, phone, service areas, hours.
2) Lead sources: website forms, FB lead ads, landing pages.
3) Qualification criteria: job type, zip codes, minimum project size, urgency.
4) Booking handoff: calendar link OR call transfer number OR “request booking” workflow.
5) Brand voice: friendly/professional, bilingual needs.

Phase 2 — Build (20–30 minutes)
- Set the initial SMS message: acknowledges lead + sets expectation.
- Configure 3–5 qualification questions max.
- Configure follow-ups (2–4 nudges) across the first 24 hours.
- Set outcomes: Qualified → booking link; Unqualified → polite close; No response → final check-in.

Phase 3 — Compliance & QA (10–15 minutes)
- Verify consent language exists on the form/ad (Partner responsibility).
- Test with 3 sample leads:
  1) ideal lead (should qualify + get booking)
  2) unqualified lead (should be filtered)
  3) no-response lead (should follow-up correctly)
- Confirm opt-out keyword behavior (STOP) is honored.

Phase 4 — Launch (same day)
- Turn on live routing.
- Notify client: “You should see instant lead texts. Here’s how booked leads appear.”

Phase 5 — Stabilize & Optimize (days 1–7)
- Review median response time and contact rate.
- If contact rate is low: shorten first message, reduce question count, improve offer clarity.
- If booked rate is low: simplify booking, use fewer steps, add urgency.

Phase 6 — Monthly Reporting (30 minutes/month)
- Fill KPI template.
- Highlight 1–2 wins + 1 bottleneck + next actions.
