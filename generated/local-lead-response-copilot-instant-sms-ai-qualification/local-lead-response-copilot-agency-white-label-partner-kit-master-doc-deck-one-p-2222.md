D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY → CLIENT)
==============================

Objective
Go live in 48 hours with instant SMS, qualification, and booking, then optimize weekly based on KPI data.

Roles
• Agency: owns client relationship + positioning + billing to client.
• Us (white‑label): build workflow, maintain prompt/script, help troubleshoot.

Step 1 — Client intake (Day 0)
Collect:
1) Business name + hours + service area (zip/cities)
2) Service categories (top 5 jobs)
3) Booking rules (lead types to book vs call)
4) Calendar link or preferred booking method
5) Lead sources (website form, FB lead ads, etc.)
6) Required questions (address/zip, job type, urgency, photos, budget range)
7) Disqualifiers (outside area, wrong job type, spam)

Step 2 — Access checklist (Day 0)
Client/agency provides:
• Form access or webhook/Zapier details
• FB lead access (if applicable)
• Calendar access (or booking link)
• Phone/SMS number choice (white‑label)

Step 3 — Build (Day 1)
We configure:
• Trigger: new lead
• First SMS: <10–30 seconds
• AI questions (3–6)
• Routing rules:
  – Qualified → book
  – Not ready → nurture
  – Out of area → disqualify
• Notifications: send summary to team

Step 4 — QA (Day 1)
Test 10 sample leads:
• Verify response time
• Verify question flow
• Verify correct status labeling
• Verify booking works

Step 5 — Launch (Day 2)
• Turn on for live leads
• Monitor first 48 hours closely
• Fix friction points: too many questions, unclear wording, wrong routing

Step 6 — Weekly optimization (ongoing)
• Reduce drop‑off by shortening question count
• Add/adjust disqualifiers to protect sales team
• Tune messaging for niche language
• Review KPI trendline weekly

Client‑facing “what to expect” script (agency can paste)
“Every new lead gets an instant text within seconds. They’ll answer a few quick questions, then we’ll book them or route them. You’ll see monthly reporting with response time, contact rate, qualified rate, and booked rate.”
