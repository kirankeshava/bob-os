Most leads go cold in minutes. If a prospect submits a form and doesn’t hear back immediately, they:

**Speed-to-lead is the real conversion lever.**

### What it does (in plain English)
1) Lead submits (website form / FB lead ad / inbound webhook)
2) They get an **instant SMS**: “Hey {name} — quick question so I can get you the right quote.”
3) The system asks **3–5 short qualification questions** (job type, urgency, zip code, budget range, etc.)
4) If qualified, it **books a call/appointment** automatically (or routes to your scheduler)
5) It writes **clean notes** into your CRM + pings your team for human takeover when needed
