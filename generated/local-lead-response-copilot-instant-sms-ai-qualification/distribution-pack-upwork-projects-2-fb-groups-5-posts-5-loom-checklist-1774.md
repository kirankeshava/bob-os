### Post 2 — “AI qualification without sounding like a bot”
I tested a bunch of SMS qualification scripts and the biggest unlock wasn’t “more AI”… it was **shorter questions**.

Working pattern for local services:
1) confirm what they need (1 line)
2) location/zip (1 line)
3) timeline (today/this week/this month)
4) one constraint (budget / property type / insurance)
5) then: book now vs human handoff

Done right, it doesn’t feel like a bot—just a fast assistant.
