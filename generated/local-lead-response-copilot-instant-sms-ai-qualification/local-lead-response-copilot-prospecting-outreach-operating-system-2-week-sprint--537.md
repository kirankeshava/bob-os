## 1) ICP (who we target first)

### A) Owner/Operator (highest-intent, fastest close)
Best fits:
- Home services with high lead value + missed-call sensitivity: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, water damage, pest control, garage doors, electricians, tree service.
- Running Google/FB lead forms or website forms.
- Has a booking link (Calendly/Housecall Pro/ServiceTitan) OR can take calls immediately.
Trigger signals:
- Ads running (FB Ad Library), “Request a quote” forms, multiple locations, hiring techs, reviews mention “didn’t call back / slow response.”
Disqualifiers (for week 1):
- No lead flow at all, no ability to answer calls, compliance-averse, extremely low ticket.

### B) Local Lead-Gen Agencies (higher volume, fewer deals)
Best fits:
- Manages 5–50 local clients, already running lead forms.
- Wants retention + differentiation (speed-to-lead as productized add-on).
- Open to white-label or referral.
Disqualifiers:
- No active clients, wants rev-share only, won’t pay setup.

## 3) 2-Week Outreach Quota Plan (goal: close 3 paid pilots)

### Weekly targets
- Demos booked: 12–15 total (aim 6–8/week)
- Show rate assumption: 70%
- Close rate assumption on demos: 25–35% (with DFY + 24h go-live)
= 3 paid pilots is realistic with 12–15 booked.

### Daily activity (Mon–Fri)
Owner/operators:
- 20 new cold emails/day
- 10 LinkedIn DMs/day
- 5 calls/day (optional, if number available)
Agencies:
- 10 new cold emails/day
- 5 LinkedIn DMs/day

Follow-ups:
- 20 follow-up touches/day (mix email + DM) across active conversations.

### Cadence (simple and aggressive)
- Day 0: Email 1 + LinkedIn DM 1
- Day 2: Follow-up 1
- Day 4: Follow-up 2 (value + quick teardown)
- Day 7: Breakup email
For replies: push to 15-min demo within 48 hours.

## 4) Pipeline (CRM-lite) Stages + exit criteria

1. **Prospect Identified** → has contact info + evidence of lead flow.
2. **Contacted** → sent email/DM.
3. **Engaged** → replied or clicked + asked question.
4. **Demo Booked** → calendar invite sent.
5. **Demo Completed** → decision maker attended.
6. **Paid Pilot** → payment received for $499 setup + $499/mo OR annual paid.
7. **Intake Complete** → config form submitted + webhook/lead source access provided.
8. **Live** → tested end-to-end, staff notified, first lead processed.

Rule: every record must always have a “Next Action” and “Next Touch Date.”
