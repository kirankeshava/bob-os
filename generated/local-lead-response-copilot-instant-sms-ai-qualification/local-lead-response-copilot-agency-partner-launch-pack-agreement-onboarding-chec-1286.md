### What Provider delivers
Provider supplies a white-label eligible “instant lead response + SMS qualification + booking workflow” system (“Service”) that:
1) sends an SMS to new leads within ~60 seconds of receipt (dependent on upstream delivery),
2) asks short qualification questions by SMS,
3) routes qualified leads to booking/call scheduling and/or notifies the business,
4) tracks results and reports key metrics.

### White-label and client ownership
- Partner may resell the Service to its clients under Partner branding.
- Partner owns the client relationship and sets end-client pricing.
- Provider is the underlying service operator; Provider may appear as the sender on invoices only if Partner requests.

### Pricing to Partner, minimums, and term
- Partner pays Provider per active client account at agreed wholesale pricing (typical: **$299–$499/account/month**).
- **Minimum commitment for priority support:** Partner maintains **3 active accounts** (or pays the equivalent minimum) to receive priority support and launch scheduling.
- Initial term: month-to-month unless a written annual agreement is signed.

### Volume discount tiers (example)
- 1–2 accounts: $499/account/mo
- 3–9 accounts: $399/account/mo
- 10–24 accounts: $349/account/mo
- 25+ accounts: $299/account/mo
Provider may adjust tiers with 30 days notice, excluding already-contracted annual terms.

### What’s included vs. usage costs
Included (unless otherwise specified):
- onboarding + initial workflow build per account
- qualification flow (up to one primary flow) + one booking route
- KPI dashboard/report export
- basic optimization suggestions

Usage/Pass-through costs (if applicable):
- SMS/voice usage and provider pass-through fees may apply depending on configuration. If pass-through applies, Provider will disclose estimated ranges before launch.

- Provide accurate client info and obtain necessary permissions/consent for SMS outreach.

- Use approved messaging templates and not request prohibited content.

### Implementation timeline / launch SLA

Provider is not responsible for delays caused by missing access, incomplete data, or client-side systems.

## 3) Agency Prospecting & Sourcing SOP (Free-First)
Goal: find agencies already generating leads for local businesses (home services, med spas, dentists, legal intake, etc.) and pitch a “speed-to-lead + AI qualification” add-on.

### Ideal partner profile (ICP)
- Runs FB/Google lead gen for local service businesses
- Has 5–50 active clients
- Complains about lead quality, no-shows, slow follow-up
- Uses GoHighLevel, Calendly, HubSpot, or basic CRM

### A) Free sourcing channels (do these first)
1. **Google search operators** (copy/paste):
   - “facebook ads agency” “roofing” + city
   - “lead generation” “plumbing” + “agency”
   - “go high level agency” + city/state
2. **Clutch / G2 / UpCity directories**
   - Filter: Advertising → PPC / Social Media → Small business
   - Capture agencies with home services case studies
3. **LinkedIn search**
   - Titles: “Founder”, “Owner”, “Head of Growth”, “Media Buyer”
   - Keywords: “home services”, “lead gen”, “PPC”, “GoHighLevel”
4. **Facebook groups** (agency/operator groups)
   - Search posts about “speed to lead”, “follow up”, “show rate”, “GHL workflows”

### B) What to capture in a simple sheet
Columns:
- Agency name
- Website
- Niche (home services/med spa/etc.)
- Decision maker name
- Email
- LinkedIn
- Notes (tools used, proof signals)
- Status (Not contacted / Sent / Replied / Call booked / Closed)

### C) Outreach angles that convert
- “You already buy leads; we make them convert.”
- “Instant SMS + 3-4 question qualification raises contact rate.”
- “Your clients stop wasting spend on unresponsive leads.”
- “You charge $499–$999/mo; we wholesale at $299–$499.”

### D) Qualification questions for agencies (on first call)
1. How many active lead-gen clients do you have today?
2. Average monthly leads per client?
3. Current follow-up process and speed-to-lead?
4. What’s the biggest retention risk (lead quality, show rate, ROI)?
5. Are you open to adding a productized conversion layer you can mark up?

### E) Close + next step
- Close a 3-account starter (meets minimum) or 10-account plan for best margin.
- Send the Partner Agreement + onboarding checklist.
- Start with one “flagship” client for a quick win and case study.
