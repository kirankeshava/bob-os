## D) 2-Minute Loom Demo Script (One-Take)
**Goal:** show lead submission → instant SMS → 3–5 questions → booking → CRM notes.

### Prep (before recording)
- Have a sample form open (or a Zapier test webhook) labeled “Test Lead”
- Have the SMS inbox visible (phone or web console)
- Have a booking page (Calendly) ready
- Have a CRM record page or a “Notes” sheet ready to demonstrate logging

### Recording script (approx timestamps)
**0:00–0:15** — “This is Local Lead Response Copilot. The KPI is <60 seconds from lead to first text. Built for agencies/local service businesses.”

**0:15–0:35** — Submit a test lead on the form. Narrate: “Lead comes in from web form / FB lead ads.”

**0:35–0:55** — Show instant SMS arriving. “We respond immediately with a friendly opener + opt-out language.”

**0:55–1:25** — Show the 3–5 questions:
1) “What service do you need?”
2) “What zip code?”
3) “How soon do you need this?”
4) “Are you the decision maker + best time?”
(Keep it fast—show 2 responses and jump.)

**1:25–1:45** — Booking: “Once qualified, we send a booking link or do a call-now handoff.” Click/open booking.

**1:45–2:00** — CRM notes: “We write the answers back to your CRM/notes so sales has context.” Show notes/tag.
