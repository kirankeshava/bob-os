## 3) Free Prospecting/Sourcing SOP (Find Agencies Without Paying)
**Goal:** find 30–50 agencies/week who already sell lead gen/ads to home services and can add Copilot as an upsell.

### 3.1 Best-Fit Agency Profile
Target agencies that:
- Run Facebook/Google ads for local services OR build websites with forms
- Talk about “leads,” “appointments,” “pipeline,” “speed to lead,” “automation”
- Serve niches like: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, solar, pest control, med spas, dentists, contractors

Avoid agencies that:
- Only do branding/design (no lead ownership)
- Enterprise-only or long procurement cycles
- No clear case studies / low activity

### 3.2 Where to Find Them (Free)
1) **LinkedIn search:**
   - Keywords: “Facebook ads home services”, “Google ads contractor”, “lead generation agency HVAC”, “local service ads agency”
   - Filter: Company headcount 1–50, location (US/CA/AU)
   - Collect: owner/founder name, agency site, contact email

2) **Google footprints:**
   - Queries: “HVAC marketing agency”, “plumbing PPC agency”, “roofing leads agency”
   - Open top 30 results; grab contact email + a specific note from their site.

3) **Clutch / UpCity directories (free browsing):**
   - Filter by local advertising/PPC + small agencies.

4) **Facebook groups (organic):**
   - “PPC for home services”, “SMMA”, “Local lead gen” groups
   - Post value-first: speed-to-lead benchmarks + free audit offer.

### 3.3 What to Collect in Your Simple Sheet
- Agency name
- Niche focus
- Founder name
- Email
- LinkedIn URL
- Website
- Note (personalization line)
- Status (Not contacted / Contacted / Replied / Call booked / Closed)

### 3.4 First Message Personalization Prompts
Use one line:
- “Saw you run Google Ads for {{niche}} — do your clients struggle with missed leads after hours?”
- “Noticed you offer FB lead ads — do you have an instant SMS follow-up in your stack yet?”
