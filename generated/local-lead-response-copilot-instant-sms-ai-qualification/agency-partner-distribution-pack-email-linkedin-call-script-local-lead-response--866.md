## 3) Partner Intro Call Script (10–15 minutes)

### Goal
Book a follow-up demo OR close a pilot commitment (minimum 10 accounts) contingent on onboarding readiness.

### Talk Track
1) **Set context (30s)**
“Appreciate the time. This is a white-label add-on you can attach to your lead gen: instant SMS + AI qualification + booking. We’re optimizing speed-to-lead because local buyers choose whoever responds first.”

2) **Qualify the agency (3 min)**
- “What verticals do you focus on?”
- “Roughly how many active clients are you driving leads for right now?”
- “Do you manage their forms/FB lead ads/CRM or just send leads?”
- “What’s the #1 complaint you hear about lead quality or close rate?”
- “Do your clients have a scheduler/dispatcher, or do they book calls?”

3) **Pain + impact (2 min)**
“Speed-to-lead is usually the silent killer. If you’re replying in minutes instead of seconds, you lose the highest-intent leads. We text instantly, ask 2–4 questions, then route/book — so your clients close more from the same ad spend.”

4) **How it works (2 min)**
- Trigger: form/lead arrives
- Instant SMS: “Hey {{Name}}, this is {{Company}} — quick question…”
- AI qualification: 2–4 questions (service, urgency, location, budget, etc.)
- Outcome: booked appointment OR routed to dispatcher OR marked unqualified

5) **Resell model + commitment (2 min)**
“Partners typically charge $499–$999/mo per location. Our partner cost is $299–$499 depending on volume. For priority support and fast rollout we ask a **10-account minimum** (you can start with your best-fit clients).”

6) **Close (1–3 min)**
Option A (soft): “Should we schedule a 20-min working session to map your first 10 accounts and send the kit?”
Option B (direct): “If we can onboard your first 10 in the next 14 days, are you comfortable starting on the 10-account plan?”

### Objection handles
- **“We already use a CRM automations tool.”**
“Perfect — we’re complementary. CRMs usually send generic follow-ups; we focus on *instant reply + qualification + booking* and reporting on response/contact/qualified/booked rates.”
- **“Will this hurt deliverability/feel spammy?”**
“We keep it conversational, short, and question-driven. It’s positioned as a human-like response from the business. The KPI report shows contact + opt-out so you can monitor.”
- **“We need proof.”**
“We’ll start with your most responsive vertical and measure: median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate. If those don’t improve, you’ll know within the first month.”
