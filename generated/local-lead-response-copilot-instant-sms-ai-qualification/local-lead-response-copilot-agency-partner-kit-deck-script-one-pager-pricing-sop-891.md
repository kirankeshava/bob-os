D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY-FACING, 48-HOUR GO-LIVE)

Objective
Launch Instant Lead Response + Qualification + Booking within 48 hours of receiving all inputs.

Step 1 — Partner Intake (Agency completes)
Send to: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
Provide for EACH client:
1) Business name + location(s)
2) Primary services offered (top 3)
3) Service area (zip/cities)
4) Hours of operation + after-hours rules
5) Calendar/booking link or preferred booking method
6) Lead sources to connect (FB Lead Ads, website forms, etc.)
7) Required qualifiers (budget, timeframe, job type, address/zip)
8) Disqualifiers (out of area, renters, job minimum, etc.)
9) Tone/voice (friendly/professional) + compliance notes
10) Escalation contacts (name + phone/email) for hot leads

Step 2 — Build the Conversation Flow
- Create opening text template
- Build 3–5 qualifying questions with branching:
  - Timeframe (urgent/this week/later)
  - Location (zip)
  - Job type (category)
  - Optional: budget / property type
- Decide routing:
  - Qualified → booking link OR request preferred call time
  - Not qualified → polite close + tag
  - No response → 2–4 follow-ups over 24–72 hours

Step 3 — Connect Lead Sources
- Verify form fields mapping (name, phone, service)
- Test with internal lead submission
- Confirm SMS deliverability

Step 4 — Booking & Handoff
- If booking link: confirm it works on mobile and has available slots
- If “call me” routing: collect best time + notify client/dispatcher
- Apply lead statuses automatically

Step 5 — QA Checklist (Before Launch)
- New lead triggers within 60 seconds
- Correct business name + tone
- Qualifying branches behave correctly
- Booking link correct and tracked
- After-hours behavior correct
- Status tagging correct

Step 6 — Go-Live + First Week Optimization
- Day 1 review: response time, reply rate, top drop-off question
- Adjust question order to reduce friction
- Confirm booked leads are showing
