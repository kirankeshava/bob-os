# 3) “3×10 Accounts” 30-Day Agency Plan (Volume Growth Playbook)
Goal: **30 client accounts in 30 days** via **3 partner agencies × 10 accounts each**.

## Offer Positioning (What agencies sell)
“Instantly text every new lead in <60 seconds, qualify them with 3–6 questions, then book calls/appointments automatically. You get faster contact, more qualified conversations, and fewer wasted follow-ups.”

## Pipeline Math (Simple)
To land 10 accounts in 30 days, a typical agency needs:
- 100 targeted outreach touches/week (email + LinkedIn + calls)
- 20 responses/week
- 8 booked demos/week
- 2–3 closes/week

## Week-by-Week Execution
### Week 1 — Seed + Fast Wins (Target: 2 accounts)
- Pick one niche to start: plumbing, HVAC, roofing, pest control, garage doors, junk removal, med spas.
- Build a list of 80–120 businesses already running lead ads (FB/Google) in your market.
- Use a 2-line pitch: “You’re paying for leads. We text them in <60 seconds and qualify + book them. Want a free 7-day trial?”
- Close 2 accounts with “trial + install” urgency.

### Week 2 — Prove Lift (Target: +3 accounts)
- Use early KPI proof: response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate.
- Ask each new client for 1 referral (“Who else in your network is buying leads?”).
- Launch 3 more accounts using the Intake Form + SOP.

### Week 3 — Bundle Into Retainers (Target: +3 accounts)
- Package as “Speed-to-Lead System” add-on to PPC/SEO.
- Raise resale price toward $799–$999 with clear ROI framing.
- Add light conversion optimization: refine qualifiers, tweak booking CTA.

### Week 4 — Scale + Lock Commitments (Target: +2 accounts)
- Offer multi-location pricing.
- Convert trial accounts into standard agreements.
- Move clients to a monthly KPI cadence and operational rhythm.

## Agency Commercials (Recommended)
- Agency pays Provider: **$299–$499/account** (tiered)
- Agency charges client: **$499–$999/account**
- Gross margin target: **$200–$500/account/month**
- At 10 accounts: $2k–$5k monthly gross margin per agency line.

## Operational Cadence (So you don’t drown)
- Day 0: Intake Form submitted
- Day 1: Build + QA
- Day 2: Launch
- Day 7: Optimize qualifiers based on replies + booked rate
- Monthly: KPI report + 15-min review call
