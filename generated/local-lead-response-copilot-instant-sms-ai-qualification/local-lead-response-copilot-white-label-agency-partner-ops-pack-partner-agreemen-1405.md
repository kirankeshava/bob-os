# 2) Agency Onboarding Checklist + 7‑Day Implementation Timeline

## A) What Partner Collects From the End Client (Intake)
**Business & goals**
- Business name, website, service area(s)
- Services to prioritize (top 3)
- Target lead types to accept vs. reject
- Operating hours + after-hours handling preference

**Lead sources (pick all)**
- Website form(s) URL + field list + where submissions go today
- Facebook Lead Ads (page access/connection details)
- Other: Google LSA, CallRail, Zapier, webhook, CRM forms

**Booking & handoff**
- Booking method: Calendly/GoHighLevel/Google Calendar/other
- Who receives booked appointments (name/email/phone)
- If “call now” route: sales rep phone number(s)

**Messaging**
- Brand voice: friendly/professional/direct
- Required compliance lines (opt-out, etc.)
- Qualification questions (max 3–5) and disqualifiers

**Reporting**
- Monthly reporting recipient email(s)
- Definition of “Qualified” and “Booked” for this business

## B) Partner Internal Setup Checklist (Runbook)
1. Create new account record (Client name + niche + timezone)
2. Connect lead source(s)
3. Configure instant SMS trigger (0–15 seconds target)
4. Install qualification flow (3–5 questions)
5. Configure lead statuses: New, Contacted, Engaged, Qualified, Booked, Not Qualified, No Response
6. Configure booking route (link or calendar) + confirmation message
7. Configure fallback route (no response → 2 nudges + closeout)
8. QA test with 3 sample leads (business hours + after hours)
9. Launch + monitor first 48 hours
10. Schedule 15‑minute optimization review at day 7

## C) 7‑Day Timeline (Agency Execution)
**Day 0 (Kickoff, 20 minutes)**
- Confirm lead sources, booking method, hours, and qualification criteria.

**Day 1 (Build)**
- Connect sources and deploy the qualification flow.
- Draft the initial message set (first text + questions + booking prompt + opt-out).

**Day 2 (QA + Compliance)**
- Run test leads end-to-end.
- Confirm opt-out language and routing.

**Day 3 (Launch)**
- Turn on for live leads.
- Watch first 10 leads; fix edge cases.

**Day 4–5 (Optimize)**
- Adjust Q1/Q2 to increase contact rate.
- Tighten qualification to reduce wasted bookings.

**Day 6 (Report Snapshot)**
- Pull early KPI baseline: response time + contact rate.

**Day 7 (Client Review Call, 15 minutes)**
- Show wins, set expectations, decide next optimization (e.g., different first message, shorter flow, different routing).
