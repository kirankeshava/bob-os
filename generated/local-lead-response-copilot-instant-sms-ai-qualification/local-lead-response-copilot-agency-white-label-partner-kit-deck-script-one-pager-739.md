C) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY + CLIENT ONBOARDING)

Objective: Launch each client account in 48–72 hours with consistent scripts, routing, and reporting.

ROLES
- Agency: owns client relationship, pricing, approvals, calendar access
- Copilot team: config, QA tests, go-live monitoring

STEP 1 — CLIENT INTAKE (15 minutes)
Collect:
1) Business name, service area ZIPs/cities
2) Services offered + “not a fit” criteria
3) Business hours + after-hours handling (capture + book next day vs emergency)
4) Primary phone(s) for handoff + backup contact
5) Booking method:
   - Calendar link (preferred)
   - “Call now” routing
   - Form to collect details then schedule
6) Qualification questions (3–6):
   - Service type
   - ZIP
   - Timing/urgency
   - Residential/commercial
   - Budget range (optional)
   - Photos (optional)
7) Compliance preferences (opt-out language, disclaimers)

STEP 2 — CONNECT LEAD SOURCE (30–60 minutes)
Pick one:
- Web form → webhook/Zapier/CRM integration
- Facebook Lead Ads → connector
- CRM pipeline (HubSpot/GoHighLevel/etc.) → new lead trigger
Confirm required fields: name, phone, lead source, service requested.

STEP 3 — SCRIPTING (20 minutes)
Provide:
- First SMS opener (friendly, brand voice)
- Question sequence
- Branching rules:
  - If out-of-area → tag “Unqualified: Out of Area”
  - If wrong service → tag “Unqualified: Wrong Service”
  - If urgent and in-hours → “Call now” + notify rep
  - If qualified → booking link + reminders
- Opt-out line: “Reply STOP to opt out.”

STEP 4 — ROUTING & NOTIFICATIONS (15 minutes)
Set:
- Who gets notified on: New lead, Qualified, Booked, Urgent
- Channels: SMS/email (as available)
- Business hours rules + escalation

STEP 5 — QA TESTS (30 minutes)
Run at least 5 test leads:
- Qualified lead (normal hours)
- Qualified lead (after hours)
- Out-of-area
- Wrong service
- No response
Validate:
- Response time recorded
- Correct tags
- Booking link works
- Notifications delivered

STEP 6 — GO-LIVE (same day)
- Turn on triggers
- Monitor first 10 live leads
- Fix wording/logic quickly based on real replies

STEP 7 — WEEK 1 OPTIMIZATION (15 minutes)
Review early metrics:
- Are people replying?
- Are questions too long?
- Is booking friction high?
Iterate scripts: shorten, reorder questions, add “call now” earlier for high intent.

MONTHLY OPS
- Export KPI report
- Review lead status breakdown
- Propose 1–2 tests (wording, offer, routing)
