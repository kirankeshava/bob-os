# 30-Day “3×10 Accounts” Partner Execution Plan (Volume Reseller Playbook)
Goal: Land **30 active client accounts in 30 days** across **3 partner agencies** or inside one agency via a focused push. This aligns with wholesale **$299–$499/account** and retail **$499–$999/account**.

## Positioning (what you sell)
“**Instant lead response + AI qualification** so your team only talks to real buyers, faster. We reduce response time to under 60 seconds, lift contact rates, and book more jobs without hiring more office staff.”

## Weekly Activity Targets (per agency)
- **Week 1:** 30 outreach conversations → 10 demos → 3 closes
- **Week 2:** 40 outreach conversations → 12 demos → 4 closes
- **Week 3:** 50 outreach conversations → 15 demos → 5 closes
- **Week 4:** 60 outreach conversations → 18 demos → 8 closes
Total target: ~20 closes per agency; with 1–3 agencies, hit 30+ accounts.

## Offer Stack (simple)
- Setup included (or setup fee optional at retail)
- “Go live in 2–5 days” promise (when intake complete)
- KPI reporting every month: median response time, contact/qualified/booked rates

## Fast Close Structure (recommended)
1) Diagnose speed-to-lead: “How long until you text/call a new lead?”
2) Quantify: missed leads/week × avg job value × close rate
3) Show the fix: instant SMS + qualification + booking
4) Risk reversal: month-to-month; cancel anytime with 30 days notice

## Rollout Sequencing (to prevent ops overload)
- Batch launches in waves of 3–5 accounts every 2–3 days.
- Use the Intake Form checklist above; no intake = not scheduled.

## KPI Targets to Promise (reasonable)
- Median response time: **< 60 seconds**
- Contact rate lift: **+20–50%** (varies by niche/lead source)
- Qualified rate: set baseline after 2 weeks
- Booked rate: improve via tighter questions + scheduling friction removal

## Monthly Business Review (MBR) Agenda (15 minutes)
1. KPI snapshot (see template)
2. Top disqualification reasons (tighten targeting)
3. No-response leads (adjust timing + follow-up cadence)
4. Booking optimization (calendar availability + handoff)
