## D) Implementation SOP (Agency Playbook)

### Overview
Goal: get each new client live in **24–72 hours** with a clean, measurable funnel.

### Step 1 — Intake (15–20 minutes)
Collect:
1) Business name + service category
2) Service area (zip/cities) and hours
3) Booking method: calendar link OR “call now” number
4) Qualifying questions (3–6):
   - job type
   - urgency (today/this week)
   - location/zip
   - budget range (optional)
   - photos (optional)
5) Escalation rules: when to notify a human
6) Disqualifiers: outside area, wrong job type, renters vs owners, etc.

### Step 2 — Connect Lead Sources
Choose at least one:
- Website form submission (webhook/Zapier-like)
- FB leads export/CRM intake
- CRM lead created trigger

### Step 3 — Configure Conversation Flow
- Write opener text (friendly, short, clear)
- Add 3–6 Qs with branching
- Add booking CTA (link) or “call now” escalation
- Set lead status labels:
  - New
  - Contacted
  - Qualified
  - Booked
  - Not Qualified
  - No Response

### Step 4 — QA (Must‑Do)
- Submit 3 test leads
- Validate response time
- Confirm correct branching
- Confirm booking + notifications
- Confirm statuses apply correctly

### Step 5 — Launch
- Turn on live triggers
- Send client “What to expect” note:
  - leads get instant text
  - your team gets notifications
  - how to handle escalations

### Step 6 — Optimize Weekly (First 30 days)
- Week 1: shorten questions if drop-off
- Week 2: adjust opener + urgency framing
- Week 3: refine disqualifiers to reduce junk
- Week 4: add “call now” for hot leads
