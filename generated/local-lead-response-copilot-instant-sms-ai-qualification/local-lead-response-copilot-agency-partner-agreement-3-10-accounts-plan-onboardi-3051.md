# 3×10 Accounts in 30 Days — Partner Execution Plan (Agency Playbook)

## Goal
Enroll **3 agencies** each with **10 client accounts** (or one agency ramping to 30) for rapid scale. This aligns with the partner pricing model ($299–$499 paid to Provider; $499–$999 charged to clients).

## Core pitch
“New leads get an instant text in <60 seconds, get qualified in 2–4 questions, and appointments get booked automatically. Speed-to-lead wins in home services.”

## Your weekly targets (per agency)
**Week 1 (Days 1–7):** Close 2–3 client accounts (fastest vertical first)
- Outreach: 100–150 past/prospect clients, 30–50 warm DMs, 10–20 short calls
- Offer: **7-day free launch + KPI baseline** (no risk)

**Week 2:** Add 3–4 more accounts
- Aim: introduce “speed-to-lead audit” using last 7 days response time + missed lead estimate

**Week 3:** Add 3–4 more accounts
- Push referrals and multi-location upsells

**Week 4:** Optimize + renew
- Use KPI report to prove lift and lock in longer retention

## Simple conversion assumptions (editable)
- 150 outreach touches → 30 replies (20%)
- 30 replies → 12 calls (40%)
- 12 calls → 4 closes (33%)
- 4 closes → 4 accounts live within 7–10 days

## What to report back to Provider weekly (so we can help you win)
Email **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to**:
- # of accounts sold, launched, pending
- Top 3 objections you’re hearing
- Avg client lead volume/week
- Any lead source integrations causing friction
