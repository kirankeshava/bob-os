## A) What this is (for agencies)
Local Lead Response Copilot is a white‑label “speed-to-lead + qualification + booking” system that instantly texts new leads (website forms, FB lead ads, etc.), asks short qualifying questions, then books calls/appointments or routes hot leads to the client.

**What agencies sell:** “Instant response + automated qualification that increases booked jobs without hiring more office staff.”

**What agencies measure:** median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate, and lead status breakdown.

## D) Implementation SOP (agency-facing, condensed)
### Step 1 — Intake (Day 0–1)
Collect:
- Business name, service area, hours, call booking link (or preferred schedule method)
- Lead sources (form URLs, FB lead forms, etc.)
- Qualification questions (3–6 max)
- Disqualifiers (out-of-area, wrong job type, budget minimum)
- Routing rules (call now vs book; who gets notified)

### Step 2 — Build (Day 1–2)
- Set “instant text” trigger for every new lead
- Configure AI-driven short question flow
- Configure outcomes: book link, call now, or notify + tag

### Step 3 — QA (Day 2)
- Test with 10 fake leads
- Verify timing, tags/status, notifications, booking handoff

### Step 4 — Launch (Day 3)
- Turn on for live leads
- Confirm first 5 leads responded in <60 seconds

### Step 5 — Optimize (Week 2+)
- Tighten qualification questions
- Adjust routing for higher contact and booked rate
