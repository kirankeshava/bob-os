# 3) Partner Onboarding Checklist + First 7 Days Launch Plan

**Goal:** Agency can launch first client account in 48–72 hours, then replicate.

## A) Pre-launch checklist (per agency)
1) Confirm expected volume tier (1–2 / 3–9 / 10–29 / 30+)
2) Confirm who owns client communication (agency vs client)
3) Confirm reporting cadence (monthly) + who receives KPI summary
4) Get agency branding preference (white-label name, email signature, etc.)

## B) Client intake checklist (per client account)
Ask the agency/client for:
- Business name, service area(s), business hours
- Primary services + “best jobs” (for qualification)
- Call handling rules (when to book, when to call, when to stop)
- Booking link or scheduling instructions (if any)
- Lead sources to connect (form URL, CRM, FB lead form, etc.)
- Disqualification rules (e.g., outside service area, budget too low)
- After-hours handling (text-only + next-day booking vs emergency routing)

## C) Build steps (Day 1–2)
1) Create client account workspace
2) Configure instant SMS response copy (1st message)
3) Configure qualification flow (2–4 questions max):
   - Job type
   - Location/ZIP
   - Timeframe
   - Optional: budget range / property type
4) Configure lead status labels: New, Contacted, Qualified, Unqualified, Booked, No Response
5) Configure routing rule:
   - Qualified → booking link OR notify for manual call
   - Unqualified → polite closeout + tag

## D) QA steps (Day 2)
- Run 3 test leads end-to-end (normal, unqualified, after-hours)
- Confirm median response time is near-instant in tests
- Confirm booked/notification path works

## E) Launch steps (Day 3)
- Go live on lead sources
- Monitor first 20 leads closely
- Adjust 1 thing only: qualification wording (keep it short)

## F) Optimize (Days 4–7)
- Review KPI snapshot:
  - Median response time
  - Contact rate
  - Qualified rate
  - Booked rate
  - Lead status breakdown
- If contact rate is low: shorten message 1 and question 1
- If qualified rate is too low: loosen disqualifiers
- If booked rate is low: tighten handoff (booking link clarity, after-hours rules)

## G) Monthly reporting (ongoing)
Agency sends client a 1-page summary using the KPI template:
- This month vs last month
- 2 wins
- 2 adjustments for next month
