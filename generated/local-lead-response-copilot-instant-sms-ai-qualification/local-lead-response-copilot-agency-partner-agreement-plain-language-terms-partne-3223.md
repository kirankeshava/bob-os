# Local Lead Response Copilot — Agency Partner Agreement (Plain-Language Terms)

**Version:** v1 (Plain-language, non-legal)

This Agency Partner Agreement (“Agreement”) explains how agencies (“Partner”) can resell **Local Lead Response Copilot (Instant SMS + AI Qualification)** (“Service”) to end-clients (“Client”). This is intended to be simple and readable. It is not legal advice.

**Service & Purpose**  
The Service instantly responds to new leads (forms / FB lead ads / web chat) via SMS, asks short AI-driven qualification questions, and routes outcomes (booked appointment, booked call, or qualified handoff) to the Client.

## 1) Partner Model (White-Label / Resell)
- Partner may present the Service as part of Partner’s offer (“white-label”).
- Partner is responsible for Client billing and relationship management (unless otherwise agreed).
- We provide the underlying automation, qualification flows, routing, and reporting framework.

## 2) Wholesale Pricing, Volume Tiers, and Minimum Commitment (Aligned to 3×10 Plan)
Wholesale fees are charged **per active client account per month**.

**Wholesale (what Partner pays us):**  
- **Starter (1–3 accounts): $499/account/mo**  
- **Growth (4–9 accounts): $399/account/mo**  
- **Scale (10–24 accounts): $349/account/mo**  
- **Partner Pro (25+ accounts): $299/account/mo**

**Suggested Retail (what Partner charges Client):**  
- **$499–$999/account/mo** depending on niche, lead volume, and booking workflow.

**Minimum Commitment for Priority Support:**  
- To receive **Priority Support** (faster response times + implementation help), Partner commits to **a minimum of 10 active accounts for 60 days** (“10×60”).  
- If Partner drops below 10 active accounts during the 60-day term, pricing reverts to the tier that matches current active accounts and Priority Support may be paused.

**3×10 Accounts Plan (Revenue Path):**  
Partner goal = onboard **30 active accounts** via 3 cohorts of 10 accounts (Weeks 1–4). Partner earns margin by charging retail ($499–$999) while paying wholesale ($299–$499).

## 3) Week 1 Free Launch Policy (Customer Trial)
During the first 7 days of rollout, the Service is offered **free to Clients** as a launch/trial. Partner may position this as a “7-day speed-to-lead pilot.” After pilot, Client converts to paid retail with Partner.

## 4) What’s Included vs Not Included
**Included (per active account):**
- Lead-to-SMS instant response workflow
- AI qualification (short question flow)
- Routing rules (booked / qualified / not qualified / unreachable)
- Basic reporting fields and monthly KPI template
- Reasonable support for setup/QA

**Not included (unless explicitly added):**
- Ad management, landing page building, creative, or lead generation
- CRM licensing fees (Client/Partner covers their CRM)
- Phone numbers/SMS fees if a third-party platform charges separately (handled when we connect tools; we prefer free tiers where available)

## 5) Implementation Responsibilities (Partner vs Us)
**Partner provides (per Client):**
- Client business info: hours, service area, offers, qualification criteria
- Booking link or calendar rules (or “call now” handoff)
- Lead sources to connect (form tool, FB lead ads, etc.)
- Destination routing: SMS number(s), email(s), CRM fields

**We provide:**
- Build the qualification flow + routing logic
- Configure integrations as available in the stack
- QA test with sample leads
- Launch checklist and monitoring guidance

## 6) Reporting & KPIs (Monthly)
Partners agree to track and share core KPIs with Clients monthly:
- **Median response time** (lead submission → first outbound SMS)
- **Contact rate** (% of leads that reply / are reached)
- **Qualified rate** (% that meet criteria)
- **Booked rate** (% booked appointment/call)
- **Lead status breakdown** (New, Contacted, Unreachable, Qualified, Not Qualified, Booked)

We provide a reporting template that Partner can copy into Google Sheets.

## 7) Support & SLAs
- Standard support is handled by email: **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to**
- **Standard Support:** responses typically within 1–2 business days.
- **Priority Support (10×60 minimum):** responses typically within 4–8 business hours (business days), plus help troubleshooting launch issues.

## 8) Acceptable Use
Partner and Client agree not to use the Service for:
- Illegal products/services, harassment, or deceptive messaging
- Sending messages without a legitimate lead opt-in
- Purchased lists or cold SMS without consent

If we detect abuse, we may pause accounts to protect deliverability.

## 9) Data Handling (Plain-Language)
- We process lead data to deliver qualification and routing.
- Partner is responsible for ensuring Client has proper consent language on lead forms/ads.
- We do not sell lead data.
- Partner/Client can request deletion of Client data when the account is closed (reasonable time required).

## 10) Billing, Pausing, and Cancellation
- Wholesale fees are billed monthly based on **active accounts**.
- Partner can pause an account (no automation running). Paused accounts may still incur minimal maintenance depending on the tech stack; we will confirm before any charges apply.
- Either party may cancel with **14 days notice**. Cancellation ends support and disables automations after the notice period.

## 11) Positioning & Claims
Partner agrees to avoid absolute guarantees. Approved language:
- “Improves speed-to-lead and increases the chance of contacting leads while they’re still shopping.”
- “Designed to reduce lead leakage and increase booked calls/appointments.”

## 12) How to Get Started
Email **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to** with:
- Partner website + niche
- Target lead sources (forms/FB lead ads)
- Expected monthly volume
- First 1–3 clients for launch cohort
