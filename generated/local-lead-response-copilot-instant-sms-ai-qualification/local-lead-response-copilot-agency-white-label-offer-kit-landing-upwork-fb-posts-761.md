## How it works (simple)
1) **Lead comes in** (form/FB lead ad/webhook).
2) **Instant SMS** goes out: “Hey {{name}}, it’s {{company}} — quick question so we can help…”
3) **AI qualification** asks 3–5 short questions (service needed, location, urgency, budget range, best time).
4) **Booking or handoff**:
   - Book to Calendly (or your scheduler)
   - Or route to the right dispatcher/rep
5) **CRM notes**: all answers + conversation summary logged to your CRM/Google Sheet.
