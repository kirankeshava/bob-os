## 2) Purpose (White‑Label Resale)
Partner will resell Copilot as a managed add-on (white-label/agency-branded optional) to Partner’s clients (“End Clients”) to improve speed-to-lead via instant SMS + automated qualification and booking.

## 3) Offer Summary (What Partner Is Selling)
Copilot provides:
- Instant SMS to new leads (from forms/FB leads/CRM integrations where available)
- AI-driven qualification (short question flow)
- Routing outcomes: book call/appointment, request more info, mark unqualified, or handoff to human
- Reporting metrics: median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate, and lead status breakdown

Partner may package Copilot with additional services (ads, landing pages, call answering, CRM, etc.).

## 4) Pricing, Margins, and Volume Tiers (Agency → Copilot)
Partner pricing to Copilot (per End Client account, per month) is based on active accounts.

**Base (1–2 accounts):** $499/account/month

**Growth (3–9 accounts):** $399/account/month

**Scale (10–24 accounts):** $349/account/month

**Partner Max (25+ accounts):** $299/account/month

### Minimum Commitment for Priority Support
To receive **Priority Support** (see Section 8), Partner commits to:
- Maintain **10+ active accounts** OR
- A minimum monthly commitment equivalent to **10 accounts at the current tier**.

If Partner drops below minimum for two consecutive billing cycles, Copilot may move Partner to Standard Support and/or adjust pricing tier.

### Recommended Partner Resale Pricing (Agency → End Client)
Partner is encouraged to charge:
- **$499–$999/account/month** depending on niche, lead volume, and included services.

## 5) Trial / Week 1 Free Launch Policy
For the first 7 days from the end client’s go-live, Partner may offer a **free trial** to end clients at Partner’s discretion. Copilot’s internal policy for Week 1 is **$0 spend**; Partner can deploy using free-tier tools or existing client systems.

## 6) Billing, Invoicing, and Payment Terms
- Billing frequency: **Monthly in advance**.
- Invoicing method: email invoice sent to Partner’s billing contact.
- Payment due: **Net 7** from invoice date unless otherwise agreed.
- Late payment: accounts may be paused after **14 days** overdue.

(If required later, Copilot can add automated payment collection; no paid tooling is required for initial partners.)

## 7) Responsibilities
### Copilot Responsibilities
- Provision and maintain the Copilot system for each End Client account.
- Implement the agreed qualification flow, routing, and booking link/CRM handoff per SOP.
- Provide monthly KPI reporting template and data exports where applicable.
- Provide support per Section 8.

### Partner Responsibilities
- Own the End Client relationship, onboarding, and primary communication.
- Ensure End Client provides required assets (numbers, calendars, questions, intake rules, compliance approvals).
- Ensure End Client understands SMS compliance requirements and obtains consent where required.
- Provide accurate configuration inputs and timely approvals to launch.

## 8) Support and SLA (Simple)
### Standard Support
- Email support to **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to**
- Response target: **within 2 business days**

### Priority Support (10+ accounts or minimum commitment)
- Email support with priority handling
- Response target: **within 1 business day**
- Implementation queue priority for new End Client launches

### Uptime / Service Expectations
Copilot targets practical reliability for SMB lead response. If a material service outage occurs, Copilot will communicate status and remediation steps to Partner.

## 9) Data, Privacy, and Lead Ownership
- End Client leads belong to the End Client / Partner relationship.
- Copilot processes lead/contact data only to deliver the service.
- Partner agrees not to request or use Copilot access to improperly obtain or reuse another End Client’s data.
- If End Client requests deletion/export, Partner will route request to Copilot and confirm identity.

## 10) Messaging Compliance (Partner/End Client)
Partner is responsible for ensuring End Clients:
- Have appropriate consent/opt-in language on forms and ads.
- Honor opt-out requests.
- Avoid prohibited content where applicable.

Copilot can include opt-out language (e.g., “Reply STOP to opt out”) if required.

## 11) Term, Termination, and Offboarding
- Term: month-to-month unless otherwise agreed.
- Either party may terminate with **30 days written notice**.
- For nonpayment, Copilot may pause accounts after the timelines in Section 6.

### Offboarding
Upon termination, Copilot will:
- Provide final KPI summary for the period (if requested)
- Disable automations and stop messaging
- Provide export of conversation logs where technically feasible and permitted

## 12) Non-Solicitation (Lightweight)
Copilot agrees not to actively solicit Partner’s End Clients for competing services during the active term of this Agreement, except where required for support/implementation.

## 13) Brand / White‑Label Usage
Partner may:
- Present the solution as “powered by Local Lead Response Copilot” OR fully white-label it as part of Partner’s service.
- Use Copilot-provided deck/one-pager/SOP content in sales materials.

Partner may not:
- Misrepresent Copilot capabilities
- Claim ownership of Copilot software
