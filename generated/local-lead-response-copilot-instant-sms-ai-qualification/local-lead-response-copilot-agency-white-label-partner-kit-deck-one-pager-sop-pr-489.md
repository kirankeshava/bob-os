SLIDE 5 — How It Works (Simple Flow)
1) Lead hits a form (website form, FB lead ad, landing page)
2) Instant SMS: “Hey {Name}, got your request—can I ask 2 quick questions?”
3) AI qualification (2–5 questions): job type, timeline, zip/service area, budget range, urgency
4) Outcome routing:
   • Booked appointment (Calendly/booking link)
   • Call connect (“Reply CALL” / call now)
   • Create a hot lead summary + push to CRM
5) Team gets notified + sees lead status.

1) Pre-Sale Qualification (Agency)
Goal: only sell to clients where speed-to-lead matters.
Checklist:
• Client gets leads from forms, FB lead ads, Google LSA, or landing pages
• Client values booked calls/appointments
• Client can take calls or has a booking calendar
• Client has service area constraints (zip codes/cities) to qualify
• Client agrees to respond to hot leads quickly (or accepts auto-booking)

2) Offer Packaging (Agency)
Recommend you sell one of these:
A) “Instant Lead Response + Booking” — $799/mo (most common)
B) “After-Hours Lead Capture” — $599/mo (good for small shops)
C) “Full Qualification + Booking + CRM Handoff” — $999/mo
Optional setup fee (agency keeps): $250–$750

3) Client Intake Form (Agency collects, then sends)
Collect the following and email to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to:
• Business name + location(s)
• Primary contact name + phone
• Business hours + after-hours instructions
• Service area (cities/zips) + disqualifiers
• Services offered (list) + top 3 priority services
• Ideal customer + typical job types
• Booking method:
  - Booking link (Calendly etc.) OR
  - Call connect number/route instructions
• Lead sources to connect:
  - Website form URL + field list OR
  - FB lead form name/page OR
  - Zapier/Make webhook availability
• SMS tone/brand voice (friendly/professional)
• Compliance notes (opt-in language on forms if applicable)

4) Connection & Setup (Copilot team)
We will:
• Connect lead intake (form/FB/Zap/webhook)
• Configure instant SMS + question flow
• Configure qualification logic + lead statuses
• Configure booking/call handoff
• Configure notifications to client/team
• Set reporting metrics and baseline

5) QA Checklist (Agency + Client)
Before go-live, validate:
• Test lead submits successfully
• SMS delivered within 30 seconds
• Questions are clear and on-brand
• Disqualified leads are tagged correctly
• Qualified leads receive booking/call instructions
• Internal notifications fire to correct recipient(s)

6) Launch Day Script (Agency)
Send to client (email/SMS):
“Your instant lead response + booking is live. Submit a test lead now, and you’ll get a text immediately. For best results, reply to hot lead notifications within 5 minutes during business hours.”

7) First Week Optimization (Agency)
After 3–5 days:
• Review early data: contact rate + qualified rate
• Reduce friction: shorten to 2–3 questions if drop-off high
• Update disqualifiers and service-area filters
• Add a fast ‘CALL’ option for urgent services

8) Ongoing Monthly Operations (Agency)
Every month:
• Export KPI report (template below)
• Share results + 1–2 recommendations:
  - tighten questions
  - route after-hours differently
  - increase booking availability
• Use report to defend retainers and expand to more locations.
