# Partner Launch Pack (48-Hour Rollout Checklist)
**Goal:** go from “new client signed” to “live + texting leads” in 2 business days.

## A) Assets to Collect from End Client (15 minutes)
1. Business name + service area(s)
2. Ideal lead types + disqualified lead types
3. Office hours + after-hours handling preference
4. Phone number(s) for call routing / booking
5. Booking link (Calendly, booking page) OR preferred appointment workflow
6. Top 5 FAQs + top 5 objections from leads
7. Required compliance line (brand name + opt-out)
8. Offer hook (e.g., “$79 service call,” “same-day estimate,” “financing available”)

## B) Consent + Form Requirements (must-have)
Partner confirms forms/ads include:
- Checkbox or clear disclosure for SMS consent
- “By submitting, you agree to receive texts…” language
- Link to privacy policy (if available)

## C) Implementation Steps (Provider/Partner)
**Step 1: Intake (Day 1)**
- Create client workspace
- Add lead source(s): form webhook / CRM / FB lead routing
- Configure routing number(s) + booking

**Step 2: Qualification Flow (Day 1)**
- Write/approve 3–6 question flow:
  - job type
  - timeline
  - location
  - budget range (optional)
  - decision maker (optional)
- Define “Qualified” thresholds and actions:
  - Qualified → book / call
  - Not qualified → polite close
  - Unknown → notify sales

**Step 3: QA Testing (Day 2)**
- Run 5 test leads:
  - Qualified scenario
  - Not qualified scenario
  - No-response scenario
  - After-hours scenario
  - Duplicate lead scenario
- Confirm:
  - SMS sends instantly
  - Opt-out works
  - Notifications fire correctly

**Step 4: Go-Live (Day 2)**
- Turn on for production leads
- Confirm first 3 real leads processed

## D) First-Week Optimization (15 minutes/day)
- Review transcripts
- Identify drop-off question(s)
- Tighten wording for clarity
- Adjust qualifying threshold if over/under qualifying

## E) “Done-For-You” Client Handoff Script (Partner → End Client)
“Your leads will now get an instant text within seconds. The system asks a few quick questions to confirm the job, urgency, and location, then it either books them or routes them to your team. We’ll review results weekly and send a monthly KPI report showing response time, contact rate, qualified rate, and booked rate.”

# KPI Definitions (So Every Agency Reports the Same Way)
Use these in your monthly report and in client check-ins.

## 1) Median Response Time
**Definition:** median time from lead creation to first outbound SMS sent.
- Target: **< 60 seconds** (ideal: < 15 seconds)

## 2) Contact Rate
**Definition:** % of leads that respond to at least one SMS message.
- Formula: contacted leads / total leads

## 3) Qualified Rate
**Definition:** % of total leads marked “Qualified” by the qualification flow.
- Formula: qualified leads / total leads

## 4) Booked Rate
**Definition:** % of total leads that booked a call/appointment OR were successfully connected.
- Formula: booked leads / total leads

## 5) Lead Status Breakdown
Report counts and % for:
- New (received)
- Messaged
- Contacted
- Qualified
- Not Qualified
- Booked
- No Response
- Opted Out

## Suggested Benchmarks (Directional)
- Contact rate: **25–45%** (varies by vertical + lead source)
- Qualified rate: **15–35%**
- Booked rate: **5–20%**

## Monthly Narrative (2 sentences)
1) What improved vs last month (response time, contact, booked)
2) What you will change next month (copy tweak, question order, routing/after-hours)
