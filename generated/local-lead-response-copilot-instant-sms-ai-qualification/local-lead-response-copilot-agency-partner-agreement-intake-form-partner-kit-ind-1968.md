> This is a plain-language agreement intended to make execution simple. It is not legal advice.

## 1) What you are reselling
LLRC is an “Instant SMS + AI Qualification” response system that: (a) texts new leads immediately after form/FB lead events, (b) asks a short sequence of qualifying questions, (c) routes qualified leads to booking/call requests, and (d) provides reporting on lead outcomes and speed-to-lead.

## 2) White-label / resale rights
- You may resell LLRC to your clients as a managed service under your brand (“white-label”).
- You may represent yourself as the primary vendor to the client.
- You must not represent LLRC as your proprietary software if asked directly; the correct phrasing is: “We use a white-labeled response system we manage for you.”
- You may not resell access to LLRC to other resellers without written permission.

## 3) Pricing, margins, and commitments (wholesale)
**Wholesale fees (what you pay LLRC):**
- **Tier A:** $499 / account / month (1–2 accounts)
- **Tier B:** $399 / account / month (3–9 accounts)
- **Tier C:** $299 / account / month (10+ accounts)

**Agency retail guidance (what you charge clients):** $499–$999 / account / month depending on niche, lead volume, and whether you include ad management.

**Minimum commitment (for priority support + wholesale pricing):**
- To receive Tier B or Tier C pricing, Partner commits to a **minimum of 3 active client accounts** for at least **60 days**.
- If Partner drops below the minimum, pricing reverts to the next applicable tier starting the next billing cycle.

**3×10 Accounts Plan (partner growth target):**
- Goal: close **3 agencies** who each place **10 client accounts** on LLRC (30 accounts total).  
- Economics example (Tier C): agency pays $299, charges $799 → **$500 gross margin/account/month**. At 10 accounts: **$5,000/mo margin per agency**, while LLRC earns $2,990/mo per agency.

## 4) Free launch policy (Week 1 / trials)
If applicable during a free launch window, Partner may onboard clients at no cost for an agreed trial period. Any trial must still follow the intake process and usage limits. After trial, accounts convert to paid wholesale tiers.

## 5) What’s included (standard service)
Per client account, LLRC provides:
- Lead capture connection support (forms/CRM/webhook as applicable)
- Instant SMS response + qualification flow (short question sequence)
- Routing rules: qualified vs unqualified vs unreachable
- Basic appointment/call handoff instructions (e.g., “Book now” link or notify sales)
- Monthly reporting export/summary (see KPI template)

**Not included unless explicitly agreed:** ad management, landing page creation, long-form chatbot builds, custom integrations beyond standard connection methods.

## 6) Implementation responsibilities
**Partner provides:**
- Client intake form completed (see Section 12)
- Confirmation of lead source(s) and where the lead data arrives
- Client-approved message copy, business hours, routing rules
- Confirmation that client has consent/lawful basis to text leads and will honor opt-outs

**LLRC provides:**
- Setup of message flow, qualification steps, tagging/status logic
- Testing plan and “go-live” confirmation
- Post-launch tweaks within the first 7 days (reasonable adjustments)

## 7) Support & service levels (SLA)
- **Standard support channel:** agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- **Response time targets (business days):**
  - P1 (system down / no leads processing): initial response within 4 business hours
  - P2 (partial outage / deliverability issue): initial response within 1 business day
  - P3 (copy changes / tweaks / questions): initial response within 2 business days

## 8) Reporting & KPIs (what we measure)
Partner agrees to track and review these monthly KPIs with clients:
- Median response time (lead created → first SMS sent)
- Contact rate (leads who reply / total leads)
- Qualified rate (qualified / replied)
- Booked rate (booked / qualified)
- Lead status breakdown (New, Contacted, Unreachable, Disqualified, Qualified, Booked)

LLRC will provide the KPI template and will assist with interpretation when asked.
