## 1) One-Page Landing Copy (paste into site/Notion)
### Headline

### Subhead
Local Lead Response Copilot instantly texts every new form/FB lead, asks 3–5 qualifying questions, and books calls/appointments on your calendar (or your client’s). Works especially well for home services and any business where speed-to-lead wins.

### The Problem (why leads “go cold”)
- Most local businesses respond in **hours**, not seconds.
- The first provider to respond usually wins the conversation.
- Staff miss leads after-hours, during jobs, or on weekends.
- Unqualified leads waste time and kill close rates.

### The Promise (simple KPI)
**We guarantee a <60-second first response for every lead** (when connected properly).  
Result: more conversations, more booked calls, less lead waste.

### How It Works (in 4 steps)
1) **Lead comes in** (website form / FB Lead Ads / landing page / webhook)
2) **Instant SMS** goes out in <60 seconds (personalized with name/service)
3) **AI qualification** asks 3–5 short questions (service type, urgency, zip code, budget range, preferred time)
4) **Auto-booking + notes**: qualified leads get a booking link or are directly booked; all answers are logged to your CRM/Google Sheet.

### Example Qualification Flow (3–5 questions)
1. “Quick one—what service do you need? (A) Repair (B) Install (C) Quote”  
2. “Is this urgent? (A) Today (B) This week (C) Just researching”  
3. “What zip code is the job in?”  
4. “Preferred time for a call/appointment? (A) Morning (B) Afternoon (C) Evening”  
5. “Anything we should know before we schedule?”

### Integrations
- **Lead sources:** Web forms, FB Lead Ads, Google Forms, Webhooks (Zapier/Make-compatible)
- **Calendars:** Calendly / Google Calendar
- **CRMs:** HighLevel (GHL), HubSpot, Airtable, Google Sheets, webhook to any CRM

### Compliance / Best Practices (non-negotiables)
- Opt-in language added to forms/ads (“By submitting, you agree to receive texts…”) 
- Easy opt-out (“Reply STOP to unsubscribe”) 
- Quiet hours + throttling available
- Message templates are reviewed with you before going live
