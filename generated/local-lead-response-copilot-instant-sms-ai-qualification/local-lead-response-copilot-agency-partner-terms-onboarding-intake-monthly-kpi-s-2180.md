## 1) Parties + Scope
This Partner Agreement (“Agreement”) is between **Local Lead Response Copilot** (“Provider”) and the signing **Agency/Partner** (“Partner”). Provider supplies a lead response + SMS qualification workflow that instantly contacts new leads, asks short qualifying questions, and routes qualified leads to booking/call scheduling.

## 2) White‑Label + Resale Rights
- Partner may resell the service under Partner’s own brand (“white‑label”) or as co‑branded.
- Partner is responsible for client-facing sales, billing (if Partner bills the end client), and first-line support.
- Provider remains the underlying platform provider and may be disclosed if required for compliance/telecom reasons.

## 3) Pricing, Commitments, and Volume Tiers (Guidance)
**Partner pays Provider (per active client account/month):** $299–$499 depending on tier and volume.  
**Partner charges end client (recommended):** $499–$999/account/month.

### Minimum Commitment for Priority Support
- To receive **priority support**, Partner maintains **minimum 3 active accounts** or a **10-account commitment** (see volume tiers below).

### Volume Discount Tiers (Example)
- **1–2 accounts:** $499/account/mo
- **3–9 accounts:** $399/account/mo
- **10–29 accounts (commitment):** $349/account/mo (10-account minimum commitment)
- **30+ accounts (commitment):** $299/account/mo (30-account minimum commitment)

**3×10 plan (path to $100k/30 days):** Partner recruits **3 agencies × 10 accounts** each. Provider revenue at $349/account on 30 accounts = **$10,470 MRR** from one cluster; scale by additional partner clusters.

## 4) What’s Included (Baseline)
- Instant lead contact via SMS (speed-to-lead)
- AI-driven qualification questions (short, conversational)
- Lead status tagging + routing (e.g., Qualified, Unqualified, No Response)
- Booking/call scheduling handoff (calendar link or appointment workflow)
- Monthly KPI reporting template (Partner sends to clients)

## 5) Implementation Responsibilities
**Partner provides:**
- Client business info (name, service area, hours, offer)
- Lead sources to connect (forms/FB leads/zap/webhooks)
- Qualification rules (what counts as “Qualified”)
- Booking destination (calendar link, phone number, dispatch, etc.)
- Compliance confirmation (see Section 8)

**Provider provides:**

- QA test flow (test lead submission)
- Launch checklist + monitoring period

## 6) Support + SLAs (Plain Language)
- **Standard support:** email via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- **Priority support (commitment required):** faster responses and same/next business day changes when feasible.
- **Response time targets (not guaranteed):**
  - Priority: within 1 business day
  - Standard: within 2 business days

## 7) Billing and Non‑Payment
- Billing is monthly per active account.
- If Partner bills end clients directly, Partner remains responsible for paying Provider on time.
- Provider may pause accounts for non-payment after notice.

## 8) Compliance, Consent, and Acceptable Use
- Partner confirms each end client has proper consent to contact leads via SMS and complies with applicable laws (TCPA/CTIA and local regulations).
- Provider will not be used for spam, purchased lists, or outreach to contacts without consent.
- Partner is responsible for client industry restrictions (e.g., regulated services) and any required disclosures.

## 9) Data Handling + Confidentiality
- Provider processes lead data solely to deliver the service.
- Partner and Provider will keep client data confidential and limit access to authorized personnel.
- Partner is responsible for what is stored in their own CRM/tools.

## 10) Branding + Case Studies
- White-label is allowed. Provider will not contact Partner’s clients unless requested by Partner for support.
- Provider may request anonymized performance stats for internal improvement.

## 11) Term and Termination
- Either party may terminate with notice (suggested: 14 days).
- Upon termination, accounts may be paused and automations disabled on the termination date.
- Partner is responsible for migrating client workflows if leaving.

## 12) Disclaimer
This is a plain-language, non-legal template. Partner should have legal counsel review before using as a binding contract.
