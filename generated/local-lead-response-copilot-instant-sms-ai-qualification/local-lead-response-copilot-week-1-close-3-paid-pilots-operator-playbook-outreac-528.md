## 6) 30-minute onboarding call script (minute-by-minute)
**Outcome:** all fields collected, agreement sent, go-live date/time confirmed.

0–3 min: Confirm objective + permissions
- “We’ll collect your lead source, message tone, qualification Qs, routing, and booking. Then we’ll go live within 24 hours.”

3–8 min: Lead sources + webhook
- Website form provider (WP/Elementor/Webflow/Jotform/etc.)
- FB Lead Ads? Google LSA? Call tracking?
- Where should leads be posted (webhook/Zapier/email parser)?

8–12 min: Business rules
- Business hours (weekday/weekend)
- After-hours behavior (SMS only, next-day booking, emergency routing)
- Service area (zips/cities), exclusions

12–18 min: Qualification questions (3–5)
Pick 3–5 from:
1) “What service do you need?”
2) “What’s your ZIP code?”
3) “When do you need it?”
4) “Are you the property owner?”
5) “Any photos/details?”
6) “Is this an emergency?”
Decide disqualifiers.

18–22 min: Booking + handoff
- Booking link (Calendly/CRM/field service tool)
- Appointment types + durations
- Confirmation + reminder behavior

22–26 min: Routing + notifications
- Who receives qualified leads (names, numbers, emails)
- Round-robin vs primary + backup
- Escalation rule if no response

26–30 min: Close + go-live
- Confirm pricing option (monthly vs annual)
- Confirm go-live target (<24h)
- “I’ll send agreement + intake link. Once intake is submitted, we build and run a live test.”
