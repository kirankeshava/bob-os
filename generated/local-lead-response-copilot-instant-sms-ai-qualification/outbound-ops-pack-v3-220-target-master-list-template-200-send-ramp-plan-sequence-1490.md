SECTION 2) 200-OUTREACH RAMP PLAN (7 DAYS)
Goal: 200 total touches (email first) + LinkedIn connects; focus on reply rate and booked demos.

Deliverability guardrails:
- Warmup emails (first 10–20): NO links, NO attachments, plain text.
- Keep daily volume conservative until bounce rate <3%.
- Do not email role inboxes if avoidable (info@, support@). Prefer direct emails.
- If a message bounces, stop sending to that domain and find a different contact.

Schedule:
Day 1: 10–20 warmups (1:1) + 20 targeted emails (Priority 5/4 only)
Day 2: 40 targeted emails + 20 LinkedIn connect requests
Day 3: 50 targeted emails + 30 LinkedIn connect requests
Day 4: 50 targeted emails
Day 5: 40 targeted emails (finish 200) + follow-ups begin
Day 6–7: Follow-up pushes + reply handling + demo confirmations

Warmup email examples (use real personalization; no links):
- “Quick question about your lead follow-up process”
- “Who handles new leads on your team?”
- “Does your team respond to form leads after hours?”
