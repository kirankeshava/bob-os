# 3×10 Accounts in 30 Days — Agency Execution Plan (Partner Playbook)

Goal: **30 End Client accounts in 30 days** via 3 partner agencies (or one agency with enough volume). This is designed to hit meaningful revenue quickly while keeping delivery manageable.

## The Math (Simple)
- Wholesale target average: **$399/account/mo** (blended)
- 30 accounts → **~$11,970 MRR** wholesale (month 1 run-rate)
- Agency retail average: **$799/account/mo**
- 30 accounts → **~$23,970 MRR** retail for the agency (strong incentive)

## ICP (Who Converts Fast)
Home services + local high-intent leads:
- Roofing, HVAC, plumbing, garage doors, pest control, water damage, cleaning, landscaping, towing
- Any business buying leads where response time determines close rate

## Offer Positioning (One Sentence)
“**We text every new lead in under 60 seconds, qualify them with 3–5 questions, and book jobs automatically—so you stop losing leads to slower competitors.**”

## Week-by-Week Execution

### Week 1 — Close 3 Clients (Proof + Pipeline)
**Target:** 30–50 outreach touches/day (email + LinkedIn + phone)
- Pick one niche (e.g., HVAC) and one geography
- Use the one-pager + deck + a short Loom (optional)
- Pitch a **free 7-day trial** (Week 1 policy) and launch within 48 hours

**Deliverables by end of Week 1:**
- 3 live accounts launched
- Baseline KPI report template populated
- 2 testimonials or quotes about speed-to-lead improvement (even informal)

### Week 2 — Close 7 More (Total 10)
**Target:** 10 demos booked, 7 closes
- Use “speed-to-lead” ROI math and real screenshots/KPIs from week 1
- Add a light guarantee: “If we don’t reduce median response time, we keep optimizing free.”

### Week 3 — Close 10 More (Total 20)
- Introduce simple upsell: missed-call text-back + after-hours routing
- Ask for referrals: “Know two other owners buying leads?”

### Week 4 — Close 10 More (Total 30)
- Tighten onboarding to same-day launches
- Run a “batch launch day” twice/week

## Channel Playbook (Fastest to Revenue)
1) **Agency’s existing client base:** easiest close; position as “conversion lift add-on.”
2) **FB lead-gen agencies:** they feel the pain of clients blaming lead quality; speed-to-lead fixes that.
3) **Local SEO agencies:** add-on for forms + GMB traffic.
4) **Outbound to owners buying leads:** “You’re paying for leads—why let them expire?”

## Sales Assets to Use
- Reseller deck (PDF)
- One-page reseller sheet
- 7-day free trial onboarding email
- KPI report template to prove results

## KPI Targets (What to Promise)
Do **not** promise revenue. Promise controllables:
- Median response time: **< 60 seconds** during business hours
- Contact rate lift: aim **+15–30%**
- Qualified rate: depends on niche; track and improve
- Booked rate: depends on offer + calendar availability; track and improve
