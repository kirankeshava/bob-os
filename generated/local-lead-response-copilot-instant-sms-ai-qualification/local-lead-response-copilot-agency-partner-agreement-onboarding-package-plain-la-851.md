Partner Onboarding Package (Copy/Paste Checklist)

Goal: Get a new client live in 48–72 hours with measurable KPIs and a clean handoff.

A) Pre-Sale (Agency Owner / Sales)
1. Confirm fit (high-intent local leads): home services, med spa, dental, legal intake, etc.
2. Set expectations: “We contact leads in <60 seconds and qualify/book automatically.”
3. Confirm client monthly lead volume range and hours of operation.
4. Choose plan price to client ($499–$999/mo) and your internal margin target.
5. Confirm go-live date and required assets deadline.

B) Client Asset Intake (Agency PM)
Collect and store in one folder:
1. Business name, address, service area, hours
2. Primary phone number (where booked leads should route)
3. Booking link (Calendly / CRM booking page) OR “request a call” workflow details
4. Lead sources list (website form URL, FB lead ads, landing pages, call tracking if any)
5. Offer + qualifiers (price floor, service types, zip codes served, urgency)
6. Disclosures (checkbox + copy) confirming permission to text
7. Brand tone guidelines (friendly/pro, concise, bilingual if needed)

C) Build Specs (Agency PM → Provider)
Email to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to with subject:
“NEW ACCOUNT SETUP — {Agency} — {Client} — Go-live {Date}”
Include:
- Lead source connection details
- Qualification questions (3–6 max) and pass/fail rules
- Booking action: book appointment link OR call request routing
- Fallback rules: if no reply after X minutes, send follow-up; after Y, mark status
- Any required tags/status labels for reporting

D) QA + Testing (Agency PM)
1. Run 5 test leads (different scenarios): qualified, disqualified, no-response, after-hours, wrong zip.
2. Verify KPIs populate: response time, contact, qualified, booked, status breakdown.
3. Confirm staff notifications are correct.
4. Approve go-live.

E) Launch Day (Agency PM)
1. Turn on live lead routing.
2. Monitor first 10 live leads end-to-end.
3. Fix any copy/tone issues within 24 hours.

F) Weekly Optimization (Agency PM)
1. Review: contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate.
2. Tighten qualification if too many junk leads; loosen if too few qualify.
3. Adjust after-hours logic.

G) Monthly Reporting (Agency → Client)
Use the KPI template and include:
- Median response time
- Contact rate
- Qualified rate
- Booked rate
- Lead status breakdown (new/attempted/contacted/qualified/booked/unqualified/no-response)
- Notes: changes made + next month experiments

Operating Cadence (Recommended)
- Week 1: baseline metrics + quick copy tweaks
- Week 2: qualification optimization
- Week 3: booking conversion improvement
- Week 4: report + renewal reinforcement
