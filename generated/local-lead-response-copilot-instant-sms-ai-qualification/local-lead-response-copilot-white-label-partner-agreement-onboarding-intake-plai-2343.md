1) What Copilot delivers
Copilot provides an instant SMS response + AI qualification workflow that:
• Responds to new leads immediately after form/FB lead submission
• Asks a short sequence of qualifying questions
• Routes outcomes to the Partner (and/or client) and supports booking calls/appointments

2) White‑label / client relationship
• Partner owns the client relationship and pricing to the end client.
• Partner may present Copilot as a white‑label solution.
• Partner agrees not to misrepresent Copilot capabilities, compliance, or results.

3) Pricing to Partner + commitments
• Partner pays Copilot: $299–$499 per active client account per month (depending on tier/volume).
• Volume tiers (example):
  – 1–2 accounts: $499/account/mo
  – 3–9 accounts: $399/account/mo
  – 10–29 accounts: $349/account/mo
  – 30+ accounts: $299/account/mo
• Minimum commitment for priority support and onboarding: 3 active accounts (or 90-day commitment for the first 1–2 accounts). Specifics to be confirmed in writing per Partner.

4) What Partner charges end clients (guidance)
Partner typically charges end clients: $499–$999 per month per location/account.

5) Setup + implementation responsibilities
Partner responsibilities (per client):
• Provide accurate business details, lead sources, and desired qualification/booking flow
• Ensure permission to text leads (opt-in language and compliance on forms/ads)
• Provide calendar/booking link and/or staff routing rules
• Provide required access/credentials where needed (or connect via approved method)

Copilot responsibilities:
• Configure reply/qualification flow and routing per the intake form
• QA test the experience end-to-end
• Provide a monthly KPI summary report template (Partner can rebrand)

6) Support + response times
• Standard support: email support via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
• Priority support eligibility: Partners meeting minimum commitment/volume tier.
• Target response time: next business day for standard; faster for priority when feasible.

7) Data + privacy
• Leads and message content are client data.
• Partner confirms they have the right to process leads and contact them.
• Copilot will only use lead data to provide the service and basic reporting.
• Partner should not send sensitive personal info unless required and permitted.

8) Reporting + KPIs
Copilot reports on (at minimum):
• Median response time
• Contact rate
• Qualified rate
• Booked rate
• Lead status breakdown (New, Contacted, Qualified, Booked, Unqualified, No Response)

9) Uptime + limitations
• Copilot is designed to improve speed-to-lead and qualification. Results depend on offer, lead quality, hours of coverage, and follow-up.
• No guarantee of revenue outcomes.

10) Non-circumvention (simple)
Partner agrees not to bypass Copilot to replicate the same service directly with Copilot’s internal materials/workflows while still using Copilot’s approach/implementation guidance.

11) Term + cancellation
• Month-to-month unless otherwise agreed.
• Either party may stop new deployments at any time.
• Accounts can be cancelled with written notice per billing cycle (recommended: 7 days before renewal).

12) Acceptance
To accept, Partner replies by email with:
“I, [Name], on behalf of [Agency], accept the Local Lead Response Copilot White‑Label Partner Agreement v1.0 and agree to the pricing/commitment tier confirmed in writing.”
