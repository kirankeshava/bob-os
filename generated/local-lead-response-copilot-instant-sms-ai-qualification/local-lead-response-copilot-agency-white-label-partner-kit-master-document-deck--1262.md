Local Lead Response Copilot automatically:

SECTION 4) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (Agency Delivery Playbook)

Goal
Launch each new client in 48–72 hours with a working lead-to-booking flow and measurable KPIs.

Roles
• Agency: owns client relationship, offer, and billing to client
• Us (copilot ops): configure workflows, AI prompts, routing, reporting

Step 0 — Pre‑sale checklist (agency)
Confirm the client has:
• A lead source (form/FB ads/call tracking) that can trigger automation
• Someone to receive notifications/handle booked calls
• A calendar booking process (or willingness to adopt one)

Step 1 — Intake collection (agency → us)
Gather and send to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to:
1) Business name + category
2) Service area (cities/zip codes)
3) Services offered (top 3)
4) Hours of operation + after-hours rules
5) Ideal customer + disqualifiers (e.g., “no apartments”, “min job $300”)
6) Booking preference:
   • Book directly to calendar OR
   • Qualify then notify team to call
7) Team routing:
   • Who receives hot lead alerts (names, phone/email)
8) Tone/brand notes (friendly/professional/urgent)

Step 2 — Define qualification script (agency + us)
Standard question set (edit per niche):
• What service do you need?
• What’s the address/zip code?
• How soon do you need help?
• Any photos/details you can share?
• Is this residential or commercial?
Optional budget guardrail:
• “Most projects like this start around $X — does that work?”

Step 3 — Build + routing rules (us)
We configure:
• Trigger from lead source
• Instant SMS responder
• Qualification logic (branching)
• Lead status tags:
  - New, Contacted, Engaged, Qualified, Booked, Not a Fit, No Response
• Notifications:
  - Hot lead → instant alert + booking link
  - Warm lead → follow-up sequence
  - Not a fit → close + tag reason

Step 4 — QA (agency + us)
Agency tests 5 scenarios:
1) Perfect lead → qualifies → books
2) Lead outside service area → tagged Not a Fit
3) After-hours lead → correct message and next-step
4) No response lead → follow-up schedule
5) Spanish/odd input → AI handles gracefully + fallback

Step 5 — Go live (agency)
• Turn on automation
• Confirm first 10 leads are logging correctly
• Ensure the team responds to hot lead notifications

Step 6 — Weekly optimization (first 30 days)
Adjust:
• Questions (remove friction)
• Disqualifiers (protect labor)
• Booking rules (increase show rate)
• Follow-ups (boost contact rate)

Ongoing monthly cadence
• Deliver KPI report (template below)
• Agree on 1–2 optimization experiments
