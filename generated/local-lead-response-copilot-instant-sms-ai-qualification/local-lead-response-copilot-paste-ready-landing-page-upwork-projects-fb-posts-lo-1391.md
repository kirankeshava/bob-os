## The Problem (Why Speed-to-Lead Wins)
Local lead gen only works if someone responds first. When leads sit for 5–30 minutes, they call the next provider—or the provider who replied fastest. Most small businesses and many agencies don’t have a reliable “instant response” system across nights/weekends, and manual follow-up gets missed.

**Result:** wasted ad spend, lower booked rates, angry clients, and churn.

---
## The Promise
### “<60 seconds response time” automation
When a lead submits a form (website, FB lead form, Google form, etc.), they immediately receive a personalized SMS. The system asks **3–5 short questions** to qualify and then **books an appointment/call automatically** (or routes to the right person).

**7-day pilot:** we set it up fast so you can prove lift before committing.

**Setup in 24 hours:** standard integrations go live next day once we have access/fields.

**White-label:** your agency name + your domain/email; client never needs to know.

---
## How It Works (Simple)
1) **Trigger**: New lead submits (form/FB ads/Zapier webhook).
2) **Instant SMS**: “Hey {Name}, this is {Company}. Quick question so we can quote accurately…”
3) **AI Qualification (3–5 Qs)**: job type, location, urgency, timeline, photos link, etc.
4) **Booking**: if qualified → Calendly/Google Calendar booking link or auto-book.
5) **CRM Notes**: transcript + summary + tags get pushed to CRM (GHL/HubSpot/Pipedrive) and/or emailed/slacked.

**Operator controls:** qualifying rules, business hours, escalation to human, stop words, compliant opt-out.
