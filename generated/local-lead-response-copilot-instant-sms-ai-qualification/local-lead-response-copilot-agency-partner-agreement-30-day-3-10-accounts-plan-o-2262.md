# 30-Day Partner Growth Plan — “3×10 Accounts” (Revenue-First)

**Goal:** Close **3 agencies** to **10 accounts each** (30 accounts).  
**Partner economics:** Partner pays Vendor **$299–$499/account** and charges clients **$499–$999/account**.  
**Why this works:** speed-to-lead is an urgent, measurable conversion lever; implementation is fast; KPIs are straightforward.

## Target Customers (Best Fit)
Home services + local high-intent:
- Roofing, HVAC, plumbing, restoration, pest control, solar, flooring, towing
- Any business buying leads from Google/FB where response time drives booking

**Targets:** 40–80 agency prospects contacted; 6–12 calls booked.
- Pick 1 niche (e.g., roofing/HVAC) to sharpen messaging.
- Offer: “Instant SMS + AI qualification + booking handoff within 60 seconds.”
- Send agency outreach using the deck + one-pager.
- Use this legitimacy URL in outreach:  
  https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4
- Book calls; qualify agencies by client volume and ability to onboard 10 accounts.

### Week 2 — Close 1st Partner (10-account commitment)
**Targets:** 1 signed partner; 3–5 client accounts ready for build.
- Close with: volume discount + minimum commitment for priority support.
- Run a “Fast Launch Sprint”: first 3 accounts live in 72 hours.
- Capture early KPIs: response time, contact rate, qualified rate.

### Week 3 — Close 2nd Partner + Scale Ops
**Targets:** 2nd signed partner; 10–15 accounts live total.
- Use performance story: “Median response time dropped from X minutes to <1 minute.”
- Partners should sell this as a conversion lift, not ‘AI.’

### Week 4 — Close 3rd Partner + Stabilize Reporting
**Targets:** 3rd signed partner; 20–30 accounts live total.
- Formalize monthly KPI cadence and QBR-style summary.
- Ask for referrals: agencies know other agencies.
