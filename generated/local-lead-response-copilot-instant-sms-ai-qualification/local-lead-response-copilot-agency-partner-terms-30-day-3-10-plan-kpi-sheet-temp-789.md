# 2) “3×10 Accounts” — 30-Day Agency Execution Plan

## Goal
Land **30 active accounts in 30 days** via **3 agency partners × 10 accounts each**.

## Unit economics (how the agency wins)
- Partner pays Provider: **$299–$499/account/month**
- Partner charges clients: **$499–$999/account/month**
- Partner margin: typically **$200–$600/account/month**

At **10 accounts**, a partner is typically netting **$2k–$6k MRR** while improving client conversions.

## Week-by-week plan

### Week 1: Recruit partners + fast proof
**Targets:** 30–50 agency conversations → 5 partner trials → 2 partners activated.
- Outreach angle: “Speed-to-lead SMS + AI qualification + booking as a white-label add-on.”
- Send: deck + one-pager + legitimacy link
  - https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4
- Close ask: “Start with 3 accounts; we’ll help you launch in 48 hours.”

**Partner close checklist:**
1) Choose tier (A/B/C)
2) Confirm niche focus (HVAC/plumbing/roofing/dentists/chiro)
3) Identify 10 target clients (existing or warm leads)
4) Book onboarding call

### Week 2: Launch first 3–5 accounts per partner
**Targets:** 6–10 accounts live.
- Use the SOP to launch accounts fast (see build checklist below).
- Establish baseline metrics (current response time/contact/booked).

### Week 3: Scale with case snippets + internal referrals
**Targets:** 15–20 accounts live.
- Turn early wins into a “micro case”:
  - “Median response time: 12 sec (was 2+ hrs). Contact rate +18%. Booked rate +9%.”
- Add referral ask to every client QBR: “Want us to roll this out to your second location / your friend’s business?”

### Week 4: Push to 30 accounts + lock commitments
**Targets:** 30 accounts live + commitments for next month.
- Move partners to Tier C (10+ accounts) for $299 pricing.
- Lock monthly minimum commitment for priority support.

So each partner needs ~40 pitches to get to 10 accounts.

You get a report every month: response time, contact rate, qualified rate, and booked rate. It’s a flat monthly add-on; we can launch in 48 hours.”

# 3) KPI Monthly Reporting Template (Google Sheets Structure)

## Tabs
1) **Summary (Monthly)**
2) **Leads (Raw)**
3) **Status Breakdown**
4) **Response Time**
5) **Bookings**

## Tab: “Leads (Raw)” — column headers
- Lead ID
- Lead Date
- Lead Source (Form/FB/Call/Other)
- Lead Name
- Lead Phone
- First SMS Sent Timestamp
- First Lead Reply Timestamp
- Contacted? (Y/N)
- Qualified? (Y/N)
- Booked? (Y/N)
- Lead Status (No Response / Not Qualified / Qualified-No Booking / Booked / Wrong Number / Spam)
- Notes

## Core metric definitions (put at top of Summary tab)
- **Median Response Time:** median of (First SMS Sent Timestamp − Lead Date)
- **Contact Rate:** Contacted Leads / Total Leads
- **Qualified Rate:** Qualified Leads / Contacted Leads
- **Booked Rate:** Booked Leads / Qualified Leads

## Tab: “Summary (Monthly)” — table layout
Columns:
- Month
- Total Leads
- Median Response Time (sec)
- Contact Rate (%)
- Qualified Rate (%)
- Booked Rate (%)
- Booked Count
- Top Lead Sources (ranked)
- Notes / Actions

## Tab: “Status Breakdown” — table layout
Rows = Lead Status
Columns:
- Count
- % of Total

Statuses to include:
- No Response
- Contacted / In Progress
- Not Qualified
- Qualified / No Booking Yet
- Booked
- Wrong Number
- Spam/Test

## Suggested improvement notes (add as a section in Summary)
- If Contact Rate < target: adjust opening message, add 2nd attempt timing, confirm phone quality.
- If Qualified Rate < target: refine qualification questions, add service area gate, add budget/timeframe question.
- If Booked Rate < target: tighten booking CTA, offer 2 time windows, reduce question count.
