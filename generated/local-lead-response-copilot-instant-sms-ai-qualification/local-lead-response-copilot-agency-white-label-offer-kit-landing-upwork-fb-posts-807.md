## The problem (and why agencies lose money)
Local leads go cold fast. If your client (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, junk removal, dental, medspa, etc.) responds in 10–30 minutes, they often lose the job to the first business that answers.

**Speed-to-lead is the conversion lever you can control.**

## The KPI we optimize
**Response in <60 seconds** via SMS for every form/Facebook/landing-page lead.  
Then we **qualify in 3–5 short questions** and **book the call/appointment** automatically.

## How it works (simple)
1) **Lead comes in** (website form, FB lead ad webhook, Zapier, CRM, etc.)
2) **Instant SMS** goes out in under a minute
3) The lead answers **3–5 questions** (service needed, urgency, location, budget range, best time)
4) We **route & book**: schedule link, calendar booking, or notify dispatcher
5) We **log notes back** into your CRM/Sheet + send your team a summary
