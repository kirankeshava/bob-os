## 1) What you can resell
You may resell Local Lead Response Copilot as a **white-label or co-branded** “instant lead response + SMS qualification + booking automation” service to your clients (“End Clients”). You may position it as part of your lead-gen, ads, web, CRM, or automation package.

## 2) Roles & responsibilities
**Provider responsibilities (we do):**
- Configure and maintain the lead-response and qualification automation per End Client.
- Provide onboarding support to Agency and (optionally) End Clients.
- Maintain core automation logic, reporting fields, and operational monitoring.

**Agency responsibilities (you do):**
- Own the client relationship, pricing, billing to End Clients, and first-line support.
- Provide accurate intake details and timely approvals.
- Ensure End Client has consent-compliant lead capture (see Section 7).

## 3) Pricing, margins, and billing
**Partner wholesale (paid by Agency to Provider):** typically **$299–$499 per account/month** depending on volume tier. 
**Agency retail guidance (charged by Agency to End Client):** typically **$499–$999 per account/month**.

### Volume tiers (example)
- **1–2 accounts:** $499/account/mo
- **3–5 accounts:** $399/account/mo
- **6–10 accounts:** $349/account/mo
- **11–25 accounts:** $319/account/mo
- **26+ accounts:** $299/account/mo

**Minimum commitment for priority support:** Agency maintains **minimum 3 active accounts** *or* agrees to a **90-day minimum term** on the first 3 accounts. If the minimum is not met, support reverts to standard (see Section 6).

**Billing cadence:** monthly in advance (or as otherwise agreed). 

## 4) Onboarding & implementation timeline
Standard build target (once intake is complete): **2–5 business days** per account.

Agency agrees to deliver required intake items (see Intake Form below) before build begins.

## 5) White-labeling & branding
- Agency may use its own brand name in End Client communications.
- Provider may remain invisible to End Clients unless Agency requests co-branded support.
- Agency may not represent the product as internally developed if asked directly; recommended phrasing: “We use a specialized lead response system we manage for you.”

## 6) Support levels & SLA
**Standard support (included):**
- Email support to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Target response time: **within 1 business day**

**Priority support (requires minimum commitment):**
- Target response time: **same business day** for urgent issues (Mon–Fri)
- Faster implementation queue for new accounts

**What qualifies as urgent:** system not sending, booking failure, or lead capture disruption.

## 7) Compliance, consent & acceptable use
Agency must ensure End Clients:
- Collect leads via forms/ads with appropriate disclosures.
- Only message leads who have provided consent to be contacted.
- Follow applicable laws and guidelines (TCPA, CTIA, local regulations).

**Prohibited use:** spam, purchased lead lists without consent, deceptive messaging, or illegal content.

## 8) Data handling & confidentiality
- Provider processes lead/contact data solely to deliver the service.
- Agency owns the End Client relationship and is responsible for End Client data governance.
- Both parties agree to keep each other’s non-public business information confidential.

## 9) Reporting & performance
Provider will supply monthly KPI reporting (or data fields) including:
- Median response time
- Contact rate
- Qualified rate
- Booked rate
- Lead status breakdown

Performance depends heavily on lead quality, offer, business hours, and follow-up; no guaranteed revenue outcomes are promised.

## 10) Term, cancellation, and offboarding
- Month-to-month unless a minimum term is agreed.
- Either party may cancel with **14 days notice** (unless minimum term applies).
- Upon cancellation, Provider will stop automations and (on request) provide export of available reporting metrics.

## 11) Non-solicitation (optional, recommended)
For 12 months after termination, Agency agrees not to solicit Provider team members for employment/contracting.

## 12) Limitation of liability (plain language)
Provider is not liable for indirect damages (lost profits, lost business) related to response delays, carrier issues, ad platform outages, or End Client operational issues.
