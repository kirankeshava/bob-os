## 2) Enrichment workflow (fast + reliable)
**Objective:** convert each row from “contact path” to **direct decision-maker**.

### Step A — Find the right person
- Agencies: Owner/Founder/CEO/Head of Growth.
- Operators: Owner/GM/Marketing Manager/Office Manager.

Sources (in order):
1) Company website: About/Team/Contact
2) LinkedIn company page → People tab
3) Google Business Profile (owner name sometimes appears in reviews)

### Step B — Get an email without paid tools
- Look for direct emails on Contact/About/Privacy pages.
- If none: use common patterns from the site (e.g., first@domain, hello@domain) and record as **Pattern-based**.
- If still none: route to **contact form + LinkedIn** and note it.

### Step C — Add confidence score
- **High:** email explicitly listed for the person.
- **Med:** correct person found; email is generic (info@/hello@).
- **Low:** only contact form available or uncertain decision-maker.

### Required fields for each enriched lead
- First name
- Company
- Role
- Email (or contact form URL)
- LinkedIn URL (person)
- Personalization line (1 sentence)
- Channel plan: Email / LinkedIn / Form
- Confidence: High/Med/Low

## 3) Deliverability-safe send plan (Week 1)
### Warmup (Days 1–2)
- Send **10–20 1:1 emails/day** (manually, not mass).
- Keep copy short, no attachments, 1 link max (use the website link or Calendly, not both in warmup).
- Rotate subject lines.

### Ramp (Days 3–7)
- **50/day** maximum if replies are coming and bounce rate stays low.
- Maintain mix: ~60% agencies / ~40% operators.

### Simple rules
- Avoid spammy words in subject (“free”, “guarantee”, “instant leads”); use neutral subjects.
- Use plain text, no images.
- If you get a bounce: mark as bounced, switch to LinkedIn/contact form.

## 7) Daily operating checklist (do this every day)
1) Enrich 25–40 new targets.
2) Send emails (warmup or ramp) and log every touch.
3) Send LinkedIn connections to the same day’s list.
4) Follow-up to non-replies at 48–72h.
5) Track metrics: Sent, Delivered, Replies, Positive replies, Demos booked.

## KPI thresholds
- Bounce rate > 5%: pause, improve enrichment.
- Reply rate < 5% after 100 sends: tighten targeting + rewrite first line/personalization.
- Positive replies < 2%: adjust offer framing (more specific outcomes: contact rate, booked calls, after-hours capture).
