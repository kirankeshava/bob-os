## 3) Pricing, Margins, and Volume Tiers
**Partner wholesale pricing (paid to Provider):**
- **Tier A (1–3 accounts):** $499 / account / month
- **Tier B (4–9 accounts):** $399 / account / month
- **Tier C (10–24 accounts):** $349 / account / month
- **Tier D (25+ accounts):** $299 / account / month

**Suggested Partner retail pricing (charged to End Clients):** $499–$999 / account / month.

### Minimum Commitment (for priority support)
- To receive **priority support + faster builds**, Partner commits to a **minimum of 3 active accounts** (or pays Tier A for 3 accounts) for at least **60 days**.
- Accounts may be swapped between End Clients month to month, as long as the active account count remains at/above the commitment.

### “3×10 Accounts” Plan (30-day scaling target)
- Goal: **3 agency partners × 10 accounts each = 30 accounts**.
- At $349 wholesale (Tier C) × 30 accounts ≈ **$10,470 MRR** to Provider.
- Agencies typically price at $699–$999; at $699, that’s **$20,970 MRR** to agencies with healthy margin.

## 4) Billing & Payment Terms
- Provider bills Partner monthly in advance for active accounts.
- Partner is responsible for collecting from End Clients.
- If Partner fails to pay by the due date, Provider may pause new lead responses for affected accounts after notice.

*(Note: Any pass-through fees like SMS/telephony can be bundled into wholesale or billed separately later. For now, keep pricing simple unless/until usage requires changes.)*

## 5) What’s Included (Service Scope)
For each End Client account:
- Lead-source integration intake (forms/FB leads/other)
- Instant SMS response and follow-up workflow
- AI qualification question flow (short, high-intent)
- Routing + booking handoff (call booking or appointment request)
- Basic reporting: response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate, lead status breakdown

**Not included (unless agreed):** ad management, landing page design, CRM migration, client phone sales training.

---

## 6) Implementation Timeline & Responsibilities
### Provider responsibilities
- Build and QA the workflow based on Partner’s submitted intake.
- Provide a test plan and “go-live” checklist.
- Provide reporting template and recommended KPI targets.

### Partner responsibilities
- Ensure End Client supplies accurate business info and lead source access.
- Ensure End Client has capacity to answer calls/confirm appointments.
- Ensure End Client consents to SMS outreach and complies with applicable laws.

**Typical timeline:** 2–5 business days from complete intake to go-live.

## 7) Support & SLAs (Simple)
- **Standard support:** email support via **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to**.
- **Priority support:** Partners meeting minimum commitment receive faster response and onboarding.

**Target response times (business days):**
- Urgent (system down, messages not sending): respond within **4 hours**.
- High (booking/logic errors): respond within **1 business day**.
- Normal (copy tweaks, minor changes): respond within **2–3 business days**.

## 8) Data Handling & Privacy
- Provider processes lead/contact data solely to deliver the Copilot service.
- Partner remains the primary relationship owner with End Clients.
- Provider will take reasonable steps to protect data, limit access, and avoid unauthorized sharing.
- Partner is responsible for ensuring End Client’s privacy policy and consent language covers SMS and automated communications.

## 9) Compliance (TCPA/A2P/Opt‑Out)
- Copilot workflows must include clear opt-out language (e.g., “Reply STOP to opt out”).
- Partner agrees to use Copilot only for leads that have provided appropriate consent.
- Provider can suspend or terminate any account that is clearly used for spam or non-consensual outreach.

---

## 10) Uptime & Service Limitations
- Provider aims for reliable operations; occasional outages may occur due to third-party dependencies.
- Provider will work in good faith to restore service quickly.
- Provider is not liable for lost revenue due to outages, ad platform issues, or End Client’s failure to answer calls.

## 11) Term, Cancellation, and Termination
- Month-to-month, unless a specific commitment is agreed.
- Partner may cancel an End Client account with **14 days notice** prior to renewal.
- Provider may terminate accounts for non-payment, compliance violations, or abusive usage.

## 12) Non‑Solicitation (Optional but Recommended)

- Partner agrees not to reverse-engineer or replicate Copilot workflows for resale using Provider’s proprietary implementation.
