## C) Implementation SOP (Agency-Run)
### 1) Client Intake (15 minutes)
Collect:
- Business name + service area + hours
- Lead sources: website form(s), landing pages, FB lead ads
- Primary goals: booked calls vs. onsite estimates
- Routing rules: who gets notified, escalation path
- Booking link (Calendly or similar) or internal scheduling process
- Compliance: opt-in language on forms/ads (SMS consent)

### 2) Script Setup (30 minutes)
Create:
- **Initial SMS (0–10 seconds):**
  “Hi {{first_name}}—got your request for {{service}}. Quick 2 questions so we can get you scheduled. What city are you in?”
- 2–4 qualification questions (service type, location, urgency, timeline)
- Disqualifiers (outside area, wrong service, spam)
- Booking CTA:
  “Perfect—want to grab a time? Here’s the booking link: {{link}}”

### 3) Build + Integrate (60 minutes)
- Connect lead source(s)
- Map fields (name, phone, service, zip)
- Turn on tags/statuses
- Set notification rules

### 4) QA Checklist (15 minutes)
- Submit 3 test leads
- Confirm SMS sent within seconds
- Confirm qualification logic works
- Confirm booking/handoff works
- Confirm statuses appear correctly

### 5) Launch (Day 1–2)
- Turn on live traffic
- Monitor first 20 leads
- Adjust question order if drop-off occurs

### 6) Week-1 Optimization
- Review KPIs
- Tighten first message and question count
- Add after-hours handling if needed

### 7) Monthly Reporting + Renewal
Send the KPI report template (below), highlight trend lines, propose 1 improvement.
