1) Parties
This Partner Agreement (“Agreement”) is between:
• Provider: Local Lead Response Copilot (“Provider”)
• Agency Partner: ______________________________ (“Partner”)
Effective date: ________________________________

2) What You’re Reselling
Provider supplies a lead-response automation system that:
• Instantly texts new leads from forms/FB lead ads/zap/webhooks
• Asks short qualification questions (AI-guided)
• Routes outcomes (booked/qualified/not qualified/no response) and optionally books calls/appointments
Partner resells this service to end clients under Partner’s brand (white-label) or co-branded, as agreed per client.

3) White‑Label Rights & Branding
• Partner may present the service as its own offering.
• Partner may not claim ownership of Provider’s underlying software/workflows.
• Partner will not reverse engineer or copy Provider’s prompts, workflows, or automation logic.
• Provider branding can be hidden; Provider can be disclosed as a subcontractor if needed for compliance.

4) Pricing, Margins, and Minimum Commitment
Wholesale (Partner pays Provider): $299–$499 per active client account per month (per agreed tier).
Suggested retail (Partner charges client): $499–$999 per account per month.

Minimum commitment for Priority Support:
• Partner commits to at least 3 active accounts for a minimum of 60 days (“Minimum Term”).
• If Partner drops below 3 active accounts during the Minimum Term, Priority Support is paused until Partner is back at 3+ accounts.

Volume discount tiers (example structure):
• 1–2 accounts: $499/account/mo
• 3–9 accounts: $399/account/mo
• 10–24 accounts: $349/account/mo
• 25+ accounts: $299/account/mo
(Provider may adjust tiers with 30 days notice; existing accounts keep pricing during the Minimum Term.)

5) Billing & Payment Terms
• Provider invoices Partner monthly in advance for active accounts.
• “Active account” = an end client account with live lead routing enabled, regardless of lead volume.
• Partner is responsible for collecting payment from end clients. Provider’s relationship is with Partner, not the end client.
• Late payment: If an invoice is more than 7 days overdue, Provider may pause automations until paid.

6) What’s Included (Baseline)
Included per active account unless otherwise specified:
• One primary lead source connection (form/FB lead ads/webhook)
• One SMS qualification flow (up to ~8 questions)
• Routing outcomes (qualified/unqualified/no response) to Partner/client via email/CRM/Zapier-style webhooks as available
• Basic monthly KPI report export (median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate, lead status breakdown)

Not included unless added:
• Custom multi-branch complex flows beyond the baseline
• Deep CRM builds, call tracking setup, paid ad management, website changes
• Compliance consulting (TCPA/CTIA/carrier registration) beyond best-practice guidance

7) Implementation SLA (What We Promise)
Once Partner provides all required intake details (see Section 8), Provider targets:
• Standard launch: 2 business days per account
• Complex/multi-location launch: 3–5 business days per account

8) Partner Responsibilities (Required Inputs)
Partner supplies for each end client:
• Business name, service area, hours, offer, and qualification criteria
• Phone number routing destination(s) for escalations (who gets notified)
• Lead source details (form link, webhook, FB lead access, etc.)
• Appointment link (if booking) and booking rules
• Compliance confirmations: client has consent language on forms/ads for SMS contact

9) SMS Consent & Compliance (Critical)
Partner agrees:
• End clients must collect proper opt-in consent for SMS follow-up.
• Partner will use compliant language on lead forms/ads (e.g., “By submitting, you agree to receive texts…”).
• Provider will not be liable for Partner/client non-compliance.
• If Provider detects risky/non-compliant usage, Provider may require changes or suspend the account.

10) Support & Escalation
Support channels:

Support scope:
• Fixing system errors, updating questions, routing tweaks, lead source reconnects.
Response targets (Priority Support, for Partners with 3+ active accounts):
• First response: within 1 business day

11) Data Handling & Confidentiality
• Partner and Provider will keep client data confidential.
• Provider may process lead data only to deliver the service.
• Partner is responsible for obtaining end-client permission to share lead data with Provider.
• Provider will take reasonable steps to protect data but does not guarantee absolute security.

12) Performance Disclaimer
Provider will use best efforts to improve speed-to-lead and qualification, but cannot guarantee:
• Specific conversion rates
• Appointment show rates
• Revenue outcomes
Results depend on lead quality, offer, pricing, location, and client follow-through.

13) Term, Renewal, and Termination
• Term begins on Effective date and continues month-to-month after any Minimum Term.
• Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice after Minimum Term.
• Provider may terminate immediately for non-payment, abuse, illegal use, or repeated compliance violations.
On termination:
• Automations are paused on end date
• Partner may export KPI data upon request within 14 days

AGENCY “NEXT STEPS” CHECKLIST (Close → Onboard → Launch in <48 hours)
Use this as the last slide / last page of your deck.

A) Close the client (15 minutes)
1) Confirm their lead sources (FB Lead Ads, web form, Google LSA, etc.)
2) Confirm goal: “Book jobs faster by texting instantly + qualifying.”
3) Confirm routing: who gets notified when a lead is qualified.
4) Set expectation: “We’ll be live in 2 business days once we have access.”

B) Client intake (send immediately after close)
Collect:
• Business name, service area, hours
• Offer + top 3 services
• Qualification rules (zip codes, budget minimum, job type)
• Booking link (optional)
• Consent language confirmation on forms/ads

C) Implementation (Partner + Provider)
1) Partner emails Provider: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
   Subject: “New Agency Account — [Client Name] — Launch Request”
   Include: intake details + lead source access instructions.
2) Provider configures SMS + qualification + routing.
3) Partner QA: submit 2–3 test leads and confirm:
   • Response time
   • Qualification logic
   • Notifications
   • Booking flow (if enabled)

D) Launch & optimize (first 7 days)
1) Go live.
2) Review KPI snapshot day 3 and day 7:
   • Median response time
   • Contact rate
   • Qualified rate
   • Booked rate
3) Adjust questions to improve qualified→booked.

E) Monthly reporting (recurring)
Send the Monthly KPI Report to the client on the 1st business day each month, including:
• Median response time
• Contact rate
• Qualified rate
• Booked rate
• Lead status breakdown
