## One-Page Landing (paste into Notion)
### The problem
Local leads go cold fast. If your client (roofer, plumber, HVAC, med spa, attorney) waits 10–30 minutes to respond, you lose the job to the fastest competitor. Agencies feel this as churn: “the leads suck” when the real issue is speed-to-lead and inconsistent follow-up.

### The KPI we optimize
**< 60 seconds to first text response** (24/7). We instantly engage every new lead and qualify them with 3–5 short questions, then book calls/appointments automatically.

### What you get (7-day pilot + setup in 24 hours)
- **Instant SMS** the moment a lead hits a form/FB Lead Ad
- **AI-driven qualification** (3–5 questions tailored to the niche)
- **Auto-booking** (Calendly/Google Calendar) or “handoff to rep”
- **CRM notes + pipeline updates** (GoHighLevel/HubSpot/Sheets)
- **White-label option** for agencies (your branding, your domain/email copy)

### How it works
1) Lead submits (website form, FB Lead Ad, landing page, chat)  
2) Lead gets an immediate text: “Hey {{name}}, got your request—2 quick questions so we can get you a quote…”  
3) Copilot asks 3–5 qualifying questions (job type, urgency, zip code, budget range, preferred time)  
4) If qualified → booking link + confirmation; if not → tagged + routed  
5) Notes + transcript pushed to CRM/pipeline and emailed/SMS’d to your team

### Integrations
- **Lead sources:** Web forms (Typeform/Jotform/WP), FB Lead Ads (via Zapier/Make), Google Ads landing pages
- **SMS:** Twilio (or your existing provider)
- **Calendars:** Calendly / Google Calendar
- **CRMs:** GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Pipedrive (or Google Sheets)

### Compliance & best practices
- Uses opt-in language and respects quiet hours where required
- Clear STOP/HELP handling supported via SMS provider defaults
- Data minimized: only qualification details needed for booking and CRM notes
