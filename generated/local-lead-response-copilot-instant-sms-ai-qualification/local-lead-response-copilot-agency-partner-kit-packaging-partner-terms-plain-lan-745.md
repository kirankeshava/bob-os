D) IMPLEMENTATION SOP (AGENCY-FACING)
----------------------------------------------------
Goal: launch each client in 2–5 business days.

Step 1 — Intake (Agency collects)
Collect from client:
• Business name, location(s), business hours
• Primary contact(s) + routing (who gets notified)
• Lead sources: website form URL(s), FB lead form, etc.
• Required lead fields (name, phone, service type, zip)
• Qualification questions (2–5 max)
• Disqualifiers (out of area, wrong service, budget, etc.)
• Booking flow: calendar link OR “request a call” rules
• Compliance notes: opt-in language (where applicable), disclaimer text

Step 2 — Build
We configure:
• Trigger for new lead
• Instant SMS copy
• AI qualification questions + branching
• Follow-up logic for no-response
• Lead status tagging (New, Contacted, Qualified, Booked, Not Qualified, No Response)

Step 3 — QA
• Test with 3–5 sample leads
• Validate: response time <60 seconds
• Validate: correct status tags
• Validate: booking link + notifications

Step 4 — Launch
• Turn on automation
• Monitor first 48 hours closely
• Adjust questions/routing if needed

Step 5 — Optimize (weekly, then monthly)
• Review KPIs
• Tighten qualification (reduce junk)
• Improve booking rate (copy tweaks)

Client-facing requirements checklist (send to client):
• Confirm lead source + access
• Confirm booking link + availability
• Confirm service area + hours
• Confirm “qualified lead” definition
