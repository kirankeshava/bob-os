### Subhead
Local Lead Response Copilot instantly texts new leads from forms/FB lead ads, asks 3–5 high-intent questions, and books calls/appointments—while your client is still “hot.” Built for agencies + home services + local high-intent businesses.

### The problem (why agencies lose money)
- Leads go cold fast: after 5 minutes, contact rates and close rates drop hard.
- Most local operators don’t answer instantly (jobsites, trucks, after-hours).
- Agencies pay for leads; clients blame “lead quality” when it’s really **response speed**.

### The KPI we manage
**Response time:** <60 seconds to first text message (24/7).  
Optional secondary KPIs: contact rate, booked appointment rate, speed-to-book.

### What it does (in plain English)
1. A lead comes in (website form, landing page, Google/Meta lead form, etc.)
2. They get an **instant SMS**: “Got it—quick question so we can help you fast…”
3. An **AI-driven SMS flow** asks 3–5 qualifying questions (job type, urgency, zip code, budget range, property type, preferred time)
4. If qualified → **books a call/appointment** (Calendly or similar) and notifies your client
5. Lead summary + answers are saved to the CRM / Google Sheet / email so the sales team has context

### Why it converts
- Captures leads **while intent is highest**
- Filters tire-kickers (and stops wasting the sales team’s time)
- Delivers a “ready-to-call” lead with notes and next step booked

### Setup promise
**Setup in 24 hours** (typical) once we have access to the lead source + booking link + qualifying questions.

### Integrations
- **Lead sources:** Web forms (Zapier/Make), Meta/Facebook Lead Ads (Zapier/Make), Google Sheets, webhook
- **Booking:** Calendly (or your preferred scheduler)
- **CRMs:** HighLevel, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Jobber/ServiceTitan via Zapier/Make (or push to email + sheet if simpler)

### Compliance & best practices (important)
- Uses opt-in language (where applicable) and respects STOP/HELP keywords.
- Designed for conversational qualification, not spam blasts.
- You/your client are responsible for ensuring leads have consent to be contacted via SMS (we provide recommended language).
