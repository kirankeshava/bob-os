## 1) One-Page Landing (paste-ready)

### Headline
**Instant SMS + AI Qualification for Local Leads (Under 60 Seconds)**

### Subhead
Stop losing high-intent leads to slow follow-up. Local Lead Response Copilot instantly texts every new lead, asks 3–5 qualifying questions, and books calls/appointments automatically.

### The problem (why this matters)
Local home service and high-intent businesses win or lose on speed-to-lead. If a new prospect waits 5–30 minutes for a reply, they often book the next provider who responds first.

### The KPI promise
**We help you achieve <60 seconds median first response time** on inbound leads from forms / landing pages / Facebook Lead Ads.

### How it works (simple)
1. **Lead comes in** (web form, FB lead ad, landing page, etc.)
2. **Instant SMS** goes out in seconds (“Hey {{name}}, got your request — quick question…”) 
3. **AI qualification** asks 3–5 short questions (job type, urgency, zip, budget, availability)
4. **Booking / handoff**
   - If qualified → books a call/appointment (Calendly or your scheduler)
   - If unqualified → tags + routes to the right follow-up
5. **CRM notes** captured automatically (lead summary + answers + qualification status)

### Integrations (common)
- **Lead sources:** Facebook Lead Ads, website forms (Webflow/WordPress/Wix), Google Ads landing pages, Typeform, Jotform
- **Scheduling:** Calendly (or your booking link)
- **CRM / tracking:** HubSpot, HighLevel (GHL), Zoho, Salesforce (via Zapier/Make)
- **Automation layer:** Zapier / Make / Webhooks

### Compliance & best practices
- Opt-in language supported (checkbox / disclosure for SMS where needed)
- STOP/HELP handling supported
- Business hours, escalation rules, and human handoff supported
- We do not provide legal advice; we implement standard consent + opt-out flows
