## 4) AGENCY IMPLEMENTATION SOP (Intake → Build → QA → Launch → Optimize)

### A) Client intake (what you collect)
**Required**
- Business name + service area
- Services offered (top 3)
- Lead sources (website form URL, landing page, FB lead ads, etc.)
- Booking destination (Calendly/housecall pro schedule link/phone handoff)
- Office hours + after-hours rules
- Disqualifiers (zip codes, job types, minimum ticket)

**Helpful**
- Common FAQs + price anchors
- Brand voice (friendly, direct, premium)
- Preferred appointment windows

### B) Build (internal)
1) Create qualification flow (3–6 questions)
2) Define routing outcomes:
   - Booked (auto)
   - Warm handoff to office
   - Needs more info (photos)
   - Disqualified
3) Configure lead source connection (webhook/Zap)
4) Configure notifications (agency/client)

### C) QA testing checklist
- Submit 3 test leads (realistic)
- Verify:
  - SMS delivered within 60 seconds
  - Logic branches work (qualified vs disqualified)
  - Booking link opens and confirms
  - Lead status updates properly

### D) Launch
- Turn on automation
- Monitor first 20 leads closely
- Adjust wording to reduce drop-off

### E) Optimization (weekly for first month)
- Review KPI funnel:
  1) Response time
  2) Contact rate
  3) Qualified rate
  4) Booked rate
- Improve the biggest bottleneck first (usually question count/wording)

### Client-facing “What to expect week 1” (copy/paste)
You’ll see:
- New leads get an instant text within ~60 seconds
- Prospects answer a few questions so we can route/quote accurately
- You’ll receive booked calls/appointments or warm handoffs
- We’ll send a KPI snapshot at month-end (and tune messaging in week 1–2)
