## 4) Implementation SOP (Agency Playbook)

### A) Client Intake Checklist (collect before build)
1) Business name, service categories, and service area
2) Business hours + after-hours rules
3) Lead sources: website forms, FB lead ads, etc.
4) Contact routing: who gets notified? (name/number/email)
5) Booking preference: call booking link or “request appointment” workflow
6) Qualification questions (3–6):
   - Example (HVAC): “Is this repair or install?” “What’s your zip code?” “How soon do you need service?”
7) Compliance note: confirm lead consent language on forms/ads (SMS permission)

### B) Build Steps (internal)
1) Create/confirm the client’s qualification flow
2) Configure instant reply message (friendly, short, clear)
3) Configure fallback rules (no response, after-hours)
4) Configure lead statuses and tags

### C) QA Checklist
- Test lead submission (simulate web + FB lead)
- Confirm response time is near-instant
- Confirm qualification questions sequence
- Confirm routing notification works
- Confirm booking handoff works
- Confirm status tagging + report fields populate

### D) Launch
- Go live during business hours
- Monitor first 10 leads
- Adjust questions for clarity if drop-off occurs

### E) Weekly Optimization (10 minutes)
- Review: contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate
- If contact rate is low: shorten first message, reduce friction, improve call-to-action
- If qualified rate is low: refine question order, remove confusing questions
- If booked rate is low: tighten booking handoff, add confirmation message
