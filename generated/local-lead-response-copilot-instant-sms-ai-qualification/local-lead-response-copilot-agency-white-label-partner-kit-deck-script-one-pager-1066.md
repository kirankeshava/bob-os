## C) Partner Implementation SOP (Agency Fulfillment)

### 1) Pre-Sale Checklist (Agency)
Collect these from the client before onboarding:
- Business name + website
- Service list (what they do / don’t do)
- Service area (cities/zip codes)
- Business hours + after-hours handling (book vs call)
- Primary phone number (for call routing if used)
- Booking link (Calendly / GoHighLevel calendar) or preferred scheduling method
- Lead sources: FB lead ads, web forms, GHL forms, etc.
- Compliance note: confirm they have consent language on forms for SMS follow-up (agency to verify)

### 2) Intake (Partner → Us)
Partner sends one email to **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to** with:
- Client name
- Lead sources + where leads should be routed
- Booking link
- Service area + hours
- Any special questions needed (max 4)

### 3) Build (Us)
We set up:
- Instant SMS trigger per lead source
- Qualification flow (2–4 questions)
- Routing rules:
  - In area + right service + high intent → booking link
  - Out of area / wrong service → polite close + tag “Not Qualified”
  - No response → follow-up sequence
- Lead status pipeline + tags

### 4) QA (Partner + Client)
Partner runs 5 test leads:
- In-area / right service
- Out-of-area
- Wrong service
- After-hours
- No response test
Verify:
- Response time (should be seconds)
- Correct questions and tone
- Correct booking/call routing
- Correct lead status updates

### 5) Go-Live
- Confirm client’s staff knows what “Qualified” and “Booked” mean
- Confirm who receives notifications (SMS/email)
- Launch date set

### 6) First-Week Optimization
- Review transcripts for friction
- Reduce question count if drop-off occurs
- Add “fast path” for urgent leads (“Can you do today/tomorrow?”)

### 7) Monthly Reporting + Retention
Send monthly KPI summary (template below) + 3 bullet insights:
- What improved
- What to tweak
- Next month’s target
