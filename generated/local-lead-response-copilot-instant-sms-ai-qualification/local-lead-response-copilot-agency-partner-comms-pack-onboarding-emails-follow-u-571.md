Discovery (3–4 min):
1) “Where do your leads come from today—FB, website forms, LSAs, something else?”
2) “Roughly how many leads per week?”
3) “Who responds and how fast (on average)?”
4) “What counts as ‘qualified’ for you (service area, job size, type of work)?”
5) “Do you want calls transferred live during business hours, or always book to calendar?”

Pitch (2 min):
“Here’s the simple system: a lead comes in → they get an instant text → we ask 3–5 questions → we tag the lead status (Qualified/Unqualified/No Response) → then we either book them directly or route them to your team. We report on median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, and booked rate monthly.”

ROI framing (1 min):
“If you’re buying leads, even 1–3 extra booked jobs per month usually pays for this many times over. The biggest win is capturing leads that would have gone cold.”

Close (2–3 min):
Option A (fast close): “We can install this in 24–48 hours. It’s {{ClientPrice}}/mo + messaging costs. Want to start with a 14-day pilot or go straight to month-to-month?”
Option B (demo close): “If I map your exact qualification flow and show you a preview, can you do 15 minutes tomorrow?”

Objections:
- “We already respond fast.” → “Great—then this ensures you respond fast 100% of the time, after hours too, and it auto-qualifies so your team talks to better leads.”
- “My leads hate texts.” → “Leads opted in by submitting a form. Text is the fastest channel. If they prefer calls, the flow can escalate to a call or booking immediately.”
- “We don’t want bots.” → “It’s short, practical questions, and it hands off to humans instantly when needed. The goal is speed and sorting, not long conversations.”
