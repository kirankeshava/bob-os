**Instant SMS + AI qualification + booking for local lead gen agencies.**

**The problem:** Most local leads go cold in minutes. If you’re buying leads from FB/Google or generating them for clients, your conversion rate is often decided by one thing: **speed-to-lead**.

**The KPI we optimize:** **< 60 seconds from lead submission → first text message.**

## What this does (in plain English)
When a new lead comes in (form, FB Lead Ads, website, or webhook), Lead Response Copilot:
1) **Texts the lead instantly** (under 60 seconds)
2) Asks **3–5 short qualifying questions** (AI-driven, but controlled)
3) **Books a call/appointment** (Calendly or your scheduler)
4) **Logs notes + qualification** to your CRM / Google Sheet

**Result:** more booked jobs, fewer missed leads, and less admin.

## How it works (simple workflow)
**Trigger:** new lead captured →
**Step 1:** instant SMS: “Hey {{name}}, got your request for {{service}} — quick question…” →
**Step 2:** 3–5 qualifying questions (service type, urgency, ZIP, job size, availability) →
**Step 3:** if qualified → booking link + confirmation →
**Step 4:** CRM notes created + lead tagged (Qualified / Not Qualified / No Response)
