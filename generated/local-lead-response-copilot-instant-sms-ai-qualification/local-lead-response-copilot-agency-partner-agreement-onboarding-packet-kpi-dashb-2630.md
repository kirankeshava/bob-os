## 1) What this is
This agreement outlines how Partner may resell Provider’s “Instant SMS + AI Qualification” lead-response system on a white‑label basis to Partner’s end clients (“Client Accounts”). This is a plain-language operating agreement, not legal advice.

## 2) White‑label + positioning
- Partner may brand the service as its own (“white‑label”).
- Provider will remain invisible to the end client unless Partner requests otherwise for support/debugging.
- Partner is responsible for all marketing/claims made to end clients.

## 3) What Provider delivers
For each activated Client Account, Provider will:
- Connect lead sources (web forms, FB lead ads, CRM/webhooks where available).
- Configure instant SMS response and AI-driven qualification questions.
- Route outcomes (booked call/appointment, notify team, tag lead status).
- Provide reporting for: **median response time**, **contact rate**, **qualified rate**, **booked rate**, and **lead status breakdown**.

## 4) What Partner delivers
Partner will:
- Own the relationship with the end client (billing, expectations, approvals).
- Provide required intake details (see Onboarding Packet below).
- Ensure the client has permission to text their leads (compliance).
- Provide first-line support to the end client for non-technical questions.

## 5) Pricing, margin, and commitments (guidance)
**Wholesale (Partner → Provider):** $299–$499 / Client Account / month (based on volume tier).  
**Retail (Partner → End Client):** $499–$999 / Client Account / month.

### Volume tiers (wholesale)
- **1–3 accounts:** $499/account/mo
- **4–9 accounts:** $399/account/mo
- **10–29 accounts:** $349/account/mo
- **30+ accounts:** $299/account/mo

### Minimum commitment for priority support
- Partner agrees to a **minimum 3 active accounts** OR a **30-day ramp plan** that targets **10 active accounts in 30 days** to receive priority build slots and faster turnaround.
- Accounts are billed while active; paused accounts may lose priority.

## 6) Priority support + response times (SLA)
- **Standard support:** business-day responses within 1–2 business days.
- **Priority support (meets commitment):** responses within 4 business hours on business days.
- Emergency issues: system outage, messages not sending, or booking links failing.

## 7) Implementation timeline
Typical timeline per Client Account (once intake is complete):
- Day 0: Intake received
- Day 1–2: Build + integration
- Day 2–3: QA + test leads
- Day 3: Go-live

## 8) Compliance + texting rules
Partner is responsible for ensuring:
- Leads have opted-in or have an established business relationship allowing SMS follow-up.
- Messages include clear identity, and where required, opt-out language (“Reply STOP to opt out”).
- Partner/client follows TCPA/CTIA and local regulations.

## 9) Data handling
- Provider will process lead data only to deliver the service.
- Partner is responsible for obtaining end-client consent to share lead data with Provider/subprocessors.
- Provider will not sell lead data.

## 10) Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep confidential pricing, templates, system details, and client lists shared during the partnership.

## 11) Term, termination, offboarding
- Either party may terminate with **30 days notice**.
- Upon termination, Provider will export available configuration notes and reporting snapshots upon request. Partner is responsible for migrating end clients.

## 12) Payment + nonpayment
- Wholesale billing occurs monthly per active Client Account.
- Nonpayment may result in account suspension.

## 13) Limits
Provider does not guarantee specific revenue outcomes. Provider guarantees system configuration and reporting accuracy to the best of reasonable effort given available integrations.

# Monthly KPI Reporting Template (Google Sheets Structure)
**Goal:** Simple monthly report agencies can send to each client showing speed-to-lead and funnel conversion.

## Tab 1: `SUMMARY (Monthly)`
**Header fields (top):**
- Client:
- Month:
- Lead sources included:
- Notes/changes this month:

**KPI table (rows):**
1. Total new leads
2. **Median first response time (seconds)**
3. Contact rate (%)
4. Qualified rate (%)
5. Booked rate (%)
6. Booked appointments (count)
7. Not booked (count)
8. Disqualified (count)

**Definitions (footnote):**
- *First response time* = time from lead created → first SMS sent.
- *Contacted* = lead replied at least once.
- *Qualified* = met criteria (service + location + urgency) OR selected “Qualified” tag.
- *Booked* = appointment/call scheduled.

## Tab 2: `LEADS (Raw)`
**Columns (row 1):**
- A: Lead ID
- B: Created date/time
- C: Source (Form/FB/Other)
- D: Name
- E: Phone
- F: Service requested
- G: ZIP/City
- H: First SMS sent date/time
- I: First response time (sec)
- J: Lead replied? (Y/N)
- K: Qualified? (Y/N)
- L: Booked? (Y/N)
- M: Lead status (dropdown: New, Contacted, No Response, Qualified, Disqualified, Booked, Not Booked)
- N: Disqualify reason (if any)
- O: Notes

**Formula examples:**
- `I2` (first response seconds): `=IF(OR(B2="",H2=""),"",(H2-B2)*86400)`

## Tab 3: `CALCS`
Assume raw data is in `LEADS (Raw)`.

**Total leads:**  
`=COUNTA('LEADS (Raw)'!A2:A)`

**Median response time (sec):**  
`=MEDIAN(FILTER('LEADS (Raw)'!I2:I,'LEADS (Raw)'!I2:I<>""))`

**Contact rate:**  
`=IFERROR(COUNTIF('LEADS (Raw)'!J2:J,"Y")/COUNTA('LEADS (Raw)'!A2:A),0)`

**Qualified rate:**  
`=IFERROR(COUNTIF('LEADS (Raw)'!K2:K,"Y")/COUNTA('LEADS (Raw)'!A2:A),0)`

**Booked rate:**  
`=IFERROR(COUNTIF('LEADS (Raw)'!L2:L,"Y")/COUNTA('LEADS (Raw)'!A2:A),0)`

**Lead status breakdown table:**
- Status | Count | % of leads
- Count formula example for “Booked”: `=COUNTIF('LEADS (Raw)'!M2:M,"Booked")`
- % formula: `=IFERROR(B2/$B$1,0)` where `$B$1` is total leads

## Tab 4: `STATUS BREAKDOWN (Client-facing)`
