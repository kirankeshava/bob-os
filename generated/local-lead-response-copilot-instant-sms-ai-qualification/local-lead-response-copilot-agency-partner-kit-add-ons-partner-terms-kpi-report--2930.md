### 1. What you’re reselling
Local Lead Response Copilot is an automation that instantly texts new leads, asks a short set of qualifying questions, and routes outcomes (booked, qualified, needs follow-up, unqualified) to the agency/client’s chosen destination.

### 2. Who owns the client relationship
You (the Partner) own the client relationship and handle your client’s billing. We bill you (the Partner) for each active client account.

### 3. Pricing + minimum commitment (for priority support)
- Standard partner cost: **$399/account/month** (your resale target: $699–$999/account/month)
- Volume tiers (if eligible):
  - **1–2 accounts:** $499/acct/mo
  - **3–9 accounts:** $399/acct/mo
  - **10–24 accounts:** $349/acct/mo
  - **25+ accounts:** $299/acct/mo
- **Minimum commitment for priority support:** **3 active accounts** OR a written plan to reach 3 accounts within 30 days.

### 4. What “active account” means
An account is considered active if it is connected to a live lead source (form/FB lead ads/etc.) and is enabled to send/receive messages.

### 5. Onboarding & implementation responsibilities
**Partner provides:** client name, service area, hours, offer, qualification questions, routing rules, booking link (if used), and access/permissions to lead source + CRM (or a destination email/number).

**We provide:** configuration, QA, and go-live support based on the Implementation SOP.

### 6. Support expectations
- Standard support: email support via **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to**
- Priority support (requires minimum commitment): faster turnaround for new builds and changes.
- Reasonable-use policy: repeated same-day rewrites of scripts and rules may be queued.

### 7. Performance expectations (what we can/can’t guarantee)
We improve speed-to-lead and create measurable outcomes. We cannot guarantee sales, booked jobs, or lead volume because those depend on the client’s marketing, pricing, availability, and market conditions.

### 8. Data handling + privacy
- We process lead information (names, phone numbers, message content) solely to deliver the service.
- Partner is responsible for ensuring the client has the right to contact the lead and is compliant with applicable laws/policies.
- If requested, we will delete an account’s data within a reasonable time after cancellation.

### 9. Messaging compliance (important)
Partner agrees not to use the system for spam or prohibited content. Partner must ensure the client’s messaging practices follow applicable regulations and platform rules.

### 10. Cancellation
Partner can cancel an account by requesting cancellation via email. Service remains active until the end of the current billing period unless otherwise agreed.

### 11. White-labeling
Partner may present the service as “powered by” their agency. If full white-label is required, we can provide neutral-facing assets and messaging upon request.
