AGENCY PARTNER AGREEMENT (PLAIN-LANGUAGE TERMS)
Local Lead Response Copilot ("Provider")
Agency Partner ("Partner")
Effective Date: ____________

1) Purpose
Provider offers Local Lead Response Copilot: instant SMS response + AI qualification + booking automation for inbound leads. Partner resells this service to its clients ("Client Accounts").

2) Relationship (White-Label)
- Partner may present the service as its own (white-label) or as “powered by Local Lead Response Copilot.”
- Provider will not contact Partner’s clients directly for sales purposes unless Partner requests it in writing.
- Partner remains the “merchant of record” for client billing unless otherwise agreed.

3) Plans, Pricing, Minimum Commitment (Agency)
- Agency wholesale pricing is per Client Account per month:
  - Tier A (10–19 accounts): $499/account/month
  - Tier B (20–49 accounts): $399/account/month
  - Tier C (50+ accounts): $299/account/month
- Minimum commitment for priority support + onboarding: 10 active paid accounts (or trial-to-paid pipeline equivalent agreed in writing).
- Partner may charge its clients $499–$999/account/month (Partner sets retail pricing).
- Add-on usage costs (e.g., SMS/telephony) may apply depending on implementation; Provider will disclose any pass-through costs prior to activation.

4) “3×10 Accounts” Growth Plan (Operating Expectation)
To align with the 30-day revenue goal, Partner agrees to execute a simple scale plan:
- Week 1: close and launch 10 accounts (Tier A)
- Week 2: launch next 10 accounts (Tier B threshold)
- Week 3–4: launch next 10 accounts (Tier B/C depending on totals)
Provider will prioritize support in proportion to Partner’s progress toward these milestones.

5) Billing & Payment Terms
- Provider invoices Partner monthly in advance based on active Client Accounts.
- Payment due within 7 days of invoice date unless otherwise agreed.
- Accounts may be paused only at month boundaries with 7 days written notice.
- Non-payment may result in suspension of automation workflows (to prevent uncontrolled lead handling).

6) Onboarding & Implementation Scope
Provider will provide:
- One implementation SOP and intake checklist
- Configuration of lead source capture (forms/FB leads/webhooks) as supported
- SMS/qualification script setup aligned to Partner’s chosen vertical
- Booking workflow setup (calendar link/routing)
- QA test plan + launch checklist

Partner will provide per Client Account:
- Business name, location(s), hours, service area
- Primary contact(s), phone number routing preferences
- Offer/pricing details for qualification
- Calendar booking link (or scheduling preferences)
- Lead sources + access method (form, CRM, FB leads, etc.)

Out of scope unless separately agreed:
- Rebuilding client websites
- Running paid ads
- Full CRM migrations
- Deep custom integrations beyond standard connectors

7) Support & Response Times (SLA)
- Standard support: email support via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Priority support eligibility: Partners at/above the 10-account minimum.
- Target response times (business days):
  - Priority: initial response within 1 business day
  - Standard: initial response within 2 business days
- Emergency definition: system-wide outage, leads not receiving any response, or booking not functioning. Emergency requests are triaged first.

8) KPI Reporting & Optimization
Provider’s default KPI set (monthly, per account):
- Median response time (seconds)
- Contact rate (reached / total leads)
- Qualified rate (qualified / reached)
- Booked rate (booked / qualified)
- Lead status breakdown (new, contacted, qualified, booked, unqualified, no-response, wrong-number)
Partner is responsible for reviewing KPIs with clients and using them to optimize.

9) Compliance (SMS + Consent)
Partner represents and warrants:
- The client has proper consent to text leads (opt-in) from forms/ads.
- The client’s form/ad copy clearly indicates SMS follow-up may occur.
- Partner will not use the system for spam, purchased lists, or prohibited content.
Provider may suspend accounts that present compliance risk.

10) Data Handling & Confidentiality
- Provider will treat Partner and client data as confidential.
- Provider will only access data necessary to run and troubleshoot the service.
- Partner is responsible for ensuring it has the right to share client data with Provider for service delivery.

11) Ownership
- Partner owns its client relationships, pricing, and positioning.
- Provider owns the underlying product, templates, and system configuration methods.

12) Term & Termination
- Month-to-month unless otherwise agreed.
- Either party may terminate with 14 days notice.
- Upon termination, Provider will:
  - stop automations at the end of the billing period
  - provide a basic export of KPI summaries upon request (where feasible)
- Partner agrees to remove Provider’s materials from new sales after termination.

13) Non-Circumvention (Optional but Recommended)
Provider agrees not to solicit Partner’s clients for 12 months after last active account date. Partner agrees not to reverse-engineer or replicate proprietary automation logic.

AGENCY ONBOARDING CHECKLIST (INTERNAL)
Use this checklist to onboard a new agency partner in <30 minutes.

A) Partner Setup
[ ] Confirm partner contact + onboarding call time
[ ] Confirm white-label preference (fully white-label vs “powered by”)
[ ] Confirm partner tier target this month (10 / 20 / 50)
[ ] Confirm minimum commitment: 10 accounts for priority support
[ ] Provide partner kit links: Deck, one-pager, SOP, KPI template, Terms
[ ] Confirm where leads will come from most often (Forms / FB Leads / CRM)

B) Sales Enablement
[ ] Share “3×10 accounts” plan and timelines
[ ] Provide vertical script options (home services default)
[ ] Provide client-forwarding email (below) for immediate use

C) First Client Account Intake (Repeat per client)
[ ] Business profile (services, zip codes, hours)
[ ] Lead sources + access (form URL, FB page access, webhook, etc.)
[ ] SMS copy: opening message + qualification questions
[ ] Booking: calendar link + routing rules
[ ] Test: submit 3 test leads, verify response + qualification + booking
[ ] Launch: confirm go-live timestamp + first-week KPI check-in

D) Enforcement Rules (Operational)
- If Partner has <10 active accounts after 14 days: move to standard support queue.
- Tier upgrades apply at next billing cycle once active account count is verified.
- Tier downgrades apply if active count stays below threshold for 2 consecutive cycles.
