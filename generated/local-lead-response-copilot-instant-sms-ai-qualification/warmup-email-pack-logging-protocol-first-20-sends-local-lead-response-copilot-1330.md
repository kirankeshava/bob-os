CRM LOGGING PROTOCOL (paste into your Sheet as notes)
- Status values: New → Ready to Send → Sent (T1) → Sent (T2) → Replied (Positive/Neutral/Negative) → Demo Booked → Closed Won/Lost.
- Touch numbering: Email #1 = T1, Email #2 follow-up = T2, LinkedIn connect = LI1, LinkedIn msg = LI2, SMS (only if compliant/opt-in) = SMS1.
- Required fields to complete per touch: Date, Channel, Template used, Personalization line used, Outcome (No reply/Reply), Next action + due date.
- Reply classification:
  - Positive: asks for details, pricing, integration, or shares calendar availability.
  - Neutral: “not now”, “send info”, “who are you”, “maybe later”.
  - Negative: “remove me”, “not interested”, “stop”. Immediately mark Do Not Contact.
