## Daily operating cadence (no paid tools)
### Day 0–1: Warm-up (protect deliverability)
1) Send 10–20 1:1 emails only (no mail-merge) from the dedicated sending inbox.
2) Keep copy short, plain text, one link max (either site or Calendly—not both) for the first 10.
3) Target lower-risk prospects first (contact@ / hello@ / info@ or agency ops emails) and a few personal addresses if publicly listed.
4) Track each send in the Google Sheet: Date, Prospect, Email used, Subject, Segment, Status=Sent, Next step date.

### Day 2–4: Ramp
- Day 2: 25 emails (max), 100% personalized first line.
- Day 3: 40 emails.
- Day 4: 50 emails.
- Always stop sending if bounce rate > 5% or spam complaints occur; shift to LinkedIn-only for that day.

### Day 5–7: Follow-ups + demo conversion
- Prioritize follow-up #1 to all “Sent-no-reply” from 48–72 hours ago.
- Respond to every reply within 15 minutes during business hours.
