## 4) Minimum Pilot KPI Dashboard (Definitions)
**Reporting cadence:** weekly email summary + optional shared sheet.

1) **Speed-to-Lead (STL)**
- Definition: time from lead submit → first SMS sent
- Target: < 60 seconds

2) **Contact Rate**
- Definition: % of leads who reply to the first SMS within 24 hours
- Target: baseline + improvement

3) **Qualified Rate**
- Definition: % of leads who meet basic filters (service area + service type + intent)

4) **Booked Rate**
- Definition: % of total leads that book a call/appointment via link or confirmed booking

5) **Show Rate**
- Definition: % of booked appointments that show/answer

6) **Rep Response SLA (internal)**
- Definition: time from qualified lead → human follow-up (if required)
- Used for escalation tuning
