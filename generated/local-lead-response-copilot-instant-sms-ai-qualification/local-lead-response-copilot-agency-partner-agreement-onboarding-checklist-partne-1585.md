Parties
This Partner Agreement (“Agreement”) is between:
- Provider: Local Lead Response Copilot (“LLRC”, “we”, “us”)

Purpose
Partner will resell or bundle LLRC to Partner’s end-clients (“Client(s)”). LLRC provides instant SMS lead response, AI-driven qualification, and booking/next-step automation.

Commercial Terms (Wholesale / Reseller)
1) Wholesale pricing (Partner pays LLRC)
- Standard: $399/account/month (1–2 accounts)
- Growth: $349/account/month (3–9 accounts)
- Scale: $299/account/month (10+ accounts)
“Account” = one Client location/brand with its own phone number(s), workflows, and reporting.

2) Recommended retail pricing (Partner charges Client)
- Typical: $499–$999/account/month depending on lead volume + booking complexity.
Partner keeps the margin between retail price and wholesale price.

3) Setup / implementation fees
- Default: $0 setup (to reduce sales friction).
- Optional: Partner may charge a setup fee to Client; LLRC does not require it.

4) Minimum commitment & priority support
- Minimum: 3 accounts for 90 days to qualify for “Priority Support” and volume pricing tiers.
- If Partner drops below the minimum during the commitment window, pricing may revert to Standard tier for remaining active accounts.

Billing & Payment
- Billing frequency: Monthly in advance per active account.
- Billing date: ________ (or first of month).
- Late payments: Service may be paused on overdue invoices after reasonable notice.

Provisioning & Ownership
- Partner owns the Client relationship.
- LLRC owns the software platform, configuration IP (templates, system prompts, standard workflows), and operational know-how.
- Client data belongs to Client. Partner is responsible for ensuring Client authorizes Partner/LLRC to process lead data.

What LLRC Provides
- Initial configuration per account based on intake (see checklist)
- SMS lead response and qualification flow
- Routing to booking link / calendar or call transfer / callback capture
- Basic reporting fields and monthly KPI summary format
- Partner support via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to

What Partner Provides
- Client acquisition, contracting, and first-line support
- Client onboarding: gathers access, brand info, offers, service area, and qualification rules
- Client compliance: confirms opt-in language on forms/ads and adherence to SMS rules
- Ensures Client responds to hot leads when needed (if human follow-up is part of the workflow)

Service Levels (Plain Language)
- Standard support: responses within 2 business days.
- Priority support (requires minimum commitment): responses within 1 business day.
- Critical issues (system down / no SMS): we will triage ASAP upon receipt.

Disclaimer: Deliverability depends on carrier filtering, lead quality, and Client’s opt-in language. LLRC will configure best practices but cannot guarantee carrier delivery.

Acceptable Use / Compliance (Important)
Partner agrees:
- Only message leads who provided consent/opt-in (e.g., form checkbox and disclosure or FB lead form consent).
- No spam, purchased lists, or cold outreach via the system.
- No prohibited content (illegal services, hate, harassment, etc.).
- If a lead opts out (STOP), the system must respect it and Partner/Client must not attempt to circumvent opt-out.

Data Processing & Security
- LLRC processes lead data only to provide the service.
- Partner should avoid sending sensitive personal data unless necessary (SSNs, medical info, etc.).
- LLRC will use reasonable safeguards but is not a regulated HIPAA/PCI vault.

Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep non-public pricing, technical details, and operational processes confidential.

Term & Termination
- Term: Month-to-month after any minimum commitment period.
- Cancellation: Provide 30 days notice before next billing cycle.
- For accounts in a commitment window: early cancellation may forfeit volume pricing and/or require payment through the commitment end (unless waived).

Client Portability
If Partner relationship ends:
- Active Client accounts may be migrated to direct billing with LLRC upon Client request.
- Partner agrees not to hold Client data hostage; LLRC agrees to cooperate with a reasonable transition.

Liability (Plain-Language)
- LLRC is not liable for lost revenue, missed calls, or indirect damages.
- Total liability (if any) is limited to amounts paid to LLRC in the prior 30 days for the impacted account.

Non-Exclusivity
Partner is free to sell other services. LLRC is free to work with other agencies.

3) PARTNER OPS SOP (INTERNAL)
========================================
Purpose
Run partner accounts with minimal founder involvement: fast provisioning, consistent QA, predictable reporting, and a clean escalation path.

Operating Rhythm
- Daily (Mon–Fri): Review critical alerts + failed sends + lead routing errors.
- Weekly: Review partner accounts with low contact/qualified/booked rates.
- Monthly: Send KPI report template back to Partner + highlight wins and action items.

Provisioning SOP (New Account)
1) Receive completed onboarding checklist from Partner (Section 2).
2) Create account record:
- Client name, niche, service area, owner contact, notification recipients
3) Configure workflow:
- First message (speed-to-lead), qualification questions (2–4), routing rules
- After-hours logic
- Disqualify logic
4) QA:
- Run 5 test scenarios (see checklist)
- Confirm timestamps captured for response-time reporting
5) Go-live confirmation email to Partner:
- Summary of flow
- Booking link / routing confirmation
- Support contact: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Legitimacy link for Client: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4

Support & Escalations
Tier 1 (Partner handles):
- Client asks “What is this text?” → explain consent + purpose
- Client wants copy tweaks, question wording, hours changes
Tier 2 (LLRC handles):
- SMS not sending / delivery plummets
- Booking link failures
- Lead source integration errors
Process:
- Partner emails agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to with:
  (a) Client name
  (b) Example lead phone number + timestamp
  (c) Description + screenshot if available
- LLRC responds within SLA depending on priority support status.

KPI Reporting SOP (Monthly)
Metrics to report per account:
- Median first response time (seconds)
- Contact rate = leads reached / total leads
- Qualified rate = qualified / reached
- Booked rate = booked / qualified
- Lead status breakdown counts: New, Contacted, Qualified, Booked, Not Qualified, No Response, Opted Out

Narrative to include:
- What improved vs last month
- Top 1–2 bottlenecks (e.g., low replies, drop-off at Q2)
- Next month actions (copy tweak, fewer questions, stronger CTA, after-hours routing)

Renewal & Expansion Motions
- If booked rate is strong: recommend scaling ad spend or adding second service line.
- If contact rate is low: review opt-in language, first-message clarity, sending number reputation, and lead quality.
- If qualified rate is low: tighten targeting or adjust questions to match the offer.

Offboarding SOP
- Confirm cancellation date
- Export/report last 30 days KPIs to Partner
- Ensure opt-out compliance remains intact
- Confirm whether Client will migrate direct to LLRC or fully terminate
