# Agency Partner Onboarding Checklist (Day 0–Day 7)

## Day 0 — Partner setup (30 minutes)
1. **Confirm tier + commitment** (Standard/Growth/Scale + 90-day minimum per account).  
2. **Confirm support channel**: all escalations to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to.  
3. **Decide your resale packaging**:
   - Standalone “Speed-to-Lead Copilot” $499–$999/mo, or
   - Bundle with ads/SEO (recommended).  
4. **Pick your niche focus (start with 1):** plumbers, HVAC, roofing, pest control, movers, med spas, legal intakes.

## Day 1 — Sales enablement (60 minutes)
1. Load these into your sales assets:
   - Reseller deck PDF (once exported)
   - One-page reseller sheet
   - ROI math: “first 5 minutes wins” narrative
2. Use the legitimacy link in outbound:  
   https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/4
3. Decide your offer: **“14-day pilot”** or **“30-day rollout”** (pilot recommended for closing fast).

## Day 2 — Client intake template (copy/paste into your onboarding form)
For every End Client, collect:
- Business name + website + Google Business profile link
- Service types + service area + hours
- Primary contact(s) + escalation contact
- Booking preference (calendar link, call routing, or both)
- Lead sources (forms/FB lead ads/CRM) + access
- Qualification rules (must-have, red flags, urgency triggers)
- Message tone (friendly/pro, short/long)
- Compliance: SMS consent language location + privacy policy URL

## Day 3 — Build + connect (Provider + Partner)
1. Create/confirm:
- Keyword-based or intent-based qualification questions (3–6 max)
- Lead status taxonomy: New, Contacted, Qualified, Not Qualified, Booked, No Response
2. Connect lead sources (forms/FB/CRM/webhook).
3. Route logic:
- Qualified → booking link and/or appointment request
- High urgency → immediate call transfer or “call now” CTA
- Not qualified → polite closeout

## Day 4 — QA testing (30–45 minutes)
Run 10 test leads end-to-end:
- Confirm speed-to-first-text
- Confirm question flow correctness
- Confirm booking link works
- Confirm notifications to the right staff
- Confirm lead status updates

## Day 5 — Launch
- Turn on for live traffic.
- Watch first 20 leads closely and flag anomalies.

## Day 6 — Optimization
- Tighten qualifying questions if too many unqualified leads reach booking.
- Reduce friction if contact rate is low (fewer questions, clearer CTA).

## Day 7 — First weekly report (send to End Client)
Include:
- **Median response time**
- **Contact rate**
- **Qualified rate**
- **Booked rate**
- Lead status breakdown + 3 recommendations
