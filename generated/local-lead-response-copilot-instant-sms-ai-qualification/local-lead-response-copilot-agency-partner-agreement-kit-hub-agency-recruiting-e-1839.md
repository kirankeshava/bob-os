## 4) Pricing, margins, and minimum commitment
### 4.1 Partner pricing (what Agency pays Provider)
- **$499 / account / month** (1–2 accounts)
- **$399 / account / month** (3–9 accounts)
- **$349 / account / month** (10–24 accounts)
- **$299 / account / month** (25+ accounts)

### 4.2 Suggested client pricing (what Agency charges its clients)
Agency may set its own pricing. Recommended:
- **$499–$999 / client / month** depending on niche, lead volume, and appointment value.

### 4.3 Minimum commitment for priority support
To receive **Priority Partner Support**, Agency agrees to a **minimum of 10 active accounts** within **30 days** of signing (the “10-in-30 commitment”).
- If Agency does not reach 10 active accounts in 30 days, Provider may move Agency to Standard Support until the threshold is reached.

## 5) “3×10 accounts” growth plan (recommended operating cadence)
Agency targets: **30 accounts in 30 days** by running three sprints:
- Sprint 1: close and launch 10 accounts (Days 1–10)
- Sprint 2: close and launch 10 accounts (Days 11–20)
- Sprint 3: close and launch 10 accounts (Days 21–30)

Provider supports Agency with onboarding docs, implementation SOP, and KPI reporting templates.

## 6) Onboarding & implementation responsibilities
### 6.1 Agency responsibilities
Agency will:
- Obtain client approvals for messaging, intake questions, and lead routing
- Provide access/credentials needed for lead sources (forms, FB lead ads, CRM)
- Ensure client’s business hours, service area, and offer details are accurate
- Be the primary point of contact for the client

### 6.2 Provider responsibilities
Provider will:
- Configure the account per the Implementation SOP
- Confirm leads are being received and responded to
- Provide monthly KPI reporting outputs (or templates) to Agency

## 7) Support, communication, and SLAs
- **Support channel:** Email to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- **Standard Support:** Response within 2 business days.
- **Priority Partner Support (10-in-30 or 10+ active accounts):** Response within 1 business day.
- **Critical incident target:** Acknowledge within 24 hours (business days).

## 8) KPIs and reporting (what “success” looks like)
Agency and Provider will track:
- **Median response time** (goal: near-instant)
- **Contact rate** (leads that respond)
- **Qualified rate** (leads that match criteria)
- **Booked rate** (calls/appointments set)
- **Lead status breakdown** (New, Contacted, Qualified, Booked, Not Qualified, No Response, Bad Data)

Provider supplies a monthly KPI template; Agency may add business metrics (revenue, close rate) if the client shares them.

## 9) Data handling & privacy
- Agency confirms it has the right to process client lead data and to message leads.
- Agency is responsible for client compliance with local messaging/consent laws and any platform policies.
- Provider will use lead data only to deliver the service and improve workflows for the account.

## 10) Acceptable use
Agency (and its clients) will not use the service for:
- Illegal, deceptive, or abusive messaging
- Spam or unsolicited outreach unrelated to a user’s lead submission
- Content that violates carrier/platform policies
Provider may suspend accounts that risk deliverability or violate policy.

## 11) Term, cancellation, and offboarding
- This template assumes month-to-month service.
- Either party may cancel with **30 days notice**.
- Upon cancellation, Provider will stop automations and provide reasonable export/hand-off info as available (e.g., workflow summary and KPI snapshots).

## 12) No guarantees
Provider does not guarantee revenue outcomes. The system is designed to improve speed-to-lead and measurable funnel KPIs; results depend on lead quality, offer, follow-up, and sales performance.
