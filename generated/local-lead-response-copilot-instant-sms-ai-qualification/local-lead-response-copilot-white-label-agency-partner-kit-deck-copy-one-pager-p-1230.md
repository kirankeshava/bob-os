## D) Implementation SOP (Agency + Our Team)

### Goal
Launch a new client in 7 days with measurable speed-to-lead improvements and clean status reporting.

### Roles
**Agency (Partner) owns:** client relationship, pricing, billing, brand voice approval, access procurement.
**We own:** technical setup, automation flow, qualification logic, testing, reporting framework.

### Step 1 — Client Intake (Day 0)
Collect:
1) Business name + hours + service area
2) Primary services (choose 3–8)
3) Qualification requirements (e.g., zip codes, job size, timeframe)
4) Desired outcome: book calendar vs “call now” routing
5) Current lead sources: website form, FB lead ads, Google LSA, GHL, etc.
6) Calendar link + availability rules
7) Team routing: who gets notified, by SMS/email, and escalation rules
8) Compliance notes (opt-in language on forms, required disclaimers)

Deliverable: completed intake form + access confirmed.

### Step 2 — Access + Connections (Day 1–2)
- Connect lead source(s): webhook/Zapier/GHL/FB lead integrations
- Verify phone number/SMS sending setup
- Connect calendar booking system (Calendly/GHL/Google calendar)
- Configure notifications (agency + client team)

Checkpoint: test lead submitted → system receives payload.

### Step 3 — Conversation Flow Build (Day 2–4)
Create:
- Opening text (brand tone)
- 2–5 qualification questions
- Branching logic (qualified/unqualified/after-hours)
- Fail-safes: “human takeover” instructions + stop keywords

Checkpoint: run 5 test leads (different outcomes) and confirm tagging/status.

### Step 4 — Go-Live + Monitoring (Day 5–7)
- Turn on live routing
- Monitor first 20 leads for:
  - deliverability
  - question completion rate
  - booking success
  - routing accuracy

Adjust:
- shorten questions if drop-off
- change order (urgency first)
- tighten qualification to protect sales team time

### Step 5 — Weekly Optimization (Ongoing)
Weekly review (15 minutes):
- response time
- contact rate
- qualified rate
- booked rate
- top drop-off question

Action examples:
- add “call me now” fast path for urgent leads
- after-hours booking path
- localized language (“What suburb are you in?”)
