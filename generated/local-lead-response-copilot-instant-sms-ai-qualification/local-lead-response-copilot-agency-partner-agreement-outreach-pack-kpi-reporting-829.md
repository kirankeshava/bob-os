# KPI Reporting Template (Copy/Paste into Google Sheets)

## Sheet 1: Monthly Summary (per Location)
**Columns:**
- Month
- Client / Location
- Total New Leads
- Leads Contacted (2-way SMS)
- Contact Rate %
- Qualified Leads
- Qualified Rate %
- Booked Appointments/Calls
- Booked Rate % (of total leads)
- Median First Response Time (seconds)
- No Response (after X attempts)
- Disqualified
- Wrong Number
- Spam/Test
- Notes / Actions Next Month

**Formulas (example):**
- Contact Rate % = Leads Contacted / Total New Leads
- Qualified Rate % = Qualified Leads / Total New Leads
- Booked Rate % = Booked Appointments / Total New Leads

## Sheet 2: Lead Status Breakdown (counts)
Statuses:
- New (received)
- Contacted
- Qualified
- Booked
- Disqualified (reason)
- No Response
- Wrong Number
- Spam/Test

## Sheet 3: Definitions (so agencies report consistently)
- **Median First Response Time:** median seconds from lead creation → first outbound SMS sent. (Median avoids outliers.)
- **Contacted:** lead sent at least 1 inbound reply OR a successful 2-way exchange.
- **Qualified:** lead meets criteria (service area, job type, timeline, budget if used).
- **Booked:** confirmed call/appointment time set (calendar booking or manual confirmation).
- **No Response:** no reply after the defined attempt policy (e.g., 6 touches over 24 hours).

## Sheet 4: “Attempt Policy” (recommended default)
- Touch 1: instant (0–15 seconds)
- Touch 2: +3 minutes
- Touch 3: +30 minutes
- Touch 4: next morning
- Touch 5: next afternoon
- Touch 6: final attempt next morning

**Escalation rule:** If lead indicates urgency (“today”, “emergency”), immediately route to live phone/dispatcher.
