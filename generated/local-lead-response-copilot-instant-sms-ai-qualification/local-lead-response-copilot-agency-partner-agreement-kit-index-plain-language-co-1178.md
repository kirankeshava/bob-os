IMPORTANT: This is a plain-language commercial agreement template for speed. It is not legal advice.

1) Parties + Purpose
This agreement is between (a) “Provider” (Local Lead Response Copilot) and (b) “Agency Partner” (the reseller). The purpose is to allow Agency Partner to resell the product to Agency Partner’s end clients (“Client Accounts”) under Agency Partner’s brand (white-label) while Provider supplies the underlying software, configuration support, and ongoing product operations.

2) Offer Summary (What Provider Delivers)
Provider supplies:
- Instant lead-response SMS workflows triggered by new leads (forms/FB lead ads/webhooks where available)
- AI-driven qualification prompts (short question flow) and routing (book call/appointment or handoff)
- Basic reporting fields for lead status + outcomes
- Support for onboarding/implementation per the SOP (intake → build → QA → launch)

Provider does NOT supply:
- Agency Partner’s advertising, lead generation, or creative
- A guarantee of booked jobs/revenue (we impact speed-to-lead and conversion mechanics)
- Legal compliance services for Agency Partner’s marketing programs

3) White-Label + Branding
Agency Partner may present the product under its own brand to its clients. Provider may remain undisclosed in Agency Partner’s sales materials.
- If a Client Account requires vendor disclosure, Provider will be disclosed as the underlying technology provider.
- Agency Partner will not misrepresent the product’s capabilities or claim guaranteed revenue outcomes.

4) Pricing, Margin + Volume Tiers (Agency Pays Provider)
Agency Partner pays Provider per active Client Account per month.
Suggested tiers (aligned to agency margin targets):
- Tier 1 (1–3 accounts): $499/account/month
- Tier 2 (4–9 accounts): $399/account/month
- Tier 3 (10–24 accounts): $349/account/month
- Tier 4 (25+ accounts): $299/account/month

Agency Partner’s recommended resale pricing to clients:
- $499–$999/account/month (Agency sets its own pricing)

5) Minimum Commitment + Priority Support
To access Tier 3+ pricing and priority support, Agency Partner agrees to:
- Minimum: 10 active Client Accounts
- Commitment term: 90 days from activation of the 10th account

If Agency Partner drops below 10 active accounts during the commitment term, pricing reverts to Tier 2 for remaining accounts OR Agency Partner pays the difference to maintain Tier 3.

6) Billing Terms
- Provider invoices monthly in advance for active accounts.
- New accounts are billed starting on the activation date.
- Late payments may result in pause of messaging/workflows for affected accounts until balance is current.
- Agency Partner is responsible for collecting payment from its clients. Provider’s relationship is with Agency Partner, not the end client (unless separately agreed).

7) Implementation, Turnaround Times + Partner Responsibilities
Provider’s standard onboarding includes: intake, configuration, QA, and launch.
Agency Partner agrees to provide for each Client Account:
- Business name, service area, hours, and booking link or scheduling instructions
- Lead sources (forms/FB ads/CRM) and access needed to connect (admin or developer access as required)
- The qualification questions and disqualifiers (or approve Provider defaults)
- Compliance confirmation: Agency Partner confirms it has permission to text leads who submit forms/ads

Target turnaround (once all inputs are received):
- Standard: 2–5 business days per account
- Priority (10+ accounts + complete intake): 1–3 business days per account

8) Support + Service Levels
- Support channel: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
- Standard support: 2 business day response target
- Priority support (10+ accounts commitment): 1 business day response target

Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain reliable operation. Agency Partner acknowledges that SMS delivery and platform integrations can be affected by third-party carriers/APIs.

9) Compliance, Consent + Messaging Rules
Agency Partner is responsible for ensuring that all leads have provided appropriate consent to be contacted by SMS and that Agency Partner’s campaigns comply with applicable laws and carrier policies (including TCPA where applicable).
Provider can supply suggested opt-out language (“Reply STOP to opt out”) and will honor STOP keywords at the workflow level where supported.
Agency Partner agrees not to use the product for spam, purchased lists without consent, or prohibited content.

10) Data Handling + Confidentiality
- Each Client Account’s lead data is confidential.
- Provider will access lead data only as needed to operate/support the service.
- Agency Partner will not share Provider’s proprietary implementation details, prompts, or workflow logic outside its organization.
- Provider may use aggregated, anonymized metrics to improve product performance.

11) KPI Reporting + Success Metrics (What We Track)
Provider supports reporting on:
- Median response time (lead created → first SMS sent)
- Contact rate (replied or reached)
- Qualified rate (meets criteria)
- Booked rate (appointment/call booked)
- Lead status breakdown (New / Contacted / Qualified / Unqualified / Booked / No-show / Won / Lost — as configured)

Agency Partner agrees to use these KPIs in monthly reporting to clients and understands results depend on lead quality, offer, and booking availability.

12) Non-Solicitation
Provider will not knowingly solicit Agency Partner’s end clients for a direct contract during the term of this agreement, except:
- If Agency Partner introduces the client for direct contracting in writing, or
- If the client independently contacts Provider and Provider confirms Agency Partner is no longer servicing the account.

13) Term, Termination + Offboarding
- Term: month-to-month unless a minimum commitment applies (Section 5).
- Either party may terminate with 30 days’ written notice after any commitment term.
- For material breach (non-payment, misuse, compliance violations), Provider may suspend service immediately.

Offboarding:
- Upon termination, Provider will disable workflows for terminated accounts on the end date.
- Upon written request, Provider will provide export of reporting metrics reasonably available for the prior 30 days.

14) Liability + No Guarantees
Provider does not guarantee conversion rates, booked jobs, or revenue outcomes. Provider is responsible for delivering the software functionality as described and providing reasonable support.

15) Acceptance
Agency Partner can accept this agreement by replying to the acceptance email with:
“I, [Name], accept the Local Lead Response Copilot Agency Partner Agreement v1.0 on behalf of [Agency Name].”
