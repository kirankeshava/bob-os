- Partner will not misrepresent capabilities, compliance, or results.

- **Wholesale (Partner → Provider):** $299–$499 per End Client account / month depending on tier and volume.
- **Minimum commitment for Priority Support + volume pricing:** **10 active End Client accounts** and **30-day minimum**.
- **Billing cadence:** monthly in advance.
- **Overages / usage-based costs (if applicable):** SMS/telephony fees can be (a) included in a fair-use allowance, or (b) billed at cost + handling. If billed separately, Provider will disclose the rate schedule prior to launch.

### End Client Relationship
- Partner owns the relationship with End Clients.
- Partner is responsible for End Client collection, contracts, and first-line support.
- Provider supplies second-line technical support and platform operations.

### Implementation & Setup Responsibilities
**Partner provides:**
- End Client intake details (see Intake Form below)
- Brand voice guidelines and approved qualification questions
- Access to forms/FB lead sources/CRM/calendar as needed

**Provider provides:**
- Account setup and workflow configuration
- QA testing for end-to-end lead capture → SMS → qualification → routing/booking
- Launch confirmation and KPI baseline guidance

### Support & SLA (Priority Support)
For Partners meeting the minimum commitment (10+ accounts):
- **Response target:** within 1 business day for support tickets; urgent incidents handled faster when possible.
- **Maintenance:** Provider may perform routine maintenance with minimal disruption.
- **Uptime:** commercially reasonable efforts (no guaranteed uptime in this template).

### Data Handling & Security
- Provider will handle lead/contact data only to deliver the service.
- Partner is responsible for ensuring End Client has appropriate consent language for SMS and that leads have opted in or have a lawful basis to contact.
- Provider will not sell lead data.

### Compliance (SMS/Calling)
- Partner and End Clients are responsible for compliance with TCPA, CTIA guidelines, carrier policies, and local laws.
- End Clients must include proper opt-out language where required (e.g., “Reply STOP to opt out”).
- Provider can provide recommended consent language, but compliance remains the Partner/End Client responsibility.

### Confidentiality
Each party will keep confidential information (pricing, configs, workflows, and non-public materials) confidential and use it only for purposes of this partnership.

### Term, Renewal, and Cancellation
- Month-to-month unless otherwise agreed.
- Either party may cancel with **14 days notice**.
- Accounts canceled mid-cycle may remain active until end of the paid period.

### Non-Solicitation (Optional)
Provider agrees not to actively solicit Partner’s End Clients directly for a period of 12 months, except where required for support escalations initiated by Partner.

### Liability Limits
- Provider is not liable for lost revenue, missed appointments, or consequential damages.

   - Email: agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to

   - Weekly snapshot for first month
   - Monthly KPI report ongoing

### B) End Client Setup (repeat per client)
**Goal:** have the first lead successfully flow through within 48 hours.

1. **Client Intake Form completed** (copy/paste template below).
2. **Lead sources connected** (choose):
   - Website form (Webhooks/Zapier/other)
   - Facebook Lead Ads
   - Google LSA (where possible via CRM)
   - Call tracking/landing page tool
3. **Routing rules confirmed:**
   - Business hours vs after-hours
   - Primary contact(s) + backup contact(s)
   - Geography/service types (if multiple)
4. **Qualification flow finalized (5–8 messages max):**
   - Service needed
   - Zip code/city
   - Timeline/urgency
   - Budget range (optional)
   - Homeowner/decision-maker (optional)
5. **Booking/call-to-action:**
   - Calendar link or booking tool
   - Or “tap to call” to dispatcher
6. **Compliance language approved:**
   - Opt-out instructions
   - Consent language on forms
7. **QA test:**
   - Submit a test lead
   - Verify SMS delivered instantly
   - Verify qualification → routing → booking works
8. **Launch + baseline:**
   - Record baseline median response time today
   - Set first-week KPI targets

## 4) “3×10 Accounts in 30 Days” Agency Plan (Volume-Based Revenue Path)

### Target (Agency)
Close **30 client accounts** in 30 days by selling to high-intent local businesses (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, pest control, junk removal, med spa, dentist).

### Economics (example)
- Agency sells at **$799/mo** average
- Agency pays Provider **$399/mo** average (blended)
- **Gross margin:** ~$400/mo per account
- **30 accounts:** ~$12,000/mo gross margin to the agency; Provider revenue ~$12,000/mo.

### Offer Structure (what agencies sell)
**“Instant Lead Response + AI Qualification + Auto-Booking”**
- <60 sec median first-response target
- Qualification flow (5–8 messages)
- Booking or call routing
- Monthly KPI report

### Pipeline math (simple)
Assume:
- 100 targeted agency outbound conversations → 20 demos → 10 closes (50% close rate on qualified demos)
To get 30 closes:
- **~60 demos total** in 30 days
- **~2 demos/day** booked

### Weekly execution cadence
**Week 1: Close 5–8 accounts**
- Focus: one vertical (e.g., HVAC + plumbing)
- Build 2 case-style ROI stories: “speed-to-lead” and “missed-lead recovery”
- Launch 10–20 outreach touches/day

**Week 2: Close 8–10 accounts**
- Add 2nd vertical (roofing + restoration)
- Add referral ask to every new client: “Know 2 other owners?”

**Week 3: Close 8–10 accounts**
- Partner with 1–2 ad agencies (they already drive leads; they need speed-to-lead)
- Run “Lead Response Audit” free offer

**Week 4: Close 5–7 accounts + stabilize onboarding**
- Tighten SOP and handoff
- Upsell annual prepay discount (agency-level)

### Daily activity targets (Partner sales rep)
- 20 targeted outbound touches/day (email + LinkedIn + calls)
- 5 live conversations/day
- 2 demos/day booked
- 1 close/day average on high-fit leads

### Minimum commitment + discounts (for scaling)
To hit 30 accounts quickly without support bottlenecks:
- Partners committing to **10+ accounts** get **priority support** and **volume pricing**.
- Recommend agencies sell multi-location bundles to get to 10 faster.
