## The problem (why leads don’t convert)
Local leads go cold fast. If the business (or your client) takes **5–30 minutes** to respond, prospects often:
- call the next provider,
- ignore voicemail,
- stop answering,
- become “unreachable” by the time someone follows up.

**Speed-to-lead wins.** The first responder captures a disproportionate share of bookings—especially in home services, local high-intent, and after-hours inquiries.

---

## KPI Promise
**< 60 seconds response time** from lead submission to first SMS touch.

**Setup in 24 hours** (typical) once we have access to the form/CRM/calendar.

---

## What it does (in plain English)
1) A lead submits your form / FB Lead Ad / landing page.
2) Our system **texts instantly**: “Hey {{name}}, quick question so we can match you with the right time/tech…”
3) The lead answers **3–5 quick questions** (service type, urgency, zip, budget range, preferred time, etc.).
4) Based on answers, we either:
   - **book a call/appointment automatically** (Calendly / Google Calendar), or
   - **route to a human** (hot lead alert to sales rep), or
   - **disqualify politely** and capture notes.
5) We push **conversation + qualification notes** into your CRM so your team (or your client) has context.

**Agency-friendly:** You can white-label this as your “speed-to-lead + booking automation” layer.
