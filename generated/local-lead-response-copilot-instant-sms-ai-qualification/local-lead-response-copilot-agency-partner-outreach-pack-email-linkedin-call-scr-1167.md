=== 6) Partner Onboarding Checklist (Internal + Partner-Facing) ===
Use this as a simple handoff checklist for the agency to launch each new client.

For each new client account, collect:
1) Business info
• Business name, service area, business hours, booking link (or calendar), phone number (handoff number)

2) Lead sources to connect
• Website form(s) URL(s)
• FB lead ads access (if applicable)
• CRM/webhook endpoint (if applicable)

3) Qualification goals
• What makes a lead “qualified” (service type, zip codes, budget range, timeline)
• Disqualifiers (out of area, wrong service, renter vs owner, etc.)

4) Conversation + booking rules
• 3–5 questions max
• If qualified → book appointment / request preferred time
• If not qualified → polite close + tag reason
• If no response → follow-up cadence (e.g., 5 min, 1 hour, next day)

5) Reporting cadence
• Confirm monthly report recipients
• KPIs tracked: median response time, contact rate, qualified rate, booked rate, lead status breakdown

6) Verification
• Test with 3 internal leads end-to-end (capture → SMS → qualify → booking/handoff)
