## D) Implementation SOP (Agency → Client → Go Live)

### Step 1 — Pre‑Sale Qualification (Agency)
Confirm:
- Lead volume (weekly/monthly)
- Lead sources (forms, FB lead ads, call tracking)
- Current response process (who follows up, when)
- Booking method (calendar link vs office scheduling)

### Step 2 — Client Intake (Required Inputs)
Collect:
1) Business name + location(s)
2) Service area/ZIPs
3) Services offered + exclusions
4) Hours of operation
5) Booking link OR scheduling instructions
6) Qualification questions (defaults provided)
7) Tone/voice (friendly/professional)
8) Primary contact phone + fallback
9) Compliance notes (opt-in language if required)

### Step 3 — Build (24 hours target)
- Configure lead source connection (form/CRM/webhook)
- Configure SMS messaging + qualification flow
- Configure booking routing rules
- Configure lead statuses + tags

### Step 4 — QA Checklist
- Test lead submission (realistic data)
- Confirm SMS sends under 60 seconds
- Confirm AI questions appear as expected
- Confirm booking link works
- Confirm lead statuses update

### Step 5 — Launch
- Turn on production routing
- Notify client of “go live” + expectations
- Set weekly check-in for week 1

### Step 6 — Optimize (Week 1–2)
- Adjust qualification questions to reduce drop-off
- Add follow-up reminders (e.g., 10 min, 2 hrs, next day)
- Refine “handoff to office” triggers

### Step 7 — Monthly Review
Deliver KPI report + 1–3 recommendations:
- improve contact rate
- tighten qualification
- adjust booking/handoff
