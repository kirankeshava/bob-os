---
What This Is
Local Lead Response Copilot is an instant SMS + AI qualification workflow for high-intent local leads.

It:
1) Texts every new lead instantly
2) Qualifies with short, friendly questions
3) Routes to booking (call/appointment) or your team
4) Writes clean notes back to your CRM/Google Sheet

---
How It Works (Simple)
Step 1 — Lead comes in
• Website form, landing page form, FB Lead Ads, Google Ads lead form, etc.

Step 2 — Instant SMS within 60 seconds
• “Hey {{name}}, this is {{business}}. Quick question so I can get you the right quote…”

Step 3 — 3–5 qualifying questions
Examples:
• What service do you need?
• What’s the address/zip?
• When do you want it done?
• Is this residential or commercial?
• Any photos/notes?

Step 4 — Booking + handoff
• If qualified: books a call/appointment on your calendar.
• If not: routes to a nurture sequence or your team.

Step 5 — CRM notes
• Conversation summary + qualification answers saved to CRM notes and/or a Google Sheet.
