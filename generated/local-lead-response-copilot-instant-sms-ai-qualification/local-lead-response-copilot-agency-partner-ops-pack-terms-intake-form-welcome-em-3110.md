### 1. Parties + Purpose
These terms describe how an agency (“Partner”) resells Local Lead Response Copilot (“Service”) to end clients (“Client Accounts”). The goal: instant lead response via SMS + qualification + booking to improve speed-to-lead and conversion.

### 2. White-Label / Resale
- Partner may resell the Service under its own brand (“white-label”) unless a Client requests otherwise.
- Partner is responsible for Client-facing sales, billing (if Partner bills the Client), and first-line support.
- The Service may be referenced as “Local Lead Response Copilot” when needed for technical or support reasons.

### 3. Pricing, Volume Tiers, Minimum Commitment
- Standard wholesale: **$499 / Client Account / month** to Partner.
- Volume tiers (wholesale):
  - **1–2 accounts:** $499/account/mo
  - **3–9 accounts:** $399/account/mo
  - **10–29 accounts:** $349/account/mo
  - **30+ accounts:** $299/account/mo
- Suggested retail guidance: Partner charges Clients **$499–$999/account/mo**.
- **Minimum commitment for Priority Support + volume pricing:** **3 Client Accounts**, with **60-day minimum**. (If Partner drops below 3 accounts, pricing reverts to the tier that matches active account count.)

### 4. What’s Included (Per Client Account)
- Connection to one lead source (form/FB lead webhook or supported intake)
- Instant SMS outreach to new leads
- AI-style qualification flow (short, structured questions)
- Routing/notifications to the business
- Booking handoff (call booking / appointment request) based on the chosen workflow
- Monthly KPI report (response time, contact, qualified, booked, lead statuses)

### 5. Implementation & Onboarding
- Typical go-live target: **48–72 hours** after Partner submits complete intake info.
- Partner must provide accurate Client Account details (see Intake Form below).
- Partner ensures Client has consent/permission to text leads and that all messaging complies with applicable laws and platform policies.

### 6. Support Model (Simple SLA)
- **Partner is first-line support** to Clients (basic questions, workflow changes, business rules).
- Our support is for Partner/admin issues: integrations, deliverability troubleshooting, bug fixes, platform behavior.
- Target response times (business days):
  - **Priority Support (3+ accounts):** respond within **1 business day**
  - **Standard:** respond within **2 business days**
- Escalation channel: email **agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to** with subject: “PARTNER ESCALATION — [Agency] — [Client]”.

### 7. Acceptable Use
Partner/Client will not use the Service for:
- Spam, purchased lead lists without consent, or harassment
- Misleading/impersonation practices
- Prohibited content (illegal goods/services)
We may suspend accounts that create deliverability risk or violate policies.

### 8. Data Handling & Confidentiality (Plain English)
- Lead data belongs to the Client.
- We use lead data only to operate the Service (respond, qualify, route, report).
- Partner should only share necessary data for setup/support.
- Partner and we will treat Client data as confidential and limit access to people who need it for service delivery.

### 9. Reporting & KPIs
Monthly reporting will include:
- **Median response time** (lead submitted → first SMS sent)
- **Contact rate** (replied/engaged)
- **Qualified rate** (met qualification criteria)
- **Booked rate** (appointments/calls set)
- **Lead status breakdown** (new, contacted, qualified, booked, not interested, unreachable)

### 10. Billing & Non-Payment
- Billing cadence: monthly, per active Client Account.
- If Partner is delinquent, we may pause new provisioning and/or suspend affected accounts until resolved.

### 11. Cancellation & Termination
- Partner can cancel any Client Account with **7 days notice** before the next billing cycle.
- If a minimum commitment applies (Priority Support/volume tier), early reduction below minimum may remove tiered pricing.
- We may terminate accounts for policy violations, abuse, or repeated non-payment.

### 12. No Guarantees (But Clear Outcomes)
We don’t guarantee revenue outcomes because conversion depends on lead quality and sales follow-up. We do commit to measurable improvements in **speed-to-lead** and a structured qualification workflow.
