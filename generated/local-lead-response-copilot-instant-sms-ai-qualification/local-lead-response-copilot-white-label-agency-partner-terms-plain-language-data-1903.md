## 4) Setup + Launch Expectations
- Partner provides required intake details (see SOP checklist).
- Standard launch target: **3–5 business days** after intake is complete.
- Partner is responsible for confirming:
  - Lead sources are connected (forms/FB leads/webhooks/CRM).
  - The client’s scheduling or booking endpoint is available.
  - Message content meets client’s business requirements.

## 5) Client Eligibility + Use Restrictions
This product is designed for legitimate, permission-based lead response.

**Not allowed:**
- Purchased lists, cold SMS blasting, deceptive offers, or any activity that violates carrier rules.
- Illegal, restricted, or deceptive industries.

We may suspend service for violations that create deliverability risk.

## 6) SMS, Consent, and Compliance Responsibilities
Because SMS compliance depends on how leads were collected, **Partner and Client** are responsible for:
- Ensuring leads have provided **express consent** to be contacted by text.
- Maintaining compliant opt-in language on forms/ads.
- Providing an opt-out mechanism (e.g., “Reply STOP to opt out”).
- Honoring opt-outs promptly.

**Provider will:**
- Help you implement standard STOP/HELP language and suppression rules.
- Provide suggested opt-in disclaimer copy when requested.
