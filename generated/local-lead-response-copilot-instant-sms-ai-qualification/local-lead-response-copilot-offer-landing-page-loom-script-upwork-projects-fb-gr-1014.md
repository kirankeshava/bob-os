1. **Lead comes in** (form / FB lead ad / call tracking / CRM)
2. **Instant SMS + AI** starts a 3–5 question conversation
3. **Qualified leads book** via your calendar link (or request a call)

- “Are you looking to schedule service in the next 7 days?”
- “What’s the address / ZIP code?”

- “What’s the best time for a tech to call?”
- “Do you own the property?”
