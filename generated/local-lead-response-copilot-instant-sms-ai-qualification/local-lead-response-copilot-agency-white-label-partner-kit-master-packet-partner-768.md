C) AGENCY IMPLEMENTATION SOP (INTAKE → BUILD → QA → LAUNCH)
============================================================

SOP: Deploy Local Lead Response Copilot for a New Client Account (Agency)

Timeline target: 48–72 hours from completed intake.

1) Intake (Day 0)
Collect:
• Business name + location(s)
• Lead sources: website form(s), landing page, CRM, FB lead ads
• Business hours + after-hours rules
• Service area + excluded zip codes (if any)
• Services offered (and “do not service” list)
• Preferred booking path:
  a) Calendar link, or
  b) Office call transfer / appointment request
• Qualification questions (3–6). Examples:
  – “What service do you need?”
  – “What’s your ZIP code?”
  – “When do you want service?”
  – “Is this for a residential or commercial property?”
• Disqualifiers (out of area, wrong service, budget, etc.)

2) Build (Day 1)
• Configure trigger from lead source
• Write/confirm:
  – First message (instant response)
  – Qualification flow
  – Follow-up sequence (no response)
  – Disqualification message
  – Booking/transfer logic
• Define lead statuses:
  – New
  – Contacted
  – Responded
  – Qualified
  – Booked
  – No response
  – Unqualified (with reason)

3) QA Test (Day 1–2)
• Submit test lead(s)
• Confirm message delivery and timing
• Confirm all answers map to correct statuses
• Confirm booking link/office handoff works
• Confirm after-hours messaging behavior

4) Launch (Day 2–3)
• Turn on live trigger
• Monitor first 10 leads
• Adjust phrasing if contact/qualified rate is low
• Confirm client can handle booked volume

5) Optimize (Week 1–2)
• Review KPI report weekly for first 2 weeks
• Optimize:
  – First message copy
  – Number of questions
  – Disqualification rules
  – Follow-up timing

6) Ongoing Ops (Monthly)
• Produce monthly KPI report
• Identify improvements:
  – Lower median response time
  – Improve contact rate via message copy
  – Improve qualified-to-booked via routing
