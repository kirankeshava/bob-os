1) What this is
You ("Agency Partner") can resell Local Lead Response Copilot as a white-label or co-branded service to your end clients ("Client Accounts"). Provider supplies the software + configuration + reporting framework. Agency Partner owns the client relationship and sets the retail price.

2) What the product does (scope)
For each Client Account, the system:
- Instantly responds to new leads via SMS (speed-to-lead)
- Asks short qualification questions (AI-driven flow)
- Routes outcomes (qualified, not qualified, no response)
- Books a call/appointment or hands off to your team

Out of scope unless explicitly agreed:
- Building or managing the client’s ad campaigns
- Writing/hosting client landing pages
- CRM migrations beyond basic field mapping
- Call center / answering services

3) Pricing to Agency Partner + minimum commitment
Week 1 is free launch / pilot for end clients (no end-client charges collected by Provider).
After pilot, Agency Partner pays Provider per active Client Account per month based on the tier you choose:
- Starter: $299 / account / month
- Pro: $399 / account / month
- Priority: $499 / account / month

Minimum commitment for discounted tiers and priority support:
- Discount tiers require a minimum of 10 active accounts OR a 60-day commitment for the first 3 accounts.
- If you drop below the threshold, pricing reverts to standard tier pricing the next billing cycle.

(You can charge clients $499–$999/account/month; you keep the margin.)

4) Volume discount guidance (example structure)
- 1–2 accounts: pay $399/account (recommended Pro)
- 3–9 accounts: pay $349/account
- 10–19 accounts: pay $319/account
- 20+ accounts: pay $299/account

5) Billing & payment terms
- Provider invoices monthly in advance based on active account count.
- Agency Partner is responsible for collecting payment from end clients.
- If an end client pauses, Agency Partner must notify Provider before the next invoice date to avoid charges.

6) Implementation & timelines
Typical setup per Client Account: 48–72 hours after receiving complete intake.
Agency Partner is responsible for:
- Ensuring the client has consent/opt-in language for SMS
- Providing access/credentials to forms/CRM/calendars
- Providing the offer details, qualification criteria, and booking rules

7) Support & priority rules
Standard support: email support via agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
Target response times (business days):
- Starter/Pro: within 24–48 hours
- Priority: within 12–24 hours

Emergency definition: system-wide outage, messages not sending, booking failure across accounts.

8) Compliance & consent (important)
Agency Partner agrees to ensure every Client Account has proper SMS consent/opt-in wording on forms and complies with TCPA/CTIA and local regulations. Provider supplies template wording, but Agency Partner and end client are responsible for using it correctly.

9) Data handling & confidentiality
- Provider will only use lead data to operate the service and improve qualification performance.
- Agency Partner and Provider agree to keep client lists, pricing, and performance data confidential.

10) White-label positioning
Agency Partner may present the service as its own (“white-label”) or co-branded.
Agency Partner will not misrepresent capabilities or claim results that are not supported by reporting.

11) Termination / offboarding
Either side can end the partnership with 14 days written notice.
On offboarding:
- Provider will export basic configuration details upon request.
- Provider will stop messaging and disable accounts at the end of the billing period.

12) No legal advice
This document is operational guidance, not legal advice. Agency Partner should have counsel review compliance language as needed.
