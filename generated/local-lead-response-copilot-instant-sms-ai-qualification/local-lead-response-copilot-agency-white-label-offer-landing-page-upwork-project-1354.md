## The Problem (Agencies lose clients here)
Local leads go cold fast. If your client (or your call center) takes 5–30 minutes to respond, they lose to the fastest competitor.

**What agencies need:** a consistent, provable **speed-to-lead KPI** that increases booked appointments without hiring more staff.

---
## The KPI Promise
**< 60 seconds response time** to every new lead (forms, FB Lead Ads, website chat/contact forms).

We automatically:
1) Text the lead instantly
2) Ask 3–5 short qualification questions
3) Book the appointment/call (or hand off the lead)
4) Push clean notes into your CRM/Google Sheet

---
## How it works (simple)
**Step 1 — Connect lead sources**
- Website forms (Webhook/Zapier/Make)
- Facebook Lead Ads
- Google/LSA lead handoff (via form/email parsing where possible)

**Step 2 — Instant SMS starts the conversation**
A friendly, brand-matched opener goes out immediately.

**Step 3 — AI qualification (3–5 questions)**
Examples (home services):
- “What type of service do you need?”
- “What’s the address/zip code?”
- “How soon are you looking to get this done?”
- “Any photos/details you can share?”

**Step 4 — Booking + routing**
- Book directly to Calendly/Google Calendar
- Or route to dispatcher/sales team when qualified

**Step 5 — CRM notes + tracking**
- Push conversation summary + tags to your CRM (HubSpot, GoHighLevel, etc.) or Google Sheets.
