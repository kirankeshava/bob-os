## The problem (why agencies lose deals)
Local service leads go cold fast. If a prospect submits a form or FB lead ad and waits 5–30 minutes, they often:
- book with the fastest competitor
- stop answering
- forget they even requested the quote

Most agencies can generate leads, but **speed-to-lead is where conversion is won or lost**.

### **< 60 seconds response time**
The moment a lead is created, they receive an SMS that:
1) confirms the request
2) asks **3–5 short questions** to qualify intent
3) routes them to **book a call/appointment automatically**
4) writes **clean notes back to your CRM**

**Setup in 24 hours.** 
**7-day pilot available.** 
**White-label ready.**

## How it works (simple)
1. **Lead comes in** from form / FB lead ad / webhook
2. **Instant SMS** goes out within seconds
3. AI asks **3–5 qualifying questions** (budget, urgency, service type, address/ZIP, timeline)
4. Based on answers, the lead is:
   - **Booked** (Calendly/Google Calendar)
   - **Routed** (call transfer, team alert, or tag)
   - **Disqualified** politely (or pushed to nurture)
5. **CRM update**: notes + qualification score + transcript
