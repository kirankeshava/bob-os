E) 7-DAY OUTBOUND EXECUTION SOP (no spend)

Goal for week: 40 targets → 40 initial touches → 25 follow-ups → 10–15 demos booked → 3 paid pilots.

Day 1 (Send initial touches)
- Send 20 owner touches (email if available; otherwise contact form).
- Send 5 agency touches.
- Log each touch in the pipeline tracker: Date, Channel, Variant used, Next follow-up date.

Day 2 (Finish initial touches + Follow-up #1 for Day-1 sends)
- Send remaining 20 owner/agency touches.
- Send Follow-up #1 to Day-1 non-responders.

Day 3 (Call/SMS assist + reply handling)
- For any target with a phone number and no email: send the SMS template.
- Respond to every reply within 10 minutes during business hours.
- Objective: book demos, not “explain via text.”

Day 4 (Follow-up #2)
- Send Follow-up #2 (“close your file”) to non-responders from Day 1.
- Re-engage “interested” replies with 2 questions (lead source + booking/routing).

Day 5 (Demo day)
- Run 7–10 minute vertical demo.
- Close for pilot: “If we can go live within 24 hours, are you good to start this week?”
- Immediately send: (1) agreement, (2) payment request, (3) intake form link.

Day 6 (Onboarding calls)
- Run the 30-minute onboarding (from your onboarding script).
- Confirm go-live date/time and recipients.

Day 7 (Hypercare)
- Monitor first lead flows, confirm messages deliver, confirm booking/routing, adjust questions.
- Ask for 1 referral: “Know another {vertical} operator who would want instant SMS follow-up?”

Operational notes
- Always include the legitimacy URL + email in first touch.
- Keep DMs/SMS short: book the demo.
- If contact info is missing: use website contact form first; if still blocked, use SMS if number present.
