4) Pricing, Margins, and How Billing Works
4.1 Standard wholesale pricing (Partner pays LLRC):
• Starter: $499 / account / month (1–2 accounts)
• Growth: $399 / account / month (3–9 accounts)
• Scale: $349 / account / month (10–24 accounts)
• Agency+ (priority support): $299 / account / month (25+ accounts)

4.2 Suggested retail (Partner charges end clients): $499–$999 / account / month depending on niche, lead volume, and automation scope.

4.3 Minimum commitment for discounted tiers:
• Growth tier requires 3 active accounts.
• Scale tier requires 10 active accounts.
• Agency+ requires 25 active accounts.
If Partner drops below the minimum, pricing reverts to the next applicable tier the following billing cycle.

4.4 Billing cadence
LLRC invoices Partner monthly in advance for active accounts. Partner is responsible for collecting from end clients.

4.5 Usage-based costs (if applicable)
If SMS/telephony/API usage fees exist in your deployment, they will be disclosed in writing and either:
(a) passed through to Partner at cost, or
(b) included in a higher flat wholesale plan.

Partner acknowledges the rollout plan:
• Month 1 goal: 10 active accounts
• Month 2 goal: 20 active accounts
• Month 3 goal: 30 active accounts

6) Onboarding + Implementation Responsibilities
6.1 Partner responsibilities
Partner will collect from each client (minimum):
• Business name, hours, service area
• Offer/lead source (forms/FB lead ads)
• Routing rules (who receives notifications)
• Calendar/booking link OR desired booking workflow
• Qualification questions + disqualifiers
• Compliance notes (opt-in language, STOP, privacy)

6.2 LLRC responsibilities
LLRC will:
• Configure intake + routing
• Configure the AI qualification flow and lead statuses
• Set appointment booking handoff (calendar link or call booking)
• Provide a go‑live checklist and QA test

7.1 Standard support (all tiers)
• Support channel: email to agent_bob_replit+lead-copilot@agentmail.to
• Response target: within 1 business day

7.2 Priority support (Agency+ / 25+ accounts OR by written addendum)
• Response target: within 4 business hours on business days
• Escalation handling for live incidents

7.3 Exclusions
Support does not include rewriting the Partner’s client offers, ad management, CRM rebuilds, or custom integrations beyond what is stated. Those can be quoted separately (if offered).

8) Data, Privacy, and Security (Plain Language)
8.1 Roles
Partner and/or end client is the data controller. LLRC acts as a data processor/sub‑processor.

8.2 Data handling
LLRC processes lead data only to deliver messaging, qualification, routing, and reporting.

8.3 Access
Partner will ensure only authorized users have access to client accounts.

8.4 Retention
Unless otherwise requested, LLRC retains lead conversation logs for operational and reporting purposes. Partner may request deletion/export for a specific account.

9) Compliance (SMS + Consent)
Partner is responsible for ensuring lead sources include appropriate opt‑in language for SMS follow‑up.
LLRC will include standard STOP/HELP handling where applicable.

10) Reporting + KPIs
LLRC provides monthly reporting templates with:
• Median response time
• Contact rate
• Qualified rate
• Booked rate
• Lead status breakdown
Partner may deliver these reports to clients under Partner branding.

11) Term, Renewal, and Termination
11.1 Term

11.2 Termination
Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice.

11.3 Non‑payment
LLRC may pause accounts for non‑payment after written notice.

11.4 Offboarding
Upon termination, Partner may request export of reporting data for active accounts and confirmation of account shutdown.

LLRC retains ownership of its software, templates, and configuration methods. Partner retains ownership of its own branding and client relationships.

13) Non‑Exclusivity
This agreement is non‑exclusive. Partner may resell other tools; LLRC may work with other agencies.

14) Liability (Plain Language)
