Below is the ready-to-paste outbound kit for Local Lead Response Copilot (Instant SMS + AI Qualification). It includes: (1) a tracking/CRM sheet schema, (2) 10 warmup 1:1 email variants to protect deliverability, and (3) two 2-step outbound sequences (agency + operator) referencing the legitimacy URL and support email.

A) TRACKING / CRM SHEET (Google Sheets columns)
Create a sheet with these columns (one row per prospect):
- Prospect Type (Agency / Operator)
- Company
- Website
- Source URL (Clutch/UpCity/LinkedIn/Maps/Upwork)
- Niche (HVAC/Plumbing/Roofing/Pest/Water Damage/Med Spa/Marketing Agency)
- Location
- Decision Maker Name
- Role (Owner/Founder/Head of Growth/Marketing Manager)
- Email
- LinkedIn URL
- Phone (only if public + relevant)
- Personalization Angle (e.g., “FB lead ads + slow response”, “after-hours missed leads”, “forms not texting”)
- Personalization Line (1 sentence)
- Sequence Used (A1/A2 or O1/O2)
- Channel (Email / LinkedIn / SMS)
- Legal Basis Note (B2B legitimate interest / existing inquiry / public business contact)
- Send Date/Time
- Touch #
- Status (Not Sent / Sent / Replied / Interested / Not Now / Bad Fit / Unsubscribe / Demo Booked)
- Reply Sentiment (Positive/Neutral/Negative)
- Demo Link Sent? (Y/N)
- Demo Date
- Notes

B) WARMUP EMAILS (send 10–20 over 1–2 days before scaling)
Goal: short, human, low/no links, ask for a quick reply.
1) Subject: Quick question
Body: Hey {{FirstName}} — I might be mixing things up. Are you the right person to talk to about lead handling / follow-up at {{Company}}?

2) Subject: Correct contact?
Body: Hi {{FirstName}}, quick one: who owns your inbound lead response process (forms, FB leads, missed calls) at {{Company}}?

3) Subject: Intro
Body: Hey {{FirstName}} — I’m Bob. Do you handle the marketing + lead intake side at {{Company}}, or is there someone else I should reach?

4) Subject: Routing
Body: Hi {{FirstName}}, does your team route new leads to techs/sales via SMS, email, or calls? Curious what you prefer.

5) Subject: Not sure if this is you
Body: Hey {{FirstName}} — should I be speaking with you or someone else about appointment setting / speed-to-lead at {{Company}}?

6) Subject: Lead response
Body: Hi {{FirstName}} — quick question: do you currently respond to new web leads within 5 minutes consistently?

7) Subject: Missed calls
Body: Hey {{FirstName}}, do you have an automated text-back for missed calls after hours, or is it manual?

8) Subject: FB leads
Body: Hi {{FirstName}} — are FB leads going to a CRM/automation today, or just email notifications?

9) Subject: Point me to the right person
Body: Hey {{FirstName}} — who’s best to talk to about improving conversion on inbound leads at {{Company}}?

10) Subject: Simple question
Body: Hi {{FirstName}} — is your lead follow-up process centralized, or does each rep/tech handle it independently?
