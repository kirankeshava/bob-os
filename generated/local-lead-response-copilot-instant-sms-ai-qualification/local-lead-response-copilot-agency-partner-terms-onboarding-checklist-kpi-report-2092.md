Partner is responsible for:

## 7) Support Levels & SLAs (Practical)

- Provider processes lead data solely to deliver the Copilot service.

- Partner is responsible for Client-facing privacy notices and obtaining necessary consent.

## 9) Messaging/Calling Compliance

- Provider can supply suggested disclosure language, but Partner/Client must approve it.

## 10) Performance Disclaimer
- Provider will make best efforts to improve speed-to-lead and qualification.
- Results depend on lead quality, client availability, offer, geography, seasonality, and follow-up.
- Provider does not guarantee revenue, booked jobs, or specific conversion rates.

## 11) Cancellation & Termination
- Partner may cancel any account with **7 days’ notice** before the next billing date.
- Provider may suspend accounts for nonpayment or misuse.
- Upon cancellation, Provider will disable automation and provide reasonable export of reporting metrics where available.

## 12) Non‑Solicitation (Lightweight)
- Provider will not knowingly bypass Partner to sell directly into Partner-managed Client accounts during the active relationship.
- Partner will not misrepresent Provider’s system or claim proprietary ownership of Provider’s underlying software.
