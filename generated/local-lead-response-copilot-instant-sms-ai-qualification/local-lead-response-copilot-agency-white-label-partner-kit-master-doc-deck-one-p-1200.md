## 3) Agency Implementation SOP (intake → build → QA → launch → optimize)

### A) Intake (Day 0)
Collect from client (or your AM):
1) Business name, hours, service area (ZIPs/cities)
2) Lead sources: website form, FB lead ads, landing pages
3) Booking preference:
   - Option A: Book to Calendly/booking link
   - Option B: Request callback + route to office
4) Qualification questions (choose 3–6):
   - service needed
   - urgency/timeline
   - location
   - property type (res/com)
   - budget range (optional)
5) Routing rules:
   - after-hours behavior
   - emergency lead handling
   - do-not-text keywords

### B) Build (Day 1)
We configure:
- Brand voice (friendly/professional)
- Opening message + compliance language
- AI-guided question flow
- Lead status categories (New, Contacted, Qualified, Booked, Unqualified, No Response)
- Booking + routing logic

### C) QA Testing (Day 1–2)
Agency verifies:
- Test lead triggers correct instant SMS
- Questions are accurate for the niche
- Booking link works
- Office notifications received
- Stop/unsubscribe keywords work

### D) Launch (Day 2–3)
- Turn on for all lead sources
- Confirm after-hours rules
- Set expectations with client: first 7 days are tuning

### E) Optimize (Weekly, first month)
- Review KPI report weekly
- Tune questions to reduce drop-off
- Adjust office handoff scripts
- Identify lead sources with low quality and update ads/forms

### Client-facing expectation script (agency can copy)
“We reply to every new lead instantly by text, ask a few quick questions to qualify, then book you or hand the lead to your office. You’ll see monthly metrics: response time, contact rate, qualified rate, and booked rate.”
