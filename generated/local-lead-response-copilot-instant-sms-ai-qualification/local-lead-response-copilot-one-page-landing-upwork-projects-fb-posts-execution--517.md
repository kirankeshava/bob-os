## How it works (simple)
1. **Lead comes in** (Website form / FB Lead Ads / landing page / call tracking form)
2. **Instant SMS**: “Hey {{name}}, it’s {{business}} — got your request. Quick question so we can give accurate pricing…”
3. **AI qualification (3–5 questions)** (job type, urgency, location, budget range, photos/measurements if relevant)
4. **Booking or handoff**
   - If qualified: **auto-book** (Calendly) or send “Tap to book” link
   - If edge case: route to human with full context
5. **CRM notes**: The full summary + answers are pushed into the CRM / Google Sheet / email
