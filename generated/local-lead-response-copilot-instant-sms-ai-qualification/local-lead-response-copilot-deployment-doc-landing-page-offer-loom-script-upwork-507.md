HOW IT WORKS (SIMPLE FLOW)
1) Lead comes in
From: Website form, Facebook Lead Ads, Google/LSA form, or webhook/Zapier.
2) Instant SMS sent
“Hey {{first_name}}, this is {{business_name}}—got your request. Quick 3 questions so we can get you scheduled.”
3) AI qualification (3–5 questions)
Examples:
• “What service do you need?”
• “What’s your ZIP code?”
• “When do you want this done?”
• “Is this for a home you own?” (optional)
4) Booking + handoff
• If qualified: sends booking link or offers times and confirms.
• If not qualified: tags/dispositions and routes appropriately.
5) CRM notes + visibility
• Writes conversation summary + answers back into CRM/Google Sheet.
• Optional: notifies your team via SMS/email/Slack.
