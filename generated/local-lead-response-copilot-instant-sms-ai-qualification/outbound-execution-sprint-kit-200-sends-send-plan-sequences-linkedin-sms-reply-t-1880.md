1) 7-Day Send Plan (200 total)
Goal: 200 outreaches → 20 replies → 10 demos booked.
Day 1–2 (Warmup): 15–20/day, 1:1 manual sends, plain text, no tracking pixels. Use only one link maximum (prefer none on Day 1). Spread across the day (max 3/hour). Log bounces.
Day 3: 40 sends (20 agency / 20 operator). Use 2 subject lines A/B.
Day 4: 50 sends. Keep copy identical; only personalize first line.
Day 5: 50 sends + follow-ups to Day 3 non-responders.
Day 6: 40 sends + follow-ups to Day 4 non-responders.
Day 7: Follow-ups + LinkedIn nudges; focus on booking.
Deliverability rules: keep formatting simple; avoid attachments; keep URLs to 0–1; don’t CC/BCC; remove hard bounces immediately.

2) Personalization Formula (45 seconds)
Agencies (Clutch/UpCity/site): “Saw you run lead gen for {niche}—especially {specific service line from site or case study}. Wondering if you’ve seen speed-to-lead issues when forms/FB leads come in after-hours.”
Operators (Google Maps/site): “Noticed you’re getting leads for {service} in {city} and you’ve got strong reviews around {keyword from reviews}. Quick question—do web/FB leads get a text back in under 60 seconds right now?”

7) Reply-to-Demo SOP (convert replies fast)
Classify reply:
A) Interested: respond <5 min → offer 2 time slots + Calendly.
B) “Send info”: send 5-bullet summary + ask 1 qualifying question.
C) Not now: ask “When should I follow up?” and set date.
D) Objection (already have CRM / call center): ask “Do you text within 60 seconds after-hours?” then position as bolt-on.
