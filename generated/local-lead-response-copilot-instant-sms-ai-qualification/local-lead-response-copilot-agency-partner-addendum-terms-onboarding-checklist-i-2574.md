## 1) Agency Partner Terms (Plain-Language, Non-Legal)
**Purpose.** These terms explain how agencies (“Partner”) resell Local Lead Response Copilot (“Service”) to end clients (“Client”) without requiring us on every sales call.

### 1.1 White-label positioning
- Partner may present the Service as “powered by” the Partner or as a white-labeled “Lead Response Copilot.”
- Partner may not claim they built proprietary carrier infrastructure or AI models if untrue; use: “We use an automated SMS + AI qualification workflow.”

### 1.2 Pricing, billing, and minimum commitment
- Partner wholesale price: **$299–$499 per Client account/month** (tiered by volume; see pricing sheet).
- Partner agrees to a **minimum commitment** of **3 active accounts** to receive priority support and onboarding.
- Accounts are billed monthly while active. If Partner pauses/cancels an account, workflows are disabled at end of billing period.

### 1.3 What’s included (baseline)
- Instant SMS response to inbound leads (form/FB lead ads/CRM lead entry) using approved scripts.
- AI-driven qualification flow (short question sequence) to categorize lead intent.
- Routing: notify office, collect details, push to calendar booking or “call now” handoff.
- Monthly KPI report template and performance review notes.

### 1.4 Responsibilities
**Partner responsibilities**
- Owns Client relationship, payment collection from Client, and first-line support.
- Ensures Client has permission to text leads (opt-in language in forms/ads).
- Provides necessary access/integration info (see intake form below).

**Our responsibilities**
- Configure workflows per the approved intake.
- Maintain service uptime best-effort and resolve platform issues.
- Provide second-line support and implementation guidance.

### 1.5 Support and SLA expectations (Week 1 launch emphasis)
- **Priority support** (Partners with 3+ active accounts): responses within **1 business day** for implementation and bug issues.
- Non-priority: best-effort within **2 business days**.
- After-hours: critical incidents only (system down).

### 1.6 Compliance, consent, and messaging rules
- Partner must ensure Clients include clear consent language on lead forms/ads, e.g. “By submitting, you agree to receive texts/calls…”
- Partner/Client must honor STOP/opt-out requests immediately.
- No prohibited content; no spamming purchased lists; only message inbound leads or explicitly opted-in contacts.

### 1.7 Data handling and privacy
- We use lead contact data only to operate the Service (qualification, routing, reporting).
- Partner remains the controller/owner of Client relationship and lead data.
- On request, we will provide exports of lead status logs for the reporting period.

### 1.8 Termination
- Either party can terminate with **14 days notice**.
- For non-payment or compliance violations, termination may be immediate.
- Upon termination, workflows are disabled and data export provided on request.

### 1.9 No guarantees
- We improve speed-to-lead and qualification consistency; outcomes depend on lead quality, offer, and follow-up. We do not guarantee revenue.

## 2) Agency Partner Onboarding Checklist (Internal)
Use this to launch each new Client account fast (target: **48–72 hours** from intake to live).

### Step A — Sales handoff (Partner)
- [ ] Client has agreed to the monthly fee and understands texting will be used to respond instantly.
- [ ] Client confirms they have consent language/opt-in on forms/ads.
- [ ] Client chooses booking method: (a) book on calendar, (b) “call now,” or (c) both.
- [ ] Client identifies business hours and after-hours handling.

### Step B — Intake + assets (Partner → Us)
- [ ] Completed Intake Form (Section 3 below)
- [ ] Access details (as applicable): CRM/webhook/zap, Facebook lead access, form provider, calendar link
- [ ] Brand voice notes + banned phrases
- [ ] List of services + service area + minimum job size (if relevant)

### Step C — Build (Us)
- [ ] Set up lead capture source → instant SMS trigger
- [ ] Configure qualification questions (max 3–5)
- [ ] Configure routing: notify office, book link, tags/status
- [ ] Create lead statuses for reporting: New, Contacted, Qualified, Not Qualified, Booked, No Response, Wrong Number/Spam

### Step D — QA (Partner + Us)
- [ ] Test lead submitted → SMS received within 60 seconds
- [ ] Test STOP keyword handling
- [ ] Verify booking link works and notifications fire
- [ ] Confirm copy tone matches Client

### Step E — Launch
- [ ] Turn on live traffic
- [ ] Day 1 check-in: verify contact/qualified/booking events in logs
- [ ] Week 1 optimization: adjust Qs, tighten routing, refine booking CTA
