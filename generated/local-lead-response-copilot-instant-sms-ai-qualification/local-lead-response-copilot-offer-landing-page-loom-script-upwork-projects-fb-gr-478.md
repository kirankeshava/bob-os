## The problem (why speed-to-lead wins)
Local service leads decay fast. If you don’t respond immediately, the prospect either:
- books the competitor who answered first,
- ignores follow-ups,
- or submits “3 quote” forms and turns into a price shopper.

**Most agencies + local businesses fail at one thing:** a human can’t respond instantly 24/7.

**What we fix:** Every lead gets an immediate, natural SMS conversation that qualifies and routes the lead to booking—without your team lifting a finger.

## How it works (simple)
1) **Lead arrives** (form/FB lead ad/webhook/Zapier).
2) **Instant SMS goes out** (<60 seconds): “Hey Sarah—this is Mike’s Roofing. Quick question so we can help…”
3) **AI asks 3–5 short qualifying questions** (service needed, urgency, location, budget range, availability).
4) **Booking / routing**
   - Book a call/estimate (Calendly, Google Calendar, GoHighLevel calendar)
   - Or route to the right team member + add notes
5) **CRM notes + tags** are saved (lead quality, job type, urgency, objections).

**Human takeover is always possible**: any time your team can jump into the thread.
