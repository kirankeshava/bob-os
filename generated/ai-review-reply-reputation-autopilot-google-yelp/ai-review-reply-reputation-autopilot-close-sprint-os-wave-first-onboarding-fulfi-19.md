## 3) 20-minute discovery script (revenue impact + objections)
### 0:00–2:00 Set frame
“Thanks for the time. In 20 minutes we’ll (1) quantify how reviews impact calls/appointments, (2) see what’s breaking today—speed, tone, negatives, or consistency—and (3) if it’s a fit I’ll recommend the smallest plan that fixes it. Sound good?”

### 2:00–6:00 Current state
1) “How many new reviews do you typically get per week/month on Google and Yelp?”
2) “Who responds today, and how long does it usually take?”
3) “Do you have any locations or just one?”
4) “Any recent 1–3 star reviews you’re worried about?”

### 6:00–10:00 Revenue linkage (prompt ROI)
1) “When someone calls, what’s the average value of a booked job/appointment?”
2) “Roughly how many calls/appointments come from Google Business Profile in a month?”
3) “If we improved responsiveness + kept your rating from slipping, what would even 1–2 extra bookings/month be worth?”

(If they don’t know numbers)
“Totally fine. We’ll use conservative math: even one prevented 1-star spiral or one recovered lead often pays for this.”

### 10:00–14:00 Risk + workflow (AI safety)
Key reassurance:
- “AI drafts; your policy controls what can be posted.”
- “We have escalation triggers for legal/medical/privacy issues.”
- “We can run in approval mode until you’re comfortable.”

Ask:
1) “Any phrases we should never use? Any compliance rules?”
2) “Who should get escalations for negative reviews?”

### 14:00–17:00 Present plan options
Option A (lower): “We respond to everything fast, brand-safe, and escalate negatives.”
Option B (higher): “Everything in A plus multi-location, higher volume, and deeper weekly KPI reporting.”

### 17:00–20:00 Close + logistics
Close line:
“If we can start today, it’s a one-time setup of {{Setup}} plus {{Monthly}} for month one. After payment we start onboarding immediately and go live within 48 hours once access is granted. Should we do Starter or Growth?”

Objection: “We can do it ourselves.”
Response: “You can—most owners just don’t do it consistently. The value is speed + consistency + escalation so you never miss a revenue-impacting review. If you did it perfectly already, you wouldn’t be on this call.”

Objection: “AI risk / wrong tone.”
Response: “We can run approval mode for the first 1–2 weeks. Plus we use prohibited topics, tone rules, and escalation triggers. Nothing sensitive gets posted automatically.”

Objection: “Too expensive.”
Response: “Let’s anchor on one recovered booking per month. If that’s not realistic, we shouldn’t do this. Is one extra booking/month realistic from stronger reviews + faster responses?”

## 5) Order form terms (email-friendly)
- **Service:** Draft/publish responses to Google Business Profile + Yelp reviews; escalation; weekly KPI report.
- **Client responsibilities:** Provide platform access; provide brand voice rules; respond to escalations within 24 hours where needed.
- **SLA:** Responses drafted/published within 1 business day for new reviews (unless in Manual approval mode).
- **Approval modes:** Manual (client approves each), Post-approval (we post, client can request edits), Auto (after calibration period).
- **Escalation:** All 1–2 star reviews escalated immediately; 3-star escalated based on trigger phrases.
- **Limitations:** No guarantees of rating increases; we provide best-practice response management.
- **Liability:** Client remains responsible for business decisions and customer outcomes; we do not provide legal/medical advice.
- **Payment:** Setup + Month 1 due upfront; renewal monthly; service may pause if unpaid.
- **Termination:** Month-to-month after month 1; cancel anytime with 14 days’ notice (no refund for time already served).

## 6) 48-hour onboarding SLA (checklist)
**T0 (immediately after payment): Send Intake Email**
- Ask for:
  1) Brand voice (3 adjectives + 2 example replies they like)
  2) Do-not-say list
  3) Offer details (if any) and what not to mention
  4) Escalation contacts (name/email/phone)
  5) Approval mode choice (Manual/Post/Auto)

**Access collection (same day):**
- Google Business Profile: request Manager access to the correct Location(s).
- Yelp: request Business Owner/agent access or invite as collaborator.

**Calibration (Day 1):**
- Create 10 sample replies (mix of 5-star, 4-star, 1–2 star) and get sign-off.
- Confirm escalation triggers and response boundaries.

**Go-live QA (Day 2):**
- Verify posting permissions work.
- Verify escalation alerts route correctly.
- Send “Go-live confirmation” message with what to expect weekly.
