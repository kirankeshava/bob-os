# (3) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable, no API dependency)
## Sources covered
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

## Response SLAs
- Growth: within **24 business hours**
- Pro: within **12 business hours**
- Agency: within **24–48 business hours**

## Posting method (no-API, deliverable immediately)
1) **Preferred:** client adds us as a **GBP Manager** (and Yelp business access where possible).
2) **Fallback (default-safe):** we draft replies in a shared doc/email; **client posts** using provided copy.

## Human-in-the-loop workflow (brand safety)
- Draft response using templates + business context.
- QA pass for: tone, claims, incentives, HIPAA/medical/legal issues, competitor mentions, profanity.
- **Auto-approve rule:** 4–5★ reviews that contain no flagged keywords/PII.
- **Approval required:** 1–3★ reviews OR any flagged content.

## Escalation rules
Escalate within SLA when:
- Rating 1–2★, or 3★ with complaint keywords (refund, fraud, lawsuit, discrimination, harassment).
- Mentions safety incidents, medical outcomes, minors, personal data, staff names + accusations.
- Requests refund/chargeback, threats, or press/social escalation.

Escalation output:
- A short internal summary
- Suggested next step (call customer, offer remediation, request details offline)
- Draft public reply + optional private message suggestion

## Explicit exclusions (to protect accounts)
- No review gating (only asking happy customers).
- No paying/incentivizing for reviews.
- No impersonation.
- No deleting/disputing reviews without explicit client authorization.
