## (3) Fulfillment Scope (week-1 deliverable) + Constraints

### Sources
- Google Business Profile reviews
- Yelp reviews

### Response & Escalation Rules (brand-safe)
- **4–5★**: can be **auto-approved** if no flags; still follow brand voice + offer rules.
- **3★**: default “service recovery” tone; **approval recommended** unless client opts into auto.
- **1–2★**: always **escalate** within SLA and draft a response that acknowledges issue + moves offline.
- **Hard flags requiring approval**: medical/legal claims, discrimination/harassment, refunds/comp, pricing disputes, employee naming, HIPAA/PHI indicators.

### SLA
- DFY Growth: respond within **24 business hours**
- DFY Pro/Agency Pro: **12 business hours**

### Posting Method (no API dependency)
Because Google/Yelp APIs are limited and approvals can be complex, week-1 delivery uses access + human-in-loop:
1) **Preferred**: client adds us as manager on GBP; for Yelp, gives **Business Owner/Manager** access.
2) **Fallback** (allowed by offer): we draft replies in a shared doc/email; client posts using provided copy. This guarantees deliverability even without API.

### What’s explicitly excluded (to stay compliant)
- No incentivized reviews / “review gating”
- No promises to remove reviews; we can provide dispute guidance but not impersonate
- No fake reviews

### Setup deliverables (what $500 setup covers)
- Brand voice intake + banned phrases + escalation contacts
- Offer/discount rules (what we can/can’t mention)
- Approval policy chosen (auto vs approval thresholds)
- Reporting baseline + access verification

## Week-1 “Human-in-the-loop” Operating SOP (outline)
1) **Onboard (Day 0–1):** collect brand voice, do/don’t say, escalation contacts, access.
2) **Daily capture (Day 1+):** check GBP/Yelp or receive notifications; log each review in tracker.
3) **Draft response:** generate response using brand rules + policy.
4) **QA:** ensure no restricted claims; match tone; confirm escalation if needed.
5) **Approve/post:**
   - Auto-post for 4–5★ if allowed and access exists.
   - For 1–3★ or flagged: send approval request; then post or provide copy for client-post.
6) **Escalate:** open ticket with summary + recommended recovery action; track resolution.
7) **Weekly KPI report:** send 1-page summary + highlights + next actions.
