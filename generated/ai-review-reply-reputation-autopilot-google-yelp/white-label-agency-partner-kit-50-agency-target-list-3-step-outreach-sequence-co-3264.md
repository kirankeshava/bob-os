## 2) What’s Included (White-label deliverables)
### A) Review Reply Workflow (Google + Yelp)
- Daily review ingestion (manual or via exports/API where allowed by platform policies)
- Reply drafted in client brand voice
- Compliance checks: no incentives, no PHI/PII disclosure, no confrontational language
- Posting method:
  - Option 1: Agency/client posts from draft queue (zero access required)
  - Option 2: We post via delegated access (where available) after onboarding

### B) Escalation Rules
Escalate within **4 business hours** when:
- Rating is **≤ 3 stars** OR
- Any review includes keywords indicating legal risk, safety, discrimination, chargebacks, refunds, fraud, harassment, or private data

Escalation package includes:
- Recommended response options (2–3)
- Suggested internal action checklist
- Optional “manager-only” short reply vs. full resolution reply

### C) Weekly KPI Report (White-label)
Delivered weekly to agency (and optionally client) with:
- Average rating (7/30/90 day)
- New reviews count
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative review volume + escalations
- Top themes (service, staff, price, wait time)
- 3 recommended operational fixes

## 6) Onboarding SOP (Agency + Location)
**Step 1 — Intake (15 minutes)**
Collect:
- Business name(s), locations, GBP/Yelp links
- Brand voice: “friendly & warm” vs “professional & concise”
- Words to avoid; competitor mentions to avoid
- Escalation contacts (email + phone)
- Special policies: refunds, warranty, hours, booking links

**Step 2 — Access / Workflow choice**
- Draft-only workflow (no access): we deliver drafts in a shared sheet / email queue
- Post-with-access workflow: client grants access where possible

**Step 3 — Go live**
- Start daily monitoring
- First week: tighter feedback loop
- After week 2: “set and forget” cadence
