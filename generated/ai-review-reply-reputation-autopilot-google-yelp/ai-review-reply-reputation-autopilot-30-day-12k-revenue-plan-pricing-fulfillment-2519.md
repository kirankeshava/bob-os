- Fallback (default for Yelp if access is slow): we draft responses daily and send “ready-to-post” copy; client posts. SLA still applies to drafting.

Response SLAs:
- Growth: 24 business hours for all new reviews
- Pro: 12 business hours for all new reviews

Escalation rules (auto-ticket + notify client contact):
- Any 1–3★ review
- Any review mentioning: refund, lawsuit/legal, discrimination, injury, medical outcome, HIPAA/PHI, safety incident
- Any suspected fake/spam review (we flag; client decides dispute)

Approval rules (human-in-the-loop):
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-approve unless banned keyword/offer/compliance flag triggers manual approval
- 1–3★ reviews: require manual approval by client OR use pre-approved “make-it-right” templates with no admissions of fault

Explicit exclusions (compliance/safety):
- No review gating, no incentivized reviews, no buying reviews
- No deletion promises; no impersonation; no disputing reviews without client authorization
- We do not provide legal advice; we recommend a neutral response and escalation

- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)

- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)
- Escalations opened vs resolved
- Top 3 customer themes (service, wait time, price, staff, etc.)
