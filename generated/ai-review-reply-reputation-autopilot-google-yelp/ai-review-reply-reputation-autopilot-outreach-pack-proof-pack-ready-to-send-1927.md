2) AI Safety & Brand Protection Workflow (summary)
- Brand voice calibration: choose tone (friendly/professional/concise), do/don’t phrases, and preferred sign-off.
- Prohibited content: no admissions of fault/liability, no medical/legal claims, no personal data, no arguments.
- Escalation triggers: any 1–2★ review; mentions of discrimination, injury, fraud, chargebacks, police, lawsuits; requests for refunds; HIPAA/PHI risk.
- Approval modes:
  (a) Manual approval: client approves every response.
  (b) Post-approval: we post, client can request edits; negative reviews always escalate.
  (c) Auto: we post within rules; negative reviews escalate.
- Audit log: every drafted response + timestamp + final action (posted/escalated/edited).
