## 4) Fulfillment Scope (what we do) + Constraints (week 1 deliverable)
### Review sources supported (week 1)
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

### SLA & operating cadence
- Check/capture reviews **daily (Mon–Fri)**.
- Draft response within SLA (Growth 24h; Pro 12h).
- Weekly KPI report sent **every Monday** covering prior week.

### Escalation rules (negative reviews)
**Escalate (ticket + notify) when any occurs:**
- 1–3★ rating
- Mentions: refund, lawsuit, discrimination, injury, HIPAA/medical specifics, fraud, safety, harassment, threats
- Staff named + accusation
- Anything requiring compensation offer or factual dispute

**Escalation deliverable:** recommended response options (2 variants) + suggested offline resolution script + request for facts from client.

### Posting method (no hard API dependency)
Because Google/Yelp APIs and permissions can be limiting, week-1 delivery uses **human-in-the-loop**:
1) **Preferred:** client grants us **GBP Manager access** (and Yelp Business Owner access if available). We draft + post.
2) **Fallback (always available):** we draft responses in a shared doc/email; client posts. We still track KPIs.

### What’s explicitly excluded (risk control)
- No review gating/incentivization that violates platform policies
- No “removal” guarantees; we can suggest dispute language but not impersonate
- No legal advice; we provide brand-safe communication drafts only

## 6) Fulfillment SOP Outline (week-1 checklist)
**Onboarding (Day 0–2)**
- Collect: locations, hours, services, offers, brand voice adjectives, do-not-say list, escalation contacts, approval preferences.
- Access: request GBP Manager; request Yelp access or confirm client-post workflow.
- Confirm SLA + escalation rules.

**Daily Ops (Mon–Fri)**
1. Capture new reviews (manual check or notification emails/screenshots).
2. Classify: 4–5★ standard vs 1–3★ escalation vs flagged keywords.
3. Draft response in brand voice (policy-safe, no incentives).
4. QA pass (tone, compliance, no admissions of liability, no sensitive data).
5. Approval:
   - Auto-approve: most 4–5★ unless flagged.
   - Require approval: all 1–3★, flagged content.
6. Post (access) or send to client to post.
7. Log: rating, platform, theme tags, response timestamp, escalation status.

**Weekly KPI Report (every Monday)**
- New reviews (#)
- Average rating change (7/30 day)
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews (#) + escalation count
- Resolved/escalated-to-resolution count
- Top themes (3 bullets) + suggested operational action
