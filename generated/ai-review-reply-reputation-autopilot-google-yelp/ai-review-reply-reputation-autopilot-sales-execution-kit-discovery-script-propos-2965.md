6) 48-HOUR ONBOARDING SOP (DFY)
Goal: go-live in 48 hours from payment + access.

T+0–2 hours (after payment)
- Send “Welcome + Onboarding Checklist” email
- Confirm approval mode (Manual / Post-approval / Autopilot)

T+2–24 hours (access + brand voice)
A) Access collection
- Google Business Profile: request “Manager” access to location(s)
- Yelp: request Business Owner/agent access (or draft-only if not possible)

B) Brand voice intake (email reply acceptable)
- 3 examples of how you want to sound
- Words/phrases to avoid
- Any offers to mention (or never mention)
- Escalation contact(s): name + email + phone
- Hours of operation and service area notes

C) Escalation rules confirmation
- Confirm triggers for immediate escalation
- Confirm if compensation/refunds can ever be offered (usually “no” in public replies)

T+24–48 hours (calibration + go-live)
- Draft 5 sample replies across: 5-star, 4-star, 3-star, 1–2 star escalation
- Get approval (if Manual) or confirm guardrails
- Start responding per SLA
- Start weekly KPI report cadence (send first baseline snapshot)

Weekly KPI report (simple):
- New reviews count
- Avg star rating
- Response rate
- Median response time
- # escalations + resolution notes
