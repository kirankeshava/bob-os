1) ICP + lead qualification (fast filter)
Target: local businesses where reviews directly drive calls/appointments (dentists, medspas, chiropractors, home services, gyms, restaurants).
Must-have signals:
- Google rating 3.6–4.6 OR rating is fine but owner rarely responds.
- 10+ new reviews/month (or 30+ reviews in last 90 days).
