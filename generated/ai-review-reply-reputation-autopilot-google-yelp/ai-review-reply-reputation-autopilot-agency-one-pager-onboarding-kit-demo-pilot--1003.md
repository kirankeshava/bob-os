# 3) 2 MINI CASE-STUDY MODULES (ANONYMIZED)

## Case Study A — Home Services (HVAC/Plumbing-style)
**Problem:** Reviews were coming in consistently, but replies were sporadic. 1★ reviews sometimes sat unanswered for weeks, and the owner only responded when angry.

**What we did:**
- Set a 24-hour reply SLA for all new Google reviews
- Created a calm, accountable voice framework (“apologize + take offline + make it right”)
- Added an escalation rule for 1–2★ so the owner approved sensitive replies quickly

**Results (first 30 days):**
- Response time improved from “days/weeks” to **<24 hours median**
- Response rate increased to **~95–100%** of new reviews
- Negative reviews were de-escalated with consistent invites to resolve offline

**Proof placeholder:** Screenshot of GBP Insights: response rate + example reply thread.

## Case Study B — Med Spa / Aesthetics
**Problem:** Staff were hesitant to reply due to privacy concerns and fear of sounding “salesy.” Positive reviews weren’t leveraged; negative comments weren’t handled consistently.

**What we did:**
- Implemented privacy-safe language rules (no confirming procedures; neutral phrasing)
- Built a premium, warm tone with short replies and subtle brand reinforcement
- Established a “negative review playbook” with escalation for anything medical/claims-related

**Results (first 6 weeks):**
- Response cadence became consistent (same-day or next-day)
- Improved perceived professionalism (customers referenced replies when booking)
- Narrative rating lift: stabilized feedback flow and reduced impact of isolated negatives
