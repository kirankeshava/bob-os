## 5) Key Terms (Plain-English)
### Access & onboarding
- Customer must provide one of the following:
  - Google Business Profile manager access (preferred), and Yelp Business account collaborator access, OR
  - exported review notifications forwarded to **agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to** plus permission to post on their behalf.

### Approval workflow (two modes)
1) **Approval required (default):** We draft and send for approval. Customer approves via email confirmation.
2) **Auto-approve rules (optional):** Customer can opt into auto-posting for 4–5 star reviews using pre-approved templates, while 1–3 star reviews always escalate for manual approval.

### Escalation rules
- Any 1–2 star review triggers an escalation note with suggested resolution steps.
- We can suggest refunds/redo language but do not authorize refunds—customer decides.

### Volume limits & fair use
- Starter includes up to 60 reviews/month; Pro up to 150. Overages require add-on.
- We may recommend pausing auto-approve if review volume spikes or brand risk increases.

### Compliance & platform limitations
- We comply with Google/Yelp policies. If a platform blocks posting due to account issues, the SLA is paused until access is restored.

### Cancellation
- Month-to-month after trial. Cancel any time; service continues through the paid period.
