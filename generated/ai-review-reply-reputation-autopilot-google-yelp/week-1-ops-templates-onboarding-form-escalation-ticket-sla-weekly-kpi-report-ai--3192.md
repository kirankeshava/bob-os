SLA RULES (WEEK 1)
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-draft + auto-approve unless flagged keywords appear.
- 3★ reviews: draft reply; send to client for approval before posting (unless client opts into auto-approve).
- 1–2★ reviews: ALWAYS escalate to client contact; require approval before posting.
- Keyword flags (always escalate + require approval): lawsuit, attorney, fraud, scam, police, unsafe, harassment, discrimination, HIPAA/medical records, chargeback, credit card, “reporting to”, “BBB”, “news”, “FTC”.

DEFAULT RESPONSE PRINCIPLES (BRAND-SAFE)
- Thank them, acknowledge concern, no admissions of fault, no personal data, move details offline, provide a clear next step.
- Never argue. Never reveal customer specifics. Never offer incentives for reviews.
