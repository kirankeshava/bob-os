# 4) Fulfillment scope (week-1 deliverable) + constraints
## Review sources
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp Business

## Posting method (no API dependency)
**Default (preferred):** client adds us as **Manager** on Google Business Profile; for Yelp, client either grants Business Owner access or we use a client-post workflow.

**Fallback (always works):** we draft responses and deliver them in a daily email/Google Doc; client posts them. This avoids API access entirely.

## Response SLA
- Growth: reply drafts prepared within **24 business hours** of new review detection.
- Pro: within **12 business hours**.

## Approval rules (human-in-the-loop)
- **Auto-approve** drafts for 4–5★ reviews unless the review references medical/legal claims, refunds, discrimination, or threats.
- **Client approval required** for: 1–3★ reviews, refund disputes, legal/medical keywords, accusations, personal data, or requests for compensation.

## Escalation rules
Escalate immediately when:
- 1★ review
- Allegations of fraud, discrimination, injury, HIPAA/PHI, legal threats
- Reviewer requests refund/chargeback
- Staff named + misconduct claim

Escalation output:
- Recommended next step (call/email template)
- Public response draft that is de-escalatory and policy-safe
- Internal resolution tracker entry (status: Open → Contacted → Resolved)

## Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
Include:
- New reviews by platform
- Average rating trend (last 4 weeks)
- Response rate
- Median response time
- # negative reviews (1–3★)
- # escalations opened/resolved
- Top 3 themes (speed, price, staff, cleanliness, etc.)

## Explicit exclusions (to stay compliant/deliverable)
- No review gating, no incentivized reviews
- No deleting reviews
- No disputing/flagging reviews without client authorization
