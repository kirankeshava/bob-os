ONBOARDING SOP (48-hour SLA)
T+0 to T+2 hours (after payment)
1) Send Intake Email + checklist + request access.
2) Create client record in tracker (see fields below).

T+2 to T+24 hours
3) Collect access:
   - Google Business Profile: request Manager access to the Location Group or Location.
   - Yelp: request Business Owner/agent access (or credentials provided securely).
4) Collect Brand Voice:
   - Tone: friendly/professional/clinical/playful
   - Must-say phrases (e.g., “call our office” / “book online”)
   - Never-say phrases (discounts, legal admissions, medical claims)
   - Escalation contacts (name/email/phone)

T+24 to T+36 hours
5) Configure guardrails:
   - Negative review triggers: ≤3 stars, mentions of “refund”, “lawsuit”, “fraud”, “injury”, “discrimination”, “HIPAA”, etc.
   - Response templates per rating band (5, 4, 3, 1–2)
   - Approval mode default (Manual for week 1 recommended)

T+36 to T+48 hours
6) QA:
   - Draft 5 sample replies from recent reviews.
   - Get client sign-off on voice and prohibited topics.
7) Go-live:
   - Start responding to new reviews per SLA.
   - Backlog handling: agree on maximum backlog count (e.g., last 30–60 days).
