5) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE; NO API DEPENDENCY)

Supported review sources in week 1:
- Google Business Profile reviews (primary)
- Yelp reviews (supported via access or client-post workflow)

Response SLA:
- Growth: 24 business hours
- Pro / Agency Pro: 12 business hours

Escalation rules (creates “damage control” system):
Escalate immediately if:
- Rating is 1–3★
- Contains legal threats, medical claims, discrimination, safety incidents, chargebacks
- Mentions employee names negatively
- Mentions refunds, lawsuits, HIPAA/PHI, or “report to BBB/Attorney General”
Escalation output:
- Internal ticket with: review text, recommended response options (2–3), risk notes, who to contact, deadline

Posting method (deliverable without engineering):
Default (recommended):
- Client adds us as Manager on Google Business Profile.
- For Yelp: client grants Business Owner/Manager access OR we run “draft + client posts” until access is available.
Fallback (always available):
- We draft responses in a shared doc or email; client posts natively.

Approval rules (human-in-the-loop):
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-approve using pre-agreed brand voice unless flagged keywords appear.
- 1–3★ reviews: require client approval before posting (unless client opts into “auto-post safe template” policy).

Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating trend (7/30-day)
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews (1–3★) count
- Escalations opened vs resolved
- Top 3 themes (e.g., wait time, cleanliness, staff friendliness)

Explicit exclusions (protects deliverability + policy):
- No incentivized reviews, no “review gating,” no fake reviews.
- No deleting reviews.
- No disputing reviews on behalf of client without explicit authorization and client submission.
