# 3) TWO MINI CASE STUDIES (ANONYMIZED MODULES)

## Case Study 1 — Local Home Services (Single Location)
**Problem:** The owner was busy in the field and reviews went unanswered for days or weeks. A few negative reviews started ranking prominently, and leads mentioned “no one responds.”

**What we did:**
- Implemented daily monitoring + reply drafting
- Set a simple brand voice: “friendly, accountable, solution-first”
- Added an escalation rule: all 1–2 star reviews required approval within 2 hours
- Started weekly KPI reporting (review velocity + response time)

**Results (first 30 days):**
- Median response time improved from ~5–7 days to **< 12 business hours**
- Response rate reached **~100% for new reviews**
- Negative reviews were de-escalated with calm, offline-resolution language; one customer updated their review after resolution
- Sales team reported fewer “are you legit?” objections on inbound calls

**Proof you can show (placeholders):** screenshots of reply timestamps + weekly KPI snapshot.

---

## Case Study 2 — Aesthetics / Med Spa (High-Intent Local Search)
**Problem:** The clinic had a strong average rating, but inconsistent replies. The most visible reviews included unanswered questions about outcomes, scheduling, and pricing transparency.

**What we did:**
- Created a premium, concierge voice style guide
- Built “approved language” blocks for common themes (wait times, pricing confusion, service satisfaction)
- Implemented Draft + Post workflow (manager access) for fast turnaround
- Added a recurring insights section: top 3 themes + suggested ops fixes

**Results (6–8 weeks narrative):**
- Response time reduced to **same-day** for most new reviews
- Improved perceived professionalism: more reviews mentioned “they responded quickly”
- Reduced escalation load: only sensitive issues required approval due to standardized, pre-approved language
- Leadership used weekly insights to fix a scheduling bottleneck that was causing repeat complaints

**Proof you can show (placeholders):** before/after response-time chart + anonymized theme summary.
