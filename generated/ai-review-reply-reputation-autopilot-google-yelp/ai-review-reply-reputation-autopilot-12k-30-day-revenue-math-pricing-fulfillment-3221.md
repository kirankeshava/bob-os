## Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable; no engineering required)
### Review sources
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp business listing

### Constraints (be explicit)
- We do **not** delete reviews, guarantee removals, or offer incentives.
- We do **not** “review-gate” (asking only happy customers to review).
- Yelp API access is limited; assume **no API** in Week 1.

### Posting method (Week 1)
**Default:** client grants access.
- Google: add us as **Manager** on GBP.
- Yelp: add us as a user on Yelp for Business.

**Fallback (zero-access option):** client-post workflow.
- We draft responses in a shared doc or email.
- Client copies/pastes to post.
- This is slower but deliverable immediately.

### Daily workflow (human-in-the-loop)
1) **Capture reviews**
- Manual check 1–2×/day OR client forwards review notifications to agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to.

2) **Draft responses**
- Generate response with brand voice rules (tone, greetings, sign-off, offers allowed, banned phrases).

3) **QA & safety pass**
- Check for: HIPAA/medical claims, legal admissions, refunds/promises, profanity mirroring, competitor mentions.

4) **Approval rules**
- 4–5★: auto-approve unless flagged.
- 1–3★: require approval OR use pre-approved playbook language.
- Any legal/medical keywords: escalate to owner contact.

5) **Post**
- Post directly (preferred) or send client-post instructions.

6) **Escalation + resolution tracking**
- Create an escalation ticket with: review link, issue category, suggested resolution script, owner contact, due date.

### Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
Metrics:
- New reviews (count)
- Average rating (weekly + trailing 30 days)
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- # negative reviews (1–3★)
- # escalations opened / resolved
- Top 3 themes (from review text)
