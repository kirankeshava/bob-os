6) 7-Day Pilot Call Script (10–15 minutes)
Goal: book the pilot or close the first month.

Opener (30 sec):
“Thanks, [Name]. In 10 minutes I’ll confirm (1) review volume, (2) who approves sensitive replies, and (3) what ‘good’ looks like. If it’s a fit, we start a 7-day pilot and you’ll see daily coverage + a KPI snapshot. Sound fair?”

Discovery (ask, then mirror back):
1) “Which platforms matter most—Google, Yelp, both?”
2) “How many locations?”
3) “Roughly how many new reviews per week?”
4) “Who responds today—and what usually breaks? (time, tone, consistency, forgetting)”
5) “Any categories you never want discussed publicly? (HIPAA/medical, pricing disputes, employee names, legal threats)”
6) “Do you want us to post directly, or draft-only for your approval?”
7) “When a 1–2★ review hits, who should we notify and how fast?”
8) “What’s the business impact when reviews go unanswered? (lost leads, team stress, refunds, reputation)”

Positioning (30–60 sec):
“Here’s the system: we capture new reviews, draft brand-safe replies in your voice, escalate negatives immediately, and send a weekly KPI email: response rate, response time, sentiment buckets, and themes. It’s ‘done-for-you operations’ powered by autopilot workflows—so it’s consistent and fast.”

Offer (tie to their answers):
“Based on [X reviews/week] and [draft vs post], I recommend starting with the 7-day pilot. We’ll cover new reviews daily, and we’ll set an escalation rule for anything below [3★] or containing [keywords].”

Common objections (quick responses):
A) “We already reply sometimes.”
“Totally—this isn’t about ‘sometimes.’ It’s about same-day coverage, consistent tone, and never missing a negative review. The KPI snapshot shows you what’s actually happening.”

B) “I don’t want robotic replies.”
“Agreed. We use your voice rules + do-not-say list, and we escalate anything sensitive. Goal is human, calm, and on-brand—no generic templates.”

C) “I don’t want to give access.”
“No problem. We can start draft-only: you approve, we adjust the voice, then switch to posting later if you want.”

D) “What if someone threatens legal action / HIPAA?”
“We do not debate facts publicly. Those get escalated immediately and we draft a neutral ‘take it offline’ response for approval.”

Close (pick one):
Option 1 (pilot close):
“Let’s start the 7-day pilot. Best email for onboarding? I’ll send the access checklist + brand voice form today.”

Option 2 (tier close):
“If you want full coverage ongoing, we’ll roll straight into the monthly plan after the pilot. Would you prefer [Tier A] or [Tier B] based on your volume?”
