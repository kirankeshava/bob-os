4) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (Week-1 deliverable) + CONSTRAINTS
Sources supported (week 1):
• Google Business Profile reviews (via manager access OR client forwards notifications / screenshots)
• Yelp reviews (via Yelp Business Owner access OR client-post workflow)

Posting methods (no API requirement):
• Preferred: client grants GBP Manager access and Yelp Business Owner access so we can post directly in native UI.
• Fallback (zero-access): we draft responses and send “copy/paste-ready” replies daily; client posts within SLA.

Daily workflow (human-in-the-loop):
1) Capture: check new reviews 1–2x/day (or via alerts the client forwards).
2) Draft: generate response in a controlled prompt using brand voice + policies.
3) QA: human QA for brand safety, compliance, and tone.
4) Approvals:
   - Auto-approve: 4–5★ with no sensitive keywords, no legal/medical claims, no refund threats.
   - Requires approval: all 1–3★ OR any flagged keywords (refund, lawsuit, HIPAA/medical, discrimination, threats).
5) Post:
   - If access granted: post directly.
   - If no access: send copy/paste response + instructions.
6) Escalate: create an escalation ticket for negative reviews; track resolution status.

Escalation rules (minimum viable):
• Escalate immediately if: 1–2★ review, allegation of harm, discrimination, illegal activity, medical/legal claims, chargeback/refund demand, employee named, threat of complaint.
• Escalation SLA: notify owner/manager within 4 business hours (Pro) / 1 business day (Growth).
• Outcome tracking: “Contacted reviewer / Offered resolution / Resolved offline / No response / Needs legal”.

What’s explicitly NOT included (to keep deliverable & compliant):
• Review gating or incentivized reviews
• Guarantee of review removal or rating increase
• Posting disputes/flags as the business without explicit authorization
• Direct messaging reviewers from personal accounts
