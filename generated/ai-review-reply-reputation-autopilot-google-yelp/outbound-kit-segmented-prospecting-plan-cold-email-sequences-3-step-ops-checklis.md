3) OUTBOUND OPS CHECKLIST + CRM STAGES

Daily sending (per inbox; keep conservative):
- Day 1–2: 10–15 new/day
- Day 3–4: 20–25 new/day
- Day 5–7: 30–40 new/day
- Day 8–14: 40–60 new/day (only if bounce <3% and complaints ~0)

List QA rules (before sending):
- Must have correct category/vertical (avoid franchises with corporate-only contact)
- Must have recent activity OR meaningful review volume
- Verify website exists; prefer businesses with a real domain (not only Facebook)
- Capture last review date + rating + review count
- Personalization snippet must be non-sensitive; avoid medical details; paraphrase if needed

Bounce/complaint thresholds:
- If hard bounce >3% in any day: stop new sends, clean list, verify emails
- If any spam complaints: reduce volume and tighten targeting; remove risky segments

Reply handling SLA:
- Same business day for positive replies
- Negative/angry replies: respond within 2 hours during business time, offer to stop emailing and ask correct contact

CRM stages (simple pipeline):
1) Prospects (not yet sent)
2) Sent (Email 1)
3) Follow-up 1 Sent
4) Follow-up 2 Sent
5) Replied — Interested
6) Replied — Not now
7) Replied — Not a fit
8) Demo Booked
9) Trial/Proof (sample replies delivered)
10) Paid
11) Lost

Operating cadence (weekly):
- Mon: build/QA 100–200 new leads; prioritize Priority A
- Tue–Thu: send new + follow-ups; log replies; push demos
- Fri: review KPIs (reply rate, positive rate, demo rate); refine subject lines and segment mix
