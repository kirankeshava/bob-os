CASE STUDY #1 — HOME SERVICES (HVAC / PLUMBING STYLE)
Problem
A local home services business had inconsistent review replies (sometimes weeks late). Several 1–2 star reviews went unanswered, and the owner was losing leads when prospects saw “no response.”

What we did
- Implemented daily review monitoring for Google Business Profile
- Set a brand voice: direct, respectful, problem-solving; no blame
- Enabled immediate escalation for 1–2 star reviews with a 4-hour approval window
- Started weekly KPI reporting: reply rate, response time, complaint themes

Results (first 30 days)
- Response time improved from “days/weeks” to same-day replies (often under 12 hours)
- Reply rate increased to near-complete coverage (all new reviews responded)
- Negative reviews were de-escalated with clear next steps (offline resolution path)
- Owner reported fewer “Are you guys legit?” calls and more booked jobs attributed to improved trust signals

CASE STUDY #2 — MED SPA / AESTHETICS STYLE (COMPLIANCE-SENSITIVE)
Problem
A multi-provider aesthetics clinic needed fast, consistent replies without risking compliance issues. Staff were uncomfortable responding publicly to complaints, and responses sounded inconsistent across team members.

What we did
- Built a compliance-safe reply framework (no personal health info, no confirmations)
- Set “Approval Mode” for 1–3 star reviews: drafts delivered fast; clinic approves before posting
- Implemented tone rules: calm, premium, empathetic; invite offline follow-up
- Added weekly theme reporting to identify repeat friction (wait times, billing confusion)

Results (first 6 weeks)
- Clinic achieved consistent, brand-aligned replies across all providers/locations
- Reduced risk: every sensitive reply followed the same approved structure
- Faster de-escalation: complaints moved to private resolution quickly, protecting the public profile
- Improved internal operations: theme report led to policy changes that reduced repeat complaints
