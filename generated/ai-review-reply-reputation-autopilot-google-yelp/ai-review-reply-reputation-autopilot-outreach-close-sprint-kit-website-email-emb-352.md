## 3) 20-Min Discovery Call Flow (compressed for closes)
**0:00–2:00 Set agenda**
“Goal: understand your review volume + process, show how the autopilot works safely, and if it’s a fit, we’ll choose a plan and get you live in 48 hours.”

**2:00–8:00 Diagnose (must-ask questions)**
1) “How many locations?”
2) “Roughly how many reviews/month on Google? On Yelp?”
3) “Who replies today, and how fast?”
4) “Any bad review in the last 60 days that hurt?”
5) “What matters most: calls, bookings, walk-ins, or online orders?”

**8:00–12:00 ROI framing prompts**
- “If we reduce negative-review ‘hang time’ from days to hours, does that protect bookings?”
- “If response coverage goes from {{X}}% to 95–100%, would that help conversions/trust?”

**12:00–16:00 Safety workflow (handle AI risk)**
“We never post risky stuff. You choose approval mode:
1) Auto-post for 4–5 star reviews only
2) Approval for all reviews
3) Hybrid: auto for positives, approve negatives.
Negative reviews always escalate with a recommended resolution script.”

**16:00–19:00 Close**
“Based on your volume, I recommend {{Plan}}. To start, it’s setup + first month upfront, and we go live within 48 hours once we have GBP/Yelp access + your brand voice.”

**19:00–20:00 Next step**
“I’ll email the 1-page scope + invoice now. Once paid, we send the access request and intake immediately.”
