48-HOUR ONBOARDING SOP (FULFILLMENT)
SLA goal: live responding within 48 hours of payment + access.

T+0–2 hours: Intake
- Capture: business name(s), locations, services, target keywords, offers, hours.
- Brand voice: 5 adjectives (e.g., warm, concise, professional), examples of “good” replies.
- Do-not-say list: pricing promises, medical/legal claims, competitor mentions.
- Escalation rules: any 1–3 star OR specific keywords (refund, lawsuit, rude, scam, discrimination) triggers escalation.

T+2–12 hours: Access (client chooses)
Option A (preferred): Google Business Profile
- Client adds us as Manager (or Communications Manager if available) to location(s).
- Confirm we can view/respond to reviews.
Option B: Yelp
- Client adds us as user/agent in Yelp for Business.
Option C (no access): Draft-only
- We draft replies and send daily email for client to post.

T+12–24 hours: Safety configuration
- Set approval mode:
  1) Manual approval: all replies queued
  2) Post-approval: we post, client can edit; negatives escalated
  3) Auto-post: only 4–5 star; negatives escalated
- Create tone library: 5–10 approved phrases, greeting/closing formats.

T+24–36 hours: QA + Pilot
- Draft 10 sample replies from recent reviews.
- Client approves/edits 3; we update tone rules.

T+36–48 hours: Go-live
- Begin responding per SLA (e.g., same-day for positives; negatives escalated within 1 hour during business hours).
- Send first weekly KPI template: response rate, avg time-to-response, rating trend, negative themes, escalation count.
