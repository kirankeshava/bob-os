## 2) Lead sourcing workflow (fast + free)
**Target niches (pick 1–2 to start):** medspas, dentists, chiropractors, home services (HVAC/plumbing), auto repair. Prioritize high LTV + appointment-driven.

**How to build leads (manual, 60–90 mins/day):**
1. Open Google Maps → search “{niche} {city}”.
2. Open top ~30 results; add to sheet if:
   - Rating < 4.6 OR
   - Has 3+ reviews in last 30 days OR
   - Has at least one 1–3★ review in last 60 days OR
   - Owner responses are missing/rare/inconsistent.
3. Capture: business name, city, phone, website, Google review link, rating, #reviews, last review date, whether owner replies, 1 recent negative snippet.
4. Do same on Yelp for the same businesses (if listed). Capture Yelp URL + recent negative snippet.

**Daily targets:** 40 new leads/day, 30 outbound touches/day, 5 follow-ups/day → goal 5–8 booked calls/week.
