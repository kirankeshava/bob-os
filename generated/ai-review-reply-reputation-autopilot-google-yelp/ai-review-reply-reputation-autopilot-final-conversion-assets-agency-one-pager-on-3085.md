# 3) Case Study Modules (Anonymized, Short, Landing‑Page Ready)

## Case Study #1 — “From days to same‑day responses” (Home Services)
**Situation:** A local home services business was receiving 10–20 reviews/week but responses were inconsistent. Negative reviews sometimes sat for days, increasing call volume complaints and refund threats.

**What we did:**
- Set up daily monitoring + brand voice rules (“professional, short, action‑oriented”)
- Auto‑drafted responses to all new Google reviews
- Escalated 1–2 star reviews to the owner within hours with 2 reply options: (a) public acknowledgment + offline resolution (b) clarification request when facts were unclear

**Results (first 30 days):**
- **Response time:** reduced from **3–5 days → same‑day** on average
- **Negative review handling:** **100% of 1–2 star reviews** received an owner‑approved response within SLA
- **Operational impact:** fewer repetitive “did anyone see my review?” calls; clearer internal follow‑up notes

**Proof placeholders:** screenshot of Google review feed (dates blurred), weekly KPI email, response time chart.


## Case Study #2 — “Protecting a premium brand tone” (Med Spa)
**Situation:** A med spa had a strong rating but inconsistent replies: some were overly casual, others overly defensive. The owner wanted a consistent premium tone and strict escalation for claims about outcomes.

**What we did:**
- Built a brand voice guide (“premium, calm, never argumentative”) with do/don’t phrases
- Added escalation triggers for medical outcome claims, pricing disputes, and refund demands
- Implemented Draft‑Only workflow so all sensitive replies were approved before posting

**Results (6 weeks):**
- **Consistency:** replies matched the premium tone across all locations
- **Risk reduction:** medical/outcome claims were **escalated before posting** (no rushed public admissions)
- **Ratings narrative:** maintained overall rating while increasing response coverage; reviewers frequently mentioned “quick response” and “professional”

**Proof placeholders:** anonymized reply examples (before/after tone), escalation email template, weekly KPI summary.
