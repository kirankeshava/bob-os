## 5) CAC/KPI Tracker — Google Sheets blueprint
Create a Google Sheet with tabs:
1) **Leads**
2) **Calls**
3) **Customers**
4) **Weekly Summary**

### Tab: Leads (columns)
- Date Added
- Channel (Upwork / Fiverr / Cold Email / Referral)
- Campaign (e.g., UW-GBP-Dentists-W1)
- Prospect Name
- Business Name
- Niche
- City
- Email/URL
- Status (Contacted/Responded/Call Booked/No Response/Closed Won/Lost)
- Notes

### Tab: Calls (columns)
- Date
- Channel
- Campaign
- Prospect
- Outcome (Show/No-show)
- Next Step

### Tab: Weekly Summary (formulas)
- Leads by channel: COUNTIF
- Responses by channel: COUNTIFS(Status,"Responded")
- Booked calls by channel: COUNTIFS(Status,"Call Booked")
- Show-rate: Shows / Booked
- Close-rate: Won / Shows

**North Star (Week-1):** booked calls. Everything else is a leading indicator.
