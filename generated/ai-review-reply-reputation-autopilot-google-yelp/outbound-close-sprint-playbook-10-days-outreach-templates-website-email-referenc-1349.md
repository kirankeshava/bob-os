## 1) Target customer + offer in one sentence
Local businesses with recurring foot traffic/appointments (dental, medspa, home services, clinics, restaurants) that have Google reviews (and/or Yelp) but respond inconsistently.

**Offer:** “We draft and (optionally) post brand-safe replies to every Google/Yelp review, escalate negatives same-day, and send a weekly KPI report—go live in 48 hours.”

## 2) Lead list template (copy into Sheet)
Columns:
- Business Name
- Niche
- City
- Website URL
- Google Maps URL
- Rating (GBP)
- Total Reviews
- Recent Reviews (last 30 days)
- Response Rate (rough: many replies vs few)
- Yelp Page URL (if any)
- Owner/Manager Name (if found)
- Email
- Phone
- Contact Form URL
- Notes (signals: negative review last 14 days, “owner responds” absent, etc.)
- Status (Not Contacted / Touched 1 / Booked / No Show / Proposal Sent / Closed Won / Closed Lost)
- Next Follow-up Date

Qualification targets (fast wins):
- 4.0–4.6 rating OR visible recent negatives
- 10+ new reviews/month (or clearly active)
- Few/no owner responses in last 30–60 days

## 3) 10-day sprint targets (high-intensity)
Goal: **10–20 closes** (setup + month 1 collected upfront).

Daily targets (adjust up if response is low):
- **Day 1–2:** Build list of 150–250 prospects
- **Days 2–10:** 50–100 outbound touches/day (mix email, calls, contact forms)
- Booking target: 2–4 discovery calls/day
- Proposal/invoice SLA: within **2 hours** after each call

Suggested funnel math:
- 500 touches → 25–50 replies → 15–25 booked calls → 8–15 closes (improve with niche focus + quick proof samples)

## 4) Outreach cadence (simple, repeatable)

**Touch 3 (Day 4):** “Quick win” email (include 1–2 example replies)
