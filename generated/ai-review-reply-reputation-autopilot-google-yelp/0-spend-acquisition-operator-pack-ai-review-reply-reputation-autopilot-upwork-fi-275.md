## Mini-Audit Template (copy/paste)
Subject/Headline: **“Your last 10 reviews — 3 missed response opportunities + 2 replies I’d post today”**

**Quick wins found (based on last 10 reviews):**
1) **Unanswered reviews:** [#] reviews have no owner reply → missed trust signal.
2) **Low-specificity replies:** [#] replies are generic ("Thanks!") → doesn’t reinforce the service/value mentioned.
3) **Negative review handling:** [#] reviews need escalation language + private-resolution path.

**Two sample replies (brand-safe):**
- Review: “[…]”
  Draft reply: “Thanks for sharing this, [Name]. We’re glad [specific detail]. If there’s anything we can do to make your next visit even better, please reach us at [contact]—we appreciate you.”
- Review: “[…]”
  Draft reply (negative): “Hi [Name]—I’m sorry this was your experience. This isn’t the standard we aim for. If you’re open to it, please email us at [contact] with the date/time so we can investigate and make it right.”

**If you want:** I can set up an autopilot where every new review gets a draft within 5–15 minutes, negatives get escalated, and you get a weekly KPI email.
