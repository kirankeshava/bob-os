A) ICP + LIST BUILD RULES (fast manual)
Target: local businesses where reviews drive bookings (dental, medspa, chiropractor, auto repair, home services, restaurants with reservations).
Filters (Google Maps): rating 4.0–4.6 (room to improve), 30+ reviews, at least 3 reviews in last 30 days, has at least 1 negative review in last 90 days, responses inconsistent or absent.
Data to capture: Business Name, City, Category, Google rating, # reviews, last review date, owner/manager name if available, phone, website, contact form URL, Yelp link, notes (“no responses”, “recent 1-star”).

B) 14-DAY CLOSE SPRINT TARGETS
Daily (Mon–Fri): 50 new outbound touches/day + 30 follow-ups/day.
Weekly targets: 250 new prospects touched → 15–25 replies → 8–12 booked calls → 3–6 closes.
SLA: proposals/invoices sent within 2 hours of call; onboarding starts within 48 hours of payment.

C) DISCOVERY-TO-CLOSE FLOW (fast)
1) Book 20-min call.
2) On call: quantify review volume + missed responses + negative review risk; confirm access feasibility (GBP/Yelp).
3) Offer two options (Starter vs Growth) and ask for decision.
4) Immediately send: (i) 1-page proposal summary email, (ii) Wave invoice for Setup + Month 1 upfront, (iii) “reply YES to accept order form terms” (email acceptance).
5) On payment: send onboarding email + intake questions + access request steps.
