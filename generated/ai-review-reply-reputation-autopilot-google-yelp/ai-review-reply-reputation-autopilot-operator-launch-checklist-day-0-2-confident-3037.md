### A) Operator

   - Escalation rules

### B) Customer
1. Respond quickly to escalations (refund/appointment/PII issues) so replies can be posted.

## First-week success criteria (tell customers upfront)
- **Response time:** replies posted within 24 hours on business days (or same-day if agreed).
- **Coverage:** 90–100% of new reviews receive a reply (excluding escalations on hold).
- **Negatives:** 100% of negative reviews escalated with a suggested resolution note.
- **Brand safety:** zero policy violations, no admissions of fault, no sharing private info.
