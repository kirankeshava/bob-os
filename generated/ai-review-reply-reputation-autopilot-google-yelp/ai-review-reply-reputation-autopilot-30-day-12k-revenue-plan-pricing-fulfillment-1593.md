Human-in-the-loop workflow (brand-safe):
1) Onboarding intake (15 min): business info, tone (friendly/professional), banned phrases, discount policy, escalation contacts, operating hours, common issues.
2) Daily capture:
   - If access granted: check new reviews 1–2x/day (or via platform notifications).
   - If no access: client forwards review notification emails OR sends screenshot/link daily.
3) Draft responses:
   - Generate response draft with brand voice rules + platform policy checks.
4) Approval rules:
   - Auto-approve: 4–5★ unless flagged for sensitive content.
   - Always require approval: 1–3★, legal/medical claims, refunds/billing disputes, harassment/threats, mentions of staff names, anything requesting private data.
5) Post:
   - Post via platform (if access) OR deliver “ready-to-post” copy in digest.
6) Escalation ticket (same day):
   - For negative reviews: create a short escalation entry with recommended next step, owner/manager contact, and a “resolution outcome” field.
7) Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
   - New reviews, average rating, rating trend (7/30 days), response rate, median response time, negative review count, escalations opened/resolved, top 3 themes, recommended ops fix.

Explicit exclusions (to keep deliverable + compliant):
- No review gating, incentivization, or “only ask happy customers” tactics.
- No promises to remove reviews.
- We can draft dispute language, but client submits disputes/appeals.
