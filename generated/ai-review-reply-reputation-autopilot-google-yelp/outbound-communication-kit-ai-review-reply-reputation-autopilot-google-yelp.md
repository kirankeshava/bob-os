5) OBJECTION HANDLING (quick responses)
========================

Objection: “I don’t want AI talking to customers.”
Response: “Totally fair. We don’t ‘freewheel’—we use approved templates + your brand voice rules. And we can do draft-only so nothing posts without your team hitting publish.”

Objection: “What if you make it worse?”
Response: “We use an escalation rule for anything sensitive. Also, we avoid arguing, avoid admitting liability, and we move complex cases offline with a consistent ‘please contact us’ workflow.”

Objection: “We already reply sometimes.”
Response: “That’s a good start. The gap is consistency + speed. We measure it weekly and make sure every review gets a timely reply, which is where the conversion lift usually comes from.”

Objection: “I’m worried about access.”
Response: “We can do draft-only, or posting with restricted manager access. Either way, you keep ownership and can revoke access anytime.”
