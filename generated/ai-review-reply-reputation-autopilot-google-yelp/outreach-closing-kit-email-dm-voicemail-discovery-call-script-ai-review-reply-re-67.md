============================
Objection: “We already reply sometimes.”
Response: “Totally. The gap we usually fix is consistency + speed. Even when a team replies ‘sometimes,’ the busy weeks are when negatives go unanswered and that’s when reputation takes a hit. We systematize it with a defined SLA and a weekly KPI report.”

Objection: “I don’t want anyone posting publicly.”
Response: “No problem. We can start draft-only. You approve posts, or we only auto-post positive/neutral while negatives stay approval-only. You control the risk settings.”

Objection: “Is this compliant? I don’t want review gating.”
Response: “We don’t do gating or incentives. We’re strictly responding to reviews, escalating issues, and improving response time/quality.”

Objection: “How is this different than a VA or generic AI?”
Response: “Two differences: brand safety and workflow. We run a consistent voice guide, escalation rules for 1–2 stars, and KPI reporting—so it’s not random AI text and it doesn’t create legal/compliance risk.”
