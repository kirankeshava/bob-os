## 2) Segmentation + Priority Routing
Capture these fields per prospect: rating, review_count, last_review_date, and **response-rate proxy** (count owner responses in last 10 reviews).

**Segments (tag 1+ per account):**
- **Not Responding:** response-rate proxy ≤ 20% OR 0 owner replies in last 10 reviews.
- **Low Rating:** Google rating < 4.2.
- **High Volume:** review_count ≥ 200 OR last_review_date within 14 days.

**Priority score (use for send order):**
- **Priority A:** (Not Responding AND High Volume) OR (Low Rating AND High Volume)
- **Priority B:** Not Responding OR Low Rating (only)
- **Priority C:** High Volume only

**Message angle by segment:**
- Not Responding → “You’re leaving revenue on the table; we respond in 12 hours; brand-safe; you approve.”
- Low Rating → “De-escalate negatives; consistent tone; faster responses; escalation workflow; weekly KPIs.”
- High Volume → “Ops/throughput: never miss reviews; weekly reporting; time savings.”

## 3) Offer (keep it low-friction)
**Core offer (in every email):**
- Drafts *brand-safe* replies to Google Business Profile + Yelp reviews

- **Approval model:** “we draft; you approve” (or “autopost after your tone is locked”)

**Primary CTA:** 10–15 minute call OR “Reply ‘yes’ and I’ll send 2 example replies based on your latest reviews.”

## 8) Daily Sending Ops (14-day ramp + QA + KPIs)

### 8A) Tooling (free-first; no spend assumed)
- Spreadsheet/CRM: Google Sheets (or free HubSpot)
- Sender: 1 inbox to start (keep volume low)
- Tracking: avoid heavy tracking initially; prioritize deliverability

### 8B) 14-day ramp (per inbox)
Day 1–2: 10–15/day (highly targeted, best personalization)  
Day 3–4: 20/day  
Day 5–7: 30/day  
Day 8–10: 40/day  
Day 11–14: 50/day  

Rules:
- Keep at least 60–70% plain-text, minimal links (1 proof link is fine)
- No attachments
- If bounce rate > 3% in a day → stop and clean list
- If spam complaints occur → reduce volume and adjust copy

### 8C) Daily activity targets (starting week 1)
- Send: 20–40/day (ramping)
- Follow-ups: 10–20/day (from earlier sends)
- Manual personalization: 100% on Priority A, 50% on Priority B, light on Priority C
- Reply SLA: respond within 1–2 hours during business day

### 8D) QA checklist (before sending)
- Business is actually in the vertical (not a supply store / school / corporate HQ)
- Rating + review_count captured
- Last review within 90 days (unless High Volume evergreen)
- Review snippet is short and safe (no sensitive personal data)
- Website exists OR GBP is active (otherwise skip)
- Email not a generic spam trap (avoid “info@” if you have an owner/manager email; if only generic exists, send anyway but lower priority)
