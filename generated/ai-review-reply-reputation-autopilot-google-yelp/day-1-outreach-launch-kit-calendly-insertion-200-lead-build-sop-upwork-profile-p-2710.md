B) Day-1 lead build SOP (200 leads, free sources)
Goal: 150 local owners (50 dentist, 50 med spa, 50 HVAC) + 50 agencies.
CSV columns for HubSpot import:
- Segment (Owner/Agency)
- Niche (Dentist/MedSpa/HVAC/Agency)
- Company
- Website
- City
- Decision maker (Name/Role if found)
- Email
- Phone
- Google Maps link
- Yelp link
- Review count (approx)
- Rating (approx)
- Notes (personalization hook)
- Source (Maps/Yelp/Clutch/LinkedIn)
- Channel (Email/Upwork/FB)

How to find 150 owners quickly:
1) Google Maps searches (do 5 cities x 3 niches x 10 leads each = 150):
   - “dentist near [City]”
   - “med spa [City]” / “medical spa [City]”
   - “HVAC [City]” / “air conditioning repair [City]”
2) Open each listing → capture: business name, website, phone, rating, review count, Google Maps link.
3) Find emails (free, fast):
   - Check website header/footer/contact page.
   - If no email: use contact form + log “FORM” in Email field (still usable for outreach).
4) Yelp add-on: search Yelp for same business name + city; capture Yelp link.
Personalization hook rule: note one of: (a) rating <4.6, (b) recent 1–3 star review, (c) no owner responses visible, (d) 50+ reviews with no recent replies.

How to find 50 agencies (white-label angle):
1) Clutch directory: “Local SEO”, “Digital Marketing for small business”, filter US/UK/CA.
2) Google: “local SEO agency multi-location”, “reputation management agency”, “white label local SEO”.
3) Grab: agency name, site, “contact” email, founder name if listed.
Personalization hook: number of case studies, mention of “multi-location”, “local SEO”, “GBP management”.
