4) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (week-1 deliverable)
Review sources supported (week 1):
• Google Business Profile reviews (GBP)
• Yelp reviews

Response SLA:
• DFY Growth: within 24 hours of review appearing (business days); DFY Pro: within 12 hours (business days)
• Agency Starter: within 24 hours; Agency Pro: priority queue (12–24 hours)

Escalation rules (negative reviews):
• Any 1–2★ review: escalation ticket created within SLA + proposed resolution steps.
• 3★ reviews: escalate if mentions staff safety, discrimination, fraud, billing disputes, medical/legal claims, threats, or refund demand.
• Keyword flags (auto-escalate): “refund”, “lawsuit”, “scam”, “HIPAA”, “insurance”, “injury”, “harassment”, “police”, “chargeback”, “discrimination”.

Posting method (no API dependency):
• Preferred: Client grants Manager access to Google Business Profile and access to Yelp Business Owner account so we can post replies directly.
• Fallback (lowest friction to start): We draft replies in a shared doc/email; client posts using provided copy. This is the default workaround until Yelp posting access is granted.

Quality + brand safety controls:
• Brand Voice Profile: tone (warm/professional), do-not-say list, offer mentions allowed, compliance requirements.
• Human QA: every reply checked for: no admissions of fault, no private data, no prohibited incentives, no policy violations.

What is explicitly NOT included (to avoid policy issues):
• No review gating/incentivization (“only leave a review if happy”).
• No buying reviews.
• No removal guarantees or unauthorized dispute submissions.

5) WEEK-1 SOP OUTLINE (operator checklist)
Onboarding (Day 0–1)
1) Collect access: GBP Manager invite; Yelp Business Owner access OR confirm client-post workflow.
2) Capture Brand Voice Profile: tone, greetings/sign-offs, brand terms, banned phrases, promo rules, escalation contact.
3) Confirm business facts: hours, phone, location, refund policy link.
4) Set reporting recipients + escalation phone/email.

Daily operations (Mon–Fri)
1) Review capture: check new reviews on GBP/Yelp (or via email alerts).
2) Draft replies: generate 2–3 variants for any 1–2★ review; 1 variant for 3–5★.
3) QA + compliance pass.
4) Approval rules:
   • Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged.
   • Require explicit approval for 1–3★ and any flagged keyword reviews.
5) Post replies: direct posting if access; else send “Copy/Paste Reply Pack” to client.
6) Escalation: create ticket, recommend offline resolution steps, track status.

Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
• New reviews (Google/Yelp)
• Average rating trend (7/30 days)
• Response rate (% replied)
• Median response time
• Negative review count (1–2★) + open/closed escalations
• Top 3 themes (service, wait time, pricing, staff)
• Recommendations for next week (1–3 bullets)
