FREE 10-REVIEW AUDIT — SOP (how to deliver in 15–20 min)
Inputs needed: GBP link (and/or Yelp), business niche, preferred tone, owner name (optional).
Steps:
1) Capture last 10 reviews: date, star rating, theme, whether responded.
2) Scorecard (0–10):
   - Response coverage (0–3)
   - Tone consistency (0–2)
   - Speed/recency (0–2)
   - Negative-review handling (0–3)
3) Identify:
   - 3 missed responses that would influence future buyers
   - 2 risk items (legal/medical claims, refunds, staff blame, HIPAA, etc.)
4) Draft 3 replies:
   - 1 positive review reply (warm, specific, invite return)
   - 1 neutral/short review reply (thank + simple invitation)
   - 1 negative review reply (de-escalate + move offline + no admissions)
5) Close with next step:
   - “If you want, I can set up an autopilot system so every review gets a brand-safe reply, negatives get escalated fast, and you get a weekly KPI email.”
