CASE STUDY #1 — HOME SERVICES (HVAC / PLUMBING)
Problem
A busy home-services company was getting reviews daily but responding inconsistently. Negative reviews sometimes sat unanswered for a week, and replies varied wildly in tone.
What we did
• Set brand voice: calm, accountable, no excuses
• Implemented escalation: all 1–2 star reviews required approval before posting
• Ran daily draft queue + same-day replies for new positives
Results (first 30 days)
• Response time: ~5 days → under 24 business hours
• Negative review handling: 0 public arguments; all escalated to a call-back workflow
• Rating lift narrative: As responses became consistent and timely, new reviewers referenced “great customer service” and “quick follow-up,” supporting a gradual rating improvement over subsequent weeks.

CASE STUDY #2 — MED SPA / AESTHETICS
Problem
A med spa had strong service quality but uneven public communication. Staff avoided replying to critical reviews due to HIPAA/privacy concerns and fear of saying the wrong thing.
