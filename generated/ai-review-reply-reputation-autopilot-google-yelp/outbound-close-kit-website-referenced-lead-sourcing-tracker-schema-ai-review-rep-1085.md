## 1) Who we’re targeting (fastest closes)
Prioritize businesses where reviews directly drive calls/appointments and owners are time-poor:
- Home services: plumbers, HVAC, roofing, pest control, junk removal
- High-ticket local: medspas, dentists, chiropractors
- Auto repair / detailing

Qualification (quick screen):
- 15+ reviews total AND at least 3 reviews in the last 90 days
- Rating 3.6–4.6 (biggest upside) OR 4.7+ but slow/no responses
- Evidence of owner/operator involvement (small chain, 1–5 locations)

## 2) Lead sourcing (20 leads in 30 minutes)
Google search queries (swap city):
- "plumber" + "[city]" + read reviews
- "HVAC" + "[city]" + read reviews
- "med spa" + "[city]" + read reviews
Pick listings where: (a) negative reviews have no reply, or (b) replies are generic/infrequent.
Capture:
- Business name
- Website
- Phone
- Google rating + review count
- Last review date
- Screenshot/notes of 1 negative review + 1 positive review with no response
