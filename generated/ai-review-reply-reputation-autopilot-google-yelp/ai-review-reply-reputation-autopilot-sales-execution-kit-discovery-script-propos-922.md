1) 20-MINUTE DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (Revenue-impact, fast close)
============================================================
Goal: Diagnose review/reputation pain, quantify revenue impact, confirm platform access (Google Business Profile + Yelp), neutralize “AI risk”, and close for Setup + Month 1 upfront.

0:00–2:00 Opener + agenda
“Thanks for taking this. Quick agenda: (1) I’ll ask a few questions about your reviews on Google/Yelp and how you handle them today, (2) we’ll quantify what improving response rate and rating could mean for calls/appointments, (3) if it’s a fit I’ll recommend one of two plans and next steps. Sound good?”

2:00–6:00 Current state (facts)
1) “How many locations do you have?”
2) “Which platforms matter most—Google, Yelp, Facebook?”
3) “Roughly how many new reviews do you get per week/month?”
4) “Do you respond to every review today? If not, what %?”
5) “Who responds (owner, manager, front desk, agency)? How long does it usually take?”
6) “Any past issues—wrong tone, HIPAA/industry compliance, customers getting upset?”

6:00–10:00 Impact + ROI prompts (revenue framing)
“Reviews are basically your best salesperson—people read them right before they call. Let’s quantify.”
Ask:
- “About how many inbound calls/leads do you get per month from Google/Yelp?”
- “What’s an average booked job / appointment value?”
- “If rating moved by +0.2 to +0.4 over time and response speed improved, what would even +1 to +3 extra bookings per month mean in revenue?”
Compute aloud: “So even 2 extra bookings x $___ = $___/month. That’s the business case for staying on top of responses and preventing negative review spirals.”

10:00–13:00 Pain + urgency
- “What’s the hardest part about responding consistently—time, writing, staff turnover, compliance, or dealing with negative reviews?”
- “When a 1–2 star review happens, what’s your escalation process today?”
- “Do you ever lose customers because you didn’t reply fast or the response wasn’t great?”

13:00–16:00 Fit check: access + workflow
Google Business Profile (GBP):
- “Do you have admin/manager access to your Google Business Profile? If yes, who can add a manager?”
Yelp:
- “Do you control the Yelp Business account? Do you know your login?”
Approval mode preference:
- “Would you prefer: (A) we draft and auto-post brand-safe replies, (B) draft and you approve in a queue, or (C) draft-only and your team posts?”

16:00–18:00 Objections (pre-handle)
Objection: “We can do it ourselves.”
Response: “Totally. The question is consistency and speed. The upside is keeping your rating strong and converting more lookers into callers. Most teams stop responding when it gets busy—our job is to make ‘busy’ irrelevant.”

Objection: “AI risk / wrong tone / compliance.”
Response: “We run a safety workflow: we capture your brand voice, prohibited topics/phrases, escalation triggers for negative reviews, and you can choose approval mode (auto vs approve). We also keep an audit log of what was drafted and posted. The goal is brand-safe, not robotic.”

Objection: “Will Google/Yelp penalize this?”
Response: “We follow platform policies and post normal owner responses. No incentives, no fake reviews, no manipulation. Just timely, professional replies.”

18:00–20:00 Close (two options)
“If I could get you to 90%+ response rate, handle Google + Yelp, escalate negatives fast, and send a weekly KPI summary, would you want that handled for you?”
Then:
“Based on what you told me, I recommend either:
- Plan 1 (lighter): responses + escalation + weekly KPIs.
- Plan 2 (full): everything in Plan 1 plus faster SLA, more volume, and multi-location support.
We can start this week. Next step is: we send a 1-page order form + invoice for Setup + Month 1 upfront, then we onboard within 48 hours once access is granted.”
