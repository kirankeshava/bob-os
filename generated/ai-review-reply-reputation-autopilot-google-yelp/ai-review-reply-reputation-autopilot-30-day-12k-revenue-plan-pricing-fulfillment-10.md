## 4) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 Deliverable, No API Dependency)
### Sources covered
- **Google Business Profile reviews** (GBP)
- **Yelp reviews**

### Posting method (week 1)
- **Preferred:** client grants **GBP Manager access** to our ops email.
- **Yelp:** either (a) client grants **Yelp Business Owner/Manager access**, OR (b) **client-post workflow**: we draft + send copy/paste response within SLA.

### Daily workflow (SOP outline)
1) **Onboarding (Day 0–2)**
   - Collect: brand voice, do/don’t language, offers/guarantees, regulated keywords, escalation contacts, hours, service list, competitor list.
   - Access: GBP manager; Yelp manager or confirm client-post flow.
   - Approvals: decide auto-approval settings and escalation triggers.

2) **Review capture (Daily)**
   - Pull new reviews via email alerts + manual check (no API required).

3) **Draft response (Same day)**
   - Generate brand-safe reply using approved tone + policy rules.

4) **Human QA + policy check (Same day)**
   - Check for: admissions of fault, compensation promises, HIPAA/medical claims, legal language, profanity, discrimination.

5) **Approval rules**
   - **4–5★:** auto-post unless flagged.
   - **1–3★:** requires explicit client approval OR use pre-approved “resolution playbook.”

6) **Escalation rules (ticket + SLA)**
   - Trigger if: 1★/2★, fraud allegation, safety incident, discrimination, legal threat, medical/privacy claims, press/social threat.
   - We notify escalation contact within **4 business hours** with recommended response + next steps.

7) **Weekly KPI report (every Monday)**
   - Metrics: new reviews, avg rating, rating delta, response rate, median response time, negative count, escalations, resolved outcomes, top themes.
   - Include: 3 actions for next week.

### Explicit constraints (protect deliverability + compliance)
- We **do not** delete reviews, guarantee rating increases, offer incentives, or perform review gating.
- We can draft dispute language, but client must submit disputes if required.
