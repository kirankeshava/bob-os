Supported sources:
- Google Business Profile (GBP)

- Fallback (default-allowable): “Client-post” workflow: we draft responses + send daily digest; client posts natively.

SLA definition:
- 24 business hours = next business day by end of day.

Escalation rules (auto-escalate if any):

- New reviews (Google, Yelp)

- Response rate %

- Top 3 themes (service, wait time, pricing, staff, etc.)

Explicit exclusions (to stay compliant and deliverable):
- No paying/incentivizing for reviews
- No “review gating” (only sending requests to happy customers)
- No deleting reviews or guaranteed removals
- No impersonation: responses are posted under the business account only with permission/access
