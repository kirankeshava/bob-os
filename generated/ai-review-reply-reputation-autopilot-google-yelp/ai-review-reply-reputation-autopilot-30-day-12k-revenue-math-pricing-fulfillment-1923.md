Posting method (human-in-the-loop, no API dependency):
- Preferred: client adds us as manager on GBP; for Yelp, add business owner/manager access where possible.
- Fallback (default-safe for week 1): we draft responses in a shared doc/email; client posts (copy/paste) daily. This removes API/access blockers and keeps service deliverable.

SLA + rules:
- Daily monitoring business days (Mon–Fri). Optional weekend monitoring as add-on later.
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-draft + auto-post (if access) unless flagged by banned keywords.
- 1–3★ reviews: always escalated; we draft a response + send to client for approval before posting.
- Flag keywords requiring approval: legal/medical claims, pricing guarantees, discrimination, threats, profanity, HIPAA/PHI, refunds/chargebacks.
- Escalations: create a ticket (email thread is fine week 1) with recommended resolution steps; track “resolved/unresolved.”

Brand safety constraints (explicit):
- We do NOT gate reviews, buy reviews, or incentivize reviews.
- We do NOT promise removal of reviews.
- We do NOT dispute reviews without client authorization.
- We avoid any claims that violate platform policies (health claims, guarantees, etc.).
