B) Weekly KPI Sheet Layout (tabs + columns)
Create a Google Sheet with 3 tabs: (1) Weekly Summary, (2) Review Log, (3) Pipeline (Sales).

Columns:
Week Start | Week End | Location | Google New Reviews | Yelp New Reviews | Total New Reviews | Google Avg Rating | Yelp Avg Rating | Overall Avg (weighted) | Replies Posted | Response Rate % | Median Response Time (hrs) | 1–3★ Count | Escalations Sent | Escalations Resolved | Top Theme 1 | Top Theme 2 | Top Theme 3 | Notes

TAB 2: Review Log (one row per review)
Date | Platform | Location | Reviewer | Stars | Review Text | Category/Theme | Drafted By | QA By | Approval Needed? (Y/N) | Approved By | Posted? (Y/N) | Post Date | Response Text (final) | Escalated? (Y/N) | Escalation Ticket ID | Outcome/Resolution Notes

TAB 3: Pipeline (Sales) — 30-day KPI dashboard
Date | Leads Added | Replies/Convos | Calls Booked | Calls Held (Shows) | Closes | Cash Collected $ | MRR Added $ | Notes | Cumulative Cash $ | Cumulative MRR $

Formulas (simple):
- Cumulative Cash = SUM(Cash Collected $ to date)
- Cumulative MRR = SUM(MRR Added $ to date)
- Show Rate = Calls Held / Calls Booked
- Close Rate (Shows) = Closes / Calls Held
