A) OPERATOR LAUNCH CHECKLIST (DAY 0–2 ACTIVATION)
Goal: From “Yes” to “active replying + escalation + weekly KPI baseline” within 48 hours.

Day 0 (Same day as close)
1) Send “Welcome / Kickoff” email
   - Include: legitimacy URL, what happens next, intake form link/location, and the two access-request emails (GBP + Yelp).
2) Collect Intake (must-have fields)
   - Business legal name + location(s) + GBP listing URL(s)
   - Yelp business URL(s) (if applicable)
   - Brand voice: 3 adjectives (e.g., warm/concise/professional)
   - Do/don’t phrases, sensitive topics, refund policy language
   - Escalation contacts + preferred channel (email/phone)
   - Response SLA targets (e.g., respond within 24 hours)
3) Access received check
   - GBP: Confirm Manager access added OR agency permissions granted.
   - Yelp: Confirm business owner/manager access or response permissions.
4) Define escalation rules (write these back to client in one paragraph)
   - Trigger examples: 1–2 star reviews, refund demands, legal threats, safety issues, discrimination claims, staff name mention, private health/financial details.

Day 1
5) Sample set creation (approval gate)
   - Create 6 draft replies:
     a) 2 positive (short + detailed)
     b) 2 neutral/3-star “service fix”
     c) 2 negative (1 de-escalation + 1 resolution invite offline)
   - Include 2 escalation notes as examples.
6) Client approval loop
   - Send samples for approval with a 1-click rubric:
     - “Approve tone” / “Too formal” / “Too salesy” / “Needs more apology” / “Mention policy”
   - Target turnaround: <24 hours.

Day 2
7) Go-live
   - Start posting replies for new reviews.
   - Backlog option: reply to last 10–25 reviews (agree scope first).
8) KPI baseline snapshot
   - Capture starting metrics:
     - Avg rating, total reviews, last response date, response rate estimate, negative reviews last 30/90 days.
9) First-week success criteria (send in writing)
   - By Day 7:
     - 90–100% of new reviews responded within SLA
     - All 1–2 star reviews escalated within 1 business day
     - Weekly KPI email delivered with “wins + risks + next actions”
