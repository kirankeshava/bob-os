7) 30-DAY KPI DASHBOARD (copy/paste into a sheet)
Create columns: Date | Leads Sent | Replies | Reply % | Calls Booked | Showed | Closes | Cash Collected | MRR Added | Notes

Daily target baseline (to support ~8 deals/mo):
- Leads Sent: 40–45/day
- Replies: 6/day (≈15% reply rate)
- Calls Booked: 2/day (≈35% of replies)
- Showed: 1–2/day (≈70% show)
- Closes: 0.25/day (≈25% close on shows) → ~7–8 closes in 30 days

Weekly rollup rows:
Week # | Leads | Replies | Calls Booked | Shows | Closes | Cash | MRR

Formulas (examples):
- Reply % = Replies / Leads Sent
- Show Rate = Showed / Calls Booked
- Close Rate = Closes / Showed
- Cash Collected = setup fees + first month payments received

Delivery KPIs (separate tab):
Client | Platform (G/Y) | New Reviews | Replies Posted | Response Rate % | Median Response Time | 1–3★ Count | Escalations Opened | Escalations Resolved | Avg Rating Trend
