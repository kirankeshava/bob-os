## 3) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 Deliverable, No API Dependency)
### Review sources covered
- **Google Business Profile** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

### Posting methods (choose per client)
1) **Preferred (fastest):** client grants **GBP Manager access** + Yelp business access where available.
2) **Fallback (no access):** we draft responses and send “Ready-to-post” copy via email/Doc; **client posts**. We still track SLA to draft.

### Response workflow (daily)
1) **Capture:** daily review check (or email alerts forwarded) for GBP/Yelp
2) **Draft:** AI draft using client’s brand voice + offer guardrails
3) **Human QA:** ensure policy-safe, non-defensive, no admissions of liability, no regulated claims
4) **Approval rules:**
   - **4–5★:** auto-approve unless flagged keywords
   - **3★:** request approval if includes specific accusations/requests; otherwise post
   - **1–2★:** always escalate + require approval before posting (unless client pre-approves a templated path)
5) **Post:** via platform access or client-post fallback
6) **Escalation handling:** open an escalation ticket, assign owner, track resolution notes

### Escalation rules (what gets flagged)
- 1–2★ reviews
- Mentions: refund, fraud, discrimination, injury, lawsuit, HIPAA/PHI, insurance, harassment, “scam”, chargeback
- Staff named + accusation
- Demands for compensation

### What is explicitly NOT included (risk control)
- Incentivizing reviews / review gating
- Deleting reviews or “guaranteed removal” claims
- Public arguments, legal admissions, medical promises
- Disputing reviews without client authorization
