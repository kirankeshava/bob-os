## 3) Fulfillment scope (simple + deliverable now)
### Review sources covered
- Google Business Profile reviews
- Yelp reviews

### Response workflow & SLA
1) **Capture reviews daily**
   - Google: via GBP notifications + daily check
   - Yelp: email alerts + daily check
2) **Draft response** using brand guardrails (voice, offers, prohibited phrases, compliance constraints)
3) **Human QA** (brand safety + policy compliance + tone)
4) **Approval rules**
   - 4–5★: auto-approve unless flagged
   - 1–3★: require approval OR follow pre-approved “negative templates” if client opts in
   - Any legal/medical/financial claims: always require approval
5) **Posting**
   - Google: posted by us via manager access (preferred)
   - Yelp: client-post by default (copy/paste), unless client grants access
6) **Escalation** (negative reviews)
   - Create escalation ticket; notify designated contact within SLA
   - Track status (acknowledged → in progress → resolved)

### Escalation rules (what gets flagged)
- 1–2★ always escalated
- 3★ escalated if contains keywords: “refund”, “fraud”, “lawsuit”, “chargeback”, “HIPAA”, “injury”, “rude”, “scam”, “report”, “BBB”, “attorney”, “health department”
- Any allegation of discrimination, safety, illegal conduct

### What is explicitly NOT included (to keep deliverable)
- Review gating or incentivization
- Removing reviews or guaranteeing takedowns
- Disputes filed without client authorization
- Managing DMs across social platforms (unless add-on)
