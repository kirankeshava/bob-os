## 1) Lead Sourcing Criteria (to build 200-lead list fast)
Target local businesses where reviews directly drive calls/appointments and owners are busy:
- Verticals: dental, medspa, chiropractic/PT, home services (HVAC/plumbing/roofing), auto repair, salons/spas.
- Signals they need help:
  - 10+ new reviews in last 90 days AND owner responses are inconsistent (0–30% response rate)
  - Rating 3.6–4.6 (room to lift) OR recent 1–2 star reviews with no response
  - Multiple locations (2–10) without centralized reputation process
  - Yelp + Google both active (ideal)

**Minimum fields for each lead:** Business Name, Owner/Manager Name (if found), Email, Phone, GBP URL, Yelp URL, City, Rating, Review velocity (last 30/90 days), Notes (unanswered negative reviews).

Daily quotas (10-day sprint):
- Build 30 leads/day (300 total)
- Contact 50–100/day (multi-channel)
- Book 2–4 discovery calls/day
- Close 1/day target once messaging is calibrated
