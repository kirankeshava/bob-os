## 6) KPI + CAC tracking blueprint (Google Sheets)
Create a Sheet with tabs:
1) **Leads_Log** columns:
- Date, Channel (Upwork/Fiverr/Email), Campaign (e.g., UPW-Dentist-US), Prospect Name, Business, Niche, Link (GBP/Yelp), Contact, Status (Contacted/Responded/Call Booked/No Show/Won/Lost), Next Action Date, Notes
2) **Calls** columns:
- Booked Date, Held? (Y/N), Channel, Deal Stage, Outcome, Est. Monthly Value
3) **Metrics** formulas:
- Booked calls by channel (COUNTIF)
- Response rate by channel
- Show rate = Calls held / calls booked
- Close rate = won / calls held
- Week-1 success gate: **>= 3 booked calls/week/channel OR cut/adjust**

**7-day cut rule:** any channel with 0 booked calls after 7 days gets paused and effort reallocated to the best-performing channel.
