# Fulfillment Scope (Week 1 deliverable, no API dependency)
## Review sources
- Google Business Profile (GBP) reviews
- Yelp reviews

## Posting method (deliverable immediately)
**Default:** client grants access.
- GBP: client adds us as **Manager** (preferred) or **Owner** (not required).
- Yelp: client adds us to Yelp Business account if possible.

**Fallback (zero-access workflow):** we draft responses and client posts.
- We send “copy/paste ready” replies by email daily (or shared sheet).

## Response SLA
- Growth: 24 business hours
- Pro: 12 business hours
- Agency Starter: 48 business hours
- Agency Pro: 24 business hours

## Escalation rules (brand safety)
Escalate (do not auto-post) if any:
- 1–3★ review
- Mentions: refund/chargeback, lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA/PHI, harassment, profanity, threats
- Service failure claims or staff names

Escalation deliverable:
- Draft response options (2) + recommended next step
- “Resolution notes” field for client

## Human-in-the-loop QA (required in Week 1)
- Every reply passes a quick checklist: tone, no admissions of fault, no incentives, no private info, no policy violations.

## Weekly KPI report (every Monday for prior week)
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating + trend
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews (1–3★) count
- Escalations opened vs resolved
- Top 3 themes (service, price, timeliness, staff, etc.)
- Suggested operational fix (1 bullet)
