==============================
A) OPERATOR LAUNCH CHECKLIST (DAY 0–2 ACTIVATION)
==============================
Goal: From “Yes” to “actively responding + weekly KPIs” inside 48 hours.

Pre-close (during/after demo)
1) Confirm scope
   - # of locations (start with 1)
   - Platforms: Google Business Profile (GBP) and/or Yelp
   - Target response SLA (e.g., respond within 24 business hours)
2) Confirm escalation path
   - Who gets notified for negatives/urgent issues? (name + email + phone)
   - What rating threshold escalates? (suggest: 1–2 star always; 3 star optional)
3) Confirm brand voice
   - Friendly/professional, short/long, use of names, promos/discount policy

Day 0 (same day as close)
4) Send Kickoff Email (use your template)
   - Include legitimacy URL + support email
   - Link intake form (Brand Voice + Escalations + Locations)
   - Include Access Request instructions (GBP + Yelp)
5) Access collection
   - GBP: request Manager access to Business Profile (or via agency)
   - Yelp: request Business Owner/Manager login OR ask them to add us as a user (if applicable)
   - If they can’t grant access: offer “Draft-only mode” while access is pending (they copy/paste replies)
6) Gather baseline data (15 minutes)
   - Current star rating (GBP/Yelp)
   - Last 30–90 days review volume
   - % responded in last 30–90 days
   - Any recent negative review themes

Day 1 (within 24 hours)
7) Configure reply rules
   - Approved phrases: greetings, apology language, compensation language, call-to-action
   - Disallowed phrases: admissions of fault, legal statements, medical claims, promises
   - Escalation triggers: rating threshold + keywords (refund, lawsuit, discrimination, injury, health claim)
8) Produce “Sample Approval Pack” (send 6 drafts)
   - 2 positive
   - 2 neutral/3-star
   - 2 negative (de-escalation + invite offline)
   Include: tone options A/B + a single question: “Approve A or B (or edits)?”

Day 2 (within 48 hours)
9) Go live
   - If access granted: begin posting responses per SLA
   - If no access: deliver daily “Draft Queue” via email with paste-ready replies
10) Confirm first-week success criteria (email)
   - Response time: <24 business hours
   - Coverage: 100% of new reviews responded (unless customer excludes certain types)
   - Negatives: escalated within 2 hours during business hours
   - Weekly KPI report delivered on Day 7

Day 7 (first week)
11) Send Week 1 report
   - New reviews count, avg rating change, response rate, median response time, negative themes + actions
12) Renewal / expansion ask
   - “Add next location?” / “Add Yelp?” / “Add SMS alerting?”

Operator QA guardrails (always)
- Never argue with reviewer.
- Never disclose personal/medical info.
- Never admit liability.
- Always invite offline resolution for negatives.
- Keep replies brand-safe and consistent.
