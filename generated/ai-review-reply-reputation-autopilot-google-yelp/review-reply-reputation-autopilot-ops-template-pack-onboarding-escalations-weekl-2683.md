## 2) Escalation Ticket + SLA Policy (Copy/Paste)

**Purpose:** Ensure negative reviews are handled fast, safely, and consistently.

### Escalation triggers (auto-escalate if any apply)
- Any 1★ or 2★ review
- 3★ review with negative sentiment (service complaint, billing dispute, staff complaint)
- Mentions of: injury, discrimination, harassment, illegal activity, police, lawsuit, HIPAA/medical privacy, insurance, refunds/chargebacks
- Reviewer claims factual inaccuracies we may want to dispute (do not dispute without approval)

### Response SLA targets
- 4–5★: draft within 24 hours (12 hours for Pro tier)
