## 5) Daily sending ops checklist (what to do every day)
### Non-negotiables (deliverability + quality)
- Send only to business-relevant roles: owner, office manager, practice manager, general manager, marketing manager.
- Keep bounce rate < 3%; pause sending if it rises.
- Don’t use tracking links/images if deliverability is shaky; keep copy plain-text.
- Personalize Priority A leads (snippet + response gap) before sending.

### Daily targets (starting point)
- Day 1–3: 20–30 new emails/day
- Day 4–7: 30–50 new emails/day
- Week 2+: 50–100 new emails/day (only if bounces/complaints stay low)
- Follow-ups: 1 follow-up pass/day (don’t let follow-ups pile up)

### Reply handling SLA
- Reply within 1–2 hours during business day.
- If they ask “price?”: respond with a simple range + offer to draft 2 samples.
- If they ask “how do you post?”: explain approval flow (approve drafts vs pre-approved guidelines).
