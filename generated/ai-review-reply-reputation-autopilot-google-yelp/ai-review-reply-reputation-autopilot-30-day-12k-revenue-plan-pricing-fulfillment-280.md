- No admissions of fault, no promises of compensation in public, no sharing customer info, no arguing.
- Encourage offline resolution with a dedicated contact method.
- No review gating or incentives.

Posting method (to ensure deliverability in week 1):
- Preferred: client grants Bob Smith manager access on GBP and Yelp Business Owner access.
- Fallback (low-friction option): “Client-post workflow” — we draft responses in a shared doc/email and client posts; SLA still applies for draft delivery.

Human-in-the-loop workflow:
1) Onboarding intake (brand voice, offers, contact info, escalation contacts, banned phrases)
2) Daily review capture: manual check 1–2x/day or client enables email alerts and forwards to agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to
3) Draft response: generate 1–2 options per review following brand voice and policies
4) QA: human check for tone, compliance, accuracy, and escalation triggers
5) Approvals:
   - Auto-approve allowed: 4–5★ with no red flags (client can opt out)
   - Approval required: 1–3★, or flagged keywords
6) Post:
   - If access granted: we post directly
   - If client-post: we send paste-ready reply text with link and instructions
7) Weekly KPI report (every Monday): new reviews, avg rating trend, response rate, median response time, negative review count, escalations opened/resolved, top themes + suggested ops fixes
