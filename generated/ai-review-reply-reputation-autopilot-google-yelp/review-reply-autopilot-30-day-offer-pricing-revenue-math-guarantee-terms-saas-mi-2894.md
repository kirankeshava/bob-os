# Service → SaaS Migration Path (reduces cost-to-serve over time)
## Phase 1 (Day 0–30): DFY + Approval (current offer)
- We monitor, draft, route for approval, and (where allowed) post.
- Goal: prove response time, voice quality, and rating protection.

## Phase 2 (Day 31–60): Hybrid (customer self-serve + our guardrails)
Feature gates to introduce:
- Customer dashboard for approvals, templates, and escalation rules
- “Auto-approve” rules by star rating, keywords, and review type
- Weekly KPI report inside the app + email

Suggested SaaS pricing (per location):
- **SaaS Basic $299/mo/location**: monitoring + drafting + approval inbox + basic rules; posting via connected account where supported.
- **SaaS Pro $499/mo/location**: advanced rules, multi-user approvals, sentiment/theme tagging, exportable reporting, agency controls.

## Phase 3 (Day 61+): SaaS-first + optional managed add-on
- Default customers to SaaS tiers.
- Keep DFY as an add-on (“Managed Replies”) for higher-volume or time-poor operators.

**Migration rule of thumb:** Move a customer to SaaS once brand voice is stable, approval rules are predictable, and review volume is consistent. Offer a “founder migration credit” (e.g., first month at $299) to lock retention.
