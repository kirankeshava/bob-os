7) Go-live: start drafting and (per approval mode) posting
8) QA: check listings, location naming, hours/services references, and no prohibited content
9) Send “We’re live” confirmation + expectations for weekly KPI email

Intake form questions (copy/paste):
- Business name + locations + website + phone
- Primary services (top 5)
- Brand voice: 3 adjectives (e.g., warm, concise, professional)
- Do/Don’t phrases (e.g., avoid “we apologize” / prefer “thank you for the feedback”)
- Offers allowed? (discounts, free consult, etc.)
- Topics to always escalate (refunds, legal, medical, discrimination, staff complaints, privacy)
- Escalation contact(s): name, email, phone
- Approval mode: Manual / Post-approval / Autopilot
- Competitor mentions: reply rule (ignore/neutral)
- Staff names: allowed or never mention
