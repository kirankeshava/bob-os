Objection Handling (quick blocks)
====================================================

1) “We’re worried about policy/compliance.”
Completely fair — we follow a policy-safe workflow: no incentives, no gating, no asking only happy customers to review, and we never share private customer details in public replies.

2) “Yelp is strict about soliciting reviews.”
Agreed. Our service focuses on responding to reviews and monitoring, not aggressive solicitation. If you want review-generation guidance, we keep it conservative and policy-aware.

3) “We tried AI replies and they sounded fake.”
That’s usually missing brand voice rules + escalation logic. We set tone, length, and do-not-say constraints, then humans review edge cases. The goal is consistent and authentic, not robotic.

4) “We don’t want to give access.”
No problem — we can operate Draft-Only: you post from your account. If you prefer posting included, we request manager-level access only and use an approval/escalation rule for negative reviews.
