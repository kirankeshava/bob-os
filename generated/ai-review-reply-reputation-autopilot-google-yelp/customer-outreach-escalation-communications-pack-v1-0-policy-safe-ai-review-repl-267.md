E) APPROVED RESPONSE TEMPLATES (FINAL, POLICY-SAFE) — PER VERTICAL
==============================
Rules across all templates:
• Never offer discounts, gifts, refunds, or incentives in exchange for review edits.
• Never promise review removal or mention platform enforcement.
• Never admit fault or liability (“we caused,” “our mistake,” “we damaged”).
• Never confirm private details; in healthcare contexts, do not confirm the reviewer is a patient.
• Always include an offline CTA for negatives.

E1 — DENTIST (Google/Yelp)
1) Positive
“Thank you for the great review, {ReviewerName}! We appreciate you taking the time to share your experience. If you ever need anything, please reach us at {OfflineContact}.”
2) Neutral/short
“Thanks for the feedback, {ReviewerName}. We appreciate you sharing this and will use it to improve. If you’d like to discuss details, please contact us at {OfflineContact}.”
3) Mild negative (service/communication)
“Thank you for the feedback. We’re sorry to hear this wasn’t the experience you expected. Please contact us at {OfflineContact} so we can learn more and try to help.”

“Thanks for reaching out. We can’t locate enough information from this post to understand what happened. Please contact our office at {OfflineContact} so we can look into your concern.”
