## Goal (30 days)
Close 3–10 local-business clients for “AI Review Reply & Reputation Autopilot” (setup + monthly). Start with $0 spend; only consider paid options after the first 1–2 paying clients.

---
## Offer (single CTA everywhere)
**Free “Last 10 Reviews” Mini-Audit (24h)** → **10-min call** → **Starter Setup (fixed price)** → **Monthly Autopilot**

### Mini-Audit deliverable (send as bullets)
1) 3 missed response opportunities (wording + tone fixes)
2) 2 risk issues (HIPAA/sensitive, refund promises, policy conflicts)
3) A “reply style” recommendation (brand voice + templates)
4) A quick-win plan for next 7 days

### Starter package (fixed price)
**“Autopilot Setup + 7 Days of Responses”**
- Brand voice + do/don’t list
- Response templates for 5-star / 4-star / 1–3-star
- Escalation rules + notification workflow
- Draft responses for new reviews for 7 days (client posts, or you post if granted access later)
- Weekly KPI snapshot

Monthly retainer ladder (later): Starter / Pro / Multi-location.

## $0 Funnel (free tools only)
### Booking
Option A (free): **Google Calendar Appointment Schedules** (if you have Google Workspace) or **Google Calendar appointment slots**.
Option B (free): **Calendly Free**.

### Intake form (free)
Use **Google Form** titled: “Reputation Autopilot – 10-Review Audit Request”.

**Google Form questions (copy/paste):**
1. Business name
2. Website (optional)
3. Google Business Profile link
4. Yelp link (if applicable)
5. Primary category (e.g., dentist, HVAC, medspa)
6. Locations (# and cities)
7. Who responds to reviews today? (owner/staff/no one)
8. Biggest issue right now: (low rating / too many reviews to answer / negative review risk / inconsistent tone)
9. Preferred brand voice (friendly/professional/playful/luxury/other)
10. Words/phrases to avoid (e.g., “refund”, “HIPAA”, competitor names)
11. Escalation contact email + phone (for 1–2 star)
12. Are you okay with us drafting responses that you approve/post? (Yes/No)

**Form confirmation message (copy):**
“Thanks—got it. We’ll review your last 10 reviews and send a mini-audit within 24 hours. If you want to walk through it live, book a 10-min slot here: [BOOKING LINK].”

### CRM (free)
Google Sheet columns:
Date | Channel | Prospect | Niche | Link (GBP/Yelp) | Status (audit sent/call booked/proposal/closed) | Next step | Notes | Est. MRR

## Upwork (lean strategy: minimize or avoid Connects)
### Positioning
Search and apply only where the client explicitly wants: review responses, reputation management, GBP management, Yelp responses, customer feedback.

### Saved searches (examples)
- “respond to google reviews”
- “reputation management”
- “google business profile reviews”
- “yelp review responses”
- “local business reputation”
- “review reply”

### Lean bidding rules (to keep spend near $0)
1) Prioritize invites/replies and low-connect jobs.
2) Apply only if: clear business name/location OR clear niche + urgent pain (bad reviews/no time).
3) Skip: vague agencies, “SEO package” bait, $5/hr bulk.
4) Follow-up is your edge: send mini-audit bullets in follow-ups.

### Daily Upwork routine (30–45 min)
1) 10 min: check invites/messages first.
2) 15 min: review saved searches; shortlist 5 jobs.
3) 15 min: apply to 2–5 jobs max (quality > volume).
4) 5 min: update CRM.

### Follow-up sequence (no spam)
- T+24h: 2 bullets mini-audit + 1 question
- T+72h: 1 risk + 1 quick win
- T+7d: close loop + offer audit

## Fiverr (organic only, no Promoted Gigs)
### Publishing checklist (fast)
1) Publish 3 gigs (Google replies, Yelp replies, Weekly KPI report).
2) Use clean thumbnails (Canva free). Text should be large and readable.
3) Add 5 tags per gig; keep descriptions scannable.
4) Turn on “availability” and respond within 1 hour when possible.

### Organic ranking routine (15 min/day)
- Refresh FAQ weekly (minor edits)
- Send 1 buyer request-style message where applicable (if Fiverr allows)
- Ask every buyer for a review after delivery

---
## Facebook Groups (organic value-first)
### Group selection (10 groups to start)
Pick owner-heavy groups in 2–3 niches you can serve strongly (e.g., dental + medspa + home services). Look for:
- 10k+ members
- daily posts
- rules allow “free audits” (not direct selling)

### Posting cadence (start small)
- Day 1: post in 3 groups
- Day 3: post in 3 groups
- Day 5: post in 4 groups
Then repeat weekly with the remaining templates.

### Comment/DM workflow
1) Post the audit offer.
2) Anyone who comments “me” gets a short reply: “Drop your GBP link + city (or DM).”
3) DM within 30 minutes with the inbound script + Google Form link.
4) Send mini-audit within 24 hours and CTA to book.
