1) **Capture** reviews

- AI-drafted + human QA pass for policy/brand safety
- Templates vary by star level and theme (service issue, billing, wait time, praise)
3) **Approval rules**
- 4–5★: auto-approve unless flagged keyword present
- 1–3★: require client approval OR use pre-approved “safe negative” template + escalate

- Preferred: we post directly via manager access
- Fallback: send client a “Copy/Paste Ready” response within SLA; client posts

Escalate within **4 business hours** if:
- 1–2★ reviews
- Mentions of safety, fraud, discrimination, health/legal threats
- Requests for refund/chargeback
- Competitor/employee allegations

Escalation ticket includes: review link, summary, suggested response, recommended offline resolution steps, owner/staff assignee, due date.

- New reviews (#) and by platform
- Average rating (this week, trailing 30 days)
- Response rate %
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)
