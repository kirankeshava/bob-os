FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO API DEPENDENCY)
Review sources supported now:
- Google Business Profile reviews (primary)
- Yelp reviews (supported via business owner access or client-post workflow)

Posting methods (explicitly designed to be deliverable immediately):
1) Preferred: client grants GBP “Manager” access and Yelp Business Owner account access (or adds us as user where available).
2) Fallback (default if Yelp access is slow): we draft responses and send client “copy/paste ready” replies daily; client posts. This keeps SLA and value without engineering.

Daily workflow SOP (operator outline)
1) Intake & calibration (Day 0–2)
- Collect brand voice, offers, sensitive topics, compliance notes, escalation contacts
- Confirm SLA and approval rules
- Draft 10 sample responses; adjust to match tone

2) Daily review capture (Mon–Fri)
- Pull new reviews from GBP + Yelp (manual check or email alerts forwarded)
- Log into tracker: date, platform, star rating, reviewer name, theme tags

3) Draft response (AI-assisted + human QA)
- Generate response with brand voice guardrails
- Human QA checks: policy compliance, no admissions of fault, no incentives, no private info, no medical/legal claims, no discriminatory language

4) Approval rules
- Auto-approve 4–5★ responses unless flagged by sensitive keywords
- Require client approval for 1–3★ OR if keywords: refund, lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA/medical, harassment, threat, fraud

5) Posting
- Post directly when access available
- Otherwise send a daily “Ready to Post” message with responses per platform

6) Escalation rules (negative reviews)
- Create escalation ticket: summary, risk level, recommended internal action, suggested public reply, suggested private outreach script
- Track status: opened → in progress → resolved

7) Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
- New reviews (count)
- Average rating (this week vs last week)
- Response rate (% responded)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)
- Escalations opened/resolved
- Top 3 themes (from tags)
