## 2) Guarantee + SLA + Key Terms (Copy/Paste)
### 24-Business-Hour Response Guarantee
If we fail to deliver drafted responses (or post after approval) within **24 business hours** for eligible reviews during an active paid month, you receive **a credit equal to 1 week of service** for that location. If response timing falls below the SLA for **two separate weeks** in the same month, you receive **the next month free** for that location.

### What counts as “eligible”
- Reviews posted to the connected **Google Business Profile** and/or **Yelp** listing(s)
- Review is visible publicly and accessible via granted access
- Excludes: platform outages, removed/filtered reviews, missing permissions

### Review volume caps
- Standard: up to **60 replies/location/month**
- High-Volume: up to **120 replies/location/month**
- Additional replies: handled by upgrading tier or an agreed overage (post-week-1 only)

### Approval workflow (default = safest)
- Default mode: **Approval-first**. We draft; you approve; we post.
- Optional (later): “Auto-post” after 12-hour window with no changes requested.

### Escalation policy
- Any 1–2★ review is flagged with: summary, risk notes, and recommended resolution language.
- We do not admit liability or offer refunds publicly unless you explicitly approve wording.

### Access requirements
- Google: add us as a manager to GBP (or provide a delegated workflow).
- Yelp: add us via the business owner tools where possible; if not, we draft for you to paste.

### Refunds/credits
- Setup fees are non-refundable once brand voice + workflow are configured.
- Monthly: service credits apply per SLA above; cancellations stop next cycle.

## 5) Service-First → SaaS Migration Path ($299–$499/location)
Goal: close customers fast with DFY + approvals, then transition to software once patterns and trust are established.

### Phase 1: DFY Trial (Days 1–7)
- We draft (and optionally post after approval)
- Deliverables: brand voice profile + first reply library
- Outcome metric: response time + reduced negative-review risk

### Phase 2: DFY Monthly (Month 1–2)
- Keep DFY as default to remove friction
- Introduce “workspace” behaviors: approval queue, canned snippets, escalation tags

### Phase 3: Hybrid (Month 2–3)
- Customer can self-serve low-risk replies using our templates
- We handle negative reviews + complex cases

### Phase 4: SaaS (Month 3+)
Offer tiers (per location):
- **SaaS Starter $299/mo**: AI drafts + approval queue + snippet library + weekly KPI report (customer posts)
- **SaaS Pro $499/mo**: multi-user, multi-location routing, auto-escalation rules, advanced reporting + integrations

Conversion incentive:
- If customer moves from DFY to SaaS, keep their effective price within **$50–$100** of current spend for 60 days to reduce churn risk.
