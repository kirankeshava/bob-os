CASE STUDY #1 (DENTAL CLINIC) — LONG MODULE (Landing/Proposal)
Headline: Same-week reputation turnaround: from “no replies” to same-day responsiveness
Problem:
A multi-chair dental clinic was receiving steady Google reviews but responding inconsistently. Negative reviews (billing confusion, wait times) were left unanswered, creating the impression the practice didn’t care.
What we did:
• Implemented daily review monitoring + brand-voice templates tailored for clinical professionalism
• Set escalation rules for billing disputes and anything mentioning pain/safety
• Drafted replies for every new review; sensitive negatives routed for approval within a 4-hour window
• Added weekly KPI email highlighting themes (wait time mentions, front-desk sentiment)

Headline: Fewer lost leads from unanswered negatives — and a cleaner first impression
Problem:
A home services business (HVAC/plumbing) had high review volume during busy season. Positive reviews went unacknowledged; negatives sat publicly for weeks, hurting trust and quote requests.
