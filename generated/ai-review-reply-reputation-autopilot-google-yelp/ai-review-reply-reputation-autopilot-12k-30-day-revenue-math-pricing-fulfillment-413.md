• Rating 1–3 stars
• Mentions: refund, lawsuit, attorney, insurance, HIPAA/PHI, discrimination, safety, “scam”, “fraud”, threats, chargeback
• Requests for private info or appointment details
• Staff named + accusation of misconduct
Escalation process:
1) Create escalation ticket with review link, screenshot, summary, recommended response options.
2) Notify designated contact by email within SLA.
3) Response requires explicit approval (no auto-post).
4) Track resolution outcome (replied? resolved? updated rating?).

Auto-approval rules
• 4–5★ reviews: auto-post if not flagged by keyword list.
• 1–3★ reviews: always require approval (or client-post) unless client opts into “pre-approved negative templates” after month 1.

• We do not remove reviews; we can recommend dispute steps but client remains account owner.
