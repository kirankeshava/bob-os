## Fulfillment scope (deliverable in Week 1) + constraints (Google/Yelp)
**Goal:** deliver without building product or relying on APIs.

### Review sources + capture
- **Google Business Profile:** capture via in-product notifications, email alerts, or daily manual check.
- **Yelp:** capture via Yelp business owner dashboard notifications or daily manual check.

### Posting method (no-API dependency)
**Default (preferred):** client grants **GBP Manager access** (and Yelp business owner access if possible). We post directly.
**Fallback (always available):** we draft + QA, then send “copy/paste-ready” responses to client for posting (same-day).

### SLAs + approval rules (human-in-the-loop)
- **4–5★ reviews:** auto-approve + post unless flagged (policy/keyword/brand conflict).
- **1–3★ reviews:** require approval or escalation workflow before posting.
- **Flag keywords:** legal threats, medical claims, discrimination, safety incidents, refund disputes, HIPAA/PII, employee names.

### Escalation rules
Create an escalation ticket when any of the following occurs:
- 1–2★ rating
- Mentions: fraud, lawsuit, safety, discrimination, medical/privacy, chargeback
- Repeat complaint pattern ≥3 times in 30 days
**Ticket includes:** review link, summary, recommended response options (2 variants), suggested offline resolution step, owner/staff assignee, due date.

### Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
- New reviews (G/Y)
- Avg rating trend (7/30/90 day)
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count + resolved count
- Top 3 themes + 1 recommended ops fix

**Explicitly excluded (policy):**
- Review gating or incentives in violation of platform policies
- “We can remove reviews” promises
- Posting disputing content without client authorization
