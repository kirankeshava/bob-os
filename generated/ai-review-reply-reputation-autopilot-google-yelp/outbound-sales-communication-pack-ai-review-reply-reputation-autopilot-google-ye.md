5) DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (10–15 MINUTES)
====================

Goal: qualify quickly, confirm review volume + risk tolerance, pick SLA, close into pilot.

A) Frame (30 seconds)
“Thanks for the time. In 10 minutes I’ll understand your review volume and brand voice, show how the autopilot works (drafts + escalation + weekly KPIs), and if it’s a fit we can start with a 7-day pilot.”

B) Diagnosis questions (6–7 minutes)
1) “Which platforms matter most—Google, Yelp, Facebook?” (We focus Google/Yelp.)
2) “How many locations? Any shared brand guidelines across them?”
3) “Roughly how many new reviews per week per location?”
4) “Who replies today, and how long does it usually take?”
5) “What’s your biggest worry—saying the wrong thing, missing negatives, or time?”
6) “Any compliance constraints? (HIPAA, refunds, legal disputes, employee issues)”
7) “Do you want draft-only (you post) or draft+post (we post with escalation rules)?”

C) Pitch the process (2 minutes)
“Here’s the simple workflow:
• New review comes in → we draft a brand-safe reply using your tone
• If it’s 1–2 star or mentions sensitive topics → it’s escalated for approval
• Otherwise it’s posted (or queued for you if draft-only)
• Every week you get KPIs: response time, volume, sentiment, themes, and recommended actions.”

D) Close (2 minutes)
“Based on what you said—{{X}} locations and about {{Y}} reviews/week—the right fit is {{SLA}} with {{draft-only/draft+post}}. We can start with a 7-day pilot. To kick off, we just need access (GBP/Yelp) and your brand voice preferences. Want to start this week?”
