1) Day-1 Lead Batch Build Plan (200 targets)
Goal: 150 local owners + 50 agencies. Build in 2–3 hours using only free sources.

A) Local Owners (150 total)
Split: 50 dentists + 50 med spas + 50 HVAC.
Source: Google Maps.
Process:
- Search footprints (rotate by city):
  • Dentists: “cosmetic dentist {city}”, “family dentistry {city}”, “dental implants {city}”
  • Med spas: “med spa {city}”, “botox {city}”, “aesthetic clinic {city}”
  • HVAC: “HVAC contractor {city}”, “air conditioning repair {city}”, “furnace installation {city}”
- For each business, capture:
  • Business Name
  • City/State
  • Phone
  • Website
  • Google rating + # reviews (visible in Maps)
  • Evidence note (e.g., “4.2★ / 86 reviews; last owner response 6 months ago”)
- Find owner/manager email (free methods):
  • Website contact page
  • “mailto:” on site
  • Footer / privacy policy
  • If only form exists: use form submission as outreach channel and log it separately (Channel=ContactForm).

B) Agencies (50 total)
Target: agencies managing multiple local clients / multi-location.
Free sources:
- Google search footprints:
  • “white label reputation management agency”
  • “local seo agency multi location”
  • “google business profile management agency”
  • “reputation management services for agencies”
- Agency directories (free browsing): Clutch category pages, DesignRush category pages (manual capture).
Data to capture:
  • Agency Name
  • Website
  • Decision maker name (Founder/Owner/Partner if listed)
  • Email (from site)
  • Positioning note (e.g., “offers GBP mgmt; no explicit review response automation”)

- HVAC: “You’re getting steady review volume—fast replies tend to lift call conversions, especially in peak season.”
- Agency: “Saw you manage GBP/local SEO for multiple clients—this could plug in as a white-label ‘review reply autopilot’ for your roster.”
