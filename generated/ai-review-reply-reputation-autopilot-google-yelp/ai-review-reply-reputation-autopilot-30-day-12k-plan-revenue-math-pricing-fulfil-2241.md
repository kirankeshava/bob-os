Escalation output:
• Ticket created (email thread or sheet row) + recommended response + recommended internal action
• Client owner/manager notified with 2 options: “approve response” or “handle offline first”

Posting method (how we deliver replies without APIs):
Default (preferred):
1) Client adds agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to as a Manager on Google Business Profile.
2) For Yelp, client adds us as a user OR provides a method for client-post.
Fallback (always available):
• We draft responses in a shared Google Doc/Sheet; client posts using copy/paste (same-day).

Human-in-the-loop QA (brand safety):
• Auto-approve policy: 4–5★ generic praise replies can be posted without explicit approval if client opts in.
• Must-approve policy: 1–3★, any escalation trigger, or any review mentioning regulated categories (medical/legal/financial) requires client approval.

Explicit exclusions (to avoid policy violations):
• No incentivizing reviews, no review gating, no “leave us 5★ for a discount” language.
• No deleting reviews or impersonation.
• No disputing reviews on behalf of client unless they explicitly request and provide authorization.
