FULFILLMENT SCOPE + SOP (WEEK-1 READY)
1) Onboarding (Day 0–2)
- Collect: brand voice, offers, banned phrases, escalation contacts, refund/redo policy, hours, location details.
- Access: request GBP manager access; request Yelp owner access; if not granted, set client-post workflow.
- Setup: create response templates (4–5★, 3★, 1–2★, service recovery, “no record found”).

2) Daily Operations (Mon–Fri)
- Review capture: check new reviews 1–2x/day (or use email alerts/screenshots).
- Drafting: generate responses using client voice rules.
- QA gate:
  a) Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged (medical/legal/financial claims, profanity, discrimination, HIPAA/PII).
  b) Require approval for 1–3★, refund demands, threats, legal/medical keywords, or staff allegations.
- Posting:
  a) If access: post directly in GBP/Yelp.
  b) If no access: send client a “ready-to-post” block with exact steps + copy.

3) Escalation Rules
- Trigger: any 1–2★; any review alleging safety, fraud, discrimination, HIPAA/PII, legal threats.
- SLA: notify business within 4 business hours (Pro) / 1 business day (Growth).
- Resolution tracking: status = Open / In progress / Resolved; record outcome and whether reviewer updated rating.

4) Weekly KPI Report (Every Monday)
- KPIs: new reviews, average rating, rating trend vs last week, response rate, median response time, # negative reviews, # escalations, % resolved, top 3 themes, recommended operational fix.
- Delivery: email summary + simple sheet snapshot.
