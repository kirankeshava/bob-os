## 6) Daily Sending Ops Checklist (do this every day)
### List hygiene + prep (30–45 min)
1) Pull today’s prospects by **Priority A first**, then B, then C.
2) Verify category fit (dentist/med spa/HVAC/plumbing) and not a mega-franchise HQ.
3) Confirm Google rating, review count, and last review date.
4) Capture **one** safe personalization snippet (quote short or paraphrase). Avoid sensitive info.
5) Identify best email(s): website contact, owner/manager, “info@”, “office@”, “admin@”.

### Sending rules
- Start low and ramp (see below). Keep under 2% hard bounce.
- Plain text emails; no attachments.
- One ask per email: “Want drafts?” or “10-min chat?”

### Reply handling SLA
- Reply within 1 business day.
- If they ask pricing: offer a 10-min call + a quick written range.
- If negative: mark Lost + note reason.

### KPI tracking (daily)
- Sent / Delivered / Bounced
- Replies (positive/neutral/negative)
- Meetings booked
