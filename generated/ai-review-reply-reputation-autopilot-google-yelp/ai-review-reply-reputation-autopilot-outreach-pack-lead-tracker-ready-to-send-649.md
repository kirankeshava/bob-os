====================
1) TARGET / ICP (who we reach out to first)
====================
Best first 20 customers (fast cash + clear pain):
- Local service businesses where reviews directly drive calls: dental, medspa, chiro/PT, urgent care, home services (HVAC/plumbing/roofing), auto repair, restaurants with high review velocity.
- Signals of pain:
  - Rating 3.5–4.6 (room to lift)
  - 10+ reviews/month OR multiple locations
  - Owner replies are inconsistent or generic
  - Recent negative review unanswered 3+ days

====================
2) LEAD SCORING (quick rubric)
====================
Score 0–10. Prioritize 7+.
- Review velocity (0–3): 0=low, 1=1–3/mo, 2=4–9/mo, 3=10+/mo
- Response gap (0–3): 0=replies to most, 2=inconsistent, 3=rarely replies
- Rating opportunity (0–2): 0=4.7+, 1=4.2–4.6, 2=3.5–4.1
- Multi-location (0–2): 0=single, 2=2+ locations
