3) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (week-1 deliverable, no API dependency)

Sources covered
- Google Business Profile reviews (GBP)
- Yelp reviews

Capture (how we detect reviews)
- Default (Week 1): manual check 1–2x/day + client enables email alerts for new reviews.
- Optional (later): API/connector-based ingestion once approved/available.

Drafting + QA workflow
1) Onboarding intake (30 min): brand voice, do/don’t phrases, offers/guarantees allowed, escalation contacts, hours, sensitive topics.
2) Daily review sweep: identify new reviews needing responses.
3) Draft response using approved brand voice + policy rules.
4) Human QA check (brand safety + compliance): remove incentives, avoid HIPAA/medical specifics, avoid admissions of fault, avoid harassment.
5) Approval rules:
   - 4–5★: auto-approve unless flagged keywords appear.
   - 1–3★: require client approval OR escalation workflow before posting.
   - Always require approval if keywords include: refund/legal/chargeback/injury/discrimination/medical diagnosis.

Posting method (deliverable week 1)
- Preferred: client adds us as manager (GBP) and business user (Yelp) so we can post natively.
- Fallback (no access): we email a “ready-to-post” response pack daily; client posts copy/paste.

Escalation rules (negative reviews)
- Trigger: 1–2★ OR 3★ with negative sentiment OR mentions of safety/legal/health.
- SLA: escalation sent within 4 business hours of detection.
- Escalation includes: summary, suggested next step, 2–3 response options, and whether to request offline resolution.
- Track status: Open → Client contacted → Resolved/Closed.

Weekly KPI Report (every Monday)
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating + trend vs prior week
- Response rate (% replied)
- Median response time
- Negative review count + open/closed escalations
- Top 3 themes (what customers praise/complain about)

Explicit constraints (to stay compliant and deliverable)
- No review gating, no incentives for positive reviews.
- We do not delete reviews.
- We do not dispute reviews on behalf of client without explicit written authorization and provided evidence.
- Yelp: API access is restricted; therefore week-1 default is access-based posting OR client-post fallback.
