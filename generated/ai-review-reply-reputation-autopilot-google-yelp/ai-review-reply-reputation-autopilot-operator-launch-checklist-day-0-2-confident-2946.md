A) OPERATOR LAUNCH CHECKLIST (DAY 0–2 ACTIVATION)
=============================
Goal: From “Yes” → fully active review responses + escalation + weekly reporting within 48 hours.

DAY 0 (CLOSE + START)
1) Confirm scope on call (or by email)
   - # of locations (start with 1 if unclear)
   - Channels: Google Business Profile (GBP), Yelp, or both
   - Review volume (avg reviews/week) + current response time
   - Escalation contact(s) + hours
   - Approval method: (A) Autopilot posting OR (B) human approval for first week
2) Send “Start Today” email (use Kickoff template from playbook) including:
   - Legitimacy URL
   - Intake form (copy/paste questions below or link to your form)
   - Access request instructions (GBP + Yelp)
   - Proposed go-live time (within 48 hours)
3) Create/Update CRM record
   - Stage → “Closed – Pending Access”
   - Add next task: “Access received?” due in 24 hours

DAY 0 INTAKE (CUSTOMER COMPLETES)
4) Customer completes Intake (minimum viable fields)
   - Business name + location(s) + URLs
   - Brand voice (3 adjectives) + do/don’t list
   - Refund/redo policy basics (if any)
   - Escalation rules (what triggers escalation)
   - Escalation contacts + preferred method (email/text)

DAY 0–1 ACCESS (MOST COMMON BOTTLENECK)
5) Google Business Profile access
   - Customer adds agent as Manager (or sends invite) OR shares via agency
   - If they can’t, collect screenshot confirming who controls GBP and coordinate
6) Yelp access
   - Customer adds user in Yelp for Business (if available) OR agrees to “draft-only” workflow (we email drafts for copy/paste)
7) If access delayed >24h
   - Switch to “Draft-Only Mode” immediately: customer forwards review notifications or sends weekly export; we reply with ready-to-post drafts.

DAY 1 QA + BRAND SAFETY SETUP
8) Build “Brand-Safe Reply Rules” (internal)
   - Tone: friendly/professional/etc.
   - Always: thank reviewer, address specifics, invite offline contact for issues
   - Never: mention incentives, admit liability, disclose private info, argue
   - Add owner-approved contact line (phone/email)
9) Prepare 6 sample replies for approval (fast)
   - 2 positive, 2 neutral, 2 negative
   - Include 1 escalation note example
10) Get approvals (15-min async)
   - Customer selects: “Approve as-is” OR “Adjust tone like this…”

DAY 2 GO-LIVE
11) Turn on workflow
   - Posting mode: Autopost OR Draft-for-approval
   - Escalation: negative review triggers alert within X hours
12) First-week success criteria (tell customer + track)
   - 90%+ of new reviews responded within 24 hours (or within their business hours)
   - 100% of 1–2★ reviews escalated same day
   - Tone consistency approved
   - Weekly KPI report delivered on day 7
13) Set reporting cadence
   - Weekly email report (same day/time)
   - Monthly executive summary optional

B) LIGHTWEIGHT CONFIDENTIALITY CLAUSE (PASTE-READY)
=============================
Confidentiality (Plain Language)
We both agree to keep each other’s non-public business information confidential. This includes customer details, internal processes, access credentials, pricing terms, and any data shared for the purpose of drafting or posting review responses. We will only use confidential information to deliver the review response and reporting service, and we won’t share it with third parties except as needed to operate the service (for example, using email tools or document storage). Either party may disclose information if required by law. This confidentiality commitment continues after the service ends.

(Operator note: Do not claim legal status; avoid “NDA” if the buyer is sensitive. If they require a formal NDA, provide later.)
