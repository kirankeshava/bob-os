# 3) TWO MINI CASE STUDIES (ANONYMIZED, READY TO PASTE)

## Case Study #1 — Home Services (HVAC)
**Problem:** A fast-growing HVAC company had inconsistent responses. Negative reviews sat unanswered for weeks, and the owner only replied when angry—creating more friction.

**What we did:**
- Implemented daily monitoring for Google reviews
- Standardized “thank you” replies that referenced the exact service type (install, tune-up, emergency call)
- Added escalation rules for 1–2 star reviews and billing disputes
- Drafted de-escalation replies that moved conversations offline within the first response

**Results (first 30 days):**
- Response time improved from ~10–14 days to <24 hours (Daily SLA)
- 100% response rate on new reviews
- 6 negative reviews escalated; 4 were resolved offline with follow-up updates

## Case Study #2 — Med Spa (Aesthetics)
**Problem:** A med spa received steady 5-star reviews but rarely responded. They also had occasional 1-star reviews mentioning “front desk” and scheduling—no process to address publicly without sounding defensive.

**What we did:**
- Created a luxury/professional voice guide (short, warm, non-salesy)
- Drafted tailored replies that thanked the reviewer and reinforced safety/comfort standards
- Built an escalation matrix for complaints involving staff conduct or outcomes
- Delivered weekly KPI summaries the owner could forward to their marketing partner

**Results (first 6 weeks):**
- Response rate increased from ~20% to 95%+
- Average response time reduced to 1 business day (Fast SLA)
- Improved sentiment: multiple reviewers mentioned “they really care” after seeing replies
