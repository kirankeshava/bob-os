## Day 0 (Within 2 hours of verbal close)
1) **Send “Day 0 Kickoff” email**
   - Include: legitimacy URL, support inbox, intake form link/text, and access-request instructions.
2) **Confirm scope + start date**
   - 1 location vs multi-location; response coverage hours; escalation expectations.
3) **Start CRM entry**
   - Move deal stage to: Closed → Paid (or Closed Pending Payment if post-week-1).

## Day 0–1 (Access + intake)
4) **Get Intake completed** (required to begin)
   - Brand voice (tone, do/don’t phrases)
   - Escalation contact + phone
   - Refund/comp policy and what you can/can’t offer
   - List of locations + GBP/Yelp URLs
5) **Send Access Request Email(s)**
   - Google Business Profile: request “Manager” or “Communications Manager” (preferred minimum).
   - Yelp: request access via Business Account / user invite (or coordinate with agency).
6) **Security sanity check**
   - Confirm we only respond to reviews; no posting outside review replies.
   - Confirm escalation rules (what triggers immediate human review).

## Day 1 (Sample drafts + approval)
7) **Pull last 20 reviews (GBP + Yelp) + classify**
   - Positive / Neutral / Negative / Contains claims / Mentions staff / Mentions pricing.
8) **Draft sample set (6 total)**
   - 2 positive, 2 neutral, 2 negative (with de-escalation + invite offline).
9) **Send Sample Approval Note**
   - Ask for “approve / edits / do-not-say list.”

## Day 2 (Go-live)
10) **Finalize voice rules**
   - Convert feedback into a mini style guide (bullets).
11) **Start production**
   - Begin replying to backlog (as agreed) + new incoming reviews.
12) **Set first-week success criteria** (confirm via email)
   - Example: “90%+ of new reviews receive a draft within 12 business hours; 100% of 1–2 star reviews escalated within 1 hour; 0 brand-risk phrases.”

## Week 1 baseline + reporting
13) **Create baseline KPI snapshot**
   - Current star rating, response rate estimate, average response time (if available), # reviews last 30 days.
14) **Schedule weekly KPI report email**
   - Same day/time each week; include wins + escalations + next actions.
