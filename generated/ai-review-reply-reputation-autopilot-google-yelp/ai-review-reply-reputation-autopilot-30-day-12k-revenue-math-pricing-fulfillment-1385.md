## (4) Fulfillment scope (week-1 deliverable) + SOP outline
### Review sources covered
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

### Response SLA rules
- Growth/Agency Starter: respond within **24 business hours** (Mon–Fri)
- Pro/Agency Pro: respond within **12 business hours** (Mon–Sat)

### Escalation rules (brand safety)
Escalate within 2 hours during business day when review contains:
- 1–2★ rating
- Legal threats, chargebacks, discrimination claims
- Health/medical claims, HIPAA/PHI risk
- Safety incidents, injuries, “scam/fraud” language
- Media/social threats (“I’m posting this on TikTok”) 

### Posting method (no API dependency)
**Default (preferred):**
- Client grants **GBP Manager access** (and Yelp Business Owner access if available).
- We draft + QA + post directly inside the platform.

**Fallback (always available): client-post workflow**
- We draft responses in a shared doc/email.
- Client posts responses using templated copy.
- We still track KPIs and escalations.

### Week-1 SOP (operator steps)
1) **Onboarding intake (Day 0–1)**
   - Brand voice sample replies + tone (friendly/professional/short)
   - “Do-not-say” list + offers/discount restrictions
   - Escalation contacts + preferred resolution path
   - Access: GBP Manager; Yelp access or confirm client-post fallback

2) **Daily review capture (Day 1+)**
   - Use platform email alerts + manual check 1–2×/day.
   - Log new reviews in tracker (date, platform, rating, theme, status).

3) **Draft responses**
   - Generate response using the brand rules.
   - Ensure policy-safe: no incentives, no private data, no admissions of liability.

4) **QA + approval logic**
   - Auto-approve for posting: 4–5★ unless flagged keywords.
   - Requires client approval: 1–3★, escalated categories, refunds/legal.

5) **Post + verify**
   - Post in GBP/Yelp OR send to client for posting.
   - Mark “posted” with timestamp.

6) **Escalation ticket**
   - Create ticket with summary, recommended action, deadline.
   - Track resolution outcome (resolved/partial/unresolved).

7) **Weekly KPI report (every Monday)**
   - New reviews, average rating, response rate, median response time
   - Negative review count, escalations created, escalations resolved
   - Top 3 themes + recommended operational fix

### Explicit exclusions (reduce risk)
- No “review gating” or incentivizing reviews
- No removal guarantees
- No impersonation of customers
- No disputing reviews without explicit authorization
