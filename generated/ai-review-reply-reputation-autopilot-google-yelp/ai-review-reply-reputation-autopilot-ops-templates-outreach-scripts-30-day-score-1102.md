====================================================
Goal: $12,000 cash-in-month

- 2 Agency Starter deals (setup $199 + $399/mo)
- Upsell buffer: at least $304 (e.g., 1x SMS add-on)

A. Daily activity minimums (outbound-first assumptions)
Assumptions: 15% reply rate, 35% book rate from replies, 70% show rate, 25% close rate on shows
To close ~8 deals / 30 days ≈ need:
- Shows needed: 8 / 0.25 = 32 shows
- Calls booked needed: 32 / 0.70 ≈ 46 booked
- Replies needed: 46 / 0.35 ≈ 132 replies
- Leads needed: 132 / 0.15 ≈ 880 leads
Daily minimums (30 days):
- New leads/day: 30
- New replies/day: 4–5
- Calls booked/day: 1–2
- Shows/day: 1
- Closes/week: 2
(Stretch +20%: 36 leads/day; 2 calls booked/day)

B. Scoreboard columns (Sheet tab: “30-Day Scoreboard”)
Day # | Date | New Leads Added | New Replies | Calls Booked | Calls Held (Shows) | New Clients Closed | Setup $ Collected | MRR Added | Add-on $ Collected | Cash Collected (total) | Notes/Blockers

C. Formulas
- Cash Collected (total) = Setup $ + Add-on $ + (if collecting first month upfront) MRR Added
- Cumulative cash = SUM(Cash Collected)
- Projected end-of-month cash = (Cumulative cash / Day #) * 30
