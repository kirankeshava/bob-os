## 6) Tracking sheet blueprint (Google Sheets)
Create a Google Sheet with these tabs:

### Tab A: Outreach Log
Columns:
- Date
- Channel (Upwork/Fiverr/Cold Email/Referral)
- Campaign (e.g., UPW-DENT-050, EMAIL-HVAC-050)
- Prospect Name
- Business Name
- Niche
- City/State
- Link (GBP/Yelp)
- Contact (email/Upwork link)
- Status (Sent/Reply/Booked/No-show/Won/Lost)
- Last touch date
- Next follow-up date
- Notes

### Tab B: Calls Pipeline
Columns:
- Date booked
- Channel
- Prospect
- Showed? (Y/N)
- Outcome (Pilot started / Not a fit / Follow-up)
- Pilot start date
- Case study candidate? (Y/N)

### Tab C: Weekly KPI Summary
Metrics (weekly):
- Outreach volume by channel
- Replies by channel
- Booked calls by channel
- Show rate
- Close rate to pilot
- Cost (Week 1 = $0)
- CAC (later): Cost / pilots started (and Cost / customers)

Formulas examples:
- Booked calls by channel: COUNTIFS(OutreachLog!A:A, ">="&WeekStart, OutreachLog!A:A, "<="&WeekEnd, OutreachLog!J:J, "Booked", OutreachLog!B:B, "Upwork")
