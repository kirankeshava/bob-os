## 6) Tracker blueprint (Google Sheets)
Create a Google Sheet with these tabs:

### Tab A: Outreach_Log
Columns:
Date | Channel (Upwork/Email/Fiverr) | Campaign (e.g., UPW_LOCAL_REPUTATION_MAYW1) | Prospect | Niche | City | Contact | Link | Message_ID | Status (Sent/Replied/Booked/No) | Reply_Date | Call_Date | Outcome (Qual/NoFit) | Notes

### Tab B: Costs (Week 1 = $0)
Date | Channel | Spend | Notes

### Tab C: KPI_Dashboard
Metrics (weekly):
- Sends (count)
- Replies (count)
- Reply rate = Replies/Sends
- Booked calls
- Booking rate = Booked/Sends
- Show rate = Shows/Booked
- Close rate (later) = Deals/Shows
- CAC by channel = Spend/Deals (or Spend/Booked in Week 1 for proxy)

Cut rule: if a channel has Spend > $0 AND Booked Calls = 0 within 7 days of first spend, pause spend and redeploy.

## 7) Definition of a “qualified conversation”
