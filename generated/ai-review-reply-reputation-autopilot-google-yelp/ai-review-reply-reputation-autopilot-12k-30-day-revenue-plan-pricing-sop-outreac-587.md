# Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 Deliverable, No API Dependency)

## Review sources supported immediately
- **Google Business Profile** (GBP)
- **Yelp**

## Posting methods (week 1)
1) **Preferred:** Client adds us as manager/user on GBP + Yelp Business Owner access.
2) **Fallback (default if access is slow):** We draft responses + client posts.
   - Delivery via daily email or shared doc with “Copy/Paste” responses.

## Response SLA (by plan)
- DFY Growth: within **24 business hours**
- DFY Pro: within **12 business hours**
- Agency Starter: within **48 business hours**

## Escalation rules (brand-safe)
Escalate (do not auto-post) if any are true:
- Rating is **1–3 stars**
- Mentions: refund request, legal threat, safety incident, discrimination, HIPAA/medical details, harassment, doxxing, chargeback, “lawsuit”, “BBB”, “attorney”, “police”
- Reviewer alleges fraud, bait-and-switch, or physical harm

Escalation workflow:
- Create incident ticket (date, platform, link, issue summary)
- Draft empathetic reply + request to move to offline resolution
- Require client approval before posting
- Track status: Open → Posted → Contacted → Resolved/Unresolved

## Human-in-the-loop QA (non-negotiable in week 1)
- Every response is checked for: tone, no admission of liability, no private info, no incentives, no review gating.
- **Auto-approve** only for 4–5★ reviews that are not flagged.

## Weekly KPI Report (sent every Monday)
Metrics:
- New reviews (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating (trend vs last week)
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)
- Escalations: opened/resolved
- Top themes (top 3)
