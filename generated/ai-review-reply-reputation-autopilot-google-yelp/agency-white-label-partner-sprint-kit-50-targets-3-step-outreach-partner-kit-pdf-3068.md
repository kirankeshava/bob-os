## 4) Partner Kit (PDF-ready)
### Offer (White-label)
- Respond to all new Google Business Profile + Yelp reviews within 24 hours.
- Brand voice alignment (tone, do/don’t, approved phrases).
- Negative review escalation: notify agency + recommended action.
- Weekly KPI report (per location + rollup).

### Pilot (Week 1 = Free)
- 7-day pilot for **1–2 locations**.
- Includes: setup + daily monitoring + posting + one weekly KPI email.
- Success criteria: response coverage %, response time, rating trend notes, negative escalations handled.

### SLA
- Response draft within 24 hours for new reviews (excluding major holidays).
- Escalation alert for ≤3-star reviews within 12 hours.
- Weekly KPI report delivered every Monday (or agreed day).

### Onboarding SOP (Agency)
1. Provide location links (GBP + Yelp) and confirm access method (agency-managed posting vs “draft-only”).
2. Provide brand voice notes: tone (friendly/professional), forbidden topics, compensation policy, contact details, escalation contact.
3. Confirm routing: who receives negative review alerts + who approves unusual cases.

### Negative Review Escalation Playbook (summary)
- 1–2 stars: alert immediately; propose offline resolution language; never argue; offer a direct contact path.
- 3 stars: respond empathetically; ask for details; offer resolution.
- Sensitive categories (medical/legal): follow compliance language and keep minimal.

### Weekly KPI Report (sample)
**Client/Location:** {{LocationName}}
- New reviews: 14 (Google 12 / Yelp 2)
- Average rating (period): 4.6
- Responses posted: 14/14 (100%)
- Median response time: 7.2 hours
- Negative reviews (≤3): 2 (escalated: 2; resolved: 1 pending: 1)
- Common themes: “staff helpful”, “wait time”
- Next-week recommendation: reduce wait-time complaints with updated signage + staffing note

### Pricing (after pilot)
- Partner base: **$199/location/month** (Google + Yelp, 24h SLA, escalation, weekly KPI)
- Volume tiers (optional): 10+ locations $179; 25+ locations $159.
- Optional revenue share: Agency retails at $299–$399/location/month; we fulfill at partner rate.
