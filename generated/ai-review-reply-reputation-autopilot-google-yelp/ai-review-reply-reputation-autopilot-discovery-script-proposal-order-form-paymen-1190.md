1) “When a potential customer sees your listing, do they call/book online? What’s a typical new customer worth?”

3) “If replies went out within 24 hours consistently—would that reduce refunds, churn, or missed appointments?”
4) “What would 2–5 extra booked jobs/appointments per month be worth?”

(ROI prompt)
“Most local businesses are leaving money on the table when reviews sit unanswered—especially negatives. The win isn’t just ‘being polite’; it’s improving conversion and stopping reputation leaks.”

10:00–14:00 Process + safety (neutralize AI risk)
“We use AI to draft, but the system is built around brand safety:
- Brand voice calibration (your examples, do/don’t phrases)
- Prohibited topics list (refunds, legal threats, HIPAA/medical, pricing guarantees—whatever you specify)
- Escalation triggers: 1–3 star reviews, keywords like ‘lawsuit’, ‘scam’, ‘refund’, ‘BBB’, etc.
- Approval modes: (A) auto-post for 4–5 star; approval required for 1–3 star; (B) approval for everything; (C) auto-post everything (rare)
- Audit log + weekly KPI report
You remain the owner of the accounts; we operate as a manager/agent with limited access.”

14:00–16:30 Qualify for access + urgency
1) “Can you add us as a manager on Google Business Profile? (We’ll send exact steps.)”
2) “For Yelp: do you have Business Owner access, and are you currently responding there?”
3) “Who should receive escalation texts/emails for negative reviews?”
4) “Any compliance requirements? (medical/legal/financial services) ”

16:30–18:30 Offer recommendation (2 options)
“Based on your volume and speed needs, I recommend either:
Option 1: Starter—best if review volume is light and you mainly want consistent replies.
Option 2: Growth—best if you want faster SLAs + more locations/reviews covered.
Either way, we can be live within 48 hours after access + brand voice intake.”

18:30–20:00 Close (setup + month 1 upfront)
“To start, it’s a one-time setup plus the first month upfront. Once paid, we send the 48-hour onboarding form and access steps immediately. Which plan do you want to start on—Starter or Growth?”

If objection: “We can do it ourselves.”
“Totally. The question is consistency + speed. The gap we close is: replies within 24 hours, brand-safe language, negative-review escalation, and weekly KPI reporting—without you thinking about it. If you do it perfectly every day, you won’t need us. Most teams don’t.”

If objection: “AI risk.”
“That’s why we use approval modes + prohibited topics + escalation. We can start with ‘approval required for everything’ for week 1, then relax to auto-post for 4–5 star once you’re comfortable.”

If objection: “Price.”
“Understood. If this produces even 1–2 extra bookings per month or prevents a couple of refunds, it pays for itself. If you want, we can start at the smallest tier and upgrade once you see the workflow.”
