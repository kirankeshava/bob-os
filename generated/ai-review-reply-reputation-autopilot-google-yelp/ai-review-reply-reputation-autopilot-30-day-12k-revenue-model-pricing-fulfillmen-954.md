## Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable, no API dependency)
### Review sources
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp Business

### Posting methods (choose per client at onboarding)
**Preferred (lowest friction):** client grants **GBP Manager access** + **Yelp Business Owner access**.
- We draft + post directly in native platforms.

**Fallback (no access): “Client-Post Workflow”**
- We draft replies in a shared queue (Google Doc/Sheet + email), client posts within 24h.
- We include copy/paste text + exact review link + posting instructions.

### Response SLA
- DFY Growth: within **24 business hours**
- DFY Pro: within **12 business hours**

### Escalation rules (brand-safe / risk control)
Auto-approve & post (unless flagged):
- 4–5★ reviews with no sensitive keywords

Require approval before posting:
- 1–3★ reviews
- Any review mentioning: refund, lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA/medical, “scam,” police, chargeback
- Any request for personal info or incentives

Escalation workflow (negative reviews)
1) Tag review as escalation, notify escalation contact (email/text) within SLA
2) Draft 2 response options: public reply + private remediation note
3) Track status: acknowledged → in-progress → resolved

### Weekly KPI report (delivered every Monday)
Metrics:
- New reviews (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating change (WoW)
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- # negative reviews (1–3★)
- # escalations opened/resolved
- Top 3 recurring themes + suggested operational fix
