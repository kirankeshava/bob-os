# 4) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable, no engineering required)
## Review sources
- **Google Business Profile reviews**
- **Yelp reviews**

## Response SLA
- Growth: **24 business hours**
- Pro: **12 business hours**

## Escalation rules (examples)
Escalate within SLA if:
- Any **1–2★** review
- Mentions **refund**, **lawsuit**, **injury**, **discrimination**, **harassment**, **HIPAA/PHI**, **insurance**, **scam/fraud**, or staff names
- Suspected competitor/fake review

Escalation output:
- “Recommended response” + “internal next step” + required approver
- Track status (Open → Awaiting Client → Posted → Resolved)

## Approval policy (human-in-the-loop)
- **Auto-approve**: 4–5★ without red-flag keywords; we post immediately.
- **Approval required**: 1–3★ OR any flagged keyword. Client approves via email/DM with “Approve / Edit” instructions.

## Posting method (to avoid API dependency)
- **Preferred:** client adds us as **Manager** on Google Business Profile.
- **Yelp:** client provides Yelp Business Owner access OR we run **client-post workflow** (we draft; client posts).

**Explicit exclusions (safety/compliance):**
- No review gating or incentivization promises
- No deletion promises
- No impersonation; replies are posted as the business through authorized access

## Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
Minimum metrics:
- New reviews (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating + trend vs prior week
- Response rate (% responded)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)
- Escalations opened/closed
- Top 3 themes (service, staff, wait time, value)
