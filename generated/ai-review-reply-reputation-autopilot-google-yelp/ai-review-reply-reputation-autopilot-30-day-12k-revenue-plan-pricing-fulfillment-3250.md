4) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO ENGINEERING)
Review sources:
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp Business

Access & posting method (no API dependency):
- Preferred: client grants us manager access on GBP; Yelp Business Owner access where possible.
- If Yelp access is not granted in week 1: we provide “draft replies” in a daily email; client posts (client-post workflow).

Daily workflow (human-in-the-loop):
1) Capture: check new reviews 1–2x/day via platform notifications or manual review.
2) Draft: generate response using approved brand voice + CTA library.
3) QA: check policy (no incentives, no HIPAA/medical claims, no admissions of liability), tone, personalization.
4) Approval rules:
   - Auto-approve and post 4–5★ unless flagged.
   - Always require approval for 1–3★, legal threats, medical terms, discrimination claims, refund requests, or staff names.
5) Post:
   - Post natively via GBP/Yelp access OR send client a paste-ready response.
6) Escalate:
   - Create escalation ticket with context, recommended next step, and deadline.
7) Report weekly:
   - Email KPI snapshot + attach/share sheet.

Response SLAs:
- Growth: 24 business hours
- Pro: 12 business hours

Escalation rules (examples):
- 1–2★: escalate within 4 business hours with recommended resolution options.
- 3★: respond normally but flag for service recovery opportunity.
- Mentions of “refund”, “lawsuit”, “injury”, “scam”, “fraud”: escalate immediately; do not argue publicly.

Explicitly excluded (week 1):
- Review gating, incentivizing reviews, astroturfing
- Promises to remove reviews
- Filing disputes without client authorization
