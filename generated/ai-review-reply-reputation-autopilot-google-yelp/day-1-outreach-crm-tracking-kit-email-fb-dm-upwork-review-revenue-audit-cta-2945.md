2) LEAD BUILD (200 prospects/day) — ZERO COST
Goal Day 1: 150 local owners + 50 agencies.

- Find owner/manager email: website contact page, staff page, or “info@/hello@/appointments@”. If none, use the contact form (log as ‘no email’ and switch to FB DM).
Agencies (50):
- Use Clutch directory + Google “local SEO agency multi-location”, “reputation management agency”, “white label local SEO”. Capture: agency name, founder name, email, website.
Dedupe rule: unique by email; if no email, unique by website + phone.

CSV columns for import:
First Name, Last Name, Email, Company, Website, Phone, City, Segment, Niche, Channel, Source URL, Notes
