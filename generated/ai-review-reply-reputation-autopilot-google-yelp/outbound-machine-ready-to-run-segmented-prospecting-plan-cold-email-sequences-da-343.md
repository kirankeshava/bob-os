# 1) Segmented Prospecting Plan (What to pull, who to prioritize, and how to message)

## Target verticals (high review velocity + high LTV)
1) **Dental practices** (cosmetic + general)
2) **Med spas / aesthetics clinics**
3) **HVAC + Plumbers** (home services)

## Core problem triggers (use these to decide who to contact first)
- **Not responding**: owner/manager responses in last 10 reviews ≤ 2 (≤20%) OR clearly no recent replies.
- **Low rating**: Google rating < **4.2** (or any recent 1–2 star review within 30 days).
- **High volume**: review count ≥ **200** OR last review within **14 days**.

## Priority scoring (simple routing)
- **Priority A**: (Not responding AND High volume) OR (Low rating AND High volume)
- **Priority B**: Not responding OR Low rating
- **Priority C**: High volume only

## Personalization requirement (what to collect per lead)
For each prospect, capture:
- **Latest review excerpt** (1–2 sentences) OR paraphrase
- **Response gap** (e.g., “no owner reply on last 6 reviews”)
- **Operational hook** (e.g., “new reviews weekly; hard to keep up”)

# 3) Daily Sending Ops + CRM Stages (14-day ramp)

## 3.1 CRM stages (minimum viable pipeline)
1) **Prospect (Ready)**: lead has email + personalization snippet + segment + priority.
2) **Sent**: email 1 sent.
3) **Replied**: any reply.
4) **Qualified**: confirmed decision-maker + has Google/Yelp activity + pain confirmed.
5) **Demo Booked**: meeting scheduled.
6) **Trial / Pilot**: sample replies delivered or pilot started.
7) **Paid**: converted.
8) **Lost**: not a fit / no need / bad timing.

## 3.2 Daily activity targets (starting)
- **New prospects prepared**: 25–50/day (with snippets)
- **New emails sent**: start 20/day/inbox → ramp to 50–100/day total (depending on inbox count)
- **Follow-ups**: 1:1 with sequence schedule (don’t exceed 40–50% of daily volume initially)
- **Manual touch points**: 5–10/day (calls or Google Business “suggest edit” is NOT recommended; keep to email/LinkedIn)

## 3.3 14-day ramp (per inbox)
- Days 1–2: 10/day
- Days 3–4: 15/day
- Days 5–6: 20/day
- Days 7–8: 25/day
- Days 9–10: 30/day
- Days 11–12: 35/day
- Days 13–14: 40/day
After day 14: increase slowly to 50/day if bounce/complaint rates are healthy.

## 3.4 List QA rules (prevent garbage leads)
Reject leads if:
- No website AND no clear phone (harder to verify legitimacy)
- Obvious franchise directory listings (unless intentionally targeted)
- Category mismatch (e.g., “cosmetic dentist” query returning orthodontists; keep if still dental and local)
- No recent reviews in 12 months (lower urgency)

## 3.5 Deliverability safety thresholds
- Hard bounce rate: **>3%** stop & clean list
- Spam complaint rate: **>0.1%** stop & review copy/targeting
- Reply rate target (early): **2–5%**; optimize subject lines + snippet quality

## 3.6 Reply handling SLA (simple)
- Positive interest: respond within **2 hours** during business day
- Negative sentiment: acknowledge + offer to stop; do not argue
- “Send samples”: deliver within **24 hours** using their real review excerpts (paraphrase if sensitive)

## 3.7 Weekly KPI tracking (outbound)
- Sent, delivered, bounced
- Opens (optional), replies, positive replies, meetings booked
- Segment performance (Not responding vs Low rating vs High volume)
- Vertical performance (Dental vs Med spa vs HVAC/Plumbing)
