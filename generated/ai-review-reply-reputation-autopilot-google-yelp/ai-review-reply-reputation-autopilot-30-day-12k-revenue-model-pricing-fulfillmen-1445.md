4) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (Week-1 deliverable, no engineering required)

Sources supported:

- Yelp Business page

Posting method (API constraints handled):
- Default: client grants Manager access to GBP; for Yelp, client adds us as user in Yelp Business account.
- Fallback (no Yelp access): we draft responses and client posts using copy/paste. This keeps offer deliverable without API approval.
- Explicit: we do NOT promise API-based auto-posting in week 1.

SLA:
- Growth: respond within 24 business hours
- Pro: respond within 12 business hours

Brand-safety QA (human-in-the-loop):
- Every response drafted using brand voice rules + banned phrases list.
- Auto-approve rules: 4–5★ reviews that do not contain sensitive keywords or specific claims.
- Required approval rules (client must approve before posting): 1–3★ reviews; legal/medical/financial claims; refund/compensation requests; harassment/defamation; HIPAA/PII risk.

Escalation rules:
Escalate immediately if:
- 1★ or 2★ rating
- Mentions: “lawsuit”, “attorney”, “scam”, “fraud”, “BBB”, “chargeback”, “HIPAA”, “license”, “police”, “discrimination”, “refund”, “unsafe”, “injury”, “infection”, “negligence”
- Named staff accusation or request for compensation
Escalation output: a short incident summary + recommended response options + internal next step.

Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
- New reviews (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating and trend
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)

3) Provide 2 response options + recommended internal resolution step

3) List 3 recommended actions
