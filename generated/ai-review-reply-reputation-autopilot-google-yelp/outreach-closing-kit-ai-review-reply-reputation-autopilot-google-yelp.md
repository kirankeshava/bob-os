8) OBJECTION HANDLING (copy blocks)
Objection: “We can just reply ourselves.”
“Totally—if it’s happening consistently. Most teams are great for the first week, then it slips. The value here is consistency + speed + saying the right thing every time, plus escalation rules so no one accidentally makes a situation worse.”

Objection: “I’m worried about AI sounding fake.”
“Valid. We don’t post generic replies. We set your tone once, then each reply references specifics from the review. And for negatives, we can require approval before anything is posted.”

Objection: “What if a review is unfair?”
“Our approach is de-escalation and professionalism. We’ll acknowledge, invite offline resolution, and avoid policy-violating claims. If you want, we tag those for you to consider disputing through platform channels.”

Objection: “We need compliance / HIPAA / sensitive info.”
“We default to privacy-safe language and never confirm customer identity or services. We can also lock a restricted phrase list and require approval for anything 3★ or below.”

Objection: “Price.”
“The cost of one lost appointment or service call often exceeds a month of replies. Our system protects conversion by improving response rate and response time—plus the weekly report shows what’s driving negatives so you can fix root causes.”
