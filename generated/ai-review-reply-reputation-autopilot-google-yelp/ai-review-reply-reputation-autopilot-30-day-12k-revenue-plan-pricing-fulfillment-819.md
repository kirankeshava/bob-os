FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO API DEPENDENCY)
Review sources supported:
- Google Business Profile reviews (GBP)
- Yelp reviews

Posting method (choose one per platform):
1) Preferred: Client grants access
- Google: add us as Manager to GBP (best)
- Yelp: add Business Owner/Manager access where possible
2) Fallback: “Client-post workflow” (still deliverable)
- We draft responses and send copy-paste ready replies daily; client posts.

SLA & operating cadence:
- Check reviews 1–2x daily (manual + alert-based)
- Draft response same day; publish within SLA (12h Pro / 24h Growth)
- Weekly KPI report every Monday (or client’s chosen weekday)

Brand-safety rules (human-in-the-loop):
- Auto-approve: 4–5★ reviews unless flagged keywords/claims
- Requires approval before posting: 1–3★ reviews, legal/medical/financial claims, discrimination/hate, refund requests, threats, or any mention of lawsuit, injury, HIPAA, “fraud,” “scam,” etc.
- Always avoid: incentives for reviews, review gating, requesting removal in exchange for compensation, disclosure of private customer info.

Escalation rules:
Trigger an escalation ticket if:
- Review is 1–2★
- Review mentions safety, fraud, discrimination, legal threat
- Reviewer requests refund/chargeback
- Reviewer claims service not provided or identity mismatch
Escalation ticket includes: review link, proposed response, recommended offline resolution steps, owner/manager to contact, and deadline. Track status (Open → In progress → Resolved).

Weekly KPI report (one-page):
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating trend (start vs end of week)
- Response rate (% responded)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews (1–3★) count
- Escalations opened/resolved
- Top 3 themes (pricing, wait time, staff, quality, etc.)
- Next-week actions (2–3 bullets)
