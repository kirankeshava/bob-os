## 3) Universal Scope + SLA Terms (paste into landing page, Upwork, Fiverr, proposals)

**Scope & Service Terms (standard):**
- Service covers drafting (and optionally posting) responses to *new* reviews on Google Business Profile and/or Yelp for the subscribed location(s).
- Review-response volume is capped by your plan. If you exceed the cap, we will (a) prioritize the most recent reviews and/or (b) quote an overage add-on.
- We do not remove reviews, suppress reviews, or offer incentives for positive reviews. We follow Google/Yelp policies.
- We are not responsible for platform outages, lost reviews, or review moderation decisions by Google/Yelp.

**Negative review handling:**
- Any review rated 1–2 stars (or containing sensitive topics: safety, discrimination, legal threats, refunds/chargebacks, medical claims, etc.) is escalated.
- If “approval required” is selected, we send a draft for approval before posting.

**SLA definitions:**
- Same-day = drafted within business hours on the same day received (Mon–Fri), excluding holidays.
- Next business day = drafted by end of the next business day.
- 48 hours = drafted within 48 hours (business days).
- Weekend coverage is optional and must be selected.

**What’s not included (unless added):**
- Direct messaging with reviewers, PR crisis management, legal responses, dispute filing, or review removal requests.
- Review generation campaigns (ask flows, SMS/email). We can refer or partner if needed.

**Required inputs:**
- Accurate business policy details (refunds/returns/appointments) and brand voice preferences.
- Access via platform user invite (preferred) or draft-only workflow.
