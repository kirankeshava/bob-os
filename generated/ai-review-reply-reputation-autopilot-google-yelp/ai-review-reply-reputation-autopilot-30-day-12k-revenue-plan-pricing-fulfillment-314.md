3) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (week-1 deliverable) + CONSTRAINTS

Supported sources:
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp

Key constraint (API):
- We do NOT require Google/Yelp APIs to start.
- Default method is human-in-the-loop posting using account access or client-post workflow.

Access & posting policy (week 1):
A) Google Business Profile (preferred):
- Client adds us as Manager on the GBP listing.
- We draft + post replies directly in GBP.
Fallback:
- If access is delayed, we send “ready-to-post” replies daily by email; client posts.

B) Yelp:
- If Yelp Business Owner access is granted, we draft + post.
- If not granted, we run “client-post mode”: we draft replies and client posts (copy/paste). This keeps delivery unblocked while reducing onboarding friction.

Response SLA:
- Growth/Agency: reply drafted within 24 business hours of detection.
- Pro: within 12 business hours.

Escalation rules (automatic escalation triggers):
- Any 1–2★ review
- Any 3★ review with keywords: “refund”, “scam”, “lawsuit”, “HIPAA”, “injury”, “fraud”, “discrimination”, “harassment”, “police”, “attorney”, “chargeback”
- Any review mentioning staff names + allegations
- Any request for private data or medical/legal advice

Approval rules (brand safety):
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-approve unless flagged by keywords above.
- 1–3★ reviews: require client approval OR we post a neutral “take-it-offline” response per pre-approved templates.

Brand voice & compliance guardrails:
- No admissions of liability.
- No medical/legal promises.
- No incentives for reviews; no review gating.
- No sharing customer private details.

Weekly KPI report (sent every Monday):
- New reviews (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating trend (7/30-day)
- Response rate
- Median response time
- Negative review count (1–2★)
- Escalations opened vs resolved
- Top 3 themes + suggested operational fixes
