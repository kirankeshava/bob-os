3) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE; NO API REQUIRED)
Review sources:
- Google Business Profile reviews (client provides Manager access preferred)
- Yelp reviews (Yelp Business Owner access preferred)

Posting method (deliverable without engineering):
- Default: We post directly when granted proper Manager/Owner access.
- Fallback (allowed): “Client-Post Workflow” — we draft replies and send copy/paste text + link to the review; client posts within SLA.

Response SLA clock starts when review is observed (daily monitoring) or client forwards alert.
Monitoring method (week 1):
- Manual checks 1–2×/day OR review alert emails forwarded to agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to.

Escalation rules (always human-in-loop):
Escalate (never auto-post) if any of the following:
- 1–3★ review
- Mentions injury, safety incident, discrimination, illegal activity, threats, chargebacks, refunds, HIPAA/medical specifics, legal action
- Profanity/harassment, doxxing, staff name accusations
Escalation output:
- Create an “Incident Note” (summary + recommended reply options + questions for owner)
- Notify designated contact within 2 business hours (Pro) / 6 business hours (Growth)

Auto-approval rules (safe automation without API):
- 4–5★ reviews can be auto-approved for posting IF they contain no restricted keywords and match brand voice rules.
- All others require explicit approval.

Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
- New reviews (count)
- Average rating and 7/30-day trend
- Response rate %
- Median response time
- Negative review count (1–3★)
- Escalations opened/closed
- Top themes (service, wait time, price, staff, cleanliness, outcomes)
- “Next week actions” (1–3 bullets)

Explicit exclusions (to stay compliant + deliverable week 1):
- No review gating (filtering who gets asked)
- No incentivizing reviews
- No deleting reviews (we can advise on platform dispute steps, but client must submit)
