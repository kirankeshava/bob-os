Daily (Mon–Sat)

   - Yelp: check Yelp Business notifications; if client-post workflow, copy review content into tracker.
2) Draft response
   - Use Brand Voice Pack rules.
   - Keep concise, non-defensive, avoid admitting fault unless client policy allows.
3) QA pass (brand + policy)
   - Check banned phrases, discounts, liability, HIPAA/PII.
4) Approve/post
   - Google: post directly via manager access.
   - Yelp: send “ready-to-post” snippet to client (email) unless access granted.
5) Escalations
   - If triggered, open ticket, ask for facts, propose resolution language, and hold posting until approval.

Weekly (every Monday)
1) KPI report generation
2) Themes + suggested operational fixes
3) Flag risks: rating drop, response time breaches, repeated complaint categories
