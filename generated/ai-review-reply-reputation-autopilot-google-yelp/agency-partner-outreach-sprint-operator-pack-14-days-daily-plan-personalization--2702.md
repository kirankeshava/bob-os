## Partner Call Script (25 minutes)
### 0–2 min: Set agenda
- “Goal: understand your client mix + current review workflow, then I’ll show how the white-label fulfillment works. If it’s a fit, we’ll pick 1–2 locations for a free 7-day pilot.”

### 2–8 min: Discovery (ask, then mirror)
1) “How many locations do you actively manage across clients?”
2) “Who replies to Google/Yelp reviews today—your team, the owner, or nobody?”
3) “What breaks first when volume increases?”
4) “Any verticals where reviews impact revenue immediately (dental, med spa, home services, restaurants)?”

### 8–14 min: Pitch (tie to their pain)
- “We act as your behind-the-scenes review response desk. Within 24 hours we draft and (optionally) post responses, keep voice consistent, flag negatives for escalation, and send a weekly KPI report you can forward to clients under your branding.”
- “You keep the client relationship; we keep the operation tight.”

### 14–18 min: Pilot framing (reduce risk)
- “Let’s run a free 7-day pilot for 1–2 locations. You’ll see tone quality, turnaround time, and reporting. If it works, we roll into partner pricing.”

### 18–22 min: Pricing + margin
- “Partner base is typically **$199/location/month** (volume tiers available). You can resell at $299–$499 depending on bundle.”
- Optional: “We can also do rev share if you prefer (e.g., you set retail, we take a fixed fulfillment rate).”

### 22–25 min: Close + next steps
- “Which 1–2 locations should we pilot? What’s the best start date?”
- Confirm operational owner + access method (GBP/Yelp or ‘draft-only’ workflow).
- “I’ll email the onboarding checklist to {email}. Reply to agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to if anything stalls.”

## Objection Handling Map
1) **‘We already reply to reviews.’**
- “Makes sense. Is it consistent within 24h across all locations? We slot in as overflow/QA with an SLA so your team stays focused on higher-margin work.”

2) **‘Concerned about brand voice / compliance.’**
- “We start with a brand voice sheet + do-not-say list. Negatives trigger escalation. You can choose draft-only until comfortable.”

3) **‘Our clients are small / low volume.’**
- “Even low volume benefits because one negative unanswered review can cost leads. Also, this is a clean add-on for retainers.”

4) **‘Price is high.’**
- “Understood—this replaces manual time and reduces churn risk. Most partners resell at $299–$499/location/month bundled with GBP/SEO, so margins stay strong.”
