Human-in-the-loop workflow (brand-safe):
1) Onboarding intake: brand voice, offers, banned phrases, compliance notes, escalation contacts.
2) Daily review check: manual check + alerts (email notifications from platforms where available).
3) Draft response: generate 2–3 options, select best, ensure policy-safe.
4) QA gate: flag any sensitive content (health, legal, discrimination, threats, refunds, chargebacks, personal data).
5) Approval rules:
   - Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged keywords.
   - Require approval for 1–3★, refund/legal requests, or anything mentioning staff names/PHI.
6) Posting:
   - If access: post directly on GBP/Yelp.
   - If no access: send “Ready-to-post” snippet to client (email) + track until posted.
7) Escalations:
   - Open escalation ticket for 1–2★ or any “refund/threat/legal/safety” review.
   - Notify client same day; request facts; propose public reply + private resolution steps.
8) Weekly KPI report (every Monday): review count, average rating trend, response rate, median response time, negative reviews, escalations opened/closed, top themes, suggested operational fix.
