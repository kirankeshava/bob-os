3) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (Week-1 deliverable) + constraints

Review sources supported immediately:
- Google Business Profile (GBP) reviews
- Yelp reviews

Key constraint: Do NOT depend on Google/Yelp APIs in Week 1.
Delivery method is human-in-the-loop:
- Preferred: client grants manager/admin access to GBP and Yelp Business Owner.
- Fallback (Yelp or GBP): “Client-post workflow” — we draft replies daily, send in a queue (email or shared doc). Client posts verbatim. This is explicitly allowed in the offer until direct posting is available.

SLA definitions:
- “Response SLA” means draft completed + ready to post (or posted if access is granted).
- Business days unless client pays for weekend coverage (optional custom).

Brand safety rules (hard rules):
- No review gating or incentivizing reviews.
- No asking customers to remove reviews.
- No medical/legal promises, no discriminatory language, no confidential PHI.
- If review implies safety issue, fraud, chargeback, legal threat, or regulated keywords: escalate.

Escalation rules:
Escalate (ticket created + client notified) when:
- 1–2★ reviews automatically
- 3★ if mentions refunds, staff misconduct, safety, discrimination, legal threats, HIPAA/PHI
- Any review referencing “lawsuit”, “BBB”, “attorney”, “police”, “fraud”, “scam”, “chargeback”
Escalation handling:
- Provide 2 draft options: (1) public reply, (2) private outreach script (call/text/email)
- Track status: New → Client Contacted → Resolved/Unresolved

Posting workflow (SOP outline):
1) Onboarding intake (15–30 min):
   - Collect business name(s), location(s), hours, service list, brand voice examples
   - Banned phrases, offer rules, and escalation contacts
   - Access: GBP manager invite; Yelp Business Owner access OR confirm client-post fallback
2) Daily review capture:
   - Mon–Fri check reviews (or use existing email alerts forwarded to agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to)
   - Log new reviews in tracker (date, stars, author, text, platform, location)
3) Drafting:
   - Generate 1 brand-safe response per review
   - Personalize with service/location cues; avoid incentives; keep within platform norms
4) QA:
   - Human QA pass: policy + tone + no promises
   - Flag any escalation
5) Approval rules:
   - 4–5★: auto-approve unless contains sensitive keywords
   - 1–3★: require client approval unless client opts into “pre-approved negative templates”
6) Posting:
   - If access: post directly in GBP/Yelp
   - If client-post: send “Ready-to-post queue” daily by email
7) Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
   - New reviews, average rating trend, response rate, median response time
   - Negative review count, escalations opened/closed, top themes
   - Next-week recommendations (1–3 bullets)
