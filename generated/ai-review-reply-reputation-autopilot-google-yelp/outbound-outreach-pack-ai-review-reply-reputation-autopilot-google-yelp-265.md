5) Call Opener + Discovery Script (15 minutes)
========================
Goal: close DFY Autopilot (Growth/Pro) with setup + first month OR book a second call with decision maker and access requirements.

OPEN (30 seconds)
“Thanks for the time. In 15 minutes I want to (1) understand how reviews currently get handled, (2) show how the Autopilot works in week 1 without new software, and (3) if it’s a fit, outline a simple onboarding to start replying within 24 hours. Sound good?”

CONTEXT QUESTIONS (5 minutes)
1) “How many locations is this for?”
2) “Where do you get most reviews—Google, Yelp, both?”
3) “Who replies today, and how quickly?”
4) “Any brand voice rules? (formal vs friendly, emojis/no emojis, promos allowed?)”
5) “What’s the biggest risk for you: slow responses, wrong tone, or negative reviews?”

PAIN + IMPACT (3 minutes)
1) “Have you had a 1–3★ review in the last 60 days that you wish you handled differently?”
2) “Do you track response rate/response time today?”
3) “When a negative review hits, who needs to be notified and how fast?”

SOLUTION (4 minutes)
“Here’s the workflow:
- We monitor new reviews daily (Google + Yelp).
- We draft brand-safe replies aligned to your voice.
- 4–5★: we can auto-approve unless flagged.
- 1–3★: we escalate immediately with a suggested resolution reply and questions for your team.
- Weekly KPI report: new reviews, rating trend, response rate, median response time, themes, and what we changed.”

ACCESS / CONSTRAINTS (1 minute)
“To post directly, we need Google Business Profile Manager access. For Yelp, either Business Owner access or we can draft and send you copy to paste until access is sorted.”

CLOSE (2 minutes)
“I recommend Growth for 1 location: $500 setup + $1,250/month. Setup covers voice guidelines, escalation rules, and connecting sources so we can start replying within 24 hours. Want to start with that so you’re covered this week?”

If objection:
- “Too expensive” → “If we prevent even 1–2 lost bookings a month, it usually pays for itself. Also, we can start month-to-month; cancel anytime.”
- “We can do it ourselves” → “Totally—this is for consistency + speed. The KPI report and escalation handling are usually what teams don’t have time to do.”
- “Need to think” → “What would you need to see to feel confident? I can send 3 drafted replies and the escalation policy today.”
