7) **Verify access**
   - GBP: confirm Manager access (or equivalent).
   - Yelp: confirm admin/manager access or agreed workflow (drafts sent for posting).

8) **Pull last 30–90 days review sample**
   - Identify categories: praise themes, service issues, staff mentions, wait times, billing/price, cleanliness.

9) **Draft 10 sample replies**
   - 5 positive, 5 negative.
   - Include 2 “hard negatives” (refund demand, no-show claims, rude staff) with de-escalation.

10) **Send Sample Approval Pack**
   - Ask for: “Approve voice” or 1–2 edits.
   - Confirm escalation triggers.

### Day 2 (Within 48 hours)
11) **Go-live** (depending on approval mode)
   - If autopost approved: begin posting on new reviews + clear backlog per agreed SLA.
   - If manual approval: send daily draft queue email at a fixed time.

12) **Confirm first-week success criteria (in writing)**
   - Response time target (e.g., <24 hours for new reviews)
   - Backlog clearance plan (e.g., last 30 days within week 1)
   - Escalation response time (e.g., <2 business hours)

13) **Schedule weekly KPI report**
   - Set recurring email: every Monday 9am local time.
   - Include: new reviews, avg rating, response rate, median response time, negatives escalated/resolved, top themes.

### First-week success criteria (what “good” looks like)
- 90%+ of new reviews receive a response within the agreed SLA.
- All 1–2 star reviews are escalated same day with a recommended action.
- Backlog plan executed (clear last 30/60/90 days per scope).
- Weekly KPI email delivered on schedule.
