7) “PROOF & SAFETY PACK” (paste into proposal or send as standalone)
Title: Brand-Safe AI Review Reply Workflow (Google + Yelp)

What we do
- Draft on-brand replies to every review (positive + neutral + negative)
- Post to Google Business Profile and/or Yelp (based on access)
- Escalate negatives to your team with a clear next-step recommendation
- Report weekly KPIs (response rate, avg rating trend, topics, unresolved issues)

Why it’s safe (how we prevent ‘AI weirdness’)
1) Brand Voice Profile
   - You approve tone (warm/professional/concise), preferred phrases, and “never say” list.
2) Guardrails
   - No medical/legal/financial claims.
   - No admissions of fault.
   - No sharing private data.
   - No arguing with reviewers.
3) Escalation Triggers (auto)
   - Any 1–2 star review
   - Allegations: fraud, injury, discrimination, harassment
   - Requests for refunds/chargebacks
   - Mentions of staff names + conflict
   - Threats of lawsuit or regulator complaint
4) Approval Modes (you choose)
   - Manual approval: you approve every reply before posting.
   - Post-approval audit: we post, you can request edits; negatives always escalate.
   - Auto-approve: we post immediately for speed; negatives still escalate.
5) Audit Log
   - We keep a record of drafted response + posted response + timestamps.

Sample reply (positive)
“Thanks so much for taking the time to share this, {{Name}}. We’re really glad you had a great experience with our team. If you ever need anything else, we’re here to help.”

Sample reply (negative with de-escalation)
“Hi {{Name}}—thank you for the feedback, and we’re sorry to hear this. We’d like to look into what happened and make it right. Please contact {{ContactMethod}} so we can get the details and follow up directly.”

Weekly KPI email snapshot
- New reviews this week (Google/Yelp)
- Response rate (7-day / 30-day)
- Avg rating trend
- Negative reviews escalated + status
- Top 3 themes (what customers mention most)
- Suggested operational fix (1 action)
