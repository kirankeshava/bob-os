10) OBJECTION HANDLING (quick responses)
Objection: “We already reply sometimes.”
Response: “Totally—this is for consistency and speed. The difference is you don’t have to remember, and negatives get handled with a clear approval rule so nothing goes out that you wouldn’t stand behind.”

Objection: “I don’t want AI writing publicly.”
Response: “Understood. Many clients start with draft-only so you approve every reply. The ‘autopilot’ part is speed + consistency, not taking control away.”

Objection: “We’re worried about compliance.”
Response: “We keep replies brand-safe, avoid sensitive details, and we don’t do anything against platform policies (no gating/incentivizing). For regulated niches, we set specific do-not-say rules and escalation triggers.”

Objection: “Price.”
Response: “Review response is one of the few actions that touches conversion directly. If we prevent even a couple of missed bookings a month by staying responsive and defusing negatives, it typically pays for itself.”
