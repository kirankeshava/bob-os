Title: White-Label Review Reply & Reputation Autopilot — Partner Program

1) Who this is for
Agencies managing local SEO, paid media, web, or listings for businesses where reviews influence conversion rate and map-pack performance: dentists, med spas, home services, hospitality, auto, restaurants, multi-location retail.

2) The problem we solve
Most local businesses (and many agencies) under-respond to reviews because it’s time-consuming and risky:
- Inconsistent tone across locations
- Slow response times to negative reviews
- Owners write emotional replies that damage brand trust
- No reporting cadence to show value

3) What we deliver (white-label)
A) Review response drafting (Google Business Profile + Yelp)
- Brand-safe, policy-aware replies
- Matches your client’s voice guidelines
- 24-hour turnaround target

B) Posting options
- Draft-only (agency/client posts)
- Draft + post (when access is granted)

C) Negative review escalation (1–3★)
- Tag + route to the right person
- Suggested remediation language
- “Do/Don’t” guidance (no HIPAA/PHI, no admissions of fault, etc.)

D) Weekly reputation KPI report (white-labeled)
- New reviews count (by platform)
- Avg rating change
- Response rate + median response time
- Negative review count + escalation outcomes
- Top themes (service, staff, wait time, pricing, etc.)
- Suggested operational fixes + review-generation prompts

4) Service Level Agreement (SLA)
- Response draft turnaround: within 24 business hours (standard)
- Priority queue option (future): within 4 business hours (not offered during free week)
- Escalations: flagged same day when detected
- Quality standard: brand-safe, non-defensive, compliant with platform policies

5) Pricing (partner)
Partner base: $199/location/month (drafts + escalation + weekly KPI report)
Options:
- Draft-only: included
- Draft + post: included when access provided
- Multi-location tiers: negotiated for 20+ locations

Week 1 policy (launch): 7-day free pilot for 1–2 locations to prove speed + quality.

6) Revenue share option (alternative model)
Two ways to structure it:
- Resell model: you charge your client $299–$499/location/month, you pay us $199.
- Rev-share model: we invoice you a wholesale rate; you keep a % margin per location.

7) Onboarding SOP (simple)
Step 1: Pick 1–2 pilot locations
- Business name, address, website
- Primary platform(s): Google, Yelp

Step 2: Provide voice + policy preferences
- Tone (friendly/professional/brief)
- Forbidden phrases (e.g., refunds, legal language)
- Escalation contacts (name/email) and hours

Step 3: Access (one of)
- Draft-only workflow: we send replies via email weekly/daily
- Post-enabled workflow: add us as manager where applicable (least-privilege)

Step 4: Go live
- We begin drafting within 24h of new reviews appearing
- Weekly KPI report delivered every Monday
