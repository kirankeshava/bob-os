## 2) Segmentation + Priority Routing
Collect: rating, review_count, last_review_date, response_rate_proxy (owner responses in last 10 reviews / 10).

**Segments**
- **Not Responding:** response_rate_proxy ≤ 0.2 OR 0 replies in last 10
- **Low Rating:** rating < 4.2
- **High Volume:** review_count ≥ 200 OR last_review_date within 14 days

**Priority tiers**
- **Priority A:** (Not Responding AND High Volume) OR (Low Rating AND High Volume)
- **Priority B:** Not Responding OR Low Rating
- **Priority C:** High Volume only

**Template routing**
- Not Responding → “response gap + speed + approval workflow”
- Low Rating → “recovery + escalation + brand-safe de-escalation”
- High Volume → “ops throughput + consistency + weekly KPIs”

## 3) Personalization Tokens (safe + fast)
Use 1–2 tokens max per email:

# 7) Daily Sending Ops (Targets + 14-day Ramp)
**Core rule:** keep deliverability perfect > volume.

**Daily activity targets (once warmed):**
- 50–100 new emails/day (split across inboxes), 30–60 follow-ups/day
- 10 LinkedIn/IG/FB owner DMs/day (optional)
- Reply SLA: same day (<= 12 business hours)

**14-day ramp (per inbox):**
- Days 1–3: 10–15/day
- Days 4–7: 15–25/day
- Days 8–14: 25–40/day
(Scale by adding inboxes rather than spiking one inbox.)

**List QA rules:**
- Sample 20 rows per 200 leads: verify category match, GBP link, rating/reviews accuracy, website works.
- Skip: franchise corporate-only contacts, businesses with no website + no reachable email, irrelevant categories.

**Bounce/complaint thresholds:**
- If hard bounces > 3% on a batch: stop, clean list.
- If spam complaints > 0.1%: pause, adjust copy/sending.
