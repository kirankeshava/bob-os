E) Tracking Sheet Blueprint (Google Sheets)
Create a Sheet with tabs: Leads, Activities, Calls, Deals, Weekly.

Leads tab columns:
LeadID | BusinessName | Niche | City | Website | GoogleLink | YelpLink | ContactName | Email | Phone | Source (Upwork/Fiverr/Email) | Campaign | DateAdded | Status (New/Contacted/Replied/Call Booked/No Fit)

Activities tab columns:
Date | LeadID | Channel | ActivityType (Proposal/Email/DM/Follow-up/Call) | TemplateUsed | Outcome (No reply/Reply/Booked/Not fit) | Notes

Calls tab columns:
LeadID | CallDate | Showed (Y/N) | Pain (rating/volume/negatives) | NextStep | Owner (Bob)

Deals tab columns:
LeadID | Stage | StartDate | TrialEnd | ProposedMonthly | Close (Y/N) | ReasonLost

Weekly tab (metrics):
WeekStart | ProposalsSent | EmailsSent | Replies | CallsBooked | CallsShowed | TrialsStarted | CAC_by_Channel (Week 2+) | Notes

Key formulas:
• Reply rate = Replies / (ProposalsSent + EmailsSent)
• Booked call rate = CallsBooked / Replies
• Show rate = CallsShowed / CallsBooked
7-day cut rule: if a channel has <2 booked calls within 7 days of consistent activity, pause it and reallocate effort.
