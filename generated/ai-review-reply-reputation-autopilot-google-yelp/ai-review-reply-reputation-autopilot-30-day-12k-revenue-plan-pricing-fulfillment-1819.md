# 3) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable; no API dependency)
## Review sources supported
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

## Posting method (deliverable now)
**Default (preferred):**
- Client adds us as **Manager** on GBP.
- Yelp: client provides **Business Owner access**.

**Fallback (always available):** “Draft + Client Post”
- We deliver **ready-to-post responses** daily in a shared doc / email.
- Client (or their staff) posts using their own login.

## Response SLA
- DFY Growth: post (or deliver draft) within **24 business hours**.
- DFY Pro: within **12 business hours**.

## Escalation rules (brand safety)
Always **escalate** (don’t auto-post) if review contains:
- Allegations of illegal activity, discrimination, fraud
- Medical/legal claims, HIPAA/PHI, threats, chargebacks
- Requests for refunds, staff name accusations
- 1–2★ review with detailed complaint (requires custom handling)

Escalation output:
- A suggested public reply + private resolution script
- “Next step” checklist for owner/manager
- Status tracked until resolved/closed

## Human-in-the-loop QA (policy)
- AI drafts every reply.
- Human QA checks for tone, policy compliance, PII, admissions of fault.
- Auto-approve rules (optional): 4–5★ praise-only reviews can be posted without explicit approval unless flagged keywords appear.
