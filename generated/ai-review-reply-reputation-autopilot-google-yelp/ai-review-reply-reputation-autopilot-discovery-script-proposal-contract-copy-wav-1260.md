“Totally fair to be cautious about AI. Our workflow is brand-safety-first:

- Approval modes: (A) manual approval before posting, (B) auto-post for 4–5 star + manual for negatives, (C) auto-post everything after calibration.
- Audit log: what was drafted, what was posted, and why.
So you control risk while getting speed.”

17:00–20:00 — Close (two options) + collect upfront

“Based on what you shared, I recommend [Plan A] if you want a lighter touch, or [Plan B] if you want faster coverage and KPI reporting. Either way, we start with a one-time setup and the first month upfront, then we go live within 48 hours once access is granted. Want me to send the proposal + invoice now and get you scheduled for onboarding?”

If price pushback:

If “we can do it ourselves”:
