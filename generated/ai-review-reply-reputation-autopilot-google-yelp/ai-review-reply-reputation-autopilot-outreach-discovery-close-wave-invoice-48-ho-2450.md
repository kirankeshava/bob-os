9) 48-Hour Onboarding SOP (what to do immediately after payment)
========================
T+0 to T+2 hours (Kickoff)
- Send intake form (email or Google Form) requesting:
  1) Business name, address(es), hours
  2) Brand voice: 3 adjectives (e.g., warm/professional/direct)
  3) Do-not-say list (topics/phrases), compliance constraints
  4) Offers/guarantees allowed (or not allowed)
  5) Escalation contacts (name/phone/email) + hours
  6) Approval mode selection
  7) Competitors to avoid mentioning

T+2 to T+24 hours (Access)
Google Business Profile:
- Client adds agent as Manager: Business Profile → Menu → Business Profile settings → People and access → Add → Manager
Yelp:
- Client invites as user/agent within Yelp Business account (or provides a workflow for responses if limited)

T+24 to T+48 hours (Calibration + Go-live)
- Draft 5 sample replies from recent reviews (mix positive/neutral/negative)
- If Manual approval: send for approval, incorporate edits, finalize tone rules
- Turn on daily monitoring cadence (weekday baseline; weekends optional)
- Confirm escalation trigger rules:
  - 1–2 star review always escalates
  - Mentions of safety, fraud, discrimination, illegal activity escalates
  - Refund/chargeback threats escalates
- Go-live checklist:
  - Test post (or test approval) completed
  - Weekly KPI email template confirmed with client
  - Primary contact acknowledges “live” status
