   - SEV3: Mild complaint (3★) requiring clarification

   - Apologize + invite offline contact (default)
   - Clarify policy (no blame)
   - Request details offline + confirm intent to resolve
   - Decline specifics due to privacy; request offline contact
10) Proposed response draft (generated by Autopilot + QA):
11) Client approval needed? Yes/No
12) Escalation contact notified? Yes/No (timestamp)
13) Outcome after 7 days:
   - Customer contacted us (Y/N)
   - Resolved (Y/N)
   - Review updated/removed (Y/N/Unknown)

SLA (Week-1, human-in-the-loop):
- 4–5★: draft within 24 hours (often same-day).
- 3★: draft within 24 hours; approval recommended.
- 1–2★ or flagged keywords: draft within 12 hours; approval required unless client opts into “autopost negative with safe template.”
- SEV1: notify escalation contact within 2 hours during business hours; otherwise by next morning.
