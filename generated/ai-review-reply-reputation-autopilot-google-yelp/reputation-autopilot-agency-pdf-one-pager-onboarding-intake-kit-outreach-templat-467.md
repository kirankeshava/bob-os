## 4) TWO MINI CASE-STUDY MODULES (ANONYMIZED)

### Case Study #1 — Multi-location Home Services (Google)
**Problem:** Reviews were coming in daily, but replies were inconsistent (often 10–20 days late). Several 1–2 star reviews had no response, creating a “they don’t care” impression in Maps.

**What we did:**
- Implemented daily monitoring + a brand voice playbook (calm, accountable, no defensiveness)
- Drafted replies for every review and escalated 1–2 stars to the ops manager within minutes
- Standardized a short resolution CTA: “Please call/text us so we can make this right”

**Results (first 30 days):**
- Avg response time improved from **~12 days → <24 hours**
- Negative reviews responded to within **same business day** (SLA)
- Team reported fewer repeat complaints because issues were routed to the right person immediately

**Proof placeholder:** screenshot of GBP “responses” timestamps + weekly KPI email.

### Case Study #2 — Aesthetics / Med Spa (Google + Yelp)
**Problem:** Owners avoided replying to sensitive reviews (pricing, wait times, treatment expectations). Yelp messages were ignored, and Google 3-star reviews were dragging perception.

**What we did:**
- Built compliant reply templates (no treatment claims; no personal info)
- Added approval rule: all 1–3 star replies drafted + sent for approval before posting
- Implemented a weekly KPI report plus “Top 3 operational fixes” insight section

**Results (6 weeks):**
- Response coverage increased from **~30% → ~95%** across platforms
- Several reviewers updated/softened follow-ups after private resolution outreach
- Net rating trend stabilized and began lifting as new positive reviews were met with consistent, warm replies
