# 2) Escalation Ticket + SLA Rules (internal checklist)

## Escalation SLA
- **New review monitoring:** daily (Mon–Fri) minimum; weekends best-effort unless Pro tier.
- **Response draft SLA:**
  - 4–5★: within 24 business hours (Growth) / 12 business hours (Pro)
  - 1–3★: draft within 12 business hours (Growth) / 6 business hours (Pro)
- **Approval SLA (client):** request approval immediately; if no response in 24h, send reminder.

## Auto-approve vs Approval-required
**Auto-approve allowed (default):**
- 4–5★ reviews with no policy flags, no mention of refunds/medical/legal claims.

**Approval required:**
- Any 1–3★ review
- Mentions of: injury, discrimination, illegal activity, HIPAA/medical specifics, legal threats, chargeback, refund demand, harassment, extortion, media.

## Negative Review Response Rules (brand-safe defaults)
- Acknowledge + apologize (without admitting liability).
- Offer a path to resolution offline (phone/email).
- Avoid arguing, blaming, or sharing customer-specific details.
- Never request review removal; never incentivize reviews.
