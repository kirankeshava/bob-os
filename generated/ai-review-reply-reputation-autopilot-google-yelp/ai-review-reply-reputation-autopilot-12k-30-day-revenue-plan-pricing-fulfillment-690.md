(5) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO API REQUIRED)
Review sources:

- Yelp reviews

Response SLA:
- Growth: draft within 24 business hours
- Pro: draft within 12 business hours

Escalation rules (1–3★ or risky content):
Escalate immediately if:
- 1–3★ rating
- Mentions of fraud, safety, discrimination, legal threats, chargebacks
- Medical/legal/privacy-sensitive terms (HIPAA-like risk), or personal data
- Requests for refunds/compensation or employee naming
Escalation deliverable:
- A short internal summary + 2–3 recommended reply options
- “Offline resolution” script (call/text/email) if appropriate
- Status tracking until resolved/closed

Posting method (constraints + workaround):
- Google: require GBP Manager access as preferred. If not granted, client-post workflow is used.
- Yelp: Yelp’s API access is restricted; default week-1 delivery uses either:
  (a) Yelp Business Owner access so we can post, OR
  (b) Client-post workflow: we draft; client copies/pastes and posts.
In all cases we deliver the drafted response text in a daily digest + shared doc, so the service is deliverable without API or custom engineering.

Human-in-the-loop QA (brand safety):
- Auto-approve rules: 4–5★ replies can be posted without waiting for client approval unless flagged by banned phrases/keywords.
- Require approval: all 1–3★ replies and any flagged content.
- Brand guardrails captured in onboarding (tone, offers, banned claims, prohibited phrases).

What’s explicitly excluded (to keep delivery honest and compliant):
- No review gating, no incentives, no fake reviews
- No promises to delete/remove reviews
- No disputing reviews on behalf of client without written authorization
