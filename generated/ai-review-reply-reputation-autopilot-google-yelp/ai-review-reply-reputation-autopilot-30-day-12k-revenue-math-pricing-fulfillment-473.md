# 3) Fulfillment Scope (week-1 deliverable, no API dependency)
## Sources supported (week 1)
- Google Business Profile reviews
- Yelp reviews

## Response SLA
- Default: **24 business hours** (Pro: 12 business hours)
- Business hours defined in onboarding (e.g., Mon–Fri).

## Escalation rules (negative reviews)
Escalate within **4 business hours** if any of the below:
- 1–2★ review
- 3★ with complaint keywords (refund, scam, rude, unsafe, lawsuit, discrimination, fraud)
- Medical/legal claims, HIPAA-sensitive content, or personally identifying info
- Threats, harassment, or extortion

Escalation package includes:
- Summary of complaint + proposed response options (2–3 variants)
- Suggested “take-offline” script + next-step checklist
- Tracking field: “resolved / unresolved / pending customer follow-up”

## Posting method (deliverable without APIs)
We explicitly do **not** require building integrations in week 1.
- **Preferred:** client grants **Google Business Profile Manager access** and/or **Yelp Business Owner/Manager** access so we can post directly.
- **Fallback (always available):** we provide “Ready-to-post” replies in a shared doc/email; client posts within 24h. (Still provides value; slightly weaker SLA control.)

## Human-in-the-loop workflow (brand safety)
- AI drafts responses using client’s brand voice rules.
- Human QA checks:
  1) no admissions of fault that create liability
  2) no incentives/review gating language
  3) no prohibited medical/legal promises
  4) tone matches brand (friendly/professional)
- Auto-approve rule (optional): 4–5★ reviews can be posted without explicit approval unless flagged by keyword rules.

## What’s explicitly excluded (to stay compliant)
- Review gating or incentivizing positive reviews
- Deleting reviews or impersonation
- Filing disputes on behalf of client without written authorization
