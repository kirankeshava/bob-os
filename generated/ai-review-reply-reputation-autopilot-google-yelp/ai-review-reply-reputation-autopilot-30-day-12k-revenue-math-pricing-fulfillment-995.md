Human-in-the-loop rules (brand safety):
Auto-approve (we post without asking) if ALL true:
- 4–5★ review
- No mention of medical/legal/financial claims, refunds, discrimination, safety incidents
- No profanity/harassment
Requires client approval before posting:
- 1–3★ reviews
- Any allegation of harm, fraud, discrimination, threats, chargebacks, HIPAA/medical specifics, legal action
- Any request for compensation/refund specifics

Escalation rules (negative reviews):
- 1–2★: always escalate within 4 business hours of detection (email + optional SMS to owner/manager)
- 3★: escalate if contains specific complaint keywords (wait time, rude staff, wrong order, refund, damage, scam)
- Escalation ticket includes: review text, suggested private resolution step, 2–3 reply options, and “do-not-say” list
