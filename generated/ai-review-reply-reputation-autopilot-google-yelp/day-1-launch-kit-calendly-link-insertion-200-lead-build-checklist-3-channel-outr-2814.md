## 2) Day-1 lead build checklist (200 leads total, free sources)
Target mix: **150 local owners** (50 dentists, 50 med spas, 50 HVAC) + **50 agencies** (marketing agencies managing local clients).

### Fields to capture (CSV/HubSpot)
- Company Name
- Website
- Owner/Contact Name (or “Owner” if unknown)
- Email (decision-maker if possible)
- Phone (optional)
- City + State
- Niche Tag (dentist / medspa / hvac / agency)
- Segment Tag (local-owner / agency)
- Channel Tag (cold-email)
- Personalization Note (1 line: something real you saw)
- Review Signal (e.g., “4.2★, last review unanswered” or “many 1★ mentions of wait time”)

### Free lead sources (use in this order)
**Local owners (Google Maps):**
1) Search: “dentist + [city]”, “med spa + [city]”, “HVAC + [city]”.
2) Open listings with visible review volume (50+ is ideal).
3) Capture: business name, site, phone, rating, recent review dates.
4) Find email (free): website contact page, footer, about page, staff page. If no email, keep for phone/FB DM later.

**Agencies (directories + websites):**
1) Clutch / DesignRush / Google: “local SEO agency multi location”, “reputation management agency”, “white label local SEO”.
2) Capture: agency name, niche focus, leadership contact, email.

### Day-1 segmentation rules
- Prioritize businesses with: (a) unanswered recent reviews, (b) 3.5–4.6 rating range, (c) clear service category (easy personalization).
- For agencies, prioritize: “multi-location”, “local SEO”, “GMB/GBP”, “reputation management” language.

## 4) Reply-to-book “Fast Lane” (convert in 2 messages)
