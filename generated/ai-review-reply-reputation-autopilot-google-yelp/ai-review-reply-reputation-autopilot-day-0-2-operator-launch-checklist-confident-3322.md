============================
1) OPERATOR LAUNCH CHECKLIST (DAY 0–2)
============================
Goal: From “Paid” → “Activated” within 48 hours. Definition of Activated: access granted OR forwarding method confirmed, brand voice captured, escalation contact set, first replies drafted/queued, weekly report scheduled.

Pre-reqs (immediately after close / payment):
A. Create/Update deal in CRM sheet
- Stage: Closed–Won → Paid (once funds received) → Activating
- Fields to fill: Business name, location(s), primary contact, phone, email, timezone, GBP/Yelp links, review volume estimate, SLA needs.

B. Send “Day 0 Kickoff Email” (use your existing template) including:
- Intake form link/questions (brand voice + escalation)
- Access request options (GBP and Yelp)
- What happens in the first week + success criteria
- Legitimacy URL + support inbox for any questions

DAY 0 (within 2 hours of payment)
1. Collect Intake (must-have fields)
- Brand voice: friendly/professional/playful, do/don’t phrases, signature name, bilingual needs.
- Escalation: who gets notified, method (email/SMS), business hours, what counts as “urgent.”
- Compliance: refund policy link, HIPAA/medical restrictions, legal disclaimers, prohibited topics.
- Locations: list, GBP profile links, Yelp business page links.

2. Request/Confirm Platform Access
Option A — Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Request Manager access to the GBP location(s) (preferred).
Option B — Yelp
- Ask for Yelp Business account access OR establish an approved-response workflow if access is restricted.

3. Set SLA and Guardrails (internal)
- Default: respond within 1 business day; negatives within 4 business hours.
- “Never say” list (from intake) + legal/compliance constraints.
- Escalation triggers: profanity, discrimination claims, safety issues, refund threats, legal threats, HIPAA/medical specifics, employee misconduct claims.

DAY 1
4. Brand-Safe Response Calibration (10–20 minutes)
- Draft 6 sample replies (3 positive, 3 negative) using the demo examples as a base.
- Send samples for approval (single email). Ask for “approve / revise tone / add 2 phrases.”

5. Workflow Setup
- Decide operating mode:
  a) Autopilot posting (we draft + post)
  b) Draft-only approval (we draft, customer posts)
- Create escalation routing:
  - Email to escalation contact with subject: “Escalation: [Platform] [Star Rating] [Issue] — needs response by [time]”

DAY 2
6. Go Live
- If autopilot: start posting approved replies.
- If draft-only: deliver first batch (e.g., last 7–14 days) with clear copy/paste format.

7. Week-1 Success Criteria (communicate + track)
- 90%+ of new reviews responded to within SLA.
- All 1–3 star reviews escalated same day.
- Tone consistency: customer approves sample set with minimal edits.
- First weekly KPI report scheduled (send day/time).

8. Confirm Ongoing Cadence
- Weekly report delivery day/time.
- Monthly review trends + recommendations (optional add-on later).

Definition of Done (Activated):
- Access confirmed (or draft-only workflow confirmed)
- Brand voice approved
- Escalation contact tested (sent a test escalation)
- First replies posted/delivered
- Weekly report scheduled
