- Method B: client forwards review alert emails to agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to
- Log new reviews into tracker: date, platform, rating, reviewer name, text, location, link

SOP 2 — Response drafting rules
- 4–5 stars: thank + specific detail + light CTA (book/call/visit) + signature
- 3 stars: thank + acknowledge + offer offline resolution + no defensiveness
- 1–2 stars: acknowledge + apologize without admitting liability + move offline + escalation ticket auto-created
- Never include: customer PII, medical/legal claims, refunds promises unless approved, competitor mentions, threats

SOP 3 — QA + approval gates
Auto-approve allowed (post without asking) if:
- 4–5 stars AND no sensitive keywords AND template-safe
Require approval before posting if:
- 1–3 stars OR mentions: lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA/PHI, harassment, refund demand, chargeback, staff named, bribery, police, “scam,” “fraud.”

SOP 4 — Posting
- If access granted: post directly in GBP/Yelp
- If client-post: send “ready-to-post” email with platform link + copy

SOP 5 — Escalations
- Create escalation ticket for any 1–2★ review or sensitive keyword match.
- Notify primary contact within 2 business hours.
- Track outcome: contacted reviewer? issue resolved? follow-up requested?

SOP 6 — Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
- Send 1-page report (template below)
