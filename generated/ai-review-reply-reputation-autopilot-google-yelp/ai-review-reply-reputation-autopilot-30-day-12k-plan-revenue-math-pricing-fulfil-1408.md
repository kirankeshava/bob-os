Review sources supported immediately:
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp Business
Capture methods (choose per client):
A) Preferred: Client grants us Manager access to GBP and business access on Yelp.
B) Fallback (default-safe for Yelp): We draft responses in a shared doc/slack/email; client posts using provided copy. This avoids API needs and avoids credential sharing.

Response SLA:
- Growth/Agency Starter: respond within 24 hours of detection
- Pro/Agency Pro: respond within 12 hours of detection

Escalation rules (always human-reviewed):
Escalate if review is 1–2 stars OR contains keywords: refund, lawsuit, fraud, discrimination, HIPAA/medical, injury, safety, harassment, police, chargeback.
Escalation includes: summary, recommended response options, request for manager input, and “resolution next step.”

Brand safety rules:
- No incentives for reviews, no review gating, no asking only happy customers.
- No promises of outcomes; no admission of liability.
- Do not reveal private customer info.
- Maintain client’s banned phrases list.

Posting rules (human-in-the-loop):
- Auto-approve posting for 4–5★ reviews using pre-approved tone + template, unless flagged.
- Require approval for 1–3★ reviews (or any flagged keyword review) before posting.

Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
- New reviews (Google/Yelp), rating average + trend vs last week
- Response rate %, median response time
- # negative reviews (1–2★), # escalations, # resolved (client-confirmed)
- Top 3 themes (what customers mention), suggested operational fix
