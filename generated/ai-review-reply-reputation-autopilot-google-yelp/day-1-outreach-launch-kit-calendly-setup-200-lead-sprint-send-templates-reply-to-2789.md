2) 200-Lead/Day Manual Sourcing Sprint (45 minutes per 50 leads)
Goal: Build 150 local-owner leads (dentist/med spa/HVAC) + 50 agency leads/day.

A) Local owners (Google Maps + Yelp)
- Search strings (rotate by city):
  • “dentist near me” / “cosmetic dentist [CITY]” / “family dental [CITY]”
  • “med spa [CITY]” / “botox [CITY]” / “aesthetic clinic [CITY]”
  • “HVAC [CITY]” / “AC repair [CITY]” / “heating and cooling [CITY]”
- Pick businesses with:
  • 15–400 reviews (enough volume to feel pain)
  • Rating 3.2–4.6 (room to improve)
  • Visible recent reviews within last 30 days
- Capture fields (CSV / HubSpot import):
  BusinessName, Niche, City, State, GoogleRating, ReviewCount, LastReviewDate, OwnerOrManagerName (if shown), Email (from site/contact), Phone, Website, GoogleMapsURL, YelpURL (if any), PersonalizationNote (1 line), Segment=LocalOwner, Channel=ColdEmail
- PersonalizationNote examples:
  • “Saw a 2-star review from last week about scheduling delays—looks like it wasn’t answered.”
  • “You’ve got 186 reviews at 4.2—reply style is inconsistent across the last 10.”

B) Agencies (Clutch + Google + LinkedIn)
- Google search operators:
  • site:clutch.co “local SEO” “United States”
  • “marketing agency” + “multi-location” + [CITY]
  • “reputation management” + “agency” + “white label”
- Qualification:
  • Mentions local SEO / GBP / multi-location / franchise
  • Looks like 10–200 local clients (case studies, packages)
- Capture fields:
  AgencyName, URL, DecisionMakerName, Title, Email, LinkedInURL, OfferFitNote (1 line), Segment=Agency, Channel=ColdEmail

Workflow (repeatable):
- 0–5 min: Choose 1 city + 1 niche.
- 5–35 min: Collect 50 businesses (1 minute each).
- 35–45 min: Find emails on their websites + add 1-line PersonalizationNote.
