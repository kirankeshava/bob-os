- 1–2★ reviews
- Mentions: refund, lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA/medical claims, threats, profanity, harassment, fraud
- Staff named negatively
- Allegations of illegal behavior

- Create escalation ticket (simple form) with: review link, category, suggested response options, recommended offline resolution step.
- Notify client escalation contact by email within SLA.

### What is explicitly excluded (policy guardrails)
- No review gating or incentives tied to only positive reviews.
- No buying reviews.
- No impersonation.
- No removal requests/disputes without client authorization.
