## 2) Day-1 lead sourcing sprint (200 prospects, zero-cost)
**Target mix (Day 1):**
- 50 dentists + 50 med spas + 50 HVAC (owners/office managers/GM) = 150
- 50 agencies (marketing agencies managing multiple local clients) = 50

**Data sources (free):**
1) **Google Maps / Google Business Profiles:** search by city + niche (e.g., “dentist Austin”, “med spa Scottsdale”, “HVAC Tampa”). Prioritize businesses with: (a) 3.5–4.6 rating OR (b) recent negative reviews OR (c) many reviews but owner not responding.
2) **Yelp:** same search; prioritize businesses with unresponded reviews.
3) **Agency directories:** Clutch, UpCity, DesignRush (free browsing). Pull agencies with 10–200 employees or positioning around local SEO/PPC.
4) **Agency websites:** look for “contact”, “team”, “about” to find founder/partner emails.

**Decision-maker rules:**
- Local: Owner, Practice Manager, Office Manager, GM, Marketing Manager.
- Agency: Founder, Managing Partner, Head of Client Services, Director of SEO/Local.

**Minimum fields to capture (CSV/HubSpot):**
- Segment (Local/Agency)
- Niche (Dentist/MedSpa/HVAC/Agency)
- Business name
- Decision-maker name
- Role
- Email
- Website
- City/State
- Rating (if visible)
- Review cue (e.g., “3 unresponded 1-star reviews in last 60 days”)
- Source URL
- Personalization note (1 line)
- Status (New/Contacted/Replied/Booked)

**Personalization note formula (fast):**
“Noticed {review cue} on Google/Yelp and it looks like responses aren’t consistent.”
