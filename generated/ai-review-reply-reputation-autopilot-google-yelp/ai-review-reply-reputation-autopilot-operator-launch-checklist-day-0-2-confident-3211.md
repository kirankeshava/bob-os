## Day 0 (Immediately after verbal yes)
1) **Send Close + Kickoff Email (Day 0)**
   - Attach/inline: start date (today/tomorrow), what access is needed, and the intake form questions.
   - Confirm: “We won’t post anything without initial approval window (24–48h) unless you opt in.”

2) **Create CRM entry + set stage = Closed/Won**
   - Set “Target activation date” = +48 hours.
   - Add stakeholders + escalation contacts.

3) **Send Access Request Emails (GBP + Yelp)**
   - Use the forwardable templates (owner/manager/agency).
   - Ask them to reply-all confirming when invites are sent.

4) **Send Intake Form (copy/paste)**
   Collect:
   - Brand voice (3 adjectives), forbidden phrases, preferred sign-off name/title.
   - Escalation contacts (name/phone/email), escalation triggers.
   - Location(s), services, hours, refund/cancellation policy link.
   - Competitors to avoid mentioning; any legal/compliance constraints.

## Day 1 (Within 24 hours)
5) **Access verification + “Ready for Sample Drafts” confirmation**
   - GBP: confirm you can view reviews and reply.
   - Yelp: confirm role/access and ability to respond.

6) **Pull last 20 reviews (Google + Yelp)**
   - Categorize: positive / neutral / negative; themes; recurring issues.

7) **Create “Sample Pack” (6 replies total)**
   - 2 positive, 2 neutral, 2 negative (with de-escalation).
   - Each reply must be: brand-safe, no admissions of fault, no policy violations.
   - Add **Escalation Note** for any negative sample: what you need from owner to resolve.

8) **Send Sample Approval Email**
   - Ask for: (a) tone edits, (b) prohibited claims, (c) whether to sign as person or brand.
   - Provide 2 options:
     - **Option A (recommended):** approve style → we draft daily and you approve for 7 days.
     - **Option B:** approve style → you opt-in to autopost for non-negative reviews (negatives still escalate).

## Day 2 (Within 48 hours)
9) **Go live on the chosen workflow**
   - If approval mode: begin queue → send daily approval digest.
   - If autopost mode: turn on autopost rules for non-negative; negatives escalate.

10) **Set escalation SOP and SLA**
   - Define response SLA: e.g., negative reviews escalated within 2 hours during business day.
   - Define owner response SLA: e.g., owner provides details within 24 hours.

11) **Schedule reporting cadence**
   - Weekly KPI email (same day/time each week).
   - Confirm recipients.

## First-week Success Criteria (what “good” looks like)
- **Coverage:** 95–100% of new reviews drafted within 24 hours.
- **Speed:** median response time reduced to <24 hours (or customer-defined target).
- **Safety:** 0 policy-violating posts; 0 unapproved sensitive replies.
- **Escalation:** 100% of negative reviews get an escalation note + owner action request.
- **Reporting:** weekly KPI report delivered on time with clear next actions.
