D) DAILY SENDING OPS CHECKLIST + CRM STAGES

Goal (first 14 days): establish deliverability + messaging fit.

Daily targets (starting point, per inbox):
- Days 1–3: 10–20 new sends/day
- Days 4–7: 25–40 new sends/day
- Days 8–14: 50–80 new sends/day (only if bounce <3% and spam complaints ~0)

Every day (Mon–Fri):
1) Pull 25–100 new prospects from the lead sheet (Priority A first)
2) QA sample 10%: correct vertical, not a franchise directory, has recent reviews
3) Personalize first line using {{recent_review_snippet}} + {{response_gap}}
4) Send Email #1 to new prospects
5) Process replies within SLA:
   - Positive/curious: reply within 2 hours with “send drafts” offer + 2 sample replies
   - Neutral: ask 1 qualifying question (who manages GBP/Yelp? approval needed?)
   - Negative: polite close
6) Send scheduled follow-ups (Email #2, #3) to non-responders
7) Log outcomes in CRM and tag reason codes (Not now / Already using tool / No access to GBP / Price)

CRM stages (minimal, works in any spreadsheet/HubSpot free):
- Prospect (not yet emailed)
- Sent (Email #1 sent)
- Engaged (replied or asked a question)
- Qualified (has GBP access + pain acknowledged + willing to see examples)
- Demo Booked
- Trial / Pilot (first week replies)
- Paid
- Lost (with reason)

KPIs to track weekly:
- Delivery rate, bounce rate (<3%)
- Reply rate (target 3–8% early)
- Qualified rate (target 1–3%)
- Demos booked (target 0.5–1% of sends)
