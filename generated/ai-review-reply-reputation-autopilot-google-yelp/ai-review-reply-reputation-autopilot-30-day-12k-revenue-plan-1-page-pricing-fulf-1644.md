# Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable, no API dependency)
## Supported review sources
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

## Access requirements (workable in week 1)
- **Preferred:** Add us as **GBP Manager** (so we can post replies directly).
- **Yelp:** if Business Owner access isn’t granted or Yelp tooling is limited, we use **Client-Post Workflow** (we draft; client posts).

## Posting methods (standard)
1) **Direct-post (best):** We post replies inside GBP/Yelp when access is granted.
2) **Client-post fallback:** We send a daily/near-daily “ready-to-post” list via email with copy/paste responses and links.

## Response SLA rules
- DFY Growth: **24 business hours**
- DFY Pro/Agency Pro: **12 business hours**

## Escalation rules (always human-in-the-loop)
Escalate immediately (no autopost) if:
- Review is **1–3★**
- Mentions: refund, lawsuit, discrimination, injury, medical/legal claims, fraud, harassment, chargeback
- Includes sensitive personal data
- Requests a specific compensation outcome

Escalation deliverable:
- Incident ticket (date, platform, reviewer, issue summary)
- 2–3 drafted response options (public) + 1 private outreach script
- Resolution tracking (pending/resolved) for weekly report

## Brand-safety guardrails
- Never admit fault/liability; avoid promises that create legal exposure
- No incentives for reviews; no “review gating” instructions
- No claims about competitors
- No sharing private customer info
