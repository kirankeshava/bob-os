## 3) Partner Kit (PDF-Ready Text)

### One-liner
**White-label review response fulfillment for agencies:** we draft + post brand-safe Google/Yelp replies within 24h, escalate negatives, and report weekly reputation KPIs.

### Who it’s for
- Local SEO agencies
- Web/lead gen agencies serving multi-location businesses
- Reputation management shops that want a higher-quality operational backend

### Problem we solve
Most local businesses don’t respond consistently to reviews. Agencies know it matters but don’t want operational overhead or brand-risk mistakes.

### Our solution (white-label)
1) **24-hour response SLA**: new reviews answered within 24 business hours.
2) **Brand voice & guardrails**: per-client voice profile + forbidden claims + escalation rules.
3) **Negative review escalation**: internal alert and recommended action plan.
4) **Weekly KPI report**: rating trend, volume, response time, negative rate, unresolved escalations.

### Deliverables
- Review responses drafted and posted (Google Business Profile + Yelp)
- Escalation ticketing for negatives (email-based if needed)
- Weekly KPI report (PDF/Doc + optional spreadsheet)

### 7-day Free Pilot (Week 1 policy)
- **Free for 7 days**
- Up to **1–2 locations**
- Includes set up + voice profile + responses + 1 KPI report
- Goal: prove speed, quality, and agency-fit

### Partner pricing (post-pilot)
(Use as starting point; negotiate by volume.)
- Base: **$199 / location / month** (Google + Yelp)
- Volume tiers (example):
  - 5–19 locations: $179/location
  - 20–49 locations: $149/location
  - 50+ locations: $129/location

### Revenue share option
- Agency bills client MSRP (example $299/location/mo)
- We charge partner rate (example $199/location/mo)
- Agency keeps margin ($100/location/mo)

### SLA
- Response time: within 24 business hours for new reviews
- Escalations: negative reviews flagged within 4 business hours
- Report: delivered weekly (Monday by 5pm local time)

### Onboarding SOP (agency)
**Inputs needed per location**
1) Business name, address, website
2) Primary services + 3 “signature” offers
3) Brand voice (pick one): Friendly / Professional / Luxury / Straightforward
4) No-go list (claims, legal/medical phrasing, discounts rules)
5) Escalation contacts + preferred resolution steps
6) Access: Google Business Profile manager access; Yelp business access (or agency provides screenshots/export if access isn’t possible)

**Setup steps**
1) Create voice profile + response templates
2) Connect review sources (GBP + Yelp)
3) Start monitoring
4) First week: daily checks + baseline KPI snapshot
