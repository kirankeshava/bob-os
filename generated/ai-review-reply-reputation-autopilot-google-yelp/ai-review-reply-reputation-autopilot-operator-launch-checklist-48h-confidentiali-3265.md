### Day 1 (Configuration + First Drafts)
1. **Set response policy** (internal):
   - Positive reviews: respond within 24h
   - Neutral: respond within 24h with invite to return
   - Negative: draft within 12h; **escalate** if policy triggers hit
2. **Create draft batch** (minimum): last 10–20 unresponded reviews (GBP + Yelp).
3. **Send Sample Approval Pack** (5 replies):
   - 2 positive, 2 negative, 1 neutral
   - Include “why this wording is brand-safe” notes
4. **Client approval SLA**: request response within 24h; if no response, proceed with safest default voice unless they request hold.

### Day 2 (Go-Live)
1. **Post approved replies** (or deliver drafts for client posting if access limited).
2. **Confirm escalation workflow works**:
   - Triggered negative review → send escalation note to escalation contact
   - Log in CRM/Tracker with status
3. **Success confirmation message** to client:
   - # replies posted
   - # escalations flagged
   - Next report date

### First-week success criteria (tell customer upfront)
- **Response time** reduced to <24 hours for new reviews (or as agreed)
- **100% of new reviews triaged** (positive/neutral/negative) within 1 business day
- **All negative reviews escalated** same-day with a recommended next action
- **Weekly KPI email delivered** with clear trends and actions
