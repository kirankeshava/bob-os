Best starter verticals (fast close): (A) dental/ortho, (B) medspa/chiro/PT, (C) home services (HVAC/plumbing/roofing).
Where to find leads:
- Google Maps: search “{city} dentist” etc. Prioritize: rating 3.2–4.6 (improvable), 15–400 reviews (active), recent negative review in last 30 days, owner not replying or inconsistent.

8) One-call close workflow (15–20 minutes)
Goal: book → diagnose → show example replies → confirm safety workflow → close with “setup + month 1 upfront, go-live in 48 hours.”
Agenda:
- 2 min: confirm outcomes (“more calls/bookings, fewer fires, protect rating”).
- 6 min: diagnose (platforms, locations, review volume, who replies today, response gaps, negative review handling).
- 5 min: mini-demo: read 1 recent positive + 1 negative review and explain how we respond + escalate.
- 4 min: terms: response SLA, approval mode (manual/post/auto), escalation contact, weekly report.
- 2 min: close: “If we collect access + brand voice today, we can be live within 48 hours. Want to start on {Plan}?”
Operational rule: proposal + invoice sent within 2 hours of call.
