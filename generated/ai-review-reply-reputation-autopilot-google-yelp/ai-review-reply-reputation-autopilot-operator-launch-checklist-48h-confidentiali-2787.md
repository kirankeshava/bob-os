1) OPERATOR LAUNCH CHECKLIST (DAY 0–2 ACTIVATION)
Goal: get access, confirm brand voice/escalation rules, approve 3 sample replies, then go live.

Day 0 (Same day as close)
A. Create customer record
- Business name, location(s), primary contact, escalation contact(s)
- Platforms: Google Business Profile (GBP), Yelp (if applicable)

B. Send Kickoff Email (use your Day-0 Kickoff template)
- Include: Intake Form link/questions + Access Request emails + What happens in first week
- Set expectation: 3 sample replies for approval within 24 hours

C. Intake Form Completion (must-have fields)
- Brand voice adjectives (e.g., warm, professional, concise)
- Words/phrases to avoid; compliance notes (HIPAA, legal, etc.)
- Escalation thresholds (1–2 star, refund requests, threats, discrimination, safety)
- Approved sign-off name (e.g., “—The [Brand] Team”)
- Hours of operation + customer support phone/email

Day 1 (Access + sample approvals)
D. Obtain access
- GBP: Add us as Manager (preferred) or Communications Manager
- Yelp: Add us as “Account Manager” or grant access via agency login flow
- If customer uses an agency: have them forward the access-request email to the agency

E. Pull last 30–90 days reviews
- Count total reviews + negative reviews (1–2 star)
- Baseline response rate and median response time (rough estimate is ok initially)

F. Create and send 3 sample replies for approval
- 1 positive (5-star)
- 1 neutral (3–4 star)
- 1 negative (1–2 star) + escalation note (internal)
Approval rule: customer replies “Approved” or requests edits; lock voice guide once approved.

Day 2 (Go live)
G. Turn on autopilot workflow
- Post approved replies on new reviews within SLA (e.g., 24 hours)
- Escalate negatives immediately per rules (email + optional SMS if provided)

H. First-week success criteria (what you report on Day 7)
- 90%+ of new reviews answered within 24 hours (or target agreed)
- 100% of 1–2 star reviews escalated within 2 hours (business hours) or next morning
- Brand voice consistency confirmed (no rewrites needed after day 3)
- Weekly KPI report delivered (volume, response rate, avg response time, sentiment, escalations)

I. If blocked (common failure points) — fix fast
- No GBP access: resend access email + ask for 10-minute screenshare
- Yelp access friction: ask who “owns” the Yelp account; request agency contact
- Voice not approved: send 2 alternative tones and ask them to pick A/B
