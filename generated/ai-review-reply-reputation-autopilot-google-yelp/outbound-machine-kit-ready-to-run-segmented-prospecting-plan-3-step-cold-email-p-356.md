## 1) Segmented Prospecting Plan (what to target first)
### Target verticals (initial 30 days)
1) Dentists / dental clinics
2) Med spas / aesthetic clinics
3) HVAC + Plumbing (home services)

### High-intent segments (use for list building + personalization)
**Segment A — Not Responding**
- Definition: In the last 10 reviews, owner/management replies ≤ 2 (≤ 20% response-rate proxy). Or clear “no responses for weeks/months.”
- Why it converts: obvious operational gap + low effort fix.
- Offer angle: “We respond within 12 hours, brand-safe, you approve.”

**Segment B — Low Rating**
- Definition: Google rating < 4.2 OR multiple recent 1–3 star reviews.
- Why it converts: direct revenue impact and urgency.
- Offer angle: “Escalate negatives fast + calm/brand-safe replies + weekly KPI report.”

**Segment C — High Volume / High Velocity**
- Definition: Review count ≥ 200 OR last review within 14 days (high review velocity).
- Why it converts: they’re getting reviews; they need throughput.
- Offer angle: “Never fall behind; consistent tone; weekly insights.”

### Priority tiers (who to email first)
- **Priority A (send first):** (Not Responding AND High Volume) OR (Low Rating AND High Volume)
- **Priority B:** Not Responding only OR Low Rating only
- **Priority C:** High Volume only

### Agency/Reseller lane (parallel, higher ticket)
Target: local marketing agencies that manage SEO/GBP for dentists, med spas, home services.
- Offer: white-label “review response autopilot” + KPI report they can brand.
- Close: per-location pricing, margin built-in, 1–5 locations to start.

## 3) Daily Sending Ops Checklist (with 14-day ramp)
### CRM stages (minimum viable pipeline)
1) **Prospect (Uncontacted)** → record created + segment + priority
2) **Sent** → email 1 sent
3) **Follow-up 1 Sent**
4) **Follow-up 2 Sent**
5) **Replied** → any reply
6) **Qualified** → has Google/Yelp + confirms decision maker + review pain
7) **Demo Booked**
8) **Trial / Pilot**
9) **Paid**
10) **Lost / Not now**

### Daily activity targets (solo operator)
- New prospects added: 25–50/day
- New emails sent: 25–75/day (depending on ramp)
- Follow-ups sent: 25–100/day (after day 7)
- Reply handling: same day; negative-intent replies within 2 hours

### 14-day sending ramp (per inbox)
- Days 1–2: 10/day
- Days 3–4: 20/day
- Days 5–6: 30/day
- Days 7–8: 40/day
- Days 9–10: 50/day
- Days 11–14: 60/day (cap here unless deliverability is excellent)

If using 2 inboxes, multiply volume by 2, but keep copy + domains diversified.

### List hygiene + QA (non-negotiable)
- Remove: franchises/corporate listings unless you have corporate contact
- Require: website OR clear contact email on site
- Verify: category matches vertical (avoid “cosmetic dentist supply” etc.)
- Bounce threshold: pause if hard bounces > 3% in any 200-send window
- Complaint threshold: pause and adjust if spam complaints > 0.1%

### Reply-handling SLA (what to do when they respond)
- Positive/curious: offer 10-min call + ask who owns Google/Yelp access
- “Not interested”: ask 1-question close (“Are review replies already covered?”)
- Angry: apologize, confirm you’ll stop, do not argue

### Weekly KPI tracking (outbound)
- Sent, Delivered, Opens (optional), Replies, Positive replies, Demos booked, Trials started, Paid
- Segment performance: Not Responding vs Low Rating vs High Volume
- Vertical performance: dental vs med spa vs HVAC/plumbing
