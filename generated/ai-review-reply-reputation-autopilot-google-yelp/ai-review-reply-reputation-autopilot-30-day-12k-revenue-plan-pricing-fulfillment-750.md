3) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO API REQUIRED)
Review sources supported week 1:
- Google Business Profile reviews
- Yelp reviews

Posting method hierarchy (to ensure deliverability without engineering):
1) Preferred: client grants us Manager access on Google Business Profile (GBP) to reply natively.
2) Yelp: if Business Owner/manager access is granted, we post replies natively.
3) Fallback (allowed by default): “Client-post workflow” — we draft replies in a shared doc/Slack/email; client posts using their credentials. We track completion.

Daily operating workflow (SOP outline):
A) Onboarding (Day 0–2)
- Collect access: GBP Manager invite; Yelp access OR confirm client-post fallback
- Collect Brand Voice Profile: tone, greeting/sign-off, offers allowed, compensation rules, banned phrases, sensitive topics (medical/legal), escalation contacts
- Confirm SLA window and holidays
- Confirm review response policy (what we will NOT do: incentives for reviews, review gating, deleting reviews, fake reviews)

B) Review capture (Daily)
- Pull new reviews via manual check and/or email alerts/screenshots
- Log each review into tracker: platform, rating, text, reviewer name, date, priority

C) Drafting + QA (Daily)
- Draft response using brand voice rules and platform policies
- Human QA check for: PII, admissions of fault, medical/legal claims, offers/discount policy, tone

D) Approval rules
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-approved unless flagged keywords
- 1–3★ reviews: always require approval OR escalation contact acknowledgment (depending on client preference)
- Flag keywords auto-escalate: “lawsuit, attorney, refund demand, discrimination, injury, fraud, HIPAA/medical details, harassment”

E) Posting + confirmation (Daily)
- Post directly (if access) OR send “ready-to-post” formatted copy to client
- Mark status: Drafted → Approved → Posted → Resolved

F) Escalation handling (Same day)
- Create an escalation ticket with: review link, suggested response, recommended offline resolution script, owner/manager next steps
- Track: contacted? resolved? updated review?

G) Weekly KPI report (Every Monday)
- New reviews count
- Avg star rating (week and trailing 30 days)
- Response rate (% replied)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews (count) + escalations (count) + resolved (count)
- Top 3 themes and suggested operational fix
