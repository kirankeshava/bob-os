Title: White-Label Review Reply & Reputation Autopilot — Partner Kit

1) One-line offer
We respond to your clients’ Google Business Profile + Yelp reviews within 24 hours in their brand voice, escalate negatives, and send a weekly reputation KPI report — fully white-label.

2) Who it’s for
• Local SEO agencies, web/SEO shops, SMB marketing firms
• Industries: dental, med spa, home services, restaurants, auto dealers, multi-location retail
• Best fit: 5–50 locations under management with recurring packages

3) Outcomes (what you can promise)
• Higher response rate + faster time-to-response (trust + conversion)
• Consistent brand-safe messaging (no risky owner replies)
• Early detection of recurring issues via negative theme tracking
• Agency retains control: escalations + approvals optional

4) Service scope
Included per location:
• Google Business Profile review responses (new reviews)
• Yelp review responses (where available/authorized)
• Brand voice profile setup (tone, do/don’t, banned phrases)
• Negative review escalation within 4 business hours
• Weekly KPI report: rating trend, response rate, median response time, negative themes, wins
Optional:
• Multi-language reply support
• “Review request” templates for SMS/email (agency-branded)

5) SLA
• Standard: respond within 24 hours (Mon–Fri)
• Escalation: negative reviews flagged within 4 business hours
• If approval workflow is enabled, turnaround depends on client approval speed; we still draft within 24 hours

6) Pricing (partner)
• Partner base: $199/location/month (recommended MSRP: $299)
• Volume discounts (example): 10–24 locations: $179; 25+ locations: $159
• 7-day free pilot: 1–2 locations, done-for-you
Revenue share option:
• You resell at your price; you keep margin. Or we can do rev share if you prefer (case-by-case).

7) Onboarding SOP (agency)
Step 1 — Choose pilot location(s)
Send:
• Business name + website
• Primary services + top 3 differentiators
• Tone (friendly/professional/playful), any banned phrases
• Escalation contact (email)

Step 2 — Access/permissions
• Google Business Profile: add partner/manager access (preferred) OR forward review notifications
• Yelp: add user access where available OR forward review notifications

Step 3 — Brand voice calibration
We generate a “voice card”:
• Greeting style
• Apology policy
• Offer/refund language policy
• Compliance constraints (healthcare/legal)
Agency approves once; then autopilot runs.

Step 4 — Go live
• Daily monitoring + 24h response SLA
• Negative escalation triggers:
  – rating ≤ 3 stars
  – mention of staff rudeness, safety, billing, discrimination, scam
  – repeat complaint patterns

8) Escalation workflow (negative reviews)
1) We draft a de-escalation response (public) + suggested private resolution steps
2) We email escalation contact with: review link, customer issue, recommended action, draft response
3) Agency/client can approve, edit, or ask us to handle directly (based on permissions)
