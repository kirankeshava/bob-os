H) Booking Call Script (10–15 minutes)
========================
1) Opener (30 sec)
“Thanks for the time. Goal today: confirm whether we can reliably keep your Google/Yelp replies caught up, protect you on negative reviews, and send simple weekly KPIs. If it’s a fit, I’ll outline the fastest start path.”

2) Diagnose (3–5 min)
- “About how many reviews do you get per week across Google/Yelp?”
- “Who replies today, and how often?”
- “Any recent negative reviews you wish had been handled differently?”
- “Do you want draft-only approval, or are you comfortable with us posting routine replies and escalating negatives?”
- “Any compliance constraints? (HIPAA/clinical language/no offers/etc.)”

3) Reflect the pain (30 sec)
“Got it: volume is [X], replies happen [frequency], and the risk is [negatives/slow response/inconsistent tone]. The fix is a simple operating system: daily replies + escalation + weekly KPIs.”

4) Offer (2 min)
“We do this as a hybrid: software-driven drafting + DFY ops so it actually happens every day.
- Daily review monitoring
- Brand-voice responses
- Negative review escalation (you approve)
- Weekly KPI summary (response time, rating trend, negatives flagged)

We can start with a 7-day pilot to prove speed/quality, then move to monthly.”

5) Close (2–3 min)
“Best next step is: access + brand voice + escalation contacts. We can be live within 24 hours.”

I) Objection Handling Snippets
========================
“Can’t you just automate this with AI?”
“You can automate drafting, but the business risk is tone + edge cases. Our system pairs AI drafting with brand rules + negative-review escalation so nothing risky gets posted.”

“We don’t get many reviews.”
“Even at low volume, consistent replies protect conversion—prospects read the last 5–10 reviews. We keep you ‘always replied’ so you never look inactive.”

“I’m worried about giving access.”
“No problem—start draft-only. We’ll deliver approved drafts in a shared doc or via email. If you later want faster turnaround, we can switch to manager access.”

“Price?”
“It depends on locations + volume + whether you want posting included. If you tell me roughly how many reviews/week and how many locations, I’ll recommend the right tier.”
