Human-in-the-loop quality and policy controls:
• Every response is drafted with brand voice + compliance rules and reviewed by a human before posting/sending to client (week 1 standard).
• Auto-approval rule (optional): 4–5★ can be posted without client approval unless flagged keywords present.
• Required approval rule: 1–3★ reviews, legal/medical/financial claims, refund requests, discrimination/safety incidents, threats, or any request for private data.

Escalation rules (negative reviews):
• Trigger: 1–2★ always; 3★ if complaint includes safety, billing, rude staff, wait time > X, or repeat customer.
• Escalation ticket includes: review link, summary, severity (Low/Med/High), recommended response options (2–3), required approvals, and “offline resolution” steps.

Explicit exclusions (to remain compliant and deliverable):
• No review gating, no incentives-for-reviews, no fake reviews
• No promises to delete/remove reviews
