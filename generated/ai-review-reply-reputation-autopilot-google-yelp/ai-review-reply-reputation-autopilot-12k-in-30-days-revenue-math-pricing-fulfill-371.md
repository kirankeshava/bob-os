1) **Onboarding intake (30–45 min)**
   - Brand voice, do/don’t phrases, offers, service list, escalation contacts, business hours.
2) **Daily review capture**
   - Preferred: client enables email alerts + grants manager access.
   - Backup: we check dashboards manually 1–2×/day.
3) **Draft responses**
   - Drafted with AI + templated brand voice.
4) **Brand-safety QA (human)**
   - Check policy compliance (no medical/legal claims, no incentives, no private data exposure).
5) **Approval rules**
   - 4–5★: auto-approve unless flagged keywords.
   - 1–3★ or sensitive keywords (refund, lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA, etc.): require client approval + escalation ticket.
6) **Posting method**
   - Google: post via GBP Manager access (preferred) OR client-post (we send copy).
   - Yelp: default to **client-post workflow** unless Yelp Owner access available.
7) **Escalation + resolution tracking**
   - Negative reviews become tickets; track contact attempts + outcome.
8) **Weekly KPI report**
   - Email each Monday: rating trend, new reviews, response rate, response time, negatives, themes, actions.

### Hard exclusions (protect deliverability)
- No “review gating” or incentivizing reviews.
- No deletion promises.
- No disputing reviews on behalf of client without explicit written authorization.
