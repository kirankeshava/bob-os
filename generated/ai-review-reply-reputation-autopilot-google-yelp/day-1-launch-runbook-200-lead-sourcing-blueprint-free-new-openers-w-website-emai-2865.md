## B) First 200-lead sourcing blueprint (free, repeatable)
### Target mix
- 150 local owners (50 dentists + 50 med spas + 50 HVAC)
- 50 agencies (white-label / local SEO / reputation / multi-location)

### Columns (CSV / HubSpot import)
Company | Niche | City | Website | Contact Name | Role | Email | Phone | Google rating | #Reviews | Yelp rating | Notes (personalization) | Source URL | Segment (Local/Agency) | Channel (Email/Upwork/FB) | Status

### Free sources + exact query patterns
1) **Google Maps (manual)**
   - Search patterns:
     - “dentist + [city]”
     - “med spa + [city]” / “medical spa + [city]”
     - “HVAC + [city]” / “air conditioning repair + [city]”
   - Pick listings with:
     - 3.5–4.6 rating OR recent negative review OR no owner responses in last 30–90 days.
   - Capture: business name, website, phone, rating, review count.

2) **Business websites → Contact/About pages**
   - Look for: owner name, practice manager, office manager, “info@”, “hello@”, “contact@”.
   - If no email displayed, use the site contact form (log as “Contact form submitted” in Notes; still counts as an outreach touch).

3) **Yelp business pages (manual)**
   - Grab Yelp rating + review count and note whether owner replies exist.
   - Use as a personalization hook (“Noticed a few reviews haven’t gotten a response yet”).

4) **Agency directories (free)**
   - Clutch.co (filter Local SEO / Reputation Management)
   - Google search: “local SEO agency + [state]”, “reputation management agency white label”, “multi location marketing agency”.
   - Capture: agency site, founder/partner name, and contact email.

### Dedupe + tagging (fast)
- Dedupe rule: same domain OR same phone number = same company.
- Tagging rule:
  - Segment: Local vs Agency
  - Niche: Dentist / MedSpa / HVAC / Agency
  - Priority:
    - P1: rating <4.2 OR negative review in last 30 days OR zero recent owner responses
    - P2: rating 4.2–4.6 with moderate reviews
    - P3: rating 4.7+ with active responses (lower urgency)

### Daily build speed targets
- Local: 25 dentists + 25 med spas + 25 HVAC per day (75/day)
- Agencies: 10–15/day
- Aim: 90 leads/day sourced; outreach uses the first 75 email-ready leads.
