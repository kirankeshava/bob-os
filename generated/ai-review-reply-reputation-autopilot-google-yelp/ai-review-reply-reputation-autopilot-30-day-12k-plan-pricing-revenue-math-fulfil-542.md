## 4) Fulfillment Scope (week-1 deliverable) + constraints

### Sources supported
- **Google Business Profile reviews**
- **Yelp reviews**

### SLA & cadence
- Review check + drafting: daily, Mon–Fri
- DFY Growth: respond in **24 business hours**
- DFY Pro: respond in **12 business hours**
- Weekly KPI report: every Monday (or agreed day)

### Posting method (no API dependency)
**Default (preferred):** client adds us as Manager/Agency access where possible.
- Google: client adds Manager access to GBP.
- Yelp: access varies; if direct posting access is not available, use client-post workflow.

**Client-post fallback (always available):**
- We draft approved responses in a shared doc/spreadsheet.
- Client posts directly in their GBP/Yelp owner dashboard.

### Brand-safety + human-in-the-loop
- Every response goes through: draft → QA check (tone/policy) → post.
- Auto-approve rule (optional): 4–5★ reviews can be posted without explicit client approval unless they contain flagged keywords.

### Escalation rules (negative review handling)
Escalate (notify client contact) if any of the following:
- Rating **≤ 3★**
- Mentions: refund, lawsuit, discrimination, injury, infection, HIPAA/medical specifics, fraud, safety, threats, chargeback
- Names a staff member with an accusation
- Mentions competitor spam or fake review suspicion

Escalation SLA:
- Notify within **4 business hours** (Pro) / **8 business hours** (Growth)
- We provide: recommended public reply + private resolution checklist

### Explicit exclusions (to keep it deliverable + compliant)
- No incentivized reviews / “review gating”
- No deletion promises
- No disputing reviews on behalf of client without explicit written authorization

## 5) Simple Fulfillment SOP Outline (operator checklist)

### Onboarding (Day 0–1)
1) Collect business info: locations, hours, services, offers, phone, booking URL.
2) Brand voice: friendly/professional, emoji policy, sign-off name, prohibited phrases.
3) Escalation contact(s): name, email, phone; preferred resolution options.
4) Access: GBP Manager access; Yelp owner access OR confirm client-post workflow.
5) Confirm response policy: discounts/refunds language, privacy language.

### Daily operations (Mon–Fri)
1) Pull new reviews from Google/Yelp (manual check or email alerts screenshot).
2) Draft responses using brand voice + policy rules.
3) QA pass (brand-safe + platform-safe).
4) Post (via access) OR send to client in “Post Queue”.
5) If escalation triggered: send escalation email + log in tracker.

### Weekly reporting (every Monday)
1) Export/record metrics: new reviews, avg rating, response rate, median response time.
2) Summarize themes (top 3 compliments/complaints).
3) List escalations + resolution status.
4) Send 1-page email report.
