## Response SLA
- Growth/Agency Starter: **within 24 business hours**
- Pro/Agency Pro: **within 12 business hours**

## Brand safety / policy rules
- No prohibited content: hate/harassment, medical/legal guarantees, PHI, discriminatory language.
- No review gating, no incentives (“discount for review”), no fake reviews.
- Never claim the reviewer is/was a customer if unclear.

## Escalation rules (negative reviews)
Escalate if any:
- 1–2★ automatically
- 3★ if mentions: “refund”, “lawsuit”, “fraud”, “scam”, “injury”, “unsafe”, “discrimination”, “health”, “HIPAA”, “insurance”, “chargeback”, “police”, “BBB”
- Any mention of employee names, sensitive incidents, or threats

Escalation SLA:
- Notify escalation contact within **4 business hours** (Pro) / **8 business hours** (Growth)
- Provide 2–3 response options + a “make it right” internal checklist.

## Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
Per location:
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating + change vs prior week
- Response rate (%)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–2★, 3★)
- Escalations opened/closed
- Top 3 themes (service, wait time, price, etc.)
- Next-week recommendations (3 bullets)

# 5) Fulfillment SOP Outline (step-by-step)
## Onboarding (Day 0–2)
1) Collect: business info, locations, hours, top services, offers allowed, “do-not-say” list, escalation contacts.
2) Brand voice selection: Friendly / Professional / Luxury / No-nonsense.
3) Approval rules:
   - Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged keywords.
   - Always require approval for 1–3★ OR flagged keywords.
4) Access setup:
   - Request GBP Manager access; Yelp Manager access.
   - If delayed: enable Draft+Client Posts workflow.

## Daily operations (Mon–Fri)
1) Capture new reviews (manual check or notification emails/screenshots).
2) Draft replies with policy + tone.
3) Human QA pass: brand safety, compliance, no promises, correct business details.
4) Route:
   - If auto-approve: post (or send to client to post).
   - If requires approval: send 2 options + recommendation.
5) Log in tracker: review link, star rating, theme, reply status, posted date/time.

## Escalations
1) Create escalation ticket with screenshot + summary.
2) Propose resolution steps + 2–3 reply drafts.
3) Track outcome: resolved/not, reviewer updated rating (if happens).
