3) Fulfillment scope (week-1 deliverable, no API dependency)

- Yelp reviews

Posting method (week 1 constraints + workaround)
- Google: Prefer GBP Manager access (add agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to as Manager). If not possible, client-post fallback (we deliver approved copy daily; client posts).
- Yelp: Yelp API access is not assumed. Default week-1 delivery is either (a) Yelp Business Owner access granted to us OR (b) client-post fallback (we draft; client posts). (Owner decision needed on which is the default gate.)

Response workflow + SLA
Daily cadence (Mon–Fri, optional weekends as add-on):
1) Capture: Check new reviews (manual check + email notifications/screenshots acceptable).
2) Draft: Generate response in the agreed brand voice and policy constraints.
3) QA: Human review for tone, compliance, and “no admissions/no liability” rules.
4) Approval rules:
   - Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged keywords appear.
   - Always require client approval for 1–3★ OR reviews referencing refunds, injury, discrimination, legal threats, HIPAA/medical claims, or employee names.
5) Post:
   - If access granted: we post directly.
   - If client-post: we send a daily “ready-to-post” message with exact copy.

Escalation rules (negative reviews)
Trigger: any 1–2★ review OR 3★ with negative sentiment.
Process:
- Create an escalation ticket (date, platform, reviewer name, complaint category, recommended resolution, owner contact).
- Notify client within SLA (Growth: same business day; Starter: next business day).
- Track outcome (responded, resolved offline, updated review yes/no).

What’s included / what’s explicitly excluded (risk controls)
Included: drafting responses, brand-voice alignment, KPI reporting, escalation routing.
Excluded: review gating, incentivizing reviews, fake reviews, disputing/removing reviews without explicit client authorization, responding as an individual employee, legal advice.
