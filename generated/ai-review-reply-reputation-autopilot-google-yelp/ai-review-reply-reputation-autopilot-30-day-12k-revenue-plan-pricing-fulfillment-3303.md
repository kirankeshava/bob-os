Human-in-the-loop workflow (deliverable immediately)
1) Onboarding intake (30 minutes)
- Brand voice (friendly/professional), do-not-say list, offers/promos allowed, staff names/roles, escalation contacts.
2) Review capture
- Preferred: client grants manager access to GBP; Yelp Business Owner access.
- Fallback: client forwards review notifications or shares screenshots; we draft responses; client posts.
3) Drafting
- AI drafts response using approved voice, length rules, and policy constraints.
4) QA + safety
- Human QA checks: no incentives for reviews, no medical/legal claims, no admission of fault, no confidential info.
5) Approval rules
- Auto-approve/post: 4–5★ unless flagged keywords.
- Require client approval: 1–3★, fraud claims, injury/medical, legal threats, discrimination, refund demands, or any “sensitive” keyword list.
6) Posting
- Preferred: we post directly using granted access.
- Fallback: send “ready-to-paste” responses via email within SLA.
7) Escalations
- Create an escalation ticket (email thread) with: review link/text, suggested next step, deadline, owner to respond.
8) Weekly KPI report (every Monday)
- Metrics + short narrative: wins, risks, top themes, recommended actions.
