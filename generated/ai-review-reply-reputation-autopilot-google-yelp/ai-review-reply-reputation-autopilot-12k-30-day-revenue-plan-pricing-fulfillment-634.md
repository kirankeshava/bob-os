C) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 Deliverable) — No API dependency
Sources supported immediately:
- Google Business Profile reviews
- Yelp reviews
Acquisition of reviews (week 1):
- Preferred: client grants GBP Manager access (read/respond)
- Yelp: default workflow = we draft; client posts (unless Yelp owner access is granted)
- Fallback: client forwards review notifications via email screenshots OR shares review links daily.

Response workflow:
1) Intake: brand voice, prohibited phrases, offers/links allowed, escalation contacts, hours, refund/discount policy.
2) Daily review capture: check GBP/Yelp notifications + manual check once/day (business days).
3) Draft response: brand-safe, policy-safe, avoids admissions of fault, avoids incentives.
4) QA (human-in-loop): check tone, compliance, and escalation triggers.
5) Approval rules:
   - Auto-approve draft for 4–5★ unless flagged keywords present.
   - Require approval for 1–3★ OR legal/medical/safety keywords OR accusations of discrimination/fraud.
6) Posting method:
   - If access: we post directly in GBP/Yelp.
   - If no Yelp access: we email/Slack the final copy to client for posting (1-click copy format).
7) Escalation ticket: created for any 1–2★ and selected 3★ reviews; track resolution.
8) Weekly KPI report: send every Monday (or chosen day) with KPI table + commentary.

Escalation triggers (examples):
- 1–2★ reviews
- Allegations: fraud, scam, discrimination, safety issue, injury
- Medical/legal claims (HIPAA/PHI risk)
- Threats of chargebacks/lawsuits
Escalation SLA: within 4 business hours (Pro) / within 1 business day (Starter/Growth).

Explicit constraints (set expectations):
- We do not remove reviews, guarantee deletions, or contact platforms to dispute without client authorization.
- No review gating/incentivization (“leave us 5 stars for a discount”).
- We will not request or publish private customer data.
