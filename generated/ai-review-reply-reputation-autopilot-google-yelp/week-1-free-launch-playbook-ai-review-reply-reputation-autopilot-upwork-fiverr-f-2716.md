### Why Fiverr still works organically
New gigs can rank with strong keywords + fast response + clear packages. No promoted spend in Week 1.

### Publish Sequence (Day 1)
1) Gig 1: “I will respond to your Google reviews with brand-safe on-brand replies”
2) Gig 2: “I will respond to your Yelp reviews professionally and consistently”
3) Gig 3: “I will deliver a weekly reputation KPI report for Google/Yelp”

### Must-have FAQ (include in each gig)

A: “Week 1 trial: I draft replies; posting can be done by you or we can coordinate access later. Drafts are delivered ready-to-post.”

Q: “How do you handle negative reviews?”
A: “I flag 1–2 star reviews immediately and draft a calm, brand-safe response plus a private resolution message. Escalations go to your designated contact.”

- Business name + category
