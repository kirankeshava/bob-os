5) WEEK-1 SOP (human-in-the-loop; no API dependency)
Step 0 — Onboarding Intake (Day 0-1)
• Collect: brand voice (friendly/professional), do-not-say list, promos, location details, escalation contact + phone/email, business hours, approval preferences.

Step 1 — Access & Monitoring Setup (Day 1)
Google:
• Preferred: client adds us as Manager to GBP.
Yelp:
• Preferred: client provides Yelp Business Owner access.
Fallback (no access): “Client-post workflow” where we draft and send ready-to-paste replies within SLA.

Step 2 — Daily Review Capture (Daily)
• Manual checks + alerts (email notifications) for new reviews.
• Log each review into tracker: platform, star rating, reviewer name, text, date.

Step 3 — Draft Response (Daily)
• Generate response using standard frameworks:
  - 5★: gratitude + specific reinforcement + invite back
  - 4★: gratitude + acknowledge improvement + invite offline note
  - 1–3★: acknowledge + apologize (if appropriate) + move offline + escalation

Step 4 — QA & Policy Check (Daily)
• Ensure: no admission of liability, no PHI, no incentives, no confrontational language.

Step 5 — Approval & Posting (Daily)
• Auto-post (if allowed) for 4–5★ non-flagged.
• Send approval request for 1–3★ and flagged reviews.
• Post via: native platform access OR send client “copy/paste” with direct review link.

Step 6 — Escalation Ticket (As needed)
• Create escalation note: issue summary, proposed resolution language, owner action needed, deadline.

Step 7 — Weekly KPI Report (Weekly)
• Send KPI email + attach report; note recurring issues and recommended operational fixes.
