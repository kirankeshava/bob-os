3) Fulfillment scope (deliverable in week 1)
Review sources supported immediately:
- Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Yelp Business

Week-1 method (no API dependency):
- Preferred: client grants us Manager access to GBP and access to Yelp Business Owner account.
- Fallback: “Draft + client posts” workflow (we send approved copy daily; client posts natively). This avoids API constraints and keeps deliverability high.

Response SLA definition:
- SLA clock starts when the review is detected in our daily review check or when client forward/alert arrives.
- 24 business hours for Growth/Starter; 12 business hours for Pro.

Approval rules (human-in-the-loop):
- Auto-approve: 4–5★ reviews unless flagged by keyword list.
- Require approval: 1–3★ reviews; any mentions of injury, refunds, discrimination, legal threats, medical claims, pricing disputes, HIPAA/PHI, or staff names.

Escalation rules:
- Escalate immediately if: 1★ with specific allegation; threats of chargeback/lawsuit; safety issue; staff misconduct claim; discrimination claim; medical/legal keywords.
- Escalation includes: summary, recommended response options, and a “next step” checklist (call customer, offer resolution channel, document outcome).

Posting method:
- If access granted: we post responses directly inside GBP/Yelp.
- If fallback: we send “copy blocks” with exact formatting and links for posting; client confirms posted.

What we explicitly do NOT do:
- No incentivized reviews, no review gating, no buying reviews.
- No deleting reviews or disputing reviews without client authorization.
