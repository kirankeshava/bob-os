1) ICP + Targeting (who to message first)
Prioritize local businesses where reviews directly drive calls/appointments and where owners are too busy to respond consistently:

9) Proof Pack: 3 example replies + safety workflow (paste into follow-ups or proposals)
Example reply (positive):
“Thanks so much for the kind words, {{ReviewerName}}. We’re really glad you had a great experience with our team. If there’s ever anything we can do to help again, please reach out—looking forward to seeing you next time!”

Example reply (neutral / 3-star):
“Hi {{ReviewerName}} — thank you for the feedback. We’re glad parts of your visit went well, and we’d love to learn what we could do to earn a 5-star experience next time. If you’re open to it, please contact us at {{ContactMethod}} so we can make this right.”

Example reply (negative / escalation-friendly):
“Hi {{ReviewerName}} — we’re sorry to hear this and we take it seriously. We’d like to look into what happened and make it right. Please contact {{EscalationContact}} with the best way to reach you (or call {{Phone}}), and we’ll follow up promptly. Thank you for bringing this to our attention.”

AI safety workflow (anti-‘AI risk’):
- Brand Voice Intake: we collect tone, do/don’t phrases, and prohibited topics.
- Approval Modes: (1) Manual approval required, (2) Auto-post for positives only + manual for negatives, or (3) Fully managed with audit log.
- Escalation Triggers: any mention of safety, discrimination, billing disputes, legal threats, refunds, staff names, or profanity routes to a human contact before anything posts.
- Audit Log: every draft stored with timestamp + platform + outcome.
