## 4) Cold Email Day-1 “Send List” Template + Manual Lead-Sourcing Checklist (free)

### CSV columns (copy/paste)
segment, niche, business_name, decision_maker_name, role, email, website, phone, city, state, google_maps_url, rating, review_count, last_review_date, personalization_note, channel, status, last_touch_date, next_touch_date, notes

### Manual lead sourcing checklist (repeatable)
1) Google Maps search: “{niche} {city}” (dentist / med spa / hvac).
2) Open top results with 4.0–4.7 rating AND 30+ reviews (high impact) OR any business with recent negative reviews.
3) Capture: business name, website, phone, rating, review count, maps URL, last review date.
4) Find email (free): website header/footer, Contact page, About page, privacy policy, or booking page.
5) Find decision maker name (free): website About/team page or LinkedIn company page. If none, use “Owner/Manager”.
6) Write 1-line personalization_note: e.g., “Saw 2 reviews unanswered in last 14 days” or “Recent 1-star mentioned ‘wait time’”.
7) Tag segment + niche + channel=email. Import to HubSpot.
