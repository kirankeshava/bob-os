Goal: confirm pain, reduce risk, pick pilot, book onboarding.

“Thanks—this will be quick. I’ll ask 5 questions about how you handle reviews now, then I’ll recommend either (a) draft-only or (b) draft+post with approvals. If it fits, we’ll start with a 7-day pilot.”

“We’re not trying to ‘game’ ratings—just protect revenue by responding faster, consistently, and safely. Every negative review is escalated for approval before anything posts.”

Option A — Draft-Only: “We draft responses daily; you click-post. Lowest risk, easiest approval.”
Option B — Draft+Post: “We post via manager access. Negative reviews always require your approval first.”

Pilot: “Let’s run a 7-day pilot so you can see the tone, speed, and escalation flow.”

5) Close + next steps (2 minutes)
“To start, I need: (1) Google Business Profile access (or draft-only), (2) your brand voice answers, (3) escalation contact for negative reviews + a phone number/email for urgent issues, and (4) your SLA choice (same-day vs next-business-day). I’ll send the checklist in one message.”
