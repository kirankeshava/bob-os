MODULE 1 — “DENTAL PRACTICE: FROM DAYS TO SAME-DAY REPLIES”
Headline:
Same-day review replies without adding front-desk workload

Problem:
A multi-provider dental office was getting 15–30 new Google reviews/month but replying sporadically (often 7–14 days later). Negative reviews sat unanswered, and the owner felt uncomfortable writing responses.

What we did:
• Set a “Professional + warm” voice, approved phrases, and HIPAA-safe rules
• Turned on daily monitoring + draft workflow
• Escalated all ≤2★ reviews with an approval requirement
• Implemented weekly KPI reporting (response time + % answered)

Results (example narrative):
• Response time improved from ~10 days to <24 hours on average
• 90%+ of new reviews received an on-brand response
• Negative reviews were addressed quickly with calm, de-escalating language
• Owner reported fewer “what happened?” calls because issues were routed offline

MODULE 2 — “HOME SERVICES: CONSISTENT RESPONSES ACROSS TECHS + LOCATIONS”
Headline:
Consistent, policy-safe replies across locations—plus negative-review escalation

Problem:
A home services brand had multiple crews and inconsistent customer experiences. Reviews mentioned individual techs and scheduling issues. The team wanted replies that sounded human, not copy-paste, but had no time.

What we did:
• Built a brand voice guide with do/don’t rules (no defensiveness, invite offline resolution)
• Enabled Draft+Post for 4–5★ reviews to keep speed high
• Set escalations for 1–2★, “refund,” “no-show,” and “rude” keywords
• Delivered weekly KPIs with an “unresolved escalations” list

Results (example narrative):
• Reply coverage increased from inconsistent to near-complete on new reviews
• Faster responses reduced public back-and-forth; more issues moved offline
• Leadership gained a weekly view of patterns (scheduling vs workmanship vs billing)
