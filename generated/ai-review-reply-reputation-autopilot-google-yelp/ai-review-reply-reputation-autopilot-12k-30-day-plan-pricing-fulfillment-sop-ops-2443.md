FULFILLMENT SCOPE (week-1 deliverable; no paid tools; no API dependency)
Review sources: Google Business Profile (GBP) + Yelp reviews.

Escalation rules (auto-flag):
- Any 1–2★ review
- 3★ with complaint keywords (refund, fraud, lawsuit, discrimination, injury, HIPAA/medical, harassment)
- Mentions of staff names, threats, safety issues, chargebacks, police, attorney.
Posting method (important for week 1):
- Google: request GBP Manager access (preferred). If not granted, client-post fallback.
- Yelp: default to client-post fallback OR request Yelp Business Owner access if available. We draft; client posts.
Approval workflow:
- 4–5★: default “auto-approve” unless client sets special constraints.
- 1–3★ or flagged keywords: requires explicit client approval before posting.
Human-in-the-loop QA:
- Every reply is checked for brand voice, policy, privacy, and admissions of fault.
What we do NOT do:
- No review gating, no incentivizing reviews, no deleting reviews, no disputing without explicit client authorization, no promises of refunds publicly.
