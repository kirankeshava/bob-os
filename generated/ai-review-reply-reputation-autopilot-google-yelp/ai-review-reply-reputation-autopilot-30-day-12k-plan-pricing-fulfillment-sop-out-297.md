4) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO API REQUIRED)
Review sources:
- Google Business Profile reviews (primary)
- Yelp reviews (secondary)
Capture method (week 1):
- Google: GBP notifications + daily manual check (AM/PM). Optional: client forwards email alerts.
- Yelp: daily manual check. If Business Owner access granted, we monitor directly; otherwise client forwards new review notifications or screenshots.

Posting method (week 1 constraints):
- Google: Preferred = GBP Manager access to post directly. Fallback = we send copy-paste reply text for client to post.
- Yelp: Default = client-post workflow (we draft, client posts). If Yelp Business Owner access granted, we can post directly.

SLA + approval rules:
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-approved responses unless flagged by banned topics.
- 1–3★ reviews: always escalated + require client approval unless client explicitly opts into “auto-post with safe template.”
- Keyword flags: legal/medical claims, refunds, discrimination, threats, HIPAA/PHI triggers, profanity, or named employees → require client approval.

Escalation rules (negative reviews):
- Create an escalation ticket within 4 business hours of detection.
- Ticket contains: review text, star rating, proposed response, recommended offline action, who should contact customer, and deadline.
- “Resolution” tracked when: customer contacted + internal action recorded (refund/redo/apology/clarification).

Quality control (brand safety):
- No admissions of fault unless client-approved.
- No incentives offered for reviews.
- No confidential info.
- Always move to offline channel for complaints.

Weekly KPI report cadence:
- Send every Monday by 12pm local time.
- Contents: New reviews, average rating, rating trend vs prior week, response rate, median response time, negative reviews count, escalations opened/closed, top 3 themes, recommended operational fix.
