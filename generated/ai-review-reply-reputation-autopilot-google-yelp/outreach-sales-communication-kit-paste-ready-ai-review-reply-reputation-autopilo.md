Goal: qualify fast → show outcome → propose pilot → close next step.

Opening (1 min)
• “Thanks for your time. In 15 minutes we’ll confirm fit, show how the review autopilot works, and decide whether to start a 7-day pilot.”

Discovery (5–7 min)
Ask:
1) “How many locations do you have?”
2) “Where do reviews matter most for you—Google, Yelp, both?”
3) “Roughly how many reviews/month do you get?”
4) “Who replies today, and what’s the typical response time?”
5) “What happens internally when you get a 1-star review?”
6) “Any compliance constraints? (HIPAA, legal, regulated claims)”
7) “What’s the cost of a missed lead / canceled appointment for you?”

Problem framing (1 min)
• “Most owners are busy; replies get delayed; negatives sit unanswered; and the tone varies. That hurts conversion and trust.”

Solution overview (3–4 min)
• “We monitor daily, draft brand-safe replies, and either (a) you approve/post, or (b) we post directly with manager access. Negatives trigger immediate escalation. You get a weekly KPI report so you can see response time + rating trend.”

Proof (1–2 min)

Offer + Pilot close (3–4 min)
