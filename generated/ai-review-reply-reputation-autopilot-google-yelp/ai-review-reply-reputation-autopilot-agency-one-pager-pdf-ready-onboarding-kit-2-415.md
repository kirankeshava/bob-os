Case Study #1 — Home Services (HVAC) | “From 6-day response lag to same-day replies”
Problem:
A multi-tech HVAC company was averaging nearly a week to respond to Google reviews. Negative reviews sat unanswered, and positive reviews weren’t leveraged to reinforce trust.
What we did:
• Implemented daily monitoring + brand-voice tuned reply templates
• Set escalation rules for 1-star reviews (approval required)
• Added weekly KPI reporting (new reviews, response time, recurring issues)
Results (first 30 days):
• Response time improved from ~6 days → < 24 hours
• 100% of new negative reviews escalated within hours (no missed “fires”)
• More consistent, professional tone across all replies
Proof placeholders:
• Screenshot: response time before/after
• Count of reviews responded to + date range

Case Study #2 — Med Spa | “De-escalation + rating lift narrative”
Problem:
A med spa received several low-star reviews related to scheduling and expectations. The owner didn’t want defensive replies, but silence was hurting trust.
What we did:
• Established a calm, premium tone guide + “never admit fault” guardrails
• Drafted empathetic replies with a private-resolution path
• Added severity tagging: public reply + immediate internal escalation
Results (60–90 day narrative):
• Faster replies reduced public back-and-forth and improved perceived accountability
• Themes identified (scheduling bottlenecks) → operational changes
• Rating trend stabilized and began lifting as new positive reviews came in (client reported improved inquiry-to-book confidence)
