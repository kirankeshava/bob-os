## Human-in-the-loop fulfillment SOP (minimum viable ops)
1) **Ingest**: watch AgentMail inbox (**agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to**) for forwarded review notifications.
2) **Triage + tag** (manual or rule-based): platform, location, star rating (if present), sentiment (pos/neutral/neg), urgency.
3) **Escalate immediately** if any:
   - <3 stars, strong negative sentiment, or keywords: refund, lawsuit/legal, health/safety, discrimination, scam, BBB, attorney.
   - Send escalation email to client escalation contact with: summary, suggested next step, and draft response options.
4) **Draft response**: produce 1 brand-safe reply. For negatives: acknowledge, apologize (where appropriate), take offline, no admissions of liability.
5) **Approval**: email the draft to the client with subject: "APPROVE/EDIT needed — {{Location}} {{Platform}} {{Stars}}★". Client replies:
   - APPROVE (post as-is)
   - EDIT: (their edit notes)
   - REJECT (we draft a new version)
6) **Post link-out**: once approved, send the exact reply + direct instructions/link to post on Google Business Profile / Yelp (client posts, avoiding API risk).
7) **Log**: record timestamps (received, drafted, approved), final text, and whether escalated.
8) **Weekly KPI email** (every 7 days):
   - Total reviews by platform/location
   - Avg rating and change vs prior week
   - Response time (avg/median) and % within SLA
   - Negative share and escalation count
   - Oldest unresponded review age

This SOP is enough to sell and fulfill the free pilot immediately while product automation catches up.
