=== 8) Objection Handling (copy blocks) ===

Objection: “We already reply sometimes.”
“Totally. The difference is consistency + speed + escalation. Customers judge trust by whether replies are timely and on-brand. We make it automatic, and we handle negatives with a tight approval loop so nothing risky gets posted.”

Objection: “I don’t want AI posting something wrong.”
“We can run ‘draft-only’ by default. Nothing gets posted without your approval for negatives (and if you want, for all reviews). The system is brand-safe, and our escalation rules prevent surprises.”

Objection: “We don’t get many reviews.”
“That’s perfect—this is light-touch. Even small volume benefits because every review is visible for years. Plus the weekly KPI report gives you a simple operational rhythm.”

Objection: “What’s the ROI?”
“When prospects compare 2–3 businesses with similar ratings, the one that responds consistently feels safer. The goal is to increase conversions from profile views to calls/bookings and reduce churn from unresolved complaints.”
