## 1) Segmented Prospecting Plan (what to target first)
**Verticals (initial):** Dentists, Med Spas/Aesthetic Clinics, HVAC/Plumbing (home services)

### Segments + Priority
Use these simple rules when you’re pulling Google Business Profile (GBP) data:
- **Not Responding:** in last 10 reviews, owner/management replies in **≤ 2** (≤20%).
- **Low Rating:** Google rating **< 4.2**.
- **High Volume:** review_count **≥ 200** OR last_review_date **≤ 14 days**.

**Priority scoring (routing):**
- **Priority A:** (Not Responding AND High Volume) OR (Low Rating AND High Volume)
- **Priority B:** Not Responding OR Low Rating
- **Priority C:** High Volume only

### Daily quotas (starting point)
When sending 50/day (ramp up later):
- 25/day Priority A (biggest pain, fastest ROI)
- 15/day Priority B
- 10/day Priority C

### Offer positioning by segment
- **Not Responding:** “We respond within 12 hours so you don’t lose the next call.”
- **Low Rating:** “We triage negatives, escalate instantly, and help you win back trust—brand-safe.”
- **High Volume:** “We take review ops off your plate; consistent voice; weekly KPI report.”

### CTA (low friction)
Primary CTA for all: **“Want me to draft 2 responses from your latest reviews so you can see the tone?”**
Backup CTA: **“Who handles your Google/Yelp review replies?”**

## 4) Daily Sending Ops Checklist + CRM Stages
### 4.1 CRM stages (simple pipeline)
1) **Prospect (Not Contacted)** — validated biz + contact
2) **Sent (Step 1)**
3) **Follow-up Due** — auto task at +2 days and +6 days
4) **Replied — Positive**
5) **Replied — Objection** (handle + move)
6) **Qualified** (has GBP/Yelp access, cares about reviews, decision maker)
7) **Demo Booked**
8) **Trial / Pilot**
9) **Paid**
10) **Lost / Not Now** (tag reason)

### 4.2 14-day sending ramp (per inbox)
- Days 1–3: 15/day
- Days 4–6: 25/day
- Days 7–10: 40/day
- Days 11–14: 60/day (only if bounce <3% and complaints ~0)

### 4.3 Daily workflow (60–90 minutes)
1) Add 25–50 new prospects to CRM (Priority A first)
2) QA: confirm category fit + website exists + last review date captured
3) Send step-1 emails
4) Process replies twice/day (AM/PM). SLA: respond within 4 business hours
5) Book calls + move stages immediately

### 4.4 Quality + deliverability thresholds
- Hard bounce: **stop list source** if >3% in a batch
- Spam/complaints: **pause sending** if any complaint trend appears
- Personalization safety: quote only short snippets; if sensitive, paraphrase (never reveal medical/legal specifics; never mention protected attributes)

### 4.5 KPIs to track weekly
- Deliverability: bounce %, spam/complaint count
- Topline: sent, open (if tracked), reply rate, positive reply rate
- Funnel: demos booked, trials, paid conversions
- Segment performance: Priority A vs B vs C reply rate
