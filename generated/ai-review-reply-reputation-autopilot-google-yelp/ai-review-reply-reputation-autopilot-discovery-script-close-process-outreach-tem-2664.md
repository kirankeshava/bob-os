# 5) 48-Hour Onboarding SOP (Operational Checklist)

**SLA promise:** From payment + access received → go-live within 48 hours.

## T+0 to T+2 hours: Confirm + Intake
- Confirm plan, locations, platforms (Google/Yelp)
- Collect escalation contacts (primary + backup)
- Collect brand voice:
  - Desired tone (warm/professional/funny/brief)
  - Words to use (“Thanks for choosing us…”) + words to avoid
  - Offers/policies allowed (refunds, discounts, rebook requests)
  - Topics to always escalate (legal threats, health claims, harassment, staff discipline)

## T+2 to T+24: Access + Safety Setup
**Google Business Profile:**
- Request Manager access via their GBP user management
- Verify we can see and respond to reviews for each location

**Yelp:**
- If they have Yelp for Business, request appropriate access or proceed draft-only

**Approval Mode selection:**
- Mode A: Auto-post 4–5 star only; negatives require approval
- Mode B: Post-approval for all replies
- Mode C: Manual approval only (drafts sent to client)

**Escalation triggers (hard rules):**
- 1–2 star reviews
- Any mention of: “lawsuit”, “attorney”, “BBB”, “fraud”, “scam”
- Medical/legal advice requests
- Personal data (phone/email/address)
- Discrimination/harassment claims

## T+24 to T+48: Go-Live QA
- Draft 10 sample responses from their recent reviews in the chosen tone
- Get approval (if in approval mode B/C)
- Turn on posting workflow
- Send “Go-live confirmation” email + weekly report schedule
