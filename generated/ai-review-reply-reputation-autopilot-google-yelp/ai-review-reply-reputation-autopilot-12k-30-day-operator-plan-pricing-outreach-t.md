Posting methods (choose per client based on access):

- Client adds us as manager to GBP.
- Yelp: client adds business user access.
B) Fallback (no access): “Draft + Client Posts”
- We draft responses daily and send a copy/paste queue to client via email.

Response workflow (daily cadence):
1) Capture new reviews
- Manual check 1–2x/day OR client forwards email alerts.
2) Draft replies
- Use brand voice rules + safe templates + constraints.
3) QA check (human-in-the-loop)
- Check: no incentives, no admissions of fault, no sensitive/medical/legal promises, no prohibited language.
4) Approval rules
- Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged by keywords.
- Require approval for 1–3★ OR any flagged keyword category (refund, lawsuit, injury, discrimination, HIPAA/medical claims, threats, profanity).
5) Post (or send to client)
- Post via account access, or send daily queue for client posting.

Escalation rules
- Negative review (≤3★) triggers escalation ticket within 12 business hours.
- Ticket includes: review text, recommended response, recommended offline resolution script, and owner escalation contact.
- Track status: Open → In progress → Resolved.

Explicit exclusions (risk control)
- No review gating, no incentivizing reviews, no “we’ll pay you” language.
- No guarantees of removing reviews.
- No disputing reviews unless client explicitly authorizes and provides required details.
