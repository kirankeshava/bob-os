SLA definitions:

- DFY Growth: respond within 24 business hours.
- DFY Pro / Agency Pro: respond within 12 business hours.

Approval rules (brand safety):
- Auto-approve: 4–5★ reviews unless flagged by keywords.
- Requires approval: 1–3★ reviews; any mention of injury, refund demand, discrimination, legality, medical claims, HIPAA/PHI, minors, threats, or “scam/fraud.”
- If unsure: do not post; escalate.

Escalation rules:
- Trigger: 1–2★ review OR 3★ with negative sentiment OR any sensitive keyword.
- Action: create an escalation ticket with (a) review text, (b) recommended public reply, (c) private resolution suggestion, (d) question list for owner.
- Target: owner response within 24h; if no response, post a neutral holding reply (pre-approved) acknowledging and inviting offline contact.

What is explicitly NOT included (to avoid platform violations):
- Review gating, incentivizing reviews, offering discounts for reviews
- Guarantee of review removal
- Disputing/flagging reviews without owner’s explicit instruction
