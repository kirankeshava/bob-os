- Collect: business details, location(s), key services, brand voice (friendly/professional), do-not-say list, approved offers/discount rules, staff escalation contacts, hours, and sensitive topics.
- Configure: review alerting (email notifications from GBP/Yelp) + shared tracking sheet.

2) Daily review capture (Mon–Fri)
- Check new reviews (GBP/Yelp dashboards or alerts).
- Log each review in tracker: platform, star rating, reviewer name, text, category/theme.

3) Draft response
- Generate brand-safe reply aligned to policies.
- Tag if “Sensitive” (medical/legal/financial), “Service recovery needed”, or “Potential fake/competitor”.

4) Approval rules (human-in-the-loop)
- 4–5★: auto-approve unless flagged by keyword/topic.
- 1–3★: requires client approval OR escalation contact approval before posting.
- Any request for refund/compensation: requires approval.

5) Post response
- Post directly if access is granted; otherwise send client copy/paste block with subject line: “Review reply ready to post (Platform / Reviewer / Date)”.

6) Escalation workflow
- Create escalation ticket when 1–2★ OR sensitive category OR explicit request for callback/refund.
- SLA: escalation sent within 12 business hours; client acknowledges within 24 business hours.
- Track resolution outcome (contacted, resolved, pending).

7) Weekly KPI reporting (every Monday)
- KPIs: new reviews (#), average rating, rating trend vs last week, response rate %, median response time, negative reviews count, escalations opened/resolved, top 3 themes, suggested action.
