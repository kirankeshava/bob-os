5) SAMPLE RESPONSE PACK (USE AS PROOF)
========================

Positive review examples (short, warm)
1) “Thanks so much, [Name]! We’re glad you had a great experience. If there’s ever anything we can do better, please let us know—looking forward to seeing you again.”
2) “We appreciate you taking the time to share this. Our team will be thrilled to hear it—thank you for choosing us!”
3) “That means a lot, [Name]. Thanks for the kind words and for trusting us. See you next time!”
4) “Thanks for the 5-star review! We’re happy we could help and we’ll be here whenever you need us.”
5) “We really appreciate your feedback. Our goal is always clear communication and great results—thanks again!”

Neutral review examples (address concern, invite offline)
1) “Thanks for the feedback, [Name]. We’re glad parts of your visit went well, and we’d love to learn what we could do to make it a 5-star experience. Please contact [email/phone] so we can follow up.”
2) “We appreciate you sharing this. We’re reviewing your notes with the team and would like to make it right—please reach out so we can help.”
3) “Thank you, [Name]. We’re sorry we missed the mark in any way. If you’re open to it, please message us with details so we can improve.”

Negative review examples (calm, non-defensive, policy-safe)
1) “Hi [Name], we’re sorry to hear this was your experience. We take feedback seriously and would like to look into what happened. Please contact us at [email/phone] with the date/time of your visit so we can help resolve this.”
2) “Hi [Name]—thank you for bringing this to our attention. This isn’t the standard we aim for. We’d like to make it right; please reach out to [contact] so we can review the details and find a solution.”
3) “Hi [Name], we understand your frustration and we’re sorry. To protect your privacy we can’t discuss details here, but we want to help. Please contact [contact] and we’ll address this promptly.”
4) “Hi [Name], we appreciate the feedback. We’re reviewing this internally and would like the chance to follow up directly. Please email [email] with any details you’re comfortable sharing.”
5) “Hi [Name], we’re sorry you felt let down. We aim for respectful service and clear expectations. Please contact [contact] so we can investigate and work toward a resolution.”
