## 7) Onboarding SOP (agency partner)
**Step 1 — Confirm scope (10 minutes)**
- Platforms: Google, Yelp, or both
- Draft-only vs draft+posting
- Response window requirements

**Step 2 — Collect brand voice inputs (15 minutes)**
- Tone: friendly/professional/playful
- Phrases to use / avoid
- Escalation contacts and thresholds
- Competitor mentions, sensitive topics, disclaimers

**Step 3 — Access & routing**
- Add us as user/manager where applicable (or provide exports)
- Choose review intake method: email forwarding, shared inbox, or tool export

**Step 4 — Go live**
- We respond to new reviews within SLA
- Negative reviews get escalated with a recommended response and next step

## 8) Escalation rules (default)
Escalate if any of the following are true:
- Rating is **1–2 stars**
- Mentions: safety, discrimination, illegal activity, refunds/chargebacks, harassment
- Mentions staff by name with accusation
- Threatens lawsuit / regulatory complaint

**Escalation message includes:** review text, suggested reply, recommended offline action, urgency.
