## 3) Outreach: who to target (fastest to close)
Primary targets (high review volume + high sensitivity to ratings):
- Med spas, dental, chiropractic, urgent care (reputation directly drives bookings)
- Home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing) (local pack + lead conversion)
- Restaurants (high review velocity; owners rarely reply consistently)

Simple qualification from public data:
- 3.5–4.6 star rating OR rating recently dipping
- 10+ reviews in the last 30 days (or 30+ in last 90 days)
- Visible gaps: many reviews unreplied-to, especially negative ones
