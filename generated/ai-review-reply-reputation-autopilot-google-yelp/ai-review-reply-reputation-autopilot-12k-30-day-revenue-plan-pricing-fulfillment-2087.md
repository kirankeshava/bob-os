# 3) Fulfillment Scope (Week-1 deliverable; no API dependency)
## Review Sources
- **Google Business Profile (GBP)** reviews
- **Yelp** reviews

## Response SLA & Cadence
- Check reviews **daily (Mon–Fri)** at minimum
- Draft responses within SLA (12h/24h depending on plan)
- Post after approval rules below

## Brand Safety Rules
- Maintain a **brand voice profile**: tone, do/don’t phrases, offers allowed, refund policy language
- Never promise outcomes; avoid regulated claims (medical/legal/financial)
- No incentives for reviews; no “review gating”

## Approval Rules (human-in-the-loop)
- **Auto-approve posting** for **4–5★** unless flagged by banned keywords
- **Approval required** for **1–3★** and any review mentioning: lawsuits, injury, discrimination, harassment, HIPAA/medical specifics, threats, chargebacks

## Escalation Rules (negative reviews)
Create an escalation ticket when:
- 1–2★ OR explicit refund demand OR safety/legal/regulatory language
Ticket includes: review link, proposed reply, recommended resolution path, owner contact, due-by timestamp.

## Posting Method (API constraints handled)
- **Preferred:** client grants **GBP Manager** access and (if possible) **Yelp Business Owner** access.
- **Default Yelp workaround (week 1):** We draft; client posts (copy/paste) via templated message. This reduces onboarding friction and avoids API dependence.

## Weekly KPI Report (every Friday)
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating & trend (7/30 days)
- Response rate %
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count
- Escalations opened vs resolved
- Top 3 recurring themes + suggested operational fixes
