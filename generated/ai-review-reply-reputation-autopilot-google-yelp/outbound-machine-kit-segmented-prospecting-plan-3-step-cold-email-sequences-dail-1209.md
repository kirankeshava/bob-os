A) SEGMENTED PROSPECTING PLAN (who to target, how to prioritize)

Verticals (start with 2 lanes, keep 3rd as overflow):
1) Dentists (general + cosmetic + orthodontics) — high trust, high review impact on patient flow
2) Med spas / aesthetics — very review-driven, frequent review velocity, premium pricing
3) HVAC/Plumbing — high lead sensitivity to rating + responsiveness; many inbound calls from Maps

Core segments (tag each lead with one primary segment; allow secondary notes):
1) NOT RESPONDING
- Definition: In the last 10 Google reviews, owner responses <= 2 (<=20%).
- Hook angle: “you’re getting reviews but leaving revenue on the table by not replying.”
- Offer angle: “we respond within 12 hours; brand-safe; you approve.”

2) LOW RATING
- Definition: Google rating < 4.2 OR repeated complaints in recent reviews.
- Hook angle: “fast, calm, policy-safe responses + escalation for unhappy customers.”
- Offer angle: “negative review escalation + resolution workflow; weekly KPI reporting.”

3) HIGH VOLUME
- Definition: Review count >= 200 OR last review within 14 days AND steady inflow.
- Hook angle: “ops problem: too many reviews to handle consistently.”
- Offer angle: “autopilot queue + approval; consistent tone; weekly KPI report.”

Priority scoring rubric (for routing + daily send order):
- Priority A: (NOT RESPONDING AND HIGH VOLUME) OR (LOW RATING AND HIGH VOLUME)
- Priority B: NOT RESPONDING only OR LOW RATING only
- Priority C: HIGH VOLUME only

Daily outbound mix (recommended, once list exists):
- 60% Priority A, 30% Priority B, 10% Priority C
- Within each day: 40% dentists, 40% med spas, 20% home services (adjust based on replies)

Personalization requirements (minimum viable personalization):
- Pull 1 recent review snippet (1 sentence) OR paraphrase to avoid quoting sensitive info.
- Note response gap: “no owner response” or “last response was X months ago” (from the last 10 reviews).
- Mention category/context: dental/med spa/HVAC to sound native.

1) Choose today’s segment mix (Priority A first).
2) Pull 50–100 leads into “Today – Send” list.
3) QA 10% sample: correct category, real local business (not directory/franchise HQ), website present, location matches.
4) Personalize: add {{recent_review_snippet}} + {{response_gap}} (minimum viable personalization).
5) Validate emails (at least syntax + domain exists; remove role emails if possible).

Send + monitor
6) Send within daily cap (see 14-day ramp).

8) Reply SLA: respond to replies within 2 business hours (same day).

Follow-ups
9) Auto/Manual send follow-up #1 to non-responders on day 2–3.
10) Send follow-up #2 on day 6–8.

Post-flight (10 min)
11) Log outcomes in CRM: Replied/Qualified/Demo/Not now/Unsubscribe.
12) Note objections for copy iteration.
