### Review Sources

### Response SLA
- Growth/Agency: **within 24 business hours**
- Pro: **within 12 business hours**

### Brand Safety / Policy
- No incentivization or “review gating” language.
- No promises of outcomes; no medical/legal claims.

### Escalation Rules (auto-ticket)
Escalate immediately if any:
- **1–3★** rating
- Mentions: “lawsuit”, “refund”, “chargeback”, “scam”, “BBB”, “attorney”, “HIPAA”, “injury”, “discrimination”, “police”, “fraud”
- Allegations of safety issues, harassment, pricing deception
- Competitor sabotage suspicion

**Escalation SLA:** acknowledge internally same day; client decision requested within 24h; final public response posted after approval.

### Posting Method (no API dependency)
- **Google:** require **GBP Manager access** (preferred) so we can post directly.
- **Yelp:** either (a) Yelp Business Owner access, OR (b) **client-post workflow**: we draft + send paste-ready responses; client posts.

## 5) Fulfillment SOP Outline (simple, repeatable)
1) **Onboarding (Day 0–1)**
   - Collect: business details, brand voice, do/don’t phrases, offers/guarantees, escalation contacts, competitors, operating hours.
   - Access: GBP manager invite; Yelp access or confirm client-post workflow.

2) **Daily review capture (Mon–Fri)**
   - Check new reviews (manual or email alerts)
   - Log into tracker: date, platform, star rating, reviewer name, review text, location.

3) **Draft responses**
   - Generate response using brand voice rules + templates
   - Include: gratitude, specific reference, resolution path, offline contact method

4) **QA + Compliance pass**
   - Remove restricted claims, promotional language that violates platform policy
   - Flag escalations

5) **Approval logic**
   - Auto-post 4–5★ unless flagged (sensitive keywords)
   - Require approval for 1–3★, or any flagged content

6) **Post + record**
   - Post directly where access exists
   - If client-post: send paste-ready response with 2 variations
   - Update tracker with posted timestamp to measure response time

7) **Weekly KPI report (every Friday)**
   - New reviews count (by platform)
   - Avg rating + trend
   - Response rate
   - Median response time
   - Negative reviews (count) + resolved/escalated
   - Top themes (3 bullets) + recommended ops fix (1 bullet)
