=== A) TARGETING (WHO TO MESSAGE FIRST) ===
Prioritize local businesses where reviews directly drive calls/appointments:
1) Dental, medspa, chiropractor/PT, urgent care
2) Home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, pest)
3) Auto repair/detailing/tire shops
4) Restaurants with high volume (only if they have dedicated manager)

Fast-close filters (pick prospects that “feel the pain”):
- Rating: 3.5–4.6 (room to lift; already getting customers)
- Review velocity: 8+ reviews/month OR any recent negative review in last 14 days
- Response gap: owner replies to <30% of recent reviews OR replies are inconsistent/low quality
- Multi-location: 2+ locations OR franchise signals (easy expansion)

Deliverable: A free mini-audit of 25 most recent reviews + 10 drafted responses + 3 negative-review response templates.
