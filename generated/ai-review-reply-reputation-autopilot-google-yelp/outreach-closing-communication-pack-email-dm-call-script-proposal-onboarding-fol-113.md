3) BOOKING + DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (10–12 MIN)
=============================

Goal: close to a 7-day pilot or first-month start.

0:00–1:00 — Set frame
“Thanks for the time. In 10 minutes I’ll understand your review volume + any risk/compliance needs, then I’ll tell you if we can get you to consistent replies within 24 hours and what the pilot looks like.”

1:00–3:00 — Current state
1) “Who currently replies to Google reviews? Yelp too?”
2) “How often do reviews sit unanswered?”
3) “Any categories you worry about? (refunds, staff complaints, health info, legal threats)”

3:00–5:00 — Impact + goal
1) “Do reviews directly affect calls/bookings for you?”
2) “If you could wave a wand, what would be true in 30 days? Faster replies? fewer escalations? better tone consistency?”

5:00–7:00 — Pitch (tight)
“What we do: brand-safe responses drafted daily for GBP + Yelp, with rules. Positives get fast replies. Anything negative/sensitive gets escalated to your approval contact. You also get a weekly report: response rate, avg response time, star distribution, and negative themes.”
(Reference legitimacy)
“You can see the overview here if helpful: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/1”

7:00–9:00 — Close
“Two ways to start:
A) 7-day pilot: we draft responses for all new reviews; you approve/post (or we post with manager access).
B) Ongoing monthly: same, plus weekly KPI report and ongoing tuning.”

Ask:
“Which is easier—pilot first, or start monthly right away?”

9:00–11:00 — Objections
Objection: “We already reply sometimes.”
Response: “Totally. The win is consistency + speed + brand tone. Most ratings improvements come from not letting negatives sit and from responding to ‘middle’ reviews quickly. We just systematize it.”

Objection: “I don’t want AI posting.”
Response: “No problem—start draft-only. You approve/post. If you later want us to post, we use manager access and an escalation rule set so anything risky never auto-posts.”

Objection: “How do you handle bad reviews?”
Response: “We acknowledge, de-escalate, and move offline. For 1–2 star, we escalate to your contact for approval if you want. Nothing confrontational, nothing that violates policy.”

11:00–12:00 — Next steps
“Great. I’ll send a short onboarding link/checklist. Once we have access, first drafts go out within 24 hours.”
