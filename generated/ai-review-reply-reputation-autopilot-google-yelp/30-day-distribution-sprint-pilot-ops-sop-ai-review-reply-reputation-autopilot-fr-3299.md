- Prioritize businesses with: (a) 10+ reviews, (b) rating 3.2–4.6, (c) recent reviews in last 14 days, (d) obvious lack of owner replies.
- Find owner/manager email via website contact page, Facebook page, or Instagram bio. If only a form exists, use it.

B) ONBOARDING (PILOT) CHECKLIST (multi-location from day 1)
1) Intake (email questions; copy/paste):
- Business name + website
- Locations (name + address + Google Maps link for each)
- Platforms (Google, Yelp)
- Brand voice: friendly/professional/funny/clinical; do’s/don’ts; banned phrases
- Escalation contact(s): name + email + phone (optional)
- SLA preference: 4h / 8h / next business day

2) Forwarding setup:
- Ask client to forward review notification emails to: agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to
- If they have multiple locations, they must forward from each location’s notification inbox or CC us.
- Location mapping: ask them to include location name in the forward subject (e.g., “FWD: New Google review – Downtown”).

3) Operating cadence during pilot:
- New review arrives → we draft within SLA → send approve/edit email with suggested reply text + direct link to post.
- If negative/risky: immediate “Escalation Alert” email.
- End of week: send Weekly KPI report email.

C) ESCALATION RULES (what triggers “Escalation Alert”)
Trigger if any:
- Star rating <= 3
- Sentiment = negative (rule-based + LLM-assisted)
- Keywords: refund, lawsuit, lawyer, legal, discrimination, racist, fraud, scam, BBB, chargeback, mold, roaches, food poisoning, infection, injury, police, harassment
Action:
- Send escalation email to escalation contact + primary owner email.
- Provide recommended response options (de-escalation + invite offline).
