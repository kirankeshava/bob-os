- Client (or staff) pastes and posts. We track compliance in weekly KPI report.

Brand safety rules (always on):
- Never admit fault/liability; never discuss refunds publicly; never request removal publicly; never share private health/financial info.
- Move sensitive conversations offline: “Please contact [phone/email] so we can make this right.”
- No review gating, incentives, or “only if happy leave a review” language.

SLA & escalation rules:
- 4–5★ reviews: auto-approve replies unless they contain restricted keywords (legal/medical claims, injury, discrimination, threats).
- 1–3★ reviews: always escalated to client contact within 4 business hours with a proposed response + recommended next step.
- Escalation triggers: threats of legal action, accusations of fraud, safety issues, discrimination, HIPAA/PHI risk, doxxing, refund disputes.
