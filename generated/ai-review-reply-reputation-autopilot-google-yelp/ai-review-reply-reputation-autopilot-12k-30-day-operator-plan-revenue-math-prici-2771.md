3) FULFILLMENT SCOPE (WEEK-1 DELIVERABLE, NO API DEPENDENCY)
Review sources:
- Google Business Profile reviews (GBP)
- Yelp reviews

Posting methods (choose per client):
A) Preferred: Manager access
- GBP: client adds us as Manager to the GBP location
- Yelp: client adds us via Yelp Business account (if available)
B) Fallback: Client-post workflow (zero access required)
- We deliver approved reply text + posting instructions daily via email; client posts

Response SLA + cadence:
- Check reviews 1–2x/day (morning/afternoon local time)
- Draft replies within SLA (12h or 24h business hours)
- Post immediately after approval rules are satisfied

Approval rules (human-in-the-loop):
- Auto-approve (optional): 4–5★ with no sensitive keywords
- Always require approval: 1–3★, mentions of injury/medical outcomes, discrimination, legal threats, chargebacks, doxxing, staff named + allegation, refunds demanded

Escalation rules:
- Create an escalation ticket when: rating ≤3★ OR any “always require approval” keyword appears
- Ticket fields: review link, reviewer name, date, star rating, issue category, draft response, recommended next step, owner contact to route to, deadline
- Resolution tracking: resolved/unresolved; follow-up if updated review occurs

Explicit exclusions (policy):
- No review gating/incentivization (“leave us 5 stars for a discount”)
- No impersonation (responses must clearly be business)
- No deletion promises; we can suggest dispute steps but client must authorize and submit

Weekly KPI report (every Monday):
- New reviews count (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating + 4-week trend
- Response rate (responded/total)
- Median response time
- Negative reviews (1–3★) + escalations opened/closed
- Top 3 themes (service, wait time, pricing, staff, etc.)
- “Next week focus” bullets (1–3 actions)
