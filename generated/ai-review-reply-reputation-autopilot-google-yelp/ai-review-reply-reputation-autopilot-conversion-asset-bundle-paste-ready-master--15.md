1) Brand name + location(s)
2) Desired tone (choose 2–3): Warm / Professional / Concise / Playful / Luxury / Clinical

5) Sign-off preference (e.g., “—The [Brand] Team”)

- Standard (24h) or Priority (12h)
- Weekend coverage? Yes/No
- Negative escalation coverage window

- Recipient emails
- Preferred day/time
- KPIs: response rate, median response time, rating change, sentiment themes, top 3 issues, wins
