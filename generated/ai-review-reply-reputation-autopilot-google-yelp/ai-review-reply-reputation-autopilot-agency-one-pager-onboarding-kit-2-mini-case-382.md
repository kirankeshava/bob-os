## 3) TWO MINI CASE STUDIES (ANONYMIZED BEFORE/AFTER MODULES)

### Case Study #1 — Home Services (HVAC/Plumbing style)
**Problem**
A multi-tech home services company was getting steady Google reviews, but responses were inconsistent. Negative reviews sat for days, and positive reviews were mostly ignored—hurting trust with new prospects.

**What we did**
- Set brand voice to “professional, quick, accountability-first”
- Implemented 24-hour SLA for all new reviews
- Added escalation rules for billing disputes and missed appointments
- Standardized response structure: thank-you + specific service mention + next-step CTA

**Before → After (first 30 days)**
- **Median response time:** ~5–7 days → **< 24 hours**
- **Response rate:** ~20–30% → **95%+**
- **Negative review handling:** reactive/late → **same-day escalation + approved replies**

**Outcome narrative**
Customers mentioned “they actually respond” and “owner reached out” in follow-up updates. Sales staff reported fewer objections about “bad reviews” because the public thread showed fast, professional resolution.

- Screenshot: GBP Insights showing response rate
- Screenshot: sample 1-star escalation thread (redacted)

### Case Study #2 — Med Spa / Aesthetics
**Problem**
A med spa had strong service quality but variable front-desk experience created occasional 1–2 star reviews. The team avoided replying due to compliance concerns and fear of sounding defensive.

**What we did**
- Implemented a “no-argument, privacy-first” reply policy
- Created compliant templates: apologize + invite offline + no medical claims
- Added approval-only rule for anything referencing treatment outcomes
- Delivered weekly themes report (wait time, scheduling, friendliness)

**Before → After (first 60 days)**
- **Response time:** 4–10 days → **< 48 business hours**
- **Review thread tone:** defensive/inconsistent → **calm, consistent, brand-safe**
- **Rating lift narrative:** stabilized reputation; fewer prospects referencing old negatives because they saw professional responses and clear resolution pathways

**Outcome narrative**
Several reviewers updated or softened their wording after being contacted privately, while new prospects saw a consistent, high-end brand voice across every review.
