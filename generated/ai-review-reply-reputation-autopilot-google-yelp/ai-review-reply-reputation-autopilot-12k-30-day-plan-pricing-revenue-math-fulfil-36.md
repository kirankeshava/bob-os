- Yelp business reviews

Posting methods (choose based on access):
1) Preferred: We get added as a manager/user on GBP and Yelp Business Owner (lowest friction).
2) Fallback: Client-post workflow — we draft responses in a shared doc/email; client posts daily (still meets most SLA, but depends on client).

Response SLA:
- DFY Growth: respond within 24 business hours of detection
- DFY Pro: respond within 12 business hours

Escalation rules (auto-escalate if any trigger matches):
- Any 1–2★ review
- 3★ review mentioning refund/chargeback/safety/legal/compliance issues
- Mentions of discrimination, injury, fraud, theft, harassment, licensing
- Photos or names of staff + accusation

Approval policy (brand-safe + human-in-loop):
- Auto-approve to post (no client approval required) for 4–5★ reviews unless flagged keywords appear.
- Require client approval before posting for 1–3★ reviews OR any flagged keywords.
- If no approval within 1 business day: post a neutral “we’d like to learn more, please contact us” holding reply (if client allows) OR keep draft queued.

Deliverables each week:
- Weekly KPI report: new reviews, average rating trend, response rate %, median response time, negative review count, escalations opened/closed, top themes, and 1–3 recommended operational fixes.

Explicit exclusions (protects deliverability + compliance):
- No incentivizing reviews, no “review gating” (asking only happy customers), no fake reviews.
- No promises to remove reviews.
- We will not dispute or contact platform support as the business without written authorization.
