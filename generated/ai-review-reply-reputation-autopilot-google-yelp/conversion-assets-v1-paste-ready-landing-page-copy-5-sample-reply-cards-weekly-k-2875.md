**Escalation #1 (Google, 2★):** Waited 40 minutes past appointment.
- Public reply posted: apology + request to contact manager.
- Recommended internal action: confirm overbooking policy; add proactive text update when running >15 minutes behind.
- Private outreach template prepared: offer a call to understand context and prevent repeat.

**Escalation #2 (Yelp, 1★):** “Didn’t feel listened to.”
- Public reply posted: privacy-safe + invite offline.
- Recommended internal action: front-desk intake checklist; confirm expectations at start.

## Compliance / Brand Safety Check
- No incentives offered
- No personal/medical details referenced
- Neutral tone maintained; no blame or admissions of fault
- Offline resolution offered for sensitive complaints

3) Add a billing clarity template: explain estimates and invite invoice review
4) Track if wait-time mentions drop from 2 → 0–1 mentions
