## 1) Segmented Prospecting Plan (Who / Why / What to say)
### Target verticals (direct-to-local)
1) **Dentists** (high LTV, high sensitivity to reputation)
2) **Med spas / aesthetic clinics** (high review velocity, high competition)
3) **HVAC + Plumbing** (urgent purchase cycles; review responsiveness influences calls)

### Agency lane (reseller)
- **Local SEO agencies**, **web design shops**, **reputation management boutiques**, **PPC agencies** serving the three verticals.
- Why: faster scale, multiple locations, easier multi-client expansion.

### Segments + priority rules (use these to route messaging)
- **Not Responding**: owner response rate in last ~10 reviews ≤ 20% OR no responses observed.
- **Low Rating**: Google rating < 4.2.
- **High Volume**: ≥ 200 total reviews OR last review within 14 days.

**Priority A** (send first):
- (Not Responding AND High Volume) OR (Low Rating AND High Volume)

**Priority B**:
- Not Responding OR Low Rating

**Priority C**:
- High Volume only

### Offer positioning by segment
- Not Responding: “**We respond within 12 hours** with brand-safe drafts; you approve before posting.”
- Low Rating: “**Negative-review escalation + recovery**: calm, policy-safe replies + alerts so you can fix issues fast.”
- High Volume: “**Ops relief**: keep response time low and tone consistent; weekly KPI report.”

### CTA (low friction)
- “Want me to send **2 sample replies** for your latest reviews?”
- “Open to a **10-minute walkthrough** of how the autopilot would work for your listing?”

## 4) Daily Sending Ops Checklist (14-day ramp + QA)
### List QA (before any send)
- Confirm category match (dentist/med spa/HVAC-plumbing)
- Exclude: franchises with corporate review teams, closed locations, listings with no website (optional rule)
- Capture: rating, review_count, last_review_date, and a safe review snippet
- Assign segment + Priority A/B/C
- Verify at least one contact method (email preferred; phone as fallback)

### Deliverability & volumes (generic ramp)
- Day 1–2: 10–20 emails/day/inbox
- Day 3–5: 25–40/day/inbox
- Day 6–10: 40–60/day/inbox
- Day 11–14: 60–80/day/inbox (only if bounces <3% and spam complaints ~0)

### Guardrails
- If hard bounce rate > 3%: stop, clean list.
- If spam complaints > 0.1%: stop, adjust copy/sending.
- Reply SLA: respond to positive replies within 2 hours; negatives within same day.

### Daily workflow (60–90 minutes)
1) Pull 25–50 Priority A leads + personalize line (review snippet + response gap)
2) Send Email 1
3) Process replies → update CRM stage
4) Book calls; send “2 sample replies” to warm leads
5) Queue follow-ups (day 2 and day 5–7)


## 5) CRM Stages (simple pipeline)
1) **Prospect (New)** — lead added, segmented, ready to send
2) **Sent (E1)** — first email sent
3) **Engaged** — opened/clicked OR replied with interest
4) **Qualified** — confirmed decision-maker + has review problem
5) **Demo Booked** — meeting scheduled
6) **Trial / Sample Replies Delivered** — samples sent or trial started
7) **Paid** — converted
8) **Lost / Not Now** — disqualified or deferred (add follow-up date)
