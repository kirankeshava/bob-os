## 4) Fulfillment scope (week-1 deliverable, no API required)
### Review sources covered
- Google Business Profile reviews (primary)
- Yelp reviews (secondary)

### Response SLA (business days)
- Growth/Agency Starter: **within 24 hours**
- Pro/Agency Pro: **within 12 hours**

### Escalation rules (negative review handling)
Escalate any review that is:
- **1–3 stars**
- Mentions refund, chargeback, legal threat, discrimination, HIPAA/medical/regulated topics, safety incident
- Mentions employee by name with serious allegation
- Contains profanity or personal data

Escalation deliverable:
- A short internal brief (what happened + suggested response options)
- A recommended public reply (brand-safe, de-escalatory)
- Optional private message template (Yelp) or follow-up script

### Posting method (human-in-the-loop to avoid API limits)
**Default (preferred):**
- Client grants us access:
  - Google: add us as **Manager** on Google Business Profile
  - Yelp: add access in Yelp Business Owner account (where possible)
- We draft + post responses directly in the native UI.

**Fallback (no access / week-1 friction reducer): “Client-post workflow”**
- We draft responses in a shared doc/email.
- Client (or their staff) posts using copy/paste.
- We include exact instructions + deep links to the review.

Policy constraints (important):
- We do **not** gate, suppress, or incentivize reviews in ways that violate platform policies.
- We do **not** promise removal of reviews.
- We do **not** dispute reviews on behalf of a client without explicit authorization and required platform steps.

### Weekly KPI report (delivered every Monday)
Metrics:
- New reviews (Google/Yelp)
- Average rating + trend vs prior week
- Response rate
- Median response time
- Negative reviews count (1–3★)
- Escalations opened vs resolved
- Top 3 themes (from review text)
- “Next week actions” (3 bullets)
