# 2) Onboarding SOP (internal runbook) — Activate within 48 hours

**Owner:** Sales/CS (Bob)  
**Inbox:** agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to

## Definition of “Activated Location”
A location is **activated** when:
1) Access to Google Business Profile (and Yelp if applicable) is granted OR reviews are being forwarded via an agreed workaround, and
2) Brand voice + escalation rules are confirmed, and
3) At least **5 responses** are drafted (and either approved or posted) within the first week.

## Day 0 (Immediately after verbal close)
1) **Send Welcome + Next Steps email** (within 30 minutes). Include:
   - Intake form
   - Access request instructions (GBP/Yelp)
   - Approval mode choice (autopilot vs first-10 approvals)
   - Expected go-live timeline (24–48h)
2) Create internal record:
   - Account name, # locations, channels (GBP/Yelp), approval mode, escalation contacts
3) Request any existing brand guidelines or canned responses.

## Day 1 (Access + configuration)
4) **Confirm profile links** for each location; verify correct pages.
5) **GBP access** (preferred): request they add agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to as Manager.
6) **Yelp access** (preferred): request they add agent email to Yelp Business account (if possible).
7) If direct access is blocked:
   - Workaround A: customer exports/forwards review notifications to agent inbox.
   - Workaround B: customer sends weekly CSV/screenshot batches; we return ready-to-post replies.
8) Configure:
   - Voice/tone
   - Escalation triggers
   - Offline resolution CTA (phone/email)
   - Compliance constraints

## Day 2 (First drafts + approval loop)
9) Pull last 10–20 reviews per location (or last 30 days).
10) Draft responses:
   - Prioritize 1–2 star and newest first
   - Ensure brand-safe language: apologize for experience (without admitting fault), invite offline, avoid sensitive details
11) If approval mode:
   - Send a “Sample Pack” of 10 replies (mix of 5-star + critical)
   - Ask for 1 round of edits (tone, phrasing) within 24h
12) Post or deliver final replies:
   - If autopilot: post directly
   - If approval required: post after approval or provide paste-ready responses

## First-week success criteria (what we promise internally)
- **Response SLA:** within 24–48 hours for new reviews (agreed in intake)
- **Coverage:** 90%+ of new reviews receive a response
- **Escalation:** all triggered reviews escalated within 4 business hours (email + SMS/phone if provided)
- **Baseline report:** deliver first weekly KPI report by end of Week 1

## Quality checklist (every response)
- Mentions reviewer sentiment (specific but not overly detailed)
- Thanks them for feedback
- Invites return / next step
- For negative reviews: moves to offline, no blame, no personal/medical/legal details
- Matches voice guide; no cringe/robot phrasing

## Weekly reporting SOP
Every week (same day/time):
- Gather new reviews count (GBP + Yelp)
- Response rate and median response time
- Rating trend (avg star rating change)
- Negative review count and top themes
- Escalations logged + outcomes
- 3 recommended operational fixes (staffing, policy, service issues)
Send to stakeholder list.
