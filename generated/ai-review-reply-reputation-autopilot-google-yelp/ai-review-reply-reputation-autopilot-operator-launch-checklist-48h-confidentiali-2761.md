## Day 0 (Immediately After Verbal Yes)

### 1) Send “Kickoff + Payment + Access” email (same day)
Include:
- Start date: **“We can start within 48 hours once access + intake are complete.”**
- Attach/inline: **Intake Form** (brand voice + escalation + locations)
- Include: **Access Request Emails** (GBP + Yelp) for them to forward
- Confirm: weekly KPI report cadence (email)
- Confirm: where to send urgent escalations (email/SMS/phone)

**Operator check:** Ensure customer has the legitimacy URL + support inbox in the footer.

### 2) Payment (before work begins)
- Send invoice/Stripe link (or your standard payment collection method).  
- **Rule:** No posting/replying on their public profiles until payment clears (post-week-1 policy; during free launch week you can run as a 7-day free trial but still collect authorization intent).

### 3) Create internal customer record
- Add to CRM sheet: Company, location(s), decision maker, date closed, start date, access status, escalation contacts.
- Create internal folder (Drive/Notion/etc.):
  - “Brand Voice” notes
  - “Escalation Rules”
  - “Approvals”
  - “Weekly Reports”

## Day 1 (Within 24 Hours)

### 4) Intake completed + confirmation
- Confirm intake received.
- If incomplete, reply with **3 bullets** max of what’s missing:
  1) Brand voice (examples)
  2) Escalation contact + threshold
  3) Locations + links

### 5) Access obtained (GBP + Yelp)
**Google Business Profile (GBP):**
- Confirm you have the correct role (ideally **Manager** or equivalent).
- Verify you can view/respond to reviews for each location.

**Yelp:**
- Confirm access to Business Owner account/messages/reviews.
- Verify you can draft/respond per Yelp workflow.

**Operator note:** If customer uses an agency, request they grant access rather than sharing passwords.

### 6) Baseline capture (before any changes)
Capture baseline metrics for each location:
- Star rating
- Total review count
- Unanswered reviews count (last 90 days)
- Avg response time (if visible) or estimate backlog age
- Negative review backlog count (≤3 stars)

Store in the customer’s “Week 0 Baseline”.

## Day 2 (Within 48 Hours)

### 7) Sample approvals (brand safety gate)
Send 3 sample drafts for approval:
- 1 positive review reply
- 1 neutral/short reply
- 1 negative review reply + de-escalation language

Ask for approval on:
- Tone (warm/professional/playful)
- Use of names/signature
- Offer language (refund/discount/invite to call)

**Approval rule:** Customer approves once; after that, you operate within the approved style guide.

### 8) Go-live actions
- Start replying to new reviews within SLA (e.g., **<24 hours** on business days).
- Work backlog in prioritized order:
  1) 1–3 star newest first
  2) 4–5 star newest first
  3) Older backlog if customer wants it

### 9) Escalation workflow is active
- If a negative review matches escalation rules, send escalation note to customer contact:
  - Review link + screenshot
  - Suggested response draft
  - Suggested remediation option(s)
  - Deadline for response (e.g., “Recommend posting within 12 hours”)

## First-Week Success Criteria (What “Good” Looks Like)
- **Response SLA met:** 90%+ of new reviews responded to within 24 business hours
- **Backlog reduction:** all ≤3-star reviews from last 30–90 days addressed (as permitted)
- **Escalation handled:** negative reviews escalated within same business day
- **Weekly KPI report delivered:** baseline + week-1 deltas
