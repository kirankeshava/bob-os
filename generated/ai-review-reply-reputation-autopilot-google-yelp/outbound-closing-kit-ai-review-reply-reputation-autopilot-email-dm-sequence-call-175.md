E) Objection Handlers (short, direct)
====================
1) “We already reply sometimes.”
“Totally—this just makes it consistent and fast. The biggest lift comes from same-day responses and never letting 1–2★ sit unanswered. We’ll keep your voice and remove the daily burden.”

2) “I don’t want robotic AI replies.”
“Agreed. We use AI to draft, but the system is trained on your tone rules and past examples. The output is human-sounding and specific to each review. You can also require approval for negatives.”

3) “Can you remove bad reviews?”
“We don’t promise removals. What we do is respond correctly, de-escalate, and document issues. If a review violates platform policy, we can flag it, but outcomes depend on Google/Yelp.”

4) “I’m worried about posting without approval.”
“No problem—start draft-only. For 1–2★, we can always require approval before anything is posted, and route escalation to your chosen contact.”

5) “What does it cost?”
“We price per location and review volume band with clear caps and SLAs. If you tell me your approximate monthly review volume and number of locations, I’ll recommend the simplest tier and we can start with a short pilot.”
