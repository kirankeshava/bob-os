3) Discovery call (20 minutes) — “close to paid” structure
Goal: confirm pain + volume + risk tolerance + access + decision-maker, then offer two plans and collect Setup + Month 1 upfront.

Minute 0–2: opener
“Thanks for taking this—goal is to see if we can (1) increase response rate + speed, (2) protect rating by escalating negatives fast, and (3) save you time. If it fits, I’ll recommend a plan and we can get you live within 48 hours.”

Minute 2–8: diagnosis questions (revenue impact)
- How many reviews/month do you typically get across Google + Yelp?
- Who responds today, and how quickly (same day, within a week, never)?
- What happens when a negative review hits—who handles it, and how fast?
- Do you track calls/appointments from Google Business Profile?
- If your rating moved +0.2–0.4 in the next 60–90 days, what would that be worth in bookings?

Minute 8–12: qualification gates
- Are you the decision maker for this?
- Do you control Google Business Profile and Yelp access (or can you add us as manager)?
- Any compliance constraints (medical/legal/financing claims) or topics to avoid?

Minute 12–16: solution + safety proof
“We don’t just ‘let AI post.’ We use a brand-voice library + prohibited topics list + escalation triggers. You choose approval mode:
1) Manual approval (you approve every reply)
2) Auto for positives / approval for negatives
3) Full autopilot with escalation alerts
We also keep an audit log and weekly KPI report.”

Minute 16–20: close
“Based on your volume, I’d recommend either {{PlanA}} or {{PlanB}}. Setup is {{SetupFee}} and month one is {{Monthly}}—we collect both upfront, then go live within 48 hours once access + brand voice intake are done. Want me to send the proposal + invoice now so we can start?”

Key objections (short answers)
- “We can do it ourselves.” → “Totally. Most teams can—until it slips for 2–3 weeks. We buy back time and consistency, and negatives get escalated fast so rating damage doesn’t linger.”
- “AI risk / wrong tone.” → “That’s why we use approval modes + prohibited topics + escalation rules. Start in Manual Approval for week 1; once you trust it, switch to autopilot.”
- “No access.” → “We only need manager access; you can remove us anytime. If access is a blocker, we can start with draft-only via email, then switch to posting once access is granted.”
