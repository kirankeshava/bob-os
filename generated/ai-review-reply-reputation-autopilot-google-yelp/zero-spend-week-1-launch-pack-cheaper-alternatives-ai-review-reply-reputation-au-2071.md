## Free 7-Day Trial: Onboarding + Fulfillment SOP
### Intake (must collect)
1) Business name + category
2) GBP link + (optional) Yelp link
3) Locations count
4) Brand voice (3 adjectives) + forbidden phrases
5) Escalation contact + threshold (default: any 1–2★ OR any claim of safety/legal issues)
6) Posting method: (A) client posts from drafts, (B) we post via manager access

### Daily operations (trial)
- Check new reviews once per day (or twice for high-volume)
- Draft responses within 12–24 hours
- Flag negatives immediately with “Escalate” label + suggested resolution steps

### Brand-safe response rules
- Never disclose private info
- Acknowledge + apologize (when appropriate) + offer offline resolution
- Keep it short; match tone; no defensiveness
- Always include next step (“Please call/email…”) when negative

### Weekly KPI report (simple, Google Doc)
- New reviews count, average rating trend
- Response rate %, median response time
- Negative review alerts + resolution status
- Top themes (service, wait time, staff, pricing)
- Next week recommendations (2 bullets)
