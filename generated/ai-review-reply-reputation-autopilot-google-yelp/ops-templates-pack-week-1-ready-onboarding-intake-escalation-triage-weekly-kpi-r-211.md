- Client:
- Location:
- Platform: (Google/Yelp)
- Review link:
- Reviewer name/handle:
- Star rating: (1/2/3)
- Review date/time:
- Detected risk tags (check): ( ) Legal threat ( ) Safety/injury ( ) Discrimination ( ) HIPAA/PHI ( ) Refund/payment ( ) Staff named ( ) Profanity ( ) Competitor

B) Severity Level (select one)
- Level 1 (Standard): unhappy experience, no legal/safety claims
- Level 2 (Sensitive): refund demand, staff named, allegations that could harm business
- Level 3 (Critical): legal threat, safety/injury claims, discrimination allegations, protected health info

C) SLA + Routing Rules
- Level 1: Draft response within 12 business hours; approval optional per client settings; post within 24 hours.
- Level 2: Draft within 6 business hours; approval required; post within 24 hours after approval.
- Level 3: Draft within 2 business hours; approval required; recommend “take offline” response; do not post until explicit approval.

D) Required Context (ask client)
1) What happened? (internal notes)
2) Is the reviewer identifiable? (customer record Y/N)
3) Desired outcome: ( ) Apology + invite offline ( ) Refund offer ( ) Rebook/redo service ( ) Clarification
4) Any facts we must NOT state publicly?

E) Draft Response (brand-safe structure)
- Opening: thank + acknowledge
- Apology (non-admission of liability)
- Take offline: direct line + name
- Close: commitment to resolve

WEEKLY KPI REPORT — GOOGLE SHEET LAYOUT (Tabs)
Tab 1: Summary (Auto)
Columns/fields:
- Week Start | Week End
- New Reviews (Total) | Google | Yelp
- Avg Rating (Current)
- Avg Rating (Prev Week)
- Rating Delta
- Responses Posted
- Response Rate = Responses Posted / New Reviews
- Median Response Time (hrs)
- Negative Reviews (1–3★)
- Escalations Opened | Resolved | Open Count
- Notes

Tab 2: Review Log (Raw)
Columns:
- Date | Platform | Location | Reviewer | Stars | Review Text | Category/Theme | Response Draft | Approved (Y/N) | Posted (Y/N) | Post Date | Response Time (hrs) | Escalation Level | Status | Link

Tab 3: Escalations
Columns:
- Open Date | Platform | Reviewer | Stars | Risk Tags | Owner Contacted (Y/N) | Draft Sent (timestamp) | Approval Received (timestamp) | Posted (timestamp) | Outcome | Notes

Tab 4: Themes
Columns:
- Week | Theme | Mentions | Example Quotes | Suggested Fix

Notes on computation:
- Response Time (hrs) = Posted timestamp - Review timestamp
- Median Response Time: MEDIANIF logic via helper column filtering Posted=Y for the week
- Rating delta: current avg rating - last week avg rating
