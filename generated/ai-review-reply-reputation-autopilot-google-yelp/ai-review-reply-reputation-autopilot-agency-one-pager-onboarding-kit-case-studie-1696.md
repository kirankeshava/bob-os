Case Study #1 — Dental Clinic (multi-provider)
Problem:
The clinic had strong service but inconsistent review follow-up. 60%+ of reviews went unanswered, and negative reviews lingered for weeks—creating a “no one listens” impression.

What we did:
• Set brand voice: calm, professional, HIPAA-safe (no treatment discussion)
• Implemented daily monitoring + 24-hour SLA
• Escalated 1–2 star reviews instantly with a short internal action note
• Used consistent “thank you + next step” structure for 4–5 star reviews

Results (narrative + KPI bullets):
Within the first month, response coverage moved from inconsistent to near-complete.
• Avg response time: ~6 days → under 24 hours
• Unanswered reviews: reduced to near zero
• Negative reviews: faster de-escalation (public reply + offline resolution prompt)
• Rating lift story: improved perceived trust, contributing to more calls/appointments (client-reported)

Case Study #2 — Med Spa (high-ticket services)
Problem:
The spa received frequent reviews but replies were sporadic and off-brand (different staff voices). A few 1-star reviews about scheduling stayed visible without response, impacting conversion from Google Maps.

What we did:
• Defined a premium, concise voice with strict do-not-say list
• Rolled out Draft+Post with approval only for 1–2 star reviews
• Added escalation routing for scheduling complaints to a named manager
• Weekly KPI email: star trend + top complaint themes + “fix list”

Results:
• Response consistency: daily coverage instead of batch replying
• Complaint containment: faster acknowledgement + offline resolution path
• Rating lift story: improved sentiment and fewer “no response” objections (client feedback)
• Operations insight: scheduling complaints flagged early, enabling process changes
