D) WEEK-1 FULFILLMENT SOP (deliverable without API builds)
------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Draft brand-safe replies quickly, post them reliably, and surface negatives for owner action.

1) Onboarding (Day 0–2)
- Collect business context + voice rules (see onboarding template below)
- Collect access method:
  - Google: add us as Manager to Google Business Profile (preferred)
  - Yelp: Business Owner access (preferred) OR client-post workflow
- Confirm response policy:
  - Auto-approve 4–5★ unless flagged
  - Require approval for 1–3★, legal/medical keywords, refund demands, threats, discrimination claims

2) Daily Operations (Mon–Fri)
- Review capture:
  - Manual check 1–2x/day OR client forwards notification emails
- Drafting:
  - Generate response in approved brand voice
  - Include: gratitude + specific reference + soft CTA (when appropriate) + compliance-safe language
- QA:
  - Ensure no prohibited claims, no incentives for reviews, no private data
- Posting:
  - If access granted: post directly in GBP/Yelp
  - If client-post workflow: paste-ready responses delivered by email/SMS to designated poster within SLA

3) Escalation Rules (1–3★ or “high risk”)
- Immediately create escalation ticket with:
  - Summary, proposed reply, recommended offline resolution steps
  - Ask for owner facts needed (order ID, appointment date, staff involved)
- SLA:
  - Draft response within 12–24 business hours (plan dependent)
  - Owner approval required before posting (unless owner opts into auto-post policy)

4) Weekly KPI Report (every Monday)
- Metrics:
  - New reviews (G/Y), average rating, review velocity, response rate, median response time
  - Negative reviews count, escalations opened/resolved, top 3 themes, “next week focus”
