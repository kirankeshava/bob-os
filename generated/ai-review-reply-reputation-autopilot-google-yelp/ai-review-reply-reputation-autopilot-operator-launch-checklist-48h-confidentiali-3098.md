## 1) Operator Launch Checklist (Day 0–2 activation in <48 hours)
Goal: move a newly closed customer from “paid” to “live replies + weekly reporting” within 48 hours.

### Day 0 (Immediately after close — same day)
1. **Update CRM stage**: Closed → Paid (or “Trial Active” during week-1 free launch). Add Start Date = tomorrow.
2. **Send Kickoff Email (Day 0)**
   - Include: legitimacy URL https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/1
   - Confirm: locations covered, response hours, escalation contact.
   - Ask them to complete Intake Form answers (paste questions from your onboarding form).
3. **Send Access Request Emails**
   - Google Business Profile access request (Manager/Owner level).
   - Yelp access request (Business Owner/Manager access).
   - If they have an agency: request they add agent_bob_replit+review-bot@agentmail.to.
4. **Collect review context quickly**
   - Ask for: top 3 services, differentiators, any compliance items (HIPAA/financial claims, etc.), “words to avoid,” and any coupons/promises they never want stated.

### Day 1 (Within 24 hours)
5. **Confirm Intake + Escalation Rules**
   - Define escalation triggers: legal threats, refund demands, safety issues, discrimination claims, medical outcomes/diagnoses, staff misconduct allegations.
   - Confirm response SLA: e.g., “respond within 24 business hours.”
6. **Create Brand Voice Profile (internal)**
   - Tone: Friendly / concise / apologetic when needed / no defensiveness.
   - Structure: Thank you → address specific point → remedy/next step → offline contact path.
   - Sign-off format: “—[Business Name] Team” or manager name.
7. **Generate Sample Set for Approval (10 minutes)**
   - Draft: 2 positive replies + 2 negative replies + 1 ‘no-show/late’ scenario.
   - Include: one Yelp-flavored reply (shorter, policy-safe) and one GBP reply.
8. **Send Approval Email**
   - Ask them to approve/adjust tone + call-to-action wording.
   - Make it binary: “Reply ‘APPROVED’ or tell us edits.”

### Day 2 (Within 48 hours)
9. **Go-Live**
   - Start responding to new reviews per SLA.
   - For any escalation: send escalation note to their contact within 1 hour of detection.
10. **Set Weekly Reporting Cadence**
   - Choose day/time (e.g., Mondays 9am local).
   - Confirm recipients.
11. **First-week success criteria (what you track + what they feel)**
   - Operational: 90%+ of new reviews responded to within SLA.
   - Brand safety: 0 policy violations, no sensitive info.
   - Outcome: improved reviewer sentiment; fewer “angry follow-ups”; owner time saved.

### Week 1 “proof of value” checklist
- Screenshot before/after: response rate, avg rating trend (if available), examples of de-escalations.
- Weekly KPI email sent on schedule.
