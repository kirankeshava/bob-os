B) ICP + TARGETING (WHO WE CONTACT FIRST)
Primary ICP: Single-location dental practices and small groups (2–10 locations) with:
- 80+ appointments/week OR 15+ hygiene appointments/day
- Uses PMS like Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, Curve, or similar
- Has an office manager + front desk team managing confirmations manually
- Typical no-show/cancel-late problem (they’ll admit it within 2 minutes)

Buyer/Champion:
- Champion: Office Manager / Front Office Lead
- Economic buyer: Owner dentist / Practice administrator (for groups)

C) QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST (FAST YES/NO)
Ask these in first call:
1) “Roughly how many appointments do you have per day (hygiene + doctor)?” (target: 20+ total)
2) “Do you have a consistent confirmation process today? Calls/texts? How long does it take daily?”
3) “What’s your no-show + last-minute cancel rate roughly?” (even ‘not sure but it’s painful’ is OK)
4) “Do you currently have two-way texting with patients?” (if no, stronger fit)
5) “If we fill even 2–4 holes/week, would that matter financially?” (should be obvious yes)
6) “Who besides you needs to approve this?” (map decision)
Disqualifiers for now (week 1–4): very low volume (<10 appts/day), already using a robust two-way confirmation + waitlist tool they love, or owner unwilling to change workflow.

F) COLD CALL SCRIPT (DENTAL OFFICE MANAGER)
Objective: book a 15-minute demo, not pitch the whole product.

Opener (to front desk):
“Hi — is the office manager available for a quick question about reducing no-shows and last-minute cancellations?”
If they ask what it’s about:
“We help dental offices cut no-shows with two-way SMS confirmations and quick rescheduling. It’s not a directory or marketing — it’s for your existing schedule.”

When office manager picks up:
“Hi [Name], Bob here. I’ll be brief. We help dental practices reduce no-shows and last-minute cancels using two-way SMS confirmations, a reschedule link, and a waitlist to fill holes. Quick question — are no-shows or late cancels still a weekly headache for you?”

If YES (or “sometimes”):
“About how many holes do you end up with in a typical week — even if it’s a rough guess?”
(They answer)
“Got it. If we could reliably recover even 2–4 of those per week, would that be meaningful?”
(They answer)
“Perfect. The easiest next step is a 15-minute walkthrough. If it’s not a fit, I’ll tell you in the first 5 minutes. What’s better — tomorrow morning or Thursday afternoon?”

“Totally fair. Are you confirming mostly by calls, texting, or both?”
- If manual calls/texts: “Makes sense — we usually help teams save time even when no-shows are ‘fine’. Want to see it quickly? 15 minutes.”
- If they have a tool: “Which tool are you using? Do you have two-way confirmations + an automated waitlist fill?”
If strong incumbent + happy: politely exit.
