Where to find 30 prospects quickly (free):
- Google Maps: pick 3 cities, collect 10 prospects/city per niche (name, website, phone, owner/manager name if listed)
- Yelp: filter by “Request a Quote/Message” + “Online booking”
- Facebook pages: often list owner name + Messenger contact

Minimum qualification checklist (use before outreach):
- Has scheduled appointments (not purely walk-in)
- Uses phone/text already OR has online booking
- Average appointment value is not tiny (roughly $75+ is ideal)
- Has at least ~10 appointments/week
- Pain signal: “no-show policy”, “deposit required”, “confirm your appointment” language on site
