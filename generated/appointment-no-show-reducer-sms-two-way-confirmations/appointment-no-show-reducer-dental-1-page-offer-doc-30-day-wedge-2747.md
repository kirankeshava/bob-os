THE WEDGE OFFER (WHAT’S IN SCOPE)
Goal: reduce no-shows and fill last-minute openings without adding front-desk work.

1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Reminder texts go out automatically (e.g., 72h + 24h + 2h)
- Patients reply: CONFIRM / CANCEL / RESCHEDULE
- Confirmations and cancellations are captured in a simple log

2) Reschedule link + smart routing
- If a patient cancels, they receive an immediate reschedule link
- Optional: send reschedule options based on your rules (e.g., hygiene vs. restorative blocks)

3) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
- Maintain a “can come in sooner” list
- When a slot opens, we text the waitlist with a first-come workflow
- Stops the phone tag loop and recovers same-week production

4) Basic recovered-revenue analytics
- Weekly summary per location:
  - Confirm rate, cancel rate, reschedule rate
  - Openings created vs. openings filled from waitlist
  - Estimated recovered revenue (based on your average visit value or by appointment type)
