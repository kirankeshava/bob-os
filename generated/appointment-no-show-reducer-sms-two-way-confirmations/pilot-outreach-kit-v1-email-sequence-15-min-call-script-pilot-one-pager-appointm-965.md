3) 15-MINUTE DEMO/ONBOARDING CALL SCRIPT (Pilot recruitment)

Goal of call: qualify, confirm consent/opt-in posture, collect minimum intake + baseline, and book go-live date.

A) Opening (1 minute)
“Hi {{Name}}, thanks for the time. I’m Bob Smith. The goal today is to understand your booking flow and see if a 7-day pilot of our two-way SMS confirmation/reminder system would measurably reduce no-shows. If it’s a fit, we’ll schedule a go-live and I’ll send a short intake checklist.”

B) Fast discovery (4 minutes)
1) “What type of appointments do you run, and roughly how many per day?”
2) “Where do no-shows hurt most—new clients, high-ticket services, or packed days?”
3) “Current reminder process: none / manual texts / EHR reminders / email?”
4) “Typical no-show or late-cancel rate (even a rough estimate)?”
5) “Average appointment value (or range)?”

C) Explain pilot scope and outcomes (3 minutes)
“During the pilot we send SMS reminders and collect confirmations with simple replies (YES/NO). If someone can’t make it, we trigger a reschedule workflow (either automated options or we route to your staff). If you have a waitlist, we can attempt to fill gaps.

We track: reminders sent, confirmation rate, reschedules handled, opt-outs, and estimated recovered revenue. After 7 days you get a weekly value report you can forward internally.”

Share legitimacy:
“You can reference our info page here: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2. Support email: agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”

D) Consent / compliance checkpoint (2 minutes)
1) “Do you already collect customer phone numbers and consent to contact for appointment-related messages?”
2) “Do you have any special rules (minors, sensitive services, do-not-text list)?”
3) “We include STOP to opt out and handle HELP messages—any internal policy we should match?”

E) Configuration intake (3 minutes)
Collect:
- Business timezone
- Business hours
- Reminder cadence preference (e.g., 24h and 2h; or 48h and 4h)
- Confirmation wording preference (friendly vs short)
- Reschedule handling preference (link vs call-back vs options)
- Escalation contact (name + phone/email)
- Waitlist approach (yes/no; simple list; any priority rules)

F) Baseline metrics (1–2 minutes)
“To quantify impact, can you share either:
- last 4 weeks no-show rate (rough %), and
- avg appointment value?
If you don’t have exact numbers, estimates are fine—we’ll use them to compute recovered revenue.”

G) Close + next steps (1 minute)
“If you’re in, next step is I send a 5-minute intake email today. We can go live on {{Date}}. During the first week we monitor daily and log any issues. You’ll get a weekly report after Day 7.”

Post-call email promise:
“I’ll send the intake checklist from agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to, and you’ll have the info page again for reference.”
