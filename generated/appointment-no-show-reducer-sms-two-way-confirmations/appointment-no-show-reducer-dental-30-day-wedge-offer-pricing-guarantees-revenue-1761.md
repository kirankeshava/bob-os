## The Problem We Fix
Dental practices lose revenue when:
- Patients don’t confirm and no-show (especially hygiene blocks).
- Patients need to reschedule but can’t reach the office during business hours.
- Cancellations happen too late to backfill the chair.

**Our goal:** Reduce no-shows and same-day gaps with **two-way SMS confirmations**, **frictionless rescheduling**, and **waitlist backfill**, plus simple reporting that translates outcomes into **recovered revenue**.

## What’s Included (In Scope)
### 1) Two-Way SMS Confirmations (Core)
- Smart reminder sequence (e.g., 72h / 24h / same-day)
- Patients can reply **CONFIRM / CANCEL / RESCHEDULE** (or tap a link)
- Staff gets a clear, consolidated view of responses

### 2) Reschedule Flow (Reduce “silent” no-shows)
- If a patient indicates they can’t make it, they receive a **reschedule link/workflow**
- Front desk receives a clean summary so they’re not stuck playing phone tag

### 3) Waitlist Gap-Fill (Backfill cancellations)
- Simple waitlist capture (by text)
- When a slot opens, we text the waitlist in priority order to fill it

### 4) Basic Recovered-Revenue Analytics (Per Location)
- Confirmations rate, cancellation lead time, reschedules completed
- “Recovered chair time” estimate translated into **$ recovered** (using practice-provided average values)
- Weekly snapshot + 30-day summary

**Not included in this 30-day wedge (by design):** deep PMS integrations, complex multi-provider routing rules, advanced multi-location BI. We keep this fast to launch and easy to say yes to.
