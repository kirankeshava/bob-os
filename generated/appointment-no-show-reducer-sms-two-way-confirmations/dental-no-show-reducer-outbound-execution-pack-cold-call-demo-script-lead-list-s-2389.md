4) COLD CALL SCRIPT (OFFICE MANAGER FIRST)
Objective: book a 15-minute demo. Do NOT pitch features first; lead with no-show impact and low-friction pilot.

Opener:
“Hi, is this the front desk or office manager? … Hey [Name], I’ll be brief. I’m Bob. We help dental practices reduce no-shows with two-way text confirmations and easy rescheduling. Quick question—are missed appointments a problem for you guys week to week?”

If YES / somewhat:
“Got it. Roughly how many appointments do you book in a typical day?”
[Let them answer]
“And when someone no-shows, do you mostly lose that slot, or are you sometimes able to backfill it?”

Bridge to value:
“Understood. The reason I’m calling: we send smart reminders, get a simple ‘Confirm / Reschedule’ reply by text, and if someone cancels we can offer the opening to a waitlist—so the chair time doesn’t go empty. We also show recovered revenue so it’s easy to see if it’s worth keeping.”

Credibility + proof:
“If you want to see what it is, we have a simple overview here: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2 and you can reach me at agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”

Close for meeting:
“Would it be crazy to do a 15-minute Zoom this week? If it’s not a fit, I’ll tell you in the first five minutes.”

If they ask price on the phone:
“It’s typically $399 per location per month plus SMS pass-through, and we do a one-time concierge setup. But I don’t want to quote you blindly—on the demo I’ll ask 3 questions about volume and your current reminder process and I’ll tell you exactly what ROI looks like.”

Key objections + responses:
A) “We already have reminders in our PMS.”
“Totally. Most do. The gap is usually two-way confirmation + frictionless rescheduling + filling last-minute holes. If your current system already does two-way and reliably backfills cancellations, you may not need us. Can I ask—can patients reply by text to confirm/reschedule, and do you have a waitlist process that runs automatically?”

B) “We don’t have many no-shows.”
“That’s great. Even if it’s just 1–2 per week, that can still be meaningful. If you tell me your average production per appointment, I can estimate whether it’s worth 15 minutes to look.”

C) “Send me an email.”
“Happy to. What’s the best email for you? I’ll send a 3-bullet overview with the link. Also—so it doesn’t get lost—should we pencil a 15-minute time, and you can cancel if it doesn’t look relevant?”

D) “We’re too busy.”
“Understood. That’s exactly why we keep it light: we run concierge setup and you don’t need to change your whole workflow. Give me 15 minutes and you’ll know if it’s worth it.”

E) “Is this HIPAA compliant?”
“We keep messages appointment-focused and minimal. On the demo I’ll walk you through exactly what we send and what we avoid, and we can tailor templates to your comfort level.”

5) 15-MINUTE DEMO SCRIPT (STRUCTURE)
Goal: qualify, compute ROI quickly, offer low-risk start, book onboarding.

Minute 0–2: Frame
“Thanks—goal is to see if we can measurably reduce no-shows for [Practice]. If yes, we can launch quickly. If not, I’ll be direct.”

Minute 2–6: Diagnose (3 questions)
1) “About how many appointments per day, and how many providers?”
2) “What’s your current reminder process (texts/calls/emails), and do patients confirm by replying?”
3) “In a typical week, how many no-shows / late cancels do you see?”

Minute 6–9: ROI math (simple)
“Even recovering 3 appointments/month can usually pay for the system. If your average production is ~$X per visit, that’s ~$3X recovered. We’ll track it so it’s not hand-wavy.”

Minute 9–12: Show workflow (plain language)
- “Patient gets reminder”
- “They reply Confirm or Reschedule”
- “If Reschedule/cancel, we offer a link and/or notify waitlist”
- “Front desk sees confirmations and filled openings; minimal extra work”
- “Dashboard shows recovered appts + estimated recovered revenue”

Minute 12–14: Offer + guarantee (pick one)
Pricing anchor:
“$399/location/month + SMS pass-through. One-time concierge setup covers templates, routing, and go-live.”
Guarantee options to present:
- Cancel anytime (no long contract)
- First 14 days risk-free (if it’s not smooth, stop)
- Performance-based: “If there’s no measurable reduction after the first full month, we’ll waive the next month’s fee.”

Minute 14–15: Close next step
“If you’re in, next step is a 20-minute onboarding where we grab your reminder templates, appointment export/access method, and your waitlist approach. We can usually launch within a few business days.”
