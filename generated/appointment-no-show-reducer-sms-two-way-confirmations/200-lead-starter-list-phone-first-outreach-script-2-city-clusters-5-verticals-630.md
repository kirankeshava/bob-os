ENRICHMENT PASS (to add emails fast, free)
For each lead:
1) Open website → find Contact / About / Team pages. Copy any emails.
2) If no email: use ‘site:domain.com email’ and ‘site:domain.com “@domain.com”’ Google queries.
3) If still none: capture contact form URL and keep as “Form Only”.
4) Capture decision-maker names: “Owner”, “Practice Manager”, “Office Manager”, “Clinic Manager”.

PHONE-FIRST OUTREACH MICRO-SCRIPT (for directory/website-only leads)
Goal: book a 15-min demo or get the right contact email.

Gatekeeper/Front desk opener (15 seconds)
“Hi — quick question. Who handles your appointment reminders and no-show reduction — is that the office manager or practice manager?”

If they ask ‘what is this about?’
“We help appointment-based clinics reduce no-shows using two-way SMS confirmations and instant reschedules. It’s a done-for-you setup in 24–48 hours. I just need the right person for a 10–15 minute walkthrough.”

If they give a name / transfer
“Perfect, thanks. Before you transfer me, what’s the best email for [Name] so I can send a 3-sentence overview and a link?”

If decision-maker is unavailable
“No problem—what’s the best email to send details and a quick demo link? Also, what’s a good time they typically return vendor calls?”

Qualifying question (once with decision-maker)
“Roughly how many appointments do you book in a week, and do you know your no-show rate or cancellation rate?”

Value line (tie to revenue)
“Even a small reduction—say 10–20% fewer no-shows—usually recovers thousands per month per location. We track recovered revenue so it’s measurable.”

Close for demo
“Are you open to a 15-minute demo this week? I’ll show how the two-way confirmations + reschedules + waitlist fill work.
