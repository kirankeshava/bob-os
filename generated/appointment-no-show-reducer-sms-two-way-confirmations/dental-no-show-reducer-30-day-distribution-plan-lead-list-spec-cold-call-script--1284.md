## Lead list build spec (what to target)
### ICP filters (fastest-close)
- Dental practices (general dentistry first; then pediatric, perio, endo)
- Single-location or small group (1–5 locations)
- Has hygienist schedule + recurring recalls (higher reminder leverage)
- Visible online booking OR active phone scheduling (either works)
- Evidence of appointment reminders already (means they value it) but no two-way confirmation/waitlist automation (common gap)

### Where to source (free)
- Google Maps search queries:
  - “dentist [city]”, “family dentistry [city]”, “dental clinic [city]”, “cosmetic dentist [city]”
- Practice websites (look for “Request Appointment” forms)
- LinkedIn (practice page + staff list) to find office manager/practice manager
- State dental association directories (if public)

### Titles to capture
Primary:
- Office Manager
- Practice Manager
- Front Office Manager
Secondary:
- Owner Dentist / Managing Dentist
- Operations Manager (for small groups)

### Qualification quick-check (before outreach)

- Are they booking 2+ weeks out? (waitlist fill is valuable)

If they ask “What is this about?”
“Totally—two-way confirmations + a reschedule link that prevents gaps, and a waitlist feature to fill last-minute cancellations. Quick overview is here if you want to verify us: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/2. What’s the best way to reach your office manager?”

### Discovery (60 seconds)
1) “About how many patients do you see in a day between hygiene + doctor columns?”
2) “Do you currently text reminders? If yes, can patients reply to confirm or reschedule?”
3) “What happens when someone cancels day-of—do you have a waitlist process, or is it mostly manual calls?”

### Close for meeting (20 seconds)
“Got it. This is exactly what we fix—two-way confirmations, automatic rescheduling, and waitlist fill with a simple recovered-revenue report. Could we set up a quick 15-minute call with [Office Manager Name] this week? I can send details from agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”
