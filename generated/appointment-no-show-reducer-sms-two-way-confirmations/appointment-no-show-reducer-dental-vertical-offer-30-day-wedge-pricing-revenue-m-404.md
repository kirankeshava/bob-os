PRIMARY VERTICAL (First 30 Days): Dental (single-location practices + small DSOs)
Why dental first: high appointment density (hygiene + restorative), meaningful no-show cost, front desk pain is constant, and the buyer (owner/office manager) can decide quickly without long compliance/procurement cycles.

ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Type: General dentistry, family dentistry, cosmetic dentistry; 1–10 locations.
- Volume: 15–60 appointments/day per location.
- No-show/cancel problem: ≥ 5 no-shows/week OR frequent last-minute cancellations.
- Current process: manual reminder calls/texts OR one-way reminders with no structured confirmations/reschedules.
- Buyer: Owner/operator, practice manager, office manager.

THE WEDGE OFFER (In Scope)
Goal: Reduce no-shows and fill gaps automatically using simple, reliable workflows.
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Patients receive reminders and can reply YES/NO.
- YES confirms; NO triggers automated reschedule flow.

2) Smart reminder schedule (simple, proven defaults)
- Example: 72h + 24h + 2h reminders (configurable).
- Language optimized for quick replies.

3) Reschedule link + automated reschedule handling
- If patient can’t make it, they get a reschedule link (or a “reply with preferred times” flow).
- Office gets a clear queue of reschedule requests.

4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
- When a cancellation/no occurs, system texts waitlist candidates in order.
- First to accept wins; office notified.

5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Tracks: confirmations, prevented no-shows (proxy), last-minute cancellations recovered, and estimated recovered revenue.
- Simple dashboard + weekly summary email.
