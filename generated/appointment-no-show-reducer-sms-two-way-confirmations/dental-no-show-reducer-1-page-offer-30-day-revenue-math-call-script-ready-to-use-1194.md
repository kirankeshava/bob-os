3) THE WEDGE OFFER (WHAT’S IN SCOPE)
Goal: measurably reduce no-shows and backfill last-minute gaps.

A) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Smart reminders (timing tuned to the practice)
- Patients can confirm or request changes by replying
- Staff sees a simple confirmation status per appointment

B) Reschedule link / assisted rescheduling
- If patient can’t make it, we route them to reschedule via link or guided response
- Minimizes back-and-forth phone tag

C) Waitlist gap-fill
- Maintain a simple waitlist (patients who want earlier times)
- When a cancellation happens, automatically message waitlist candidates to fill the opening

D) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Track: confirmations, reschedules, filled gaps, estimated chair time recovered
- Simple “recovered revenue” report so the owner can see ROI

Not included (first 30 days): complex PMS integrations, custom patient portals, multi-channel campaigns beyond SMS, deep BI dashboards.
