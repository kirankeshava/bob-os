E) OBJECTION HANDLING (SHORT)
- “We’re too busy.” → “That’s exactly why this helps—fewer gaps and fewer manual calls. Pilot is lightweight; we can start with just confirmations + reschedules.”
- “Patients hate texts.” → “We only text appointment-specific messages and they can opt out. Most patients prefer SMS over voicemail.”
- “We use [Solution].” → “Great—then we’re comparing outcomes. Do you have two-way confirmations + automatic reschedules + waitlist fill in one flow? If not, it’s additive.”

F) WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE IN 7 DAYS (PILOT)
- Baseline no-show / late cancel count for the week
- Confirmation rate (YES/NO)
- # reschedules captured
- # gaps filled from waitlist (even partial)
- Simple recovered revenue estimate (chair time saved × typical value)
