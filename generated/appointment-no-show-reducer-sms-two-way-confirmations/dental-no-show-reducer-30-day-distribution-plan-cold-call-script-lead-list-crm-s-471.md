## Cold Call Script (Dental Office Manager / Front Desk)
**Purpose:** book a 10–15 minute demo. Keep it short and respectful.

### Opener (15 seconds)
“Hi, is this the front desk? Great—who’s the best person to talk to about appointment confirmations and last-minute cancellations—office manager?”

If they say “That’s me” (or transfer):
“Hi [Name], this is Bob. I’ll be brief. We help dental offices cut no-shows and fill last-minute gaps using two-way text confirmations and a simple waitlist. It’s not a new phone system—just fewer empty chair hours. Can I ask two quick questions to see if it’s even relevant?”

### Qualifying questions (60–90 seconds)
1) “Roughly how often do you deal with same-day cancellations or no-shows—daily, weekly, or rarely?”
2) “When you get a cancellation, do you already have a waitlist process that reliably fills those openings?”

If pain exists:
“Got it. The reason I’m calling is we built a lightweight system that texts patients to confirm, lets them reschedule by link, and if they cancel we automatically offer the slot to your waitlist. We also show recovered appointments so you can see the revenue impact per month. Quick overview is here: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j-dbyyfbxp.picard.replit.dev/sites/2
What’s the best email to send details and a couple sample messages?”

### Booking the demo (direct ask)
“Would it be crazy to do a 15-minute screen share this week? If it’s not clearly worth it, we’ll drop it.”

Offer two times:
“I have [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time]. Which is better?”

## Objection Handling (tight, dental-specific)
### “We already use reminders (Demandforce/Weave/etc.)”
“Makes sense—most offices do. The gap we cover is *two-way confirmation + automated reschedule + waitlist fill* when someone cancels, plus a simple ‘recovered production’ number. If you’re already filling cancellations consistently, we’ll know in 10 minutes and I’ll back off. Worth a quick compare?”

“Understood. Quick question—if you could recover even 2 hygiene appointments a month that would usually pay for it. If we can’t show measurable recovered appointments, you can cancel. Want to sanity-check the math on a 10–15 minute call?”

### “Send info”
“Happy to. What’s the best email? I’ll send from agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to and include the overview page. When I follow up, is it better by phone or email?”

### “We’re too busy / call back later”
“Totally get it. What day/time is actually realistic—two options: [Option A] or [Option B]? I’ll keep it to 10 minutes.”
