Goal: book a 15-min demo. Be respectful of time; use ROI language.

Qualifying Q1 (no-show/late cancel pain):
“Roughly, how often do you deal with same-day cancellations or no-shows — is it more like a few a week or more like daily?”

What does your calendar look like tomorrow morning or Thursday afternoon?”

6) ACTIVITY-TO-REVENUE MATH (to align daily effort)
Assumptions (conservative for cold outbound):
- 200 leads/week touched
- 5% reply/positive response = 10 conversations
- 50% to demo = 5 demos
- 40% to trial = 2 trials
- 50% close from trial = 1 new customer/week

In 30 days: 4 customers.
Cash collected in month 1 (example): setup fee + month 1 MRR per location.
- If setup is $399 and MRR is $399: 4 customers = $3,192 collected.
To hit $10k collected in 30 days you need either:
- Higher volume (e.g., 3–4 closes/week), OR
- Multi-location deals, OR
- Slightly higher setup fee for concierge launch (still free trial service week 1 if policy requires), OR
- Aggressive focus on groups (2–10 locations) so one close is 2–5 locations.
Operational takeaway: prioritize small groups/DSOs in the lead list (2–10 locations) while still calling singles.
