## 3) Day-1 Time-Blocked Execution Checklist (3.5–5 hours)
### Block A (60–90 min): Build first 50 leads
Pick 1–2 city clusters (example: Phoenix + Mesa; Tampa + St. Petersburg) and 2 verticals (Dentist + Chiro).
Free sources:
- Google Maps: “dentist near [city]”, “chiropractor [city]”
- Yelp (business name + phone)
- Practice websites (contact page)
Capture the columns above. Dedupe by phone + website.

### Block B (30 min): Prep sending list
Sort by: has direct email first, then contact form.
Write 1 personalization note per lead (fast, 5–10 words).

### Block C (60–75 min): Send 50 cold emails (plain text)
- 2–3 sentences + one question
- No images, no attachments
- Include legitimacy URL + contact email
Log “Status=Contacted” and “Last Touch Date”.

### Block D (60 min): Place 20 calls
Call during local business hours. If voicemail, leave the voicemail script below.
Log outcome: Answered/VM/No answer/Wrong number.

### Block E (20–30 min): Craigslist post (1 per city cluster)
Post in “Services > Small biz ads” (or closest category).
Headline: “Reduce No-Shows: 2-Way Text Confirmations + Reschedules (Free Setup)”
Body: use template in section 6.

### Block F (10 min): End-of-day KPI report
Update KPI tracker and set next-day follow-ups (reply to all emails within 15 min when possible).
