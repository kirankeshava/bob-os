## 4) Objection Handling (tight, dental-specific)
### Objection: “We already use [Solution].”
“Understood. Two quick checks: (1) do patients reply YES/NO and does that update your team reliably, and (2) when someone cancels, do you have an automated way to offer that slot to a waitlist? If either is ‘not really,’ that’s the gap we fill. Can I show you in 15 minutes and you can compare?”

### Objection: “We don’t have no-show issues.”
“Nice — that’s rare. Quick sanity check: in the last two weeks, did you have any same-day cancellations or unfilled hygiene openings? Even 2 openings a week is often $1k–$2k+ in production. We track that and help backfill. Worth a quick look?”

### Objection: “How much does it cost?”
“For most single locations it’s **$399/month per location**, plus SMS pass-through at cost, and a one-time **concierge setup** so we can launch quickly. If we can’t show measurable improvement early, you can cancel — we keep it low-risk. Happy to cover exact pricing after I understand your volume in a 15-minute demo.”

### Objection: “We don’t want patients to feel spammed.”
“Same. We set message timing and frequency conservatively, and only send what’s needed: confirm, reschedule, and optional waitlist offers. The goal is fewer interruptions for your staff and fewer empty chairs.”

### Objection: “HIPAA / compliance?”
“We keep messages minimal — no diagnosis, no procedure details. It’s appointment logistics: confirm/reschedule. We’ll align the exact wording to what you’re comfortable with.”

### Objection: “We don’t have time to set this up.”
“That’s why we do concierge setup. You give us your preferred reminder timing, your cancellation window, and either an appointment export or basic schedule rules. Most practices can be live quickly without changing your PMS.”
