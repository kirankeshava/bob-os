THE NARROW WEDGE (WHAT WE DO IN 30 DAYS)
Goal: Reduce no-shows + fill last-minute gaps with a simple two-way SMS workflow that patients actually respond to.

IN SCOPE (MONTH 1)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
   • Automated reminders (e.g., 72h + 24h + day-of)
   • Patients can reply: CONFIRM / C to confirm, RESCHEDULE / R to reschedule, or CANCEL
   • Staff gets a simple view of responses (no more calling everyone)

2) Smart reschedule link (low friction)
   • If patient can’t make it, they get a reschedule path instead of ghosting
   • Captures intent and reduces total lost appointments

3) Waitlist fill for openings
   • When a cancellation happens, we trigger an outreach to waitlist patients
   • First-come/first-served logic (or staff-approval mode)

4) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
   • Confirm rate, reschedule rate, cancellation rate
   • “Appointments saved” and estimated revenue recovered
   • Weekly summary email
