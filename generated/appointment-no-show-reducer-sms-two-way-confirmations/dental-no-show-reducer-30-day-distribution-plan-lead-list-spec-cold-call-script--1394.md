3) ICP + QUALIFICATION (WHO WE TARGET FIRST)
Ideal Customer Profile (Dental):
- Single-location or small DSO (2–10 locations)
- 60+ appointments/week OR 250+ appointments/month
- Existing no-show rate they feel pain from (even if they don’t know exact %)
- Office manager owns scheduling + recalls; owner cares about production
- Uses any PMS (Dentrix, Eaglesoft, OpenDental, Curve Dental, etc.); we can start without deep integration

Qualification checklist (ask on first call):
- “Roughly how many appointments do you have per week?”
- “What types of visits no-show the most? (hygiene, new patient, crown seat, etc.)”
- “How are reminders done today? (call/text/email) One-way or can patients confirm?”
- “When a patient cancels/no-shows, do you have a waitlist to fill the hole?”
- “Who would need to approve a 2-week pilot at ~$399 to prove this?”

Disqualifiers (don’t waste time):
- Under ~30 appointments/week AND low perceived pain
- Already using robust two-way confirmations + automated waitlist fill and happy with it
- No clear owner/manager decision path within 14 days
