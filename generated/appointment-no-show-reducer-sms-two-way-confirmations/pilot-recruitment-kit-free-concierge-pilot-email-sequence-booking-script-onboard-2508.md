BOOKING / ON-CALL SCRIPT (15 minutes)
Goal: confirm fit, collect baseline, agree on reminder timing + reschedule rules, confirm consent/opt-out handling.

1) Opener (30 sec)
“Thanks {Name}. Goal today is to confirm this is a fit, grab your baseline, and agree on simple reminder + reply rules. Pilot is 7 days and free; we’ll send a weekly report showing confirmations/reschedules and an estimate of revenue recovered.”

2) Fit + volume (2 min)
- “How many appointments per week, roughly?”
- “What’s your current no-show rate (even a guess)?”
- “Average value per appointment?”

3) Workflow (6 min)
- “What scheduling system do you use (or is it manual)?”
- “Typical lead time: are most bookings same-day, 1–3 days out, or 1+ week out?”
- Reminder timing proposal: “We usually start with 24 hours + 2 hours before. Does that work?”
- Confirmations: “When someone replies YES, do you want it logged only, or also sent to your staff inbox?”
- Reschedules: “If someone replies RESCHEDULE, should we (a) offer a call-back link/number, or (b) notify staff to reach out?”
- Waitlist: “Do you keep a waitlist for earlier slots? If yes, how do you want those people contacted?”

4) Compliance + fail-safe (3 min)
- “We include STOP to opt-out and honor it immediately.”
- “If anything fails (e.g., calendar sync issue), we alert you right away and pause automation until confirmed.”
- Confirm escalation contact: name + best email/phone.

5) Close (3 min)
- “We’ll run this for 7 days. You’ll get a weekly report with confirmations, reschedules, waitlist fills (if used), and recovered revenue estimate.”
- “I’ll send a confirmation email with what we agreed and the baseline metrics. Once you reply ‘approved,’ we’ll go live.”
