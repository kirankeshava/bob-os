## 3) Day-1 lead batch framework (100 targets) — capture format + sourcing steps
Because we’re using free sources, the fastest approach is to build a “targets list” first, then enrich with emails/phones from each website.

### City Cluster A (example): Phoenix metro
Vertical targets (aim 10 each): Dentist, Chiro, Med Spa, PT, Optometry

### City Cluster B (example): Dallas metro
Vertical targets (aim 10 each): Dentist, Chiro, Med Spa, PT, Optometry

### How to pull 100 targets in ~2–3 hours (free)
1) Google search queries (copy/paste):
- "{{city}} dentist" + “appointment”
- "{{city}} chiropractor" + “book now”
- "{{city}} med spa" + “book appointment”
- "{{city}} physical therapy" + “schedule”
- "{{city}} optometry" + “book online"
2) Open top results + Google Maps listings.
3) Capture: business name, website, main phone, address.
4) Click website → find Contact/About/Team pages → capture owner/manager name + email (or contact form URL).
5) If no direct email: capture front desk email, or use contact form + phone for call/SMS.

### Lead capture columns (exact)
- Business Name
- Vertical (Dentist/Chiro/MedSpa/PT/Optometry)
- City
- Website URL
- Contact Name
- Role (Owner/Manager/Office Manager)
- Email
- Phone
- Source (Google/Maps/Directory)
- Notes (hours, #locations, online booking tool)
