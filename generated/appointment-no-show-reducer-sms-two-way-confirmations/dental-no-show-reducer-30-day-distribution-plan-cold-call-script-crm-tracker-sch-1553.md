1) 30-DAY DISTRIBUTION PLAN (DENTAL)
Goal: Collect $10k+ in 30 days (setup fees + month-1 MRR) from dental practices (single-location + small groups).

Target customer (who answers fast):
- Single-location dental practice OR small group (2–10 locations)
- High hygiene volume (many short appointments)
- Owner/operator involved OR strong office manager
- Pain signal: frequent no-shows/late cancels, constant schedule gaps, staff time wasted on reminder calls

Lead sources (free-first):
- Google Maps: “dentist”, “family dentistry”, “cosmetic dentistry”, “pediatric dentist” in 2–3 chosen metros
- Practice websites “Contact” pages (office manager email often listed)
- State dental association directories (often free)
- LinkedIn search for titles: Office Manager, Practice Manager, Operations Manager, Dental Administrator

- Outreach/day: 50 cold emails + 30 LinkedIn DMs + 10 cold calls
- Follow-ups: 40 follow-ups/day (mix of email + call)

Day 1: Email #1 + (optional) LinkedIn connect
Day 2: 1st call attempt (office) + voicemail + short follow-up email
Day 4: Email #2 (short, ROI-focused) + LinkedIn DM

2) COLD-CALL SCRIPT (OFFICE MANAGER / FRONT DESK)
Objective: book a 15-minute demo with the office manager/owner.

Opener (10 seconds):
“Hi, is this the front desk? This is Bob Smith. Quick question—who handles appointment confirmations and last-minute cancellations for the schedule?”

If they ask “what is this about?”
“Totally—calling because we help dental practices reduce no-shows and fill last-minute openings using two-way text confirmations and a simple waitlist. It’s specifically for hygiene-heavy schedules.”

If they transfer to Office Manager/Practice Manager:
“Hi [Name], Bob Smith here. I’ll be brief. We help dental offices cut no-shows/late cancels by sending two-way SMS confirmations, an easy reschedule link, and we can fill openings from a waitlist. We also track recovered revenue per month so you can see the impact.

If it’s okay, can I ask one quick baseline question—about how many patients do you see per day and do no-shows/late cancels create schedule holes each week?”

Qualifying questions (pick 2–3):
- “On an average week, how many no-shows or <24-hour cancels do you see?”
- “Do you currently text patients from your PMS or is it mostly manual calls?”
- “When there’s a cancellation, do you have a real waitlist process or is it ad hoc?”
- “Who would need to approve something like this—owner, you, or both?”

ROI one-liner (keep it simple):
“Most practices only need to save 1–2 appointments a month for it to pay for itself—especially hygiene and production blocks.”

Call-to-action (book demo):
“The easiest next step is a 15-minute walkthrough. I’ll show exactly how two-way confirmations and waitlist fill works, and you can decide if it’s worth piloting. What does your calendar look like tomorrow or Thursday?”
