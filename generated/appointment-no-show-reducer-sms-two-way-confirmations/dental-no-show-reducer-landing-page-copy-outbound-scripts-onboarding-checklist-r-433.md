## Onboarding checklist (lightweight) + Week-1 launch plan

### What we need from the practice (Day 0–1)
1) Primary contact: office manager/front desk lead + owner approver
2) Business info: practice name, address, hours, “from” phone label preference
3) Reminder policy inputs:
   - Which appointment types (hygiene, restorative, consults)
   - Reminder schedule (e.g., 72h + 24h + 2h)
   - Confirmation window (e.g., ask to confirm 48h prior)
4) Reschedule flow:
   - Reschedule link destination OR preferred method (text back preferred times)
   - Rules for same-day cancellations
5) Waitlist:
   - Existing waitlist list (names + mobile) OR start capturing moving forward
   - Priority rules (hygiene vs emergency)
6) Data feed:
   - Daily/weekly export of upcoming appointments (CSV) OR schedule access method
7) Compliance/consent:
   - Confirm patients have opted into SMS (or add opt-in language to forms)

### Week-1 implementation plan
**Day 1:** Configure message templates + schedules; import next 7 days appointments; enable two-way confirmation.

**Day 2–3:** Turn on reschedule automation for “2 = reschedule”; define staff handoff for special cases.

**Day 4:** Enable waitlist fill for cancellations (start with 1–2 appointment types).

**Day 5:** Send first weekly report: confirmations, reschedules saved, waitlist fills, estimated revenue recovered.

### Success criteria (first 14 days)
- ≥80% of upcoming appointments receive confirmation request
- Confirm/reschedule response rate is visible to staff
- At least 1–3 gaps filled via reschedule + waitlist texts
- Weekly summary delivered to decision maker
