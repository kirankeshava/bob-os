THE 30-DAY WEDGE OFFER (What we implement first)
Goal: Reduce no-shows and fill last-minute gaps using simple two-way SMS flows that require minimal staff time.

In scope (per location)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
   - Reminder texts that ask patients to confirm (“Reply YES to confirm, NO to reschedule”)
   - Flag unconfirmed appointments for follow-up

2) Smart reminder cadence
   - Example cadence: 72h + 24h + 2h (adjustable by appointment type)
   - Different messaging for hygiene vs restorative (optional)

3) Reschedule link (fast path)
   - Patients who reply NO get a reschedule option immediately
   - Optional: collect preferred times and route to staff

4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
   - Maintain a simple waitlist (patient opt-in)
   - When a cancellation happens, automatically text the waitlist to fill the slot

5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics
   - Weekly summary: confirmations, saved appointments, filled gaps, estimated revenue recovered
   - Per-location reporting (useful for DSOs)
