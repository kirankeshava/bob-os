=== 4) KPI BASELINE + WEEK-1 OUTCOMES CAPTURE (STRUCTURE + FORMULAS) ===
Capture BEFORE pilot (baseline):
- Appointments per week (A)
- No-show rate % (NS%)
- Late cancellation rate % (LC%) (optional)
- Average appointment value $ (V)
- Working days/week (D)

During pilot (week 1):
- Reminders sent (RS)
- Confirmations YES (Y)
- Confirmations NO (N)
- Reschedule requests (R)
- Reschedules completed (RC)
- Waitlist offers sent (WOS)
- Waitlist fills (WF)
- Opt-outs STOP (OO)
- Manual escalations to staff (ME)

Outcome calculations (simple, conservative):
- Baseline expected no-shows/week = A * NS%
- Estimated saves from confirmations/reschedules = min(RC + WF, Baseline expected no-shows/week)
- Estimated recovered revenue/week = Estimated saves * V
- Confirmation rate = Y / RS
- Reschedule completion rate = RC / R
- Waitlist fill rate = WF / WOS

Weekly report headline fields:
- “Estimated recovered revenue this week: $___”
- “No-show risk prevented: ___ appointments”
- “Top reasons: ___ (if captured)”
- “Next week tweaks: reminder timing / wording / reschedule cutoff / waitlist rules”
