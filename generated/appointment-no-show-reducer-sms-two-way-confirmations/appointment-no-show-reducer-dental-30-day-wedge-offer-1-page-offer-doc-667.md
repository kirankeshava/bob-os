APPOINTMENT NO-SHOW REDUCER (DENTAL) — 30-DAY WEDGE OFFER

What this is
A simple SMS system that reduces dental no-shows by confirming appointments two-way (patients reply Y/N), making rescheduling frictionless, and filling last-minute gaps from a waitlist. Includes lightweight analytics to show recovered revenue per location.

Chosen vertical (first 30 days): Dental (single-location + small groups)
Why dental: frequent scheduled visits, meaningful $/chair-hour cost of no-shows, and a fast decision path (owner + office manager).

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
• General dentistry, orthodontics, perio, endo (non-emergency scheduled care)
• 1–10 providers, 1–5 locations (single practice or small group/DSO)
• 250+ appointments/month OR consistent no-show/cancel problems
• Front desk is overloaded with reminder calls and rescheduling

Core outcomes (what you should expect)
• Fewer no-shows via two-way confirmations
• Faster reschedules from “can’t make it” patients
• More filled chairs from waitlist outreach
• Clear reporting of recovered appointments and estimated recovered revenue

In-scope features (the wedge)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
• Patient receives reminders and replies Y/N
• “YES” confirms and logs status
• “NO” triggers an automated reschedule path

2) Smart reminders
• Configurable cadence (e.g., 72h / 24h / 2h)
• Message variations to reduce habituation
• Quiet hours and opt-out compliance language

3) Reschedule link (no phone tag)
• “Can’t make it?” message includes a reschedule flow
• Staff can approve/adjust if needed

4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
• Build a short-notice waitlist (patients opt in)
• When cancellations occur, system contacts waitlist candidates
• First-come confirmations are logged for staff follow-up

5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
• Confirmations vs. unconfirmed
• Cancellations recovered into reschedules
• Slots filled from waitlist
• Estimated revenue recovered (using your avg production/visit)

What’s explicitly out of scope (for the first 30 days)
• Deep PMS integrations requiring custom engineering
• Complex multi-department routing beyond a single location workflow
• Full CRM/marketing automation beyond reminders + waitlist
