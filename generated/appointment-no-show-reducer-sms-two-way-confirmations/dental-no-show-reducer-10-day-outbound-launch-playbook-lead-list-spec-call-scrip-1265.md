A) ICP + Qualification (Dental)
Primary ICP: single-location dental practices and small groups (2–10 locations) with a busy hygiene schedule.
Must-have qualifiers (ask on first call):
1) 15+ appointments/day OR 300+ appointments/month.
2) No-show/cancel-without-notice problem acknowledged by office manager/owner.
3) Uses a PMS (Dentrix, Open Dental, Eaglesoft, Curve, etc.) and can export a schedule (CSV or daily printout) OR allow a manual daily list.
4) Uses texting today OR is willing to text patients.
5) Decision maker reachable (owner, practice manager, or office manager who can implement).
Disqualifiers (skip or deprioritize): brand-new practice (<6 months), extremely low volume, refuses texting, no ability to export any schedule.
