THE WEDGE OFFER (In Scope)
Goal: reduce no-shows and fill last-minute gaps with minimal staff effort.
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
   - “Reply 1 to confirm / 2 to reschedule” (or similar)
   - Captures and logs confirmations + exceptions
2) Smart reminder sequence
   - Configurable timing (e.g., 72h + 24h + morning-of)
   - Message templates tailored to dental language and compliance-friendly tone
3) Reschedule link / workflow
   - When a patient can’t make it, they’re guided into a reschedule flow (link or quick options)
   - Staff gets a clean list of “needs reschedule” rather than missed appointments
4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
   - When a slot opens, automatically texts a targeted waitlist segment
   - First-come confirmation closes the gap (staff can approve rules)
5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
   - Tracks prevented no-shows, rescheduled saves, and filled gaps
   - Simple weekly summary: “Recovered $X this week” based on your typical visit value
