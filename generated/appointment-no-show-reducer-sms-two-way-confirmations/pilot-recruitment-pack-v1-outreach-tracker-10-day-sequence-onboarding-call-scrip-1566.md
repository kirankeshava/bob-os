1) Context (1 minute)

This is our legitimacy page: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2 and you can reach us at agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”

2) Qualify volume + pain (3 minutes)
- “About how many appointments per week per location?”
- “What’s your current no-show rate (even a rough estimate)?”
- “Average value per appointment (or average invoice)?”
- “Any specific times that are worst for no-shows (mornings, Mondays, etc.)?”

3) Baseline (2 minutes)
“We’ll track baseline vs pilot. If you have last 4 weeks counts of scheduled vs no-show, great. If not, we’ll start with your estimate and refine.”
Capture:
- appointments/week
- no-show %
- avg appointment value

4) Messaging + timing config (4 minutes)
- “Timezone + business hours?”
- “Reminder cadence preference (common: 24h + 3h; or 48h + 24h)?”
- “Do you want confirmations required (YES/NO) or just nudges?”
- “Reschedule window rules: allow reschedule up to X hours before?”
- “If someone replies NO, should we offer reschedule automatically or route to staff?”
- “Waitlist: do you have one? If yes, how do you want gap-fills handled?”

5) Consent + opt-out posture (3 minutes)
- “Do you already have SMS consent language in your intake forms?”
- “We include opt-out on messages. If someone replies STOP, we immediately stop. If they reply HELP, we provide support contact.”
- “We will only message your appointment holders for operational reminders/confirmations.”

6) Close + next steps (2 minutes)
“Let’s run a 7-day pilot starting {Date}. We’ll monitor daily and send the weekly report on {ReportDay}. If you like the results, we continue month-to-month; if not, we stop—no hard feelings.”
Confirm:
- Pilot start date
- Owner escalation contact (email/phone)
- Where to send weekly report
