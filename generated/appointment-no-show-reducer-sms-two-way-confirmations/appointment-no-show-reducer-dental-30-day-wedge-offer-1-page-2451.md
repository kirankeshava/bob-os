Narrow wedge offer (what’s in scope)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
• Reminder sequence before appointments
• Patients reply: CONFIRM / CANCEL (or similar)
• Staff sees a simple status view (confirmed / needs follow-up)

2) Smart reschedule link
• If patient cancels, they receive an automated reschedule prompt/link
• Goal: move cancellation into a rebooked slot, not a lost visit

3) Waitlist gap-filling (simple + effective)
• Build a waitlist of patients who want earlier openings
• When a slot opens, we automatically text the waitlist to fill the gap

4) Basic analytics (recovered revenue per location)
• Track: confirmations, cancellations caught early, reschedules completed, filled gaps
• Report a conservative “recovered chair time” estimate and revenue range
