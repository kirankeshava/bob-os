## The wedge offer (what’s included)
### 1) Two-way SMS confirmations (core)
- Text reminders that ask patients to reply **C** to confirm or **R** to reschedule
- Automatic tagging of outcomes: Confirmed / Needs reschedule / No response

### 2) Smart reminder sequences
- Multi-touch reminders (timing set to your preference)
- Escalation rules: if no response, send a follow-up prompt

### 3) Reschedule flow (reduces churned appointments)
- Simple reschedule link or assisted reschedule workflow
- Captures intent before the patient disappears

### 4) Waitlist fill for gaps
- When a cancellation happens, send an invite to your waitlist to fill the opening
- First-come/first-confirm logic (configurable)

### 5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Track: confirmations, reschedules saved, gaps filled
- Estimate **recovered production** based on your average appointment value (or hygiene vs treatment buckets if you provide them)

**Out of scope (for the 30-day wedge):** deep PMS integrations, custom mobile app, multi-step patient campaigns unrelated to appointments, complex enterprise reporting.
