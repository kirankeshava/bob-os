# Appointment No-Show Reducer (Dental)
## Reduce no-shows with 2-way SMS confirmations + automated reschedules + waitlist fills

**Who this is for (ICP):**
- **Dental practices** (single-location and small DSOs, 1–10 locations)
- Best fit: **80–600 appointments/month/location**, hygiene + doctor schedules, recurring recalls
- Decision makers: **Owner dentist / practice manager / office manager**

**The problem (why dental buys fast):**
No-shows and late cancels create immediate revenue leakage, idle chair time, and staff churn. Most practices still rely on manual calls/texts and inconsistent reminder workflows.

---
## The 30-day wedge: what we implement (in scope)
**1) Smart SMS reminders (multi-touch)**
- Automated reminder sequence tuned for dental (e.g., 72h / 24h / same-day)
- Messaging templates for hygiene vs. doctor, new patient vs. existing

**2) Two-way confirmations (YES/NO flows)**
- Patients reply **“YES”** to confirm
- If **“NO”** (or “reschedule”), we trigger the reschedule workflow

**3) Reschedule link + staff assist**
- One-tap reschedule link in SMS
- Optional: route “needs help” replies to front desk for manual slot selection

**4) Waitlist fill to backfill cancellations**
- Maintain a simple waitlist list (by preference/time windows)
- When a slot opens, send targeted outreach to fill the gap

**5) Recovered revenue analytics (per location)**
- Weekly snapshot: confirmations, saves (reschedules), filled gaps, estimated revenue recovered
- “Before/after” no-show rate trendline once baseline is established

**Out of scope (for the wedge):**
- Deep PMS integrations, complex multi-brand messaging, insurance/billing workflows, custom mobile apps
