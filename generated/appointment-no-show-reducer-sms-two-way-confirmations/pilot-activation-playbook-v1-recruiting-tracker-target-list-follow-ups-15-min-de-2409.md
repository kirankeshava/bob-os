2) 30-PROSPECT TARGET LIST (fastest-to-close niches + how to find)
Goal: target businesses with (a) high no-show pain, (b) booked schedules, (c) measurable $/appointment, (d) simple decision-making.

A) Highest-likelihood niches (prioritize in this order)
1. Med spas / aesthetics clinics
- Search: “med spa [city]”, “botox clinic [city]”, “laser hair removal [city]”
- Why: high ticket, frequent no-shows, owner-led decisions
2. Dental offices (esp. cosmetic/implants)
- Search: “dentist [city]”, “cosmetic dentist [city]”, “implant dentist [city]”
- Why: expensive chair time, clear value math
3. Physical therapy / chiropractic
- Search: “physical therapy [city]”, “chiropractor [city]”
- Why: recurring appointments, high drop-off/no-show risk
4. Independent salons with booked stylists (color specialists)
- Search: “hair color specialist [city]”, “balayage [city]”, “salon booking online [city]”
- Why: schedule gaps hurt, simple reminders help
5. Auto detailing / service centers (by appointment)
- Search: “auto detailing appointment [city]”, “car service appointment [city]”
- Why: missed slots are pure lost revenue

B) Prospecting rules (to get to 30 quickly)
- Pick ONE metro area to start (dense prospects = faster).
- Use Google Maps: open listing → capture website, phone, hours.
- Prefer businesses that: show “By appointment”, online booking link, or “Text us”.
- Prefer owner-operated or <5 locations (fast yes/no).

4) If someone cancels, we can optionally ping a waitlist to fill the gap.
