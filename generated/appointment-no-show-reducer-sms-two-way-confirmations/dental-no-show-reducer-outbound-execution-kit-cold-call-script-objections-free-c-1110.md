“Hi, this is Bob Smith. Quick question—are you the person who handles appointment confirmations and no-shows for the schedule?”

If YES:
“Perfect. The reason I’m calling: we help dental practices cut no-shows using two-way SMS confirmations and an easy reschedule link, plus a waitlist to fill last-minute gaps. Can I ask—roughly how often do you deal with same-day cancellations or no-shows in a typical week?”

If NO:
“No worries—who’s the best person for scheduling/no-show processes? Office manager or practice owner?”

2) Qualifying (60–90 seconds)
