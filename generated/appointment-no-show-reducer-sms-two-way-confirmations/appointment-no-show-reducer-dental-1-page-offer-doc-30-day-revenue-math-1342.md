### Wedge Offer (What’s In Scope)
**Goal:** reduce no-shows + capture cancellations by converting uncertainty into confirmations and by filling gaps from a waitlist.

**Included (per location):**
1) **Two-way SMS confirmations**
- Patients receive SMS reminders and can reply **“1” to confirm** or **“2” to reschedule** (or similar)
- Staff can see responses and status

2) **Smart reminder cadence** (simple, proven)
- Example: 72h + 24h + morning-of (adjustable)
- Rules to avoid over-texting

3) **Reschedule link / workflow**
- If a patient indicates they can’t make it, they are routed into a reschedule flow (link or staff-assisted)

4) **Waitlist fill workflow**
- When a slot opens, send an SMS blast to the waitlist segment
- First-come confirmations are captured and routed back to staff

5) **Recovered revenue analytics (basic, practical)**
- Track: confirmations, reschedules saved, cancellations filled, estimated revenue recovered
- Simple weekly summary per location

**Out of scope (for the 30-day wedge):** deep PMS integration development, custom apps, complex multi-department routing, and extensive patient marketing campaigns.
