OBJECTION HANDLING (SHORT)
1) “We already text patients.”
“Totally—most do. The difference is two-way confirmations + automated reschedule + waitlist fill, and we track recovered appointments so you can prove ROI. Worth a 15-minute look?”

2) “We don’t have time / we’re busy.”
“That’s exactly why it helps—less manual calling and fewer holes in the schedule. The setup is lightweight; we can start from a daily schedule export during the pilot. Can we pencil 15 minutes?”

3) “Patients don’t respond.”
“Even partial response rates reduce uncertainty. The big lift is catching ‘I need to reschedule’ early and filling the gap. If it doesn’t reduce no-shows measurably, you can cancel—no long-term contract.”

4) “Send info.”
“Absolutely. What’s the best email? I’ll send the overview link and two bullet points. After you look, should I follow up tomorrow or the next day?”
(Then send email immediately with URL + contact email.)

5) “We can’t change systems / integration concerns.”
“We can start without deep integration. For the pilot, we just need your upcoming appointments (export/CSV) and your preferred reminder rules. If it works, we can discuss tighter workflows.”

• Recover Y appointments/week (even 2–3 is meaningful)
• Reduce manual confirmation calls by Z hours/week
