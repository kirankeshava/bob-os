SECTION 5) PILOT SUCCESS CRITERIA (WHAT “WORKING” MEANS)
Track daily and roll up weekly:
1) Delivery rate: > 95% messages delivered (or documented reasons)
2) Confirmation rate: % of appointments with explicit YES (target: baseline + meaningful lift)
3) Reschedule capture: # reschedules handled via text vs lost no-shows
4) No-show rate delta: compare to baseline (last 4 weeks if available)
5) Waitlist fill (if enabled): # openings filled + time-to-fill
6) Estimated recovered revenue/week:
Recovered Revenue = (Prevented No-Shows * Avg Appointment Value) + (Filled Waitlist Slots * Avg Appointment Value)
Where Prevented No-Shows = (Expected No-Shows from baseline – Observed No-Shows during pilot)
