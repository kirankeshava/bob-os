## 2) Lead List Build Spec (Free Sources)
**Primary ICP:** Single-location dental practices and small groups (2–10 locations).  
**Best signals (prioritize):**
- 4+ operatories / multiple dentists listed
- Strong hygiene cadence (“hygiene appointments available”) on site
- Google rating count **200+** (proxy for volume)
- Open evenings/weekends (proxy for schedule density)

**Where to find leads (free):**
1) **Google Maps** search queries:
   - “Dentist [City]”
   - “Family dentistry [City]”
   - “Cosmetic dentist [City]”
   - “Dental clinic [City]”
2) Practice websites (Contact/About pages)
3) Facebook pages (often list office manager)
4) State dental association directory (if publicly accessible)

**Target geographies (pick 2–3 metros to start):**
- Choose cities with many independent practices (not dominated by DSOs). Example: Phoenix, Dallas, Tampa, Denver, Charlotte (final selection can be arbitrary—just commit and build density).

**Data fields to capture per lead:**
- Practice name
- Location (city/state)
- Website
- Phone
- Owner/dentist name (if listed)
- Office manager name (if listed)
- Email (front desk/hello/info + any direct emails)
- Notes (signals: multi-chair, hygiene focus, staffing mention)
