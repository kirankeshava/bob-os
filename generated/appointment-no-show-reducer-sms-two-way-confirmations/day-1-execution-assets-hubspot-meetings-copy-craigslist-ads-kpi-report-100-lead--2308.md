## 4) 100-lead “Quick Pull” method (free sources; 60–90 minutes)
Goal: assemble 100 prospects with business name, website, phone, and (if visible) owner/manager email.

**City cluster A (example):** Phoenix AZ + Scottsdale AZ
**City cluster B (example):** Tampa FL + St. Petersburg FL

**Verticals + Google Maps queries (copy/paste):**
1) Dentist: "dentist" + [city]
2) Chiropractor: "chiropractor" + [city]
3) Med spa: "med spa" + [city] (also try "aesthetic clinic")
4) Physical therapy: "physical therapy" + [city]
5) Optometry: "optometrist" + [city]

**How to capture fast (per listing):**
- Open listing → open website → look for Contact/About
- Capture: Business Name | City | Phone | Website | Contact Email (if listed) | Notes (hours, #locations, any mention of no-shows/waitlist)
- If no email on site: capture contact form URL and use phone-first for that lead.

**Target mix (100 total):**
- 20 per vertical across the two clusters (10/vertical/cluster)

**Qualification notes to tag in CRM:**
- If multiple locations: tag “multi-location”
- If online booking present: tag “online booking”
- If hours show heavy booking: tag “high volume (guess)”

Once the 100 are captured, import to HubSpot and run Day-1: 50–100 emails + 20–40 calls with the prepared scripts.
