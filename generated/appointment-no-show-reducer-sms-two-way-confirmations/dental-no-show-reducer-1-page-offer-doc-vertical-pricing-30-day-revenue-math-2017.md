## The Wedge Offer (Scope for first 30 days)
### 1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Automated reminders (timing rules by appointment type)
- Patient can reply **CONFIRM / C / YES** to confirm
- Patient can reply **RESCHEDULE** to start rescheduling flow

### 2) Reschedule link + automation
- One-tap reschedule link (practice-approved workflow)
- Captures reason + preferred times
- Routes to front desk or scheduling workflow to close the loop fast

### 3) Waitlist fill for last-minute openings
- Patients opt into “earlier appointment” list
- When a cancellation happens, system texts the waitlist in order
- First-come confirmation reserves the slot (practice can approve if desired)

### 4) Recovered-revenue analytics (simple, per location)
- Confirm rate, reschedule rate, waitlist fills
- Estimated recovered production based on your average appointment value
- Weekly summary report to owner/manager

**Not in scope (initial wedge):** full PMS deep integration, complex multi-department routing, outbound review requests, marketing campaigns.

## ROI Story (how we justify this in 60 seconds)
Dental no-shows are expensive because they create unusable chair time. If a practice recovers **even 2 appointments/month** (hygiene or doctor) through confirmations/reschedules/waitlist fills, the system typically pays for itself.

**Simple math example:**
- Average value per missed appointment: $150–$350+ (varies by procedure)
- Recover 3 missed appointments/month = $450–$1,050+ recovered
- Cost: $399/month + SMS pass-through

We show this as a weekly “recovered production” estimate so the owner sees the impact.
