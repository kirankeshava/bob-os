POST 1 (Education)
Title: Quick way to cut no-shows without hiring more front desk staff
Text:
One of the simplest operational fixes I’ve seen is switching reminders from “one-way” to “two-way.”

Instead of “Reminder: your appointment is tomorrow,” use: “Reply YES to confirm or NO to reschedule.”

Why it works:
- You surface conflicts early
- You create a clean reschedule path (instead of ghosting)
- Staff time shifts from chasing people to filling openings

If you want, I can share a simple reminder cadence that works well for clinics/med spas/dental.
