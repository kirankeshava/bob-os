B) LEAD LIST BUILD SPEC (DENTAL)
Target account type:
- Single-location dental practices OR small groups (2–10 locations).
- Appointment-heavy (hygiene recalls + restorative), not purely specialty referral-only.
- Visible online booking or clear phone-based appointment scheduling.
- Has staff (front desk/office manager); avoid solo-only practices with no admin.

Best metros to start (pick 2–3 for density + reachable time zones):

- Secondary: Tampa, Denver, Charlotte

Data fields to collect (Google Sheet columns):
- Practice Name
- Website URL
- Main Phone
- Address / City
- Owner name (if available)
- Office Manager name (priority)
- Front Desk lead name (optional)
- Emails (office manager + info@ + scheduling@)
- LinkedIn URL (owner/office manager)
- PMS hint (if listed: Dentrix/OpenDental/Eaglesoft)
- Notes (new patient specials, online booking, etc.)
- Status (Not contacted / Emailed / Replied / Booked / No show / Closed won / Closed lost)
- Next step date

Where to source (free):
1) Google Maps: search “dentist”, “family dentist”, “dental clinic” in chosen metros.
2) Yelp: filter by “Dentists” and location.
3) Zocdoc: practices with active scheduling (often high no-show risk).
4) State dental association directories (often have practice listings).
5) Practice websites: scrape “Contact” + “Team” pages for office manager name.

Prioritization scoring (quick 0–5):
- 2 points: online booking OR “request appointment” form
- 1 point: multiple providers listed (>=2 dentists/hygienists)
- 1 point: open evenings/weekends (more scheduling volume)
- 1 point: multiple locations (group)
Work the highest score first.
