## 5) 15-minute Onboarding Call Script (concierge pilot)
Goal: qualify + capture baseline + confirm consent + define reminder rules + agree success metrics.

1) Context (1 min)
- “Thanks — goal is to reduce no-shows without creating more front-desk work. Pilot is 7 days, concierge setup, and we’ll report outcomes weekly.”

2) Quick qualification (3 min)
- How many appointments/week (approx.)?
- Biggest pain: no-shows, late cancels, reschedules, gaps?
- Avg appointment value (rough)?

3) Baseline metrics (3 min)
- “Over the last ~4 weeks, what’s your rough no-show rate?”
- “Late cancellations (same-day) roughly?”
- “If you track it: how many unfilled gaps/week?”

4) Workflow rules (5 min)
- Timezone + business hours
- Reminder timing: e.g., 24h + 2h before
- Confirmation: clients reply YES to confirm
- If NO / RESCHEDULE: preferred action (manual call vs. link vs. propose times)
- Waitlist: do they have one? how to contact? (text list / call list)

5) Compliance + safety (2 min)
- “Messages include STOP to opt-out and HELP for support. If any integration fails, we alert you and pause automation.”
- Confirm they only message clients with permission (existing customers / appointment reminders).

6) Success criteria + next steps (1 min)
- “We’ll track confirmations, reschedules, opt-outs, and estimate recovered revenue weekly. If you like results, we can continue after the pilot.”

Close:
- Confirm best owner/manager contact for escalations.
- Confirm go-live date/time.
