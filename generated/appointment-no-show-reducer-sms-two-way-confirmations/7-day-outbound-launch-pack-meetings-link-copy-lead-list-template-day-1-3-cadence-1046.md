## C) Free Lead Sourcing SOP (fast, repeatable)
**Goal:** 200 leads for 2 city clusters in 1–2 days without paid tools.

1) Pick 2 city clusters (example: Phoenix AZ + Mesa AZ; or Dallas TX + Plano TX).
2) For each vertical, run Google Maps searches:
- “dentist Phoenix AZ”
- “chiropractor Phoenix AZ”
- “med spa Phoenix AZ”
- “physical therapy Phoenix AZ”
- “optometrist Phoenix AZ”
3) Open each listing → capture: company name, phone, website, GBP URL.
4) Find decision-maker contact:
- Website → About/Team/Contact pages
- Look for: Owner, Practice Manager, Office Manager, Clinic Manager
- If no email listed: use contact form + capture generic email if present (info@, hello@). If nothing, keep phone-only and route to call/SMS.
5) Add Notes: “Online booking?”, “Multiple locations?”, “Open weekends?”, “Mentions no-show policy?”

**Minimum viable record:** company name + location + phone + website. Email is a bonus; calls can still book demos.
