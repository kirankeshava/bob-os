Qualification checklist (fast)
Ask these on the first call/demo:
1) “Roughly how many appointments do you have per day?” (target 15+)
2) “What’s your no-show or last-minute cancellation situation like?” (any pain works; best if 3–10%+)
3) “How do reminders go out today—manual calls, texting, or your PMS?”
4) “Do patients confirm by replying, and does that update your schedule automatically (or do staff chase it)?”
5) “When someone cancels late, do you have a waitlist you actually use to fill the gap?”
6) “If we reliably fill even 2–4 extra appointments/month, would that be meaningful?” (should be an obvious yes)
7) “Who besides you needs to sign off to start?”
