WHO THIS IS FOR (ICP)

PROBLEM (WHY NOW)
No-shows and late cancellations create same-day holes that are hard to backfill. Every hole is lost production, wasted staff time, and lower patient care throughput. Most reminder tools send one-way blasts; they don’t reliably capture confirmations, route reschedules, or activate a waitlist fast enough.

THE 30-DAY WEDGE OFFER (WHAT YOU GET)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Patients can reply CONFIRM / CANCEL / RESCHEDULE (or simple Yes/No).
- Non-responders get smart follow-ups.

2) Smart reminder sequences
- Typical cadence: 72h, 24h, 2h (customizable by appointment type).
- Message templates tuned for dental (hygiene vs. operative vs. consult).

3) Reschedule link + assisted rescheduling
- Patients who can’t make it are routed to a reschedule flow (link) or a controlled handoff back to the front desk.

4) Waitlist fill (same-day gap recovery)
- When a cancellation happens, the system texts a targeted waitlist segment to fill the opening.
- Simple rules: by provider, procedure type, time window.

5) Recovered revenue analytics (basic, per location)
- Track: confirmation rate, cancellation lead time, same-day holes reduced, waitlist fills.
- Report an estimate of production recovered (you set average value per appointment type).
