3) WEDGE OFFER (What’s In Scope)
Goal: reduce no-shows and fill gaps automatically with minimal staff effort.

A) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Reminder sequence (e.g., 72h / 24h / 2h)
- Patient replies: CONFIRM / CANCEL / RESCHEDULE
- Auto-tag outcomes and alert staff when needed

B) Reschedule link workflow
- When patient cancels/requests reschedule, send a link (or guided SMS flow) to pick a new time
- Reduces phone calls and saves the appointment slot faster

C) Waitlist gap fill
- Maintain a waitlist of patients who want earlier times
- When a slot opens, send targeted SMS offers to fill the gap

D) Basic recovered-revenue analytics
- Weekly snapshot per location:
  - confirmations collected
  - cancellations captured early
  - appointments rescheduled
  - waitlist fills
  - estimated revenue recovered (practice provides average production value per appointment type, or we use a conservative default until refined)
