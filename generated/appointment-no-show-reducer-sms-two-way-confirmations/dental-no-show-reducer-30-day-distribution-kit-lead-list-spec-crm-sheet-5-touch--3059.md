## ICP + list-building wedge (what to target)
**Primary ICP:** single-location dental practices and small groups (2–10 locations) doing hygiene + restorative scheduling.

**Best-fit signals (qualifying before outreach):**
- Takes appointments by phone/text (front desk staffed)
- Has hygiene schedule (high volume) + frequent reschedules
- Open 4–6 days/week, multiple providers
- Mentions “text us” or online forms (indicates willingness to use SMS)

**Disqualifiers (skip initially):**
- Emergency-only clinics
- Very new practices with tiny schedule
- Practices already advertising a sophisticated two-way confirmation platform prominently (not impossible, just lower win rate week 1)
