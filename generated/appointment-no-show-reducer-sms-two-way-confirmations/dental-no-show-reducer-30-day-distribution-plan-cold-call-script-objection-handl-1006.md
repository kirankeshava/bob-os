1) **‘We already send reminders.’**
“Totally—most offices do. The difference is **two-way** confirmation + automated reschedule + **waitlist fill**. Reminders notify; this actually converts ‘can’t make it’ into a reschedule and tries to refill the opening. Worth 15 minutes to compare?”

“Agreed. This is minimal messaging—only for upcoming appointments and only to people already scheduled. Patients can confirm with one word. The goal is fewer calls and fewer gaps.”

3) **‘We don’t have time to set this up.’**
“That’s why we do concierge setup. We only need your next 2–4 weeks schedule export and your messaging preferences. Most offices are live in a few days.”

“For one location it’s **$399/month** plus SMS pass-through, and right now setup is **$499 one-time**. It’s designed to pay for itself if it saves even a couple appointments. If you want, I can walk you through the math on a 15-minute call.”

“Happy to. What’s the best email? I’ll send a short overview and a link to the proof page. Also—so I don’t send generic info—are your gaps mainly hygiene or procedures?”
