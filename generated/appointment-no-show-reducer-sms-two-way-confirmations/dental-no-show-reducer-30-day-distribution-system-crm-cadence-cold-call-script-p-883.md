## ICP (dental) + quick qualification
### Ideal customer profile
- General dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, perio; single location or 2–10 locations.
- 1–8 provider chairs with steady appointment volume.
- Front desk staffed (at least 1 full-time coordinator).
- Using a PMS (Dentrix, Open Dental, Eaglesoft, etc.) OR can export daily appointment lists.

### Qualifying questions (ask in first 5 minutes)
1) “Roughly how many appointments per day, and what % no-show or late-cancel do you see?”
2) “Do you currently text patients reminders? If yes, can patients reply ‘C’ to confirm or text to reschedule?”
3) “When someone cancels last-minute, do you have a waitlist and how do you fill gaps today?”
4) “Who owns the schedule health metric—office manager, front desk lead, or owner?”
5) “If we could reliably recover even 3–5 appointments/month, is that worth solving this quarter?”

**Green lights:** no-show/late-cancel pain, no two-way confirmations, manual rescheduling burden, empty-chair gaps, wants measurable ROI.
