2) 15-MINUTE PILOT CLOSE CALL SCRIPT (Concierge pilot)

Goal: book pilot + secure baseline numbers + confirm consent + schedule go-live.

Intro (1 min)
“Hey {Name}, thanks for taking 15 minutes. I’m Bob. Quick context: we help appointment-based businesses cut no-shows using two-way SMS confirmations and automated reschedules. If it’s useful, we’ll launch a free 7-day pilot and measure results; if not, we’ll stop.”

Discovery (4 min)
1) “Roughly how many appointments do you run per week?”
2) “What’s the typical no-show rate (even a guess)?”
3) “Average value per appointment (or first visit)?”
4) “What’s your current reminder process (if any)?”
5) “Do you ever have last-minute cancellations that could be filled from a waitlist?”

Quantify (2 min)
“Even a small change matters. Example: if you do {Appts/Week} per week and no-shows drop by {X} appointments, at ${AvgValue} each, that’s about ${Recovered}/week recovered.”

Offer (3 min)
“Here’s the pilot: we run two-way SMS confirmations + reschedules for 7 days. We set it up concierge-style, monitor daily, and send a weekly value report with confirmations/reschedules/fills and an estimate of revenue recovered.”

Risk reversal + reliability (2 min)
“We also bake in fail-safes: STOP/HELP handling, time zone checks, and if anything fails (like a calendar update), we alert you immediately and fall back safely.”

Close (3 min)
“Want to be one of the 2–3 pilot locations? If yes, we can go live within 48 hours.
Next steps are simple: we collect your reminder timing rules, business hours/timezone, and who gets alerts. Then we run a small preflight test and start.”

Scheduling line:
“Which is better for go-live: {Date/Time Option A} or {Option B}?”

3) PILOT REQUIREMENTS + CONSENT CHECKLIST (One page)

This reduces onboarding delays and prevents compliance issues.

A) Required setup info
- Business name + address
- Primary timezone (critical for reminders and DST)
- Business hours + closed days
- Services/appointment types (if different rules by type)
- Reminder schedule (recommended starting point):
  - 24 hours before: reminder + confirm prompt
  - 2 hours before: reminder for unconfirmed only
- Reschedule rules:
  - Same-day reschedule allowed? (yes/no)
  - Minimum notice window (e.g., 2 hours)
- Waitlist rules (optional):
  - How to prioritize (first-come, VIP, etc.)
  - How late to offer openings (e.g., until 2 hours before)

B) Consent/opt-in and compliance
- Confirm your clients have consented to receive SMS reminders (existing relationship + standard appointment messaging).
- All messages include opt-out language where required (e.g., “Reply STOP to opt out”).
- We honor STOP immediately and do not message that number again.
- HELP replies get a human-readable support response and the support email: agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to

C) Owner escalation contact (fail-safes)
- Name + phone/email for urgent issues
- Backup contact
- Preferred alert method if an integration fails (SMS/email)

D) Baseline metrics to capture (for the weekly report)
- Last 2–4 weeks: appointments scheduled per week
- No-shows per week (or estimated %)
- Average appointment value (or contribution margin proxy)

E) Pilot success criteria (agree before go-live)
- Target: increase confirmations and reduce no-shows vs baseline
- Deliverable: weekly value report with measurable deltas and recovered revenue estimate
