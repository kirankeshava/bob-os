APPOINTMENT NO-SHOW REDUCER (DENTAL) — 30-DAY WEDGE OFFER

What it is
A lightweight “no-show prevention + gap-filling” system for dental practices. We reduce missed appointments by sending smart SMS reminders, collecting two-way confirmations, making it easy to reschedule, and filling last-minute openings from a waitlist. Includes basic analytics that estimates recovered revenue per location.

Primary vertical (first 30 days)
Dental practices (single-location and small groups/DSOs up to ~10 locations).
Why dental: high appointment density, meaningful no-show cost, and a relatively fast decision cycle (owner/operator + office manager).

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- General dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, perio, ortho, endo with recurring scheduled visits.
- 1–10 locations, with 1+ dedicated front-desk/office manager.
- Feels daily/weekly pain from: last-minute cancellations, no-shows, unconfirmed patients, and “hole-punching” schedules.
- Has a consistent appointment book (at least ~15 appointments/day).

The wedge offer (in scope)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Automated reminders leading up to appointments
- Patients reply YES/NO to confirm/cancel
- Confirmation status tracked per appointment

2) Reschedule link (for “NO” or non-confirmers)
- Simple reschedule workflow to prevent lost visits
- Keeps staff from playing phone tag

3) Waitlist fill
- When a cancellation happens, message a waitlist segment
- First responders get slotted into the opening (simple rules-based flow)

4) Recovered-revenue analytics (basic)
- Dashboard/report per location
- Tracks: confirmation rate, cancellation lead time, filled openings, estimated revenue recovered

Out of scope (for this 30-day wedge)
- Deep PMS integrations requiring custom engineering
- Complex multi-department routing/IVR
- Custom apps for patients

Implementation (concierge setup)
Week 1 launch plan (what we handle):
- Configure practice hours, reminder timing, and confirmation keywords
- Import upcoming appointment list (CSV export is sufficient)
- Set up reschedule link workflow and waitlist rules
- Go live within 3–7 days depending on data access

Pricing (per location)
- $399/month per location
- SMS usage billed at pass-through cost (no markup), visible monthly
- Limited-time concierge setup: $399 one-time per location (includes initial configuration + first import + staff handoff)

Why this is easy to say yes to (ROI story)
Dental no-shows are expensive because they create same-day holes that rarely get filled. If we prevent or backfill just 2–4 appointments/month, most practices cover the monthly fee.
Simple framing:
- If an average visit is worth $200–$350+, preventing/filling a handful of missed visits typically pays for the service.
- The product doesn’t require patients to download anything; it fits front-desk workflow.
