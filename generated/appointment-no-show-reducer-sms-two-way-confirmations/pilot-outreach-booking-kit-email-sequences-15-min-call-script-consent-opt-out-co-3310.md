4) 15-MINUTE PILOT QUALIFICATION / DEMO CALL SCRIPT
Goal: book pilot start date, capture baseline metrics, confirm consent/opt-out, and define success.

Minute 0–2: Context
- “Thanks—what’s your role and how are appointments booked today?”
- “Roughly how many appointments/week and what’s the current no-show or late-cancel situation?”

Minute 2–6: Pain + baseline
- “What happens operationally when someone no-shows?”
- “Do you have a waitlist? If yes, how do you fill gaps today?”
- Capture baseline (best estimate is fine):
  - appts/week, no-shows/week, avg appointment value, timezone, business hours

Minute 6–10: How the pilot works (concierge)
- “We send SMS reminders and collect confirmations (YES/NO/RESCHEDULE). If they can’t make it, we help reschedule and optionally offer openings to a waitlist.”
- “During the pilot you get a weekly value report: confirmations, reschedules, gaps filled, and recovered revenue estimate.”

Minute 10–13: Compliance + edge cases
- “We include STOP to opt out on every message. If someone replies STOP, they’re suppressed immediately.”
- “If anything fails (calendar/booking sync), we alert you and pause sending rather than spam customers.”

Minute 13–15: Close
- “If we start {{Day}}, we’ll run 7 days and send a report at the end. If it’s clearly working, we can talk about continuing after the free pilot.”
- Confirm next steps: intake questions, test number, and go-live date.
