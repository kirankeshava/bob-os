OBJECTION HANDLING (DENTAL-SPECIFIC)
1) “We already use reminders”
“Totally—most offices do. The difference is two-way confirmations + automated reschedules + waitlist fill. Reminders alone don’t capture a clean YES/NO and don’t automatically backfill openings. If you’re open to it, I can ask 2 questions and tell you if it’s redundant.”

2) “We don’t have a big no-show problem”
“Good—that usually means you’re doing something right. Even then, most practices still have last-minute cancels. If we could recover just 2 appointments a week, it typically pays for itself. Want to sanity-check with a 15-min look?”

3) “We’re too busy / no time to implement”
“That’s exactly why we keep it light: we set up templates, timing rules, and the waitlist flow for you. You just tell us how you want confirmations handled. We aim to launch in about a week.”

4) “How much is it?”
“$399 per month per location, plus SMS pass-through, and a one-time concierge setup (typically $499) to launch quickly. I can send the full breakdown by email—what’s best?”

5) “Send me info”
