4) Qualification checklist (use on call)
A practice is a “yes” if:
- 1+ providers OR active hygiene schedule with daily appointments
- They experience no-shows/late cancels weekly (even 1–3/week)
- They currently confirm by phone, manual texting, or one-way reminders
- They can provide an appointment export (CSV) or allow a daily schedule snapshot
- They have a clear decision maker (owner/office manager) who can approve a 7-day free launch
A practice is a “no/later” if:
- Very low appointment volume (<10/day) and no meaningful no-show impact
- No ability/willingness to text patients (rare)
- No operational owner (can’t decide anything)
