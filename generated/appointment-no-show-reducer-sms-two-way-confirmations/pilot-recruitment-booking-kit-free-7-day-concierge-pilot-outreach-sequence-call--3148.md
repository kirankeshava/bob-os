C) 15-MINUTE DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (pilot qualification)
Objective: qualify + secure pilot start date + collect baseline metrics.

0:00–1:30 Intro
“Thanks {{Name}}. I’m Bob. We help appointment-based businesses cut no-shows with SMS reminders + two-way confirmations and a simple weekly value report. This is a free 7-day concierge pilot. I’ll ask a few questions, then if it fits, we’ll outline the setup and a go-live date.”

1:30–6:00 Current state & pain
1) “About how many appointments do you run per week?”
2) “Roughly what’s your no-show rate or late-cancel rate today?”
3) “What do you currently use for reminders (manual calls/texts, your PMS, another tool)?”
4) “Where does it hurt most: lost revenue, staff idle time, or schedule gaps?”

6:00–9:00 Workflow fit
5) “Do you want confirmations required (YES/NO), or just reminders?”
6) “When someone says ‘No’ or ‘reschedule’, who should handle it—our automation first, or your front desk immediately?”
7) “Do you keep a waitlist for openings? If yes, how do you fill cancellations today?”

9:00–12:00 Compliance & escalation
8) “How do patients/clients provide consent for SMS today (forms, online booking checkbox, intake, etc.)?”
9) “We always include STOP to opt out and HELP for support. Any internal policy constraints?”
10) “Who should we alert if something fails (calendar/API issue) or if a message looks risky/unclear?”

12:00–14:00 Baseline metrics (5 minutes, even if gathered after)
“To quantify results, can you share last 4 weeks totals:
- scheduled appointments
- attended
- no-shows
- typical appointment value (or average revenue per visit)
If you don’t have exact numbers, estimates are fine for the first report.”

14:00–15:00 Close
“If you’re good with it, let’s pick a pilot start date. We’ll send a short pilot confirmation email with what we need, and you’ll get a weekly value report. Best email for that is {{email}}?”
