5) Objection Handling (tight, dental-specific)
Objection: “We already send reminders from our software.”
Response: “That’s common. The difference is two-way confirmation + automated reschedule + waitlist fill. Most default reminders are one-way and don’t recover cancellations fast. We measure recovered appointments so you can see if it’s incremental.”

Objection: “Patients don’t like texts.”
Response: “We only message opted-in patients and keep it minimal: confirm, reschedule, or stop. In dental, patients generally prefer text over voicemail. If anyone opts out, they’re suppressed automatically.”

Objection: “We’re too busy / no time to set up.”
Response: “That’s why we do concierge setup. We just need your appointment export and your confirmation/reschedule preferences. Pilot can be live in 48 hours.”
