THE WEDGE OFFER (What we implement in week 1)
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
   - Patients receive reminders and can reply C to confirm or R to reschedule
   - Non-responders get an additional nudge

2) Smart reminder cadence
   - Typical flow: 72h + 24h + 2h (configurable by appointment type)

3) Reschedule link (frictionless)
   - If a patient can’t make it, they can reschedule via link instead of disappearing

4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
   - When a cancellation occurs, we text the waitlist to fill openings fast

5) Basic analytics: “Recovered revenue” per location
   - Tracks confirmations, reschedules, fills, and estimated chair-time recovered
   - Simple weekly summary + monthly rollup
