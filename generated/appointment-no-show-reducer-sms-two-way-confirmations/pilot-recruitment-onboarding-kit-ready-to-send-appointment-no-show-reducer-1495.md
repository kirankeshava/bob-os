4) 15-Min Onboarding Call Script (Concierge Pilot)
Goal: confirm fit, collect baseline, set reminder rules, and confirm opt-in/consent.

A) Fit + baseline (3–4 min)
- Roughly how many appointments per week?
- Current no-show rate (or best estimate)?
- Average appointment value (or average revenue per visit)?
- Biggest failure mode: forgetfulness, late cancellations, or scheduling confusion?

B) Operations rules (6–7 min)
- Business timezone + hours
- Services that should NOT be auto-rescheduled (if any)
- Reminder timing: e.g., 24h + 2h before, or 48h + 4h
- Confirmation wording preference (short/neutral vs friendly)
- Reschedule rules: allow reschedules up to X hours before? Same-day?
- Waitlist: do you have one now? If yes, how do we contact them and in what order?

C) Compliance + escalation (3–4 min)
- Confirm you have consent to text clients (booking flow already captures mobile + permission). If not, we can run reminder-only to those who opted in.
- Escalation contact for failures (owner/manager phone + email)
- Confirm STOP/HELP handling requirement

D) Close (1 min)
- “We’ll launch with a small subset first (e.g., tomorrow’s appointments) and monitor daily. You’ll get a weekly report at day 7 with recovered revenue estimate.”

7) Baseline-to-Outcome Metric Calculation (simple)
- Baseline no-show rate = (no-shows last 4 weeks) / (appointments last 4 weeks)
- Pilot no-show rate = (no-shows during pilot) / (appointments during pilot)
- No-shows avoided = (baseline rate – pilot rate) * appointments during pilot
- Estimated recovered revenue = no-shows avoided * average appointment value
