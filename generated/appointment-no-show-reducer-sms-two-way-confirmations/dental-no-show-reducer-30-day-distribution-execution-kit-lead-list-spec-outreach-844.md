OBJECTION HANDLING (short, dental-specific)

1) “We already send reminders.”
“Totally—most offices do. The difference is two-way confirmations and automated reschedules. One-way reminders still leave you discovering a no-show at the appointment time. If we can get a ‘Confirm/Reschedule’ reply 24–48 hours earlier, you can fill that chair time. Are your reminders two-way today?”

2) “Patients don’t respond / we tried texting.”
“That’s common when the message is generic. We use short choice-based prompts—CONFIRM or RESCHEDULE—and we follow up intelligently based on non-response. Even a small lift matters. If you recovered just 2 hygiene slots/month, would that be meaningful?”

3) “We don’t have time to manage another system.”
“That’s exactly why it’s automated. Setup is concierge-style; after that, your team mainly handles exceptions—patients who choose reschedule. We can start with just confirmations + reschedule link and add waitlist later.”

4) “What does it cost?”
“It’s $399/month per location plus SMS pass-through, and a one-time concierge setup ($299–$499 depending on complexity). If it doesn’t measurably reduce holes in the first 14 days, you can cancel risk-free. The quick question is: roughly what’s one missed hygiene visit worth to you?”

5) “Send me info.”
