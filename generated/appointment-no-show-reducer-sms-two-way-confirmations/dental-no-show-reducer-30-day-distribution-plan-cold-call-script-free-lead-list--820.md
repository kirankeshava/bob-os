## Cold-call script (front desk → office manager)
**Goal:** get to office manager and book a 15-minute demo. Keep it tight.

“Hi—this is Bob. Quick question: who handles appointment confirmations and last-minute reschedules for the practice—an office manager or someone up front?”

If they ask “what is this about?”
“Totally—calling because we help dental practices reduce no-shows with two-way SMS confirmations and quick rescheduling. I just need to speak with the person who owns scheduling for 30 seconds.”

### When you reach the office manager (or scheduler)
“Hi [Name], Bob here. I’ll be brief. We help dental offices cut hygiene and chair-time no-shows using two-way text confirmations and an easy reschedule link, and when there’s a cancellation we can text a waitlist to backfill. It’s simple, and we show recovered revenue per month.

“Got it. The quickest next step is a 15-minute walkthrough. I’ll show how confirmations + reschedules + waitlist fill work and what we’d need from you to launch in a week. What’s better—today after lunch or tomorrow morning?”

If I can show that in 15 minutes, is it worth a look?”
