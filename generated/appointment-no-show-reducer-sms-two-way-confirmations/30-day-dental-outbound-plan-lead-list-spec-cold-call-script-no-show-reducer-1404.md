OBJECTION HANDLING (quick hits)
1) “We don’t have no-shows.”
“Great—then this will be a fast ‘no’. Roughly how many last-minute cancels or same-day holes do you see in a typical week? Even 2–3 can be meaningful.”
2) “Patients hate texts.”
“We only message patients who’ve opted into SMS and we keep it minimal. Two-way confirmations usually reduce back-and-forth because patients can confirm or reschedule in seconds.”
3) “We’re too busy to implement.”
“That’s why we include concierge setup. We can launch in ~7 days if you can export your schedule or provide appointment data. Minimal staff time.”
4) “We use [PMS]—does it integrate?”
“We start lightweight: schedule export or simple daily feed. If deeper integration is needed later, we can scope it after proving ROI.”
