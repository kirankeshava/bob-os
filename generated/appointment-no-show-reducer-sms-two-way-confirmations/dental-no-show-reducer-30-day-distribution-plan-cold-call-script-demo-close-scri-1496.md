3) QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST (ASK ON FIRST CALL)
Operational:
- How many providers (dentists, hygienists)?
- Roughly how many appointments/day? Hygiene mix?
- How are reminders done today (manual calls, PMS reminders, generic texting tool)?
- Where do no-shows hit most (hygiene recall, new patient, production appointments)?

Economics:
- How many no-shows/week or late cancels/week (rough estimate OK)?
- Average production per missed slot (hygiene vs dentist)?
- What’s the value of filling even 2–5 extra slots/week?

Workflow fit:
- Can we send SMS reminders + receive confirmations? (Yes/No)
- Who will handle “reschedule requests” that come in (office manager/front desk)?
- Do you maintain any waitlist today (even informal)?

Decision + timing:
- Who decides (owner + office manager)?
- If we launch in 7 days, are you open to a 30-day pilot?
