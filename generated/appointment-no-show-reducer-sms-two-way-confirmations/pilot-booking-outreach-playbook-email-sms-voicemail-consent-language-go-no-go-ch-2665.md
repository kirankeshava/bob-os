PILOT GO/NO-GO READINESS CHECKLIST (to avoid failed installs)
GO if all are true:
- Clear timezone and business hours are provided.
- Appointment types have average value (rough estimate is fine).
- There is a single source of truth for appointments (calendar/system) and a named owner contact for escalations.
- The business agrees to include STOP/HELP language and respects opt-outs.
- There is a defined reschedule policy (how far out, same provider vs any provider, etc.).

NO-GO / Delay pilot if any are true:
- No ability to identify upcoming appointments reliably (manual books with no consistent record).
- The business cannot confirm they have customer consent / permission to text.
- No single point of contact to handle exceptions (e.g., reschedule requests that don’t fit rules).
