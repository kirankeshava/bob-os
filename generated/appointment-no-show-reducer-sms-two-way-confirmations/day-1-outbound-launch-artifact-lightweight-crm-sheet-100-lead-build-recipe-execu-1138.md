B) 100-LEAD BUILD RECIPE (FREE SOURCES ONLY)
============================
Goal: Build 100 prospects fast (50 Phoenix, 50 Dallas) across Chiro, Med Spa, Dental.
Target mix per city:
- 20 Chiropractors
- 15 Med Spas / Aesthetics
- 15 Dentists

Step 1: Use Google Maps (fastest)
For each city, run these searches and open results in new tabs:
Phoenix searches:
- “chiropractor Phoenix AZ”
- “med spa Phoenix AZ”
- “aesthetics Phoenix AZ”
- “dentist Phoenix AZ”
Dallas searches:
- “chiropractor Dallas TX”
- “med spa Dallas TX”
- “aesthetic clinic Dallas TX”
- “dentist Dallas TX”

Step 2: For each business, capture:
- Business name, phone, website, address, Google Maps URL.

Step 3: Find the best email (priority order)
1) Website footer/contact page (look for: office manager, practice manager)
2) “About” page for owner name; then search site for email
3) If no email visible: use contact form URL (store it in Notes; still call)
Email patterns to look for:
- info@, office@, hello@, scheduling@, appointments@, admin@, manager@, frontdesk@

Step 4: Add a quick qualification note (30 seconds)
- If they advertise “same-day”, “new patient special”, “online booking”, “text us”, “free consult”, mark No-show Risk Signals.
- Estimate volume: if they have multiple providers/locations or heavy promos, mark Med/High.

Step 5: Dedupe
Before adding a new record, search the sheet by Business Name + Phone.
