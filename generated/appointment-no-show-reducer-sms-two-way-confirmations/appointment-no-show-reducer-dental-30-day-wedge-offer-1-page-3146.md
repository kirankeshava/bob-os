THE WEDGE OFFER (WHAT WE DO IN 7 DAYS)

In scope (v1):
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Smart reminders that ask patients to confirm
- Patients reply YES/NO; confirmations are logged automatically

2) Reschedule link + “no” handling
- If a patient can’t make it, they get a reschedule flow immediately
- Captures reason + preferred times to reduce back-and-forth

3) Waitlist fill for openings
- When a cancellation happens, send a broadcast to the waitlist (opted-in)
- First-to-claim flow to fill gaps quickly

4) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Confirm rate, cancel/reschedule rate, waitlist fills
- Estimated revenue recovered from filled openings (simple, owner-friendly)
