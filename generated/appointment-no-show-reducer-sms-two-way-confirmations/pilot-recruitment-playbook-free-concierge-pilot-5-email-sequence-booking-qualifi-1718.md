BOOKING + QUALIFICATION SCRIPT (15 minutes)
1) Context (30s): “Thanks—goal is to reduce no-shows this week using SMS confirmations + easy reschedule, then send you a weekly results report.”
2) Qualify (2 min):
   - “Roughly how many appointments/day?”
   - “Typical appointment value?”
   - “Current no-show or late-cancel rate estimate?”
3) Baseline capture (2 min, minimum viable):
   - “In the last ~2 weeks, about how many no-shows/late cancels did you have?”
   - “Avg value per appointment?”
   - Compute baseline lost revenue = no-shows * avg value (note: conservative)
4) Workflow decisions (6 min):
   - Timezone + business hours + days closed
   - Reminder schedule: default 24h + 2h before
   - Confirmation wording preference (friendly vs. clinical)
   - When patient replies NO: offer reschedule link OR ask preferred time windows
   - Waitlist: “Do you have a list of people who want earlier slots?” If yes, define how to notify.
   - Owner escalation: “If we can’t reach someone or a calendar update fails, who gets alerted?”
5) Compliance/consent (2 min):
   - “Do customers already provide their phone number for appointment reminders?”
   - “We include STOP to opt out in messages; OK to proceed?”
6) Close (2 min):
   - Set go-live date/time
   - Confirm success metric: “Reduce no-shows by X or increase confirmations by Y.”
   - “You’ll get a weekly report email from agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”
