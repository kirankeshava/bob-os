- Appointment-based dental practice with consistent weekly schedule volume
- Has measurable no-show/late cancel problem (even “a few per week”)
- Has someone who can approve operational changes (owner/manager)
- Willing to use SMS for confirmations (patients already texting with office is ideal)

- No patient phone numbers captured
- Refuses texting as a channel
- No operational owner/manager access after 2 attempts
