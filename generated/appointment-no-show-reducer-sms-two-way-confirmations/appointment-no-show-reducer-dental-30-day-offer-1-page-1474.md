THE WEDGE OFFER (what we implement)
Goal: reduce no-shows and fill gaps using two-way confirmations and automated rescheduling.

In scope (per location):
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
• Patients receive reminders (configurable cadence).
• They reply YES/NO to confirm.
• Confirmations update a simple dashboard and alert the team on non-responses/NO.

2) Smart reminder cadence (default)
• 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before appointment (customizable by appointment type).

3) Reschedule link (frictionless)
• If patient replies NO (or wants to move), they get a reschedule path.
• Office team gets notified to finalize if needed.

4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
• Maintain a waitlist of patients who want earlier slots.
• When a cancellation happens, the system texts the waitlist in priority order; first to confirm gets the slot.

5) Recovered-revenue analytics (basic)
• Track: confirmation rate, no-show rate trend, rescued appointments (filled gaps).
• Simple $ recovered estimate per location (based on average production per appointment you provide).

NOT in scope (first 30 days)
• Complex multi-system integrations, custom patient portals, insurance/claims workflows.
• Fully automated PMS write-backs if the PMS doesn’t support it (we start with a lightweight workflow and upgrade later).
