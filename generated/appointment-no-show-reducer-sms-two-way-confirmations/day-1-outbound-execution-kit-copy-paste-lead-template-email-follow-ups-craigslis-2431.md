## 7) Free Lead Sourcing SOP (build 200 leads today)
Goal: 2 city clusters × 5 verticals × ~20 leads each = 200.

### Pick city clusters (example)
- Cluster A: Phoenix + Scottsdale + Tempe
- Cluster B: Tampa + St Petersburg + Clearwater

### Verticals
Dentist, Chiropractor, Med Spa, Physical Therapy, Optometry.

### Google Maps method (manual, fast)
1) Search: “{{city}} {{vertical}}” (e.g., “Scottsdale chiropractor”).
2) Open top 20 listings → capture: business name, website, phone, maps URL.
3) Visit website → look for “Contact”, “About”, “Team”, “Meet the Doctor”. Capture owner/manager name + email if listed.
4) If no email listed, capture contact form URL + general email if present.
5) Add Notes: #locations (if obvious), online booking link (if any), any mention of “text reminders”.
6) Dedupe by website + phone.

### Email finding (free)
- Often listed on Contact/About pages.
- Try “site:{{domain}} email” or “site:{{domain}} contact”.
- If only a form exists, still add the lead (call-first).

### Prioritization
Call-first if: phone is clear and business seems busy (multiple providers, many reviews). Email-first if: owner/manager email is visible.

### Day-1 send/call targets
- 50–100 emails (plain text)
- 20–40 calls
- Log outcomes immediately in Status + Notes + Next Step.
