1) “We already send reminders from our system.”
“Totally. The difference is two-way confirmation + reschedule capture. One-way reminders still leave you chasing patients. Two-way lets the patient tell you ‘yes’ or ‘I need to move it’ early enough to refill the slot—then we quantify what was recovered.”

2) “We don’t want more work for the front desk.”
“Agreed—that’s the point. Confirmations happen automatically. If someone needs to reschedule, you get a simple request instead of a phone tag. And the waitlist fill can be as manual or automatic as you want.”

3) “We’re too busy / call back later.”
“Understood. That’s why I’m asking for 15 minutes. If it’s not an obvious win we’ll end the call. What’s better—tomorrow 11:30 or Thursday 2:00?”
