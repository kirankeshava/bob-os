1) Chosen Vertical (first 30 days)
Dental practices (single-location + small groups/DSOs up to ~10 locations).
Why dental first: high appointment density, clear no‑show cost, simple workflow (confirm → reschedule → backfill), typically fast decision path (owner + office manager).

2) ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
Best fit:
- 1–10 locations; hygiene + doctor schedules filled weekly
- 15+ appointments/day/location (or 300+/month)
- No‑show / late cancel problem: ~3%+ no-shows OR frequent same‑day gaps
- Uses a PMS (Dentrix/Open Dental/Eaglesoft/etc.) and can export appointments daily OR provide a simple schedule feed
Buyer: owner/operator or practice manager/office manager.
Users: front desk, hygiene coordinator.

3) Wedge Offer (scope for first 30 days)
A. Two-way SMS confirmations (core)
- Automated reminders (e.g., 72h/24h/2h) with “Confirm / Reschedule” replies
- Captures confirmation status in a simple dashboard
- Alerts staff for non-responders

B. Reschedule flow (reduce last-minute gaps)
- Patient can request new time via link or reply (two-way)
- Staff gets a clean “reschedule requested” queue (with reason + best times)

C. Waitlist gap-fill
- Create a waitlist of patients who want earlier openings
- When a gap appears, system texts waitlist in order; first to accept gets it

D. Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Tracks: confirmations, reschedules captured, gaps filled
- “Estimated revenue recovered” using your average visit value (set during onboarding)

Not included (for this 30-day wedge): full PMS integration marketplace, complex multi-department routing, insurance/payment collection, marketing automation.
