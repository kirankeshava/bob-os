THE WEDGE OFFER (WHAT’S IN SCOPE)
Goal: reduce no-shows and late cancellations by driving two-way confirmations and making rescheduling frictionless.

1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Patients receive reminders and can reply “1 to confirm / 2 to reschedule” (or similar)
- Office gets a simple feed of responses (confirmed / needs reschedule)

2) Smart reminder sequence
- Configurable timing (e.g., 72h, 24h, 2h) and message tone
- Confirmation logic reduces noise (fewer redundant reminders to confirmed patients)

3) Self-serve reschedule link
- When a patient can’t make it, they receive a link to request a new time
- Office can approve/adjust (keeps control; reduces back-and-forth calls)

4) Waitlist gap-fill (simple but high impact)
- Maintain a list of patients who want earlier appointments
- When a slot opens, send an SMS blast to the waitlist with “reply YES” to claim
- First responder gets routed to staff for quick confirm

5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Weekly snapshot: confirmations, prevented no-shows, last-minute saves, gap-fill wins
- “Recovered revenue” estimate using your stated average production per appointment
