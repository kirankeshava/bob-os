1) Chosen vertical (first 30 days)
Dental practices: single-location and small groups/DSOs (2–10 locations).
Why dental: high appointment density, meaningful no-show cost, and typically faster decision cycle (owner + office manager/front desk).

2) ICP (ideal customer profile)
Primary buyer: owner-dentist, practice administrator, or office manager.
Best-fit practice:
- 1–10 locations
- 8+ chairs or consistently booked hygiene schedule
- 300+ appointments/month per location (hygiene + restorative)
- Reports no-shows/late cancels as a persistent problem OR already uses reminders but still has gaps

3) The narrow wedge offer (scope)
Goal: reduce no-shows and fill same-week cancellations with minimal staff effort.
In-scope (per location):
A) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Smart reminders (e.g., 72h/48h/24h) with a simple “Reply 1 to confirm / 2 to reschedule” flow
- Escalation rules for non-responses
B) Reschedule flow
- A reschedule link/process that routes to the front desk workflow (simple and fast)
- Captures intent early so openings can be reused
C) Waitlist fill
- Text a short list of opt-in patients when a cancellation happens
- First-come confirmation logic to fill the gap
D) Basic recovered-revenue analytics
- Cancellations/no-shows prevented (confirmed appointments)
- Slots refilled from waitlist
- Estimated revenue recovered (you set your avg $/visit and/or hygiene value)

Out of scope (for the first 30 days): full PMS replacement, complex multi-department routing trees, custom patient marketing campaigns.

7) Qualification checklist (use on every call)
Operational readiness
- Do you have patient mobile numbers captured for most appointments?
- Can you export upcoming appointments daily/weekly (or provide a schedule report)?
- Who owns the schedule (office manager/front desk) and can approve workflow changes?
No-show pain
- How many no-shows/late cancels per week for hygiene and for doctor columns?
- Are same-week cancellations the main issue, or day-before/day-of?
Economics
- What’s your average hygiene visit value? (or production/hour)
- What’s your current fill rate when a cancellation happens?
Decision + timeline
- Who signs off (owner vs manager)?
- Can we run a 7-day launch with a single location first?

If they fail qualification:
- No patient mobile numbers, no ability to export schedule, or no owner/manager buy-in → deprioritize.
