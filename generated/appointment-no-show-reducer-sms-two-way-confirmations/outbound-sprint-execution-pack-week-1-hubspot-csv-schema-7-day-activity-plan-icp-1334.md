4) QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST (use on calls before offering a demo)
Goal: qualify in 3–5 minutes.
- Appointment volume: “How many appointments per week per location?” (Ideal: 75+/wk)
- No-show/late cancel: “Rough %?” (Ideal: 5%+)
- Value per visit: “Average $ per appointment?” (Ideal: $100+)
- Scheduling owner: “Who owns scheduling decisions—office manager, owner, or corporate?”
- Current process: “Do you already send reminders? Email or SMS? One-way or two-way confirmations?”
- Systems: “What do you use for scheduling? (Calendly, Acuity, Jane, Mindbody, Dentrix, ChiroTouch, etc.)”
Disqualify/Nurture if:
- Very low volume (<30/wk), no phone number, no control over scheduling, or already has robust two-way confirmations + waitlist fill and they’re happy.
