PRIMARY VERTICAL (First 30 days)
Dental practices (single-location offices + small DSOs with 2–10 locations).
Why dental: (1) High appointment density, (2) meaningful no-show cost (chair time + hygienist capacity), (3) simple workflows (confirm / cancel / reschedule), (4) decision maker reachable (owner + office manager).

IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)
- 1–10 locations, 3+ operatories, hygiene schedule is frequently tight.
- 15+ appointments/day per location.
- Reports 5%+ no-show / late-cancel rate OR frequent same-day schedule gaps.
- Uses any mainstream PMS (doesn’t matter at wedge stage) and has a front desk team.
- Wants a simple system that reduces phone tag and keeps the schedule full.

THE WEDGE OFFER (What we implement)
Goal: reduce no-shows and fill last-minute gaps with two-way confirmations + fast rescheduling + waitlist fill.
In scope (per location):
1) Two-way SMS confirmations: patients can reply Y to confirm, N to cancel, or RESCHEDULE.
2) Smart reminders: configurable cadence (e.g., 72h + 24h + 2h) tuned for dental.
3) Reschedule link/workflow: when a patient can’t make it, we route them to reschedule (and notify staff).
4) Waitlist fill: when a slot opens, we text a prioritized waitlist and book the first responder.
5) Basic analytics: no-show rate trend + confirmations + “recovered appointments” estimate + recovered revenue estimate.

Out of scope (for this 30-day wedge): full PMS deep integration, custom patient portal, complex multi-department routing. If needed, we start with the simplest data path that works (export, daily list, or lightweight sync) to get results fast.
