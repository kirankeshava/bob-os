## 2) Screening / Booking Script (15 minutes)
Goal: qualify, set expectations, secure “yes” for a 7-day pilot, and schedule onboarding.

**0:00–1:00 Intro**
- “Hi {{Name}}, thanks for taking the time. I’m Bob. In 15 minutes I’ll confirm whether we can reduce no-shows for you and what a 7‑day pilot would look like.”
- Legitimacy: “If you want to reference anything after the call, the site is https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2 and email is agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”

**1:00–5:00 Current state (baseline)**
Ask and record:
1) “Roughly how many appointments per week per location?”
2) “What’s your estimated no-show rate today?”
3) “Average value per appointment (or first visit)?”
4) “Do you currently send reminders? (SMS/email/phone). Are they one-way or two-way?”
5) “When do no-shows hurt most—new clients, high-ticket services, specific days?”

**5:00–8:00 Workflow fit**
1) “Do you want clients to be able to confirm by replying YES?”
2) “When someone replies NO, do you prefer: reschedule link, staff callback, or waitlist fill?”
3) “Do you keep a waitlist today? If not, would you want a simple ‘next available’ list?”
4) “Do you have multiple staff/providers and risk of double-booking?”

**8:00–11:00 Pilot scope & expectations**
- “Pilot is concierge-only: we configure reminders, confirmation rules, and reschedule handling. We monitor daily and catch edge cases (STOP/HELP, late replies, timezone).”
- “Success metrics we’ll report weekly: confirmation rate, reschedules captured, waitlist fills, and estimated recovered revenue.”
- “We’ll also track failures safely: if anything can’t be verified (like calendar access), we alert you rather than guessing.”

**11:00–13:00 Eligibility + compliance**
- “Do you already have customer phone numbers and consent to text (e.g., standard appointment SMS consent)?”
- “We must honor STOP immediately; are you okay with that?”
- “Who is the escalation contact if something looks off?”

**13:00–15:00 Close**
If qualified:
- “You’re a fit. Want to be one of the 2–3 free pilots? If yes, the next step is a 25–30 minute onboarding where we collect reminder timing, reschedule rules, and baseline numbers.”
- Schedule onboarding time.
If not qualified:
- “I don’t want to waste your time—here’s what you’d need first: {{missing item}}. If you fix that, we can revisit.”
