B) IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP) + QUALIFYING CRITERIA
Primary ICP (fastest decision cycle):
- General dentistry practices, single location or 2–10 locations (small DSO)
- 6+ operatories OR multiple hygienists OR high daily appointment volume
- Accepts new patients and cares about schedule utilization
- Decision-maker reachable: owner dentist and/or office manager (often de facto operator)

High-fit signals:
- Mentions “missed appointments”, “late cancellations”, “no-show fees not enforced”, “hygiene gaps”, “reactivation”, “waitlist”
- Online booking or digital forms (indicates openness to tools)

Disqualifiers (for first 30 days):
- Very low volume (solo practitioner with sparse schedule)
- No texting allowed / refuses SMS communication
- Cannot export appointments or provide any daily schedule feed (even manual)

Qualification questions (use on call/demo):
1) “About how many appointments do you have per day—hygiene + doctor side?”
2) “What’s your rough no-show / same-day cancellation rate today?”
3) “Do you currently text patients? If yes, is it one-way or can they confirm by replying?”
4) “When someone cancels, do you have a waitlist and is it used consistently?”
5) “Who owns the schedule utilization KPI—office manager or doctor/owner?”
