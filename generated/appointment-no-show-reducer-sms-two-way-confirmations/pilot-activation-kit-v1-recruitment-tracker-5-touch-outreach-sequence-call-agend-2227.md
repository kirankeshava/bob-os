3) 15-MINUTE DISCOVERY / PILOT CLOSE AGENDA
Goal: leave the call with (a) pilot commitment, (b) baseline metrics, (c) reminder/reschedule rules.

Minute 0–2: Context
- “What’s your current reminder process? Where do no-shows hurt most (service types/times)?”

Minute 2–6: Baseline capture (required for week-1 proof)
- Weekly appointments (#)
- No-show rate estimate (% last 4 weeks)
- Average appointment value ($)
- Cancellation window policy

Minute 6–10: Workflow decisions
- Reminder schedule (e.g., 24h and 2h)
- Confirm/reschedule behavior:
  - If YES: mark confirmed
  - If NO: offer 2–3 reschedule options or route to staff
  - If RESCHEDULE: same as NO
- Waitlist: do they have one? (yes/no; how stored)

Minute 10–13: Compliance + guardrails
- Confirm they only message customers who have provided their number for appointment communication
- Confirm opt-out language included (STOP)
- Escalation contact if calendar/integration fails

Minute 13–15: Close
- “If I can run this as a concierge pilot at no cost for 7 days and send a weekly value report, are you comfortable starting on {{StartDate}}?”
- Schedule go-live date and who approves messaging tone.

4) PILOT CONSENT + OPT-OUT BLURB (use in onboarding email + first message footer)
“By providing your mobile number when booking, you agree to receive appointment-related text messages (reminders and confirmations). Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.”
