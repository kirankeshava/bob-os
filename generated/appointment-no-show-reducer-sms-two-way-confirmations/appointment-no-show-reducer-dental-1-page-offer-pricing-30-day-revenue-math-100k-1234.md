IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)
• 1–10 locations; 1–6 providers/location; hygiene + restorative schedule.
• 30–200 appointments/week/location.
• No-show / late-cancel problem is visible (≥3 per week) OR schedule has frequent holes.
• Has an office manager/front desk lead who owns reminders/confirmations.

THE WEDGE OFFER (what we implement)
Goal: reduce no-shows + fill gaps by turning reminders into two-way conversations.
In scope per location:
1) Two-way SMS confirmations: Patients can confirm/cancel/reschedule by texting back.
2) Smart reminders: timed reminders (e.g., 72h/24h/2h) based on appointment type.
3) Reschedule flow: when a patient cancels/requests change, they get a simple reschedule path.
4) Waitlist fill: if a slot opens, we broadcast to an opt-in waitlist to backfill openings.
5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics: monthly snapshot per location (confirmed %, prevented cancellations, slots filled, estimated revenue recovered).

NOT in scope (for the first 30 days)
• Deep PMS integrations requiring paid connectors (we start lightweight).
• Reputation management, review requests, or marketing campaigns.
• Complex multi-department routing beyond basic office-manager workflow.
