## Qualification checklist (use on first call)
A lead is “Qualified” if 4/6 are true:
1) 200+ appointments/month (or feels “fully booked most days”)
2) No-show + late-cancel is at least ~3% (or they complain about it)
3) They currently do reminders but not true two-way confirmations + automated reschedules
4) They can define a $ value per hour/slot (hygiene and/or chair time)
5) They have a waitlist (even informal) or frequent “can you get me in sooner?” calls
6) Office manager can approve a $399/mo ops tool (or can bring owner into a 15-min decision call)
Disqualifiers:
- Extremely low volume (e.g., <80 appts/month)
- Refuses SMS or lacks consent process (we can help, but if they won’t do it, it won’t work)
