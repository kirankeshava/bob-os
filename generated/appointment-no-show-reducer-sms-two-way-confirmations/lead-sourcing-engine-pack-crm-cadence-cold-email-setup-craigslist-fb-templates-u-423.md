3) OUTREACH CADENCE (email + call)
Day 1: Email #1 (value + question) + optional call 2–4 hours later
Day 3: Email #2 (quick case math + offer)
Day 6: Email #3 (short “should I close the file?”)
Day 10: Email #4 (resource + waitlist angle)

Rules:

- If they click/visit but no reply: call same or next day.
- If “not now”: move to Nurture and set Next Touch Date +30 days.

4) COLD EMAIL INFRASTRUCTURE — NO-SPEND CHECKLIST
Goal: maximize deliverability; avoid spam flags.
A) Domain/inbox
- Preferred: separate sending domain (e.g., get[brand].com). If no spend allowed, use existing email temporarily for 1:1 low-volume outreach only.
- Create 1–2 inboxes dedicated to outbound.

B) DNS authentication (must-do)
- SPF: authorize sending provider
- DKIM: enabled in provider
- DMARC: p=none initially, then tighten later
- Custom tracking domain if available; if not, avoid click tracking early.

C) Warmup plan (manual, free)
Week 1:
- Day 1–2: 10/day (handwritten, plain text)
- Day 3–4: 15/day
- Day 5–7: 20/day
Week 2: 30–40/day (still plain text, minimal links)
Week 3+: 50–80/day if reply rates hold and spam complaints = 0

D) Sending best practices
- Plain text, short sentences, no images.
- 0–1 links max (use the legitimacy URL only when needed).
- Avoid spammy words (FREE!!!, guarantee, etc.).
- Use a real signature with name + email.

E) Tracking (free options)
- Use CRM “Last Touch/Next Touch” fields for tracking.
- Avoid open tracking pixel during warmup (can hurt deliverability).
