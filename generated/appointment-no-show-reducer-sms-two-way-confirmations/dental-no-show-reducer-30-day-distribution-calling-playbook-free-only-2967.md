“Hi—this is Bob. Quick question: who handles your appointment reminders and no-show follow-up—would that be you or your office manager?”

“Perfect. The reason I’m calling: we help dental practices reduce no-shows and late cancellations using two-way text confirmations, an easy reschedule link, and a waitlist to fill openings. It’s not a big software rip-and-replace—more like a lightweight layer.

Can I ask: do you currently text patients to confirm, and can patients reply to confirm/cancel?”

- “Is hygiene where you feel it the most, or restorative too?”
