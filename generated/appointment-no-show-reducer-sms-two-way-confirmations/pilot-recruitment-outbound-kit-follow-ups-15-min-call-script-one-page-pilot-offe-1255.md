=== 3) 15-Minute Pilot Discovery/Onboarding Call Script ===

Goal of the call:
1) Confirm they’re a fit (appointment-based, no-show pain, ability to message clients)
2) Capture baseline metrics + operational rules
3) Close a 7-day concierge pilot with a clear start date and success criteria

0:00–1:30 Intro + positioning
“Thanks {{Name}} — I’m Bob Smith. We help appointment-based businesses reduce no-shows using SMS reminders with two-way confirmations. Clients can confirm, cancel, or reschedule by replying, and we track the outcomes in a weekly value report. Quick legitimacy/info page here if helpful: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2. You can always reach us at agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to.”

1:30–4:00 Qualify the workflow
• “What type of appointments do you run (services, typical durations)?”
• “How do you schedule today (software or manual)?”
• “Roughly how many appointments per week per location?”
• “What’s your current no-show rate (even a guess)?”
• “What happens today when someone cancels late or doesn’t show?”
Fit check (say out loud): “This works best if (a) you have enough volume for the numbers to show quickly, and (b) you’re willing to let SMS confirmations drive simple actions like confirm/reschedule.”

4:00–7:00 Baseline metrics (capture for proof)
Ask and record:
• Timezone: ___
• Weekly appointments: ___
• Current no-shows/week (or %): ___
• Average appointment value ($): ___
• Typical lead time (same-day vs booked ahead): ___
Compute on call (rough): “If you do {{weekly}} appts/week and no-show is {{%}}, that’s about {{no-shows}} missed appts/week. At ${{avg}}, that’s roughly ${{lost}} potential. If we recover even 20–40% of that, it’s meaningful.”

7:00–11:00 Pilot setup rules (high-leverage specifics)
• Reminder timing: “Do you prefer 48h + 4h, or 24h + 2h, or something else?”
• Message tone: “Friendly and short, or more formal?”
• Reschedule policy: “If someone replies RESCHEDULE, do you want to (a) send them a link, (b) propose 2–3 times, or (c) have staff call them?”
• Waitlist: “Do you have a list of clients who want earlier slots? If a cancellation happens, can we notify waitlist?”
• Owner/staff escalation: “If anything looks wrong, who is the escalation contact (name + phone/email)?”
• Compliance: “We include STOP to opt out and HELP for support. Are you OK with that standard language?”

11:00–13:30 Close the 7-day pilot
“Here’s the pilot: we run this for 7 days with concierge monitoring. We’ll report confirmations, reschedules, any filled gaps, and estimated recovered revenue. If it’s not clearly working, you don’t continue. If it is, we convert to a paid plan after the pilot.”
Close questions:
• “Do you want to start this week or next?”
• “Which location/provider should we start with first?”

13:30–15:00 Next steps + calendar
• Confirm go-live date/time and timezone.
• Confirm what they’ll send you immediately after the call (baseline numbers, reminder preference, any scheduling access they can share).
• “I’ll email a short recap from agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to with the checklist and start date.”
