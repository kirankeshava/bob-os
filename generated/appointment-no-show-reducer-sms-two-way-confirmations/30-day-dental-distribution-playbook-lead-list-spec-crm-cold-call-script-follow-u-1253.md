## ICP + qualification (use to focus list)
**Ideal customer profile (Dental):**
- Single location or 2–10 locations.
- 1,000+ appointments/month OR consistently booked hygiene/doctor schedules.
- Reports no-show/cancel issues OR has same-day gaps.
- Has an office manager / front desk team; owner is reachable.

**Qualification checklist (ask on first call):**
1) “Roughly how many appointments per week do you run?”
2) “How often do you get same-day cancellations or no-shows?”
3) “Do you already text patients? If yes, can patients reply to confirm/reschedule?”
4) “Do you keep a short-call list/waitlist today? How is it managed?”
5) “If we filled 2–5 extra appointments/month, would that be meaningful?”

Disqualifiers (for now):
- Very low volume (e.g., <200 appts/month).
- No texting allowed / refuses patient SMS.
- Can’t commit to a 15-minute workflow review.

**Primary source: Google Maps**
Search queries (copy/paste):
- “dentist near me”
- “family dentistry”
- “cosmetic dentist”
- “dental clinic”
- “pediatric dentist” (optional)

Filters:
- Prioritize practices with websites and visible phone numbers.
- Prioritize 4.0+ rating (operationally stable) AND those with recent reviews mentioning “appointment”, “waiting”, “reschedule”, “text/reminder”.
- Avoid hospital/academic clinics initially.

**Secondary sources:**
- State dental association member directories.
- Local chamber of commerce business directory.
- “Dentist + city” lists from reputable local directories (verify each).

**Decision-maker titles to find:**
- Office Manager, Practice Manager, Operations Manager, Front Desk Lead
- Owner Dentist, Managing Dentist

How to find contacts (free):
- Practice website → “About/Team” page.
- Call and ask: “Who manages scheduling and patient communications?”
- If no name: use role-based email on website (info@, office@) and address to Office Manager.
