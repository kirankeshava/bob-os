## 7-day distribution execution plan (no spend)

1) Pick **2 metros** (dense dental markets; easier list building). Example: Phoenix + Dallas.
2) Define ICP filters:

Sequencing per account (simple multichannel):

- Touch 2 (Day 3): Call again + short “bump” email.
