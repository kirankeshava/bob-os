THE WEDGE OFFER (What we implement)
Goal: Fewer no-shows + fewer unfilled gaps, without adding staff time.
In-scope features (per location):
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
   • Patients can reply YES/NO (or tap confirm link)
   • Non-responders get smart follow-ups
2) Smart reminder sequence
   • Configurable timing windows (e.g., 72h/24h/2h) and office hours
3) Reschedule flow
   • “Can’t make it?” prompt routes to a reschedule link or office workflow
   • Captures intent early so the slot can be refilled
4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
   • When a patient cancels/declines, we message your waitlist segment to fill the opening
   • First-come-first-served logic; staff can approve if desired
5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics
   • Dashboard/email summary showing: confirmations, cancels captured early, slots refilled, and estimated revenue recovered per location
