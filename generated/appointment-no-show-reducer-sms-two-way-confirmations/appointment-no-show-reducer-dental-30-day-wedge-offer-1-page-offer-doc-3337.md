### The Wedge Offer (What’s Included)
1) **Two-way SMS confirmations**
- “Reply 1 to confirm / 2 to reschedule” style messages
- Confirms are logged; non-responses are nudged

2) **Smart reminder sequence (configurable)**
- Example: 72h, 24h, and 2h before appointment
- Different flows for hygiene vs. restorative (optional)

3) **Reschedule link + escalation rules**
- If patient replies “reschedule,” they get a guided next-step
- Optional escalation to front desk when needed (edge cases)

4) **Waitlist gap-fill (simple + effective)**
- When a slot opens, we notify opted-in waitlist patients in order
- First to respond gets the slot; others get follow-ups

5) **Recovered-revenue analytics (basic, per location)**
- # confirmations captured
- # reschedules processed
- # slots filled from waitlist
- **Estimated revenue recovered** (using your provided average value per appt type)

**Implementation promise:** focus on the smallest feature set that produces measurable reduction in no-shows within 14–30 days.
