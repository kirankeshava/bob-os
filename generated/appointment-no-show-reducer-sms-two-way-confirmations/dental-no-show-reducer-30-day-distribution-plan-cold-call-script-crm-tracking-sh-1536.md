COLD CALL SCRIPT (Dental)
ROLE: You are calling a dental practice. You want either the office manager/practice admin or owner. Keep it short.

1) FRONT DESK GATEKEEPER OPEN
“Hi — is the office manager available for a quick question about missed appointments and confirmations?”
If they ask what it’s about:
“Totally — we help practices reduce no-shows using two-way SMS confirmations and easy rescheduling, so the schedule stays full. It’s not a directory or ads. Who’s best to speak with?”

2) OFFICE MANAGER / PRACTICE ADMIN OPEN (15 seconds)
“Hi [Name], this is Bob. Quick one — do you currently confirm patients by text, and can patients reply to confirm or reschedule?”
(Stop. Let them answer.)

If YES (they text already):
“Got it. When a patient needs to reschedule last minute, do you have a waitlist process that automatically fills the opening, or is it manual calls?”
Then:
“We built a small system that does two-way confirmations + reschedule link, then fills openings from a waitlist. It also tracks recovered revenue per month so you can see if it’s working. Here’s the proof page we share: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2. If it’s not useful, you can cancel anytime. Worth 12 minutes to see how it plugs into your current workflow?”

If NO (they don’t do two-way confirmations):
