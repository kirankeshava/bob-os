**Opener (10 seconds)**
“Hi, is this the office manager? … Hi [Name], I’m Bob. Quick call—do you handle confirmations and last‑minute cancellations there?”

“Got it. The reason I’m calling: we run a simple two‑way SMS confirmation + reschedule flow that cuts no‑shows and helps fill gaps from a waitlist. It’s not a new phone system—just automated texting and a lightweight dashboard that shows recovered revenue. Can I ask one question to see if it’s even relevant?”

**Qualifying question (pick one)**
1) “On a typical week, do you lose more from no‑shows or from last‑minute cancellations?”

“Yep—that’s exactly what we fix. Patients get reminders, but the key is they can reply CONFIRM or RESCHEDULE. If they reschedule, we open that slot and text the waitlist to backfill it. We track how many appointments we recovered so you can quantify it.”
