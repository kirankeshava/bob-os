1) Open Google Maps and search:
- “chiropractor Phoenix AZ”
- “med spa Phoenix AZ”
- “dentist Phoenix AZ”

2) For each location, capture:
- Business name, website, phone, address, Google Maps URL.
3) Find emails (free):
- Visit website → Contact page / footer
- Look for: office@, info@, appointments@, manager@, admin@, hello@
- Capture contact name/title if listed (Office Manager/Practice Manager).
4) Dedupe:
- Remove duplicates by website domain + phone.
- If multi-location groups, keep each location as separate row but note “group” in Notes.
