B) ICP + QUALIFICATION (fast decision + high no-show pain)
Ideal customer profile (dental):
- Single-location practice OR small group (2–10 locations)
- 5+ chairs OR high hygiene schedule volume
- Uses a PMS (Dentrix/OpenDental/Eaglesoft/etc.) but doesn’t require deep integration initially
- Has frequent same-day gaps and/or 5+ no-shows per week
- Decision maker reachable: owner/dentist, practice manager, office manager

Quick qualification checklist (ask on first call):
1) How many appointments per day (doctor + hygiene)?
2) Roughly how many no-shows/cancellations per week?
3) Do you currently send reminders (text/email/calls)? Is it one-way or two-way?
4) When someone cancels, do you have a waitlist and do you actually fill gaps?
5) Who owns scheduling process: front desk vs office manager?
6) Are you open to a 7-day free launch if we can show recovered visits?

Disqualifiers (do not spend time):
- “We’re not taking new patients” and schedule already overbooked with no gaps
- No texting allowed in their workflow or extreme legal constraints they won’t move on
- Cannot provide any appointment export/list (even minimal)

E) OBJECTION HANDLING (DENTAL-SPECIFIC)
1) “We already have reminders.”
“Totally — most do. The difference is two-way confirmations + automated reschedule + waitlist fill. One-way reminders still leave you with same-day holes when someone cancels. Are patients able to confirm/cancel by replying today, and do you automatically backfill from a waitlist?”

2) “Patients hate texts.”
“We keep it minimal and consent-based, and it’s focused on reducing missed visits. Most practices find patients prefer a quick confirm-by-text rather than calls. We can tune frequency and timing.”

3) “We don’t want another system.”
“Understood. This is intentionally lightweight. We can start with just confirmations + reschedules for 7 days and show measurable recovered visits before you commit.”

4) “Integration is a problem.”
“We can start without deep integration: a daily/weekly appointment export is enough to prove value. If it’s working, we can discuss deeper integration later.”

5) “How much does it cost?”
“We run a free 7-day launch so you can see confirmations and reschedules working. After that it’s a simple per-location monthly plan. If it doesn’t reduce gaps measurably, you can cancel — no lock-in.”
