THE WEDGE OFFER (WHAT WE DO IN WEEK 1)
Goal: reduce no-shows and late cancellations by making confirmation/reschedule frictionless and filling gaps from a waitlist.

IN SCOPE FEATURES
1) Two-way SMS confirmations
- Patients receive a text that asks them to confirm.
- Replies are captured (YES/NO) and logged.

2) Smart reminder sequence
- Configurable reminders (e.g., 72h / 24h / 2h).
- Message copy tailored for dental (hygiene vs. production appointments if you choose to separate later).

3) Reschedule flow
- If patient can’t make it, they receive a reschedule prompt/link (or a simple SMS-based flow).
- Front desk gets a clear signal to rebook rather than discovering a no-show.

4) Waitlist fill (gap recovery)
- When a cancellation/no-confirm occurs, the system can message a waitlist segment to fill the slot.
- Simple “first to reply wins” workflow to help the front desk recover chair time.

5) Basic recovered-revenue analytics (per location)
- Tracks: confirmations, prevented no-shows (proxy), filled gaps, and estimated revenue recovered.
- Simple weekly summary to show ROI in dollars.

OUT OF SCOPE (FOR THIS 30-DAY WEDGE)
- Deep PMS integrations requiring paid connectors.
- Complex multi-step marketing automations unrelated to appointment attendance.
- Long-term recall campaigns (possible upsell later).
