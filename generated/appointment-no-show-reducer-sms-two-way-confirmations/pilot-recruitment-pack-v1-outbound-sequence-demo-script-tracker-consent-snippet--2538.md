D) 15-MINUTE DEMO / PILOT ONBOARDING CALL SCRIPT
============================================================
Goal: book pilot + collect baseline metrics + confirm consent/opt-in approach.

1) Set context (1 min)
“Thanks—this is a 7-day free concierge pilot to reduce no-shows with two-way SMS confirmations. At the end we’ll send you a weekly report quantifying outcomes and estimated revenue recovered.”

2) Qualify quickly (3 min)
- “About how many appointments do you have per week?”
- “Roughly what’s your average appointment value (or typical first-visit value)?”
- “What’s your best estimate of your no-show rate over the last month?”
- “Do you already send reminders? If yes, what timing and channel?”

3) Explain workflow (4 min)
- “We send reminders at your chosen times (e.g., 24h + 2h).”
- “Patients reply YES to confirm; NO/RESCHEDULE routes to rescheduling prompts.”
- “If someone opts out (STOP), we stop immediately.”
- “If anything fails (e.g., calendar sync), we alert you and fall back safely (no spam, no double booking).”

4) Pilot setup requirements (5 min)
- Timezone + business hours
- Services (if different durations)
- Reminder timing rules
- Reschedule rules (same week vs next available)
- Waitlist rules (if used)
- Owner escalation contact
- Baseline numbers (the 3 from above) to calculate recovered revenue

5) Consent / compliance confirmation (1–2 min)
“Where do phone numbers come from (online booking form / intake / existing clients)? We’ll use only customers who’ve provided their number for appointment communication. If you’d like, we can add a one-line consent notice to your intake/booking confirmation.”

Close:
“If you confirm the above today, we can go live within 24–48 hours and start reporting after week 1.”
