B) 15-MINUTE ONBOARDING / CLOSE SCRIPT (BOOKING + DATA COLLECTION)
====================

Goal: Close a 7-day free concierge pilot today. Collect baseline metrics and operational rules.

1) Open (60 seconds)
“Thanks for taking the time. I’m Bob. The goal today is simple: confirm whether you’re a fit for a 7-day free pilot to reduce no-shows using two-way SMS confirmations, reschedules, and optional waitlist fills. If we can’t measure improvement vs. baseline, we stop.”

2) Confirm the appointment flow (2 minutes)
- “What type of appointments do you book? (services, duration, typical lead time)”
- “How do bookings come in today? (phone, online, walk-in)”
- “Where is your schedule tracked? (software name) / do you have multiple staff calendars?”

3) Baseline capture (3 minutes)
- “In the last 4 weeks, roughly how many appointments/week?”
- “What’s your estimated no-show rate (or late cancellations)?”
- “Average value per appointment (or range)?”
- “Do no-shows create empty chair time that can’t be recovered same-day?”

4) Pilot success criteria (2 minutes)
“Success for the 7-day pilot means we can show at least one of:
- increased confirmation rate
- fewer no-shows / earlier cancellations
- measurable reschedules completed
- gaps filled from waitlist
…and we’ll estimate recovered revenue conservatively based on your avg appointment value.”

5) Rules + customer experience settings (4 minutes)
- Timezone + business hours: “What timezone are you in? When should we avoid messaging?”
- Reminder schedule: “Do you want reminders 24h + 2h before? Or something else?”
- Confirmation logic: “Do you want customers to confirm every time?”
- Reschedule rules: “If someone says ‘reschedule’, should we offer 2–3 options or route to staff?”
- Waitlist: “Do you currently have a waitlist? How many people could opt into short-notice openings?”

6) Compliance + opt-out (1 minute)
“We’ll include opt-out language. Customers can reply STOP to end messages. If anyone opts out, we don’t message again. You confirm you have permission to message your customers about their appointments.”

7) Close + next steps (2 minutes)
“Great—if you’re in, we’ll start the 7-day pilot on {{StartDate}}. Today I’ll email a short pilot agreement summary and the few items we need (timezone, reminder timing, baseline numbers). You’ll get a weekly report at day 7. Best email for that report?”
