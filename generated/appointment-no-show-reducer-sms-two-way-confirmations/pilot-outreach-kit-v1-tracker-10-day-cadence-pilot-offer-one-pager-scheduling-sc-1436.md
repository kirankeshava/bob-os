2) 10-Day Pilot Recruitment + Activation Cadence (30 prospects → 2–3 pilots)
Day 0 (Prep): Build list of 30 local businesses with online booking and visible phone/email. Prioritize niches with high no-show cost (medspa, dental, chiro/PT, salons, clinics).
Day 1: Send Email Touch #1 (personalized opener + pilot offer). Log in tracker.
Day 2: Send LinkedIn connection + short DM to the same prospect (if profile found). If no LinkedIn, do Email Touch #2.
Day 3: Follow-up Email Touch #2 (value + 2 questions). Add legitimacy URL again.
Day 4: If phone is publicly posted and compliant, send SMS Touch #1 (very short) OR call front desk for owner/manager email.
Day 5: Email Touch #3 (“close the loop” + ask for referral). 
Day 6–7: Run 15-min onboarding calls for responders. Collect baseline (last 4 weeks no-show rate + average appointment value).
Day 8: Go-live for Pilot #1 (concierge setup). Confirm opt-out language and business hours/timezone.
Day 9: Start daily monitoring + incident log. Capture confirmations/reschedules.
Day 10: Repeat go-live for Pilot #2 (and #3 if ready). Schedule weekly report send date.
Success targets by Day 10: 30 outreaches → 6–10 replies → 3 booked calls → 2–3 pilots live.
