2) ICP + QUALIFICATION (WHO WE TARGET FIRST)
Primary ICP (fast decision cycle):
- Dental practices with 2–10 ops OR 1–3 doctors; single-location and small groups
- Front desk/office manager handles scheduling and confirmations
- Appointment volume: 15+ scheduled visits/day OR 300+/month
- Reported no-show or late-cancel pain (even “just a few a week”)

Disqualifiers (deprioritize early):
- Practices that already use two-way confirmation effectively (e.g., robust integrated reminders with high response rates)
- Very low appointment volume (<150/mo)
- No ability/willingness to send SMS to patients (policy restrictions)

Quick qualification questions (ask on first call):
1) “About how many patients are on the schedule per day?”
2) “What’s your current no-show / same-day cancel situation like?”
3) “Do you do any two-way texting right now, or is it mostly one-way reminders?”
4) “If a slot opens, do you have a waitlist process or is it manual calling?”
5) “Who besides you needs to approve something like this—doctor/owner?”
