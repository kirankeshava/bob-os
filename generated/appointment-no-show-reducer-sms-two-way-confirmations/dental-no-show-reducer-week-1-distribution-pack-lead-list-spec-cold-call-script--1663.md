A) Lead list build spec (free, repeatable)

- 6+ operatories, 1+ hygienist schedule, 10+ appointments/day.

B) Cold-call script (Office Manager first)
Objective: Book a 15-minute demo. Keep it short, respectful, specific.

Opening (10 seconds):
“Hi—this is Bob. Quick question: are you the office manager for [Practice Name]?”

If yes:
“Great. I’ll be brief— I help dental practices cut no-shows and last-minute cancels using two-way text confirmations and easy reschedule links. It’s not marketing—just keeps today’s schedule full. Can I ask: do you lose a few appointments a week to no-shows/cancels?”

If they say yes / sometimes:
“Got it. We typically recover a few appointments per week by getting patients to confirm or reschedule early, and then filling gaps from a waitlist. We also show recovered revenue by location so it’s obvious what you’re getting.
I can show you in 15 minutes how it works. Would today at 2:30 or tomorrow at 10 work?”

If they say “We already use reminders / our PMS does that”:
“Totally—most systems send reminders. The difference is two-way confirmations + automation when they reply ‘No’ or ‘Reschedule’, plus waitlist fill to backfill openings. Basic reminders don’t usually prevent the gap; they just notify.
Worth a quick look to see if it would reduce the scramble at the front desk. Same question—15 minutes today or tomorrow?”

If they say “Send info”:
“Sure—what’s the best email for you? I’ll send a 1-page overview and the link.
Also, if we don’t put 15 minutes on the calendar it tends to get buried. Can we pencil a quick time and you can cancel if it’s not relevant?”

If they ask “What is it / do you have a site?”
“Yes—here’s the overview page: https://6ff73b98-dcff-4f53-a10d-9ad392f63342-00-1n4h4nuiq6w8j.picard.replit.dev/sites/2 and you can reach me at agent_bob_replit+no-show-bot@agentmail.to. The demo is easiest because I’ll map it to your workflow.”

Close (always):

D) 15-minute demo script (structure + close)
Goal: Diagnose, show workflow, propose 7-day free launch (Week 1 policy), set expectation for conversion at day 7.

0–2 min: Set agenda
“Thanks—goal is to understand your no-show/cancel problem, show the two-way confirmation + reschedule + waitlist flow, and decide if it’s worth a 7-day free launch. Sound good?”

2–6 min: Qualify (ask, then mirror)
1) “Roughly how many appointments per day across doctors + hygiene?”
2) “How many no-shows or same-day cancels per week?”
3) “How do you handle it today—manual calls/texts? PMS reminders? Any waitlist?”
4) “When a patient cancels, how often can you fill the opening?”

- “You get a simple weekly report: confirmations, reschedules, filled gaps, and estimated recovered revenue.”
