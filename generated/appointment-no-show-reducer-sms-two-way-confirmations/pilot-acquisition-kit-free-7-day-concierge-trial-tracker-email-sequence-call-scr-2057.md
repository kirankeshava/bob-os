C) 15-MINUTE PILOT DEMO / ONBOARDING CALL SCRIPT
=============================
Goal: qualify + secure consent + schedule go-live.

1) Context (1 min)
“Thanks for the time. I’m Bob. We reduce no-shows with SMS reminders + two-way confirmations and a weekly report that quantifies recovered revenue.”

2) Quick qualification (4 min)
- “About how many appointments per week?”
- “What’s your rough no-show rate over the last month?”
- “Average value of an appointment?”
- “Do you have a waitlist or same-day fill process today?”

3) Workflow overview (4 min)
- “We send reminders at {24h and 2h} (adjustable).”
- “Client texts back YES (confirmed), NO (cancel), or RESCHEDULE.”
- “If reschedule/cancel opens a gap, we can notify a waitlist.”
- “STOP opt-out honored immediately; HELP provides instructions.”

4) Consent + compliance (3 min)
- “Do you already have SMS consent language in your intake/booking flow?”
- “For the pilot, we need confirmation you have consent to text appointment reminders, and we include opt-out in messages.”
- “We’ll only message about scheduled appointments; no marketing messages.”

5) Pilot logistics + success metric (2 min)
- “Pilot is free for 7 days. We monitor daily and send a weekly value report.”
- “Success for week 1: increase confirmations, reduce no-shows, capture reschedules, and document any gaps filled.”

Close
“Are you comfortable proceeding with the free pilot? If yes, let’s pick a go-live date within the next 48 hours and I’ll send a short pilot confirmation email.”
