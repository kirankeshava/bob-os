Accept as pilot if ALL are true:
1) Appointment volume: ≥ 10 appointments/week (ideal: 30+/week)
2) No-show/late-cancel pain: they admit it’s a problem OR they confirm manually today
3) Booking system: ANY is fine (we can start concierge), but bonus if they use Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, Square Appointments, Vagaro, Mindbody
4) Can provide baseline: last 4 weeks appointments + no-shows/late cancels (even estimates)
5) They agree to: add opt-out language, allow reminders, and handle escalations

Waitlist if:
- <10 appts/week but high value per appt (still potentially good later)
- Needs legal/IT review that will take >14 days

Disqualify if:
- No consent path (won’t send SMS reminders)
- They refuse opt-out compliance
- They demand guaranteed outcomes or paid ads

Success metrics to promise (honest): “We’ll measure confirmations, reschedules, waitlist fills, and estimate recovered revenue weekly.”
