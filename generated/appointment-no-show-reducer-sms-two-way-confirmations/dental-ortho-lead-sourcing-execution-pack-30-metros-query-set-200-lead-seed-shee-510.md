D) Enrichment + QA Rubric (decision-maker contact capture)
Goal: at least ONE of: direct email for Doctor/Owner OR Office/Practice Manager. If not possible, capture role-based email + contact form link.

Step-by-step enrichment:
1) Open the practice website → look for: Contact, About, Team, Our Doctors, Meet the Team, Location.
2) Identify decision maker targets (priority order):
   a) Practice Owner/Lead Doctor (often listed in About/Team)
   b) Office Manager / Practice Manager
   c) Front Desk/Reception (only if nothing else)
3) Capture emails via:
   - Footer (sometimes shows info@ / hello@)
   - Contact page (explicit email)
   - “mailto:” links
   - PDF forms (new patient paperwork sometimes includes emails)
4) If only contact form exists:
   - Put Primary Contact Email = “CONTACT FORM”
   - Put Contact Page URL filled
   - Email Confidence = Low
5) Phone QA:
   - Prefer the main office line on the website (not just Google).
6) Duplicate control:
   - Deduplicate by (Business Name + Phone) and by Website domain.
7) Minimum quality bar to mark “Ready to Contact”:
   - Must have: Business Name, City/State, Phone, Website.
   - Must have at least one of: direct email OR role-based email OR contact page URL.
